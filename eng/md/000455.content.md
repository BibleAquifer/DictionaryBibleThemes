## 4480: milk (id: 4480)

A common source of food and nourishment, associated with rich and productive land. The term is also used figuratively as a form of spiritual food suitable for those who have yet to reach maturity in the Christian faith.

Milk as a source of nourishment
-------------------------------

**[DEU 32:13–32:14](https://ref.ly/Deut32:13-Deut32:14)** *See also* [GEN 18:8](https://ref.ly/Gen18:8); [JDG 4:19](https://ref.ly/Judg4:19); [2SA 17:29](https://ref.ly/2Sam17:29); [PRO 27:27](https://ref.ly/Prov27:27); [EZK 25:4](https://ref.ly/Ezek25:4); [1CO 9:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:7) *illustrating the support of the ministry*
