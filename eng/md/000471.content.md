## 4512: staff (id: 4512)

**[GEN 32:10](https://ref.ly/Gen32:10)** *See also* [EXO 12:11](https://ref.ly/Exod12:11); [MAT 10:5–10:10](https://ref.ly/Matt10:5-Matt10:10) parallels [MRK 6:7–6:13](https://ref.ly/Mark6:7-Mark6:13), [LUK 9:1–9:6](https://ref.ly/Luke9:1-Luke9:6)
