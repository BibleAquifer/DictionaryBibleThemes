## 2423: gospel, essence of (id: 2423)

The chief characteristic and fundamental doctrine of the gospel is that Jesus Christ is both Lord and Saviour.

Jesus Christ as Lord
--------------------

### The universal lordship of Jesus Christ

**[MAT 28:18](https://ref.ly/Matt28:18)** *See also* [DAN 7:13–7:14](https://ref.ly/Dan7:13-Dan7:14); [LUK 10:22](https://ref.ly/Luke10:22); [JHN 3:35](https://ref.ly/John3:35), [JHN 17:2](https://ref.ly/John17:2); [ACT 10:36](https://ref.ly/Acts10:36); [ROM 14:9](https://ref.ly/Rom14:9); [1CO 15:27](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:27); [EPH 1:20–1:22](https://ref.ly/Eph1:20-Eph1:22); [PHP 2:9–2:10](https://ref.ly/Phil2:9-Phil2:10); [COL 1:15–1:20](https://ref.ly/Col1:15-Col1:20)

### Personal implications of the lordship of Jesus Christ

**[LUK 12:8–12:9](https://ref.ly/Luke12:8-Luke12:9)**; **[ROM 10:9](https://ref.ly/Rom10:9)** *See also* [MAT 7:21–7:27](https://ref.ly/Matt7:21-Matt7:27) parallel [LUK 6:46–6:49](https://ref.ly/Luke6:46-Luke6:49); [JHN 13:13–13:14](https://ref.ly/John13:13-John13:14)
