## 4016: life, human (id: 4016)

Seen by Scripture as the climax of the work of creation, life is a gift of God and is to be treated with reverence and respect.

Life is from God
----------------

**[GEN 2:7](https://ref.ly/Gen2:7)**; **[ACT 3:15](https://ref.ly/Acts3:15), [ACT 17:25](https://ref.ly/Acts17:25)** *See also* [1SA 2:6](https://ref.ly/1Sam2:6); [JOB 33:4](https://ref.ly/Job33:4); [PSA 139:13](https://ref.ly/Ps139:13); [DAN 5:23](https://ref.ly/Dan5:23); [ACT 17:28](https://ref.ly/Acts17:28); [JAS 4:14–4:15](https://ref.ly/Jas4:14-Jas4:15)
