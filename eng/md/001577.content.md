## 6242: adultery (id: 6242)

Sexual relations involving at least one partner who is married to someone else. Such relations break the marriage covenant and are condemned in Scripture. Jesus Christ extended the understanding of adultery to include inner lust.

Adultery is condemned by God
----------------------------

### God's law forbids it

**[EXO 20:14](https://ref.ly/Exod20:14)** parallel [DEU 5:18](https://ref.ly/Deut5:18) *See also* [LEV 18:20](https://ref.ly/Lev18:20); [MAT 19:18](https://ref.ly/Matt19:18) parallels [MRK 10:19](https://ref.ly/Mark10:19), [LUK 18:20](https://ref.ly/Luke18:20); [JAS 2:11](https://ref.ly/Jas2:11)

### It is listed with other evil practices

[PSA 50:18](https://ref.ly/Ps50:18); ; [JER 7:9–7:10](https://ref.ly/Jer7:9-Jer7:10); ; [HOS 4:1–4:2](https://ref.ly/Hos4:1-Hos4:2); ; [LUK 18:11](https://ref.ly/Luke18:11); ; [1TI 1:9–1:10](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:9-1Tim1:10)
