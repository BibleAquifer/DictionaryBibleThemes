## 5278: cripples (id: 5278)

People whose walk, physical or spiritual, is hindered by deformity, permanent or temporary.

Physically crippled from birth
------------------------------

**[ACT 3:2](https://ref.ly/Acts3:2)** *See also* [ACT 14:8](https://ref.ly/Acts14:8)

Physically crippled in life
---------------------------

### By accident

**[2SA 4:4](https://ref.ly/2Sam4:4)** *See also* [2SA 9:3](https://ref.ly/2Sam9:3), [2SA 9:13](https://ref.ly/2Sam9:13)

### By God

**[GEN 32:24–32:25](https://ref.ly/Gen32:24-Gen32:25)** *God had a purpose in the crippling of Jacob: it was a mark of his meeting with God.* *See also* [GEN 32:31–32:32](https://ref.ly/Gen32:31-Gen32:32); [HEB 11:21](https://ref.ly/Heb11:21)

### By Satan

**[LUK 13:11](https://ref.ly/Luke13:11)** *See also* [LUK 13:16](https://ref.ly/Luke13:16)

Cripples healed
---------------

### By Jesus Christ

**[LUK 7:22](https://ref.ly/Luke7:22)** parallel [MAT 11:4–11:5](https://ref.ly/Matt11:4-Matt11:5) *See also* [MAT 15:30–15:31](https://ref.ly/Matt15:30-Matt15:31); [JHN 5:1–5:9](https://ref.ly/John5:1-John5:9)

### By others

**[ACT 3:7](https://ref.ly/Acts3:7)** *See also* [ACT 4:9–4:10](https://ref.ly/Acts4:9-Acts4:10), [ACT 8:7](https://ref.ly/Acts8:7), [ACT 14:9–14:10](https://ref.ly/Acts14:9-Acts14:10)

Cripples and OT sacrifice
-------------------------

**[LEV 21:18–21:19](https://ref.ly/Lev21:18-Lev21:19)** *A man must be without defect. Physical disability is a defilement to holiness.* *See also* [LEV 21:6](https://ref.ly/Lev21:6)

### Crippled animals

**[MAL 1:8](https://ref.ly/Mal1:8)** *Animals, like people, must be holy, without defect, when brought to the LORD.* *See also* [EXO 12:5](https://ref.ly/Exod12:5); [LEV 3:1](https://ref.ly/Lev3:1), [LEV 4:3](https://ref.ly/Lev4:3), [LEV 22:18–22:25](https://ref.ly/Lev22:18-Lev22:25); [NUM 6:14](https://ref.ly/Num6:14), [NUM 28:3](https://ref.ly/Num28:3); [DEU 15:21](https://ref.ly/Deut15:21), [DEU 17:1](https://ref.ly/Deut17:1); [MAL 1:13–1:14](https://ref.ly/Mal1:13-Mal1:14)
