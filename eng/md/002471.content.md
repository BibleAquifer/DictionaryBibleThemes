## 9023: death, of unbelievers (id: 9023)

The departure from the present life of unrepentant people to await final banishment from God.

Death is inevitable
-------------------

**[PSA 49:10](https://ref.ly/Ps49:10), [PSA 89:48](https://ref.ly/Ps89:48)** *See also* [2SA 14:14](https://ref.ly/2Sam14:14); [JOB 30:23](https://ref.ly/Job30:23); [PSA 37:36](https://ref.ly/Ps37:36); [ECC 3:19–3:20](https://ref.ly/Eccl3:19-Eccl3:20), [ECC 8:8](https://ref.ly/Eccl8:8)

The premature death of unbelievers is the consequence of sin
------------------------------------------------------------

**[PRO 10:27](https://ref.ly/Prov10:27)** *OT writers, for whom God's blessing on the righteous occurs mostly in the present life, often saw early or sudden death as divine judgment on the wicked.* *See also* [1CH 10:13](https://ref.ly/1Chr10:13); [JOB 22:16](https://ref.ly/Job22:16); [PSA 9:17](https://ref.ly/Ps9:17), [PSA 37:1–37:2](https://ref.ly/Ps37:1-Ps37:2), [PSA 37:22](https://ref.ly/Ps37:22), [PSA 37:38](https://ref.ly/Ps37:38), [PSA 55:23](https://ref.ly/Ps55:23), [PSA 139:19](https://ref.ly/Ps139:19); [PRO 5:21–5:23](https://ref.ly/Prov5:21-Prov5:23), [PRO 11:19](https://ref.ly/Prov11:19), [PRO 14:12](https://ref.ly/Prov14:12); [ISA 17:14](https://ref.ly/Isa17:14); [EZK 3:19](https://ref.ly/Ezek3:19), [EZK 18:4](https://ref.ly/Ezek18:4), [EZK 18:20](https://ref.ly/Ezek18:20); [DAN 5:30](https://ref.ly/Dan5:30); [LUK 12:20](https://ref.ly/Luke12:20)
