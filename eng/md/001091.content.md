## 5522: servants, working conditions of (id: 5522)

Those who serve are to be treated fairly and with respect. Jesus Christ himself chose to be a servant and lays responsibility of service on those who follow.

Servants are to be treated fairly
---------------------------------

### They are not to be exploited

**[EPH 6:9](https://ref.ly/Eph6:9)** *See also* [LEV 25:39–25:40](https://ref.ly/Lev25:39-Lev25:40), [LEV 25:53](https://ref.ly/Lev25:53)

### They are to be paid fairly and promptly

**[DEU 24:14–24:15](https://ref.ly/Deut24:14-Deut24:15)** *See also* [GEN 29:15](https://ref.ly/Gen29:15); [LEV 19:13](https://ref.ly/Lev19:13); [NUM 18:29–18:31](https://ref.ly/Num18:29-Num18:31); [1KI 5:6](https://ref.ly/1Kgs5:6); [JOB 7:1–7:2](https://ref.ly/Job7:1-Job7:2); [JER 22:13](https://ref.ly/Jer22:13); [MAT 20:4](https://ref.ly/Matt20:4), [MAT 20:8](https://ref.ly/Matt20:8); [ROM 4:4](https://ref.ly/Rom4:4); [COL 4:1](https://ref.ly/Col4:1); [2TH 3:10](https://ref.ly/2Thess3:10)
