## 8258: fruitfulness, spiritual (id: 8258)

Fruitfulness is used by Scripture as a symbol of spiritual maturity and well\-being.

Spiritual fruitfulness described
--------------------------------

**[GAL 5:22–5:23](https://ref.ly/Gal5:22-Gal5:23)** *The results of the Holy Spirit's activity in the life of a Christian believer.* *See also* [PSA 92:12](https://ref.ly/Ps92:12); [PRO 11:30](https://ref.ly/Prov11:30); [ISA 32:17](https://ref.ly/Isa32:17); [JER 11:16](https://ref.ly/Jer11:16); [EZK 19:10](https://ref.ly/Ezek19:10); [EPH 5:9](https://ref.ly/Eph5:9); [COL 1:10](https://ref.ly/Col1:10); [JAS 3:17](https://ref.ly/Jas3:17)

Spiritual fruitfulness expected in God's people
-----------------------------------------------

**[JHN 15:16](https://ref.ly/John15:16)** *See also* [ISA 5:1–5:2](https://ref.ly/Isa5:1-Isa5:2); [MAT 7:16–7:20](https://ref.ly/Matt7:16-Matt7:20) parallel [LUK 6:43–6:44](https://ref.ly/Luke6:43-Luke6:44) *Such fruitfuless, seen in a person's behaviour, is evidence of their true character*; [ROM 7:4](https://ref.ly/Rom7:4)
