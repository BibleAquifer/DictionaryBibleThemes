## 5513: sceptre (id: 5513)

A staff, generally ornate, held as a symbol of sovereign authority.

God's own authority is symbolised as a sceptre
----------------------------------------------

**[PSA 45:6](https://ref.ly/Ps45:6)** *See also* [GEN 49:10](https://ref.ly/Gen49:10); [NUM 24:17](https://ref.ly/Num24:17); [PSA 60:7](https://ref.ly/Ps60:7) parallel [PSA 108:8](https://ref.ly/Ps108:8); [ISA 30:31](https://ref.ly/Isa30:31)

The sceptre is a symbol of Jesus Christ's reign
-----------------------------------------------

**[HEB 1:8](https://ref.ly/Heb1:8)** *See also* [PSA 45:6](https://ref.ly/Ps45:6); [GEN 49:10](https://ref.ly/Gen49:10); [NUM 24:17](https://ref.ly/Num24:17); [PSA 110:2](https://ref.ly/Ps110:2); [REV 2:27](https://ref.ly/Rev2:27); [PSA 2:9](https://ref.ly/Ps2:9); [REV 19:15](https://ref.ly/Rev19:15), [REV 12:5](https://ref.ly/Rev12:5)
