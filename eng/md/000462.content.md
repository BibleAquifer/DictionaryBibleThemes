## 4494: papyrus (id: 4494)

A large plant growing in marshland, sometimes to a height of six metres.

Papyrus was used for making boats
---------------------------------

[EXO 2:3](https://ref.ly/Exod2:3); ; [JOB 9:26](https://ref.ly/Job9:26); ; [ISA 18:2](https://ref.ly/Isa18:2)

Papyrus indicates an ample water supply
---------------------------------------

[JOB 8:11–8:13](https://ref.ly/Job8:11-Job8:13); ; [ISA 35:7](https://ref.ly/Isa35:7) *Papyrus growing in what was once desert depicts the blessing coming on God's redeemed people.*
