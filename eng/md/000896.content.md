## 5309: eunuchs (id: 5309)

Castrated men. In the Bible eunuchs are usually portrayed as having positions of responsibility in royal courts. Though eunuchs were excluded from certain privileges under the old covenant, they were at no disadvantage under the new.

-------------------------

**[EST 1:10–1:11](https://ref.ly/Esth1:10-Esth1:11)** *See also* [2KI 20:16–20:18](https://ref.ly/2Kgs20:16-2Kgs20:18) parallel [ISA 39:5–39:7](https://ref.ly/Isa39:5-Isa39:7); [EST 2:1–2:3](https://ref.ly/Esth2:1-Esth2:3), [EST 2:15](https://ref.ly/Esth2:15), [EST 4:4–4:11](https://ref.ly/Esth4:4-Esth4:11); [JER 38:7](https://ref.ly/Jer38:7); [ACT 8:27](https://ref.ly/Acts8:27)

-------------------------------

**[MAT 19:10–19:12](https://ref.ly/Matt19:10-Matt19:12)** *Jesus Christ teaches that castration is not the only legitimate reason for celibacy. Some men may be called to voluntary celibacy because of the demands of their role in the kingdom of heaven.*
