## 8449: imitating (id: 8449)

Following the example or copying the actions of another person. Christians are called upon to become Christlike. Becoming like Jesus Christ is, however, the result, not the condition, of conversion.

The people of God are to imitate God
------------------------------------

**[LEV 11:44–11:45](https://ref.ly/Lev11:44-Lev11:45)** *See also* [LEV 19:2](https://ref.ly/Lev19:2), [LEV 20:7](https://ref.ly/Lev20:7), [LEV 20:26](https://ref.ly/Lev20:26), [LEV 21:8](https://ref.ly/Lev21:8); [1PE 1:16](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:16); [EPH 5:1](https://ref.ly/Eph5:1)

No\-one can obtain salvation by imitating Jesus Christ
------------------------------------------------------

**[JHN 3:16–3:18](https://ref.ly/John3:16-John3:18)** *Jesus Christ teaches that salvation (eternal life) is a consequence of faith in him. He never links salvation with imitating him or any other act or acts.* *See also* [EPH 2:8–2:9](https://ref.ly/Eph2:8-Eph2:9); [2TI 1:8–1:9](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:8-2Tim1:9)
