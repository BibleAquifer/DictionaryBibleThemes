## 7366: freewill offering (id: 7366)

An offering that in some ways resembled the thank\-offering or peace offering. It was completely voluntary, prompted by an occasion of celebration or spiritual significance. The animal offered was eaten at a festive banquet.

Instructions for presenting a freewill offering
-----------------------------------------------

### The animal to be offered

[LEV 22:17–22:22](https://ref.ly/Lev22:17-Lev22:22), [LEV 22:23](https://ref.ly/Lev22:23) *Some slightly deformed animals were perhaps acceptable because the freewill offering was voluntary.*

### Eating the offering

[LEV 7:16](https://ref.ly/Lev7:16)

Freewill offerings were associated with joy and celebration
-----------------------------------------------------------

**[PSA 54:6–54:7](https://ref.ly/Ps54:6-Ps54:7)** *See also* [DEU 16:9–16:12](https://ref.ly/Deut16:9-Deut16:12) *during the Feast of Weeks*

Occasions for making freewill offerings
---------------------------------------

### In maintaining public worship and at times of religious renewal

**[EXO 35:20–35:29](https://ref.ly/Exod35:20-Exod35:29)** *Building the temple.* *See also* [2CH 31:14](https://ref.ly/2Chr31:14) *Hezekiah's reformation*[EZR 1:4–1:6](https://ref.ly/Ezra1:4-Ezra1:6), [EZR 2:68](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:68), [EZR 7:16](https://ref.ly/Ezra7:16), [EZR 8:28](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:28); [NEH 7:70–7:72](https://ref.ly/Neh7:70-Neh7:72)[EZK 46:11–46:12](https://ref.ly/Ezek46:11-Ezek46:12) *Ezekiel's visionary ideal temple*

### As an additonal offering at certain festivals

[NUM 29:39](https://ref.ly/Num29:39); [DEU 12:5–12:6](https://ref.ly/Deut12:5-Deut12:6), [DEU 12:17–12:18](https://ref.ly/Deut12:17-Deut12:18)

Prophetic criticism of bragging over freewill offerings
-------------------------------------------------------

**[AMO 4:5](https://ref.ly/Amos4:5)** *A sarcastic attack on the offering of freewill gifts as a way to gain prestige.*
