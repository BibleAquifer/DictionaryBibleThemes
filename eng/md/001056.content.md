## 5484: punishment, by God (id: 5484)

God's chastening of his children and his judgment on impenitent sinners and supernatural evil powers.

God's punishment of his children
--------------------------------

### Through the discipline of suffering and hardship

**[HEB 12:5–12:7](https://ref.ly/Heb12:5-Heb12:7)** *See also* [PRO 3:11–3:12](https://ref.ly/Prov3:11-Prov3:12); [DEU 4:36](https://ref.ly/Deut4:36), [DEU 8:5](https://ref.ly/Deut8:5); [2SA 7:14](https://ref.ly/2Sam7:14); [JOB 5:17](https://ref.ly/Job5:17)JOB [33:19](https://ref.ly/Job33:19), [JOB 36:10](https://ref.ly/Job36:10)[PSA 32:3–32:4](https://ref.ly/Ps32:3-Ps32:4), [PSA 94:12–94:13](https://ref.ly/Ps94:12-Ps94:13), [PSA 118:18](https://ref.ly/Ps118:18), [PSA 119:75](https://ref.ly/Ps119:75); [ZEP 3:7](https://ref.ly/Zeph3:7); [JAS 1:12](https://ref.ly/Jas1:12); [REV 3:19](https://ref.ly/Rev3:19)

### Through death

**[1CO 11:30–11:32](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:30-1Cor11:32)** *See also* [LEV 10:1–10:3](https://ref.ly/Lev10:1-Lev10:3); [NUM 11:1–11:3](https://ref.ly/Num11:1-Num11:3); [1SA 4:17–4:21](https://ref.ly/1Sam4:17-1Sam4:21); [2SA 6:6–6:8](https://ref.ly/2Sam6:6-2Sam6:8) parallel [1CH 13:9–13:11](https://ref.ly/1Chr13:9-1Chr13:11)

God's punishment of the ungodly
-------------------------------

### Their punishment is certain

**[ROM 1:18](https://ref.ly/Rom1:18)** *See also* [PRO 10:24](https://ref.ly/Prov10:24); [ISA 13:11](https://ref.ly/Isa13:11), [ISA 26:21](https://ref.ly/Isa26:21), [ISA 66:16](https://ref.ly/Isa66:16); [JHN 5:28–5:29](https://ref.ly/John5:28-John5:29); [EPH 5:6](https://ref.ly/Eph5:6); [COL 3:6](https://ref.ly/Col3:6); [1TH 5:3](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:3); [HEB 2:2–2:3](https://ref.ly/Heb2:2-Heb2:3)

### Their punishment is just

**[PSA 96:13](https://ref.ly/Ps96:13)** *See also* [GEN 18:25](https://ref.ly/Gen18:25); [EXO 9:27](https://ref.ly/Exod9:27); [PSA 98:9](https://ref.ly/Ps98:9); [JHN 5:28–5:30](https://ref.ly/John5:28-John5:30); [ROM 3:5–3:8](https://ref.ly/Rom3:5-Rom3:8); [1PE 2:23](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:23); [REV 16:5–16:7](https://ref.ly/Rev16:5-Rev16:7)

### Their punishment involves destruction in hell

**[LUK 12:5](https://ref.ly/Luke12:5)** parallel [MAT 10:28](https://ref.ly/Matt10:28) *See also* [MAT 5:29–5:30](https://ref.ly/Matt5:29-Matt5:30)

### Fire as an image of destruction

**[MAT 5:22](https://ref.ly/Matt5:22)** *See also* [ISA 33:14](https://ref.ly/Isa33:14), [ISA 66:15](https://ref.ly/Isa66:15); [NAM 1:6](https://ref.ly/Nah1:6); [MAL 4:1](https://ref.ly/Mal4:1); [MAT 13:40–13:43](https://ref.ly/Matt13:40-Matt13:43); [MRK 9:43](https://ref.ly/Mark9:43), [MRK 9:48](https://ref.ly/Mark9:48); [LUK 16:22–16:24](https://ref.ly/Luke16:22-Luke16:24); [JUD 1:7](https://ref.ly/Jude1:7), [JUD 1:23](https://ref.ly/Jude1:23); [REV 20:14–20:15](https://ref.ly/Rev20:14-Rev20:15)

### Their punishment will involve exclusion from God's presence

**[MAT 7:23](https://ref.ly/Matt7:23)** *See also* [MAT 22:13](https://ref.ly/Matt22:13), [MAT 25:41](https://ref.ly/Matt25:41); [LUK 13:24–13:28](https://ref.ly/Luke13:24-Luke13:28); [REV 21:27](https://ref.ly/Rev21:27)

### Their punishment will be eternal

**[2TH 1:8–1:9](https://ref.ly/2Thess1:8-2Thess1:9)** *Interpreters differ over whether “everlasting destruction” implies an experience which continues for ever, or annihilation that is everlasting in the sense of being irreversible.* *See also* [DAN 12:2](https://ref.ly/Dan12:2); [MAT 25:46](https://ref.ly/Matt25:46); [REV 14:11](https://ref.ly/Rev14:11)

### The punishment of the second death

**[REV 2:11](https://ref.ly/Rev2:11)** *The “second death” refers to the final state of the unrepentant.* *See also* [REV 20:6](https://ref.ly/Rev20:6), [REV 21:8](https://ref.ly/Rev21:8)

### There will be degrees of punishment

**[MRK 12:40](https://ref.ly/Mark12:40)** parallel [LUK 20:47](https://ref.ly/Luke20:47) *See also* [MAT 11:22](https://ref.ly/Matt11:22), [MAT 11:24](https://ref.ly/Matt11:24); [LUK 12:47–12:48](https://ref.ly/Luke12:47-Luke12:48)
