## 9413: heaven, inheritance of (id: 9413)

Heaven is the secure inheritance, of priceless value, awaiting the redeemed. There, faithful service will be rewarded and the redeemed will be given resurrection bodies for service in the heavenly realm.

Believers inherit the kingdom of heaven
---------------------------------------

### Their inheritance is secure

**[MAT 25:34](https://ref.ly/Matt25:34)** *See also* [LUK 12:32](https://ref.ly/Luke12:32), [LUK 22:28–22:29](https://ref.ly/Luke22:28-Luke22:29); [JAS 2:5](https://ref.ly/Jas2:5)

### The value of their inheritance is beyond human calculation

**[1CO 2:9–2:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:9-1Cor2:10)** *See also* [ACT 20:32](https://ref.ly/Acts20:32); [EPH 1:18](https://ref.ly/Eph1:18), [EPH 2:6–2:7](https://ref.ly/Eph2:6-Eph2:7)

### As those adopted into God's family they are heirs of God and Christ

**[ROM 8:17](https://ref.ly/Rom8:17)** *See also* [GAL 4:7](https://ref.ly/Gal4:7); [TIT 3:7](https://ref.ly/Titus3:7); [HEB 6:17](https://ref.ly/Heb6:17)

The nature of the heavenly inheritance
--------------------------------------

### It is inviolable

**[1PE 1:3–1:5](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:3-1Pet1:5)** *See also* [REV 21:25](https://ref.ly/Rev21:25), [REV 21:27](https://ref.ly/Rev21:27)

### It is a response to faith and love

**[JAS 2:5](https://ref.ly/Jas2:5)** *See also* [COL 1:12](https://ref.ly/Col1:12), [COL 3:24](https://ref.ly/Col3:24); [HEB 11:7](https://ref.ly/Heb11:7)

### It is for overcomers

**[REV 21:7](https://ref.ly/Rev21:7)** *See also* [1JN 4:4](https://ref.ly/1John4:4), [1JN 5:3–5:5](https://ref.ly/1John5:3-1John5:5); [REV 2:7](https://ref.ly/Rev2:7), [REV 2:11](https://ref.ly/Rev2:11), [REV 2:17](https://ref.ly/Rev2:17), [REV 2:26–2:28](https://ref.ly/Rev2:26-Rev2:28), [REV 3:5](https://ref.ly/Rev3:5), [REV 3:12](https://ref.ly/Rev3:12), [REV 3:21](https://ref.ly/Rev3:21)

### The heavenly treasure is to be sought

**[MAT 6:19–6:21](https://ref.ly/Matt6:19-Matt6:21)** *See also* [MAT 13:44](https://ref.ly/Matt13:44), [MAT 19:21](https://ref.ly/Matt19:21); [LUK 12:33](https://ref.ly/Luke12:33); [2CO 4:18](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:18); [PHP 3:8](https://ref.ly/Phil3:8); [1TI 6:18–6:19](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:18-1Tim6:19); [REV 3:18](https://ref.ly/Rev3:18)

Heavenly rewards
----------------

### Service will be rewarded in heaven

**[1CO 3:11–3:14](https://ref.ly/1Cor3:11-1Cor3:14)** *See also* [MAT 5:12](https://ref.ly/Matt5:12) parallel [LUK 6:23](https://ref.ly/Luke6:23); [REV 22:12](https://ref.ly/Rev22:12)

### The prospect of heavenly rewards should be a spur to present service

**[PHP 3:14](https://ref.ly/Phil3:14)** *See also* [HEB 11:26](https://ref.ly/Heb11:26)

### Rewards will vary

**[MAT 16:27](https://ref.ly/Matt16:27)** *See also* [DAN 12:3](https://ref.ly/Dan12:3); [MAT 25:20–25:23](https://ref.ly/Matt25:20-Matt25:23) parallel [LUK 19:15–19:19](https://ref.ly/Luke19:15-Luke19:19); [1CO 3:8](https://ref.ly/1Cor3:8); [2CO 9:6](https://ref.ly/2Cor9:6); [REV 22:12](https://ref.ly/Rev22:12)

### Endurance for Jesus Christ's sake will be specially rewarded

**[MAT 5:10–5:12](https://ref.ly/Matt5:10-Matt5:12)** parallel [LUK 6:22–6:23](https://ref.ly/Luke6:22-Luke6:23)

### Rewards for service include crowns signifying position and authority

**[1PE 5:4](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:4)** *See also* [1TH 2:19](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:19); [2TI 4:8](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:8)

### The redeemed will share Jesus Christ's position in glory

**[EPH 2:6](https://ref.ly/Eph2:6)** *See also* [JHN 14:3](https://ref.ly/John14:3), [JHN 17:24](https://ref.ly/John17:24); [1JN 3:2](https://ref.ly/1John3:2); [REV 22:4](https://ref.ly/Rev22:4)
