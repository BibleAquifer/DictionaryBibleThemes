## 3140: Holy Spirit, as teacher (id: 3140)

Having instructed God's people in the OT and Jesus Christ's disciples in the NT, the Spirit of truth continues to reveal the truth of God to believers.

The Holy Spirit as teacher in the OT
------------------------------------

### The Spirit teaches on practical matters

**[1CH 28:12–28:13](https://ref.ly/1Chr28:12-1Chr28:13)** *See also* [EXO 31:2–31:6](https://ref.ly/Exod31:2-Exod31:6) parallel [EXO 35:30–35:35](https://ref.ly/Exod35:30-Exod35:35); [1CH 28:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr28:19)

### The Spirit teaches the ways of God

**[PSA 143:10](https://ref.ly/Ps143:10)** *See also* [NEH 9:20](https://ref.ly/Neh9:20), [NEH 9:30](https://ref.ly/Neh9:30); [ISA 48:16–48:17](https://ref.ly/Isa48:16-Isa48:17)

**[1PE 1:10–1:11](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:10-1Pet1:11)** *See also* [HEB 9:8](https://ref.ly/Heb9:8), [HEB 10:15–10:17](https://ref.ly/Heb10:15-Heb10:17)
