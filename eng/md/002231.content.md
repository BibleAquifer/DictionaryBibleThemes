## 8444: honouring God (id: 8444)

Giving the reverence and respect to God which is his due. All God's people have a duty to honour him in every aspect of their lives, while unbelievers and even animals can also honour him.

God's people must honour him
----------------------------

**[PSA 22:23](https://ref.ly/Ps22:23)** *See also* [MAL 1:6](https://ref.ly/Mal1:6), [MAL 2:1–2:2](https://ref.ly/Mal2:1-Mal2:2); [JHN 5:22–5:23](https://ref.ly/John5:22-John5:23)
