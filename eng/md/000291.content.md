## 3293: Holy Spirit, witness of (id: 3293)

The Holy Spirit bears witness to the person and work of Jesus Christ, and enables believers to bear effective witness to him.

The Holy Spirit bears witness to Jesus Christ
---------------------------------------------

### The Spirit's testimony

**[1JN 4:2–4:3](https://ref.ly/1John4:2-1John4:3)** *See also* [ACT 5:32](https://ref.ly/Acts5:32); [1CO 12:3](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:3)

### At Jesus Christ's baptism

**[JHN 1:32–1:34](https://ref.ly/John1:32-John1:34)** *See also* [MAT 3:16](https://ref.ly/Matt3:16) parallels [MRK 1:10](https://ref.ly/Mark1:10), [LUK 3:22](https://ref.ly/Luke3:22)

### After Jesus Christ's ascension

**[JHN 15:26](https://ref.ly/John15:26)** *See also* [JHN 14:26](https://ref.ly/John14:26), [JHN 16:13–16:14](https://ref.ly/John16:13-John16:14); [1JN 5:6](https://ref.ly/1John5:6)

The Holy Spirit's witness enables believers to be effective
-----------------------------------------------------------

**[HEB 10:14–10:17](https://ref.ly/Heb10:14-Heb10:17)**
