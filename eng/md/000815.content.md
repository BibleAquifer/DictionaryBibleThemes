## 5227: basket (id: 5227)

Flat, open baskets to carry bread
---------------------------------

### In the chief baker's dream

*See also* [GEN 40:16–40:18](https://ref.ly/Gen40:16-Gen40:18)

### For offering unleavened bread

**[EXO 29:23](https://ref.ly/Exod29:23)** parallel [LEV 8:26](https://ref.ly/Lev8:26) *See also* [EXO 29:2–29:3](https://ref.ly/Exod29:2-Exod29:3) parallel [LEV 8:1–8:2](https://ref.ly/Lev8:1-Lev8:2); [NUM 6:15–6:17](https://ref.ly/Num6:15-Num6:17)

Baskets used to hold food
-------------------------

### To store household provisions

**[DEU 28:5](https://ref.ly/Deut28:5)** *Signifying the continued provision of food for the household.* *See also* [DEU 28:17](https://ref.ly/Deut28:17)

### To collect food

**[MAT 14:20](https://ref.ly/Matt14:20)** parallels [MRK 6:43](https://ref.ly/Mark6:43), [LUK 9:17](https://ref.ly/Luke9:17), [JHN 6:13](https://ref.ly/John6:13) *See also* [MAT 13:48](https://ref.ly/Matt13:48), [MAT 15:37](https://ref.ly/Matt15:37) parallel [MRK 8:8](https://ref.ly/Mark8:8); [MAT 16:9–16:10](https://ref.ly/Matt16:9-Matt16:10) parallel [MRK 8:19–8:20](https://ref.ly/Mark8:19-Mark8:20)
