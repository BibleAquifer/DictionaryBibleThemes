## 7470: temple, significance of (id: 7470)

The temple was both idealised and spiritualised by OT and NT writers.

The OT concept of an ideal temple in the future age of salvation
----------------------------------------------------------------

**[ISA 2:3](https://ref.ly/Isa2:3)** parallel [MIC 4:2](https://ref.ly/Mic4:2) *See also* [ISA 56:4–56:7](https://ref.ly/Isa56:4-Isa56:7), [ISA 66:20](https://ref.ly/Isa66:20); [JER 33:11](https://ref.ly/Jer33:11); [EZK 37:28](https://ref.ly/Ezek37:28), [EZK 40:2–40:4](https://ref.ly/Ezek40:2-Ezek40:4) *Ezekiel received a vision of a rebuilt temple to which the glory of the LORD had returned.
