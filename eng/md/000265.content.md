## 3215: Holy Spirit, and peace (id: 3215)

The Holy Spirit brings a sense of well\-being, contentment and wholeness to believers whatever their outward circumstances. Peace is therefore an indication of the Holy Spirit's presence.

Peace is part of the nature of the Holy Spirit
----------------------------------------------

### The Spirit is likened to a dove, the symbol of peace

**[MAT 3:16](https://ref.ly/Matt3:16)** parallels [MRK 1:10](https://ref.ly/Mark1:10), [LUK 3:22](https://ref.ly/Luke3:22), [JHN 1:32](https://ref.ly/John1:32)

### As part of the Godhead the Spirit is the God of peace

*See also* [PHP 4:9](https://ref.ly/Phil4:9); [1TH 5:23](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:23); [HEB 13:20](https://ref.ly/Heb13:20)

Peace is a resource that the Holy Spirit gives to believers
-----------------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 14:17](https://ref.ly/Rom14:17)** *See also* [JHN 14:15–14:19](https://ref.ly/John14:15-John14:19) *The Greek word “parakletos” means “one called alongside”, hence “comforter” or “advocate”*; [ROM 15:13](https://ref.ly/Rom15:13)

The fruit of the Spirit includes peace
--------------------------------------

**[GAL 5:22–5:25](https://ref.ly/Gal5:22-Gal5:25)** *See also* [ROM 8:6](https://ref.ly/Rom8:6)

The Holy Spirit brings peace to God's people concerning their circumstances
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

### He changes situations to bring peace

**[ISA 32:14–32:18](https://ref.ly/Isa32:14-Isa32:18)** *See also* [ISA 63:14](https://ref.ly/Isa63:14); [ACT 9:31](https://ref.ly/Acts9:31)

### He brings peace of mind to those facing difficult situations

**[PHP 1:19](https://ref.ly/Phil1:19)** *See also* [HAG 2:5](https://ref.ly/Hag2:5); [JHN 16:5–16:21](https://ref.ly/John16:5-John16:21), [JHN 20:21–20:22](https://ref.ly/John20:21-John20:22)
