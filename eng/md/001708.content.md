## 7027: church, purpose and mission of (id: 7027)

The church is called to praise and glorify God, to establish Jesus Christ's kingdom, and to proclaim the gospel throughout the world.

God's purposes for the church
-----------------------------

### To praise God

**[1PE 2:9](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:9)** *See also* [EPH 1:5–1:6](https://ref.ly/Eph1:5-Eph1:6), [EPH 1:11–1:12](https://ref.ly/Eph1:11-Eph1:12), [EPH 1:14](https://ref.ly/Eph1:14); [HEB 13:15](https://ref.ly/Heb13:15); [1PE 2:5](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:5)

**[ROM 8:29–8:30](https://ref.ly/Rom8:29-Rom8:30)** *See also* [MAT 13:43](https://ref.ly/Matt13:43); [JHN 17:24](https://ref.ly/John17:24); [ROM 9:23](https://ref.ly/Rom9:23); [1CO 2:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:7); [PHP 3:21](https://ref.ly/Phil3:21); [COL 3:4](https://ref.ly/Col3:4); [2TH 2:14](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:14); [REV 2:26–2:27](https://ref.ly/Rev2:26-Rev2:27), [REV 3:4–3:5](https://ref.ly/Rev3:4-Rev3:5), [REV 3:21](https://ref.ly/Rev3:21)

**[MAT 16:18–16:19](https://ref.ly/Matt16:18-Matt16:19)** *See also* [MAT 27:40](https://ref.ly/Matt27:40) parallel [MRK 15:29](https://ref.ly/Mark15:29); [JHN 2:19–2:22](https://ref.ly/John2:19-John2:22); [1CO 3:9](https://ref.ly/1Cor3:9); [EPH 2:21–2:22](https://ref.ly/Eph2:21-Eph2:22), [EPH 4:11–4:13](https://ref.ly/Eph4:11-Eph4:13); [HEB 3:3–3:6](https://ref.ly/Heb3:3-Heb3:6); [1PE 2:5](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:5)

### To challenge Satan's dominion

**[EPH 3:10–3:11](https://ref.ly/Eph3:10-Eph3:11)** *“rulers and authorities in the heavenly realms” refers to the powers of evil.* *See also* [MAT 16:18](https://ref.ly/Matt16:18); [EPH 6:12](https://ref.ly/Eph6:12); [1JN 2:14](https://ref.ly/1John2:14)
