## 5809: compassion, human (id: 5809)

An attitude of care and concern, grounded in pity and sympathy towards others. Christian compassion towards others should reflect the compassion of God for his people.

Compassion is a vital requirement in Christian character
--------------------------------------------------------

**[EPH 4:32](https://ref.ly/Eph4:32)**; **[COL 3:12](https://ref.ly/Col3:12)** *See also* [2CO 1:3–1:5](https://ref.ly/2Cor1:3-2Cor1:5); [1PE 3:8](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:8)
