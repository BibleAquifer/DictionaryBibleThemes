## 5936: riots (id: 5936)

The hostile reactions of crowds who take the law into their own hands, frequently in opposition to a messenger of God.

Riotous responses to unpopular actions or teaching
--------------------------------------------------

[GEN 19:4–19:11](https://ref.ly/Gen19:4-Gen19:11) *the hostility of the men of Sodom against Lot*; ; [JDG 6:30–6:31](https://ref.ly/Judg6:30-Judg6:31) *The men of Ophrah call for the death of Gideon*; ; [JER 26:8–26:11](https://ref.ly/Jer26:8-Jer26:11) *The people react to Jeremiah's message*; ; [LUK 4:28–4:30](https://ref.ly/Luke4:28-Luke4:30) *The people of Nazareth expel Jesus Christ*; ; [ACT 19:23–19:34](https://ref.ly/Acts19:23-Acts19:34) *the anger of the Ephesians over Paul*; ; [2CO 6:5](https://ref.ly/2Cor6:5)
