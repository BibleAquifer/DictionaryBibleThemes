## 7203: ark, Noah's (id: 7203)

The craft built at God's command by Noah and his family as a means of escape from the flood. The ark is seen as corresponding to the redemption offered to believers through Jesus Christ.

The significance of the ark elsewhere in Scripture
--------------------------------------------------

### An instance of judgment

**[MAT 24:38–24:39](https://ref.ly/Matt24:38-Matt24:39)** parallel [LUK 17:26–17:27](https://ref.ly/Luke17:26-Luke17:27) *Judgment clearly divides humanity into the saved and the lost.* *See also* [2PE 2:5](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:5)

### An example of faith

**[HEB 11:7](https://ref.ly/Heb11:7)** *Noah took God at his word in circumstances which must have appeared ridiculous.*

### A symbol of salvation

**[1PE 3:20–3:21](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:20-1Pet3:21)** *The link between the ark and baptism is in the twin ideas of water and salvation.*
