## 7396: Most Holy Place (id: 7396)

The inner sanctuary of the tabernacle set up at Sinai and of Solomon's temple, where the ark of the covenant was kept. It is a symbol of the unapproachable presence of God.

Terms used to describe the Most Holy Place
------------------------------------------

[EXO 26:33](https://ref.ly/Exod26:33) *“the Most Holy Place” (or “Holy of Holies”), in contrast to the “Holy Place” where the altar stood. This superlative suggests increasing closeness to the presence of God. The distinction is not always observed, however. In Leviticus chapter 16 “Holy Place” is used regularly (translated in NIV as “Most Holy Place” in verses 2, 16, 17, 20, 27, and as “sanctuary area” in verse 3, though it is the inner sanctuary that is referred to, as the phrase “behind the curtain” (verse 2\) shows)*; ; [1KI 6:16](https://ref.ly/1Kgs6:16) *the “inner sanctuary” in contrast to the outer*; ; [HEB 9:7](https://ref.ly/Heb9:7) *literally “the second (tabernacle)” in contrast to the first or outer*

The structure and design of the Most Holy Place
-----------------------------------------------

### In the wilderness tabernacle

The only details concern the curtains forming the walls of the two sanctuaries, and in particular the dividing curtain which separates the Most Holy Place from the outer sanctuary: [EXO 26:31–26:33](https://ref.ly/Exod26:31-Exod26:33), [EXO 36:35–36:36](https://ref.ly/Exod36:35-Exod36:36), [EXO 40:21](https://ref.ly/Exod40:21)

### In Solomon's temple

[1KI 6:16](https://ref.ly/1Kgs6:16), [1KI 6:19–6:20](https://ref.ly/1Kgs6:19-1Kgs6:20) parallel [2CH 3:8](https://ref.ly/2Chr3:8); ; [1KI 6:23–6:28](https://ref.ly/1Kgs6:23-1Kgs6:28) parallel [2CH 3:10](https://ref.ly/2Chr3:10); ; [1KI 6:31–6:32](https://ref.ly/1Kgs6:31-1Kgs6:32); ; [2CH 3:14](https://ref.ly/2Chr3:14)

### In the temple in Ezekiel's vision

[EZK 41:4](https://ref.ly/Ezek41:4), [EZK 41:15–41:21](https://ref.ly/Ezek41:15-Ezek41:21), [EZK 41:23](https://ref.ly/Ezek41:23), [EZK 45:3](https://ref.ly/Ezek45:3) *In Ezekiel's vision a portion of the land was sacred to the LORD and called the Most Holy Place.*

The significance and function of the Most Holy Place
----------------------------------------------------

### To house the ark of the covenant

**[EXO 26:33](https://ref.ly/Exod26:33)** *See also* [EXO 40:3](https://ref.ly/Exod40:3); [1KI 6:19](https://ref.ly/1Kgs6:19), [1KI 8:6–8:9](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:6-1Kgs8:9) parallel [2CH 5:7–5:9](https://ref.ly/2Chr5:7-2Chr5:9)

### To house the incense altar (in Solomon's temple)

**[1KI 6:22](https://ref.ly/1Kgs6:22)**; **[HEB 9:3–9:4](https://ref.ly/Heb9:3-Heb9:4)** *[Ex 30:6](https://ref.ly/Exod30:6) and [40:5](https://ref.ly/Exod40:5) suggest that the incense altar was housed in front of the curtain, i.e., in the Holy Place, the outer sanctuary.*

### To be a symbol of the presence of God

**[PSA 28:2](https://ref.ly/Ps28:2)** *See also* [EXO 26:33](https://ref.ly/Exod26:33) *The curtain veiling the Most Holy Place represents the unapproachable presence of the Holy God*; [LEV 16:2](https://ref.ly/Lev16:2)

The Most Holy Place on the Day of Atonement
-------------------------------------------

**[HEB 9:7](https://ref.ly/Heb9:7)** *See also* [LEV 16:16–16:17](https://ref.ly/Lev16:16-Lev16:17)

The NT understanding of the Most Holy Place as the place of God's heavenly presence
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ enters the Most Holy Place as Mediator

**[HEB 9:8](https://ref.ly/Heb9:8)** *See also* [HEB 6:19–6:20](https://ref.ly/Heb6:19-Heb6:20), [HEB 9:11–9:12](https://ref.ly/Heb9:11-Heb9:12), [HEB 9:24](https://ref.ly/Heb9:24)

### Entry to the Most Holy Place is open to all believers

**[HEB 10:19–10:22](https://ref.ly/Heb10:19-Heb10:22)** *See also* [MAT 27:51](https://ref.ly/Matt27:51) parallel [LUK 23:45](https://ref.ly/Luke23:45) *By Jesus Christ's death the dividing curtain has been torn in two.*
