## 1431: prophecy, methods of OT (id: 1431)

Prophets relayed their messages from God in a variety of ways, choosing methods that would make the meaning clear, attract attention or convince the people of the truth of their words.

God speaks through the prophets
-------------------------------

**[HEB 1:1](https://ref.ly/Heb1:1)**

### Prophecy directly proclaimed

**[JON 3:1–3:4](https://ref.ly/Jonah3:1-Jonah3:4)** *See also* [1KI 13:1–13:3](https://ref.ly/1Kgs13:1-1Kgs13:3), [1KI 14:6–14:16](https://ref.ly/1Kgs14:6-1Kgs14:16), [1KI 20:22](https://ref.ly/1Kgs20:22); [ISA 7:13–7:17](https://ref.ly/Isa7:13-Isa7:17); [JER 26:18](https://ref.ly/Jer26:18); [ACT 11:27–11:28](https://ref.ly/Acts11:27-Acts11:28); [1CO 14:29](https://ref.ly/1Cor14:29)
