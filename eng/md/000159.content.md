## 2057: Jesus Christ, obedience of (id: 2057)

The selfless obedience of Jesus Christ to the will of God his Father, through which the redemption of humanity is accomplished. Christ also shows himself willing to submit to earthly authorities and sets an example which believers are called to imitate.

### He was obedient to the Jewish law

[MAT 5:17–5:18](https://ref.ly/Matt5:17-Matt5:18), [MAT 22:37–22:40](https://ref.ly/Matt22:37-Matt22:40) parallels [MRK 12:29–12:31](https://ref.ly/Mark12:29-Mark12:31), [LUK 10:26–10:28](https://ref.ly/Luke10:26-Luke10:28); ; [MRK 2:23–2:28](https://ref.ly/Mark2:23-Mark2:28); ; [JHN 7:19](https://ref.ly/John7:19), [JHN 10:37–10:38](https://ref.ly/John10:37-John10:38) *Jesus Christ's obedience and commitment were to the spirit of God's law; his attitudes to Jewish legalism were often seen as disobedience. Disputes over the Sabbath are one example.*
