## 5882: impartiality (id: 5882)

The ability to be unbiased towards any one race, class or person. This attitude is demonstrated by God and is to be emulated by the human race.

God is impartial
----------------

**[DEU 10:17](https://ref.ly/Deut10:17)** *See also* [2CH 19:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr19:7)

### God treats people impartially

**[MAT 5:45](https://ref.ly/Matt5:45)** *See also* [JOB 25:3](https://ref.ly/Job25:3), [JOB 34:19](https://ref.ly/Job34:19); [PRO 22:2](https://ref.ly/Prov22:2); [ROM 2:11](https://ref.ly/Rom2:11); [EPH 6:9](https://ref.ly/Eph6:9)

### God judges impartially

**[COL 3:25](https://ref.ly/Col3:25)** *See also* [JOB 34:11–34:12](https://ref.ly/Job34:11-Job34:12); [PSA 62:12](https://ref.ly/Ps62:12); [JER 17:10](https://ref.ly/Jer17:10); [ROM 2:9–2:11](https://ref.ly/Rom2:9-Rom2:11); [2CO 5:10](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:10); [1PE 1:17](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:17); [REV 22:12](https://ref.ly/Rev22:12)

The impartiality of Jesus Christ
--------------------------------

### Jesus Christ's teaching was impartial

**[LUK 20:21](https://ref.ly/Luke20:21)** parallels [MAT 22:16](https://ref.ly/Matt22:16), [MRK 12:14](https://ref.ly/Mark12:14) *See also* [MAT 20:1–20:16](https://ref.ly/Matt20:1-Matt20:16) *a parable of Jesus Christ about divine impartiality*

**[JHN 4:9](https://ref.ly/John4:9)** *Jesus Christ contravened accepted boundaries of partiality in this encounter.* *See also* [MAT 9:10–9:12](https://ref.ly/Matt9:10-Matt9:12) parallels [MRK 2:15–2:17](https://ref.ly/Mark2:15-Mark2:17), [LUK 5:29–5:31](https://ref.ly/Luke5:29-Luke5:31); [MAT 15:22–15:28](https://ref.ly/Matt15:22-Matt15:28) parallel [MRK 7:26–7:29](https://ref.ly/Mark7:26-Mark7:29); [MAT 20:20–20:23](https://ref.ly/Matt20:20-Matt20:23) parallel [MRK 10:35–10:40](https://ref.ly/Mark10:35-Mark10:40)
