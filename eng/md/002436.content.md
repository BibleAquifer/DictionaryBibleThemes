## 8816: ridicule, nature of (id: 8816)

Scripture declares that ridicule of the weak, the poor and the godly is unacceptable. Those who ridicule them invite judgment upon themselves.

Warnings against ridicule
-------------------------

### Ridicule is foolish

**[PRO 11:12](https://ref.ly/Prov11:12)** *See also* [PRO 1:20–1:22](https://ref.ly/Prov1:20-Prov1:22), [PRO 14:6](https://ref.ly/Prov14:6), [PRO 14:9](https://ref.ly/Prov14:9)

### Ridicule creates trouble

**[PRO 9:12](https://ref.ly/Prov9:12)** *See also* [PRO 19:29](https://ref.ly/Prov19:29), [PRO 22:10](https://ref.ly/Prov22:10), [PRO 29:8](https://ref.ly/Prov29:8), [PRO 30:17](https://ref.ly/Prov30:17); [MAT 5:22](https://ref.ly/Matt5:22) *“Raca” probably means “empty\-head”, a term of derision.*

### Ridicule is a sign of the last days

**[2PE 3:3–3:4](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:3-2Pet3:4)** *See also* [JUD 1:18](https://ref.ly/Jude1:18)
