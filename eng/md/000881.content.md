## 5293: defence, human (id: 5293)

God's people are to defend the needy and those in their care. They are also to defend the truth entrusted to them.

Defence of the needy
--------------------

### God commands his people to defend the needy

**[PRO 31:8–31:9](https://ref.ly/Prov31:8-Prov31:9)** *See also* [ISA 1:17](https://ref.ly/Isa1:17), [ISA 10:1–10:2](https://ref.ly/Isa10:1-Isa10:2), [ISA 58:6–58:7](https://ref.ly/Isa58:6-Isa58:7), [ISA 58:9–58:10](https://ref.ly/Isa58:9-Isa58:10)

### Examples of those who defended the needy

**[PSA 72:4](https://ref.ly/Ps72:4)** [EXO 2:17](https://ref.ly/Exod2:17); ; [ACT 7:24](https://ref.ly/Acts7:24); [RUT 2:15–2:16](https://ref.ly/Ruth2:15-Ruth2:16) *Boaz*

### Neglect of the cause of the needy is condemned

**[PSA 82:2–82:4](https://ref.ly/Ps82:2-Ps82:4)** *See also* [JOB 5:4](https://ref.ly/Job5:4); [ISA 1:23–1:24](https://ref.ly/Isa1:23-Isa1:24), [ISA 29:20–29:21](https://ref.ly/Isa29:20-Isa29:21); [JER 5:26–5:29](https://ref.ly/Jer5:26-Jer5:29), [JER 22:15–22:17](https://ref.ly/Jer22:15-Jer22:17)

Church leaders are to defend the flock of God
---------------------------------------------

**[ACT 20:28–20:31](https://ref.ly/Acts20:28-Acts20:31)** *See also* [JHN 21:15–21:17](https://ref.ly/John21:15-John21:17); [1TI 4:6](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:6); [TIT 1:10–1:11](https://ref.ly/Titus1:10-Titus1:11); [HEB 13:17](https://ref.ly/Heb13:17); [1PE 5:2](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:2)

Christians are urged to defend the faith
----------------------------------------

### It is the responsibility of all believers

**[JUD 1:3](https://ref.ly/Jude1:3)** *See also* [PHP 1:27–1:28](https://ref.ly/Phil1:27-Phil1:28); [2TH 2:15](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:15)

### It is the responsibility of Christian leaders in particular

**[2TI 1:13–1:14](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:13-2Tim1:14)** *See also* [1TI 1:3–1:10](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:3-1Tim1:10), [1TI 6:20–6:21](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:20-1Tim6:21); [TIT 1:12–1:14](https://ref.ly/Titus1:12-Titus1:14)
