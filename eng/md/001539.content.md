## 6178: hardness of heart (id: 6178)

Primarily, in Scripture, a persistent inner refusal to hear and obey the word of God. Also, in a more general sense, an uncaring or unsympathetic attitude towards other people.

Kinds of hardness of heart
--------------------------

### As disobedience towards God

**[PSA 95:8–95:9](https://ref.ly/Ps95:8-Ps95:9)** *Referring to the disobedience of the Israelites in their wilderness wanderings.* *See also* [HEB 3:7–3:9](https://ref.ly/Heb3:7-Heb3:9)**[EPH 4:18](https://ref.ly/Eph4:18)** *See also* [PRO 28:14](https://ref.ly/Prov28:14); [ZEC 7:12](https://ref.ly/Zech7:12); [ROM 2:5](https://ref.ly/Rom2:5); [HEB 3:12–3:15](https://ref.ly/Heb3:12-Heb3:15), [HEB 4:5–4:7](https://ref.ly/Heb4:5-Heb4:7)

### As the work of God

**[EXO 10:20](https://ref.ly/Exod10:20)** *God's judgment on Pharaoh was to confirm him in his stubbornness so that he might feel the full weight of his wrath*; **[ISA 6:10](https://ref.ly/Isa6:10)** *See also* [JHN 12:40](https://ref.ly/John12:40)

### As an uncaring attitude towards other people

**[DEU 15:7](https://ref.ly/Deut15:7)** *See also* [PSA 17:10](https://ref.ly/Ps17:10), [PSA 73:7](https://ref.ly/Ps73:7), [PSA 119:70](https://ref.ly/Ps119:70); [MAT 19:8](https://ref.ly/Matt19:8) parallel [MRK 10:5](https://ref.ly/Mark10:5); [JAS 2:15–2:16](https://ref.ly/Jas2:15-Jas2:16); [1JN 3:17](https://ref.ly/1John3:17)
