## 5185: stomach (id: 5185)

As well as the physical stomach, the term is used in association with God's judgment or in a figurative sense.

Stomach as part of the physical body
------------------------------------

**[1CO 6:13](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:13)** *See also* [JOB 40:16](https://ref.ly/Job40:16); [SNG 7:2](https://ref.ly/Song7:2); [DAN 2:32](https://ref.ly/Dan2:32)

### Jonah in the stomach of a huge fish

**[MAT 12:40](https://ref.ly/Matt12:40)** *See also* [JON 1:17–2:1](https://ref.ly/Jonah1:17-Jonah2:1)

Ritual laws concerning the stomach
----------------------------------

**[MRK 7:18–7:19](https://ref.ly/Mark7:18-Mark7:19)** parallel [MAT 15:17](https://ref.ly/Matt15:17) *See also* [LEV 11:41–11:45](https://ref.ly/Lev11:41-Lev11:45)

Afflictions of the stomach
--------------------------

[LUK 15:16](https://ref.ly/Luke15:16) *hunger*; ; [1TI 5:23](https://ref.ly/1Tim5:23) *illness*

Fatal wounding in the stomach
-----------------------------

[JDG 3:21](https://ref.ly/Judg3:21); ; [2SA 2:23](https://ref.ly/2Sam2:23), [2SA 3:27](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:27), [2SA 4:6](https://ref.ly/2Sam4:6), [2SA 20:10](https://ref.ly/2Sam20:10)

The stomach and God's judgment
------------------------------

### Severe stomach pain

**[JER 30:6–30:7](https://ref.ly/Jer30:6-Jer30:7)** *See also* [NUM 5:20–5:22](https://ref.ly/Num5:20-Num5:22), [NUM 5:27](https://ref.ly/Num5:27)

### Crawling on one's stomach as a sign of submission

**[GEN 3:14](https://ref.ly/Gen3:14)** *Part of God's curse upon Satan.* *See also* [PSA 72:9](https://ref.ly/Ps72:9); [ISA 49:23](https://ref.ly/Isa49:23), [ISA 65:25](https://ref.ly/Isa65:25); [MIC 7:17](https://ref.ly/Mic7:17)

### Hunger as a result of God's judgment

**[MIC 6:14](https://ref.ly/Mic6:14)** *See also* [JOB 20:12–20:15](https://ref.ly/Job20:12-Job20:15), [JOB 20:20–20:23](https://ref.ly/Job20:20-Job20:23); [PRO 13:25](https://ref.ly/Prov13:25); [ISA 9:20](https://ref.ly/Isa9:20); [EZK 7:19](https://ref.ly/Ezek7:19)

### An empty stomach as a warning

**[AMO 4:6](https://ref.ly/Amos4:6)**

The stomach and God's blessing
------------------------------

In the OT a full stomach was one sign of God's blessing: [PSA 34:9–34:10](https://ref.ly/Ps34:9-Ps34:10), [PSA 37:19](https://ref.ly/Ps37:19), [PSA 37:25](https://ref.ly/Ps37:25); ; [PRO 10:3](https://ref.ly/Prov10:3)
