## 9130: future, the (id: 9130)

God's promises about the future are certain because it is under God's control. Christians should live on the basis that these promises, which focus on the return of Jesus Christ, will be fulfilled.

God controls the future
-----------------------

### God knows the future

**[EXO 3:19–3:20](https://ref.ly/Exod3:19-Exod3:20)** *See also* [DEU 31:21](https://ref.ly/Deut31:21); [2SA 7:19](https://ref.ly/2Sam7:19) parallel [1CH 17:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr17:17); [PSA 139:4](https://ref.ly/Ps139:4); [ISA 42:9](https://ref.ly/Isa42:9), [ISA 46:10](https://ref.ly/Isa46:10); [DAN 2:28](https://ref.ly/Dan2:28); [ACT 2:23](https://ref.ly/Acts2:23), [ACT 3:18](https://ref.ly/Acts3:18)
