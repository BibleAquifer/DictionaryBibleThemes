## 8298: love, for one another (id: 8298)

Scripture instructs God's people to love one another and illustrates what this means in practice.

Reasons for loving one another
------------------------------

### God commands it

**[GAL 5:14](https://ref.ly/Gal5:14)** *See also* [LEV 19:18](https://ref.ly/Lev19:18)LEV [19:34](https://ref.ly/Lev19:34); [DEU 10:19](https://ref.ly/Deut10:19)[MAT 22:39](https://ref.ly/Matt22:39) parallel [MRK 12:31](https://ref.ly/Mark12:31) *the second greatest commandment*; [JHN 15:12](https://ref.ly/John15:12) *Jesus Christ commands his disciples to love one another*; [ROM 13:10](https://ref.ly/Rom13:10); [1TH 4:9](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:9); [HEB 13:1](https://ref.ly/Heb13:1); [JAS 2:8](https://ref.ly/Jas2:8); [1PE 1:22](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:22), [1PE 2:17](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:17); [1JN 3:23](https://ref.ly/1John3:23), [1JN 4:21](https://ref.ly/1John4:21); [2JN 1:5](https://ref.ly/2John1:5)

**[1JN 4:11](https://ref.ly/1John4:11)** *See also* [MAL 2:10](https://ref.ly/Mal2:10); [1CO 8:11–8:13](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:11-1Cor8:13); [1JN 3:16](https://ref.ly/1John3:16)

### Love maintains fellowship

**[1PE 4:8](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:8)** *See also* [PRO 10:12](https://ref.ly/Prov10:12), [PRO 17:9](https://ref.ly/Prov17:9); [EPH 4:2](https://ref.ly/Eph4:2)

**[1TH 2:8](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:8)** *See also* [PRO 17:17](https://ref.ly/Prov17:17); [2CO 12:15](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:15); [GAL 5:13](https://ref.ly/Gal5:13); [PHP 2:30](https://ref.ly/Phil2:30), [PHP 4:10](https://ref.ly/Phil4:10); [1TH 1:3](https://ref.ly/1Thess1:3)
