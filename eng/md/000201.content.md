## 2363: Jesus Christ, preaching and teaching of (id: 2363)

A vital feature of Jesus Christ's ministry, focusing on his authoritative proclamation of the kingdom of God.

Jesus Christ's mission as preaching and teaching
------------------------------------------------

**[LUK 4:43](https://ref.ly/Luke4:43)** parallel [MRK 1:38](https://ref.ly/Mark1:38) *See also* [MAT 11:5](https://ref.ly/Matt11:5) parallel [LUK 7:22](https://ref.ly/Luke7:22); [MRK 6:6](https://ref.ly/Mark6:6); [JHN 7:16](https://ref.ly/John7:16); [ACT 1:1](https://ref.ly/Acts1:1)

Jesus Christ was regarded as a teacher and prophet
