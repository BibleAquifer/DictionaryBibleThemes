### The fiftieth year as the Year of Jubilee

**[LEV 25:10–25:11](https://ref.ly/Lev25:10-Lev25:11)** *The word “jubilee” refers to the liberation of slaves, rather than to a fixed period of time. The number 50 represents the year following seven periods of seven years, i.e., 49 years \+ 1\.*
