## 6209: pagans (id: 6209)

OT use of the term \`\`pagan''
------------------------------

### Used of foreign adherents of heathen religions

**[ISA 2:6](https://ref.ly/Isa2:6)** *See also* [2KI 23:4–23:7](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:4-2Kgs23:7); [ZEP 1:4–1:6](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:4-Zeph1:6)

### Used of heathen religions, peoples and countries

**[ISA 57:8](https://ref.ly/Isa57:8)** *See also* [LAM 1:10](https://ref.ly/Lam1:10); [AMO 7:17](https://ref.ly/Amos7:17)

In the Gospels `pagans'' is synonymous with`Gentiles''
------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 6:7](https://ref.ly/Matt6:7)** *See also* [MAT 5:47](https://ref.ly/Matt5:47), [MAT 6:32](https://ref.ly/Matt6:32) parallel [LUK 12:30](https://ref.ly/Luke12:30); [MAT 18:17](https://ref.ly/Matt18:17)

The term \`\`pagans'' is used to refer to people who were neither Jews nor Christians
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Pagans as idolaters

**[1CO 12:2](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:2)** *See also* [1CO 10:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:7); [EXO 32:6](https://ref.ly/Exod32:6); [1CO 10:20](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:20)

### Pagans as immoral

**[1PE 4:3](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:3)** *See also* [1CO 5:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor5:1)

### Many believers lived among the pagans

**[1PE 2:12](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:12)** *See also* [3JN 1:7](https://ref.ly/3John1:7)
