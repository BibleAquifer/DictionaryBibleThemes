## 1115: God, purpose of (id: 1115)

God has a plan for his creation which he will certainly accomplish. It is carried out through the control of circumstances and his choice and use of people, and above all in the life, death, resurrection and second coming of Jesus Christ.

God's purpose will be accomplished
----------------------------------

**[ISA 46:10–46:11](https://ref.ly/Isa46:10-Isa46:11)** *See also* [PSA 135:6](https://ref.ly/Ps135:6); [PRO 16:4](https://ref.ly/Prov16:4); [ECC 3:14](https://ref.ly/Eccl3:14); [ISA 14:24](https://ref.ly/Isa14:24), [ISA 14:26–14:27](https://ref.ly/Isa14:26-Isa14:27), [ISA 25:1](https://ref.ly/Isa25:1), [ISA 55:11](https://ref.ly/Isa55:11); [EPH 1:11](https://ref.ly/Eph1:11)

God's purpose prevails
----------------------

### It stands for ever

**[PSA 33:11](https://ref.ly/Ps33:11)** *See also* [JOB 42:2](https://ref.ly/Job42:2); [JER 23:20](https://ref.ly/Jer23:20)

### It cannot be thwarted by human beings

**[PRO 19:21](https://ref.ly/Prov19:21)** *See also* [JOB 9:12](https://ref.ly/Job9:12), [JOB 23:13](https://ref.ly/Job23:13); [ISA 8:10](https://ref.ly/Isa8:10); [DAN 4:35](https://ref.ly/Dan4:35)

### It cannot be thwarted by evil powers

**[REV 17:17](https://ref.ly/Rev17:17)** *See also* [1SA 16:14](https://ref.ly/1Sam16:14) *The implication is that evil is subject to God's control and operates within the determined limits of his sovereign purpose*; [JOB 1:12](https://ref.ly/Job1:12), [JOB 2:6](https://ref.ly/Job2:6); [2CO 12:7](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:7)

### It prevails in people's lives and circumstances

**[PRO 16:9](https://ref.ly/Prov16:9)** *See also* [PRO 20:24](https://ref.ly/Prov20:24), [PRO 21:1](https://ref.ly/Prov21:1)

### It is fulfilled through his choice of individuals

**[EPH 1:11](https://ref.ly/Eph1:11)** *God's choice of individuals does not invalidate their free will to do or not to do anything, nor remove their answerability to God for their actions.* *See also* [GEN 18:19](https://ref.ly/Gen18:19); [ROM 11:5](https://ref.ly/Rom11:5); [1CO 1:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:1); [EPH 1:4–1:5](https://ref.ly/Eph1:4-Eph1:5); [2TH 2:13](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:13)

### Examples of the actions of evil people serving God's purpose

**[ACT 2:23](https://ref.ly/Acts2:23)** *See also* [GEN 50:20](https://ref.ly/Gen50:20); [ISA 10:5–10:7](https://ref.ly/Isa10:5-Isa10:7)

### It is God's purpose to redeem the world through the cross
