## 7758: preachers, call of (id: 7758)

God declares his word through called and anointed preachers. He overcomes their reluctance, helps their weakness, authorises their message and confirms the truth of what they declare.

God's call of preachers
-----------------------

### It is not dependent upon background

**[AMO 7:14–7:15](https://ref.ly/Amos7:14-Amos7:15)** *See also* [JER 1:1–1:5](https://ref.ly/Jer1:1-Jer1:5); [MAT 4:18–4:20](https://ref.ly/Matt4:18-Matt4:20) parallels [MRK 1:16–1:19](https://ref.ly/Mark1:16-Mark1:19), [LUK 5:1–5:11](https://ref.ly/Luke5:1-Luke5:11); [ACT 9:10–9:16](https://ref.ly/Acts9:10-Acts9:16)

**[EXO 4:10–4:12](https://ref.ly/Exod4:10-Exod4:12)** *See also* [JER 1:6–1:9](https://ref.ly/Jer1:6-Jer1:9); [ACT 4:13](https://ref.ly/Acts4:13)
