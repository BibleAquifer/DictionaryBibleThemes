## 5942: security (id: 5942)

Guaranteed safety, whether natural or spiritual, from danger or loss. Scripture stresses that ultimate security is to be found only in God himself.

The security of Israel
----------------------

### Their security was found in God himself

**[DEU 33:27](https://ref.ly/Deut33:27)** *See also* [DEU 33:26](https://ref.ly/Deut33:26), [DEU 33:28–33:29](https://ref.ly/Deut33:28-Deut33:29); [EZR 8:21–8:23](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:21-Ezra8:23); [JER 31:10–31:11](https://ref.ly/Jer31:10-Jer31:11)

### They were secure from danger and loss

**[ISA 54:17](https://ref.ly/Isa54:17)** *See also* [EZK 34:27–34:28](https://ref.ly/Ezek34:27-Ezek34:28)
