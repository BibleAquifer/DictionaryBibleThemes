## 5473: proof, through testing (id: 5473)

God seeks proof of genuine love, faith and growth in the lives of his people. To this end, he tests them in various ways. However, when people test him, it is often a symptom of lack of trust.

Reasons for God's testing of Israel
-----------------------------------

### To refine them

**[ZEC 13:8–13:9](https://ref.ly/Zech13:8-Zech13:9)** *See also* [PSA 66:10–66:12](https://ref.ly/Ps66:10-Ps66:12); [PRO 17:3](https://ref.ly/Prov17:3); [ISA 48:10](https://ref.ly/Isa48:10); [JER 6:27–6:30](https://ref.ly/Jer6:27-Jer6:30), [JER 9:7](https://ref.ly/Jer9:7)

### To prove their obedience

**[EXO 16:4](https://ref.ly/Exod16:4)** *See also* [EXO 15:25–15:26](https://ref.ly/Exod15:25-Exod15:26); [DEU 8:2–8:5](https://ref.ly/Deut8:2-Deut8:5), [DEU 8:15–8:16](https://ref.ly/Deut8:15-Deut8:16); [JDG 2:22](https://ref.ly/Judg2:22), [JDG 3:1–3:4](https://ref.ly/Judg3:1-Judg3:4); [1CH 29:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:17)
