## 5614: weights and measures, laws concerning (id: 5614)

God expects his people to use accurate weights and measures, and to use just balances. This is underlined in the Law, the Prophets and the wisdom literature.

The law demanded honesty in using weights and measures
------------------------------------------------------

**[LEV 19:35–19:36](https://ref.ly/Lev19:35-Lev19:36)** *Cheating by falsifying weights and measures was common in a culture where there was no standardisation or regulatory authority.* *See also* [DEU 25:13–25:16](https://ref.ly/Deut25:13-Deut25:16)

The prophets called for the right use of weights and measures
-------------------------------------------------------------

### A call for honesty

**[EZK 45:9–45:10](https://ref.ly/Ezek45:9-Ezek45:10)**

### Condemnation of dishonesty

**[AMO 8:4–8:6](https://ref.ly/Amos8:4-Amos8:6)** *See also* [HOS 12:7–12:8](https://ref.ly/Hos12:7-Hos12:8); [MIC 6:10–6:14](https://ref.ly/Mic6:10-Mic6:14)

Instruction from Proverbs on weights and measures
-------------------------------------------------

**[PRO 20:10](https://ref.ly/Prov20:10)** *See also* [PRO 11:1](https://ref.ly/Prov11:1), [PRO 16:11](https://ref.ly/Prov16:11) *Merchants carried stones of different sizes in a pouch for weighing silver for payment*; [PRO 20:23](https://ref.ly/Prov20:23), [PRO 22:28](https://ref.ly/Prov22:28) *To move a boundary stone was in effect to steal land.*
