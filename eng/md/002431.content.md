## 8810: riches, dangers of (id: 8810)

Material wealth can be a source of both physical and spiritual danger, against which believers are clearly warned to be on their guard.

Riches are spiritually dangerous
--------------------------------

### Love of riches a root of many evils

**[1TI 6:10](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:10)** *See also* [JOS 7:11](https://ref.ly/Josh7:11), [JOS 7:20–7:21](https://ref.ly/Josh7:20-Josh7:21); [1TI 3:3](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:3)

### They may lead to divided loyalty

**[MAT 6:24](https://ref.ly/Matt6:24)** parallel [LUK 16:13](https://ref.ly/Luke16:13) *See also* [MAT 4:9–4:10](https://ref.ly/Matt4:9-Matt4:10); [JAS 4:4](https://ref.ly/Jas4:4)

### They may hinder people's response to the gospel

[MAT 13:22](https://ref.ly/Matt13:22) parallels [MRK 4:19](https://ref.ly/Mark4:19), [LUK 8:14](https://ref.ly/Luke8:14); ; [MAT 19:21–19:22](https://ref.ly/Matt19:21-Matt19:22) parallels [MRK 10:21–10:22](https://ref.ly/Mark10:21-Mark10:22), [LUK 18:22–18:23](https://ref.ly/Luke18:22-Luke18:23)

### They may create false security

**[1TI 6:17](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:17)** *See also* [JOB 31:24–31:28](https://ref.ly/Job31:24-Job31:28); [PSA 52:6–52:7](https://ref.ly/Ps52:6-Ps52:7); [PRO 11:28](https://ref.ly/Prov11:28); [JER 49:4](https://ref.ly/Jer49:4)

### They may make people proud

**[EZK 28:5](https://ref.ly/Ezek28:5)** *See also* [PSA 62:10](https://ref.ly/Ps62:10); [PRO 18:23](https://ref.ly/Prov18:23), [PRO 28:11](https://ref.ly/Prov28:11); [HOS 12:8](https://ref.ly/Hos12:8)

### They may cause unbelief and anxiety

**[ECC 5:12](https://ref.ly/Eccl5:12)** *See also* [JOB 20:17–20:20](https://ref.ly/Job20:17-Job20:20); [PRO 15:6](https://ref.ly/Prov15:6)

### They may lead people to forget God

**[PRO 30:8–30:9](https://ref.ly/Prov30:8-Prov30:9)** *See also* [DEU 8:10–8:14](https://ref.ly/Deut8:10-Deut8:14), [DEU 32:15](https://ref.ly/Deut32:15); [NEH 9:25–9:26](https://ref.ly/Neh9:25-Neh9:26); [HOS 13:6](https://ref.ly/Hos13:6)

Riches are physically dangerous
-------------------------------

### Working for them may cause excessive tiredness

**[PRO 23:4](https://ref.ly/Prov23:4)** *See also* [JHN 6:26–6:27](https://ref.ly/John6:26-John6:27)

### Enjoying them may lead to self\-indulgence

**[JAS 5:5](https://ref.ly/Jas5:5)** *See also* [HAG 1:3–1:5](https://ref.ly/Hag1:3-Hag1:5); [MAT 23:25](https://ref.ly/Matt23:25); [LUK 16:19–16:21](https://ref.ly/Luke16:19-Luke16:21); [1TI 6:8–6:9](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:8-1Tim6:9)

### Misusing them may foster oppression and injustice

**[LEV 19:13](https://ref.ly/Lev19:13)** *See also* [DEU 24:12–24:15](https://ref.ly/Deut24:12-Deut24:15); [PRO 22:16](https://ref.ly/Prov22:16); [ISA 1:23](https://ref.ly/Isa1:23); [JER 22:13](https://ref.ly/Jer22:13); [MIC 6:12](https://ref.ly/Mic6:12); [MAL 3:5](https://ref.ly/Mal3:5); [JAS 2:6](https://ref.ly/Jas2:6), [JAS 5:4](https://ref.ly/Jas5:4)
