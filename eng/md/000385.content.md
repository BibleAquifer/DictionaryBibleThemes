## 4287: universe (id: 4287)

All existing things, including the earth and its creatures and all the heavenly bodies. The universe was made by God and is sustained by Jesus Christ.

------------------------------

**[PSA 115:15](https://ref.ly/Ps115:15)**; **[JER 10:16](https://ref.ly/Jer10:16)**; **[REV 4:11](https://ref.ly/Rev4:11)** *See also* [GEN 14:19](https://ref.ly/Gen14:19); [2KI 19:15](https://ref.ly/2Kgs19:15); [ECC 11:5](https://ref.ly/Eccl11:5); [ISA 37:16](https://ref.ly/Isa37:16), [ISA 44:24](https://ref.ly/Isa44:24); [ACT 14:15](https://ref.ly/Acts14:15); [ROM 11:33–11:36](https://ref.ly/Rom11:33-Rom11:36); [1CO 8:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:6); [EPH 3:9](https://ref.ly/Eph3:9); [HEB 11:3](https://ref.ly/Heb11:3)
