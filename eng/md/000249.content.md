## 3025: Holy Spirit, personality of (id: 3025)

The Holy Spirit is not an impersonal power but a real person with his own personality.

The Holy Spirit is involved in personal relationships
-----------------------------------------------------

### He is in relationship with the Father and the Son

**[MAT 28:19](https://ref.ly/Matt28:19)** *See also* [JHN 16:14–16:15](https://ref.ly/John16:14-John16:15); [2CO 13:14](https://ref.ly/2Cor13:14); [1PE 1:2](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:2); [JUD 1:20–1:21](https://ref.ly/Jude1:20-Jude1:21)

### He is in relationship with Christians

**[ACT 15:28](https://ref.ly/Acts15:28)**

### The Spirit can be treated in personal ways

**[ACT 5:3](https://ref.ly/Acts5:3)** *See also* [ACT 5:9](https://ref.ly/Acts5:9); [HEB 10:29](https://ref.ly/Heb10:29)

Personal characteristics of the Holy Spirit
-------------------------------------------

### The Spirit is wise

**[ISA 11:2](https://ref.ly/Isa11:2)**; **[EPH 1:17](https://ref.ly/Eph1:17)** *See also* [JHN 14:26](https://ref.ly/John14:26); [ROM 8:27](https://ref.ly/Rom8:27)

### The Spirit can experience emotion and pain

**[EPH 4:30](https://ref.ly/Eph4:30)** *See also* [ISA 63:10](https://ref.ly/Isa63:10); [HEB 10:29](https://ref.ly/Heb10:29)
