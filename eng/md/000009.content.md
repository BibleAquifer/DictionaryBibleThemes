## 1040: God, fatherhood of (id: 1040)

Primarily signifying God's paternal relationship to Jesus Christ, the term also refers to God's fatherly relationship to his creation, especially to believers as the “children of God”.

Aspects of the fatherhood of God
--------------------------------

### He is the Creator and provider

**[1CO 8:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:6)** *See also* [DEU 32:6](https://ref.ly/Deut32:6), [DEU 32:18](https://ref.ly/Deut32:18); [ISA 64:8](https://ref.ly/Isa64:8); [ACT 17:24–17:28](https://ref.ly/Acts17:24-Acts17:28)

### He shows love and compassion

**[HOS 11:1](https://ref.ly/Hos11:1)** *See also* [PSA 103:13](https://ref.ly/Ps103:13); [JER 3:19](https://ref.ly/Jer3:19); [2CO 1:3](https://ref.ly/2Cor1:3)

### He exercises his providence and care

**[PSA 68:5](https://ref.ly/Ps68:5)** *See also* [MAT 6:26](https://ref.ly/Matt6:26), [MAT 6:31–6:33](https://ref.ly/Matt6:31-Matt6:33) parallel [LUK 12:29–12:31](https://ref.ly/Luke12:29-Luke12:31); [MAT 7:11](https://ref.ly/Matt7:11) parallel [LUK 11:13](https://ref.ly/Luke11:13)

### He disciplines and corrects

**[DEU 8:5](https://ref.ly/Deut8:5)** *See also* [PRO 3:11–3:12](https://ref.ly/Prov3:11-Prov3:12); [HEB 12:5–12:6](https://ref.ly/Heb12:5-Heb12:6)

God as the Father of Jesus Christ
---------------------------------

**[MAT 3:17](https://ref.ly/Matt3:17)** parallels [MRK 1:11](https://ref.ly/Mark1:11), [LUK 3:22](https://ref.ly/Luke3:22) *See also* [MAT 11:27](https://ref.ly/Matt11:27); [LUK 2:49](https://ref.ly/Luke2:49); [JHN 5:17–5:18](https://ref.ly/John5:17-John5:18), [JHN 17:11](https://ref.ly/John17:11), [JHN 20:17](https://ref.ly/John20:17); [ROM 15:6](https://ref.ly/Rom15:6); [EPH 1:3](https://ref.ly/Eph1:3)**[1PE 1:3](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:3)** *See also* [1JN 1:3](https://ref.ly/1John1:3)

God is the father of Israel
---------------------------

### He cares for his covenant people

**[JER 31:9](https://ref.ly/Jer31:9)** *See also* [EXO 4:22](https://ref.ly/Exod4:22); [DEU 14:1](https://ref.ly/Deut14:1), [DEU 32:6](https://ref.ly/Deut32:6), [DEU 32:18](https://ref.ly/Deut32:18); [ISA 63:16](https://ref.ly/Isa63:16); [MAL 2:10](https://ref.ly/Mal2:10)

### He is the father of the kings of Israel

**[2SA 7:13–7:14](https://ref.ly/2Sam7:13-2Sam7:14)** parallel [1CH 17:12–17:13](https://ref.ly/1Chr17:12-1Chr17:13) *This description of God's fatherhood involves the maintenance of a special relationship between God and David's descendants, which ultimately includes Jesus Christ.* *See also* [PSA 2:7](https://ref.ly/Ps2:7), [PSA 89:26–89:27](https://ref.ly/Ps89:26-Ps89:27)

God is the father of Christian believers
----------------------------------------

### He has an intimate relationship with believers

**[MAT 6:9](https://ref.ly/Matt6:9)** parallel [LUK 11:2](https://ref.ly/Luke11:2) *See also* [JHN 20:17](https://ref.ly/John20:17); [2CO 6:18](https://ref.ly/2Cor6:18); [GAL 3:26](https://ref.ly/Gal3:26); [1JN 2:13](https://ref.ly/1John2:13)

### He adopts believers into his family

**[JHN 1:12–1:13](https://ref.ly/John1:12-John1:13)** *See also* [ROM 8:14–8:17](https://ref.ly/Rom8:14-Rom8:17); [GAL 4:5–4:7](https://ref.ly/Gal4:5-Gal4:7); [1JN 3:1](https://ref.ly/1John3:1)

The fatherhood of God has implications for the church
-----------------------------------------------------

### Character: children are to reproduce the Father's likeness

**[MAT 5:48](https://ref.ly/Matt5:48)**

### Unity: one Father means one family

**[EPH 4:3–4:6](https://ref.ly/Eph4:3-Eph4:6)** *See also* [MAL 2:10](https://ref.ly/Mal2:10); [MAT 23:9](https://ref.ly/Matt23:9); [EPH 3:14–3:15](https://ref.ly/Eph3:14-Eph3:15)

### Status: believers are related to Jesus Christ

**[MAT 12:50](https://ref.ly/Matt12:50)** parallels [MRK 3:35](https://ref.ly/Mark3:35), [LUK 8:21](https://ref.ly/Luke8:21) *See also* [ROM 8:29](https://ref.ly/Rom8:29); [HEB 2:11](https://ref.ly/Heb2:11)

### Inheritance: believers are heirs of the Father's kingdom

**[ROM 8:17](https://ref.ly/Rom8:17)** *See also* [LUK 12:32](https://ref.ly/Luke12:32); [GAL 3:29](https://ref.ly/Gal3:29); [COL 1:12](https://ref.ly/Col1:12); [TIT 3:7](https://ref.ly/Titus3:7)

### Submission: believers are to revere the Father

**[1PE 1:17](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:17)** *See also* [MAT 6:9](https://ref.ly/Matt6:9) parallel [LUK 11:2](https://ref.ly/Luke11:2); [EPH 3:14](https://ref.ly/Eph3:14)
