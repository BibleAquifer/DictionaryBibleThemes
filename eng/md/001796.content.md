## 7361: Feast of Weeks (id: 7361)

This feast, which took place seven weeks after Passover in Sivan (May/June), was also known as the Feast of Pentecost. It expressed joy and thankfulness to God for the harvest.

The Feast of Weeks was one of three compulsory feasts
-----------------------------------------------------

[EXO 23:16](https://ref.ly/Exod23:16), [EXO 34:22](https://ref.ly/Exod34:22)

Offerings made at the Feast of Weeks
------------------------------------

### The firstfruits of the wheat harvest were offered to God

[NUM 28:26](https://ref.ly/Num28:26); ; [DEU 16:10](https://ref.ly/Deut16:10) *The offerings brought were to be in proportion to the harvest.*

### Two loaves of bread were also offered

[LEV 23:17](https://ref.ly/Lev23:17)

### Burnt offerings and sin offerings were made

[LEV 23:18–23:20](https://ref.ly/Lev23:18-Lev23:20) parallel [NUM 28:27–28:30](https://ref.ly/Num28:27-Num28:30)

The Feast of Weeks (Pentecost) in the NT
----------------------------------------

**[ACT 2:1–2:4](https://ref.ly/Acts2:1-Acts2:4)** *The term “Pentecost” refers to the 50 days that separate this feast from the Passover Sabbath.*
