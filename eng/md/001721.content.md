## 7135: Israel, as the people of God (id: 7135)

God called Israel out of Egypt to be his own treasured possession and his covenant faithfulness with them is maintained, despite their persistent disobedience.

God's election of Israel
------------------------

### The nation was founded upon God's promises to the patriarchs

**[GEN 12:1–12:3](https://ref.ly/Gen12:1-Gen12:3)** *See also* [GEN 17:1–17:8](https://ref.ly/Gen17:1-Gen17:8), [GEN 35:9–35:13](https://ref.ly/Gen35:9-Gen35:13)

### Israel as God's chosen possession

**[1KI 8:53](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:53)** *See also* [EXO 19:3–19:6](https://ref.ly/Exod19:3-Exod19:6); [DEU 7:6](https://ref.ly/Deut7:6), [DEU 32:7–32:12](https://ref.ly/Deut32:7-Deut32:12); [PSA 135:3–135:4](https://ref.ly/Ps135:3-Ps135:4)

### God redeemed Israel from Egypt

**[EXO 6:6–6:7](https://ref.ly/Exod6:6-Exod6:7)** *See also* [EXO 3:7–3:10](https://ref.ly/Exod3:7-Exod3:10); [2SA 7:22–7:24](https://ref.ly/2Sam7:22-2Sam7:24) parallel [1CH 17:20–17:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr17:20-1Chr17:22)

### God enters into a special covenant relationship with Israel

[EXO 24:3–24:8](https://ref.ly/Exod24:3-Exod24:8)

### Israel's privileged status within the covenant

**[ROM 9:4–9:5](https://ref.ly/Rom9:4-Rom9:5)** *See also* [ROM 3:1–3:2](https://ref.ly/Rom3:1-Rom3:2); [PHP 3:4–3:6](https://ref.ly/Phil3:4-Phil3:6)

Jesus Christ, the Saviour of Israel
-----------------------------------

### Jesus Christ's ministry was specifically to the nation of Israel

**[MAT 10:5–10:6](https://ref.ly/Matt10:5-Matt10:6)** *See also* [MAT 15:21–15:28](https://ref.ly/Matt15:21-Matt15:28) parallel [MRK 7:24–7:30](https://ref.ly/Mark7:24-Mark7:30)

### Jesus accepts the title \`\`Christ'' (Messiah), God's anointed deliverer for Israel

**[MAT 16:16–16:17](https://ref.ly/Matt16:16-Matt16:17)** parallels [MRK 8:29](https://ref.ly/Mark8:29), [LUK 9:20](https://ref.ly/Luke9:20) *Although Jesus accepted the title “Messiah” (Christ), he did not fulfil contemporary Israelite hopes of a political deliverer.*

### Jesus Christ declares to Israel the good news of God's reign

**[MRK 1:14–1:15](https://ref.ly/Mark1:14-Mark1:15)** *Jesus Christ takes up Isaiah's message of good news, that God is coming in sovereign power to restore Israel (see [Isa 40:9–10](https://ref.ly/Isa40:9-Isa40:10), [52:7](https://ref.ly/Isa52:7)).* *See also* [MAT 4:23](https://ref.ly/Matt4:23), [MAT 9:35](https://ref.ly/Matt9:35); [LUK 8:1](https://ref.ly/Luke8:1)

God's faithfulness to Israel maintained
---------------------------------------

### Membership of the true Israel is based on God's election, not merely on physical descent from Abraham

[ROM 9:6–9:18](https://ref.ly/Rom9:6-Rom9:18)

### A remnant of Israel has been elected by grace

[ROM 11:1–11:12](https://ref.ly/Rom11:1-Rom11:12)

### Paul declares that \`\`all Israel'' will be saved

[ISA 59:20–59:21](https://ref.ly/Isa59:20-Isa59:21); ; [ROM 11:25–11:32](https://ref.ly/Rom11:25-Rom11:32) *The phrase “all Israel” is interpreted as either: (1\) the total number of the elect (either Jews, or Jews and Gentiles) of every generation, or (2\) the great majority of Jews of the final generation who will be saved when Christ returns.*

The new Israel
--------------

### The church as the new Israel

**[GAL 6:15–6:16](https://ref.ly/Gal6:15-Gal6:16)**; **[PHP 3:3](https://ref.ly/Phil3:3)** *See also* [1PE 2:9–2:10](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:9-1Pet2:10)

### The church described as the 12 tribes of Israel

**[JAS 1:1](https://ref.ly/Jas1:1)** *See also* [REV 7:4–7:8](https://ref.ly/Rev7:4-Rev7:8) *Some see this as a reference to members of actual Jewish tribes, but others see it as symbolic of all the faithful believers who live through the time of tribulation*; [REV 21:12](https://ref.ly/Rev21:12) *The number 12 emphasises the continuity of the NT church and the OT people of God.*

### The apostles as the 12 leaders of the new Israel
