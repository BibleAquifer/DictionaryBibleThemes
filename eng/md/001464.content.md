## 5951: slander (id: 5951)

False and malicious talk about others. It not only harms those against whom it is directed, but also brings ruin to those who propagate it.

Slander condemned
-----------------

### Slander is sinful

**[MRK 7:21–7:23](https://ref.ly/Mark7:21-Mark7:23)** parallel [MAT 15:19–15:20](https://ref.ly/Matt15:19-Matt15:20) *See also* [JER 9:4–9:6](https://ref.ly/Jer9:4-Jer9:6); [EZK 22:9](https://ref.ly/Ezek22:9); [ROM 1:29–1:31](https://ref.ly/Rom1:29-Rom1:31); [2CO 12:20](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:20); [2TI 3:2–3:4](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:2-2Tim3:4)

### Slander is forbidden

**[LEV 19:16](https://ref.ly/Lev19:16)** *See also* [EXO 23:1](https://ref.ly/Exod23:1); [PRO 3:30](https://ref.ly/Prov3:30), [PRO 10:18](https://ref.ly/Prov10:18), [PRO 12:22](https://ref.ly/Prov12:22); [EPH 4:31](https://ref.ly/Eph4:31); [COL 3:8](https://ref.ly/Col3:8); [TIT 2:3](https://ref.ly/Titus2:3), [TIT 3:1–3:2](https://ref.ly/Titus3:1-Titus3:2); [JAS 4:11](https://ref.ly/Jas4:11); [1PE 2:1](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:1)

### Slander is destructive

**[PRO 16:27](https://ref.ly/Prov16:27)** *See also* [PRO 11:9](https://ref.ly/Prov11:9), [PRO 26:28](https://ref.ly/Prov26:28), [PRO 30:10](https://ref.ly/Prov30:10)
