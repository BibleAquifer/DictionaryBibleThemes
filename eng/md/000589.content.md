## 4915: completion (id: 4915)

The bringing to fulfilment of a work, such as the work of creation or redemption.

Creation was completed by the seventh day
-----------------------------------------

**[GEN 2:1–2:2](https://ref.ly/Gen2:1-Gen2:2)**; **[DEU 32:4](https://ref.ly/Deut32:4)** *See also* [GEN 1:3–1:4](https://ref.ly/Gen1:3-Gen1:4) *Genesis chapter 1 records the creation completed on each successive day as being “good”*; [EXO 20:11](https://ref.ly/Exod20:11); [JHN 1:3](https://ref.ly/John1:3); [COL 1:16](https://ref.ly/Col1:16); [HEB 1:2](https://ref.ly/Heb1:2), [HEB 4:3](https://ref.ly/Heb4:3), [HEB 11:3](https://ref.ly/Heb11:3)
