## 8149: revival, nature of (id: 8149)

Scripture indicates that a number of characteristics precede revival, including repentance, humility and obedience.

Characteristics which precede revival
-------------------------------------

### God's people long for renewal of their lives

**[PSA 80:18](https://ref.ly/Ps80:18)** *See also* [PSA 74:22](https://ref.ly/Ps74:22), [PSA 80:1–80:17](https://ref.ly/Ps80:1-Ps80:17), [PSA 80:19](https://ref.ly/Ps80:19), [PSA 85:6](https://ref.ly/Ps85:6)

### God's people must repent

**[2CH 7:14](https://ref.ly/2Chr7:14)** *See also* [1KI 8:46–8:50](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:46-1Kgs8:50) parallel [2CH 6:26–6:27](https://ref.ly/2Chr6:26-2Chr6:27); [ISA 64:1–64:7](https://ref.ly/Isa64:1-Isa64:7); [HOS 5:15](https://ref.ly/Hos5:15); [ACT 3:19](https://ref.ly/Acts3:19)

### God's people experience a new awareness of sin

**[2KI 22:11](https://ref.ly/2Kgs22:11)** *See also* [PSA 32:3–32:5](https://ref.ly/Ps32:3-Ps32:5)

### God's people need to be humble

**[ISA 57:15](https://ref.ly/Isa57:15)** *See also* [PSA 149:4](https://ref.ly/Ps149:4); [ISA 66:2](https://ref.ly/Isa66:2); [MIC 6:6–6:8](https://ref.ly/Mic6:6-Mic6:8)

### God's people are revived through God's initiative

**[ISA 59:16](https://ref.ly/Isa59:16)** *See also* [JER 24:7](https://ref.ly/Jer24:7), [JER 33:6–33:9](https://ref.ly/Jer33:6-Jer33:9); [TIT 3:5](https://ref.ly/Titus3:5)

Characteristic results of revival
---------------------------------

### People experience inward change

**[HEB 8:10–8:12](https://ref.ly/Heb8:10-Heb8:12)** *See also* [JER 31:33–31:34](https://ref.ly/Jer31:33-Jer31:34); [EZK 11:19](https://ref.ly/Ezek11:19); [ACT 2:42–2:47](https://ref.ly/Acts2:42-Acts2:47)

### People live obedient lives

**[EZK 11:20](https://ref.ly/Ezek11:20)** *See also* [EPH 4:1–4:3](https://ref.ly/Eph4:1-Eph4:3); [1TH 1:7–1:8](https://ref.ly/1Thess1:7-1Thess1:8)

### People are zealous for God's work

**[EZR 5:1–5:2](https://ref.ly/Ezra5:1-Ezra5:2)** *See also* [HAG 1:12–1:15](https://ref.ly/Hag1:12-Hag1:15)

### People are generous in giving

**[EXO 36:5](https://ref.ly/Exod36:5)** *See also* [1CH 29:6–29:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:6-1Chr29:9); [2CH 31:3–31:8](https://ref.ly/2Chr31:3-2Chr31:8); [ACT 11:28–11:30](https://ref.ly/Acts11:28-Acts11:30)

### People delight in worshipping God

**[EZR 3:11](https://ref.ly/Ezra3:11)** *See also* [ISA 12:1–12:6](https://ref.ly/Isa12:1-Isa12:6)

### People are joyful

**[ACT 13:49–13:52](https://ref.ly/Acts13:49-Acts13:52)** *See also* [ISA 35:1–35:10](https://ref.ly/Isa35:1-Isa35:10); [ACT 8:5–8:8](https://ref.ly/Acts8:5-Acts8:8)
