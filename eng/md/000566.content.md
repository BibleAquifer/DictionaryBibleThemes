## 4842: north (id: 4842)

The geographical direction opposite to south, often used to demonstrate the vast extent between the two.

North as a point of the compass
-------------------------------

**[JOB 37:22](https://ref.ly/Job37:22)** *See also* [GEN 13:14](https://ref.ly/Gen13:14); [DEU 2:3](https://ref.ly/Deut2:3); [JOS 18:5](https://ref.ly/Josh18:5); [ECC 11:3](https://ref.ly/Eccl11:3); [ISA 14:31](https://ref.ly/Isa14:31); [JER 1:13–1:15](https://ref.ly/Jer1:13-Jer1:15), [JER 3:18](https://ref.ly/Jer3:18) *Although geographically situated to the east of Israel, invaders from Mesopotamia had to enter the country from the north, following the land route around the desert areas*; [JER 46:6](https://ref.ly/Jer46:6); [EZK 8:5](https://ref.ly/Ezek8:5), [EZK 26:7](https://ref.ly/Ezek26:7); [AMO 8:12](https://ref.ly/Amos8:12); [ZEP 2:13](https://ref.ly/Zeph2:13)
