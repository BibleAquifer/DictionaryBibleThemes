## 5714: men (id: 5714)

God created both men and women in his image and likeness. Scripture portrays men in both their strengths and weaknesses, their successes and failures. While noting that they are incomplete without women, and share equally in the privileges and tasks entrusted to them by God, men have nevertheless been given specific responsibilities by God.

The place of men
----------------

### Men are equal with women in creation

**[GEN 1:27](https://ref.ly/Gen1:27)** *See also* [GEN 5:2](https://ref.ly/Gen5:2)

### Men are incomplete without women

**[GEN 2:18](https://ref.ly/Gen2:18)** *See also* [GEN 2:21–2:25](https://ref.ly/Gen2:21-Gen2:25); [1CO 11:11–11:12](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:11-1Cor11:12)

### Absence of men in society seen as a curse

**[ISA 3:1–3:3](https://ref.ly/Isa3:1-Isa3:3)** *See also* [LAM 5:14](https://ref.ly/Lam5:14); [EZK 17:11–17:14](https://ref.ly/Ezek17:11-Ezek17:14)

### Men are equal with women in redemption

**[GAL 3:28](https://ref.ly/Gal3:28)** *See also* [ACT 2:17–2:18](https://ref.ly/Acts2:17-Acts2:18); [JOL 2:28–2:29](https://ref.ly/Joel2:28-Joel2:29)

The role of men
---------------

### As leaders in marriage and family life

**[EPH 5:22–5:24](https://ref.ly/Eph5:22-Eph5:24)** *See also* [1CO 11:3](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:3); [COL 3:18–3:21](https://ref.ly/Col3:18-Col3:21)

### As leaders in Israelite society and worship

**[NUM 1:16](https://ref.ly/Num1:16)** *See also* [EXO 34:23–34:24](https://ref.ly/Exod34:23-Exod34:24); [NUM 3:10–3:15](https://ref.ly/Num3:10-Num3:15); [DEU 1:9–1:15](https://ref.ly/Deut1:9-Deut1:15); [1SA 8:1](https://ref.ly/1Sam8:1); [EZR 10:16–10:17](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:16-Ezra10:17)

### As leaders in the early church

**[1TI 3:2](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:2)** *See also* [ACT 6:2–6:6](https://ref.ly/Acts6:2-Acts6:6); [1TI 2:12](https://ref.ly/1Tim2:12) *The role of teaching and exercising authority is here restricted to men. Interpretations vary as to whether Paul is reflecting cultural circumstance or establishing theological principle*; [1TI 3:8](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:8)

The character requirements of men
---------------------------------

### To be prayerful and godly

**[1TI 2:8](https://ref.ly/1Tim2:8)** *See also* [DAN 6:10–6:11](https://ref.ly/Dan6:10-Dan6:11); [1TI 4:7–4:8](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:7-1Tim4:8), [1TI 6:11–6:12](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:11-1Tim6:12)

### To be courageous

**[1CO 16:13](https://ref.ly/1Cor16:13)** *While courage can be exercised by all, it is identified here as a specifically male characteristic.* *See also* [ACT 4:13](https://ref.ly/Acts4:13), [ACT 27:21–27:25](https://ref.ly/Acts27:21-Acts27:25)

### To be determined and decisive

**[MAT 11:12](https://ref.ly/Matt11:12)** *See also* [1CH 12:38](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:38)

### To be humble

**[1PE 5:5](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:5)** *See also* [NUM 12:3](https://ref.ly/Num12:3); [DAN 10:12](https://ref.ly/Dan10:12); [MAT 11:29](https://ref.ly/Matt11:29)

### To love their wives

**[COL 3:19](https://ref.ly/Col3:19)** *See also* [PRO 5:15–5:19](https://ref.ly/Prov5:15-Prov5:19); [EPH 5:25–5:33](https://ref.ly/Eph5:25-Eph5:33)

### To be good fathers to their children

**[EPH 6:4](https://ref.ly/Eph6:4)** *See also* [GEN 37:3–37:4](https://ref.ly/Gen37:3-Gen37:4) *Jacob's favouritism was the cause of resentment developing in the family*; [1SA 3:12–3:13](https://ref.ly/1Sam3:12-1Sam3:13) *Eli is rebuked for having failed to father his sons properly*; [COL 3:21](https://ref.ly/Col3:21)

### To exercise proper sexual restraint

**[1TH 4:3–4:7](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:3-1Thess4:7)** *That Paul is particularly thinking of men here seems apparent from the NIV footnote at verse 4\.* *See also* [LEV 18:1–18:22](https://ref.ly/Lev18:1-Lev18:22); [PRO 6:23–6:29](https://ref.ly/Prov6:23-Prov6:29); [MAT 5:27–5:28](https://ref.ly/Matt5:27-Matt5:28); [1CO 5:1–5:2](https://ref.ly/1Cor5:1-1Cor5:2), [1CO 7:2–7:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:2-1Cor7:5)

Examples of godly men

[GEN 6:9](https://ref.ly/Gen6:9) *Noah*; ; [GEN 22:1–22:12](https://ref.ly/Gen22:1-Gen22:12) *Abraham*; ; [EXO 32:30–32:32](https://ref.ly/Exod32:30-Exod32:32) *Moses*; ; [1SA 24:1–24:7](https://ref.ly/1Sam24:1-1Sam24:7) *David*; ; [2KI 18:1–18:7](https://ref.ly/2Kgs18:1-2Kgs18:7) *Hezekiah*; ; [JOB 1:1–1:8](https://ref.ly/Job1:1-Job1:8) *Job*; ; [DAN 6:10](https://ref.ly/Dan6:10) *Daniel*; ; [ACT 4:13–4:20](https://ref.ly/Acts4:13-Acts4:20) *Peter and John*; ; [ACT 7:54–7:60](https://ref.ly/Acts7:54-Acts7:60) *Stephen*; ; [HEB 11:4–11:28](https://ref.ly/Heb11:4-Heb11:28) *OT heroes of faith*; ; [JAS 5:17–5:18](https://ref.ly/Jas5:17-Jas5:18) *Elijah*

Examples of godly men who had moments of failure
------------------------------------------------

[GEN 20:1–20:3](https://ref.ly/Gen20:1-Gen20:3) *Abraham was deceitful*; ; [NUM 20:2–20:12](https://ref.ly/Num20:2-Num20:12) *Moses and Aaron showed unbelief*; ; [2SA 11:1–11:27](https://ref.ly/2Sam11:1-2Sam11:27) *David committed adultery with Bathsheba and plotted Uriah's death*; ; [1KI 19:1–19:5](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:1-1Kgs19:5) *Elijah became fearful and wanted to die*; ; [JON 1:1–1:3](https://ref.ly/Jonah1:1-Jonah1:3) *Jonah ran from God's call on his life*; ; [MAT 26:69–26:75](https://ref.ly/Matt26:69-Matt26:75) parallels [MRK 14:66–14:72](https://ref.ly/Mark14:66-Mark14:72), [LUK 22:55–22:62](https://ref.ly/Luke22:55-Luke22:62), [JHN 18:16–18:18](https://ref.ly/John18:16-John18:18), [JHN 18:25–18:27](https://ref.ly/John18:25-John18:27) *Peter denied Jesus Christ*; ; [ACT 16:36–16:40](https://ref.ly/Acts16:36-Acts16:40) *Paul and Barnabas fell into sharp disagreement.*

Examples of ungodly men
-----------------------

[GEN 19:4–19:5](https://ref.ly/Gen19:4-Gen19:5) *The ungodliness of the men of Sodom lay in sexual immorality*; ; [GEN 38:8–38:10](https://ref.ly/Gen38:8-Gen38:10) *Onan's ungodliness lay in failing to fulfil his obligations to his brother's wife*; ; [NUM 16:1–16:35](https://ref.ly/Num16:1-Num16:35) *Korah's ungodliness lay in rebellion against God's leaders*; ; [1SA 2:12](https://ref.ly/1Sam2:12) *Eli's sons' ungodliness lay in their abuse of the sacrifices*; ; [1KI 16:29–16:33](https://ref.ly/1Kgs16:29-1Kgs16:33) *Ahab's ungodliness lay in intermarriage and idolatry*; ; [2KI 21:1–21:9](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:1-2Kgs21:9) parallel [2CH 33:1–33:9](https://ref.ly/2Chr33:1-2Chr33:9) *Manasseh's ungodliness lay in idolatry, divination and human sacrifice*; ; [MAT 2:16](https://ref.ly/Matt2:16) *Herod's ungodliness lay in the wholesale slaughter of infants*; ; [MAT 14:3–14:11](https://ref.ly/Matt14:3-Matt14:11) parallel [MRK 6:17–6:28](https://ref.ly/Mark6:17-Mark6:28) *Herod the tetrarch's ungodliness lay in immorality and murder*; ; [ACT 8:9–8:23](https://ref.ly/Acts8:9-Acts8:23) *Simon's ungodliness lay in the practice of magic*; ; [JUD 1:4–1:19](https://ref.ly/Jude1:4-Jude1:19) *The ungodliness of men in the church lay in their rejection of apostolic doctrine and lifestyle.*

Examples of fearful men
-----------------------

[GEN 26:7–26:9](https://ref.ly/Gen26:7-Gen26:9) *Isaac's fear of the Philistines led him to lie*; ; [NUM 13:26–13:33](https://ref.ly/Num13:26-Num13:33) *The spies' fear led them to give a bad report*; ; [1SA 28:5–28:7](https://ref.ly/1Sam28:5-1Sam28:7) *Saul's fear of the Philistines led him to enquire of a medium when the LORD did not answer him*; ; [2SA 3:7–3:11](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:7-2Sam3:11) *Ish\-Bosheth's fear of his brother led him to stay silent concerning sin*; ; [JHN 19:4–19:16](https://ref.ly/John19:4-John19:16) parallels [MAT 27:19–27:26](https://ref.ly/Matt27:19-Matt27:26), [MRK 15:9–15:15](https://ref.ly/Mark15:9-Mark15:15), [LUK 23:13–23:25](https://ref.ly/Luke23:13-Luke23:25) *Pilate's fear of the crowd led him to condemn Jesus Christ, even though he knew he was innocent*; ; [ACT 24:24–24:25](https://ref.ly/Acts24:24-Acts24:25) *Felix' fear arose from the message of the gospel.*

Examples of weak men
--------------------

[LEV 26:36–26:39](https://ref.ly/Lev26:36-Lev26:39) *Men who are disobedient to God will be weak*; ; [1SA 3:12–3:13](https://ref.ly/1Sam3:12-1Sam3:13) *Eli's weakness lay in failing to restrain his sons*; ; [1KI 21:1–21:16](https://ref.ly/1Kgs21:1-1Kgs21:16) *Ahab's weakness lay in his allowing his wife to rule his life and his kingdom.*
