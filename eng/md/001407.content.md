## 5889: ingratitude (id: 5889)

The absence of due thankfulness. Scripture gives examples of human ingratitude to God for all the blessings and gifts which he provides for them. It is especially important for Christians to give thanks to God.

The ingratitude of human beings to one another
----------------------------------------------

### Examples of ingratitude

[GEN 40:23](https://ref.ly/Gen40:23) *the chief butler towards Joseph*; ; [NUM 16:13–16:14](https://ref.ly/Num16:13-Num16:14) *Dathan and Abiram to Moses*; [JDG 8:35](https://ref.ly/Judg8:35), [JDG 9:18](https://ref.ly/Judg9:18); [1SA 25:21](https://ref.ly/1Sam25:21) *Nabal to David*; ; [2CH 24:22](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:22) *King Joash to Jehoiada*; ; [ECC 9:15](https://ref.ly/Eccl9:15) *the citizens to the wise man who delivered a city*
