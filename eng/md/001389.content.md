## 5870: greed, condemnation of (id: 5870)

Greed is condemned by Scripture as contrary to the purposes of God.

Greed is a feature of the fallen world
--------------------------------------

**[PSA 10:3](https://ref.ly/Ps10:3)**; **[ROM 1:29](https://ref.ly/Rom1:29)** *See also* [1CO 5:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor5:10)
