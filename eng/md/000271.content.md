## 3233: Holy Spirit, and sanctification (id: 3233)

The work of the Holy Spirit in enabling believers to lead holy lives, dedicated to the service of God and conformed to his likeness.

The Spirit of holiness is promised
----------------------------------

**[MAT 3:11](https://ref.ly/Matt3:11)** parallel [LUK 3:16](https://ref.ly/Luke3:16) *“fire” implies the Holy Spirit's work of purification and judgment.*

Sanctification is a special work of the Holy Spirit
---------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 15:16](https://ref.ly/Rom15:16)** *See also* [1CO 6:11](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:11); [GAL 5:5](https://ref.ly/Gal5:5); [1PE 1:2](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:2)

The Holy Spirit requires believers to be sanctified
---------------------------------------------------

**[2TH 2:13](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:13)** *Sanctification is a necessary part of being a Christian.* *See also* [1CO 6:18–6:19](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:18-1Cor6:19)

The Holy Spirit enables believers to be sanctified
--------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 8:4](https://ref.ly/Rom8:4)** *See also* [ROM 8:13](https://ref.ly/Rom8:13); [EPH 5:18](https://ref.ly/Eph5:18)

The Holy Spirit produces sanctification
---------------------------------------

**[GAL 5:22–5:23](https://ref.ly/Gal5:22-Gal5:23)** *See also* [ROM 14:17](https://ref.ly/Rom14:17); [2TI 1:7](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:7)

The process of sanctification
-----------------------------

**[2CO 3:18](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:18)**
