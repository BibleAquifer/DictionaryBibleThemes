## 1165: God, uniqueness of (id: 1165)

No\-one and nothing is comparable to the triune God in his nature or comparable to him in his character and activity.

There is no God except the LORD
-------------------------------

**[ISA 45:5–45:6](https://ref.ly/Isa45:5-Isa45:6)** *God alone stands outside nature as its Creator. All other beings are created by him.* *See also* [DEU 4:35](https://ref.ly/Deut4:35), [DEU 6:4](https://ref.ly/Deut6:4), [DEU 32:3](https://ref.ly/Deut32:3); [1KI 8:60](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:60); [PSA 83:18](https://ref.ly/Ps83:18), [PSA 86:10](https://ref.ly/Ps86:10); [ISA 43:10–43:11](https://ref.ly/Isa43:10-Isa43:11), [ISA 44:6–44:8](https://ref.ly/Isa44:6-Isa44:8), [ISA 45:18](https://ref.ly/Isa45:18); [1CO 8:4–8:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:4-1Cor8:6); [EPH 4:6](https://ref.ly/Eph4:6); [1TI 2:5](https://ref.ly/1Tim2:5)
