## 4627: creatures (id: 4627)

Generally, living beings within God's creation, especially the exotic animals seen in visions, which often have a deep symbolic significance.

Creatures representing angelic beings who worship God
-----------------------------------------------------

**[EZK 1:5–1:6](https://ref.ly/Ezek1:5-Ezek1:6)**; **[REV 4:6–4:8](https://ref.ly/Rev4:6-Rev4:8)** *The strange creatures of Ezekiel and Revelation seem to stand for the fulness and variety of God's creation.* *See also* [EZK 1:7–1:25](https://ref.ly/Ezek1:7-Ezek1:25), [EZK 3:12–3:13](https://ref.ly/Ezek3:12-Ezek3:13), [EZK 10:15–10:22](https://ref.ly/Ezek10:15-Ezek10:22) *The creatures are here identified as cherubim*; [REV 4:9–4:11](https://ref.ly/Rev4:9-Rev4:11), [REV 5:6–5:14](https://ref.ly/Rev5:6-Rev5:14), [REV 6:1–6:3](https://ref.ly/Rev6:1-Rev6:3), [REV 6:5–6:6](https://ref.ly/Rev6:5-Rev6:6), [REV 7:11](https://ref.ly/Rev7:11), [REV 14:3](https://ref.ly/Rev14:3), [REV 15:7](https://ref.ly/Rev15:7), [REV 19:4](https://ref.ly/Rev19:4)

Creatures representing kings or kingdoms
----------------------------------------

**[DAN 7:17](https://ref.ly/Dan7:17), [DAN 8:20–8:21](https://ref.ly/Dan8:20-Dan8:21)** *The “beasts” here are unnatural, and represent the hostility of human empires to the rule of God. The horns represent individual kings within an imperial dynasty.*

Creatures representing Satan
----------------------------

The beast here represents Satan, with the horns representing royal authority at a time of Roman persecution of the church: [REV 12:3–12:4](https://ref.ly/Rev12:3-Rev12:4), [REV 12:7–12:9](https://ref.ly/Rev12:7-Rev12:9), [REV 13:1](https://ref.ly/Rev13:1); [DAN 7:2–7:8](https://ref.ly/Dan7:2-Dan7:8), [DAN 7:19–7:26](https://ref.ly/Dan7:19-Dan7:26), [DAN 8:3–8:12](https://ref.ly/Dan8:3-Dan8:12); ; [REV 11:7](https://ref.ly/Rev11:7), [REV 13:2–13:8](https://ref.ly/Rev13:2-Rev13:8), [REV 13:11–13:18](https://ref.ly/Rev13:11-Rev13:18), [REV 17:3](https://ref.ly/Rev17:3), [REV 17:7–17:13](https://ref.ly/Rev17:7-Rev17:13)
