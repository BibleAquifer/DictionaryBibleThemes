## 4548: wineskin (id: 4548)

A container or sack, made from animal skin to hold wine. Jesus Christ used the image of old and new wineskins to explain the differences between himself and John the Baptist.

Wineskins used to store or carry wine
-------------------------------------

**[1SA 10:2–10:4](https://ref.ly/1Sam10:2-1Sam10:4)** *See also* [1SA 1:24](https://ref.ly/1Sam1:24); [PSA 119:83](https://ref.ly/Ps119:83); [JER 13:12–13:14](https://ref.ly/Jer13:12-Jer13:14); [HAB 2:15–2:16](https://ref.ly/Hab2:15-Hab2:16)

Old and new wineskins
---------------------

**[JOB 32:17–32:20](https://ref.ly/Job32:17-Job32:20)** *New wine was stored in new wineskins, which expanded with the wine as it fermented.* *See also* [JOS 9:3–9:13](https://ref.ly/Josh9:3-Josh9:13)
