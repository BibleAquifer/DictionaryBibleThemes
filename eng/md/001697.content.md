## 6755: union with Christ, nature of (id: 6755)

The nature of the union of Jesus Christ with believers is explained using a number of central images. The NT stresses the reality, closeness and considerable benefits of this union.

A covenantal relationship
-------------------------

**[ISA 54:5–54:8](https://ref.ly/Isa54:5-Isa54:8)** *The marriage covenant is used to illustrate the intimacy of the relationship between God and his people.* *See also* [JER 3:14](https://ref.ly/Jer3:14), [JER 31:32](https://ref.ly/Jer31:32); [HOS 2:7](https://ref.ly/Hos2:7), [HOS 2:16](https://ref.ly/Hos2:16)
