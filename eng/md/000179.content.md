## 2233: Son of Man (id: 2233)

A Hebrew and Aramaic term, often used to mean a human being in general. In the Gospels Jesus Christ uses it to refer to himself in his earthly ministry and his future death, exaltation and coming as judge and Saviour.

Sons of men as a term for humanity in general
---------------------------------------------

### Human beings are inferior to God

**[PSA 115:16](https://ref.ly/Ps115:16)** *The Hebrew term here translated “man” literally means “sons of men”. Many of the OT references cited in this theme follow this pattern.* *See also* [PSA 11:4](https://ref.ly/Ps11:4), [PSA 33:13–33:14](https://ref.ly/Ps33:13-Ps33:14), [PSA 45:2](https://ref.ly/Ps45:2)

### Human beings are dependent on God's care

**[PSA 8:4](https://ref.ly/Ps8:4)** *See also* [PSA 36:7](https://ref.ly/Ps36:7), [PSA 80:17](https://ref.ly/Ps80:17), [PSA 107:8](https://ref.ly/Ps107:8), [PSA 107:15](https://ref.ly/Ps107:15), [PSA 107:21](https://ref.ly/Ps107:21), [PSA 107:31](https://ref.ly/Ps107:31)

### Human beings are mortal

**[ECC 9:3](https://ref.ly/Eccl9:3)** *See also* [ECC 3:18–3:19](https://ref.ly/Eccl3:18-Eccl3:19); [EZK 31:14](https://ref.ly/Ezek31:14)

### Human beings are sinful and untrustworthy

**[PSA 146:3](https://ref.ly/Ps146:3)** *See also* [PSA 14:2–14:3](https://ref.ly/Ps14:2-Ps14:3) parallel [PSA 53:2–53:3](https://ref.ly/Ps53:2-Ps53:3); [MIC 5:7](https://ref.ly/Mic5:7)

Son of Man as a term for individual men
---------------------------------------

### The sons of Adam

[GEN 4:1–4:2](https://ref.ly/Gen4:1-Gen4:2) *“a man” is literally “son of Adam” in Hebrew*; ; [GEN 5:3–5:4](https://ref.ly/Gen5:3-Gen5:4)

### Ezekiel

**[EZK 2:1](https://ref.ly/Ezek2:1)** *Ezekiel is called “son of man” 93 times, though this form of address is unique to him in the OT. It may emphasise his remoteness or his humanity.* *See also* [EZK 2:3](https://ref.ly/Ezek2:3), [EZK 2:6](https://ref.ly/Ezek2:6), [EZK 2:8](https://ref.ly/Ezek2:8), [EZK 3:1](https://ref.ly/Ezek3:1)

### Other people

[JOB 25:6](https://ref.ly/Job25:6); ; [DAN 8:17](https://ref.ly/Dan8:17)

A son of man as a ruler of God's future kingdom
-----------------------------------------------

**[DAN 7:13](https://ref.ly/Dan7:13)** *The Aramaic “one like a son of man” stresses this person's humanity and mysterious identity*; **[DAN 7:13–7:14](https://ref.ly/Dan7:13-Dan7:14)** *He enters God's presence and is given final authority over God's kingdom*; **[DAN 7:27](https://ref.ly/Dan7:27)** *He shares the kingdom with God's people.* *See also* [DAN 7:18](https://ref.ly/Dan7:18) *“saints of the Most High” have sometimes been interpreted as angels*

Son of Man as a title for Jesus Christ
--------------------------------------

### Used by Jesus Christ to emphasise his humanity and authority

**[MRK 2:10–2:11](https://ref.ly/Mark2:10-Mark2:11)** parallels [MAT 9:6](https://ref.ly/Matt9:6), [LUK 5:24](https://ref.ly/Luke5:24)**[MRK 8:31](https://ref.ly/Mark8:31)** parallel [LUK 9:22](https://ref.ly/Luke9:22)

### The Son of Man receives God's kingdom
