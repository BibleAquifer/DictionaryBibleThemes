## 2012: Jesus Christ, authority of (id: 2012)

The right of Jesus Christ to speak and act on his Father's behalf in forgiving sin, pronouncing judgment and promising eternal life to those who believe in him.

Jesus Christ's authority underlines his divine status
-----------------------------------------------------

**[JHN 8:28](https://ref.ly/John8:28)** *See also* [JHN 8:58](https://ref.ly/John8:58), [JHN 14:6](https://ref.ly/John14:6); [1CO 1:24](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:24); [COL 2:10](https://ref.ly/Col2:10)

--------------------------------------

### It is given by God the Father

**[JHN 3:35](https://ref.ly/John3:35)** *See also* [MAT 28:18](https://ref.ly/Matt28:18); [LUK 10:22](https://ref.ly/Luke10:22); [JHN 5:26–5:27](https://ref.ly/John5:26-John5:27), [JHN 7:16](https://ref.ly/John7:16), [JHN 10:18](https://ref.ly/John10:18), [JHN 12:49–12:50](https://ref.ly/John12:49-John12:50), [JHN 17:2](https://ref.ly/John17:2)

### It is limited by the Father

**[MRK 10:40](https://ref.ly/Mark10:40)** *See also* [MAT 24:36](https://ref.ly/Matt24:36) parallel [MRK 13:32](https://ref.ly/Mark13:32); [PHP 2:6–2:8](https://ref.ly/Phil2:6-Phil2:8)

**[MAT 12:24–12:28](https://ref.ly/Matt12:24-Matt12:28)** parallels [MRK 3:22–3:26](https://ref.ly/Mark3:22-Mark3:26), [LUK 11:15–11:20](https://ref.ly/Luke11:15-Luke11:20) *See also* [MAT 21:23–21:27](https://ref.ly/Matt21:23-Matt21:27) parallels [MRK 11:27–11:33](https://ref.ly/Mark11:27-Mark11:33), [LUK 20:1–20:8](https://ref.ly/Luke20:1-Luke20:8); [JHN 2:18](https://ref.ly/John2:18)
