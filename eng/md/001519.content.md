## 6134: coveting, prohibition of (id: 6134)

Coveting is forbidden by God and can lead to terrible consequences. It occasionally refers to a commendable earnest desiring.

Coveting is forbidden by God
----------------------------

### In the Ten Commandments

**[EXO 20:17](https://ref.ly/Exod20:17)** parallel [DEU 5:21](https://ref.ly/Deut5:21)**[ROM 7:7–7:8](https://ref.ly/Rom7:7-Rom7:8)** *Coveting is provoked by the law that forbids it.* *See also* [ROM 13:9–13:10](https://ref.ly/Rom13:9-Rom13:10)

### Through the prophets

[ISA 5:8](https://ref.ly/Isa5:8), [ISA 57:17](https://ref.ly/Isa57:17); ; [EZK 33:31](https://ref.ly/Ezek33:31); ; [AMO 2:6](https://ref.ly/Amos2:6), [AMO 5:11–5:12](https://ref.ly/Amos5:11-Amos5:12), [AMO 8:5–8:6](https://ref.ly/Amos8:5-Amos8:6); ; [MIC 2:2](https://ref.ly/Mic2:2)
