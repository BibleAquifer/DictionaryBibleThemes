## 8481: self-sacrifice (id: 8481)

The willingness to leave one's possessions or to give them to others; to deny oneself to the point of death or to give oneself in the service of God or other people.

Self\-sacrifice as forfeiting one's present comfort for the sake of others
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[RUT 2:11](https://ref.ly/Ruth2:11)** *See also* [GEN 44:33–44:34](https://ref.ly/Gen44:33-Gen44:34); [HEB 10:34](https://ref.ly/Heb10:34), [HEB 11:24–11:27](https://ref.ly/Heb11:24-Heb11:27)

Self\-sacrifice as giving one's possessions to others
-----------------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ commends the willingness to give away one's possessions

**[MAT 19:21](https://ref.ly/Matt19:21)** parallels [MRK 10:21](https://ref.ly/Mark10:21), [LUK 18:22](https://ref.ly/Luke18:22) *See also* [LUK 12:33](https://ref.ly/Luke12:33)

### God rewards those who give their possessions away

**[LUK 18:28–18:30](https://ref.ly/Luke18:28-Luke18:30)** parallels [MAT 19:27–19:29](https://ref.ly/Matt19:27-Matt19:29), [MRK 10:28–10:30](https://ref.ly/Mark10:28-Mark10:30)

### Sacrificing one's possessions as demonstrated in the life of the church

*See also* [ACT 2:44–2:45](https://ref.ly/Acts2:44-Acts2:45), [ACT 4:32–4:37](https://ref.ly/Acts4:32-Acts4:37); [2CO 8:1–8:5](https://ref.ly/2Cor8:1-2Cor8:5)

Self\-sacrifice as leaving all and following Jesus Christ
---------------------------------------------------------

### Such sacrifice is a condition of discipleship

**[LUK 14:33](https://ref.ly/Luke14:33)** *See also* [MAT 10:37](https://ref.ly/Matt10:37) parallel [LUK 14:26](https://ref.ly/Luke14:26) *This means loving Jesus Christ over and above one's immediate family.*

### Those willing to leave everything to follow Jesus Christ

[LUK 5:27–5:28](https://ref.ly/Luke5:27-Luke5:28); ; [PHP 3:8](https://ref.ly/Phil3:8)

Self\-sacrifice as the metaphorical laying down of one's life for others
------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Followers of Jesus Christ are required to give themselves in the service of others

**[JHN 12:24–12:25](https://ref.ly/John12:24-John12:25)** *See also* [MAT 10:38](https://ref.ly/Matt10:38) parallel [LUK 14:27](https://ref.ly/Luke14:27); [MAT 16:24–16:25](https://ref.ly/Matt16:24-Matt16:25) parallels [MRK 8:34–8:35](https://ref.ly/Mark8:34-Mark8:35), [LUK 9:23–9:24](https://ref.ly/Luke9:23-Luke9:24); [LUK 17:33](https://ref.ly/Luke17:33); [1JN 3:16–3:18](https://ref.ly/1John3:16-1John3:18)

### Examples of those who demonstrated such self\-sacrifice

**[2CO 4:10–4:11](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:10-2Cor4:11)** *See also* [1CO 9:19–9:23](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:19-1Cor9:23), [1CO 15:30–15:31](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:30-1Cor15:31); [2CO 11:23–11:29](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:23-2Cor11:29), [2CO 12:15](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:15)

Self\-sacrifice as the literal laying down of one's life
--------------------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ warns his disciples to be prepared to give their lives

**[MAT 10:21–10:22](https://ref.ly/Matt10:21-Matt10:22)** parallels [MRK 13:12–13:13](https://ref.ly/Mark13:12-Mark13:13), [LUK 21:16–21:17](https://ref.ly/Luke21:16-Luke21:17)

### Sacrificing one's life is of no value without love

*See also* [1CO 13:3](https://ref.ly/1Cor13:3)

### Examples of those willing to give their lives for the sake of God and others

**[ACT 20:23–20:24](https://ref.ly/Acts20:23-Acts20:24)** *See also* [EXO 32:32](https://ref.ly/Exod32:32); [1CH 11:15–11:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:15-1Chr11:19); [EST 4:14–4:16](https://ref.ly/Esth4:14-Esth4:16); [MAT 26:39](https://ref.ly/Matt26:39) parallels [MRK 14:35–14:36](https://ref.ly/Mark14:35-Mark14:36), [LUK 22:42](https://ref.ly/Luke22:42); [PHP 2:17](https://ref.ly/Phil2:17)

### Examples of those who gave their lives in God's service

**[ACT 7:59–7:60](https://ref.ly/Acts7:59-Acts7:60)** *See also* [JDG 11:36–11:38](https://ref.ly/Judg11:36-Judg11:38); [PHP 2:5–2:8](https://ref.ly/Phil2:5-Phil2:8); [HEB 11:36–11:38](https://ref.ly/Heb11:36-Heb11:38); [REV 20:4](https://ref.ly/Rev20:4)
