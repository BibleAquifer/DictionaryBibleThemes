## 8749: false teachers (id: 8749)

Those who teach error and in so doing lead others astray. They are to be distinguished from false prophets who are equally condemned in Scripture.

The OT prophets warned God's people against being led astray by false teachers
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[ISA 9:16](https://ref.ly/Isa9:16)**; **[EZK 34:2](https://ref.ly/Ezek34:2)** *See also* [ISA 3:12](https://ref.ly/Isa3:12), [ISA 8:20](https://ref.ly/Isa8:20); [JER 2:8](https://ref.ly/Jer2:8), [JER 10:21](https://ref.ly/Jer10:21), [JER 23:1](https://ref.ly/Jer23:1); [EZK 22:26](https://ref.ly/Ezek22:26); [MAL 2:7–2:8](https://ref.ly/Mal2:7-Mal2:8)

Jesus Christ condemned false teachers
-------------------------------------

### He opposed legalism and hypocrisy

**[MAT 5:19](https://ref.ly/Matt5:19)**

### He identified the teachers of the law and Pharisees as false teachers

**[MAT 23:1–23:33](https://ref.ly/Matt23:1-Matt23:33)** parallel [LUK 11:42–11:52](https://ref.ly/Luke11:42-Luke11:52) *See also* [MAT 15:14](https://ref.ly/Matt15:14)

### He warned his followers against them

**[MAT 16:6](https://ref.ly/Matt16:6), [MAT 16:12](https://ref.ly/Matt16:12)** parallels [MRK 8:15](https://ref.ly/Mark8:15), [LUK 12:1](https://ref.ly/Luke12:1)
