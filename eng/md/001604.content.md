## 6615: atonement, necessity and nature of (id: 6615)

Scripture stresses the seriousness and reality of human sin, and that human beings are unable to atone for their own sins. In his grace, God provides a means by which the situation can be remedied.

Atonement is necessary because of human sinfulness
--------------------------------------------------

### Atonement is necessary because sin cuts people off from God

**[ISA 59:2](https://ref.ly/Isa59:2)** *See also* [ISA 64:7](https://ref.ly/Isa64:7); [EZK 39:23](https://ref.ly/Ezek39:23); [HAB 1:13](https://ref.ly/Hab1:13); [JHN 9:31](https://ref.ly/John9:31)

### Atonement is necessary because sin provokes God's wrath

**[EPH 2:1–2:3](https://ref.ly/Eph2:1-Eph2:3)** *See also* [GEN 6:5–6:7](https://ref.ly/Gen6:5-Gen6:7); [EXO 32:30–32:35](https://ref.ly/Exod32:30-Exod32:35); [ROM 1:18–1:20](https://ref.ly/Rom1:18-Rom1:20), [ROM 2:8](https://ref.ly/Rom2:8)

God's gracious nature is the basis for atonement
------------------------------------------------

### Atonement is grounded in God's reluctance to punish sinners

**[EZK 18:32](https://ref.ly/Ezek18:32)** *See also* [EZK 33:11](https://ref.ly/Ezek33:11); [1TI 2:1–2:4](https://ref.ly/1Tim2:1-1Tim2:4); [2PE 3:9](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:9)

### Atonement is grounded in God's readiness to forgive sin

**[EXO 34:6–34:7](https://ref.ly/Exod34:6-Exod34:7)** *The punishment of subsequent generations makes God's abhorrence of sin quite clear, but the extension of his love and forgiveness “to thousands” puts the emphasis in these verses on God's grace and compassion.* *See also* [PSA 145:8](https://ref.ly/Ps145:8); [DAN 9:9](https://ref.ly/Dan9:9); [JON 4:2](https://ref.ly/Jonah4:2)

### Atonement is grounded in God's covenant love

**[NUM 14:19](https://ref.ly/Num14:19)** *The Hebrew word for “great love” means God's loving faithfulness to those within the covenant.* *See also* [PSA 25:6–25:7](https://ref.ly/Ps25:6-Ps25:7), [PSA 103:8–103:12](https://ref.ly/Ps103:8-Ps103:12); [JOL 2:13](https://ref.ly/Joel2:13)

God's provision of atonement is a means of dealing with sin
-----------------------------------------------------------
