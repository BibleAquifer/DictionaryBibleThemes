## 5368: kingship (id: 5368)

The sovereignty and rule of a king. Scripture stresses that all human authority derives ultimately from God, and that human kings are required to acknowledge and respond to this divine authority.

* **Associated Articles:** 5369: kingship, divine (ID: `5369@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 5370: kingship, human (ID: `5370@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
