## 8736: evil, warnings against (id: 8736)

Scripture repeatedly warns against evil. It exposes it and reveals its consequences.

God opposes evil
----------------

**[PSA 34:16](https://ref.ly/Ps34:16)** *See also* [PRO 6:16–6:19](https://ref.ly/Prov6:16-Prov6:19); [ISA 31:2](https://ref.ly/Isa31:2); [MIC 2:1](https://ref.ly/Mic2:1)

---------------------------------

**[LEV 20:23](https://ref.ly/Lev20:23)**; **[2CO 6:14](https://ref.ly/2Cor6:14)** *See also* [EZK 20:18](https://ref.ly/Ezek20:18); [LUK 12:1](https://ref.ly/Luke12:1), [LUK 12:15](https://ref.ly/Luke12:15); [ACT 13:40](https://ref.ly/Acts13:40); [1CO 10:21](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:21); [2PE 3:17](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:17)

God's law given to counter evil
-------------------------------

### By exposing evil

**[ROM 7:7](https://ref.ly/Rom7:7)** *See also* [ROM 3:20](https://ref.ly/Rom3:20), [ROM 5:20](https://ref.ly/Rom5:20), [ROM 7:13](https://ref.ly/Rom7:13); [GAL 3:19](https://ref.ly/Gal3:19)
