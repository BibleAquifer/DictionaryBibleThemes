## 5800: blasphemy (id: 5800)

The profaning, desecration and taking in vain of the name of God, or the reviling of any of his works or deeds. It is strongly forbidden by God as dishonouring to his name.

Blasphemy strongly forbidden
----------------------------

**[EXO 20:7](https://ref.ly/Exod20:7)** parallel [DEU 5:11](https://ref.ly/Deut5:11) *See also* [EXO 22:28](https://ref.ly/Exod22:28); [LEV 18:21](https://ref.ly/Lev18:21), [LEV 19:12](https://ref.ly/Lev19:12), [LEV 22:32](https://ref.ly/Lev22:32); [NUM 15:30–15:31](https://ref.ly/Num15:30-Num15:31)

------------------------------
