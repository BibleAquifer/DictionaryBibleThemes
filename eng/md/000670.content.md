## 5067: suicide (id: 5067)

The deliberate taking of one's own life, a rare occurrence in Scripture.

Psychological causes of suicide
-------------------------------

### A sense of guilt leads to suicide

**[MAT 27:5](https://ref.ly/Matt27:5)** *See also* [ACT 1:18–1:19](https://ref.ly/Acts1:18-Acts1:19)

### Defeat leads to suicide

**[1SA 31:4](https://ref.ly/1Sam31:4)** *See also* [1SA 31:5–31:6](https://ref.ly/1Sam31:5-1Sam31:6) parallel [1CH 10:4–10:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr10:4-1Chr10:5); [2SA 1:9](https://ref.ly/2Sam1:9), [2SA 17:23](https://ref.ly/2Sam17:23); [1KI 16:18](https://ref.ly/1Kgs16:18)

### Defiance leads to suicide

[JDG 16:26–16:30](https://ref.ly/Judg16:26-Judg16:30); ; [2SA 17:23](https://ref.ly/2Sam17:23)

### Failure leads to attempted suicide

[ACT 16:27](https://ref.ly/Acts16:27)

Desire for death is more frequently expressed in the Bible than actual suicide
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### The desire for death in a prophet's depression

**[1KI 19:3–19:4](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:3-1Kgs19:4)** *See also* [JER 15:10](https://ref.ly/Jer15:10), [JER 20:14–20:18](https://ref.ly/Jer20:14-Jer20:18); [JON 4:1–4:3](https://ref.ly/Jonah4:1-Jonah4:3) *A desire for death does not lead to taking of one's own life, but to entrusting the place and time of one's death to the Lord.*

### The desire for death arising from grief and pain

**[JOB 10:1](https://ref.ly/Job10:1)** *See also* [JOB 3:1–3:4](https://ref.ly/Job3:1-Job3:4), [JOB 7:15–7:16](https://ref.ly/Job7:15-Job7:16)

### The desire for death because of judgment

**[REV 9:6](https://ref.ly/Rev9:6)** *See also* [HOS 10:8](https://ref.ly/Hos10:8); [REV 6:16](https://ref.ly/Rev6:16)

Life hated because of its apparent futility
-------------------------------------------

**[ECC 2:17](https://ref.ly/Eccl2:17)** *See also* [PSA 90:8–90:10](https://ref.ly/Ps90:8-Ps90:10); [ECC 4:1–4:3](https://ref.ly/Eccl4:1-Eccl4:3)

Suicide usurps God's sovereignty over life
------------------------------------------

### God is Creator, and thus has authority over his creation

**[JOB 10:12](https://ref.ly/Job10:12)** *See also* [GEN 2:7](https://ref.ly/Gen2:7), [GEN 2:17](https://ref.ly/Gen2:17); [PSA 90:1–90:10](https://ref.ly/Ps90:1-Ps90:10), [PSA 139:13–139:15](https://ref.ly/Ps139:13-Ps139:15)

### God controls life and death

**[JOB 1:21](https://ref.ly/Job1:21)** *See also* [EXO 23:25–23:26](https://ref.ly/Exod23:25-Exod23:26); [DEU 32:39](https://ref.ly/Deut32:39); [JOB 30:23](https://ref.ly/Job30:23); [ECC 8:8](https://ref.ly/Eccl8:8); [LUK 12:22–12:25](https://ref.ly/Luke12:22-Luke12:25); [HEB 9:27](https://ref.ly/Heb9:27)

Taking life, including one's own, is sin
----------------------------------------

**[EXO 20:13](https://ref.ly/Exod20:13)**

### God's command that believers love themselves is violated by suicide

*See also* [LEV 19:18](https://ref.ly/Lev19:18); [MAT 22:39](https://ref.ly/Matt22:39); [EPH 5:29](https://ref.ly/Eph5:29)

### God's command that believers should love others is violated by suicide

[MAT 7:12](https://ref.ly/Matt7:12) *Relatives and friends are hurt when a loved one commits suicide.*
