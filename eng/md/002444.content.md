## 8825: self-righteousness, and the gospel (id: 8825)

Self\-righteousness is an obstacle to the gospel, which insists that true righteousness can only be found in Jesus Christ.

The real issue of righteousness
-------------------------------

**[JOB 9:2](https://ref.ly/Job9:2)** *See also* [1SA 6:20](https://ref.ly/1Sam6:20); [JOB 4:17](https://ref.ly/Job4:17), [JOB 15:14](https://ref.ly/Job15:14), [JOB 32:2](https://ref.ly/Job32:2) *Although Elihu had misjudged Job, his main point, that only God was righteous, was correct*; [JOB 40:8](https://ref.ly/Job40:8); [PSA 130:3](https://ref.ly/Ps130:3)

True righteousness is not the result of human effort
----------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 3:20](https://ref.ly/Rom3:20)** *Even though God's law tells people what God requires, human beings cannot keep it because of the weakness of their sinful human natures.* *See also* [ROM 9:31](https://ref.ly/Rom9:31); [GAL 2:16](https://ref.ly/Gal2:16), [GAL 3:11](https://ref.ly/Gal3:11); [EPH 2:8–2:9](https://ref.ly/Eph2:8-Eph2:9); [TIT 3:5](https://ref.ly/Titus3:5); [1JN 2:16](https://ref.ly/1John2:16)

Self\-righteousness is a contradiction in terms
-----------------------------------------------

**[2CH 6:36](https://ref.ly/2Chr6:36)**; **[ISA 64:6](https://ref.ly/Isa64:6)** *See also* [EZR 9:15](https://ref.ly/Ezra9:15); [PSA 14:3](https://ref.ly/Ps14:3); [ECC 7:20](https://ref.ly/Eccl7:20); [DAN 9:7](https://ref.ly/Dan9:7); [ROM 3:10](https://ref.ly/Rom3:10), [ROM 3:23](https://ref.ly/Rom3:23)

God calls people from their pretended self\-righteousness
