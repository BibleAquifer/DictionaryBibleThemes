## 5083: Adam, and Jesus Christ (id: 5083)

Paul presents Jesus Christ as both the last Adam, succeeding where the first Adam failed, and the second man, standing at the head of a new humanity.

The contrast between the first and second Adam
----------------------------------------------

**[ROM 5:14](https://ref.ly/Rom5:14)** *By his sin Adam brought universal misery on humanity. In this he is the foreshadower and prototype of Jesus Christ, who through his righteous act brings the universal hope of redemption.*

The contrast in nature of the first Adam and the second Adam
------------------------------------------------------------

### Adam was a living being; Jesus Christ is a life\-giving being

**[1CO 15:45](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:45)**

### Adam was natural, Jesus Christ is spiritual

**[1CO 15:46](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:46)**

### Adam was earthly, Jesus Christ is heavenly

**[1CO 15:47](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:47)**

The contrast in the consequences of the actions of the first Adam and the second Adam
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Adam brought death, Jesus Christ brings life

### Adam brought judgment, Jesus Christ brings justification

**[ROM 5:16](https://ref.ly/Rom5:16), [ROM 5:18–5:19](https://ref.ly/Rom5:18-Rom5:19)**
