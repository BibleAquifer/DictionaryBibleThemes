## 5176: ring (id: 5176)

An ornament or a status symbol, sometimes used in sacred furnishings or as a charm.

Rings worn as jewellery
-----------------------

### Gold nose rings, worn especially by women

These were often given by a suitor as a symbol of betrothal: [GEN 24:22](https://ref.ly/Gen24:22), [GEN 24:30](https://ref.ly/Gen24:30), [GEN 24:47](https://ref.ly/Gen24:47); ; [PRO 11:22](https://ref.ly/Prov11:22); ; [ISA 3:21](https://ref.ly/Isa3:21); ; [EZK 16:12](https://ref.ly/Ezek16:12)

### The signet ring as a sign of authority

[GEN 41:42](https://ref.ly/Gen41:42); ; [NUM 31:50](https://ref.ly/Num31:50); ; [EST 3:10](https://ref.ly/Esth3:10), [EST 3:12](https://ref.ly/Esth3:12), [EST 8:8](https://ref.ly/Esth8:8); ; [DAN 6:17](https://ref.ly/Dan6:17); [JER 22:24](https://ref.ly/Jer22:24); ; [HAG 2:23](https://ref.ly/Hag2:23)

### Gold ear\-rings, worn by men and women

Ear\-rings were sometimes used as charms or amulets: [GEN 35:4](https://ref.ly/Gen35:4); ; [EXO 32:2–32:3](https://ref.ly/Exod32:2-Exod32:3), [EXO 35:22](https://ref.ly/Exod35:22); ; [JDG 8:24–8:25](https://ref.ly/Judg8:24-Judg8:25)
