## 4121: Satan, as the enemy of God (id: 4121)

Scripture provides numerous examples of ways in which Satan opposes the presence and purposes of God in his world.

----------------------

**[1CH 21:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr21:1)** *See also* [JOB 2:1–2:7](https://ref.ly/Job2:1-Job2:7); [ZEC 3:1–3:2](https://ref.ly/Zech3:1-Zech3:2); [1TH 2:18](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:18)

**[REV 12:10](https://ref.ly/Rev12:10)** *See also* [JOB 1:8–1:11](https://ref.ly/Job1:8-Job1:11); [ZEC 3:1–3:2](https://ref.ly/Zech3:1-Zech3:2)

Satan's role as adversary earns him varied titles
-------------------------------------------------

### The devil

He is the adversary because he deceives: [REV 12:9](https://ref.ly/Rev12:9), [REV 20:2–20:3](https://ref.ly/Rev20:2-Rev20:3)

### Belial

He is the adversary because he is the troublemaker or scoundrel (the same Hebrew word is used in the OT passages as in [2Co 6:15](https://ref.ly/2Cor6:15) to describe Satan's work): [DEU 13:13](https://ref.ly/Deut13:13); ; [1KI 21:10](https://ref.ly/1Kgs21:10); ; [PRO 6:12](https://ref.ly/Prov6:12); ; [2CO 6:15](https://ref.ly/2Cor6:15)

### The dragon

He is the adversary because he is the enemy: [REV 12:4](https://ref.ly/Rev12:4), [REV 12:13–12:14](https://ref.ly/Rev12:13-Rev12:14)

### The serpent

He is the adversary because he thwarts spiritual insight: [GEN 3:1–3:5](https://ref.ly/Gen3:1-Gen3:5), [GEN 3:13](https://ref.ly/Gen3:13); ; [2CO 11:3](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:3)

### The prince of this world

He is the adversary because his rule is temporary, not absolute: [JHN 12:31](https://ref.ly/John12:31), [JHN 14:30](https://ref.ly/John14:30), [JHN 16:11](https://ref.ly/John16:11)

### The angel of the Abyss

[REV 9:11](https://ref.ly/Rev9:11) *He is the adversary because he is the destroyer.*
