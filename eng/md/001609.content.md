## 6624: confession, of sin (id: 6624)

An admission of sin on the part of individuals or groups of people. Genuine repentance and confession herald divine forgiveness.

Confession of sin leads to divine forgiveness
---------------------------------------------

### Under the old covenant

**[PRO 28:13](https://ref.ly/Prov28:13)** *See also* [LEV 16:21](https://ref.ly/Lev16:21) *Part of the ritual of the Day of Atonement involved the high priest confessing the sins of the nation, while laying his hands on the head of a sacrificial goat*; [LEV 26:40–26:42](https://ref.ly/Lev26:40-Lev26:42); [1KI 8:33–8:36](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:33-1Kgs8:36) parallel [2CH 6:24–6:27](https://ref.ly/2Chr6:24-2Chr6:27); [1KI 8:46–8:52](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:46-1Kgs8:52) parallel [2CH 6:36–6:39](https://ref.ly/2Chr6:36-2Chr6:39); [PSA 32:3–32:5](https://ref.ly/Ps32:3-Ps32:5)
