## 5778: adorning (id: 5778)

A form of ornamentation, using especially jewels and precious metals, to enhance the beauty of buildings or people.

Buildings were adorned with various materials
---------------------------------------------

**[2CH 3:6](https://ref.ly/2Chr3:6)** *See also* [PSA 45:8](https://ref.ly/Ps45:8); [ISA 60:13](https://ref.ly/Isa60:13); [AMO 3:15](https://ref.ly/Amos3:15)

-------------------------------

**[HOS 10:1](https://ref.ly/Hos10:1)** *See also* [JER 10:3–10:4](https://ref.ly/Jer10:3-Jer10:4)
