## 5219: authority, of human institutions (id: 5219)

The exercise of God\-established freedom to regulate society for the good of its members.

Without authority sin is unrestrained
-------------------------------------

**[ROM 1:24–1:26](https://ref.ly/Rom1:24-Rom1:26)** *See also* [GEN 6:5](https://ref.ly/Gen6:5) *This was before the giving of the law*; [JDG 21:25](https://ref.ly/Judg21:25); [ROM 13:4](https://ref.ly/Rom13:4)

All human authorities are instituted and established by God
-----------------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 13:1–13:2](https://ref.ly/Rom13:1-Rom13:2)** *See also* [GEN 9:5–9:6](https://ref.ly/Gen9:5-Gen9:6); [DAN 2:21](https://ref.ly/Dan2:21), [DAN 2:37–2:38](https://ref.ly/Dan2:37-Dan2:38), [DAN 4:17](https://ref.ly/Dan4:17), [DAN 5:18–5:21](https://ref.ly/Dan5:18-Dan5:21), [DAN 5:26](https://ref.ly/Dan5:26); [JHN 19:11](https://ref.ly/John19:11); [1PE 2:13](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:13)
