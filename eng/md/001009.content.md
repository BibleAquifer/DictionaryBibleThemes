## 5430: oaths, human (id: 5430)

Solemn, binding statements made in God's name. The OT forbids the making of oaths in the name of other gods and the NT suggests that it is best to refrain from making oaths.

Oaths are considered binding
----------------------------

**[NUM 30:2](https://ref.ly/Num30:2)** *See also* [DEU 23:21](https://ref.ly/Deut23:21); [MAT 23:16–23:22](https://ref.ly/Matt23:16-Matt23:22)

Oaths to be taken only in God's name
------------------------------------

**[DEU 6:13](https://ref.ly/Deut6:13)** *See also* [PSA 24:3–24:4](https://ref.ly/Ps24:3-Ps24:4); [JER 12:16](https://ref.ly/Jer12:16)

False oaths forbidden
---------------------

**[LEV 19:12](https://ref.ly/Lev19:12)** *See also* [EXO 20:7](https://ref.ly/Exod20:7) parallel [DEU 5:11](https://ref.ly/Deut5:11); [LEV 6:3–6:5](https://ref.ly/Lev6:3-Lev6:5); [ZEC 5:3–5:4](https://ref.ly/Zech5:3-Zech5:4)
