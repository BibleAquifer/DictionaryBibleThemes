## 9513: hell, as incentive to action (id: 9513)

The reality of hell should affect the way people live.

The importance of making the right choice
-----------------------------------------

**[MAT 7:13–7:14](https://ref.ly/Matt7:13-Matt7:14)** *See also* [DEU 30:19](https://ref.ly/Deut30:19); [JER 21:8](https://ref.ly/Jer21:8); [LUK 13:23–13:25](https://ref.ly/Luke13:23-Luke13:25)

A personal spiritual life is vital
----------------------------------

### It is more important than short\-term worldly gain

**[MAT 16:26](https://ref.ly/Matt16:26)** parallels [MRK 8:36](https://ref.ly/Mark8:36), [LUK 9:25](https://ref.ly/Luke9:25) *See also* [LUK 12:20](https://ref.ly/Luke12:20), [LUK 16:19–16:25](https://ref.ly/Luke16:19-Luke16:25)

### It is not just outward appearance

**[MAT 3:7–3:10](https://ref.ly/Matt3:7-Matt3:10)** parallel [LUK 3:7–3:9](https://ref.ly/Luke3:7-Luke3:9)

### Religious activity alone is not only valueless but perilous

**[MAT 7:21–7:23](https://ref.ly/Matt7:21-Matt7:23)** *See also* [PRO 14:12](https://ref.ly/Prov14:12); [JER 23:11–23:12](https://ref.ly/Jer23:11-Jer23:12); [LUK 13:26–13:28](https://ref.ly/Luke13:26-Luke13:28); [JHN 15:6](https://ref.ly/John15:6)

Believers must maintain their loyalty to God
--------------------------------------------

**[MAT 10:28](https://ref.ly/Matt10:28)** parallel [LUK 12:4–12:5](https://ref.ly/Luke12:4-Luke12:5) *See also* [DEU 4:23–4:24](https://ref.ly/Deut4:23-Deut4:24); [ISA 8:12–8:13](https://ref.ly/Isa8:12-Isa8:13)

Temptations to sin must be dealt with drastically
-------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 5:29–5:30](https://ref.ly/Matt5:29-Matt5:30)** *See also* [MAT 18:8–18:9](https://ref.ly/Matt18:8-Matt18:9); [MRK 9:43–9:48](https://ref.ly/Mark9:43-Mark9:48)

Some attitudes and practices that lead to hell
----------------------------------------------

### Deliberately continuing in sin

**[HEB 10:26–10:27](https://ref.ly/Heb10:26-Heb10:27)** *See also* [NUM 15:30](https://ref.ly/Num15:30); [HEB 6:4–6:6](https://ref.ly/Heb6:4-Heb6:6); [2PE 2:20](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:20)

### Lack of spiritual response

[MAT 22:13](https://ref.ly/Matt22:13), [MAT 24:51](https://ref.ly/Matt24:51), [MAT 25:30](https://ref.ly/Matt25:30)

### Wilfully ignoring divine activity

**[MAT 11:20–11:24](https://ref.ly/Matt11:20-Matt11:24)** parallel [LUK 10:13–10:15](https://ref.ly/Luke10:13-Luke10:15) *Sodom, destroyed by God, was notoriously sinful.* *See also* [MAT 23:33](https://ref.ly/Matt23:33), [MAT 25:41–25:46](https://ref.ly/Matt25:41-Matt25:46)

### Contempt for fellow humans

**[MAT 5:22](https://ref.ly/Matt5:22)** *The term “fool” implied the despising of another as worthless and godless.* *See also* [JAS 3:6](https://ref.ly/Jas3:6)
