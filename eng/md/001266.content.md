## 5727: old age, attitudes to (id: 5727)

Old age is generally held in honour and the aged in society are to be respected. Because of the wisdom associated with their advancing years elders had an important role of authority and guidance in Israelite society and the NT speaks of the leadership of elders within the church.

The role and status of elders
-----------------------------

### Elders of non\-Israelite nations

[GEN 50:7](https://ref.ly/Gen50:7); ; [NUM 22:7](https://ref.ly/Num22:7)

### Elders in Israel

Israel's elders were probably the heads of families, qualified to lead by virtue of age and experience: [EXO 3:16–3:18](https://ref.ly/Exod3:16-Exod3:18), [EXO 4:29–4:31](https://ref.ly/Exod4:29-Exod4:31), [EXO 19:7](https://ref.ly/Exod19:7); [NUM 11:25](https://ref.ly/Num11:25) *The number of elders is reduced to 70 who are anointed by the Spirit for leadership alongside Moses.*; [DEU 19:11–19:12](https://ref.ly/Deut19:11-Deut19:12), [DEU 22:15–22:18](https://ref.ly/Deut22:15-Deut22:18), [DEU 25:7](https://ref.ly/Deut25:7); [2SA 5:3](https://ref.ly/2Sam5:3) *Israel's elders accept David as king*; ; [MAT 26:57](https://ref.ly/Matt26:57) parallel [MRK 14:53](https://ref.ly/Mark14:53) *Jewish elders were among those who tried Jesus Christ. By NT times they had become involved with religious as well as civil affairs and their title related more to their role than to age.*

### God as one \`\`advanced in years''

**[DAN 7:9](https://ref.ly/Dan7:9)** *This description of God as the “Ancient of Days” implies that he possesses the respectability, authority and wisdom to judge that accompanies age. It also points to his eternity.* *See also* [DAN 7:13](https://ref.ly/Dan7:13), [DAN 7:22](https://ref.ly/Dan7:22); [REV 1:14](https://ref.ly/Rev1:14)
