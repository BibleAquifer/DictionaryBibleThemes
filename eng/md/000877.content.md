## 5289: debt (id: 5289)

God is the Maker and sustainer of all things and therefore all creatures owe everything to him. Scripture provides guidance concerning both debt and debtors.

-------------------------------------

**[1CO 4:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor4:7)** *See also* [DEU 8:17–8:18](https://ref.ly/Deut8:17-Deut8:18); [1CH 29:11–29:13](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:11-1Chr29:13); [PSA 24:1–24:2](https://ref.ly/Ps24:1-Ps24:2); [ECC 5:19](https://ref.ly/Eccl5:19); [ACT 14:17](https://ref.ly/Acts14:17), [ACT 17:25](https://ref.ly/Acts17:25); [1TI 6:17](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:17)
