## 9155: millennium (id: 9155)

The period of 1,000 years referred to in Revelation chapter 20, during which Satan is bound and believers reign with Jesus Christ. This millennium is understood in different ways: as a period of unparalleled peace and prosperity for the church and the gospel at the end of this age, after which Christ will return (postmillennialism); as the time following Christ's return when he will reign on earth (premillennialism); as a symbolic reference to the period between Christ's first and second comings (amillennialism).

Characteristics of the millennium
---------------------------------

### Satan is bound

**[REV 20:1–20:3](https://ref.ly/Rev20:1-Rev20:3)** *“the Abyss” is the subterranean place of confinement for Satan and evil spirits.* *See also* [LUK 8:31](https://ref.ly/Luke8:31) parallels [MAT 8:29](https://ref.ly/Matt8:29), [MRK 5:7](https://ref.ly/Mark5:7)

### Martyred believers are raised to reign with Jesus Christ

**[REV 20:4–20:6](https://ref.ly/Rev20:4-Rev20:6)** *Those who have suffered for the gospel receive their promised reward.* *See also* [ROM 8:17](https://ref.ly/Rom8:17); [2TI 2:11–2:12](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:11-2Tim2:12); [REV 2:10](https://ref.ly/Rev2:10)

### Satan's release and final overthrow

**[REV 20:7–20:10](https://ref.ly/Rev20:7-Rev20:10)** *See also* [EZK 38:1–38:4](https://ref.ly/Ezek38:1-Ezek38:4), [EZK 38:15–38:16](https://ref.ly/Ezek38:15-Ezek38:16), [EZK 38:18–38:22](https://ref.ly/Ezek38:18-Ezek38:22), [EZK 39:2–39:5](https://ref.ly/Ezek39:2-Ezek39:5); [ZEC 14:2](https://ref.ly/Zech14:2); [ROM 16:20](https://ref.ly/Rom16:20)

The millennium will be a golden age on earth
--------------------------------------------

**[ISA 2:2–2:4](https://ref.ly/Isa2:2-Isa2:4)** parallel [MIC 4:1–4:3](https://ref.ly/Mic4:1-Mic4:3) *The OT looks forward to a period of universal peace and prosperity on earth. Both pre\- and postmillennialists see this as a reference to Jesus Christ's future earthly reign.* *See also* [ISA 11:6–11:9](https://ref.ly/Isa11:6-Isa11:9), [ISA 51:4–51:5](https://ref.ly/Isa51:4-Isa51:5), [ISA 65:25](https://ref.ly/Isa65:25)

The premillennial interpretation
--------------------------------

### The premillennial order of events

[REV 19:11–19:13](https://ref.ly/Rev19:11-Rev19:13) *Jesus Christ's return. Premillennialists understand the events of Revelation chapter 19 to precede those of Revelation chapter 20*; ; [REV 20:2–20:3](https://ref.ly/Rev20:2-Rev20:3) *Satan bound for 1,000 years*; ; [REV 20:4–20:6](https://ref.ly/Rev20:4-Rev20:6) *a “first resurrection” of believers to share Jesus Christ's reign over the earth*; ; [REV 20:7–20:10](https://ref.ly/Rev20:7-Rev20:10) *Satan's release and final overthrow*; ; [REV 20:11–20:15](https://ref.ly/Rev20:11-Rev20:15) *the general resurrection of all people to face God's judgment*

### A first resurrection of believers

**[1CO 15:22–15:25](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:22-1Cor15:25)** *This passage allows the idea of a gap between the resurrection of believers and the eternal age.* *See also* [1TH 4:16–4:17](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:16-1Thess4:17)

### God's future earthly reign

**[ZEC 14:9](https://ref.ly/Zech14:9)** *See also* [DAN 2:44](https://ref.ly/Dan2:44); [ZEP 3:11](https://ref.ly/Zeph3:11); [ZEC 14:16–14:21](https://ref.ly/Zech14:16-Zech14:21)

### Believers will reign with Jesus Christ

**[MAT 19:28](https://ref.ly/Matt19:28)** *Premillennialists take this to refer to an earthly reign.* *See also* [LUK 22:29–22:30](https://ref.ly/Luke22:29-Luke22:30); [1CO 6:2](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:2); [REV 5:9–5:10](https://ref.ly/Rev5:9-Rev5:10)

### Difficulties with premillennialism

Scriptures that depict a single resurrection of both the righteous and the wicked: [DAN 12:2](https://ref.ly/Dan12:2); ; [JHN 5:28–5:29](https://ref.ly/John5:28-John5:29); [MAT 13:40–13:43](https://ref.ly/Matt13:40-Matt13:43), [MAT 13:49–13:50](https://ref.ly/Matt13:49-Matt13:50), [MAT 25:31–25:32](https://ref.ly/Matt25:31-Matt25:32)

The postmillennial interpretation
---------------------------------

### The rapid spread of the gospel

**[MAT 24:14](https://ref.ly/Matt24:14)** parallel [MRK 13:10](https://ref.ly/Mark13:10) *See also* [MAT 13:31–13:33](https://ref.ly/Matt13:31-Matt13:33) parallels [MRK 4:31–4:32](https://ref.ly/Mark4:31-Mark4:32), [LUK 13:18–13:21](https://ref.ly/Luke13:18-Luke13:21)

### A future turning to God

[PSA 22:27–22:28](https://ref.ly/Ps22:27-Ps22:28); ; [ISA 19:19–19:24](https://ref.ly/Isa19:19-Isa19:24); ; [ZEC 2:11](https://ref.ly/Zech2:11)

### Difficulties with postmillennialism

The fortunes of the gospel and the church appear to be at a low ebb before Jesus Christ returns: [MAT 24:9–24:12](https://ref.ly/Matt24:9-Matt24:12) parallel [LUK 21:12–21:17](https://ref.ly/Luke21:12-Luke21:17); ; [2TI 3:1–3:5](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:1-2Tim3:5), [2TI 3:12–3:13](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:12-2Tim3:13); [MAT 28:18](https://ref.ly/Matt28:18); ; [ACT 2:33–2:36](https://ref.ly/Acts2:33-Acts2:36), [ACT 7:55–7:56](https://ref.ly/Acts7:55-Acts7:56)

The amillennial interpretation
------------------------------

### Satan was defeated at Jesus Christ's first coming

**[JHN 12:31](https://ref.ly/John12:31)** *See also* [MAT 12:29](https://ref.ly/Matt12:29) parallels [MRK 3:27](https://ref.ly/Mark3:27), [LUK 11:21–11:22](https://ref.ly/Luke11:21-Luke11:22); [LUK 10:18–10:20](https://ref.ly/Luke10:18-Luke10:20); [COL 2:14–2:15](https://ref.ly/Col2:14-Col2:15)

### Interpreting the \`\`first resurrection''

As a reference to the soul entering heaven after death: [LUK 23:43](https://ref.ly/Luke23:43); ; [2TI 4:7–4:8](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:7-2Tim4:8); [ROM 6:5](https://ref.ly/Rom6:5); ; [EPH 2:6](https://ref.ly/Eph2:6); ; [COL 3:1](https://ref.ly/Col3:1)

### Dangers of the amillennialist view

The future hope of believers is not only a spiritual reality: [ROM 8:19–8:21](https://ref.ly/Rom8:19-Rom8:21); ; [REV 21:1](https://ref.ly/Rev21:1); [1PE 5:8](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:8); ; [1JN 5:19](https://ref.ly/1John5:19); ; [REV 12:17](https://ref.ly/Rev12:17)
