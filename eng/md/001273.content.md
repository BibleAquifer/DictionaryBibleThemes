## 5735: sexuality (id: 5735)

Men and women are intended to complement one another and to take pleasure in one another. God has provided for the full and glad expression of this in marriage.

Sexuality comes from God
------------------------

### It was created by God

**[GEN 1:27](https://ref.ly/Gen1:27)**

### God intended men and women to complement one another

**[1CO 11:11](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:11)** *See also* [GEN 2:18–2:22](https://ref.ly/Gen2:18-Gen2:22)

Sexual differences
------------------

### In strength

[1PE 3:7](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:7)

### In role

[1CO 11:3–11:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:3-1Cor11:5), [1CO 14:34–14:35](https://ref.ly/1Cor14:34-1Cor14:35); ; [EPH 5:22–5:25](https://ref.ly/Eph5:22-Eph5:25); ; [COL 3:18–3:19](https://ref.ly/Col3:18-Col3:19); ; [1TI 2:12–2:14](https://ref.ly/1Tim2:12-1Tim2:14); ; [1PE 3:1](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:1)

### In dress

**[DEU 22:5](https://ref.ly/Deut22:5)** *See also* [1CO 11:14–11:15](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:14-1Cor11:15)

Sexual activity
---------------

### It was ordained by God

**[GEN 1:28](https://ref.ly/Gen1:28)** *This command was made before the fall, and no guilt attaches to it.*

### Marriage in its proper context

**[GEN 2:23–2:24](https://ref.ly/Gen2:23-Gen2:24)** *“one flesh” implies both bodily union and an inseparable intimate relationship.* *See also* [MAT 19:4–19:6](https://ref.ly/Matt19:4-Matt19:6) parallel [MRK 10:6–10:9](https://ref.ly/Mark10:6-Mark10:9); [1CO 7:2–7:4](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:2-1Cor7:4); [EPH 5:31–5:33](https://ref.ly/Eph5:31-Eph5:33)

### Sexual desire

**[SNG 4:3–4:15](https://ref.ly/Song4:3-Song4:15)** *“Song of Songs” celebrates the beauty of sex in a loving context. It contains much delicate and intimate imagery that applies directly to human lovers and can also be applied to Jesus Christ and the church.* *See also* [GEN 3:16](https://ref.ly/Gen3:16); [SNG 1:2–1:4](https://ref.ly/Song1:2-Song1:4), [SNG 4:16](https://ref.ly/Song4:16), [SNG 7:11–7:13](https://ref.ly/Song7:11-Song7:13), [SNG 8:10](https://ref.ly/Song8:10)

### Abstinence is possible

[MAT 19:12](https://ref.ly/Matt19:12); ; [1CO 7:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:1), [1CO 7:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:5)
