## 6616: atonement, in OT (id: 6616)

The OT laid down complex regulations by which the guilt of sin could be removed through the sacrificial system. Particular emphasis was placed upon the role of the high priest, who was required to make annual atonement for the sins of the people.

The covenantal framework of atonement
-------------------------------------

### As God's covenant partners, the Israelites undertook to keep his laws

**[EXO 24:3](https://ref.ly/Exod24:3)** *See also* [DEU 26:17](https://ref.ly/Deut26:17); [JOS 24:24](https://ref.ly/Josh24:24)

### The sin offering made atonement for unintentional sins under the covenant

**[LEV 9:7](https://ref.ly/Lev9:7)** *See also* [LEV 4:13–4:14](https://ref.ly/Lev4:13-Lev4:14); [NUM 15:22–15:26](https://ref.ly/Num15:22-Num15:26)

### The guilt offering atoned for sins where reparation was required

**[LEV 19:20–19:22](https://ref.ly/Lev19:20-Lev19:22)** *See also* [LEV 6:1–6:7](https://ref.ly/Lev6:1-Lev6:7)

### Deliberate flouting of God's law could not be atoned for

**[NUM 15:30–15:31](https://ref.ly/Num15:30-Num15:31)** *See also* [NUM 35:33](https://ref.ly/Num35:33); [1SA 3:14](https://ref.ly/1Sam3:14)

### The Day of Atonement provided for the removal of the nation's sin

**[LEV 16:34](https://ref.ly/Lev16:34)** *See also* [EXO 30:10](https://ref.ly/Exod30:10); [LEV 16:1–16:33](https://ref.ly/Lev16:1-Lev16:33) *The nation's sin was atoned for by sprinkling the atonement cover in the Most Holy Place with the blood of the sacrificial goat; the removal of the people's sin was symbolised by the driving of the scapegoat into the wilderness*; [HEB 9:7](https://ref.ly/Heb9:7)

### The atonement cover

**[EXO 25:17–25:22](https://ref.ly/Exod25:17-Exod25:22)** *The locating of God's presence above the atonement cover in the tabernacle demonstrated that it was only on the basis of atonement that God could accompany his people.* *See also* [EXO 30:6](https://ref.ly/Exod30:6); [LEV 16:2](https://ref.ly/Lev16:2); [NUM 7:89](https://ref.ly/Num7:89)

### Atonement was effected by the blood of the sacrifice

**[LEV 17:11](https://ref.ly/Lev17:11)** *The blood signified that the life of the animal had been given in place of that of the worshipper.* *See also* [HEB 9:22](https://ref.ly/Heb9:22)

The role of priests in making atonement
---------------------------------------

### Priests were dedicated to God in order to make atonement for others

**[EXO 29:44](https://ref.ly/Exod29:44)** *See also* [LEV 8:22–8:30](https://ref.ly/Lev8:22-Lev8:30)

### The priests had to make atonement for their own sins

**[HEB 5:1–5:3](https://ref.ly/Heb5:1-Heb5:3)** *See also* [LEV 9:8–9:11](https://ref.ly/Lev9:8-Lev9:11)

### The priests represented the people before God to atone for their sin

**[HEB 5:1](https://ref.ly/Heb5:1)** *See also* [EXO 28:36–28:38](https://ref.ly/Exod28:36-Exod28:38); [LEV 10:16–10:17](https://ref.ly/Lev10:16-Lev10:17)
