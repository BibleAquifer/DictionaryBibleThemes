## 7155: saints (id: 7155)

The people of God, especially in relation to their being set apart from the world to serve him. Believers are called upon to lead holy lives worthy of their calling.

Saints are set apart for God
----------------------------

**[1CO 1:2](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:2)** *To be holy is to be set apart. The same Greek word is used in the NT for both “holy” and, in the plural, “saints”.* *See also* [DEU 7:6](https://ref.ly/Deut7:6), [DEU 26:19](https://ref.ly/Deut26:19); [PSA 4:3](https://ref.ly/Ps4:3); [ROM 1:7](https://ref.ly/Rom1:7); [HEB 10:10](https://ref.ly/Heb10:10)
