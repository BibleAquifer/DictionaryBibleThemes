## 5629: work, as ordained by God (id: 5629)

God ordained work as the normal routine of living. Every legitimate human task, therefore, is of intrinsic worth, however menial it may seem, and is potentially a means of glorifying God.

Work is ordained by God
-----------------------

**[GEN 1:27–1:28](https://ref.ly/Gen1:27-Gen1:28)** *See also* [EXO 20:9](https://ref.ly/Exod20:9) parallel [DEU 5:13](https://ref.ly/Deut5:13); [PSA 104:23](https://ref.ly/Ps104:23) *Work is part of the rhythm of life.*

God's purposes in ordaining work
--------------------------------

### That people should be self\-supporting

**[GEN 3:19](https://ref.ly/Gen3:19)** *See also* [PSA 128:2](https://ref.ly/Ps128:2); [1TH 4:12](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:12)

### That people should find self\-fulfilment

**[ECC 2:24](https://ref.ly/Eccl2:24)** *See also* [PRO 14:23](https://ref.ly/Prov14:23); [ECC 3:22](https://ref.ly/Eccl3:22), [ECC 5:19](https://ref.ly/Eccl5:19)

### That people should serve others

**[EPH 4:28](https://ref.ly/Eph4:28)** *See also* [PRO 31:15](https://ref.ly/Prov31:15); [1TH 2:9](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:9); [1TI 5:8](https://ref.ly/1Tim5:8)

### That people should glorify God

**[COL 3:17](https://ref.ly/Col3:17)** *See also* [1CO 10:31](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:31); [EPH 6:5–6:8](https://ref.ly/Eph6:5-Eph6:8) parallel [COL 3:22–3:24](https://ref.ly/Col3:22-Col3:24)

Consequences of viewing work as God's ordinance
-----------------------------------------------

### Work is seen as a moral duty

**[TIT 3:14](https://ref.ly/Titus3:14)** *See also* [PRO 6:6](https://ref.ly/Prov6:6); [ECC 9:10](https://ref.ly/Eccl9:10); [1TH 4:11](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:11); [2TH 3:7–3:12](https://ref.ly/2Thess3:7-2Thess3:12)

### Any legitimate work may be seen as God's calling

**[GEN 2:15](https://ref.ly/Gen2:15)** *See also* [EXO 31:1–31:6](https://ref.ly/Exod31:1-Exod31:6), [EXO 35:30–35:35](https://ref.ly/Exod35:30-Exod35:35); [PSA 78:70–78:71](https://ref.ly/Ps78:70-Ps78:71); [MAT 13:55](https://ref.ly/Matt13:55) parallel [MRK 6:3](https://ref.ly/Mark6:3); [ROM 13:6](https://ref.ly/Rom13:6); [1CO 7:17](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:17), [1CO 7:20–7:24](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:20-1Cor7:24)

### Work is seen as a stewardship from God himself

**[COL 3:23–3:24](https://ref.ly/Col3:23-Col3:24)** *See also* [MAT 25:14–25:30](https://ref.ly/Matt25:14-Matt25:30) parallel [LUK 19:12–19:27](https://ref.ly/Luke19:12-Luke19:27); [EPH 6:5–6:8](https://ref.ly/Eph6:5-Eph6:8)
