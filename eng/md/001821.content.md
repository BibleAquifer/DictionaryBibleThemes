## 7410: phylactery (id: 7410)

A leather box containing four sections of the law ([Ex 11:13–21](https://ref.ly/Exod11:13-Exod11:21)) written on parchment, and which Jewish men tied to the forehead and left arm (close to the heart) before morning prayers. The practice of wearing phylacteries arose, probably after the exile, from a literal interpretation by pious Jews of passages such as [Ex 13:9](https://ref.ly/Exod13:9).

The significance of wearing phylacteries
----------------------------------------

### A reminder of God's deliverance

**[EXO 13:11–13:16](https://ref.ly/Exod13:11-Exod13:16)** *See also* [EXO 13:1–13:10](https://ref.ly/Exod13:1-Exod13:10)

### A reminder of God's requirements

**[DEU 11:13–11:21](https://ref.ly/Deut11:13-Deut11:21)** *Phylacteries, when worn, were to be an outward sign of the importance of taking God's word into the heart*; **[PSA 119:11](https://ref.ly/Ps119:11)**; **[PRO 3:1–3:3](https://ref.ly/Prov3:1-Prov3:3)** *See also* [DEU 6:4–6:9](https://ref.ly/Deut6:4-Deut6:9), [DEU 30:14](https://ref.ly/Deut30:14); [ROM 10:8](https://ref.ly/Rom10:8); [DEU 32:46](https://ref.ly/Deut32:46); [PSA 37:31](https://ref.ly/Ps37:31), [PSA 40:8](https://ref.ly/Ps40:8); [PRO 6:21](https://ref.ly/Prov6:21), [PRO 7:2–7:3](https://ref.ly/Prov7:2-Prov7:3); [JER 31:33](https://ref.ly/Jer31:33)

### A sign of allegiance and belonging

**[ISA 44:5](https://ref.ly/Isa44:5)**; **[REV 7:3](https://ref.ly/Rev7:3)** *See also* [SNG 8:6](https://ref.ly/Song8:6); [REV 9:4](https://ref.ly/Rev9:4), [REV 14:1](https://ref.ly/Rev14:1)

The practice of wearing phylacteries
------------------------------------

**[MAT 23:5](https://ref.ly/Matt23:5)** *Jesus Christ condemns the Pharisees for wearing phylacteries for outward show.*
