## 3130: Holy Spirit, as Counsellor (id: 3130)

The Holy Spirit is the one who comforts, advises and strengthens Christians, drawing them closer to Jesus Christ.

The Holy Spirit is Counsellor in addition to Jesus Christ
---------------------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ is a Counsellor

**[1JN 2:1](https://ref.ly/1John2:1)** *One Greek word “Parakletos” underlies this phrase which is primarily a legal term. It can also bear the meaning “comforter”.*

### The Spirit is another Counsellor

**[JHN 14:16–14:17](https://ref.ly/John14:16-John14:17)** *The word “another” means “another of the same kind”. After the ascension, the Spirit is to assume the ministry of Jesus Christ.*

### The Counsellor is the gift of the exalted Christ

**[JHN 16:7](https://ref.ly/John16:7)** *See also* [JHN 7:38–7:39](https://ref.ly/John7:38-John7:39); [ACT 2:33](https://ref.ly/Acts2:33)

The Counsellor comforts and reassures believers
-----------------------------------------------

[JHN 14:16–14:18](https://ref.ly/John14:16-John14:18); ; [ACT 11:12](https://ref.ly/Acts11:12) *Peter is reassured about going to the house of Cornelius*; ; [ROM 8:16](https://ref.ly/Rom8:16) *Believers are reassured that they are God's children.*

The Counsellor strengthens and equips the church
------------------------------------------------

**[EPH 3:16](https://ref.ly/Eph3:16)** *See also* [ACT 4:31](https://ref.ly/Acts4:31) *The Jerusalem church is strengthened in the face of opposition*; [ACT 9:31](https://ref.ly/Acts9:31) *the church in Judea, Galilee and Samaria*; [ROM 8:26](https://ref.ly/Rom8:26)

The Counsellor teaches and instructs believers
----------------------------------------------

### The Spirit reminds the disciples of Jesus Christ's teaching

**[JHN 14:26](https://ref.ly/John14:26)** *See also* [JHN 16:14](https://ref.ly/John16:14); [1JN 5:6–5:8](https://ref.ly/1John5:6-1John5:8)

### The Spirit teaches the church further truth

**[JHN 16:13](https://ref.ly/John16:13)** *See also* [1CO 2:9–2:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:9-1Cor2:10); [1JN 2:27](https://ref.ly/1John2:27)

The Counsellor helps the church in its mission
----------------------------------------------

### The Spirit testifies to Jesus Christ and helps the church to do likewise

**[JHN 15:26–15:27](https://ref.ly/John15:26-John15:27)** *See also* [ACT 1:8](https://ref.ly/Acts1:8)

### The Spirit convicts the unbelieving world

[JHN 16:8–16:11](https://ref.ly/John16:8-John16:11); ; [1CO 14:24–14:25](https://ref.ly/1Cor14:24-1Cor14:25)

### Examples of the Spirit's help in witness and mission

[ACT 4:8](https://ref.ly/Acts4:8) *Peter before the Sanhedrin*; ; [1CO 2:3–2:4](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:3-1Cor2:4) *Paul in Corinth*; ; [1TH 1:5](https://ref.ly/1Thess1:5) *Paul and Silas in Thessalonica*

### The Counsellor draws believers closer to Jesus Christ

**[EPH 3:16–3:17](https://ref.ly/Eph3:16-Eph3:17)** *See also* [JHN 14:23](https://ref.ly/John14:23); [ROM 8:9–8:11](https://ref.ly/Rom8:9-Rom8:11)
