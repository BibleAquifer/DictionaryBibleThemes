## 5897: judging others (id: 5897)

God's people are to exercise fairness and impartiality when called upon to exercise judgment in legal or church matters. However, the temptation to pass judgment upon the life or testimony of other individuals is clearly warned against.

Judging others in a legal situation
-----------------------------------

### Fairness and impartiality are required by God

**[DEU 16:18–16:20](https://ref.ly/Deut16:18-Deut16:20)** *See also* [EXO 23:6–23:7](https://ref.ly/Exod23:6-Exod23:7); [LEV 19:15](https://ref.ly/Lev19:15); [DEU 1:16–1:17](https://ref.ly/Deut1:16-Deut1:17); [PRO 24:23](https://ref.ly/Prov24:23); [EZK 18:5–18:8](https://ref.ly/Ezek18:5-Ezek18:8)

### Judges are appointed by God

**[1KI 3:28](https://ref.ly/1Kgs3:28)** *See also* [EXO 18:13–18:16](https://ref.ly/Exod18:13-Exod18:16); [1SA 7:15](https://ref.ly/1Sam7:15), [1SA 12:1–12:4](https://ref.ly/1Sam12:1-1Sam12:4)

Jesus Christ refuses to judge others
------------------------------------

**[JHN 8:3–8:11](https://ref.ly/John8:3-John8:11)** *See also* [LUK 12:13–12:14](https://ref.ly/Luke12:13-Luke12:14); [JHN 7:24](https://ref.ly/John7:24), [JHN 8:15–8:16](https://ref.ly/John8:15-John8:16)

Judging others in the church
----------------------------

### Judgmental attitudes condemned

**[MAT 7:1–7:5](https://ref.ly/Matt7:1-Matt7:5)** parallel [LUK 6:37–6:42](https://ref.ly/Luke6:37-Luke6:42) *See also* [ROM 2:1–2:3](https://ref.ly/Rom2:1-Rom2:3), [ROM 14:1–14:13](https://ref.ly/Rom14:1-Rom14:13); [COL 2:16](https://ref.ly/Col2:16); [JAS 2:1–2:4](https://ref.ly/Jas2:1-Jas2:4), [JAS 4:11–4:12](https://ref.ly/Jas4:11-Jas4:12)

### A discerning attitude commended

**[1CO 5:12–5:13](https://ref.ly/1Cor5:12-1Cor5:13)** *See also* [1CO 5:1–5:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor5:1-1Cor5:5), [1CO 6:1–6:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:1-1Cor6:6)

### Evaluation of ministries commended
