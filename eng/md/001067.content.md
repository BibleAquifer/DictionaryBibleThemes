## 5496: revenge, examples of (id: 5496)

Ungodly people are often vengeful, and even Christians are not always exemplary in this respect. However, each case should be considered on its merits, and the claims of justice taken into account.

Revenge among the ungodly
-------------------------

**[LUK 6:11](https://ref.ly/Luke6:11)** parallels [MAT 12:14](https://ref.ly/Matt12:14), [MRK 3:6](https://ref.ly/Mark3:6) *See also* [GEN 4:24](https://ref.ly/Gen4:24) *Compare Lamech's words with those of Jesus Christ in [Mt 18:21–22](https://ref.ly/Matt18:21-Matt18:22)*; [1KI 19:2](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:2) *Jezebel*; [1KI 22:27](https://ref.ly/1Kgs22:27) *Ahab*; [EST 3:6](https://ref.ly/Esth3:6) *Haman*; [EZK 25:15](https://ref.ly/Ezek25:15) *the Philistines*[MRK 6:19](https://ref.ly/Mark6:19), [MRK 6:24](https://ref.ly/Mark6:24)[ACT 5:33](https://ref.ly/Acts5:33) *the Sanhedrin*; [ACT 23:12](https://ref.ly/Acts23:12) *unbelieving Jews*
