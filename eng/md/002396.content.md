## 8769: idolatry, in OT (id: 8769)

Scripture provides illustrations of idolatry from various periods in the history of the people of God. Idolatry is seen as a constant temptation for believers, especially in times of national or personal stress.

Idolatry among the Gentiles
---------------------------

[JDG 11:24](https://ref.ly/Judg11:24), [JDG 16:23–16:24](https://ref.ly/Judg16:23-Judg16:24); ; [2KI 5:18](https://ref.ly/2Kgs5:18); ; [ISA 36:18–36:20](https://ref.ly/Isa36:18-Isa36:20), [ISA 37:38](https://ref.ly/Isa37:38), [ISA 46:1](https://ref.ly/Isa46:1); ; [EZK 8:14](https://ref.ly/Ezek8:14); ; [ACT 14:11–14:13](https://ref.ly/Acts14:11-Acts14:13); ; [1CO 8:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:5)
