## 5554: status (id: 5554)

A person's standing in society in the sight of others. The status of individuals in the sight of the world often differs radically from their status in the sight of God, but it is the latter that really matters.

Factors contributing to high social status
------------------------------------------

### High status associated with a position of secular authority

**[DAN 2:37–2:38](https://ref.ly/Dan2:37-Dan2:38)** *See also* [2SA 15:1–15:6](https://ref.ly/2Sam15:1-2Sam15:6); [1CH 29:26–29:28](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:26-1Chr29:28)

### High status based upon a position of domestic authority

**[MAL 1:6](https://ref.ly/Mal1:6)** *See also* [PSA 123:2](https://ref.ly/Ps123:2); [LUK 17:7–17:10](https://ref.ly/Luke17:7-Luke17:10); [1CO 11:3](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:3)
