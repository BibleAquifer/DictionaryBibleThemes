## 7406: Passover (id: 7406)

One of the major OT feasts. It specifically commemorates the exodus from Egypt as an act of God's deliverance. In Israel's calendar of feasts it was always celebrated with the Feast of Unleavened Bread.

The institution of the Passover
-------------------------------

**[EXO 12:25–12:27](https://ref.ly/Exod12:25-Exod12:27)** *The Hebrew term “Passover” derives from a verb meaning “to pass over” with the sense of “to spare”.*

### It was celebrated by Jesus Christ

**[LUK 22:15](https://ref.ly/Luke22:15)** *At Passover time Jesus Christ himself was sacrificed and he inaugurated the Lord's Supper.* *See also* [MAT 26:17–26:19](https://ref.ly/Matt26:17-Matt26:19) parallels [MRK 14:12–14:16](https://ref.ly/Mark14:12-Mark14:16), [LUK 22:7–22:13](https://ref.ly/Luke22:7-Luke22:13); [JHN 2:23](https://ref.ly/John2:23) *Jesus Christ observed the Passover like other Jews.*
