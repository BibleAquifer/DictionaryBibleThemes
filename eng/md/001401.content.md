## 5883: impatience (id: 5883)

A refusal to wait for people or developments, frequently displaying a lack of faith.

Examples of impatience
----------------------

[GEN 16:1–16:2](https://ref.ly/Gen16:1-Gen16:2) *Abraham and Sarah who did not wait for God to give them the son he had promised*; ; [GEN 25:30](https://ref.ly/Gen25:30) *Esau's impatience cost him his birthright*; ; [NUM 20:9–20:11](https://ref.ly/Num20:9-Num20:11) *Moses*; [NUM 21:4](https://ref.ly/Num21:4); ; [PSA 106:13](https://ref.ly/Ps106:13); ; [ISA 59:7](https://ref.ly/Isa59:7); [1SA 13:6–13:12](https://ref.ly/1Sam13:6-1Sam13:12) *Saul made the offering without waiting for Samuel*; ; [2SA 13:2](https://ref.ly/2Sam13:2) *Amnon with his half\-sister, Tamar*; ; [2SA 14:29](https://ref.ly/2Sam14:29) *Absalom with Joab*; ; [2SA 18:14](https://ref.ly/2Sam18:14) *Joab with Absalom*; ; [2KI 5:11–5:12](https://ref.ly/2Kgs5:11-2Kgs5:12) *Naaman*; ; [2KI 6:33](https://ref.ly/2Kgs6:33) *King Joram*; [JOB 6:11](https://ref.ly/Job6:11), [JOB 21:4](https://ref.ly/Job21:4); [JOB 32:16](https://ref.ly/Job32:16) *Elihu*; ; [MAT 18:29–18:30](https://ref.ly/Matt18:29-Matt18:30) *the unforgiving debtor*; ; [LUK 15:2](https://ref.ly/Luke15:2) *The younger son could not wait for his inheritance*; ; [JHN 21:3](https://ref.ly/John21:3) *Peter*; ; [ACT 20:16](https://ref.ly/Acts20:16) *Paul*; ; [1CO 11:20–11:21](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:20-1Cor11:21) *the Corinthian Christians at their communal meal*; ; [REV 6:10–6:11](https://ref.ly/Rev6:10-Rev6:11) *the martyrs waiting for the Lord to avenge their deaths*
