## 7386: incense (id: 7386)

An expensive resin from southern Arabia and the fragrant aroma it emits when offered in worship ritual. It is also called frankincense.

Regulations governing the offering of incense
---------------------------------------------

### The priest was to keep incense burning on the altar daily

**[2CH 13:11](https://ref.ly/2Chr13:11)** *See also* [EXO 30:7–30:9](https://ref.ly/Exod30:7-Exod30:9); [NUM 4:16](https://ref.ly/Num4:16); [DEU 33:10](https://ref.ly/Deut33:10); [1SA 2:28](https://ref.ly/1Sam2:28); [1CH 6:49](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:49), [1CH 9:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:29); [LUK 1:9](https://ref.ly/Luke1:9)
