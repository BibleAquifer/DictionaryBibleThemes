## 6040: sinners (id: 6040)

All who have sinned. The term tends to be used in particular of those regarded as outside God's covenant of grace, whose sins are therefore not forgiven.

The universality of sinful behaviour
------------------------------------

### Only Jesus Christ is sinless

**[1PE 2:22](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:22)** *See also* [ISA 53:9](https://ref.ly/Isa53:9); [2CO 5:21](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:21); [HEB 4:15](https://ref.ly/Heb4:15), [HEB 7:26](https://ref.ly/Heb7:26); [1JN 3:5](https://ref.ly/1John3:5)

### Everyone else has sinned

**[GAL 3:22](https://ref.ly/Gal3:22)** *See also* [1KI 8:46](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:46); [ROM 3:9–3:19](https://ref.ly/Rom3:9-Rom3:19), [ROM 3:23](https://ref.ly/Rom3:23), [ROM 5:12](https://ref.ly/Rom5:12), [ROM 5:19](https://ref.ly/Rom5:19)
