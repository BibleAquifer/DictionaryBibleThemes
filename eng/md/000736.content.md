## 5147: deafness (id: 5147)

The condition of being wholly, or partly, without hearing. It can be expressed in physical or spiritual terms and is often used to depict spiritual rebellion.

God's control of deafness
-------------------------

**[EXO 4:11](https://ref.ly/Exod4:11)** *See also* [PSA 94:9](https://ref.ly/Ps94:9); [PRO 20:12](https://ref.ly/Prov20:12); [LUK 1:20](https://ref.ly/Luke1:20), [LUK 1:62](https://ref.ly/Luke1:62)
