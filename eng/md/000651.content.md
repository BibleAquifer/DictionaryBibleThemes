## 5043: names, significance of (id: 5043)

Labels that idenitfy individuals. They may also denote aspects of character or reputation.

A name may reflect an individual's nature
-----------------------------------------

**[1SA 25:25](https://ref.ly/1Sam25:25)** *See also* [GEN 17:5](https://ref.ly/Gen17:5) *“Abraham” means “father of many”*; [GEN 32:27–32:29](https://ref.ly/Gen32:27-Gen32:29) *“Israel” means “he struggles with God”*; [EXO 34:14](https://ref.ly/Exod34:14); [MRK 5:9](https://ref.ly/Mark5:9) parallel [LUK 8:30](https://ref.ly/Luke8:30)

### Additional names or nicknames

**[MRK 3:17](https://ref.ly/Mark3:17)** *In NT times an additional name or surname reflecting someone's character was sometimes appended to the given name.* *See also* [LUK 6:15](https://ref.ly/Luke6:15); [JHN 1:42](https://ref.ly/John1:42) *“Cephas” and “Peter” mean “rock” in Aramaic and Greek respectively*; [ACT 4:36](https://ref.ly/Acts4:36)

A name may indicate an individual's reputation and standing in the community
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

### A good name indicates a good reputation

**[PRO 22:1](https://ref.ly/Prov22:1)** *See also* [PRO 3:4](https://ref.ly/Prov3:4), [PRO 10:7](https://ref.ly/Prov10:7); [ECC 7:1](https://ref.ly/Eccl7:1); [SNG 1:3](https://ref.ly/Song1:3)

### A bad name indicates a bad reputation

**[NEH 6:13](https://ref.ly/Neh6:13)** *See also* [DEU 22:13–22:19](https://ref.ly/Deut22:13-Deut22:19); [PRO 25:9–25:10](https://ref.ly/Prov25:9-Prov25:10)

Survival of one's name is important
-----------------------------------

**[RUT 4:9–4:10](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:9-Ruth4:10)** *See also* [GEN 48:15–48:16](https://ref.ly/Gen48:15-Gen48:16); [NUM 27:1–27:4](https://ref.ly/Num27:1-Num27:4); [DEU 25:7–25:9](https://ref.ly/Deut25:7-Deut25:9); [2SA 18:18](https://ref.ly/2Sam18:18); [JOB 18:17–18:19](https://ref.ly/Job18:17-Job18:19)

### Preservation of a name indicates divine blessing

**[ISA 56:4–56:5](https://ref.ly/Isa56:4-Isa56:5)** *See also* [GEN 12:1–12:3](https://ref.ly/Gen12:1-Gen12:3); [2SA 7:9](https://ref.ly/2Sam7:9) parallel [1CH 17:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr17:8); [1KI 1:47](https://ref.ly/1Kgs1:47); [ISA 66:22](https://ref.ly/Isa66:22)

-----------------------------------------------

**[JER 11:19](https://ref.ly/Jer11:19)** *See also* [DEU 7:24](https://ref.ly/Deut7:24), [DEU 9:14](https://ref.ly/Deut9:14); [2SA 14:7](https://ref.ly/2Sam14:7); [2KI 14:27](https://ref.ly/2Kgs14:27)

Obliteration of a name is often linked with divine judgment
-----------------------------------------------------------
