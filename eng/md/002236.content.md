## 8450: martyrdom (id: 8450)

The term “martyr” comes from the Greek word for “witness”, reminding believers that the supreme act of witness for the gospel is to give up one's life for its sake.

Jesus Christ as martyr
----------------------

### Jesus Christ had to suffer and die

**[MRK 8:31](https://ref.ly/Mark8:31)** *In that Jesus Christ gives up his life, he may be thought of as a martyr. But the NT stresses that he is far more than this. Through his death alone come all the benefits of salvation, in which all believers can share.* *See also* [ISA 53:10](https://ref.ly/Isa53:10)

### Jesus Christ's life is given as a ransom for believers

**[MAT 20:28](https://ref.ly/Matt20:28)** parallel [MRK 10:45](https://ref.ly/Mark10:45) *See also* [LUK 9:22](https://ref.ly/Luke9:22); [JHN 10:11](https://ref.ly/John10:11); [ACT 2:23](https://ref.ly/Acts2:23)
