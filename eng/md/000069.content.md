## 1346: covenants, nature of (id: 1346)

A solemn agreement or promise, sometimes confirmed by sacrifice or by sharing in a meal, by which two or more parties commit themselves to the rights and responsibilities demanded by their relationship and their agreed course of action, and accept the serious consequences of breaking faith.

Sealing a covenant
------------------

### By sharing a meal

[GEN 26:30](https://ref.ly/Gen26:30), [GEN 31:53–31:54](https://ref.ly/Gen31:53-Gen31:54); [MAT 26:26–26:29](https://ref.ly/Matt26:26-Matt26:29) parallels [MRK 14:22–14:25](https://ref.ly/Mark14:22-Mark14:25), [LUK 22:17–22:20](https://ref.ly/Luke22:17-Luke22:20); ; [1CO 11:23–11:25](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:23-1Cor11:25)

### By offering a sacrifice

[JER 34:18–34:19](https://ref.ly/Jer34:18-Jer34:19) *The Hebrew expression for making a covenant means, literally, “to cut a covenant”. This may refer to the solemn practice, described here, of cutting an animal in half and walking between its pieces*; ; [GEN 15:9–15:18](https://ref.ly/Gen15:9-Gen15:18); ; [EXO 24:4–24:8](https://ref.ly/Exod24:4-Exod24:8); ; [PSA 50:5](https://ref.ly/Ps50:5); ; [HEB 12:24](https://ref.ly/Heb12:24) *Sacrifice is an important part in sealing covenants made by God.*
