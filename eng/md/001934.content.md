## 7775: prophets, lives of (id: 7775)

God called individuals from differing backgrounds to speak as prophets on his behalf, sometimes in difficult circumstances.

The backgrounds of the prophets
-------------------------------

[EXO 2:1–2:10](https://ref.ly/Exod2:1-Exod2:10) *Moses was brought up in the Egyptian royal court*; ; [1SA 1:21–1:28](https://ref.ly/1Sam1:21-1Sam1:28) *Samuel was a temple servant at Shiloh*; ; [1KI 19:19–19:21](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:19-1Kgs19:21) *Elisha was a farmer*; ; [EZK 1:3](https://ref.ly/Ezek1:3) *Ezekiel was a priest living in exile in Babylonia.*; [AMO 1:1](https://ref.ly/Amos1:1), [AMO 7:14](https://ref.ly/Amos7:14)
