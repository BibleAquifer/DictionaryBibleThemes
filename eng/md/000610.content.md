## 4970: seasons, of the year (id: 4970)

Distinctive periods of the year marked by certain climatic conditions. Seasons are examples of God's perfect order in the world. They represent the balanced cycle of creation which is sustained by God and through which his provision and mercy are made known.

Seasons are appointed by God
----------------------------

### Seasons are created by God

**[PSA 74:16–74:17](https://ref.ly/Ps74:16-Ps74:17)** *See also* [GEN 1:14](https://ref.ly/Gen1:14), [GEN 8:22](https://ref.ly/Gen8:22); [PSA 104:19](https://ref.ly/Ps104:19)

### Seasons are sustained by God

**[JER 5:24](https://ref.ly/Jer5:24)** *See also* [JOB 38:32–38:33](https://ref.ly/Job38:32-Job38:33); [EZK 34:26](https://ref.ly/Ezek34:26); [ACT 14:17](https://ref.ly/Acts14:17)

The agricultural seasons of Palestine
-------------------------------------

### The dry and rainy seasons

**[JOL 2:23](https://ref.ly/Joel2:23)** *The rainy season, accounting for over 90 per cent of the national rainfall, runs from October to March/April. Its beginning is marked by the autumn rains in October/November; the lighter spring showers in April/May announce the coming of the dry season.* *See also* [EZR 10:13](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:13); [HOS 6:3](https://ref.ly/Hos6:3); [JAS 5:7](https://ref.ly/Jas5:7)LEV [26:3–26:4](https://ref.ly/Jas26:3-Jas26:4); [DEU 11:13–11:14](https://ref.ly/Deut11:13-Deut11:14), [DEU 28:12](https://ref.ly/Deut28:12)

### Seedtime and harvest

**[PRO 20:4](https://ref.ly/Prov20:4)** *Ploughing and sowing take place from October to December when the autumn rains have begun to soften the ground. Most crops are harvested between April and June.* *See also* [EXO 34:21](https://ref.ly/Exod34:21); [NUM 13:20](https://ref.ly/Num13:20); [JOB 5:26](https://ref.ly/Job5:26); [PSA 1:3](https://ref.ly/Ps1:3)

------

### Spring as a time of growth

**[SNG 2:11–2:13](https://ref.ly/Song2:11-Song2:13)** *Spring marks a pleasant interval between the heavy winter rains and the onset of the dry season.* *See also* [PRO 27:25](https://ref.ly/Prov27:25); [JOL 2:22](https://ref.ly/Joel2:22); [MAT 24:32](https://ref.ly/Matt24:32) parallels [MRK 13:28](https://ref.ly/Mark13:28), [LUK 21:29–21:30](https://ref.ly/Luke21:29-Luke21:30)
