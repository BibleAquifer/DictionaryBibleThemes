## 5636: work, and rest (id: 5636)

In creation, God has established a pattern of work and rest that is to be a model for believers.

Work and rest built into creation
---------------------------------

**[EXO 20:11](https://ref.ly/Exod20:11)** *See also* [GEN 2:1–2:3](https://ref.ly/Gen2:1-Gen2:3); [PSA 104:19–104:23](https://ref.ly/Ps104:19-Ps104:23)
