## 8628: worship, hindrances to (id: 8628)

True worship goes beyond mere form and can therefore be hindered by a wrong relationship to God or to others.

Worship that is merely formal is unacceptable
---------------------------------------------

**[1SA 15:22](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:22)** *See also* [ISA 1:13](https://ref.ly/Isa1:13); [EZK 33:31](https://ref.ly/Ezek33:31); [HOS 6:6](https://ref.ly/Hos6:6); [MAT 6:5](https://ref.ly/Matt6:5); [2TI 3:5](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:5)
