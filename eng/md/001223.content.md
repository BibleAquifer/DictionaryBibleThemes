## 5677: divorce, amongst believers (id: 5677)

Jesus Christ challenged the liberal approach to divorce taken by some rabbis of his day, and upheld the idea of marriage as a lifelong commitment. Remarriage after divorce is generally classed as adultery and, where possible, divorced parties should seek reconciliation. There are circumstances, however, where remarriage is permissible.

The liberal attitude to divorce
-------------------------------

**[MAT 19:3](https://ref.ly/Matt19:3)** *See also* [DEU 21:14](https://ref.ly/Deut21:14), [DEU 24:1](https://ref.ly/Deut24:1)

The undesirability of divorce
-----------------------------

### God's plan for marriage

**[MAT 19:4–19:6](https://ref.ly/Matt19:4-Matt19:6)** parallel [MRK 10:6–10:9](https://ref.ly/Mark10:6-Mark10:9) *See also* [GEN 1:27](https://ref.ly/Gen1:27), [GEN 2:24](https://ref.ly/Gen2:24); [MAL 2:15](https://ref.ly/Mal2:15); [EPH 5:31](https://ref.ly/Eph5:31)

### Divorce is permitted because of human sinfulness

**[MAT 19:8](https://ref.ly/Matt19:8)** parallel [MRK 10:5](https://ref.ly/Mark10:5)

### Christians should not seek divorce

**[1CO 7:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:10)** *This command, echoing Jesus Christ's words, applies where both parties are believers.* *See also* [1CO 7:12–7:14](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:12-1Cor7:14)

Remarriage after divorce
------------------------

### Believers should seek reconciliation

**[1CO 7:11](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:11)** *See also* [DEU 24:2–24:4](https://ref.ly/Deut24:2-Deut24:4) *Marriage to another after divorce prevents reconciliation.*

### Remarriage after divorce is classed as adultery

**[LUK 16:18](https://ref.ly/Luke16:18)** parallel [MRK 10:11–10:12](https://ref.ly/Mark10:11-Mark10:12) *This is a general rule, though there may be exceptions (see below).* *See also* [ROM 7:2–7:3](https://ref.ly/Rom7:2-Rom7:3); [1CO 7:39](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:39)

### Remarriage may be permissible

[1CO 7:27–7:28](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:27-1Cor7:28) *“unmarried” means literally “freed from a wife” and thus may be applied to those widowed or properly divorced. It is no sin for them to (re)marry.*

### Possible grounds for divorce and remarriage

**[MAT 19:9](https://ref.ly/Matt19:9)**; **[1CO 7:15](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:15)** *See also* [JER 3:8](https://ref.ly/Jer3:8) *God divorced Israel because of her adultery*; [MAT 1:18–1:19](https://ref.ly/Matt1:18-Matt1:19), [MAT 5:32](https://ref.ly/Matt5:32)
