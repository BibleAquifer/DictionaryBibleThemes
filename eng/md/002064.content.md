## 8220: courage, in facing enemies (id: 8220)

In the OT, courage is shown principally in the leading of God's people against their enemies.

God commands his people to be courageous
----------------------------------------

**[JOS 1:6–1:9](https://ref.ly/Josh1:6-Josh1:9)** *The LORD to Joshua as he was about to lead the Israelites into Canaan.* *See also* [JOS 1:18](https://ref.ly/Josh1:18), [JOS 8:1](https://ref.ly/Josh8:1), [JOS 10:8](https://ref.ly/Josh10:8); [JDG 7:9–7:11](https://ref.ly/Judg7:9-Judg7:11); [2CH 20:15](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:15), [2CH 20:17](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:17)
