## 8775: libertinism (id: 8775)

A form of abuse of Christian freedom which refuses to acknowledge any obligation for Christians to obey the law or to restrain sin.

Grace must not lead to libertinism
----------------------------------

**[ROM 6:1–6:2](https://ref.ly/Rom6:1-Rom6:2), [ROM 6:15](https://ref.ly/Rom6:15)** *See also* [ROM 3:5–3:8](https://ref.ly/Rom3:5-Rom3:8); [GAL 2:17–2:21](https://ref.ly/Gal2:17-Gal2:21)

Believers must resist sin
-------------------------

**[ROM 6:12](https://ref.ly/Rom6:12), [ROM 6:14](https://ref.ly/Rom6:14)** *See also* [HEB 12:1–12:2](https://ref.ly/Heb12:1-Heb12:2); [1JN 5:16–5:18](https://ref.ly/1John5:16-1John5:18)
