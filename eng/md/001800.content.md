## 7370: guilt offering (id: 7370)

An offering that resembled the sin offering, but was offered especially in cases where restitution could be made for an unintentional sin.

Circumstances for making the guilt offering
-------------------------------------------

### Unintentional sin against God

**[LEV 5:17](https://ref.ly/Lev5:17)** *See also* [LEV 5:15](https://ref.ly/Lev5:15)

### Sin against a neighbour where restitution can be made

[LEV 5:16](https://ref.ly/Lev5:16) *Where restitution is possible it is a requirement*; ; [LEV 6:1–6:5](https://ref.ly/Lev6:1-Lev6:5) *A surcharge of twenty per cent was to be added*; ; [NUM 5:5–5:8](https://ref.ly/Num5:5-Num5:8) *Restitution must be made to God if the wronged person is not accessible.*

The nature of the guilt offering
--------------------------------

### A ram without defect

**[LEV 5:18](https://ref.ly/Lev5:18)**

### Regulations governing the offering

*See also* [LEV 7:1–7:6](https://ref.ly/Lev7:1-Lev7:6); [NUM 18:9](https://ref.ly/Num18:9)

Other situations requiring a guilt offering
-------------------------------------------

[LEV 14:12–14:14](https://ref.ly/Lev14:12-Lev14:14) *cleansing from infectious diseases*; ; [LEV 19:20–19:22](https://ref.ly/Lev19:20-Lev19:22) *wronging a man by intercourse with a slave girl promised to him*; ; [NUM 6:12](https://ref.ly/Num6:12) *cleansing a Nazirite after contact with a dead body*; ; [EZR 10:19](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:19) *for returning exiles who had intermarried*
