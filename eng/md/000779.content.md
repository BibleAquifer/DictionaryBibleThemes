## 5190: thigh (id: 5190)

The human thigh
---------------

### A symbol of strength

[JOB 40:17](https://ref.ly/Job40:17); ; [DAN 2:32](https://ref.ly/Dan2:32)

### Weapons strapped to the thigh

[JDG 3:16](https://ref.ly/Judg3:16), [JDG 3:21](https://ref.ly/Judg3:21)

### The hand is placed under the thigh when taking an oath

[GEN 24:2](https://ref.ly/Gen24:2), [GEN 24:9](https://ref.ly/Gen24:9), [GEN 47:29](https://ref.ly/Gen47:29)

### The priests' undergarments covered the thigh

**[EXO 28:42–28:43](https://ref.ly/Exod28:42-Exod28:43)** *See also* [EXO 20:26](https://ref.ly/Exod20:26); [LEV 6:10](https://ref.ly/Lev6:10), [LEV 16:3–16:4](https://ref.ly/Lev16:3-Lev16:4); [EZK 44:17–44:18](https://ref.ly/Ezek44:17-Ezek44:18)

### The wasting of the thigh

The wasting away of the thigh is part of the judgment on an unfaithful wife: [NUM 5:21–5:22](https://ref.ly/Num5:21-Num5:22), [NUM 5:27](https://ref.ly/Num5:27)

### The thigh as a place of identification

[REV 19:16](https://ref.ly/Rev19:16)
