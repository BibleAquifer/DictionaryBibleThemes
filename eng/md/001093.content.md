## 5524: servants, bad (id: 5524)

Bad servants are characterised by disobedience, idleness and dishonesty.

Characteristics of bad servants
-------------------------------

### Idleness

**[2TH 3:11](https://ref.ly/2Thess3:11)** *See also* [PRO 18:9](https://ref.ly/Prov18:9); [MAT 25:24–25:30](https://ref.ly/Matt25:24-Matt25:30) parallel [LUK 19:20–19:26](https://ref.ly/Luke19:20-Luke19:26)

### Dishonesty

**[LUK 16:12](https://ref.ly/Luke16:12)** *See also* [GEN 21:25](https://ref.ly/Gen21:25); [LEV 6:2](https://ref.ly/Lev6:2); [2KI 5:20–5:25](https://ref.ly/2Kgs5:20-2Kgs5:25); [EPH 6:6](https://ref.ly/Eph6:6); [COL 3:22](https://ref.ly/Col3:22)
