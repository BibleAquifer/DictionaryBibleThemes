## 5378: law, OT (id: 5378)

OT laws and legal traditions govern every aspect of the life of the covenant people of God.

Kinds of OT law
---------------

### Criminal law

**[EXO 21:12–21:14](https://ref.ly/Exod21:12-Exod21:14)** *Criminal law deals with crimes against persons punishable by a court.*

### Civil law

**[DEU 16:18–16:20](https://ref.ly/Deut16:18-Deut16:20)** *Civil law deals with the upholding in court of legally established rights against possible abuse.* *See also* [DEU 15:12–15:18](https://ref.ly/Deut15:12-Deut15:18)

### Social law

**[EXO 22:21–22:22](https://ref.ly/Exod22:21-Exod22:22)** *Social law deals with behaviour or standards of behaviour which are not strictly enforceable in the courts.* *See also* [DEU 24:19–24:22](https://ref.ly/Deut24:19-Deut24:22)

### Cultic law

Cultic law deals explicitly with the ritual or religious life of the people of God. Leviticus chapters 1–7 are totally devoted to this kind of law: [LEV 1:10–1:13](https://ref.ly/Lev1:10-Lev1:13), [LEV 4:13–4:21](https://ref.ly/Lev4:13-Lev4:21), [LEV 7:11–7:18](https://ref.ly/Lev7:11-Lev7:18)
