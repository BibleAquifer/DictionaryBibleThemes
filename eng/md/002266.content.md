## 8484: spiritual warfare, enemies in (id: 8484)

Christians are at war with the world, the flesh and the devil. Satan empowers the world to attack believers externally, persecuting, deceiving and seducing them. Internally, sinfulness frustrates the efforts of believers to serve God fully.

The world is an enemy
---------------------

**[JAS 4:4](https://ref.ly/Jas4:4)** *“world” here refers to the sinful human race, hostile to the truth and to the people of God.* *See also* [ROM 12:2](https://ref.ly/Rom12:2); [1JN 2:15–2:17](https://ref.ly/1John2:15-1John2:17), [1JN 5:4](https://ref.ly/1John5:4)

### The world hates Christians

**[JHN 15:19–15:20](https://ref.ly/John15:19-John15:20)** *See also* [MAT 5:10–5:12](https://ref.ly/Matt5:10-Matt5:12) parallel [LUK 6:22](https://ref.ly/Luke6:22); [JHN 16:2](https://ref.ly/John16:2), [JHN 17:14](https://ref.ly/John17:14); [1PE 4:12–4:16](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:12-1Pet4:16); [1JN 3:13](https://ref.ly/1John3:13); [REV 13:7](https://ref.ly/Rev13:7) *The beast may represent the world's antichristian persecution.*

### The world has its own false teaching and religion

**[COL 2:8](https://ref.ly/Col2:8)** *See also* [1TI 4:1](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:1); [2TI 4:3–4:4](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:3-2Tim4:4); [1JN 4:1](https://ref.ly/1John4:1); [2JN 1:7–1:11](https://ref.ly/2John1:7-2John1:11); [JUD 1:3–1:4](https://ref.ly/Jude1:3-Jude1:4); [REV 13:11](https://ref.ly/Rev13:11), [REV 19:20](https://ref.ly/Rev19:20) *This beast is thought to represent antichristian religion.*

### The world seeks to seduce Christians

**[1JN 2:16](https://ref.ly/1John2:16)** *See also* [LUK 12:15](https://ref.ly/Luke12:15), [LUK 12:19](https://ref.ly/Luke12:19); [2TI 4:10](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:10); [TIT 2:12](https://ref.ly/Titus2:12); [HEB 11:24–11:25](https://ref.ly/Heb11:24-Heb11:25); [REV 17:1–17:5](https://ref.ly/Rev17:1-Rev17:5) *Babylon may represent the world's seduction.*
