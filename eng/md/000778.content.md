## 5189: teeth (id: 5189)

Scripture often uses teeth symbolically, to refer to the power of individuals or to human rage (as in the “gnashing of teeth”).

Teeth in a literal sense
------------------------

### In eating

**[NUM 11:33](https://ref.ly/Num11:33)** *See also* [DAN 7:5](https://ref.ly/Dan7:5), [DAN 7:7](https://ref.ly/Dan7:7), [DAN 7:19](https://ref.ly/Dan7:19); [ZEC 9:7](https://ref.ly/Zech9:7); [REV 9:8](https://ref.ly/Rev9:8)

### As an object of beauty

**[SNG 4:2](https://ref.ly/Song4:2)** *See also* [GEN 49:12](https://ref.ly/Gen49:12); [SNG 6:6](https://ref.ly/Song6:6)

### As an object of fear

**[JOB 41:14](https://ref.ly/Job41:14)** *See also* [DEU 32:24](https://ref.ly/Deut32:24); [JOB 20:16](https://ref.ly/Job20:16); [JOL 1:6](https://ref.ly/Joel1:6)

Teeth used symbolically
-----------------------

### To represent offensive weapons

[PSA 57:4](https://ref.ly/Ps57:4); ; [PRO 30:14](https://ref.ly/Prov30:14)

### To symbolise military power

**[ISA 41:15](https://ref.ly/Isa41:15)** *See also* [JOL 1:6](https://ref.ly/Joel1:6); [AMO 1:3](https://ref.ly/Amos1:3); [HAB 3:12](https://ref.ly/Hab3:12)

### God rescues his people from the teeth of their enemies

**[PSA 124:6](https://ref.ly/Ps124:6)** *See also* [JOB 4:10](https://ref.ly/Job4:10); [DAN 6:20–6:21](https://ref.ly/Dan6:20-Dan6:21)

### Breaking the teeth of the wicked ends their power

**[PSA 3:7](https://ref.ly/Ps3:7)** *See also* [JOB 29:17](https://ref.ly/Job29:17); [PSA 58:6](https://ref.ly/Ps58:6) *Enemies are often described as dangerous animals.*
