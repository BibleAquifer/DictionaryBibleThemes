## 5667: children, responsibilities to God (id: 5667)

The responsibilities of children to God as their heavenly Father include honouring and obeying him.

The responsibilities of children to their heavenly Father
---------------------------------------------------------

### To honour and obey him in reverent fear

**[PSA 34:11](https://ref.ly/Ps34:11)** *See also* [PRO 2:5](https://ref.ly/Prov2:5), [PRO 9:10](https://ref.ly/Prov9:10)

### To seek wisdom

**[PRO 4:5–4:7](https://ref.ly/Prov4:5-Prov4:7)** *See also* [PRO 1:1–1:4](https://ref.ly/Prov1:1-Prov1:4), [PRO 8:32–8:33](https://ref.ly/Prov8:32-Prov8:33), [PRO 10:1](https://ref.ly/Prov10:1)

### To listen and learn

**[PRO 1:8–1:9](https://ref.ly/Prov1:8-Prov1:9)** *See also* [PRO 4:1–4:4](https://ref.ly/Prov4:1-Prov4:4), [PRO 23:22](https://ref.ly/Prov23:22)

### To praise God

**[PSA 8:2](https://ref.ly/Ps8:2)** *See also* [MAT 21:15–21:16](https://ref.ly/Matt21:15-Matt21:16)

### To join in worship

**[NEH 12:43](https://ref.ly/Neh12:43)** *See also* [JOS 8:35](https://ref.ly/Josh8:35)
