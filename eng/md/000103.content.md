## 1449: signs, purposes of (id: 1449)

Signs are given to confirm God's word. They may warn the rebellious or encourage the faithful. People may seek signs as a result of a genuine desire to serve God.

Signs are given to demonstrate God's power

**[DEU 34:10–34:12](https://ref.ly/Deut34:10-Deut34:12)** *See also* [DEU 4:34](https://ref.ly/Deut4:34), [DEU 6:21–6:22](https://ref.ly/Deut6:21-Deut6:22), [DEU 7:19](https://ref.ly/Deut7:19), [DEU 26:8](https://ref.ly/Deut26:8); [1CH 16:11–16:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:11-1Chr16:12) parallel [PSA 105:4–105:5](https://ref.ly/Ps105:4-Ps105:5); [JER 32:21](https://ref.ly/Jer32:21)
