## 8712: denial of Jesus Christ (id: 8712)

The making of a conscious, informed decision not to accept Jesus Christ or his claims and teaching.

Jesus Christ was denied by the Jewish people
--------------------------------------------

**[JHN 1:11](https://ref.ly/John1:11)** *See also* [MAT 11:16–11:19](https://ref.ly/Matt11:16-Matt11:19) parallel [LUK 7:31–7:35](https://ref.ly/Luke7:31-Luke7:35); [MAT 13:54–13:58](https://ref.ly/Matt13:54-Matt13:58) parallel [MRK 6:1–6:6](https://ref.ly/Mark6:1-Mark6:6); [MAT 26:63–26:68](https://ref.ly/Matt26:63-Matt26:68) parallels [MRK 14:61–14:65](https://ref.ly/Mark14:61-Mark14:65), [LUK 22:66–22:71](https://ref.ly/Luke22:66-Luke22:71); [LUK 4:16–4:30](https://ref.ly/Luke4:16-Luke4:30); [JHN 5:37–5:43](https://ref.ly/John5:37-John5:43); [ACT 4:8–4:12](https://ref.ly/Acts4:8-Acts4:12); [PSA 118:22](https://ref.ly/Ps118:22)

**[ACT 13:46](https://ref.ly/Acts13:46)** *See also* [MAT 21:40–21:41](https://ref.ly/Matt21:40-Matt21:41) parallels [MRK 12:9](https://ref.ly/Mark12:9), [LUK 20:15–20:16](https://ref.ly/Luke20:15-Luke20:16); [ACT 18:5–18:6](https://ref.ly/Acts18:5-Acts18:6)
