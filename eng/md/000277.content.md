## 3251: Holy Spirit, filling with (id: 3251)

To be filled with the Holy Spirit is to be energised and controlled by the third person of the Godhead in such a way that under the acknowledged lordship of Jesus Christ the full presence and power of God are experienced. Spirit\-filling leads to renewal, obedience, boldness in testimony and an arresting quality in believers' lives.

People filled with the Holy Spirit before the ministry of Jesus Christ
----------------------------------------------------------------------

### In the OT

[EXO 31:3](https://ref.ly/Exod31:3) *Bezalel filled with the Spirit for artwork on the tabernacle*; ; [DEU 34:9](https://ref.ly/Deut34:9) *Joshua filled with the Spirit to succeed Moses*

### In the events surrounding the birth of John the Baptist

[LUK 1:15](https://ref.ly/Luke1:15) *John the Baptist filled with the Spirit from birth*; ; [LUK 1:41](https://ref.ly/Luke1:41) *Elizabeth filled with the Spirit and speaks words of praise*; ; [LUK 1:67](https://ref.ly/Luke1:67) *Zechariah prophesies about the life of John and God's salvation*

Jesus Christ is full of the Holy Spirit
---------------------------------------

**[LUK 4:1](https://ref.ly/Luke4:1)** *See also* [LUK 10:21](https://ref.ly/Luke10:21); [JHN 3:34](https://ref.ly/John3:34); [ACT 10:38](https://ref.ly/Acts10:38)

NT terminology
--------------

**[ACT 1:5](https://ref.ly/Acts1:5), [ACT 2:4](https://ref.ly/Acts2:4), [ACT 10:47](https://ref.ly/Acts10:47)** *While these terms are virtually synonymous when used of initial experiences of the Spirit, “filled” is also used to designate subsequent experiences and renewings of the same divine power, while the other terms are not.*

### \`\`Be filled with the Spirit'': an apostolic command

**[EPH 5:18](https://ref.ly/Eph5:18)** *The present tense of the verb implies the need to be regularly filled and re\-filled with the Spirit.*

### \`\`Filled with the Spirit'': a conscious experience of God's power

**[ACT 2:4](https://ref.ly/Acts2:4)** *See also* [ACT 4:31](https://ref.ly/Acts4:31), [ACT 9:17](https://ref.ly/Acts9:17)

### \`\`Full of the Spirit'': a consistent quality of Christian character

**[ACT 6:3–6:5](https://ref.ly/Acts6:3-Acts6:5)** *See also* [ACT 11:24](https://ref.ly/Acts11:24), [ACT 13:52](https://ref.ly/Acts13:52)

### \`\`Filled with the Spirit'': the inspiration to speak words of witness, challenge or rebuke

[ACT 4:8](https://ref.ly/Acts4:8) *Peter testifying before the Sanhedrin*; ; [ACT 7:55](https://ref.ly/Acts7:55) *Stephen testifying to the Sanhedrin, seeing the glory of God*; ; [ACT 13:9](https://ref.ly/Acts13:9) *Paul rebuking Elymas*
