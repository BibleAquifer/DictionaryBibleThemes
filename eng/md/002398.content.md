## 8771: idolatry, objections to (id: 8771)

Scripture presents idolatry as absurd and irrational, offensive to God and a source of spiritual and moral danger.

Idolatry is disparaged
----------------------

### The absurdity of making idols

**[HAB 2:18](https://ref.ly/Hab2:18)** *See also* [DEU 4:28](https://ref.ly/Deut4:28); [ISA 40:18–40:20](https://ref.ly/Isa40:18-Isa40:20), [ISA 41:6–41:7](https://ref.ly/Isa41:6-Isa41:7); [JER 10:3–10:9](https://ref.ly/Jer10:3-Jer10:9); [HOS 10:6](https://ref.ly/Hos10:6); [HAB 2:19](https://ref.ly/Hab2:19)

### Idols represent falsehood and fraud

**[PSA 40:4](https://ref.ly/Ps40:4)** *See also* [JER 2:5](https://ref.ly/Jer2:5), [JER 10:14](https://ref.ly/Jer10:14), [JER 16:19](https://ref.ly/Jer16:19), [JER 51:17–51:18](https://ref.ly/Jer51:17-Jer51:18); [HOS 12:1](https://ref.ly/Hos12:1); [AMO 2:4](https://ref.ly/Amos2:4)

### Bowing down to idols is inappropriate

**[PSA 135:17–135:18](https://ref.ly/Ps135:17-Ps135:18)** parallel [PSA 115:5–115:8](https://ref.ly/Ps115:5-Ps115:8) *See also* [ISA 2:8](https://ref.ly/Isa2:8), [ISA 44:15–44:17](https://ref.ly/Isa44:15-Isa44:17), [ISA 46:6](https://ref.ly/Isa46:6); [JER 1:16](https://ref.ly/Jer1:16); [MIC 5:13](https://ref.ly/Mic5:13)

God abominates idols
--------------------

**[EZK 7:20](https://ref.ly/Ezek7:20)** *Two terms, that is “idols” and “vile images”, meaning “abomination” are found here in parallel. A third, punning on the word for “dung” is often used by Ezekiel for idols.* *See also* [DEU 7:25](https://ref.ly/Deut7:25); [2KI 23:24](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:24); [EZK 5:9](https://ref.ly/Ezek5:9), [EZK 5:11](https://ref.ly/Ezek5:11), [EZK 6:4](https://ref.ly/Ezek6:4)
