## 5843: embarrassment (id: 5843)

A state of discomfort and shame, caused by a sense of guilt over something that has been said or done.

Embarrassment may be a sign of misunderstanding or mistrust
-----------------------------------------------------------

**[JHN 13:6–13:8](https://ref.ly/John13:6-John13:8)** *See also* [MRK 2:16–2:17](https://ref.ly/Mark2:16-Mark2:17) *Jesus Christ's enemies approached the disciples knowing they would be embarrassed by the company he was keeping*; [MRK 3:21](https://ref.ly/Mark3:21) *The family of Jesus Christ were embarrassed by what they were hearing about him.*

Love includes not causing unnecessary embarrassment
---------------------------------------------------

**[DEU 24:10–24:11](https://ref.ly/Deut24:10-Deut24:11)**
