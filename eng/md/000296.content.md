## 4007: creation, and God (id: 4007)

The natural world is sustained by God and speaks of God.

God sustains the creation
-------------------------

### He upholds the natural order

**[HEB 1:3](https://ref.ly/Heb1:3)** *See also* [JOB 38:33–38:37](https://ref.ly/Job38:33-Job38:37); [PSA 104:1–104:35](https://ref.ly/Ps104:1-Ps104:35), [PSA 135:6–135:7](https://ref.ly/Ps135:6-Ps135:7), [PSA 145:16–145:17](https://ref.ly/Ps145:16-Ps145:17); [MAT 10:29–10:30](https://ref.ly/Matt10:29-Matt10:30); [COL 1:17](https://ref.ly/Col1:17)

### He sustains humanity

**[ACT 17:28](https://ref.ly/Acts17:28)** *See also* [JOB 33:4](https://ref.ly/Job33:4); [PSA 36:6](https://ref.ly/Ps36:6); [DAN 4:34–4:35](https://ref.ly/Dan4:34-Dan4:35); [1CO 8:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:6)

### Creation is upheld for the good of humanity

**[GEN 8:22](https://ref.ly/Gen8:22)** *See also* [GEN 9:12–9:16](https://ref.ly/Gen9:12-Gen9:16); [MAT 6:11](https://ref.ly/Matt6:11)

### God's sustaining power reserves the world for judgment

**[2PE 3:7](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:7)** *See also* [2PE 3:9–3:12](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:9-2Pet3:12) *Time is being given for sinners to repent.*

God has given humanity responsibility to preserve creation
----------------------------------------------------------

**[GEN 1:28](https://ref.ly/Gen1:28)** *See also* [GEN 2:15](https://ref.ly/Gen2:15), [GEN 9:1–9:3](https://ref.ly/Gen9:1-Gen9:3); [PSA 8:6–8:8](https://ref.ly/Ps8:6-Ps8:8), [PSA 115:16](https://ref.ly/Ps115:16); [HEB 2:8](https://ref.ly/Heb2:8); [JAS 3:7](https://ref.ly/Jas3:7)

Creation is spoilt by sin
-------------------------

**[GEN 3:17–3:19](https://ref.ly/Gen3:17-Gen3:19)** *See also* [ROM 8:20–8:22](https://ref.ly/Rom8:20-Rom8:22); [HEB 6:8](https://ref.ly/Heb6:8)
