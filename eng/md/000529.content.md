## 4802: blight (id: 4802)

A disease ruinous to crops. In the OT blight was seen as evidence of God's judgment.

-----------------------------

**[DEU 28:22](https://ref.ly/Deut28:22)** *See also* [HOS 9:16](https://ref.ly/Hos9:16); [AMO 4:9](https://ref.ly/Amos4:9); [HAG 2:17](https://ref.ly/Hag2:17)
