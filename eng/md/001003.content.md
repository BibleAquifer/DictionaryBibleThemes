## 5424: nationalism (id: 5424)

A concern to preserve or proclaim the distinctive features of a nation or people.

The recognition of different national groups
--------------------------------------------

**[GEN 10:32](https://ref.ly/Gen10:32)** *[Ge 10:32](https://ref.ly/Gen10:32) is the summary conclusion of the table of nations which begins in [Ge 10:1](https://ref.ly/Gen10:1).* *See also* [GEN 17:4–17:6](https://ref.ly/Gen17:4-Gen17:6), [GEN 19:36–19:38](https://ref.ly/Gen19:36-Gen19:38); [EXO 3:8](https://ref.ly/Exod3:8); [1KI 9:20](https://ref.ly/1Kgs9:20) parallel [2CH 8:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr8:7)
