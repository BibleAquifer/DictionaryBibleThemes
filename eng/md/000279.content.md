## 3257: Holy Spirit, gift of (id: 3257)

In the OT the gift of the Holy Spirit was restricted to individuals for particular tasks, but at Pentecost and on subsequent occasions, the Holy Spirit is given to all believers.

The gift of the Holy Spirit in the OT
-------------------------------------

### The Spirit is given to individuals for specific tasks

[1SA 10:6](https://ref.ly/1Sam10:6) *to Saul, for kingship*; ; [EXO 31:2–31:5](https://ref.ly/Exod31:2-Exod31:5) *to Bezalel, for craftsmanship*

### The gift of the Spirit can be withdrawn

**[1SA 16:14](https://ref.ly/1Sam16:14)**; **[PSA 51:11](https://ref.ly/Ps51:11)** *See also* [JDG 16:20](https://ref.ly/Judg16:20); [1KI 22:24](https://ref.ly/1Kgs22:24)

The gift of the Holy Spirit is foretold
---------------------------------------

### He is promised by John the Baptist

[MAT 3:11](https://ref.ly/Matt3:11) parallels [MRK 1:8](https://ref.ly/Mark1:8), [LUK 3:16](https://ref.ly/Luke3:16), [JHN 1:33](https://ref.ly/John1:33)

### He is promised by Jesus Christ

[LUK 24:49](https://ref.ly/Luke24:49); ; [JHN 7:37–7:39](https://ref.ly/John7:37-John7:39), [JHN 14:16](https://ref.ly/John14:16), [JHN 14:26](https://ref.ly/John14:26), [JHN 15:26](https://ref.ly/John15:26), [JHN 16:7](https://ref.ly/John16:7); ; [ACT 1:4–1:5](https://ref.ly/Acts1:4-Acts1:5), [ACT 1:8](https://ref.ly/Acts1:8), [ACT 11:16](https://ref.ly/Acts11:16)

The gift of the Holy Spirit before Pentecost
--------------------------------------------

### Individuals filled with the Spirit

**[LUK 1:15](https://ref.ly/Luke1:15)** *See also* [LUK 1:41](https://ref.ly/Luke1:41), [LUK 1:67](https://ref.ly/Luke1:67), [LUK 2:25–2:27](https://ref.ly/Luke2:25-Luke2:27)

### Jesus Christ receives the gift of the Spirit

**[MAT 3:16](https://ref.ly/Matt3:16)** parallels [MRK 1:10](https://ref.ly/Mark1:10), [LUK 3:22](https://ref.ly/Luke3:22), [JHN 1:32](https://ref.ly/John1:32)

### Jesus Christ bids his disciples receive the Holy Spirit

*See also* [JHN 20:22](https://ref.ly/John20:22)

The gift of the Holy Spirit at Pentecost and after
--------------------------------------------------

### At Pentecost

**[ACT 2:2–2:4](https://ref.ly/Acts2:2-Acts2:4)** *See also* [ACT 2:33](https://ref.ly/Acts2:33)

### The Spirit was given on later occasions

**[ACT 10:44](https://ref.ly/Acts10:44)** *See also* [ACT 8:15–8:17](https://ref.ly/Acts8:15-Acts8:17), [ACT 9:17](https://ref.ly/Acts9:17), [ACT 19:6](https://ref.ly/Acts19:6); [GAL 3:5](https://ref.ly/Gal3:5); [HEB 2:4](https://ref.ly/Heb2:4)

### The gift of the Spirit is for all believers

**[ACT 2:38–2:39](https://ref.ly/Acts2:38-Acts2:39)** *See also* [ACT 2:17](https://ref.ly/Acts2:17), [ACT 5:32](https://ref.ly/Acts5:32), [ACT 10:45](https://ref.ly/Acts10:45), [ACT 11:17](https://ref.ly/Acts11:17), [ACT 15:8](https://ref.ly/Acts15:8); [ROM 8:9](https://ref.ly/Rom8:9); [GAL 3:14](https://ref.ly/Gal3:14); [1TH 4:8](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:8); [HEB 2:4](https://ref.ly/Heb2:4)

### God's willingness to give the Spirit

**[LUK 11:13](https://ref.ly/Luke11:13)** *See also* [JHN 3:34](https://ref.ly/John3:34)

God's purposes require the gift of the Holy Spirit
--------------------------------------------------

### To give assurance of acceptance by God

**[ACT 15:8](https://ref.ly/Acts15:8)** *See also* [ACT 10:47](https://ref.ly/Acts10:47), [ACT 11:17](https://ref.ly/Acts11:17); [1JN 3:24](https://ref.ly/1John3:24), [1JN 4:13](https://ref.ly/1John4:13)

### To convict the world of sin

[JHN 16:7–16:8](https://ref.ly/John16:7-John16:8); ; [ACT 2:37](https://ref.ly/Acts2:37)

### To provide spiritual gifts

[ACT 2:4](https://ref.ly/Acts2:4), [ACT 10:45–10:46](https://ref.ly/Acts10:45-Acts10:46), [ACT 19:6](https://ref.ly/Acts19:6); ; [1CO 12:4–12:11](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:4-1Cor12:11)

### To work miracles, signs and wonders

[ROM 15:18–15:19](https://ref.ly/Rom15:18-Rom15:19); ; [GAL 3:5](https://ref.ly/Gal3:5); ; [HEB 2:4](https://ref.ly/Heb2:4)

### To give boldness in preaching and witnessing

**[ACT 1:8](https://ref.ly/Acts1:8)** *See also* [ACT 4:31](https://ref.ly/Acts4:31), [ACT 5:32](https://ref.ly/Acts5:32)

### To cultivate the fruit of the Spirit

**[ROM 14:17](https://ref.ly/Rom14:17)** *See also* [ROM 5:5](https://ref.ly/Rom5:5), [ROM 15:13](https://ref.ly/Rom15:13); [GAL 5:16](https://ref.ly/Gal5:16)

### To sanctify the believer

[2TH 2:13](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:13); ; [1PE 1:2](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:2)
