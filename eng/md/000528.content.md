## 4801: black (id: 4801)

A dark colour often associated with impending judgment or with evil.

Black as descriptive of colour
------------------------------

**[MAT 5:36](https://ref.ly/Matt5:36)** *See also* [EXO 10:15](https://ref.ly/Exod10:15) *the plague of locusts*; [LEV 11:13–11:14](https://ref.ly/Lev11:13-Lev11:14) parallel [DEU 14:12–14:13](https://ref.ly/Deut14:12-Deut14:13) *unclean food*; [LEV 13:31](https://ref.ly/Lev13:31); [DEU 4:11](https://ref.ly/Deut4:11); [1KI 18:45](https://ref.ly/1Kgs18:45); [JOB 28:3](https://ref.ly/Job28:3), [JOB 30:28](https://ref.ly/Job30:28), [JOB 30:30](https://ref.ly/Job30:30); [SNG 5:11](https://ref.ly/Song5:11); [LAM 4:7–4:8](https://ref.ly/Lam4:7-Lam4:8)ZEC [6:2](https://ref.ly/Lam6:2), [ZEC 6:6](https://ref.ly/Zech6:6); [REV 6:5](https://ref.ly/Rev6:5)[REV 6:12](https://ref.ly/Rev6:12)

Black as the opposite of light
------------------------------

**[JOB 3:5](https://ref.ly/Job3:5)** *Job cursing the day of his birth.* *See also* [ISA 5:30](https://ref.ly/Isa5:30), [ISA 45:7](https://ref.ly/Isa45:7); [AMO 5:8](https://ref.ly/Amos5:8)

Black in the setting of impending judgment
------------------------------------------

**[JOL 2:2](https://ref.ly/Joel2:2)** *See also* [AMO 8:9](https://ref.ly/Amos8:9); [ZEP 1:15](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:15)

Black as a symbol of evil
-------------------------

**[JOB 34:22](https://ref.ly/Job34:22)** *See also* [JHN 3:19](https://ref.ly/John3:19); [2PE 2:17](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:17); [JUD 1:13](https://ref.ly/Jude1:13)
