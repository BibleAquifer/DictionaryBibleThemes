## 8822: self-justification (id: 8822)

A self\-centred attitude by which people defend their actions and beliefs without reference to God. Scripture insists that the confidence and ultimate justification of believers lie with God, and condemns those who look elsewhere for these.

The impossibility of self\-justification
----------------------------------------

### Self\-justification is impossible before a holy God

**[JOB 25:4](https://ref.ly/Job25:4)** *See also* [1SA 6:20](https://ref.ly/1Sam6:20); [MAL 3:2](https://ref.ly/Mal3:2); [REV 6:17](https://ref.ly/Rev6:17)

### Justification is achieved by God, not by people

**[ROM 5:1–5:2](https://ref.ly/Rom5:1-Rom5:2)** *See also* [ROM 3:27–3:30](https://ref.ly/Rom3:27-Rom3:30); [GAL 2:20–2:21](https://ref.ly/Gal2:20-Gal2:21)

How self\-justification occurs
------------------------------

### Self\-justification occurs through ignoring God and his ways

**[NEH 9:16](https://ref.ly/Neh9:16)** *See also* [LUK 16:14–16:15](https://ref.ly/Luke16:14-Luke16:15)

### Self\-justification occurs through pride

**[LUK 18:9–18:14](https://ref.ly/Luke18:9-Luke18:14)** *See also* [MAT 23:1–23:7](https://ref.ly/Matt23:1-Matt23:7) parallels [MRK 12:38–12:39](https://ref.ly/Mark12:38-Mark12:39), [LUK 20:45–20:46](https://ref.ly/Luke20:45-Luke20:46); [ROM 2:17–2:27](https://ref.ly/Rom2:17-Rom2:27)

### Self\-justification occurs through legalism

**[LUK 10:25–10:29](https://ref.ly/Luke10:25-Luke10:29)** *See also* [GAL 3:1–3:5](https://ref.ly/Gal3:1-Gal3:5), [GAL 5:4](https://ref.ly/Gal5:4)
