## 5020: human nature (id: 5020)

Human nature was created by God, is corrupted by sin and can only be renewed and recreated through the grace of God. Redemption was achieved by Jesus Christ taking upon himself sinful human nature on the cross, so delivering from the power and penalty of sin.

------------------------------

**[EXO 4:11](https://ref.ly/Exod4:11)**; **[ISA 45:12](https://ref.ly/Isa45:12)** *See also* [GEN 1:26–1:27](https://ref.ly/Gen1:26-Gen1:27); [PSA 95:6](https://ref.ly/Ps95:6), [PSA 100:3](https://ref.ly/Ps100:3); [PRO 22:2](https://ref.ly/Prov22:2); [JER 27:5](https://ref.ly/Jer27:5)

The components of human nature
------------------------------

### The soul

**[PSA 116:7](https://ref.ly/Ps116:7)** *The Hebrew and Greek words for “soul” are translated in various ways by the NIV, including “breath” and “life”. It refers chiefly to the principle of life.* *See also* [MRK 8:35–8:37](https://ref.ly/Mark8:35-Mark8:37); [LUK 1:46](https://ref.ly/Luke1:46); [1PE 1:9](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:9), [1PE 2:11](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:11)

### The spirit

**[JAS 2:26](https://ref.ly/Jas2:26)** *The Hebrew and Greek words for “spirit” are translated in various ways by the NIV, including “breath”, “heart” and “mind”. It often refers to the identity\-giving aspect of human nature.* *See also* [JOB 34:14](https://ref.ly/Job34:14); [PSA 77:3](https://ref.ly/Ps77:3), [PSA 142:3](https://ref.ly/Ps142:3); [MAT 26:41](https://ref.ly/Matt26:41); [LUK 8:55](https://ref.ly/Luke8:55); [ACT 7:59](https://ref.ly/Acts7:59); [ROM 8:16](https://ref.ly/Rom8:16); [HEB 4:12](https://ref.ly/Heb4:12)

### The flesh

**[JER 17:5](https://ref.ly/Jer17:5)** *The Hebrew and Greek words for “flesh” are translated in various ways by the NIV, including “body”, “flesh” and “human nature”. The term can refer specifically to the frailty of human nature or to human nature in opposition to God.* *See also* [GEN 6:3](https://ref.ly/Gen6:3); [JOB 10:4](https://ref.ly/Job10:4); [PSA 78:39](https://ref.ly/Ps78:39); [MAT 26:41](https://ref.ly/Matt26:41); [PHP 3:3–3:5](https://ref.ly/Phil3:3-Phil3:5)
