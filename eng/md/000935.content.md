## 5348: injustice, nature and source of (id: 5348)

Characterised by deception, dishonesty and heartlessness, injustice is the oppression of the needy and the weak. Its origin is Satan and it works through the sinful hearts of men and women. **[PSA 64:6](https://ref.ly/Ps64:6)**

The sources of injustice
------------------------

### The devil

**[1JN 3:10](https://ref.ly/1John3:10)** *The word “devil” means “slanderer”.* *See also* [JHN 8:44](https://ref.ly/John8:44); [REV 2:10](https://ref.ly/Rev2:10); [2TH 2:9–2:11](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:9-2Thess2:11) *The word “Satan” means “adversary”\-\-the same person as the devil*; [JAS 3:14–3:15](https://ref.ly/Jas3:14-Jas3:15); [REV 2:13](https://ref.ly/Rev2:13)

### The world

**[1JN 2:16](https://ref.ly/1John2:16)** *“the world” here means society without God and hostile to him.* *See also* [EXO 23:2](https://ref.ly/Exod23:2); [PSA 69:4](https://ref.ly/Ps69:4); [PRO 31:4–31:5](https://ref.ly/Prov31:4-Prov31:5); [EZK 22:12](https://ref.ly/Ezek22:12); [MAT 5:11–5:12](https://ref.ly/Matt5:11-Matt5:12); [JHN 15:18–15:19](https://ref.ly/John15:18-John15:19)
