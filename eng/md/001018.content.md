## 5440: perjury (id: 5440)

Swearing falsely, usually in a legal context, either by taking a false oath or by breaking one's oath later. God hates perjury.

God hates perjury
-----------------

**[ZEC 8:16–8:17](https://ref.ly/Zech8:16-Zech8:17)** *See also* [PSA 24:3–24:4](https://ref.ly/Ps24:3-Ps24:4); [ISA 48:1](https://ref.ly/Isa48:1); [JER 5:2](https://ref.ly/Jer5:2), [JER 7:9–7:10](https://ref.ly/Jer7:9-Jer7:10); [HOS 10:4](https://ref.ly/Hos10:4); [ZEC 5:3–5:4](https://ref.ly/Zech5:3-Zech5:4); [MAL 3:5](https://ref.ly/Mal3:5); [1TI 1:9–1:10](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:9-1Tim1:10)
