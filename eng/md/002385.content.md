## 8756: folly, examples of (id: 8756)

Human folly is seen in rebellion against God, ignoring his way and will, a failure to understand him: and moral or spiritual irresponsibility.

Examples of human folly
-----------------------

### The folly of God's people

**[JER 4:22](https://ref.ly/Jer4:22)** *See also* [DEU 32:6](https://ref.ly/Deut32:6) *rejecting God*; [JER 10:8](https://ref.ly/Jer10:8) *worshipping idols*; [HOS 7:11](https://ref.ly/Hos7:11) *seeking foreign help rather than trusting God*

### The folly of Israel's leaders

**[JER 10:21](https://ref.ly/Jer10:21)** *The shepherds are those entrusted with the task of guiding and caring for God's people*; **[EZK 13:3](https://ref.ly/Ezek13:3)** *See also* [MAT 23:16–23:17](https://ref.ly/Matt23:16-Matt23:17)

### Particular demonstrations of human folly

[NUM 12:1–12:11](https://ref.ly/Num12:1-Num12:11) *Aaron and Miriam*; [1SA 13:13](https://ref.ly/1Sam13:13), [1SA 26:21](https://ref.ly/1Sam26:21); [1SA 25:25](https://ref.ly/1Sam25:25) *Nabal. In Hebrew this name means “fool”.*; [2SA 24:10](https://ref.ly/2Sam24:10) parallel [1CH 21:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr21:8); [2CH 16:7–16:9](https://ref.ly/2Chr16:7-2Chr16:9) *Asa, king of Judah*; ; [ISA 19:11–19:13](https://ref.ly/Isa19:11-Isa19:13) *Pharaoh's advisors*; ; [MAT 7:26](https://ref.ly/Matt7:26) *the foolish builder*; ; [MAT 25:1–25:3](https://ref.ly/Matt25:1-Matt25:3) *the foolish virgins*; ; [LUK 12:16–12:21](https://ref.ly/Luke12:16-Luke12:21) *the rich fool*; ; [GAL 3:13](https://ref.ly/Gal3:13) *the Galatian Christians*

Paul's \`\`foolishness''
------------------------

**[2CO 11:16–11:21](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:16-2Cor11:21)** *Paul engages in what he admits is the foolish boasting of his accomplishments in order to challenge the claims of false “apostles”.* *See also* [2CO 11:1](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:1), [2CO 12:6](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:6), [2CO 12:11](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:11)
