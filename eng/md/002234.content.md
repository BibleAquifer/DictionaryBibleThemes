## 8447: hospitality, examples of (id: 8447)

Scripture provides many examples of friendship and generosity towards strangers, and encourages believers to be hospitable in turn.

Examples of hospitality in the OT
---------------------------------

**[GEN 18:2–18:5](https://ref.ly/Gen18:2-Gen18:5)** *See also* [GEN 19:1–19:3](https://ref.ly/Gen19:1-Gen19:3) *Lot entertains visitors from God*; [GEN 24:22–24:25](https://ref.ly/Gen24:22-Gen24:25) *Abraham's servant is welcomed in Rebekah's home*; [EXO 2:20](https://ref.ly/Exod2:20) *Moses is welcomed by a priest of Midian*; [JDG 13:15](https://ref.ly/Judg13:15) *Manoah receives the angel of the LORD.*[JDG 19:1–19:4](https://ref.ly/Judg19:1-Judg19:4), [JDG 19:20–19:21](https://ref.ly/Judg19:20-Judg19:21)[2SA 17:27–17:29](https://ref.ly/2Sam17:27-2Sam17:29) *hospitality shown to David and his people*; [1KI 17:7–17:16](https://ref.ly/1Kgs17:7-1Kgs17:16) *The widow of Zarephath cares for Elijah despite her poverty*; [2KI 4:8–4:10](https://ref.ly/2Kgs4:8-2Kgs4:10) *a Shunammite provides for Elisha*; [NEH 5:17](https://ref.ly/Neh5:17) *Nehemiah shows hospitality to fellow Jews.*

Hospitality shown to Jesus Christ
---------------------------------

**[MAT 9:10](https://ref.ly/Matt9:10)** parallels [MRK 2:15](https://ref.ly/Mark2:15), [LUK 5:29](https://ref.ly/Luke5:29) *Jesus Christ received hospitality from people of all social backgrounds.* *See also* [MAT 8:14–8:15](https://ref.ly/Matt8:14-Matt8:15) parallels [MRK 1:29–1:31](https://ref.ly/Mark1:29-Mark1:31), [LUK 4:38–4:39](https://ref.ly/Luke4:38-Luke4:39) *at Peter's house*[MAT 26:6–26:7](https://ref.ly/Matt26:6-Matt26:7) parallel [MRK 14:3](https://ref.ly/Mark14:3); [JHN 12:1–12:3](https://ref.ly/John12:1-John12:3)[LUK 7:36](https://ref.ly/Luke7:36) *at the house of Simon the Pharisee*; [LUK 10:38](https://ref.ly/Luke10:38) *at the home of Mary and Martha*; [LUK 14:1](https://ref.ly/Luke14:1) *at the house of a prominent Pharisee*; [LUK 19:5–19:7](https://ref.ly/Luke19:5-Luke19:7) *at Zacchaeus' house*; [LUK 24:29](https://ref.ly/Luke24:29) *with disciples in Emmaus*; [JHN 2:2](https://ref.ly/John2:2) *at the wedding at Cana*
