## 9312: resurrection, significance of Jesus Christ's (id: 9312)

Jesus Christ's resurrection represents a demonstration of the power of God, the confirmation of the divinity of Jesus Christ and the grounds of hope for Christian believers.

Jesus Christ's resurrection was a demonstration of God's power
--------------------------------------------------------------

### The power of God the Father

**[EPH 1:18–1:20](https://ref.ly/Eph1:18-Eph1:20)** *See also* [MAT 22:29–22:32](https://ref.ly/Matt22:29-Matt22:32); [ACT 2:24](https://ref.ly/Acts2:24), [ACT 3:15](https://ref.ly/Acts3:15), [ACT 10:40](https://ref.ly/Acts10:40), [ACT 13:29–13:30](https://ref.ly/Acts13:29-Acts13:30); [GAL 1:1](https://ref.ly/Gal1:1); [COL 2:12](https://ref.ly/Col2:12)

### The power of the Holy Spirit

[ROM 1:4](https://ref.ly/Rom1:4); ; [1TI 3:16](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:16); ; [1PE 3:18](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:18)

The resurrection confirmed Jesus Christ as the Son of God
---------------------------------------------------------

**[JHN 20:30–20:31](https://ref.ly/John20:30-John20:31)** *John calls Jesus Christ's miracles “signs” (see also [Jn 2:11](https://ref.ly/John2:11); [6:2](https://ref.ly/John6:2)) and his resurrection is the climax, confirming his identity beyond all doubt*; **[ROM 1:4](https://ref.ly/Rom1:4)** *See also* [PSA 2:7](https://ref.ly/Ps2:7); [ACT 13:33](https://ref.ly/Acts13:33)

The centrality of the resurrection of Jesus Christ
--------------------------------------------------

### As the basis of faith

**[1CO 15:14–15:15](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:14-1Cor15:15)** *See also* [ACT 3:15](https://ref.ly/Acts3:15), [ACT 4:33](https://ref.ly/Acts4:33), [ACT 17:18](https://ref.ly/Acts17:18), [ACT 24:21](https://ref.ly/Acts24:21); [ROM 10:9](https://ref.ly/Rom10:9); [2TI 2:8](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:8); [HEB 6:1–6:2](https://ref.ly/Heb6:1-Heb6:2)

### As the basis of believers' justification

[ROM 4:25](https://ref.ly/Rom4:25), [ROM 8:34](https://ref.ly/Rom8:34)

### As the basis of Christian hope

[ACT 24:15](https://ref.ly/Acts24:15); ; [1CO 15:19](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:19)

### As the basis of believers' resurrection

**[1CO 15:20–15:23](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:20-1Cor15:23)** *The Law of Moses ([Ex 23:16](https://ref.ly/Exod23:16)) provided for an offering of the firstfruits of crops to God. The firstfruits were the guarantee of the full harvest to come. The NT sees the resurrection of Jesus Christ as the firstfruits of the full ingathering of all God's people when Jesus Christ comes again.* *See also* [JHN 14:19](https://ref.ly/John14:19); [ACT 26:23](https://ref.ly/Acts26:23); [ROM 8:11](https://ref.ly/Rom8:11)
