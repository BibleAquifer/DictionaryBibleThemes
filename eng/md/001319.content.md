## 5797: bereavement, God's comfort in (id: 5797)

God cares for the bereaved, and can turn their mourning into joy and hope.

God comforts the bereaved and the sorrowful
-------------------------------------------

**[LAM 3:32–3:33](https://ref.ly/Lam3:32-Lam3:33)**; **[2CO 1:3–1:4](https://ref.ly/2Cor1:3-2Cor1:4)** *See also* [PSA 116:8](https://ref.ly/Ps116:8); [MAT 5:4](https://ref.ly/Matt5:4); [REV 7:17](https://ref.ly/Rev7:17)

### Mourning turned into joy

**[PSA 30:11](https://ref.ly/Ps30:11)** *See also* [ISA 61:1–61:3](https://ref.ly/Isa61:1-Isa61:3); [JER 31:13](https://ref.ly/Jer31:13); [JHN 16:20](https://ref.ly/John16:20)

### Despair turned into hope

**[1TH 4:13–4:14](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:13-1Thess4:14)** *“Falling asleep” was a euphemistic way of talking about dying.*

### Fear turned into assurance

**[PSA 23:4](https://ref.ly/Ps23:4)**; **[ISA 25:8–25:9](https://ref.ly/Isa25:8-Isa25:9)** *In the OT, death was generally feared because it meant the loss of meaningful existence. However, like several references, this one looks beyond the grave and anticipates NT hope for all who trust in God*; **[ROM 8:38–8:39](https://ref.ly/Rom8:38-Rom8:39)** *Paul's conclusion after working through the implications of the gospel.* *See also* [JHN 11:25–11:26](https://ref.ly/John11:25-John11:26); [1CO 15:55–15:57](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:55-1Cor15:57); [2CO 5:6–5:8](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:6-2Cor5:8); [PHP 1:21–1:24](https://ref.ly/Phil1:21-Phil1:24); [REV 21:4](https://ref.ly/Rev21:4)

God's care for widows and orphans

**[DEU 10:18](https://ref.ly/Deut10:18)**; **[PSA 10:14](https://ref.ly/Ps10:14)** *See also* [PSA 68:5](https://ref.ly/Ps68:5), [PSA 146:9](https://ref.ly/Ps146:9); [JER 49:11](https://ref.ly/Jer49:11); [HOS 14:3](https://ref.ly/Hos14:3)

Jesus Christ cared for the bereaved
-----------------------------------

**[LUK 7:11–7:13](https://ref.ly/Luke7:11-Luke7:13)**; **[JHN 11:33](https://ref.ly/John11:33)** *At the grave of his friend, Lazarus.* *See also* [LUK 8:49–8:56](https://ref.ly/Luke8:49-Luke8:56)

Caring for the bereaved
