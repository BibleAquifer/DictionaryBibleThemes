## 4669: locust (id: 4669)

The most destructive of all biblical insects, also used figuratively as a devouring pest.

There was no defence against locusts
------------------------------------

**[JOL 2:4–2:5](https://ref.ly/Joel2:4-Joel2:5)** *See also* [PRO 30:27](https://ref.ly/Prov30:27)

Locusts were frequently a sign of God's judgment
------------------------------------------------

**[DEU 28:38](https://ref.ly/Deut28:38)**; **[AMO 4:9](https://ref.ly/Amos4:9)** [EXO 10:4](https://ref.ly/Exod10:4), [EXO 10:12–10:14](https://ref.ly/Exod10:12-Exod10:14), [EXO 10:19](https://ref.ly/Exod10:19); [DEU 28:42](https://ref.ly/Deut28:42) *curses for disobedience*; ; [1KI 8:37](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:37) parallel [2CH 6:28](https://ref.ly/2Chr6:28); ; [2CH 7:13–7:14](https://ref.ly/2Chr7:13-2Chr7:14); ; [PSA 78:46](https://ref.ly/Ps78:46), [PSA 105:34](https://ref.ly/Ps105:34); ; [JOL 1:4](https://ref.ly/Joel1:4), [JOL 2:25](https://ref.ly/Joel2:25); ; [AMO 7:1](https://ref.ly/Amos7:1)

Locusts were a source of food
-----------------------------

**[LEV 11:22](https://ref.ly/Lev11:22)** *See also* [MAT 3:4](https://ref.ly/Matt3:4) parallel [MRK 1:6](https://ref.ly/Mark1:6)

Locusts used figuratively
-------------------------

### Of numerous and destructive invaders

**[JDG 7:12](https://ref.ly/Judg7:12)**; **[ISA 33:4](https://ref.ly/Isa33:4)** *See also* [JDG 6:5](https://ref.ly/Judg6:5); [JER 46:23](https://ref.ly/Jer46:23), [JER 51:14](https://ref.ly/Jer51:14), [JER 51:27](https://ref.ly/Jer51:27); [NAM 3:15–3:17](https://ref.ly/Nah3:15-Nah3:17)

### Of terrifying end\-time activity

**[REV 9:3](https://ref.ly/Rev9:3)** *See also* [REV 9:7](https://ref.ly/Rev9:7)
