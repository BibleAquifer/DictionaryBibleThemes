## 2333: Jesus Christ, attitude to OT (id: 2333)

Jesus Christ's teaching was based largely on the OT, which he treated as God's inspired and authoritative word, but which he had the authority to interpret.

Jesus Christ appeals to the OT in its entirety
----------------------------------------------

**[LUK 11:50–11:51](https://ref.ly/Luke11:50-Luke11:51)** *The murders of Abel and Zechariah occur in the first ([Ge 4:8](https://ref.ly/Gen4:8)) and last ([2Ch 24:21](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:21)) books of the Hebrew OT (Chronicles comes at the close of the OT according to the Hebrew arrangement).* *See also* [LUK 24:27](https://ref.ly/Luke24:27), [LUK 24:44](https://ref.ly/Luke24:44) *The Law, the Prophets and the Psalms are the three divisions of the Hebrew OT.*
