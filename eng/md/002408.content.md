## 8783: neglect (id: 8783)

The shirking of one's duties or responsibilities, whether to God or to other people. The consequences of such neglect are often serious.

The Israelites sometimes neglected their religious duties
---------------------------------------------------------

### They neglected to support the Levites

**[DEU 14:27](https://ref.ly/Deut14:27)** *See also* [DEU 12:19](https://ref.ly/Deut12:19); [NEH 13:10](https://ref.ly/Neh13:10)

### They neglected the temple

**[NEH 13:11](https://ref.ly/Neh13:11)** *See also* [2CH 24:5](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:5); [MAL 1:6–1:14](https://ref.ly/Mal1:6-Mal1:14)

### Such neglect earned them the judgment of God

**[2CH 29:6–29:9](https://ref.ly/2Chr29:6-2Chr29:9)**
