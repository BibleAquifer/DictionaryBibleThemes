## 5504: rights (id: 5504)

The basis of a claim to respect, and just and fair treatment. God has rights over his creation as a result of his sovereignty and authority. Through being the height of his creation, human beings also have rights, which are to be respected.

The rights that God gives
-------------------------

### God gives rights to the poor and needy

**[EXO 22:22](https://ref.ly/Exod22:22)** *See also* [EXO 22:25–22:27](https://ref.ly/Exod22:25-Exod22:27); [LEV 19:9–19:10](https://ref.ly/Lev19:9-Lev19:10); [JOB 36:6](https://ref.ly/Job36:6)

### God gives rights to slaves

**[LEV 22:11](https://ref.ly/Lev22:11)** *See also* [EXO 21:2–21:6](https://ref.ly/Exod21:2-Exod21:6) parallel [DEU 15:12–15:18](https://ref.ly/Deut15:12-Deut15:18); [EXO 21:7–21:11](https://ref.ly/Exod21:7-Exod21:11), [EXO 21:26–21:27](https://ref.ly/Exod21:26-Exod21:27)
