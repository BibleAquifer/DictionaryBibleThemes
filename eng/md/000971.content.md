## 5389: lending (id: 5389)

Freely giving the temporary use of property or possessions at no personal advantage. Scripture lays down conditions for lending and emphasises the responsibilities that it brings.

The motivation for lending
--------------------------

### Generosity

**[PSA 37:25–37:26](https://ref.ly/Ps37:25-Ps37:26)** *See also* [DEU 15:11](https://ref.ly/Deut15:11); [PRO 3:27–3:28](https://ref.ly/Prov3:27-Prov3:28); [MAT 5:42](https://ref.ly/Matt5:42)

### Pity

**[DEU 15:7–15:8](https://ref.ly/Deut15:7-Deut15:8)** *See also* [LEV 25:35–25:36](https://ref.ly/Lev25:35-Lev25:36); [MRK 14:7](https://ref.ly/Mark14:7)

### Not necessarily expecting repayment

[LUK 6:34–6:36](https://ref.ly/Luke6:34-Luke6:36)
