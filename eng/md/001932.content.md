## 7773: prophets, role of (id: 7773)

As agents of God, prophets acted in various capacities, as was required of them, in order to carry out his will and fulfil his purpose.

Prophets as God's spokesmen
---------------------------

### They heralded impending judgment

**[JON 3:4](https://ref.ly/Jonah3:4)** *See also* [1KI 16:7](https://ref.ly/1Kgs16:7); [2KI 22:15–22:17](https://ref.ly/2Kgs22:15-2Kgs22:17) parallel [2CH 34:23–34:25](https://ref.ly/2Chr34:23-2Chr34:25); [JER 1:14–1:16](https://ref.ly/Jer1:14-Jer1:16), [JER 5:14–5:17](https://ref.ly/Jer5:14-Jer5:17); [EZK 5:8–5:12](https://ref.ly/Ezek5:8-Ezek5:12), [EZK 38:17](https://ref.ly/Ezek38:17)

**[2KI 17:13](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:13)** *See also* [2CH 24:19](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:19); [JER 3:12–3:13](https://ref.ly/Jer3:12-Jer3:13), [JER 35:15](https://ref.ly/Jer35:15), [JER 44:4](https://ref.ly/Jer44:4); [ZEC 1:3–1:6](https://ref.ly/Zech1:3-Zech1:6)

**[EZK 25:8–25:11](https://ref.ly/Ezek25:8-Ezek25:11)** *See also* [JER 46:13](https://ref.ly/Jer46:13), [JER 47:1](https://ref.ly/Jer47:1), [JER 49:34](https://ref.ly/Jer49:34), [JER 50:1](https://ref.ly/Jer50:1); [EZK 25:2](https://ref.ly/Ezek25:2), [EZK 25:12–25:14](https://ref.ly/Ezek25:12-Ezek25:14), [EZK 26:3](https://ref.ly/Ezek26:3); [AMO 1:9](https://ref.ly/Amos1:9); [EZK 29:2](https://ref.ly/Ezek29:2); [AMO 1:3](https://ref.ly/Amos1:3), [AMO 1:6](https://ref.ly/Amos1:6), [AMO 1:13](https://ref.ly/Amos1:13), [AMO 2:1](https://ref.ly/Amos2:1)
