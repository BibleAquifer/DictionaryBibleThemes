## 2377: kingdom of God, entry into (id: 2377)

Entering or inheriting the kingdom of God is the privilege of those who acknowledge and live by the rule of God and have become part of the new order of salvation and righteousness in Christ.

Entry into the kingdom of God is of vital importance
----------------------------------------------------

### It is costly

**[MAT 13:44](https://ref.ly/Matt13:44)**; **[ACT 14:22](https://ref.ly/Acts14:22)** *See also* [MAT 8:19–8:20](https://ref.ly/Matt8:19-Matt8:20) parallel [LUK 9:57–9:58](https://ref.ly/Luke9:57-Luke9:58); [MAT 13:45–13:46](https://ref.ly/Matt13:45-Matt13:46); [LUK 18:29–18:30](https://ref.ly/Luke18:29-Luke18:30); [2TH 1:5](https://ref.ly/2Thess1:5); [REV 1:9](https://ref.ly/Rev1:9)

### It is a matter of urgency

**[LUK 9:59–9:62](https://ref.ly/Luke9:59-Luke9:62)** parallel [MAT 8:21–8:22](https://ref.ly/Matt8:21-Matt8:22)

Conditions of entry into the kingdom of God
-------------------------------------------

### Childlike trust

**[MRK 10:15](https://ref.ly/Mark10:15)** parallel [LUK 18:17](https://ref.ly/Luke18:17) *See also* [MAT 18:3](https://ref.ly/Matt18:3)

### To be born again of God's Spirit

**[JHN 3:3](https://ref.ly/John3:3)** *See also* [JHN 3:5](https://ref.ly/John3:5); [1CO 15:50](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:50)

### Obedience to God's will

**[MAT 7:21](https://ref.ly/Matt7:21)**

Warnings about entry into the kingdom of God
--------------------------------------------

### The way is narrow

**[LUK 13:24–13:28](https://ref.ly/Luke13:24-Luke13:28)** *See also* [MAT 7:13–7:14](https://ref.ly/Matt7:13-Matt7:14), [MAT 23:13](https://ref.ly/Matt23:13)

### The wicked will not inherit the kingdom

**[1CO 6:9–6:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:9-1Cor6:10)** *See also* [MAT 5:20](https://ref.ly/Matt5:20); [MRK 9:43–9:47](https://ref.ly/Mark9:43-Mark9:47); [GAL 5:19–5:21](https://ref.ly/Gal5:19-Gal5:21); [EPH 5:5](https://ref.ly/Eph5:5)

### The need for readiness and watchfulness

**[MAT 24:42–24:44](https://ref.ly/Matt24:42-Matt24:44)** *See also* [MAT 24:37–24:39](https://ref.ly/Matt24:37-Matt24:39) parallel [LUK 17:26–17:27](https://ref.ly/Luke17:26-Luke17:27); [MAT 25:13](https://ref.ly/Matt25:13); [LUK 12:35–12:40](https://ref.ly/Luke12:35-Luke12:40)

### Entry is not based on outward appearances nor granted to all who claim to know the Lord

**[MAT 7:21–7:23](https://ref.ly/Matt7:21-Matt7:23)** *See also* [MAT 13:24–13:30](https://ref.ly/Matt13:24-Matt13:30), [MAT 13:47–13:50](https://ref.ly/Matt13:47-Matt13:50); [LUK 13:25–13:27](https://ref.ly/Luke13:25-Luke13:27)

The kingdom of God is a kingdom of grace
----------------------------------------

### It belongs to those qualified by God

**[COL 1:12–1:13](https://ref.ly/Col1:12-Col1:13)** *See also* [LUK 12:32](https://ref.ly/Luke12:32), [LUK 22:29–22:30](https://ref.ly/Luke22:29-Luke22:30)

### It belongs to the poor and the poor in spirit

**[LUK 6:20](https://ref.ly/Luke6:20)** parallel [MAT 5:3](https://ref.ly/Matt5:3) *See also* [MAT 11:5](https://ref.ly/Matt11:5) parallel [LUK 7:22](https://ref.ly/Luke7:22); [MAT 19:23–19:24](https://ref.ly/Matt19:23-Matt19:24) parallels [MRK 10:23–10:25](https://ref.ly/Mark10:23-Mark10:25), [LUK 18:24–18:25](https://ref.ly/Luke18:24-Luke18:25) *It is hard for the rich to enter the kingdom of God*; [JAS 2:5](https://ref.ly/Jas2:5), [JAS 5:1](https://ref.ly/Jas5:1)

### It belongs to the childlike

**[MAT 19:14](https://ref.ly/Matt19:14)** parallels [MRK 10:14](https://ref.ly/Mark10:14), [LUK 18:16](https://ref.ly/Luke18:16)

### It belongs to sinners

**[MRK 2:17](https://ref.ly/Mark2:17)** parallels [MAT 9:12–9:13](https://ref.ly/Matt9:12-Matt9:13), [LUK 5:31–5:32](https://ref.ly/Luke5:31-Luke5:32) *See also* [MAT 21:31](https://ref.ly/Matt21:31)

### It belongs to those who are persecuted for Jesus Christ's sake

**[MAT 5:10](https://ref.ly/Matt5:10)** parallel [LUK 6:22–6:23](https://ref.ly/Luke6:22-Luke6:23)

### It belongs to Gentiles as well as to Jews

**[MAT 8:11](https://ref.ly/Matt8:11)** *See also* [MAT 21:43](https://ref.ly/Matt21:43), [MAT 22:8–22:10](https://ref.ly/Matt22:8-Matt22:10) *those initially uninvited represent the Gentiles*; [LUK 14:21–14:24](https://ref.ly/Luke14:21-Luke14:24)
