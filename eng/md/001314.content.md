## 5792: appetite (id: 5792)

A hunger or desire for something. Although the primary reference of the term is to a hunger for food, it can also refer to other human desires. Scripture distinguishes between healthy and unhealthy appetites, indicating what it is appropriate to seek after.

Appetite as physical hunger
---------------------------

**[NUM 11:6](https://ref.ly/Num11:6)**; **[PRO 6:30](https://ref.ly/Prov6:30)** *See also* [PSA 107:5–107:9](https://ref.ly/Ps107:5-Ps107:9); [PRO 16:26](https://ref.ly/Prov16:26); [ECC 6:7](https://ref.ly/Eccl6:7); [ISA 29:8](https://ref.ly/Isa29:8); [JER 31:25](https://ref.ly/Jer31:25), [JER 50:19](https://ref.ly/Jer50:19); [LAM 2:12](https://ref.ly/Lam2:12)
