## 8326: purity, moral and spiritual (id: 8326)

Moral and spiritual purity is demanded by God of his people. God calls his people to be holy, just as he is holy.

Divine purity
-------------

### The purity of God

**[HAB 1:13](https://ref.ly/Hab1:13)** *See also* [JOB 4:17](https://ref.ly/Job4:17); [PSA 18:26](https://ref.ly/Ps18:26); [1JN 1:5](https://ref.ly/1John1:5)

**[1JN 3:3](https://ref.ly/1John3:3)** *See also* [MAT 17:2](https://ref.ly/Matt17:2); [HEB 7:26](https://ref.ly/Heb7:26)
