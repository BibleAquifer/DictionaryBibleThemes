## 8835: unbelief, nature and effects of (id: 8835)

Unbelief, or the failure to trust God, holds back his blessing and leads to condemnation and spiritual death.

The nature of unbelief
----------------------

### Not trusting God

**[PSA 78:22](https://ref.ly/Ps78:22)**

### Rebelling against God

**[HEB 3:12](https://ref.ly/Heb3:12)** *See also* [PSA 78:40](https://ref.ly/Ps78:40); [HEB 3:16](https://ref.ly/Heb3:16)

### Disobeying God

**[HEB 11:31](https://ref.ly/Heb11:31)** *NIV footnote.* *See also* [EPH 2:2](https://ref.ly/Eph2:2); [HEB 3:18–3:19](https://ref.ly/Heb3:18-Heb3:19), [HEB 4:11](https://ref.ly/Heb4:11)

### Questioning God's truthfulness

**[1JN 5:10](https://ref.ly/1John5:10)** *See also* [PSA 106:24](https://ref.ly/Ps106:24); [1JN 1:10](https://ref.ly/1John1:10)

Unbelief as the result of the devil's work
------------------------------------------

### Taking away God's word

**[LUK 8:12](https://ref.ly/Luke8:12)** parallels [MAT 13:19](https://ref.ly/Matt13:19), [MRK 4:15](https://ref.ly/Mark4:15)

### Causing spiritual blindness

**[2CO 4:4](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:4)** *See also* [ROM 1:21](https://ref.ly/Rom1:21); [2CO 4:3](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:3); [EPH 4:18](https://ref.ly/Eph4:18)

The consequences of persistent unbelief
---------------------------------------

**[HEB 3:19](https://ref.ly/Heb3:19)** *The “rest” that God's people enjoyed on entering the promised land is here taken to symbolise the final reward prepared by God for his people.* *See also* [HEB 4:1–4:2](https://ref.ly/Heb4:1-Heb4:2), [HEB 4:6](https://ref.ly/Heb4:6); [JUD 1:5](https://ref.ly/Jude1:5)

### Exclusion from the people of God

**[ROM 11:20](https://ref.ly/Rom11:20)** *See also* [HEB 6:4–6:6](https://ref.ly/Heb6:4-Heb6:6)

### Death in sin

**[JHN 8:24](https://ref.ly/John8:24)** *See also* [PSA 106:43](https://ref.ly/Ps106:43); [MRK 4:12](https://ref.ly/Mark4:12); [ACT 10:43](https://ref.ly/Acts10:43)
