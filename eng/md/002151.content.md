## 8322: perfection, human (id: 8322)

Human wholeness in the image of God, given at creation and lost in the fall, is to be fully restored in Jesus Christ.

Humanity was created perfect
----------------------------

**[GEN 1:26–1:27](https://ref.ly/Gen1:26-Gen1:27)** *See also* [GEN 5:1](https://ref.ly/Gen5:1); [EZK 28:12–28:15](https://ref.ly/Ezek28:12-Ezek28:15) *The king of Tyre is described in terms of the creation and fall of humanity*; [JAS 3:9](https://ref.ly/Jas3:9)

Perfection was lost through sin
-------------------------------

**[ROM 3:23](https://ref.ly/Rom3:23)** *See also* [GEN 2:17](https://ref.ly/Gen2:17), [GEN 3:16–3:19](https://ref.ly/Gen3:16-Gen3:19), [GEN 3:23](https://ref.ly/Gen3:23); [JOB 15:14–15:16](https://ref.ly/Job15:14-Job15:16); [EZK 28:15–28:17](https://ref.ly/Ezek28:15-Ezek28:17); [ROM 1:21](https://ref.ly/Rom1:21)
