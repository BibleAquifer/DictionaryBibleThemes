## 8438: giving, of time (id: 8438)

People are stewards of a deposit of time given by God, and will be held responsible for the way in which they use it.

Time is governed by God
-----------------------

### God determines every lifespan

**[JOB 14:5](https://ref.ly/Job14:5)** *See also* [PSA 31:15](https://ref.ly/Ps31:15), [PSA 74:16–74:17](https://ref.ly/Ps74:16-Ps74:17), [PSA 139:16](https://ref.ly/Ps139:16); [ACT 17:26](https://ref.ly/Acts17:26)

### God will demand an account of how time has been spent

**[ECC 3:17](https://ref.ly/Eccl3:17)** *See also* [MAT 12:36](https://ref.ly/Matt12:36); [1PE 4:3–4:5](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:3-1Pet4:5)

Time may be squandered
----------------------

### In ungodly living

**[1PE 4:3](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:3)** *See also* [JOL 2:25](https://ref.ly/Joel2:25); [EPH 2:2–2:3](https://ref.ly/Eph2:2-Eph2:3); [COL 3:5–3:7](https://ref.ly/Col3:5-Col3:7)

### By neglecting God\-given opportunities

**[JER 8:20](https://ref.ly/Jer8:20)** *See also* [PRO 20:4](https://ref.ly/Prov20:4); [MAT 25:11–25:12](https://ref.ly/Matt25:11-Matt25:12); [LUK 13:25](https://ref.ly/Luke13:25), [LUK 19:44](https://ref.ly/Luke19:44); [ACT 24:25](https://ref.ly/Acts24:25); [REV 2:21](https://ref.ly/Rev2:21)

Time may be saved
-----------------

### By understanding its brevity

**[PSA 90:12](https://ref.ly/Ps90:12)** *See also* [JHN 12:35](https://ref.ly/John12:35); [1CO 7:29–7:31](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:29-1Cor7:31)

### By remembering its uncertainty

**[JHN 9:4](https://ref.ly/John9:4)** *See also* [ECC 8:5–8:7](https://ref.ly/Eccl8:5-Eccl8:7); [JAS 4:13–4:15](https://ref.ly/Jas4:13-Jas4:15)

### By making the most of present opportunities

**[EPH 5:16](https://ref.ly/Eph5:16)** *See also* [PSA 32:6](https://ref.ly/Ps32:6); [ECC 12:1](https://ref.ly/Eccl12:1); [EST 4:14](https://ref.ly/Esth4:14); [MAT 4:17](https://ref.ly/Matt4:17); [MRK 1:15](https://ref.ly/Mark1:15); [2CO 6:1–6:2](https://ref.ly/2Cor6:1-2Cor6:2); [GAL 6:10](https://ref.ly/Gal6:10); [COL 4:5](https://ref.ly/Col4:5); [HEB 3:7–3:13](https://ref.ly/Heb3:7-Heb3:13)

### By allocating it wisely

**[1CH 12:32](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:32)** *See also* [EXO 18:13–18:26](https://ref.ly/Exod18:13-Exod18:26); [ACT 6:1–6:4](https://ref.ly/Acts6:1-Acts6:4)
