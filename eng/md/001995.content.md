## 8027: faith, testing of (id: 8027)

The means through which the genuineness of faith is proved and Christian character developed. God promises to help his people during times of testing.

God allows faith to be tested
-----------------------------

### Testing proves the genuineness of faith

**[1PE 1:6–1:7](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:6-1Pet1:7)** *See also* [MAT 13:20–13:21](https://ref.ly/Matt13:20-Matt13:21) parallels [MRK 4:16–4:17](https://ref.ly/Mark4:16-Mark4:17), [LUK 8:13](https://ref.ly/Luke8:13)

### Testing develops Christian character

**[JAS 1:2–1:4](https://ref.ly/Jas1:2-Jas1:4)** *See also* [ROM 5:3–5:4](https://ref.ly/Rom5:3-Rom5:4)

### Testing purifies God's people

**[ISA 48:10](https://ref.ly/Isa48:10)** *See also* [PSA 66:10–66:12](https://ref.ly/Ps66:10-Ps66:12); [JER 9:7](https://ref.ly/Jer9:7); [ZEC 13:9](https://ref.ly/Zech13:9); [MAL 3:2–3:3](https://ref.ly/Mal3:2-Mal3:3); [1PE 4:17](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:17)

### Examples of God testing faith

Abraham is told to sacrifice Isaac: [GEN 22:1–22:2](https://ref.ly/Gen22:1-Gen22:2); ; [HEB 11:17–11:19](https://ref.ly/Heb11:17-Heb11:19); [DEU 8:2–8:5](https://ref.ly/Deut8:2-Deut8:5) *God tested Israel in the desert*; ; [JDG 7:1–7:8](https://ref.ly/Judg7:1-Judg7:8) *God reduces Gideon's army*; ; [MAT 15:21–15:28](https://ref.ly/Matt15:21-Matt15:28) parallel [MRK 7:24–7:30](https://ref.ly/Mark7:24-Mark7:30) *Jesus Christ appears to discourage the Canaanite woman*; ; [JHN 11:1–11:6](https://ref.ly/John11:1-John11:6) *Jesus Christ delays going to Bethany when Lazarus is ill.*
