## 4836: light, and people of God (id: 4836)

Scripture often uses light as a symbol of the people of God, and especially the manner in which believers are able and required to reflect the glory of God in a dark world of sin.

The people of God as the light of the world
-------------------------------------------

**[MAT 5:14–5:16](https://ref.ly/Matt5:14-Matt5:16)** *See also* [PSA 37:5–37:6](https://ref.ly/Ps37:5-Ps37:6); [PRO 4:18](https://ref.ly/Prov4:18), [PRO 13:9](https://ref.ly/Prov13:9); [ISA 58:8–58:10](https://ref.ly/Isa58:8-Isa58:10), [ISA 60:1–60:5](https://ref.ly/Isa60:1-Isa60:5), [ISA 62:1](https://ref.ly/Isa62:1); [DAN 12:3](https://ref.ly/Dan12:3); [LUK 2:32](https://ref.ly/Luke2:32); [ACT 13:47](https://ref.ly/Acts13:47)

Moses on Mount Sinai: [EXO 34:29–34:30](https://ref.ly/Exod34:29-Exod34:30), [EXO 34:35](https://ref.ly/Exod34:35); [2SA 23:3–23:4](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:3-2Sam23:4) *the righteous ruler, fully realised in Jesus Christ*; ; [JOB 29:21–29:25](https://ref.ly/Job29:21-Job29:25) *Job as a counsellor*; ; [JHN 5:35](https://ref.ly/John5:35) *John the Baptist*; ; [ACT 6:15](https://ref.ly/Acts6:15) *Stephen*; ; [PHP 2:15](https://ref.ly/Phil2:15) *the Philippian church*
