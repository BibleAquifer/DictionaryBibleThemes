## 5839: dissatisfaction (id: 5839)

The human feeling of emptiness and unfulfilment, at either a physical or spiritual level.

Hunger and thirst are never permanently satisfied
-------------------------------------------------

Dissatisfaction as a result of divine judgment
----------------------------------------------
