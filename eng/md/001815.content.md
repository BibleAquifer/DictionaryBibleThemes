## 7398: New Moon festival (id: 7398)

The festival which marked the consecration to God of each new month in the year.

New Moon festivals were required under the Mosaic law
-----------------------------------------------------

**[NUM 28:11](https://ref.ly/Num28:11)**

New Moon festivals were times of celebration
--------------------------------------------

### They were marked by blowing of trumpets

**[NUM 10:10](https://ref.ly/Num10:10)** *See also* [PSA 81:3](https://ref.ly/Ps81:3)

### They were celebrated with offerings

[NUM 28:11–28:15](https://ref.ly/Num28:11-Num28:15); ; [1CH 23:30–23:31](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:30-1Chr23:31); ; [2CH 2:4](https://ref.ly/2Chr2:4), [2CH 8:12–8:13](https://ref.ly/2Chr8:12-2Chr8:13), [2CH 31:3](https://ref.ly/2Chr31:3) *reformed temple worship under Hezekiah*; ; [NEH 10:32–10:33](https://ref.ly/Neh10:32-Neh10:33) *New Moon festivals were restored on the return from exile*; ; [EZK 45:17](https://ref.ly/Ezek45:17), [EZK 46:1](https://ref.ly/Ezek46:1), [EZK 46:3](https://ref.ly/Ezek46:3), [EZK 46:6](https://ref.ly/Ezek46:6)

### They were times when normal work ceased

David was to dine with Saul: [1SA 20:5](https://ref.ly/1Sam20:5), [1SA 20:18](https://ref.ly/1Sam20:18), [1SA 20:24](https://ref.ly/1Sam20:24); [2KI 4:23](https://ref.ly/2Kgs4:23) *the Shunammite woman and her husband*; ; [AMO 8:5](https://ref.ly/Amos8:5) *Some saw them as a hindrance to business.*

A wrong inner attitude at New Moon celebrations incurs God's anger
