## 5791: anger, human (id: 5791)

Human anger arises for a number of reasons, some of which are acceptable and others which are not. Scripture stresses the potentially destructive aspects of human anger and urges moderation.

Reasons for human anger
-----------------------

### Jealousy

**[GEN 4:4–4:5](https://ref.ly/Gen4:4-Gen4:5)** *See also* [1SA 17:28](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:28), [1SA 18:8](https://ref.ly/1Sam18:8); [1KI 21:4](https://ref.ly/1Kgs21:4); [LUK 15:28](https://ref.ly/Luke15:28)

### Pride

**[JON 4:1](https://ref.ly/Jonah4:1)** *See also* [2SA 3:8](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:8), [2SA 6:8](https://ref.ly/2Sam6:8); [2KI 5:11](https://ref.ly/2Kgs5:11); [2CH 26:19](https://ref.ly/2Chr26:19)

**[2CH 16:10](https://ref.ly/2Chr16:10)** *Hanani had prophesied against the king.* *See also* [NUM 24:10](https://ref.ly/Num24:10); [JER 37:15](https://ref.ly/Jer37:15)
