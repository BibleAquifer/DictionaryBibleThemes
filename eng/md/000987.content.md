## 5406: mercenaries (id: 5406)

Soldiers who offers their services for money, irrespective of the nationality of the army in question.

Mercenaries in the Egyptian army
--------------------------------

**[JER 46:20–46:21](https://ref.ly/Jer46:20-Jer46:21)** *These mercenaries were drawn from Cush to the south of Egypt, Put to the west of Egypt and Lydia in north Africa.*
