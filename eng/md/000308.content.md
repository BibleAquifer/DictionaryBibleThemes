## 4027: world, as fallen (id: 4027)

The world has fallen into sin and rebellion against its Creator. As a result, it has come under the power of Satan.

-------------------------------------

### He is prince of this world

**[JHN 14:30](https://ref.ly/John14:30)** *See also* [JHN 12:31](https://ref.ly/John12:31); [2CO 4:4](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:4); [1JN 4:4](https://ref.ly/1John4:4)

**[1JN 5:19](https://ref.ly/1John5:19)** *See also* [EPH 2:1–2:4](https://ref.ly/Eph2:1-Eph2:4)
