## 8824: self-righteousness, nature of (id: 8824)

Self\-righteousness fosters the sinful human illusions of sinlessness, sufficiency and freedom.

The claim to self\-righteousness
--------------------------------

**[ROM 10:3](https://ref.ly/Rom10:3)** *See also* [LUK 10:29](https://ref.ly/Luke10:29), [LUK 18:9](https://ref.ly/Luke18:9); [PHP 3:4–3:6](https://ref.ly/Phil3:4-Phil3:6)

The illusion of self\-righteousness
-----------------------------------

### That people are sinless

**[1JN 1:8](https://ref.ly/1John1:8)** *See also* [PRO 20:9](https://ref.ly/Prov20:9); [HOS 12:8](https://ref.ly/Hos12:8)

### That people are guiltless

**[PRO 16:2](https://ref.ly/Prov16:2)** *See also* [JER 2:34–2:35](https://ref.ly/Jer2:34-Jer2:35)

### That people are deserving

**[DEU 9:4](https://ref.ly/Deut9:4)** *See also* [EZK 28:2](https://ref.ly/Ezek28:2)

The results of self\-righteousness
----------------------------------

### Self\-deception

**[1JN 1:5–1:6](https://ref.ly/1John1:5-1John1:6)** *See also* [PRO 30:12](https://ref.ly/Prov30:12); [JER 17:9](https://ref.ly/Jer17:9); [MAT 23:23–23:24](https://ref.ly/Matt23:23-Matt23:24), [MAT 23:27–23:28](https://ref.ly/Matt23:27-Matt23:28); [LUK 16:15](https://ref.ly/Luke16:15); [HEB 3:13](https://ref.ly/Heb3:13)
