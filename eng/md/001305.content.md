## 5782: agony (id: 5782)

Intense suffering of body, mind or spirit, brought about by illness, death or the judgment of God.

-----------------

### Agony of body

**[PSA 6:2](https://ref.ly/Ps6:2)** *See also* [PSA 31:10](https://ref.ly/Ps31:10), [PSA 42:10](https://ref.ly/Ps42:10); [ISA 21:3](https://ref.ly/Isa21:3); [JHN 16:21](https://ref.ly/John16:21); [REV 9:5](https://ref.ly/Rev9:5), [REV 16:10](https://ref.ly/Rev16:10)

### Agony of heart
