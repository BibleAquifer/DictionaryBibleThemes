## 8430: fasting, nature of (id: 8430)

Scripture frequently refers to individuals fasting and describes the different kinds of fast which may be undertaken.

Kinds of fasting
----------------

### Abstaining from food and drink

**[EZR 10:6](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:6)** *See also* [DAN 9:3](https://ref.ly/Dan9:3); [ACT 23:12](https://ref.ly/Acts23:12)

### Abstaining from food only

**[1SA 1:7](https://ref.ly/1Sam1:7)** *See also* [DAN 6:18](https://ref.ly/Dan6:18)

The duration of fasting
-----------------------

### One day: the normal fast period

[LEV 23:32](https://ref.ly/Lev23:32); ; [JDG 20:26](https://ref.ly/Judg20:26); ; [2SA 1:12](https://ref.ly/2Sam1:12)

### Three days

[EST 4:16](https://ref.ly/Esth4:16); ; [ACT 9:9](https://ref.ly/Acts9:9)

### Seven days

[1SA 31:13](https://ref.ly/1Sam31:13) parallel [1CH 10:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr10:12); ; [2SA 12:16–12:20](https://ref.ly/2Sam12:16-2Sam12:20)

### Forty days

These 40\-day fasts are all in unique circumstances and are not prescribed: [EXO 34:28](https://ref.ly/Exod34:28); ; [1KI 19:8](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:8); ; [MAT 4:2](https://ref.ly/Matt4:2) parallel [LUK 4:2](https://ref.ly/Luke4:2)

Regular observances of fasting
------------------------------

### Those prescribed by the Law

**[LEV 16:29–16:31](https://ref.ly/Lev16:29-Lev16:31)** *NIV footnote at verse 29\.* *See also* [LEV 23:26–23:32](https://ref.ly/Lev23:26-Lev23:32); [NUM 29:7](https://ref.ly/Num29:7); [ACT 27:9](https://ref.ly/Acts27:9)

### Those observed in Jewish tradition

**[ZEC 8:19](https://ref.ly/Zech8:19)** *See also* [EST 9:30–9:31](https://ref.ly/Esth9:30-Esth9:31); [ZEC 7:2–7:5](https://ref.ly/Zech7:2-Zech7:5)

### Those observed by the Pharisees

[LUK 18:11–18:12](https://ref.ly/Luke18:11-Luke18:12)

Involuntary fasts
-----------------

### Those imposed by decree

**[2CH 20:3](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:3)** *See also* [1SA 14:241](https://ref.ly/1Sam14:241)KI [13:8–13:9](https://ref.ly/1Sam13:8-1Sam13:9), [1KI 13:16](https://ref.ly/1Kgs13:16)[1KI 21:9](https://ref.ly/1Kgs21:9); [JER 36:9](https://ref.ly/Jer36:9)

### Those imposed by circumstances

[ACT 27:33](https://ref.ly/Acts27:33); ; [2CO 6:5](https://ref.ly/2Cor6:5), [2CO 11:27](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:27)
