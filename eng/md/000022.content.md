## 1105: God, power of (id: 1105)

God is all\-powerful (that is omnipotent), and is able to do whatever he wills. His power is limited only by his character.

God is all\-powerful
--------------------

**[MRK 10:27](https://ref.ly/Mark10:27)** parallel [MAT 19:26](https://ref.ly/Matt19:26) *See also* [GEN 18:14](https://ref.ly/Gen18:14); [PSA 93:1](https://ref.ly/Ps93:1); [MRK 14:36](https://ref.ly/Mark14:36); [LUK 1:37](https://ref.ly/Luke1:37); [2CO 4:7](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:7)
