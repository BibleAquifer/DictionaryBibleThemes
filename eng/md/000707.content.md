## 5113: Peter, the disciple (id: 5113)

The son of Jonas or John, he was called Simon until Jesus Christ gave him the name Peter meaning “rock”.

Peter's background and family
-----------------------------

### He lived in Capernaum

[MAT 8:14](https://ref.ly/Matt8:14) parallels [MRK 1:29](https://ref.ly/Mark1:29), [LUK 4:38](https://ref.ly/Luke4:38); ; [JHN 1:44](https://ref.ly/John1:44) *He originally came from Bethsaida.*

### He was a fisherman by trade

[LUK 5:10](https://ref.ly/Luke5:10)

Peter as Jesus Christ's disciple
--------------------------------

### He was called by Jesus Christ

[MAT 4:18–4:20](https://ref.ly/Matt4:18-Matt4:20) parallel [MRK 1:16–1:20](https://ref.ly/Mark1:16-Mark1:20); ; [LUK 5:10](https://ref.ly/Luke5:10); ; [JHN 1:40–1:42](https://ref.ly/John1:40-John1:42)

### He witnessed Jesus Christ perform many miracles

[MAT 8:14–8:15](https://ref.ly/Matt8:14-Matt8:15) parallels [MRK 1:30–1:31](https://ref.ly/Mark1:30-Mark1:31), [LUK 4:38–4:39](https://ref.ly/Luke4:38-Luke4:39); ; [MAT 17:24–17:27](https://ref.ly/Matt17:24-Matt17:27); ; [LUK 5:4–5:8](https://ref.ly/Luke5:4-Luke5:8); ; [JHN 21:2–21:6](https://ref.ly/John21:2-John21:6)

### He was chosen to be with Jesus Christ on certain occasions

[MAT 17:1–17:2](https://ref.ly/Matt17:1-Matt17:2) parallels [MRK 9:2](https://ref.ly/Mark9:2), [LUK 9:28–9:29](https://ref.ly/Luke9:28-Luke9:29) *at the transfiguration of Jesus Christ*; ; [MAT 26:36–26:37](https://ref.ly/Matt26:36-Matt26:37) parallel [MRK 14:32–14:33](https://ref.ly/Mark14:32-Mark14:33) *in the Garden of Gethsemane*; ; [MRK 5:37](https://ref.ly/Mark5:37) parallel [LUK 8:51](https://ref.ly/Luke8:51)

### He sometimes acted as spokesman for the other disciples

He asked Jesus Christ to teach them: [MAT 15:15](https://ref.ly/Matt15:15), [MAT 18:21](https://ref.ly/Matt18:21); [MAT 16:15–16:16](https://ref.ly/Matt16:15-Matt16:16) parallels [MRK 8:29](https://ref.ly/Mark8:29), [LUK 9:20](https://ref.ly/Luke9:20); ; [JHN 6:69](https://ref.ly/John6:69); [MAT 17:4](https://ref.ly/Matt17:4) parallels [MRK 9:5](https://ref.ly/Mark9:5), [LUK 9:33](https://ref.ly/Luke9:33) *He asked Jesus Christ to prolong the transfiguration experience*; ; [MAT 19:27](https://ref.ly/Matt19:27) parallels [MRK 10:28](https://ref.ly/Mark10:28), [LUK 18:28](https://ref.ly/Luke18:28); ; [MRK 1:36–1:37](https://ref.ly/Mark1:36-Mark1:37); ; [LUK 5:4–5:5](https://ref.ly/Luke5:4-Luke5:5); ; [JHN 6:68](https://ref.ly/John6:68)

### His impetuous nature interacted with his devotion to Jesus Christ

[MAT 14:28–14:29](https://ref.ly/Matt14:28-Matt14:29) *He wanted to walk to Jesus Christ on the water*; ; [MAT 16:22](https://ref.ly/Matt16:22) parallel [MRK 8:32](https://ref.ly/Mark8:32) *He tried to prevent Jesus Christ from going to his death*; ; [MAT 26:35](https://ref.ly/Matt26:35) parallels [MRK 14:31](https://ref.ly/Mark14:31), [LUK 22:33](https://ref.ly/Luke22:33), [JHN 13:37](https://ref.ly/John13:37) *He vowed that he was willing to die for Jesus Christ*; ; [JHN 13:6–13:8](https://ref.ly/John13:6-John13:8), [JHN 18:10](https://ref.ly/John18:10)

### He was the subject of both special commendation and rebuke

[MAT 16:17–16:19](https://ref.ly/Matt16:17-Matt16:19) *He was commended for his confession of faith in Jesus Christ*; ; [MAT 16:23](https://ref.ly/Matt16:23) parallel [MRK 8:33](https://ref.ly/Mark8:33) *He was rebuked for his opposition to Jesus Christ's prediction of his suffering*; ; [MAT 26:34](https://ref.ly/Matt26:34) parallels [MRK 14:30](https://ref.ly/Mark14:30), [LUK 22:34](https://ref.ly/Luke22:34), [JHN 13:38](https://ref.ly/John13:38) *He was warned that he would disown Jesus Christ*; ; [MAT 26:40–26:41](https://ref.ly/Matt26:40-Matt26:41) parallel [MRK 14:37–14:38](https://ref.ly/Mark14:37-Mark14:38) *He was rebuked for falling asleep in the Garden of Gethsemane*; ; [JHN 18:10–18:11](https://ref.ly/John18:10-John18:11), [JHN 21:20–21:22](https://ref.ly/John21:20-John21:22)
