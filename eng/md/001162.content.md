## 5597: victory, as an act of God (id: 5597)

God is the source of all victory, which he grants to those who obey his commands and put their confidence in him. This principle holds true in all believers' conflicts, whether physical or spiritual.

God is the source of all victory
--------------------------------

**[DEU 20:1–20:4](https://ref.ly/Deut20:1-Deut20:4)** *See also* [2CH 20:15](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:15); [PSA 18:35](https://ref.ly/Ps18:35); [1CO 15:57](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:57); [2CO 2:14](https://ref.ly/2Cor2:14)

Victory is achieved by God, not people
--------------------------------------

**[PSA 20:7–20:8](https://ref.ly/Ps20:7-Ps20:8)** *See also* [1SA 17:45–17:47](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:45-1Sam17:47); [PSA 44:3–44:7](https://ref.ly/Ps44:3-Ps44:7), [PSA 60:11–60:12](https://ref.ly/Ps60:11-Ps60:12), [PSA 146:3](https://ref.ly/Ps146:3); [PRO 21:31](https://ref.ly/Prov21:31)

Victory should be ascribed to God
---------------------------------

**[PSA 118:15](https://ref.ly/Ps118:15)** *See also* [EXO 15:1](https://ref.ly/Exod15:1); [PSA 21:1](https://ref.ly/Ps21:1); [1CO 15:57](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:57); [REV 19:1–19:2](https://ref.ly/Rev19:1-Rev19:2)

Victory depends on faithfulness
-------------------------------

### God grants victory to those who are faithful to him

**[1CH 22:13](https://ref.ly/1Chr22:13)** *See also* [EXO 23:20–23:23](https://ref.ly/Exod23:20-Exod23:23); [PSA 112:8](https://ref.ly/Ps112:8); [PRO 2:7](https://ref.ly/Prov2:7)

### Unfaithfulness to God brings defeat

**[NUM 14:41–14:43](https://ref.ly/Num14:41-Num14:43)** *See also* [DEU 28:15](https://ref.ly/Deut28:15), [DEU 28:25](https://ref.ly/Deut28:25); [2CH 24:20](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:20)

Examples of God giving victory
------------------------------

[EXO 17:8–17:15](https://ref.ly/Exod17:8-Exod17:15) *to Israel over the Amalekites*; [JOS 6:2–6:5](https://ref.ly/Josh6:2-Josh6:5), [JOS 6:20](https://ref.ly/Josh6:20); [JDG 7:15–7:22](https://ref.ly/Judg7:15-Judg7:22) *to Gideon over the Midianites*; ; [JDG 15:12–15:19](https://ref.ly/Judg15:12-Judg15:19) *to Samson over 1,000 Philistines*; ; [1SA 7:7–7:12](https://ref.ly/1Sam7:7-1Sam7:12) *to the Israelites at Mizpah*; ; [1SA 17:38–17:50](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:38-1Sam17:50) *to David over Goliath*; ; [1KI 18:36–18:39](https://ref.ly/1Kgs18:36-1Kgs18:39) *to Elijah at Mount Carmel*; ; [1CH 11:4–11:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:4-1Chr11:9) *when David conquered Jerusalem*
