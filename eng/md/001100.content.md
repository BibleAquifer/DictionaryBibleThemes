## 5532: sleep (id: 5532)

A state of slumber or rest, reflecting tiredness and the need for refreshment. Although sleep is a natural physical process, Scripture also uses the term in a number of important spiritual senses.

* **Associated Articles:** 5533: sleep, physical (ID: `5533@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 5534: sleep, spiritual (ID: `5534@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 5535: sleep, and death (ID: `5535@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
