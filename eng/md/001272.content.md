## 5734: relationships (id: 5734)

God created people to be in relationship with himself and with one another in friendship, marriage, family, society and the church.

Human beings are created to be in relationship
----------------------------------------------

**[REV 21:3](https://ref.ly/Rev21:3)** *See also* [ISA 43:6–43:7](https://ref.ly/Isa43:6-Isa43:7); [HOS 11:1](https://ref.ly/Hos11:1); [2CO 6:18](https://ref.ly/2Cor6:18)

**[GEN 2:18](https://ref.ly/Gen2:18)** *See also* [PSA 127:4](https://ref.ly/Ps127:4); [PRO 17:6](https://ref.ly/Prov17:6), [PRO 17:17](https://ref.ly/Prov17:17), [PRO 18:24](https://ref.ly/Prov18:24), [PRO 27:10](https://ref.ly/Prov27:10); [ECC 4:9–4:12](https://ref.ly/Eccl4:9-Eccl4:12)
