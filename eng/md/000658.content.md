## 5052: responsibility, to God (id: 5052)

Even though God is sovereign, all people everywhere are accountable to him for everything they do, say or think.

People are responsible for their own sins
-----------------------------------------

### God is not responsible for human sin

**[DEU 24:16](https://ref.ly/Deut24:16)**; **[EZK 18:4](https://ref.ly/Ezek18:4)** *See also* [JOB 19:4](https://ref.ly/Job19:4); [PSA 51:4–51:5](https://ref.ly/Ps51:4-Ps51:5); [JER 31:30](https://ref.ly/Jer31:30); [EZK 18:20](https://ref.ly/Ezek18:20), [EZK 33:1–33:20](https://ref.ly/Ezek33:1-Ezek33:20); [ROM 6:23](https://ref.ly/Rom6:23)

### Death is the result of sin

**[ROM 5:12](https://ref.ly/Rom5:12), [ROM 6:23](https://ref.ly/Rom6:23)** *See also* [GEN 3:1–3:7](https://ref.ly/Gen3:1-Gen3:7); [ISA 43:27](https://ref.ly/Isa43:27); [EZK 18:4](https://ref.ly/Ezek18:4); [1CO 15:21–15:22](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:21-1Cor15:22)

The law heightens human responsibility for sin
----------------------------------------------

### It brings knowledge of sin

**[ROM 5:20](https://ref.ly/Rom5:20)** *Because the law brings knowledge of sin, it prevents people from claiming ignorance of the nature and seriousness of that sin.* *See also* [ROM 7:7–7:9](https://ref.ly/Rom7:7-Rom7:9); [GAL 3:19](https://ref.ly/Gal3:19)

### It applies not only to actions but also to words and thoughts

**[MAT 5:22](https://ref.ly/Matt5:22), [MAT 5:28](https://ref.ly/Matt5:28)** *See also* [JER 17:9–17:10](https://ref.ly/Jer17:9-Jer17:10); [MAT 15:18–15:19](https://ref.ly/Matt15:18-Matt15:19)

### It brings judgment

**[ECC 12:14](https://ref.ly/Eccl12:14)**; **[MAT 12:36–12:37](https://ref.ly/Matt12:36-Matt12:37)**; **[1CO 4:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor4:5)** *See also* [JOB 15:5–15:6](https://ref.ly/Job15:5-Job15:6); [ROM 2:16](https://ref.ly/Rom2:16), [ROM 14:12](https://ref.ly/Rom14:12); [2CO 5:10](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:10); [1PE 4:5](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:5); [JUD 1:15](https://ref.ly/Jude1:15)

Ignorance does not remove responsibility
----------------------------------------

**[LEV 5:17](https://ref.ly/Lev5:17)** *See also* [LUK 12:48](https://ref.ly/Luke12:48); [ACT 3:17–3:19](https://ref.ly/Acts3:17-Acts3:19); [ROM 1:20](https://ref.ly/Rom1:20)

The responsibility of humanity to repent and believe
----------------------------------------------------

### It is commanded by God

**[ACT 17:30](https://ref.ly/Acts17:30)** *See also* [EZK 18:30](https://ref.ly/Ezek18:30); [MAT 4:17](https://ref.ly/Matt4:17); [MRK 1:15](https://ref.ly/Mark1:15), [MRK 6:12](https://ref.ly/Mark6:12); [LUK 24:46–24:47](https://ref.ly/Luke24:46-Luke24:47)

### Jesus Christ's life and ministry heightens the responsibility to repent

**[JHN 3:18](https://ref.ly/John3:18), [JHN 15:22](https://ref.ly/John15:22)** *See also* [MAT 11:20–11:24](https://ref.ly/Matt11:20-Matt11:24), [MAT 12:41–12:42](https://ref.ly/Matt12:41-Matt12:42) parallel [LUK 11:31–11:32](https://ref.ly/Luke11:31-Luke11:32); [JHN 12:48](https://ref.ly/John12:48)
