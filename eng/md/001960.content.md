## 7923: fellowship, in the gospel (id: 7923)

A mutual participation in the blessings of God's grace. Believers are united with one another on the basis of their common reception of the benefits of salvation.

Fellowship and the community of God's people
--------------------------------------------

### God calls out a community of people, for fellowship with himself

**[DEU 7:6](https://ref.ly/Deut7:6)** *See also* [EXO 19:5–19:6](https://ref.ly/Exod19:5-Exod19:6); [1PE 2:5](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:5), [1PE 2:9–2:11](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:9-1Pet2:11)

### God will bless a people united in fellowship

[MAT 18:19–18:20](https://ref.ly/Matt18:19-Matt18:20); ; [2CH 7:14](https://ref.ly/2Chr7:14); ; [JER 31:23–31:25](https://ref.ly/Jer31:23-Jer31:25); ; [1CO 11:29–11:34](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:29-1Cor11:34) *Wrong relationships between God's people hinder the flow of his blessing.*

### God will restore fellowship with his scattered people

**[MIC 2:12](https://ref.ly/Mic2:12)** *See also* [ISA 11:12–11:13](https://ref.ly/Isa11:12-Isa11:13); [JER 3:18](https://ref.ly/Jer3:18), [JER 31:1](https://ref.ly/Jer31:1), [JER 50:4–50:5](https://ref.ly/Jer50:4-Jer50:5)
