## 4912: chance (id: 4912)

The occurrence of events at random, as undesigned happenings. While people may perceive events as random, in Scripture all events are subject to God's sovereignty.

Chance in human life
--------------------

### Chance events

**[2CH 18:33–18:34](https://ref.ly/2Chr18:33-2Chr18:34)** parallel [1KI 22:34–22:38](https://ref.ly/1Kgs22:34-1Kgs22:38) *See also* [1SA 6:7–6:9](https://ref.ly/1Sam6:7-1Sam6:9); [PRO 26:10](https://ref.ly/Prov26:10)

### Human life seems to be governed by chance, not merit

**[ECC 9:11](https://ref.ly/Eccl9:11)** *See also* [ECC 2:17–2:19](https://ref.ly/Eccl2:17-Eccl2:19)

### Providence at work through apparent chance

**[JON 1:17](https://ref.ly/Jonah1:17)** *See also* [RUT 2:3](https://ref.ly/Ruth2:3); [LUK 23:44–23:45](https://ref.ly/Luke23:44-Luke23:45) parallels [MAT 27:45](https://ref.ly/Matt27:45), [MAT 27:51](https://ref.ly/Matt27:51), [MRK 15:33](https://ref.ly/Mark15:33), [MRK 15:38](https://ref.ly/Mark15:38); [ACT 16:26](https://ref.ly/Acts16:26)

### The casting of lots and the element of chance

### Urim and Thummim, seeming to be dependent on chance

**[EXO 28:29–28:30](https://ref.ly/Exod28:29-Exod28:30)** *The Urim and Thummim were used by the high priest to declare God's will. It was a form of casting lots showing either an affirmative or negative response to an enquiry.* *See also* [NUM 27:21](https://ref.ly/Num27:21); [1SA 28:4–28:6](https://ref.ly/1Sam28:4-1Sam28:6); [EZR 2:62–2:63](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:62-Ezra2:63) parallel [NEH 7:64–7:65](https://ref.ly/Neh7:64-Neh7:65)
