## 6160: fathers, sin of the (id: 6160)

Children may suffer on account of the sins committed by their forebears, just as they may also be blessed for their sake. Nevertheless, Scripture teaches that each individual must take responsibility for his or her own sin.

Children suffer the ill\-effects of their fathers' sins
