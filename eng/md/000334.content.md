## 4127: Satan, defeat of (id: 4127)

Though defeated on Jesus Christ's cross, Satan is still active in this world, but his final overthrow by God is certain.

Satan is defeated by Jesus Christ
---------------------------------

### Jesus Christ is stronger than Satan

**[JHN 14:30](https://ref.ly/John14:30)** *See also* [MAT 12:28](https://ref.ly/Matt12:28); [LUK 13:16](https://ref.ly/Luke13:16); [ACT 10:38](https://ref.ly/Acts10:38); [GAL 1:4](https://ref.ly/Gal1:4)

### Jesus Christ came to destroy Satan's work

**[1JN 3:8](https://ref.ly/1John3:8)** *See also* [HEB 2:14–2:15](https://ref.ly/Heb2:14-Heb2:15)

### Satan is defeated on the cross

**[JHN 12:31](https://ref.ly/John12:31)** *See also* [MAT 25:41](https://ref.ly/Matt25:41); [LUK 10:18](https://ref.ly/Luke10:18); [JHN 16:11](https://ref.ly/John16:11)
