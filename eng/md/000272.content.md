## 3236: Holy Spirit, and Scripture (id: 3236)

The Holy Spirit inspired the original writing of all the Scriptures, and illumines their meaning to believers.

The Holy Spirit inspired the writing of Scripture
-------------------------------------------------

### The Spirit inspired the writers of the OT

**[2SA 23:1–23:2](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:1-2Sam23:2)** *See also* [NEH 9:30](https://ref.ly/Neh9:30); [EZK 2:2](https://ref.ly/Ezek2:2), [EZK 11:24–11:25](https://ref.ly/Ezek11:24-Ezek11:25); [MIC 3:8](https://ref.ly/Mic3:8); [ZEC 7:12](https://ref.ly/Zech7:12)

### The NT recognises OT writings as inspired by the Holy Spirit

**[2TI 3:16](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:16)** *“God\-breathed” means “breathed out by God” rather than “inspired”*; **[2PE 1:20–1:21](https://ref.ly/2Pet1:20-2Pet1:21)** *See also* [MAT 22:43](https://ref.ly/Matt22:43) parallel [MRK 12:36](https://ref.ly/Mark12:36); [ACT 1:16](https://ref.ly/Acts1:16), [ACT 4:25](https://ref.ly/Acts4:25), [ACT 28:25–28:27](https://ref.ly/Acts28:25-Acts28:27); [HEB 3:7–3:11](https://ref.ly/Heb3:7-Heb3:11), [HEB 10:15–10:17](https://ref.ly/Heb10:15-Heb10:17); [1PE 1:11](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:11)

### The Spirit inspired the writers of the NT

**[EPH 3:4–3:5](https://ref.ly/Eph3:4-Eph3:5)** *This revelation by the Spirit probably includes the apostles' understanding of the gospel and their preaching as well as the writings of the NT.* *See also* [1CO 7:40](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:40); [REV 2:7](https://ref.ly/Rev2:7), [REV 2:11](https://ref.ly/Rev2:11), [REV 2:17](https://ref.ly/Rev2:17), [REV 2:29](https://ref.ly/Rev2:29), [REV 3:6](https://ref.ly/Rev3:6), [REV 3:13](https://ref.ly/Rev3:13), [REV 3:22](https://ref.ly/Rev3:22)

The Holy Spirit illumines the meaning of Scripture
--------------------------------------------------

**[1CO 2:12–2:16](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:12-1Cor2:16)** *“what God has freely given” refers to “God's secret wisdom” (verse 7\), the “deep things of God” (verse 10\) and the “thoughts of God” (verse 11\).* *See also* [JHN 14:26](https://ref.ly/John14:26), [JHN 16:13–16:15](https://ref.ly/John16:13-John16:15); [2CO 3:14–3:17](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:14-2Cor3:17); [HEB 9:8](https://ref.ly/Heb9:8); [1JN 2:20](https://ref.ly/1John2:20), [1JN 2:27](https://ref.ly/1John2:27)

The relationship between the word and the Holy Spirit
-----------------------------------------------------

### The association of God's Spirit and breath with his word(s)

[PSA 33:6](https://ref.ly/Ps33:6); ; [ISA 59:21](https://ref.ly/Isa59:21); ; [JHN 3:34](https://ref.ly/John3:34), [JHN 6:63](https://ref.ly/John6:63); ; [EPH 6:17](https://ref.ly/Eph6:17) *“word” here probably means “the gospel” rather than Scripture as a whole.*

### The Spirit is essential for obeying God's law

**[ROM 2:29](https://ref.ly/Rom2:29)** *Reliance on the letter of the law alone is useless.* *See also* [ROM 7:6](https://ref.ly/Rom7:6); [2CO 3:6](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:6); [GAL 3:2](https://ref.ly/Gal3:2), [GAL 3:5](https://ref.ly/Gal3:5), [GAL 5:18](https://ref.ly/Gal5:18)

### The Spirit enables believers to fulfil the law

**[ROM 8:4](https://ref.ly/Rom8:4)** *See also* [GAL 5:4–5:5](https://ref.ly/Gal5:4-Gal5:5)
