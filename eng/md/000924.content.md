**[LEV 19:13](https://ref.ly/Lev19:13)** *See also* [DEU 24:14–24:15](https://ref.ly/Deut24:14-Deut24:15); [JAS 5:4](https://ref.ly/Jas5:4)

[LEV 25:50](https://ref.ly/Lev25:50)
