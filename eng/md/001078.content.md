## 5509: rulers (id: 5509)

Officials who, acting as God's servants, govern others and exercise authority over them. Also the originators of the laws of their realms. In the NT, rulers are also spiritual powers.

Officials exercising authority over people
------------------------------------------

**[MAT 20:25](https://ref.ly/Matt20:25)** parallel [MRK 10:42](https://ref.ly/Mark10:42) *See also* [2CH 23:20](https://ref.ly/2Chr23:20); [PSA 2:2](https://ref.ly/Ps2:2); [PRO 28:2](https://ref.ly/Prov28:2); [ISA 16:1](https://ref.ly/Isa16:1); [DAN 5:7](https://ref.ly/Dan5:7); [HOS 13:10](https://ref.ly/Hos13:10); [LUK 1:52](https://ref.ly/Luke1:52), [LUK 12:11](https://ref.ly/Luke12:11); [ACT 7:27](https://ref.ly/Acts7:27); [EXO 2:14](https://ref.ly/Exod2:14); [1CO 2:6–2:8](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:6-1Cor2:8)

Rulers are God's representatives
--------------------------------

**[PSA 58:1](https://ref.ly/Ps58:1)** *The Hebrew word translated “rulers” literally means “gods” a title given to those whose position called for them to act as earthly representatives of God's heavenly court.* *See also* [PSA 82:1](https://ref.ly/Ps82:1); [JHN 10:34–10:35](https://ref.ly/John10:34-John10:35); [PSA 82:6](https://ref.ly/Ps82:6); [ACT 23:5](https://ref.ly/Acts23:5); [EXO 22:28](https://ref.ly/Exod22:28)
