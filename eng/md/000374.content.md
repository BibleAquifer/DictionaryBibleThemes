## 4254: mountains (id: 4254)

These natural features of Palestine are often used as poetic symbols when describing God or his dealings with his people. Specific mountains are remembered because of their association with important events in the history of Israel. Some of the key events in the life and ministry of Jesus Christ take place on mountain sites.

Mountains and the character of God
----------------------------------

### Mountains and the steadfastness of God

**[PSA 125:2](https://ref.ly/Ps125:2)** *See also* [PSA 11:1](https://ref.ly/Ps11:1), [PSA 90:2](https://ref.ly/Ps90:2); [ISA 54:10](https://ref.ly/Isa54:10)

### Mountains tremble at God's awesome presence

**[JDG 5:5](https://ref.ly/Judg5:5)** *The violent physical response to the presence of the holy God evokes a sense of his might and majesty.* *See also* [EXO 19:16–19:19](https://ref.ly/Exod19:16-Exod19:19); [PSA 97:5](https://ref.ly/Ps97:5); [ISA 64:1](https://ref.ly/Isa64:1); [MIC 1:3–1:4](https://ref.ly/Mic1:3-Mic1:4); [HAB 3:3–3:6](https://ref.ly/Hab3:3-Hab3:6)
