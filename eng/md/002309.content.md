## 8634: amen (id: 8634)

A Hebrew term expressing the ideas of truth and reliability, often used in prayer and adoration.

Amen as an expression of worship
--------------------------------

**[1CH 16:36](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:36)**; **[EPH 3:21](https://ref.ly/Eph3:21)**; **[REV 7:11–7:12](https://ref.ly/Rev7:11-Rev7:12)** *See also* [NEH 5:13](https://ref.ly/Neh5:13), [NEH 8:6](https://ref.ly/Neh8:6) *The repetition of “Amen” here conveys Ezra's intensity of feeling as he read the Book of the Law. The response of the people represents a solemn, formal acceptance of God's commandments*; [PSA 72:19](https://ref.ly/Ps72:19), [PSA 106:48](https://ref.ly/Ps106:48); [ROM 9:5](https://ref.ly/Rom9:5) *“Amen” can mean either “Yes indeed, it is so” or “So be it”*; [ROM 11:36](https://ref.ly/Rom11:36), [ROM 16:25–16:27](https://ref.ly/Rom16:25-Rom16:27); [1CO 14:16](https://ref.ly/1Cor14:16); [GAL 1:3–1:5](https://ref.ly/Gal1:3-Gal1:5); [PHP 4:20](https://ref.ly/Phil4:20); [1TI 1:17](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:17), [1TI 6:15–6:16](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:15-1Tim6:16); [2TI 4:18](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:18); [HEB 13:20–13:21](https://ref.ly/Heb13:20-Heb13:21); [1PE 4:11](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:11), [1PE 5:10–5:11](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:10-1Pet5:11); [2PE 3:17–3:18](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:17-2Pet3:18); [JUD 1:24–1:25](https://ref.ly/Jude1:24-Jude1:25); [REV 1:4–1:7](https://ref.ly/Rev1:4-Rev1:7), [REV 19:4](https://ref.ly/Rev19:4) *the worship of heaven*
