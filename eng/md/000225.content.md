## 2505: Jesus Christ, ascension of (id: 2505)

The return of Jesus Christ to his Father in order to establish his kingdom, having completed his work on earth.

Jesus Christ's ascension described
----------------------------------

**[ACT 1:9–1:11](https://ref.ly/Acts1:9-Acts1:11)** *As in the OT, the cloud symbolises God's presence.* *See also* [MRK 16:19](https://ref.ly/Mark16:19); [LUK 24:51](https://ref.ly/Luke24:51); [ACT 1:2](https://ref.ly/Acts1:2); [EPH 4:10](https://ref.ly/Eph4:10); [1TI 3:16](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:16); [HEB 9:24](https://ref.ly/Heb9:24); [1PE 3:22](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:22); [REV 12:5](https://ref.ly/Rev12:5)
