## 5506: roof (id: 5506)

Most buildings in biblical times had flat roofs. With the hot climate, they had a wider range of uses than merely providing shelter for the building.

Uses of roofs
-------------

### Shelter erected on the roof to make an extra room

**[2KI 4:10](https://ref.ly/2Kgs4:10)** *See also* [2SA 16:22](https://ref.ly/2Sam16:22); [PRO 21:9](https://ref.ly/Prov21:9), [PRO 25:24](https://ref.ly/Prov25:24)

### Guests lodged on the roof

**[JOS 2:8](https://ref.ly/Josh2:8)** *See also* [1SA 9:25–9:26](https://ref.ly/1Sam9:25-1Sam9:26)

### The roof used as a place for leisure and relaxation

**[2SA 11:2](https://ref.ly/2Sam11:2)** *See also* [DAN 4:29](https://ref.ly/Dan4:29); [MAT 24:17](https://ref.ly/Matt24:17) parallels [MRK 13:15](https://ref.ly/Mark13:15), [LUK 17:31](https://ref.ly/Luke17:31)

### The roof used as a place of religious observance

**[NEH 8:16](https://ref.ly/Neh8:16)** *See also* [2KI 23:12](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:12); [JER 19:13](https://ref.ly/Jer19:13), [JER 32:29](https://ref.ly/Jer32:29); [ZEP 1:5](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:5); [ACT 10:9](https://ref.ly/Acts10:9)

In cities, some roofs were open, public places
----------------------------------------------

**[ISA 15:3](https://ref.ly/Isa15:3)** *See also* [JDG 9:50–9:52](https://ref.ly/Judg9:50-Judg9:52); [2SA 18:24](https://ref.ly/2Sam18:24); [JER 48:38](https://ref.ly/Jer48:38); [MAT 10:26–10:27](https://ref.ly/Matt10:26-Matt10:27) parallel [LUK 12:2–12:3](https://ref.ly/Luke12:2-Luke12:3)

Roofs were usually constructed with a thick layer of clay
---------------------------------------------------------

Roof as a synonym for house
---------------------------
