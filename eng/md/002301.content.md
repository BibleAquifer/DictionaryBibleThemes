## 8624: worship, reasons for (id: 8624)

The supreme reason for human existence is to worship God for his love, greatness and saving deeds.

To worship is a divine command
------------------------------

**[MAT 4:10](https://ref.ly/Matt4:10)** parallel [LUK 4:8](https://ref.ly/Luke4:8) *See also* [DEU 6:13](https://ref.ly/Deut6:13); [EXO 23:25](https://ref.ly/Exod23:25); [2KI 17:36](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:36); [1CH 16:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:29); [PSA 22:23](https://ref.ly/Ps22:23), [PSA 29:2](https://ref.ly/Ps29:2), [PSA 68:26](https://ref.ly/Ps68:26), [PSA 113:1](https://ref.ly/Ps113:1), [PSA 117:1](https://ref.ly/Ps117:1), [PSA 148:11–148:13](https://ref.ly/Ps148:11-Ps148:13), [PSA 150:6](https://ref.ly/Ps150:6); [1TI 2:8](https://ref.ly/1Tim2:8); [REV 14:7](https://ref.ly/Rev14:7)
