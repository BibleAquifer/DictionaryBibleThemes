## 7774: prophets, false (id: 7774)

Men or women who falsely claim to speak on God's behalf. They are denounced in the OT and NT for leading people astray. Scripture provides criteria by which true prophets may be recognised and distinguished from false prophets, who often speak from base motives.

Criteria for recognising true prophets
--------------------------------------

### A true prophet's word will be fulfilled

**[DEU 18:21–18:22](https://ref.ly/Deut18:21-Deut18:22)** *See also* [JER 28:8–28:9](https://ref.ly/Jer28:8-Jer28:9)

### A true prophet's teaching commends righteous behaviour

**[DEU 13:1–13:4](https://ref.ly/Deut13:1-Deut13:4)** *See also* [EZK 13:17–13:23](https://ref.ly/Ezek13:17-Ezek13:23), [EZK 14:4–14:8](https://ref.ly/Ezek14:4-Ezek14:8); [LAM 2:14](https://ref.ly/Lam2:14)

### A true prophet's godly life will reflect his calling

**[MAT 7:15–7:20](https://ref.ly/Matt7:15-Matt7:20)** *See also* [ISA 28:7](https://ref.ly/Isa28:7); [JER 23:10–23:11](https://ref.ly/Jer23:10-Jer23:11), [JER 23:14](https://ref.ly/Jer23:14); [ZEP 3:4](https://ref.ly/Zeph3:4)

### A true prophet will acknowledge Jesus Christ as divine

**[1JN 4:1–4:6](https://ref.ly/1John4:1-1John4:6)**

The motivations of false prophets
---------------------------------

### Their messages originate with themselves

**[JER 23:16](https://ref.ly/Jer23:16)** *See also* [JER 14:13–14:14](https://ref.ly/Jer14:13-Jer14:14), [JER 23:25–23:32](https://ref.ly/Jer23:25-Jer23:32), [JER 23:36](https://ref.ly/Jer23:36); [EZK 13:1–13:7](https://ref.ly/Ezek13:1-Ezek13:7)

### Their messages may be occasioned by popular demand

**[JER 29:8–29:9](https://ref.ly/Jer29:8-Jer29:9)** [NUM 22:35](https://ref.ly/Num22:35), [NUM 22:38](https://ref.ly/Num22:38), [NUM 23:12](https://ref.ly/Num23:12), [NUM 23:26](https://ref.ly/Num23:26), [NUM 24:12–24:13](https://ref.ly/Num24:12-Num24:13); ; [1KI 22:13–22:14](https://ref.ly/1Kgs22:13-1Kgs22:14); [ISA 30:10–30:11](https://ref.ly/Isa30:10-Isa30:11); ; [JER 5:30–5:31](https://ref.ly/Jer5:30-Jer5:31); ; [MIC 2:11](https://ref.ly/Mic2:11)

### Their messages may be given for financial reward

**[NEH 6:12–6:13](https://ref.ly/Neh6:12-Neh6:13)** *See also* [JER 6:13–6:14](https://ref.ly/Jer6:13-Jer6:14); [EZK 13:19](https://ref.ly/Ezek13:19); [MIC 3:11](https://ref.ly/Mic3:11); [2PE 2:1–2:3](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:1-2Pet2:3)

### Their messages may be the result of deception

**[1KI 22:19–22:23](https://ref.ly/1Kgs22:19-1Kgs22:23)** parallel [2CH 18:18–18:22](https://ref.ly/2Chr18:18-2Chr18:22) *See also* [EZK 14:9](https://ref.ly/Ezek14:9)

### Their messages may be inspired by divination

**[JER 14:14](https://ref.ly/Jer14:14)** *See also* [EZK 13:6–13:7](https://ref.ly/Ezek13:6-Ezek13:7), [EZK 22:28](https://ref.ly/Ezek22:28); [ACT 13:6](https://ref.ly/Acts13:6)
