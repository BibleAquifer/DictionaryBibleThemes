## 8302: love, abuse of (id: 8302)

Scripture warns that love can be misdirected and shows a number of examples.

Exaggerated love of self
------------------------

### Self\-interest is condemned

**[PHP 2:3–2:4](https://ref.ly/Phil2:3-Phil2:4)** *See also* [MAT 16:25](https://ref.ly/Matt16:25) parallels [MRK 8:35](https://ref.ly/Mark8:35), [LUK 9:24](https://ref.ly/Luke9:24); [ROM 14:15](https://ref.ly/Rom14:15), [ROM 15:1](https://ref.ly/Rom15:1); [1CO 8:9](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:9), [1CO 10:24](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:24); [GAL 5:26](https://ref.ly/Gal5:26); [JAS 3:14–3:15](https://ref.ly/Jas3:14-Jas3:15)

### Examples of self\-love

[EST 6:6](https://ref.ly/Esth6:6) *Haman*; ; [ISA 5:8](https://ref.ly/Isa5:8); ; [DAN 4:30](https://ref.ly/Dan4:30) *Nebuchadnezzar*; ; [2TI 3:2](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:2); ; [JAS 5:5](https://ref.ly/Jas5:5)
