## 5047: opportunities, in life (id: 5047)

Scripture views life as divinely ordained to include occasions when certain forms of behaviour are appropriate.

The need to discern opportune moments
-------------------------------------

**[ECC 3:1](https://ref.ly/Eccl3:1)**

Every possible opportunity should be taken to do good
-----------------------------------------------------

**[GAL 6:10](https://ref.ly/Gal6:10)** *See also* [LUK 10:33](https://ref.ly/Luke10:33); [JHN 9:4](https://ref.ly/John9:4); [EPH 5:16](https://ref.ly/Eph5:16); [COL 4:5](https://ref.ly/Col4:5); [2TI 4:2](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:2)

Examples of individuals who seized opportunities to serve God
-------------------------------------------------------------

Rahab saved the spies and her household: [JOS 2:8–2:14](https://ref.ly/Josh2:8-Josh2:14), [JOS 6:23](https://ref.ly/Josh6:23); [2KI 5:1–5:3](https://ref.ly/2Kgs5:1-2Kgs5:3) *Naaman's wife's maid helped Naaman by telling her mistress about Elisha*; ; [NEH 2:2–2:5](https://ref.ly/Neh2:2-Neh2:5) *Nehemiah gained permission to rebuild the walls of Jerusalem.*; [EST 4:14](https://ref.ly/Esth4:14), [EST 7:3–7:6](https://ref.ly/Esth7:3-Esth7:6); [DAN 2:14–2:16](https://ref.ly/Dan2:14-Dan2:16) *Daniel offered to interpret Nebuchadnezzar's dream*; ; [ACT 18:24–18:26](https://ref.ly/Acts18:24-Acts18:26) *Priscilla and Aquila taught Apollos.*

Some opportunities should be rejected
-------------------------------------

[1SA 24:3–24:4](https://ref.ly/1Sam24:3-1Sam24:4), [1SA 24:10](https://ref.ly/1Sam24:10), [1SA 26:6–26:11](https://ref.ly/1Sam26:6-1Sam26:11)

Opportunities to do evil may have appalling consequences
--------------------------------------------------------

[GEN 4:8](https://ref.ly/Gen4:8) *Cain made an opportunity to murder Abel*; ; [GEN 25:29–25:31](https://ref.ly/Gen25:29-Gen25:31) *Jacob took the opportunity to “buy” Esau's birthright.*; [GEN 37:18–37:20](https://ref.ly/Gen37:18-Gen37:20), [GEN 37:27](https://ref.ly/Gen37:27); [DAN 6:4–6:5](https://ref.ly/Dan6:4-Dan6:5) *Daniel's enemies sought and found an opportunity to lay a charge against him.*; [MAT 26:16](https://ref.ly/Matt26:16) parallels [MRK 14:11](https://ref.ly/Mark14:11), [LUK 22:6](https://ref.ly/Luke22:6)

Giving others an opportunity to do wrong must be avoided
--------------------------------------------------------

[1TI 5:14](https://ref.ly/1Tim5:14)
