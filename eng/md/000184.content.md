## 2312: Jesus Christ, as king (id: 2312)

Jesus Christ is declared king at his birth: he descends from the royal line of David. People rejected his kingly claims at his crucifixion but God exalted him to his rightful place of power and majesty. At the end of time Jesus Christ will rule the nations for ever.

Jesus Christ descends from the royal line of David
--------------------------------------------------

### He was the Son of David

**[MAT 1:1](https://ref.ly/Matt1:1)** *The Messiah was expected to come from David's line. The genealogy given by Matthew follows the royal line of the kings descended from David.* *See also* [MAT 9:27](https://ref.ly/Matt9:27), [MAT 12:23](https://ref.ly/Matt12:23), [MAT 15:22](https://ref.ly/Matt15:22); [LUK 1:32–1:33](https://ref.ly/Luke1:32-Luke1:33), [LUK 2:4](https://ref.ly/Luke2:4), [LUK 3:31](https://ref.ly/Luke3:31)

### He was born in Bethlehem, the town of David

**[JHN 7:42](https://ref.ly/John7:42)** *See also* [MAT 2:3–2:6](https://ref.ly/Matt2:3-Matt2:6); [MIC 5:2](https://ref.ly/Mic5:2); [LUK 2:15–2:16](https://ref.ly/Luke2:15-Luke2:16)

Jesus Christ is regarded as king
--------------------------------

**[JHN 1:49](https://ref.ly/John1:49)** *By Nathanael.* *See also* [MAT 2:1–2:2](https://ref.ly/Matt2:1-Matt2:2) *by the Magi*; [JHN 6:15](https://ref.ly/John6:15) *by the crowds*; [ACT 17:7](https://ref.ly/Acts17:7) *by Jason of Thessalonica*

Jesus Christ refused worldly kingdoms
-------------------------------------

**[JHN 18:36](https://ref.ly/John18:36)** *See also* [MAT 4:8–4:10](https://ref.ly/Matt4:8-Matt4:10) parallel [LUK 4:5–4:8](https://ref.ly/Luke4:5-Luke4:8); [JHN 6:15](https://ref.ly/John6:15)

Jesus Christ entered Jerusalem as a king
----------------------------------------

**[MAT 21:1–21:9](https://ref.ly/Matt21:1-Matt21:9)** parallel [JHN 12:12–12:15](https://ref.ly/John12:12-John12:15) *See also* [ZEC 9:9](https://ref.ly/Zech9:9)

Jesus Christ's kingship is an issue at his trial
------------------------------------------------

### Pilate questions Jesus Christ

**[LUK 23:2–23:3](https://ref.ly/Luke23:2-Luke23:3)** parallels [MAT 27:11](https://ref.ly/Matt27:11), [MRK 15:2](https://ref.ly/Mark15:2), [JHN 18:33](https://ref.ly/John18:33) *Pilate's first words to Jesus Christ are the same in all the Gospels.*

### Jesus Christ claims to be a spiritual king

**[JHN 18:36–18:37](https://ref.ly/John18:36-John18:37)** *See also* [MAT 27:11](https://ref.ly/Matt27:11) parallels [MRK 15:2](https://ref.ly/Mark15:2), [LUK 23:3](https://ref.ly/Luke23:3)

### Pilate appeals to the crowd to accept Jesus Christ as their king

**[MRK 15:9](https://ref.ly/Mark15:9)**

### The crowd reject Jesus Christ as king

**[MRK 15:12–15:13](https://ref.ly/Mark15:12-Mark15:13)** *See also* [JHN 19:12–19:15](https://ref.ly/John19:12-John19:15)

### Jesus Christ is mocked as king

**[MAT 27:27–27:30](https://ref.ly/Matt27:27-Matt27:30)** parallels [MRK 15:16–15:20](https://ref.ly/Mark15:16-Mark15:20), [JHN 19:2–19:3](https://ref.ly/John19:2-John19:3)

Jesus Christ is declared king at his crucifixion
------------------------------------------------

**[JHN 19:19–19:22](https://ref.ly/John19:19-John19:22)** parallels [MAT 27:37](https://ref.ly/Matt27:37), [MRK 15:26](https://ref.ly/Mark15:26), [LUK 23:38](https://ref.ly/Luke23:38) *Jesus Christ's claim to be king was the “crime” for which he was executed.*

God has exalted Jesus Christ to his rightful place as king
----------------------------------------------------------
