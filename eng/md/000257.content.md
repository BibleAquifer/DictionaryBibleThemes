## 3120: Holy Spirit, descriptions of (id: 3120)

Though himself invisible, the Holy Spirit may appear in symbolic form and his activities may be described metaphorically.

The dove
--------

**[MAT 3:16](https://ref.ly/Matt3:16)** parallels [MRK 1:10](https://ref.ly/Mark1:10), [LUK 3:22](https://ref.ly/Luke3:22) *See also* [JHN 1:32](https://ref.ly/John1:32)

The wind or breath
------------------

**[ACT 2:2](https://ref.ly/Acts2:2)** *This phenomenon accompanied the filling with the Spirit.* *See also* [EZK 37:9–37:14](https://ref.ly/Ezek37:9-Ezek37:14) *The same Hebrew word is here translated “breath”, “wind” and “spirit”*; [JHN 3:8](https://ref.ly/John3:8), [JHN 20:22](https://ref.ly/John20:22)

The fire
--------

**[ACT 2:3–2:4](https://ref.ly/Acts2:3-Acts2:4)** *See also* [ISA 4:4](https://ref.ly/Isa4:4); [MAT 3:11–3:12](https://ref.ly/Matt3:11-Matt3:12) parallel [LUK 3:16–3:17](https://ref.ly/Luke3:16-Luke3:17); [1TH 5:19](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:19)

The oil of anointing
--------------------

[ISA 61:1](https://ref.ly/Isa61:1); ; [ACT 10:38](https://ref.ly/Acts10:38)

Water
-----

**[JHN 7:37–7:39](https://ref.ly/John7:37-John7:39)** *See also* [ISA 44:3](https://ref.ly/Isa44:3); [JHN 4:14](https://ref.ly/John4:14); [1CO 12:13](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:13)

**[EPH 1:13](https://ref.ly/Eph1:13)** *See also* [EPH 4:30](https://ref.ly/Eph4:30)
