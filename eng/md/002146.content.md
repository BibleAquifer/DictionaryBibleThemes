## 8316: orthodoxy, in NT (id: 8316)

Acceptance of the truth, especially about Jesus Christ, that is revealed by the Holy Spirit in the gospel and is passed on through the teaching of sound doctrine.

Orthodoxy is based on Jesus Christ who is the truth
---------------------------------------------------

**[EPH 4:21](https://ref.ly/Eph4:21)** *See also* [JHN 1:14](https://ref.ly/John1:14), [JHN 14:6](https://ref.ly/John14:6)
