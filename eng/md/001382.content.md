## 5863: flattery (id: 5863)

Insincere and undeserved praise or words of encouragement, used with the intention of gaining favour with others.

Flattery is deceitful
---------------------

**[PSA 12:2–12:3](https://ref.ly/Ps12:2-Ps12:3)** *See also* [PSA 5:9](https://ref.ly/Ps5:9), [PSA 55:20–55:21](https://ref.ly/Ps55:20-Ps55:21), [PSA 62:4](https://ref.ly/Ps62:4); [PRO 27:6](https://ref.ly/Prov27:6); [JER 9:8](https://ref.ly/Jer9:8)

The results of flattery are ruinous
-----------------------------------

**[PRO 26:28](https://ref.ly/Prov26:28)** *See also* [PRO 29:5](https://ref.ly/Prov29:5)

Flattery is rejected by godly people
------------------------------------

**[JOB 32:21–32:22](https://ref.ly/Job32:21-Job32:22)** *See also* [GAL 1:10](https://ref.ly/Gal1:10); [1TH 2:5–2:6](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:5-1Thess2:6)

Honesty is preferable to flattery
---------------------------------

**[PRO 28:23](https://ref.ly/Prov28:23)** *See also* [PRO 16:13](https://ref.ly/Prov16:13), [PRO 27:9](https://ref.ly/Prov27:9)

False teachers use flattery
---------------------------

**[ROM 16:17–16:18](https://ref.ly/Rom16:17-Rom16:18)** *See also* [2TI 4:3–4:4](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:3-2Tim4:4); [JUD 1:16–1:19](https://ref.ly/Jude1:16-Jude1:19)

Examples of flattery
--------------------

[2SA 14:17–14:20](https://ref.ly/2Sam14:17-2Sam14:20) *The woman from Tekoa flatters David*; ; [2SA 15:2–15:6](https://ref.ly/2Sam15:2-2Sam15:6) *Absalom to Israelites*; ; [DAN 6:21](https://ref.ly/Dan6:21) *Daniel flatters the king*; ; [MAT 22:16](https://ref.ly/Matt22:16) parallels [MRK 12:14](https://ref.ly/Mark12:14), [LUK 20:21](https://ref.ly/Luke20:21) *the Pharisees and Herodians to Jesus Christ*; ; [LUK 20:21](https://ref.ly/Luke20:21) *The spies from the teachers of the law flatter Jesus Christ*; ; [ACT 12:22](https://ref.ly/Acts12:22) *The crowds flatter Herod.*
