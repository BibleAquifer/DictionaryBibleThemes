## 4824: famine, spiritual (id: 4824)

A state of spiritual hunger arising from a failure to experience the presence and joy of God. Scripture describes such experiences, indicates one ultimate cause to be unbelief and points to ways in which this famine can be relieved.

----------------------------------

**[AMO 8:11–8:12](https://ref.ly/Amos8:11-Amos8:12)** *See also* [1SA 3:1](https://ref.ly/1Sam3:1); [PSA 74:9](https://ref.ly/Ps74:9); [LAM 2:9](https://ref.ly/Lam2:9); [EZK 7:26](https://ref.ly/Ezek7:26); [LUK 23:8–23:9](https://ref.ly/Luke23:8-Luke23:9)
