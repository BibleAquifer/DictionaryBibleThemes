## 8831: syncretism (id: 8831)

The incorporation into religious faith and practice of elements from other religions, resulting in a loss of integrity and assimilation to the surrounding culture.

Syncretism in Israel
--------------------

### God's claim to exclusive worship, the basis of the rejection of syncretism

**[EXO 20:3](https://ref.ly/Exod20:3)** parallel [DEU 5:7](https://ref.ly/Deut5:7) *See also* [DEU 6:13–6:15](https://ref.ly/Deut6:13-Deut6:15); [JOS 24:14–24:15](https://ref.ly/Josh24:14-Josh24:15); [1KI 18:21](https://ref.ly/1Kgs18:21)

### Syncretism forbidden to Israel

**[EXO 34:15–34:16](https://ref.ly/Exod34:15-Exod34:16)** *See also* [DEU 7:1–7:6](https://ref.ly/Deut7:1-Deut7:6); [JOS 23:16](https://ref.ly/Josh23:16); [1SA 7:3](https://ref.ly/1Sam7:3)

### Israelite practice of syncretism

**[JER 19:4–19:5](https://ref.ly/Jer19:4-Jer19:5)** *See also* [2KI 17:34–17:41](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:34-2Kgs17:41), [2KI 21:1–21:7](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:1-2Kgs21:7) parallel [2CH 33:1–33:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr33:1-2Chr33:7); [EZK 8:9–8:16](https://ref.ly/Ezek8:9-Ezek8:16)

### The consequence of syncretism

### Punishment for syncretism

**[ZEP 1:4–1:5](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:4-Zeph1:5)** *See also* [DEU 17:2–17:7](https://ref.ly/Deut17:2-Deut17:7); [JER 19:10–19:13](https://ref.ly/Jer19:10-Jer19:13); [HOS 2:2–2:13](https://ref.ly/Hos2:2-Hos2:13)
