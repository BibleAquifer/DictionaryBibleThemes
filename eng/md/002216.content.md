## 8427: evangelism, kinds of (id: 8427)

Scripture recognises that evangelism takes place in a variety of contexts, and offers models for evangelism in today's church.

Public evangelism
-----------------

### Preaching in synagogues

**[ACT 14:1](https://ref.ly/Acts14:1)** *Paul begins his evangelism in Gentile cities by building links within the Jewish community.* [ACT 13:14–13:16](https://ref.ly/Acts13:14-Acts13:16), [ACT 13:42–13:44](https://ref.ly/Acts13:42-Acts13:44); [ACT 17:2](https://ref.ly/Acts17:2) *in Thessalonica*; ; [ACT 17:10](https://ref.ly/Acts17:10) *in Berea*; ; [ACT 17:17](https://ref.ly/Acts17:17) *in Athens*; ; [ACT 18:4](https://ref.ly/Acts18:4) *in Corinth*; ; [ACT 19:8](https://ref.ly/Acts19:8) *in Ephesus*

**[ACT 17:19–17:23](https://ref.ly/Acts17:19-Acts17:23)** *See also* [ACT 5:25](https://ref.ly/Acts5:25), [ACT 10:27–10:28](https://ref.ly/Acts10:27-Acts10:28), [ACT 16:13](https://ref.ly/Acts16:13), [ACT 19:9](https://ref.ly/Acts19:9)

### Making a public defence to accusers

**[ACT 21:37–22:1](https://ref.ly/Acts21:37-Acts22:1)** *See also* [ACT 4:7–4:12](https://ref.ly/Acts4:7-Acts4:12), [ACT 7:1–7:2](https://ref.ly/Acts7:1-Acts7:2), [ACT 24:10](https://ref.ly/Acts24:10), [ACT 26:1](https://ref.ly/Acts26:1)

Evangelism among strategic people
---------------------------------

### Speaking to prominent people

**[ACT 18:8](https://ref.ly/Acts18:8)** *Though the gospel is for all people without distinction, evangelism among certain people and groups enabled the message to spead further.* *See also* [ACT 13:7](https://ref.ly/Acts13:7) *Paul before Sergius Paulus, proconsul of Cyprus*; [ACT 24:24](https://ref.ly/Acts24:24) *Paul before Felix and his wife*; [ACT 25:8–25:9](https://ref.ly/Acts25:8-Acts25:9) *Paul before Festus*; [ACT 25:11–25:12](https://ref.ly/Acts25:11-Acts25:12) *Paul appeals to Caesar*; [ACT 26:2–26:3](https://ref.ly/Acts26:2-Acts26:3) *Paul before King Agrippa*; [ACT 28:7–28:8](https://ref.ly/Acts28:7-Acts28:8) *Paul in the home of the chief official on Malta*

### Evangelising households

**[ACT 16:34](https://ref.ly/Acts16:34)** *The conversion of the head of the household was often accompanied by the conversion of the whole family group.* *See also* [JHN 4:53](https://ref.ly/John4:53); [ACT 11:14](https://ref.ly/Acts11:14), [ACT 18:8](https://ref.ly/Acts18:8)
