## 8282: intolerance (id: 8282)

Unwillingness to allow or permit something beyond certain levels. God's intolerance should be the model for God's people, but they should avoid becoming wrongly intolerant.

God is intolerant of evil
-------------------------

### God is pure and cannot tolerate evil

**[HAB 1:13](https://ref.ly/Hab1:13)** *See also* [NUM 25:3](https://ref.ly/Num25:3); [JOS 7:1](https://ref.ly/Josh7:1); [2SA 6:7](https://ref.ly/2Sam6:7) parallel [1CH 13:10](https://ref.ly/1Chr13:10); [2KI 17:11](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:11); [PSA 5:6](https://ref.ly/Ps5:6); [ROM 1:18](https://ref.ly/Rom1:18); [REV 21:27](https://ref.ly/Rev21:27)

### God demonstrates his intolerance as the last resort

**[JER 44:22](https://ref.ly/Jer44:22)** *See also* [EXO 4:10–4:14](https://ref.ly/Exod4:10-Exod4:14), [EXO 34:6–34:7](https://ref.ly/Exod34:6-Exod34:7); [NUM 14:18](https://ref.ly/Num14:18); [NEH 9:30](https://ref.ly/Neh9:30); [ACT 17:30](https://ref.ly/Acts17:30); [ROM 2:3–2:5](https://ref.ly/Rom2:3-Rom2:5)

Human intolerance can be misguided
----------------------------------

### It can be a form of hypocrisy

**[LUK 6:41–6:42](https://ref.ly/Luke6:41-Luke6:42)** *See also* [MAT 23:29–23:30](https://ref.ly/Matt23:29-Matt23:30); [LUK 13:14–13:15](https://ref.ly/Luke13:14-Luke13:15); [JHN 12:5–12:6](https://ref.ly/John12:5-John12:6)

### It can amount to exclusiveness

**[MAT 23:13](https://ref.ly/Matt23:13)** *See also* [ACT 15:1](https://ref.ly/Acts15:1)

### It can be a form of oppression

Intolerance of minority, afflicted or underprivileged groups oftens leads to oppression: [EXO 1:11–1:14](https://ref.ly/Exod1:11-Exod1:14), [EXO 23:9](https://ref.ly/Exod23:9); ; [PRO 14:31](https://ref.ly/Prov14:31), [PRO 28:3](https://ref.ly/Prov28:3); ; [ECC 5:8](https://ref.ly/Eccl5:8); ; [MAT 20:31](https://ref.ly/Matt20:31) parallels [MRK 10:48](https://ref.ly/Mark10:48), [LUK 18:39](https://ref.ly/Luke18:39)

### It can be harshly condemnatory

**[JHN 15:19](https://ref.ly/John15:19)** *See also* [NUM 11:27–11:29](https://ref.ly/Num11:27-Num11:29); [JDG 12:5–12:6](https://ref.ly/Judg12:5-Judg12:6); [MAT 21:15–21:16](https://ref.ly/Matt21:15-Matt21:16), [MAT 23:16](https://ref.ly/Matt23:16), [MAT 23:18](https://ref.ly/Matt23:18)
