## 8748: false religion (id: 8748)

The worship of anything that is not God, including any form of religion that fails to do justice to the glory and majesty of God. Scripture criticises both the practices and the beliefs of false religions.

The nature of false religion
----------------------------

### Man\-made images are worshipped

**[ACT 17:29](https://ref.ly/Acts17:29)** *See also* [DEU 4:28](https://ref.ly/Deut4:28); [PSA 115:4–115:7](https://ref.ly/Ps115:4-Ps115:7), [PSA 135:15–135:17](https://ref.ly/Ps135:15-Ps135:17); [ISA 44:10–44:20](https://ref.ly/Isa44:10-Isa44:20); [JER 10:3–10:5](https://ref.ly/Jer10:3-Jer10:5), [JER 16:20](https://ref.ly/Jer16:20); [DAN 3:4–3:7](https://ref.ly/Dan3:4-Dan3:7); [ACT 19:26](https://ref.ly/Acts19:26)

### Created things are worshipped

**[ROM 1:25](https://ref.ly/Rom1:25)** *See also* [2KI 21:3](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:3) parallel [2CH 33:5](https://ref.ly/2Chr33:5); [JER 8:2](https://ref.ly/Jer8:2), [JER 19:13](https://ref.ly/Jer19:13); [EZK 8:16](https://ref.ly/Ezek8:16)

### Secular authorities are worshipped

**[REV 13:4](https://ref.ly/Rev13:4)** *The dragon in Revelation may be understood as the devil and the beast as the antichrist: the forces at work behind the human authorities that defy the rule of God.* *See also* [ACT 12:21–12:23](https://ref.ly/Acts12:21-Acts12:23); [REV 13:8](https://ref.ly/Rev13:8), [REV 13:12](https://ref.ly/Rev13:12)

### Demons are worshipped

**[DEU 32:17](https://ref.ly/Deut32:17)** *See also* [LEV 17:7](https://ref.ly/Lev17:7); [PSA 106:37–106:38](https://ref.ly/Ps106:37-Ps106:38); [1CO 10:20](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:20); [REV 9:20](https://ref.ly/Rev9:20)

False religion is based on man\-made traditions, myths and philosophies
