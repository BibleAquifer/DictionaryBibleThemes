## 8351: teachableness (id: 8351)

A quality in those who desire to learn, present in the disciples of Jesus Christ and seen in Christ's relationship to his Father. Unwillingness to receive the truth leads to spiritual darkness.

The importance of teachableness
-------------------------------

### For spiritual growth

**[HEB 12:5–12:12](https://ref.ly/Heb12:5-Heb12:12)** *See also* [HEB 5:11–5:14](https://ref.ly/Heb5:11-Heb5:14)

### For a life of obedience

**[PHP 4:9](https://ref.ly/Phil4:9)** *See also* [JAS 1:25](https://ref.ly/Jas1:25); [REV 3:3](https://ref.ly/Rev3:3)

### For discerning God's guidance

**[PRO 3:5–3:6](https://ref.ly/Prov3:5-Prov3:6)** *See also* [PSA 25:4–25:5](https://ref.ly/Ps25:4-Ps25:5); [ROM 12:2](https://ref.ly/Rom12:2)
