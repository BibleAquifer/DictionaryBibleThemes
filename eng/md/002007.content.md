## 8110: athletics (id: 8110)

Sports engaged in by athletes. Scripture uses the image of running in a race to represent the Christian life. Believers should show similar perseverance as they press on to their heavenly reward.

The Christian life is like running a race
-----------------------------------------

**[ACT 20:24](https://ref.ly/Acts20:24)** *See also* [GAL 2:2](https://ref.ly/Gal2:2), [GAL 5:7](https://ref.ly/Gal5:7); [PHP 2:16](https://ref.ly/Phil2:16); [1TI 1:18–1:19](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:18-1Tim1:19)

Christians, like athletes, are to be single\-minded
---------------------------------------------------

**[2TI 2:4–2:5](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:4-2Tim2:5)** *See also* [1CO 9:24–9:27](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:24-1Cor9:27)

Christians, like athletes, are to persevere
-------------------------------------------

**[HEB 12:1](https://ref.ly/Heb12:1)** *See also* [PHP 3:12–3:14](https://ref.ly/Phil3:12-Phil3:14); [1TI 6:12](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:12); [REV 3:11](https://ref.ly/Rev3:11)

The reward at the end of the race of life
-----------------------------------------

**[2TI 4:7–4:8](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:7-2Tim4:8)** *See also* [1CO 9:25](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:25); [JAS 1:12](https://ref.ly/Jas1:12); [1PE 5:4](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:4); [REV 2:10](https://ref.ly/Rev2:10) *The “crown” referred to is the garland or wreath awarded to the winner in athletics contests.*

The race is not necessarily won by the swiftest
