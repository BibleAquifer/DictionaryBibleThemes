## 6659: freedom, acts of in OT (id: 6659)

The OT provides many examples of God delivering his people from bondage, of which the greatest is the exodus. These acts of deliverance foreshadow and prepare the way for the redeeming work of God through Jesus Christ, revealed in the NT.

God's people regain their freedom
---------------------------------

### The exodus from Egypt as an act of deliverance

**[EXO 20:2](https://ref.ly/Exod20:2)** *See also* [EXO 12:42](https://ref.ly/Exod12:42), [EXO 16:6](https://ref.ly/Exod16:6), [EXO 16:32](https://ref.ly/Exod16:32) *The people of Israel were frequently reminded that God had delivered them from Egypt*; [JOS 24:6](https://ref.ly/Josh24:6); [JDG 6:8](https://ref.ly/Judg6:8); [2SA 7:6](https://ref.ly/2Sam7:6); [1KI 8:16](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:16); [2CH 7:22](https://ref.ly/2Chr7:22); [PSA 80:8](https://ref.ly/Ps80:8); [JER 2:6](https://ref.ly/Jer2:6), [JER 11:4](https://ref.ly/Jer11:4); [HOS 12:9](https://ref.ly/Hos12:9); [AMO 2:10](https://ref.ly/Amos2:10); [MIC 6:4](https://ref.ly/Mic6:4); [HAG 2:5](https://ref.ly/Hag2:5); [HEB 8:9](https://ref.ly/Heb8:9); [JUD 1:5](https://ref.ly/Jude1:5)
