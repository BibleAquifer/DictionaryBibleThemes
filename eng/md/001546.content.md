## 6187: immorality (id: 6187)

Behaviour which is contrary to the will of God for his people. Although the term may be used to refer to a wide range of immoral actions, it is used especially in relation to unacceptable sexual behaviour.

* **Associated Articles:** 6188: immorality, nature of sexual (ID: `6188@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 6189: immorality, examples of sexual (ID: `6189@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
