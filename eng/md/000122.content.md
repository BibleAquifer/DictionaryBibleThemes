## 1613: Scripture, purpose of (id: 1613)

Scripture has been given by God to lead people to faith and salvation. Through Scripture believers are nurtured in faith and led to spiritual maturity.

Scripture is intended to lead people to salvation
-------------------------------------------------

**[2TI 3:14–3:15](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:14-2Tim3:15)** *See also* [PSA 19:7–19:11](https://ref.ly/Ps19:7-Ps19:11); [JHN 20:30–20:31](https://ref.ly/John20:30-John20:31); [ROM 10:8](https://ref.ly/Rom10:8)
