## 5104: Moses, foreshadower of Jesus Christ (id: 5104)

Moses foreshadows Jesus Christ in his role as a law\-giver, as a mediator between God and his people and as a prophet who declared the will of God. In these matters, Jesus Christ brought to perfection the work begun through Moses.
