## 4330: glass (id: 4330)

A substance remarkable for its transparency. Two phenomena seen by John in his vision in the book of Revelation are likened to glass.

The sea of glass
----------------

**[REV 15:2](https://ref.ly/Rev15:2)** *See also* [REV 4:2–4:6](https://ref.ly/Rev4:2-Rev4:6)

The gold in the new Jerusalem likened to glass
----------------------------------------------

**[REV 21:21](https://ref.ly/Rev21:21)** *See also* [REV 21:15–21:18](https://ref.ly/Rev21:15-Rev21:18)
