## 2565: Jesus Christ, second coming of (id: 2565)

Jesus Christ will return visibly and in glory at the end of history to raise the dead, judge the world, destroy all evil and opposition to God and consummate his kingdom. Believers are encouraged to be prepared for his return, waiting with eagerness and joy.

NT terms for the second coming
------------------------------

### \`\`Parousia''

Meaning literally `presence'' or`coming'', the term was used in the NT world of a royal visit: [MAT 24:3](https://ref.ly/Matt24:3); ; [JAS 5:7–5:8](https://ref.ly/Jas5:7-Jas5:8); ; [2PE 3:4](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:4); ; [1JN 2:28](https://ref.ly/1John2:28)

### \`\`Apocalypsis''

Meaning literally `unveiling'',`apocalypsis'' suggests that the second coming will make visible that which is already true of Jesus Christ but presently hidden from human sight: [LUK 17:30](https://ref.ly/Luke17:30); ; [1CO 1:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:7); ; [2TH 1:7](https://ref.ly/2Thess1:7); ; [1PE 1:7](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:7), [1PE 4:13](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:13)

### \`\`Epiphaneia''

Meaning literally `appearing'' or`manifestation'', the term suggests coming out into view from a hidden background: [2TH 2:8](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:8); ; [1TI 6:14](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:14); ; [2TI 4:1](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:1); ; [TIT 2:13](https://ref.ly/Titus2:13)
