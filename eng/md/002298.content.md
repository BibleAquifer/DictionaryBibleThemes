## 8620: prayer, practicalities of (id: 8620)

Scripture commends a life of prayer, characterised by simplicity of expression, sincerity of heart and trust in the promises of God. It gives guidance on how, when and where to pray.

Scripture stresses the importance of prayer
-------------------------------------------

**[1TH 5:16–5:18](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:16-1Thess5:18)** *See also* [ACT 6:3–6:4](https://ref.ly/Acts6:3-Acts6:4); [ROM 12:12](https://ref.ly/Rom12:12)

Judgment comes on those who do not pray
---------------------------------------

**[PSA 79:6](https://ref.ly/Ps79:6)** *See also* [PSA 53:4](https://ref.ly/Ps53:4); [JER 10:21](https://ref.ly/Jer10:21); [ZEP 1:4–1:6](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:4-Zeph1:6); [JAS 4:2](https://ref.ly/Jas4:2)

**[MAT 6:7–6:8](https://ref.ly/Matt6:7-Matt6:8)** *See also* [ECC 5:1–5:3](https://ref.ly/Eccl5:1-Eccl5:3); [LUK 18:9–18:14](https://ref.ly/Luke18:9-Luke18:14)
