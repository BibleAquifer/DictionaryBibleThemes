## 6135: coveting, and other sins (id: 6135)

Covetousness is associated with other sins and brings trouble with it.

Coveting is linked with other sins
----------------------------------

### Idolatry

**[EPH 5:5](https://ref.ly/Eph5:5)** *See also* [1CO 10:6–10:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:6-1Cor10:7); [COL 3:5](https://ref.ly/Col3:5)

### Pride

[PSA 10:3](https://ref.ly/Ps10:3)

### Sexual sin

**[MAT 5:28](https://ref.ly/Matt5:28)** *See also* [1TH 4:5](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:5)

### Laziness

[PRO 21:25–21:26](https://ref.ly/Prov21:25-Prov21:26)

Covetousness is one of many sins which pollute a person
-------------------------------------------------------

**[MRK 7:21–7:23](https://ref.ly/Mark7:21-Mark7:23)**

Love of money is covetousness
-----------------------------

### It is a source of evil

**[1TI 6:9–6:10](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:9-1Tim6:10)** *See also* [PRO 10:2](https://ref.ly/Prov10:2), [PRO 22:16](https://ref.ly/Prov22:16), [PRO 28:20](https://ref.ly/Prov28:20), [PRO 28:22](https://ref.ly/Prov28:22), [PRO 30:8–30:9](https://ref.ly/Prov30:8-Prov30:9)

### Examples of those who love money

**[LUK 16:14](https://ref.ly/Luke16:14)**; **[JHN 12:5–12:6](https://ref.ly/John12:5-John12:6)** *See also* [MAT 23:25](https://ref.ly/Matt23:25) parallel [LUK 11:39](https://ref.ly/Luke11:39); [2PE 2:3](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:3), [2PE 2:14](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:14) *False teachers are “experts in greed”.*

### Believers must not be lovers of money

**[HEB 13:5](https://ref.ly/Heb13:5)**; **[1PE 5:2](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:2)** *Elders must not be greedy for money.* *See also* [EXO 18:21](https://ref.ly/Exod18:21); [EPH 5:3](https://ref.ly/Eph5:3); [1TI 3:1–3:3](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:1-1Tim3:3)

### Love of money characterises the last days

**[2TI 3:1–3:2](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:1-2Tim3:2)**
