## 2227: Immanuel (id: 2227)

An OT name given to Jesus Christ, meaning “God with us”.

Actual uses of the name Immanuel
--------------------------------

**[ISA 7:14](https://ref.ly/Isa7:14)** *The name was given originally to a child of Isaiah's day who would signify God's salvation and judgment for Judah*; **[ISA 8:7–8:10](https://ref.ly/Isa8:7-Isa8:10)** *The original fulfilment of the Immanuel prophecy would bring judgment on Judah*; **[MAT 1:22–1:23](https://ref.ly/Matt1:22-Matt1:23)** *Isaiah's prophecy is fulfilled through the virginal conception of Jesus Christ.*
