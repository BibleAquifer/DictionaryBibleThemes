## 7968: spiritual gifts, nature of (id: 7968)

In both the OT and the NT God graciously pours out gifts on his people. They are to be welcomed and used for the good of all.

OT examples of God giving special gifts
---------------------------------------

### God's gracious provision from above

**[JOL 2:23–2:24](https://ref.ly/Joel2:23-Joel2:24)** *This gracious provision of material needs from above can be seen as foreshadowing God's bestowal of spiritual gifts.* *See also* [EXO 16:4](https://ref.ly/Exod16:4), [EXO 16:8](https://ref.ly/Exod16:8), [EXO 16:13–16:14](https://ref.ly/Exod16:13-Exod16:14); [DEU 11:14](https://ref.ly/Deut11:14); [1KI 17:6](https://ref.ly/1Kgs17:6); [JOB 5:10](https://ref.ly/Job5:10); [ISA 55:10](https://ref.ly/Isa55:10)

### Distribution of land to tribes

**[JOS 13:6–13:7](https://ref.ly/Josh13:6-Josh13:7)** *The Hebrew word for “give” often includes the sense of “apportion” or “assign”.* *See also* [EZK 47:21](https://ref.ly/Ezek47:21)

### The future promise of spiritual gifts

**[JOL 2:28–2:29](https://ref.ly/Joel2:28-Joel2:29)** *See also* [ACT 2:17–2:18](https://ref.ly/Acts2:17-Acts2:18)
