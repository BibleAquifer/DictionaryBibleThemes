## 5572: sword (id: 5572)

A hand\-wielded blade in common use throughout biblical times as a weapon of war and in OT times as an instrument of execution. The sword is also a symbol of aggression, authority and power.

The sword as the principal weapon of war
----------------------------------------

### It was in common use

**[EXO 17:13](https://ref.ly/Exod17:13)** *See also* [NUM 21:24–21:35](https://ref.ly/Num21:24-Num21:35); [JOS 11:10–11:12](https://ref.ly/Josh11:10-Josh11:12), [JOS 19:47](https://ref.ly/Josh19:47)

### Swordsmen fought and fell in great numbers

**[JDG 3:28–3:30](https://ref.ly/Judg3:28-Judg3:30)** *See also* [JDG 20:17](https://ref.ly/Judg20:17); [2SA 24:9](https://ref.ly/2Sam24:9)

### Some swordsmen showed great strength and ability

**[2SA 23:10](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:10)** *See also* [1SA 21:8–21:9](https://ref.ly/1Sam21:8-1Sam21:9); [2SA 23:12](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:12)

The sword used for other purposes
---------------------------------

### As an instrument of execution and decapitation

**[2SA 1:14–1:16](https://ref.ly/2Sam1:14-2Sam1:16)** *See also* [1SA 15:33](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:33), [1SA 17:51](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:51), [1SA 22:18–22:19](https://ref.ly/1Sam22:18-1Sam22:19); [1KI 19:1](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:1); [ACT 12:2](https://ref.ly/Acts12:2); [ROM 8:35](https://ref.ly/Rom8:35)

### For murder

**[2KI 19:37](https://ref.ly/2Kgs19:37)** *See also* [JOS 6:21](https://ref.ly/Josh6:21); [1KI 2:34](https://ref.ly/1Kgs2:34); [ACT 12:2](https://ref.ly/Acts12:2)

### For committing suicide

**[1SA 31:4–31:6](https://ref.ly/1Sam31:4-1Sam31:6)** parallel [1CH 10:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr10:4) *See also* [ACT 16:27](https://ref.ly/Acts16:27)

Metaphorical use of the sword
-----------------------------

### To symbolise God's opposition to the sinful

**[GEN 3:24](https://ref.ly/Gen3:24)** *See also* [NUM 22:21–22:23](https://ref.ly/Num22:21-Num22:23), [NUM 22:31](https://ref.ly/Num22:31); [DEU 33:29](https://ref.ly/Deut33:29)

### To symbolise warfare

[LEV 26:6–26:8](https://ref.ly/Lev26:6-Lev26:8)

### To symbolise temporal power

**[EZK 32:11–32:12](https://ref.ly/Ezek32:11-Ezek32:12)** *See also* [PSA 45:3](https://ref.ly/Ps45:3); [MIC 5:6](https://ref.ly/Mic5:6)

### To symbolise suffering

**[LUK 2:34–2:35](https://ref.ly/Luke2:34-Luke2:35)**

### To symbolise judgment

**[EXO 5:3](https://ref.ly/Exod5:3)**; **[MAT 10:34](https://ref.ly/Matt10:34)** *See also* [JDG 7:20–7:22](https://ref.ly/Judg7:20-Judg7:22); [2CH 20:9](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:9); [PSA 7:12](https://ref.ly/Ps7:12); [ISA 31:8](https://ref.ly/Isa31:8); [JER 50:35–50:37](https://ref.ly/Jer50:35-Jer50:37); [EZK 11:8–11:10](https://ref.ly/Ezek11:8-Ezek11:10), [EZK 21:3](https://ref.ly/Ezek21:3), [EZK 33:2](https://ref.ly/Ezek33:2)
