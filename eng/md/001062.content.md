## 5491: refugees (id: 5491)

Fugitives driven from home because of personal, political or religious persecution, or because of physical danger.

Examples of refugees
--------------------

[GEN 16:6–16:8](https://ref.ly/Gen16:6-Gen16:8) *Hagar*; ; [GEN 46:1](https://ref.ly/Gen46:1); ; [EXO 12:37–12:39](https://ref.ly/Exod12:37-Exod12:39); ; [RUT 1:1](https://ref.ly/Ruth1:1); ; [MAT 2:13–2:15](https://ref.ly/Matt2:13-Matt2:15) *Mary, Joseph and Jesus Christ*; ; [ACT 18:2](https://ref.ly/Acts18:2)
