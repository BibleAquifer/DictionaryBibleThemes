## 1170: God, unity of (id: 1170)

God acts as a unity in all his deeds. The three persons of the Trinity are united in one Godhead. They are interrelated with one another, share the same attributes and co\-operate in the same work. God's characteristics are consistent, expressing his integral divine nature.

God is one
----------

**[DEU 6:4](https://ref.ly/Deut6:4)** *See also* [MRK 12:29](https://ref.ly/Mark12:29), [MRK 12:32](https://ref.ly/Mark12:32); [1CO 8:4](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:4); [GAL 3:20](https://ref.ly/Gal3:20); [EPH 4:5–4:6](https://ref.ly/Eph4:5-Eph4:6); [1TI 2:5](https://ref.ly/1Tim2:5); [JAS 2:19](https://ref.ly/Jas2:19)
