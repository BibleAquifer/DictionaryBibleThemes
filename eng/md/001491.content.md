## 5979: waste (id: 5979)

The misuse and squandering of resources. God condemns the waste of resources which he provides, especially spiritual provisions such as grace and opportunity. Sometimes extravagance in God's service is mistaken for waste.

Waste of material resources
---------------------------

### Squandering is folly

**[LUK 15:13–15:16](https://ref.ly/Luke15:13-Luke15:16)** *See also* [PRO 21:20](https://ref.ly/Prov21:20), [PRO 29:3](https://ref.ly/Prov29:3); [LUK 16:1](https://ref.ly/Luke16:1); [JAS 5:1–5:6](https://ref.ly/Jas5:1-Jas5:6); [1PE 4:3–4:5](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:3-1Pet4:5)

### As a consequence of yielding to temptation

**[PRO 29:3](https://ref.ly/Prov29:3)** *See also* [PRO 5:7–5:14](https://ref.ly/Prov5:7-Prov5:14)

### Jesus Christ avoids waste

**[JHN 6:12–6:13](https://ref.ly/John6:12-John6:13)**

### Spiritual teaching

**[MAT 7:6](https://ref.ly/Matt7:6)** *Spiritual teaching is wasted if inappropriate to the spiritual capacity of the hearers.* *See also* [PRO 9:7](https://ref.ly/Prov9:7), [PRO 23:9](https://ref.ly/Prov23:9); [1CO 2:14](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:14)

Examples of wasted lives
------------------------

### Esau

Esau lived for the pleasures of the moment and threw away his spiritual inheritance: [GEN 25:29–25:34](https://ref.ly/Gen25:29-Gen25:34); ; [HEB 12:16](https://ref.ly/Heb12:16)

### Samson

[JDG 16:18–16:21](https://ref.ly/Judg16:18-Judg16:21) *Though Samson did retrieve something at the end of his life, his spiritual gift of supernatural strength was largely wasted through his indulgence in sexual immorality.*

### King Saul

[1SA 16:21](https://ref.ly/1Sam16:21); ; [2SA 1:17–1:27](https://ref.ly/2Sam1:17-2Sam1:27) *Through natural strength and spiritual gifts Saul had the prospect of establishing a successful royal dynasty. The future was lost through disobedience and the end of his reign was wasted through jealousy.*

### Judas Iscariot

[MAT 27:3–27:5](https://ref.ly/Matt27:3-Matt27:5) *Judas had the best of opportunities\-\-three years with Jesus Christ. This was thrown away in a moment of personal ambition.*
