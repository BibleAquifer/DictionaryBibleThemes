## 6735: repentance, examples of (id: 6735)

Scripture provides examples to illustrate the importance of repentance for individuals and communities.

The repentance of individuals
-----------------------------

### The call to personal repentance

**[ACT 2:38](https://ref.ly/Acts2:38)** *See also* [2TI 2:19](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:19)

### Examples of individual repentance

[NUM 22:31–22:35](https://ref.ly/Num22:31-Num22:35) *Balaam*; ; [2SA 24:10](https://ref.ly/2Sam24:10) parallel [1CH 21:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr21:8) *David, after taking a census*; ; [1KI 21:27–21:29](https://ref.ly/1Kgs21:27-1Kgs21:29) *Ahab*; ; [2KI 22:19](https://ref.ly/2Kgs22:19) *Josiah*; ; [JOB 42:6](https://ref.ly/Job42:6) *Job*; ; [PSA 51:1–51:17](https://ref.ly/Ps51:1-Ps51:17) *David, following his adultery with Bathsheba*; ; [LUK 15:21](https://ref.ly/Luke15:21) *the prodigal son*; ; [LUK 18:13](https://ref.ly/Luke18:13) *the tax collector in the temple*; ; [MAT 26:75](https://ref.ly/Matt26:75) parallels [MRK 14:72](https://ref.ly/Mark14:72), [LUK 22:61–22:62](https://ref.ly/Luke22:61-Luke22:62) *Peter's sorrow after denying Jesus Christ*; ; [JHN 21:15–21:17](https://ref.ly/John21:15-John21:17) *Peter's confession and reinstatement*; ; [ACT 8:22–8:24](https://ref.ly/Acts8:22-Acts8:24) *Simon the sorcerer's repentance may not have been genuine.*

Corporate repentance
