## 4925: delay, divine (id: 4925)

Delays by God do not result from any failure on his part, but reflect unrealistic human expectations concerning his actions. God delays his vengeance in order to give time for human repentance.

God delays to allow for repentance
----------------------------------

### To give time for repentance

**[LUK 13:6–13:9](https://ref.ly/Luke13:6-Luke13:9)**; **[ROM 2:4](https://ref.ly/Rom2:4)**; **[2PE 3:8–3:9](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:8-2Pet3:9)** *See also* [MAT 3:7–3:11](https://ref.ly/Matt3:7-Matt3:11) parallel [LUK 3:7–3:10](https://ref.ly/Luke3:7-Luke3:10); [1TI 2:4](https://ref.ly/1Tim2:4); [JAS 5:7](https://ref.ly/Jas5:7); [REV 2:21](https://ref.ly/Rev2:21)

**[1KI 21:29](https://ref.ly/1Kgs21:29)** *See also* [1KI 21:21–21:28](https://ref.ly/1Kgs21:21-1Kgs21:28)
