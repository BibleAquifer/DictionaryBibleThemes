## 1035: God, faithfulness of (id: 1035)

God's perfect loyalty and consistency in being true to his name, his character and his word.

God's faithfulness is an integral part of his nature
----------------------------------------------------

**[NUM 23:19](https://ref.ly/Num23:19)** *God's limitless power and steadfast love imply complete faithfulness which contrasts with human nature*; **[LAM 3:22–3:23](https://ref.ly/Lam3:22-Lam3:23)** *See also* [EXO 34:6](https://ref.ly/Exod34:6); [DEU 7:8–7:9](https://ref.ly/Deut7:8-Deut7:9), [DEU 32:4](https://ref.ly/Deut32:4); [ROM 4:21](https://ref.ly/Rom4:21); [2TI 2:13](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:13)

God is faithful to his name and character
-----------------------------------------

**[2TI 2:13](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:13)** *See also* [NEH 9:8](https://ref.ly/Neh9:8); [PSA 106:8](https://ref.ly/Ps106:8); [1TH 5:24](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:24); [HEB 6:13–6:18](https://ref.ly/Heb6:13-Heb6:18)
