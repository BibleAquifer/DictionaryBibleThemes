## 5523: servants, good (id: 5523)

Scripture identifies certain qualities which are found in good servants and requires that these qualities should be found in believers' service of God.

Qualities found in good servants
--------------------------------

### Diligence

**[ECC 9:10](https://ref.ly/Eccl9:10)** *See also* [1KI 11:28](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:28); [PRO 10:4](https://ref.ly/Prov10:4), [PRO 22:29](https://ref.ly/Prov22:29); [MAT 25:14–25:23](https://ref.ly/Matt25:14-Matt25:23) parallel [LUK 19:12–19:19](https://ref.ly/Luke19:12-Luke19:19)

### Faithfulness

**[1CO 4:2](https://ref.ly/1Cor4:2)** *See also* [EXO 21:5–21:6](https://ref.ly/Exod21:5-Exod21:6); [2SA 15:21](https://ref.ly/2Sam15:21); [2CH 34:11–34:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr34:11-2Chr34:12); [NEH 13:13](https://ref.ly/Neh13:13); [PRO 25:13](https://ref.ly/Prov25:13); [DAN 6:4](https://ref.ly/Dan6:4)

### Honesty

### Obedience

**[EPH 6:5](https://ref.ly/Eph6:5)** *See also* [LUK 17:10](https://ref.ly/Luke17:10); [COL 3:22](https://ref.ly/Col3:22)

**[EPH 6:6–6:7](https://ref.ly/Eph6:6-Eph6:7)** *See also* [1CH 28:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr28:21); [TIT 2:9](https://ref.ly/Titus2:9)
