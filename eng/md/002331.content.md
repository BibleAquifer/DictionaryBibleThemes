## 8672: striving with God (id: 8672)

Striving with God can refer both to the fruitless opposition of the ungodly to God's will and purposes, and to the struggle of the righteous with God, as they seek to find his will and obey it.

The striving of the wicked with God
-----------------------------------

### It is the result of a guilty conscience

**[ACT 26:14](https://ref.ly/Acts26:14)**

### Striving against God cannot succeed

**[2CH 13:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr13:12)** *See also* [PRO 21:30](https://ref.ly/Prov21:30); [ISA 8:10](https://ref.ly/Isa8:10); [ACT 11:17](https://ref.ly/Acts11:17)

### It leads to the destruction of those who attempt it

**[PSA 2:2–2:5](https://ref.ly/Ps2:2-Ps2:5)** *See also* [JOB 15:20–15:26](https://ref.ly/Job15:20-Job15:26), [JOB 15:30](https://ref.ly/Job15:30)

Ways in which the wicked strive with God
----------------------------------------

### By resisting the Holy Spirit

**[ACT 7:51](https://ref.ly/Acts7:51)**

### By arguing with God

**[ROM 9:20](https://ref.ly/Rom9:20)** *See also* [JOB 40:2](https://ref.ly/Job40:2); [ISA 45:9–45:11](https://ref.ly/Isa45:9-Isa45:11)

### Through unbelief

[NUM 14:1–14:4](https://ref.ly/Num14:1-Num14:4), [NUM 14:10–14:11](https://ref.ly/Num14:10-Num14:11), [NUM 20:13](https://ref.ly/Num20:13); ; [PSA 95:8–95:9](https://ref.ly/Ps95:8-Ps95:9)

### By attacking God's people

**[ACT 5:39](https://ref.ly/Acts5:39)** *To attack God's people is to attempt to attack God himself.* *See also* [1SA 17:45](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:45); [ISA 37:22–37:25](https://ref.ly/Isa37:22-Isa37:25); [ACT 9:4–9:5](https://ref.ly/Acts9:4-Acts9:5)

Jacob strives with God
----------------------

**[GEN 32:24–32:28](https://ref.ly/Gen32:24-Gen32:28)** *Jacob's struggle with God represents the conflict within himself between self\-will and dependence on God. As “Jacob”, the supplanter, he had overcome Esau and Laban, but God demonstrates his superior power in the wrestling match so that as “Israel” he will be submissive to the divine will.* *See also* [HOS 12:2–12:5](https://ref.ly/Hos12:2-Hos12:5)
