## 8205: childlikeness (id: 8205)

An attitude of simple trust and faith, to be distinguished from childishness. It is commended by Scripture as an appropriate attitude for the believer to take towards God.

God treats his people like children
-----------------------------------

**[PSA 103:13](https://ref.ly/Ps103:13)** *See also* [NUM 11:12](https://ref.ly/Num11:12); [ISA 49:15](https://ref.ly/Isa49:15), [ISA 66:13](https://ref.ly/Isa66:13); [JER 31:20](https://ref.ly/Jer31:20); [HOS 11:1–11:4](https://ref.ly/Hos11:1-Hos11:4); [ROM 8:14–8:17](https://ref.ly/Rom8:14-Rom8:17); [GAL 4:1–4:7](https://ref.ly/Gal4:1-Gal4:7)
