## 2215: Jesus Christ, Son of David (id: 2215)

A Messianic title. Jesus Christ comes as a descendant of David to fulfil God's promises in the OT of a future king who would establish the kingdom of God on earth.

OT promises of a future king from David's line
----------------------------------------------

**[2SA 7:12–7:14](https://ref.ly/2Sam7:12-2Sam7:14)** parallel [1CH 17:11–17:13](https://ref.ly/1Chr17:11-1Chr17:13) *These promises are known as “the Davidic covenant”*; **[ISA 16:5](https://ref.ly/Isa16:5)** *See also* [PSA 132:11–132:12](https://ref.ly/Ps132:11-Ps132:12), [PSA 132:17](https://ref.ly/Ps132:17); [ISA 9:7](https://ref.ly/Isa9:7), [ISA 11:1](https://ref.ly/Isa11:1); [JER 23:5](https://ref.ly/Jer23:5), [JER 33:15](https://ref.ly/Jer33:15); [EZK 34:23–34:24](https://ref.ly/Ezek34:23-Ezek34:24), [EZK 37:24–37:25](https://ref.ly/Ezek37:24-Ezek37:25); [JHN 7:42](https://ref.ly/John7:42)

NT statements verifying that Jesus Christ is descended from David
-----------------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 1:1](https://ref.ly/Matt1:1)** *See also* [LUK 3:23](https://ref.ly/Luke3:23), [LUK 3:31](https://ref.ly/Luke3:31); [ROM 1:3](https://ref.ly/Rom1:3); [2TI 2:8](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:8); [REV 22:16](https://ref.ly/Rev22:16)

Jesus Christ inherits the throne of David
-----------------------------------------

**[LUK 1:32–1:33](https://ref.ly/Luke1:32-Luke1:33)** *See also* [LUK 1:69](https://ref.ly/Luke1:69); [JHN 1:49](https://ref.ly/John1:49), [JHN 18:37](https://ref.ly/John18:37); [MAT 27:37](https://ref.ly/Matt27:37) parallels [MRK 15:26](https://ref.ly/Mark15:26), [LUK 23:38](https://ref.ly/Luke23:38), [JHN 19:19](https://ref.ly/John19:19)

Jesus Christ is more than a merely human king descended from David
------------------------------------------------------------------

**[MRK 12:35–12:37](https://ref.ly/Mark12:35-Mark12:37)** *Jesus Christ claim is that he is not simply a human or political king, he is the Lord, the Son of God.* *See also* [PSA 110:1](https://ref.ly/Ps110:1); [JHN 18:36](https://ref.ly/John18:36)
