## 2069: Jesus Christ, pre-eminence of (id: 2069)

Jesus Christ excels over all of creation and reigns supreme over everthing in the created order.

The scope of Jesus Christ's pre\-eminence
-----------------------------------------

### Over all people

**[JHN 17:2](https://ref.ly/John17:2)** *See also* [MAT 25:31–25:32](https://ref.ly/Matt25:31-Matt25:32); [ROM 10:12](https://ref.ly/Rom10:12)

### Over his enemies

**[1CO 15:25–15:26](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:25-1Cor15:26)** *See also* [ACT 2:32–2:35](https://ref.ly/Acts2:32-Acts2:35); [PSA 110:1](https://ref.ly/Ps110:1); [EPH 4:8](https://ref.ly/Eph4:8) *Paul probably interpreted “captives” to refer to the spiritual enemies of Christ defeated at the cross.*

### Over every power and authority

**[COL 2:15](https://ref.ly/Col2:15)** *See also* [EPH 1:21](https://ref.ly/Eph1:21)

### Over all traditions and institutions

[MAT 12:8](https://ref.ly/Matt12:8) parallels [MRK 2:28](https://ref.ly/Mark2:28), [LUK 6:5](https://ref.ly/Luke6:5)

### Over all things

**[1CO 15:28](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:28)** *See also* [MAT 11:27](https://ref.ly/Matt11:27) parallel [LUK 10:22](https://ref.ly/Luke10:22); [MAT 28:18](https://ref.ly/Matt28:18); [JHN 3:35](https://ref.ly/John3:35), [JHN 13:3](https://ref.ly/John13:3), [JHN 16:33](https://ref.ly/John16:33); [ACT 10:36](https://ref.ly/Acts10:36); [PHP 3:21](https://ref.ly/Phil3:21); [HEB 2:8](https://ref.ly/Heb2:8)

Descriptions of Jesus Christ's pre\-eminence
--------------------------------------------

### He is the head

**[COL 2:10](https://ref.ly/Col2:10)** *See also* [EPH 1:9–1:10](https://ref.ly/Eph1:9-Eph1:10), [EPH 1:22](https://ref.ly/Eph1:22), [EPH 4:15](https://ref.ly/Eph4:15), [EPH 5:23–5:24](https://ref.ly/Eph5:23-Eph5:24); [COL 1:18](https://ref.ly/Col1:18)

### He is the capstone

**[MAT 21:42](https://ref.ly/Matt21:42)** parallels [MRK 12:10–12:11](https://ref.ly/Mark12:10-Mark12:11), [LUK 20:17](https://ref.ly/Luke20:17) *By so saying Jesus Christ applied this verse to himself. The capstone/cornerstone was the most significant stone of a building's structure.* *See also* [PSA 118:22](https://ref.ly/Ps118:22); [ACT 4:11](https://ref.ly/Acts4:11); [EPH 2:20](https://ref.ly/Eph2:20); [1PE 2:7](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:7)

### He is the Chief Shepherd

[JHN 10:11](https://ref.ly/John10:11); ; [HEB 13:20](https://ref.ly/Heb13:20); ; [1PE 5:4](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:4)

The reasons for Jesus Christ's pre\-eminence
--------------------------------------------

### His divine origin

**[JHN 3:31](https://ref.ly/John3:31)** *See also* [JHN 1:1–1:2](https://ref.ly/John1:1-John1:2), [JHN 17:24](https://ref.ly/John17:24)

### His equality with the Father

**[JHN 10:30](https://ref.ly/John10:30)** *See also* [COL 1:19](https://ref.ly/Col1:19), [COL 2:9](https://ref.ly/Col2:9)

### His authority derives from the Father

[JHN 5:22–5:27](https://ref.ly/John5:22-John5:27)

### He possesses the rights of the firstborn

**[COL 1:15–1:17](https://ref.ly/Col1:15-Col1:17)** *As the firstborn son has the rights and privileges of an heir, so Jesus Christ is heir of all things by virtue of his pre\-existence and sonship.* *See also* [ROM 8:29](https://ref.ly/Rom8:29); [HEB 1:6](https://ref.ly/Heb1:6)

### He has a superior name

**[PHP 2:9](https://ref.ly/Phil2:9)** *See also* [ACT 4:12](https://ref.ly/Acts4:12); [HEB 1:4](https://ref.ly/Heb1:4)

### He is the First and the Last

**[REV 1:17](https://ref.ly/Rev1:17)** *This title implies that all things are summed up in Christ and find their purpose in him.* *See also* [REV 2:8](https://ref.ly/Rev2:8), [REV 22:13](https://ref.ly/Rev22:13)
