## 8846: ungodliness (id: 8846)

The state of believing in God, while adopting a lifestyle which seems to deny this. Believers are warned to guard against ungodliness.

Expressions of ungodliness
--------------------------

### A defiant spirit towards God

**[MAT 7:6](https://ref.ly/Matt7:6)** *Jesus Christ is teaching his disciples not to share the riches of spiritual truth with persons who are persistently unappreciative, vicious and defiant.* *See also* [JOB 36:8–36:13](https://ref.ly/Job36:8-Job36:13); [PSA 73:8–73:11](https://ref.ly/Ps73:8-Ps73:11)

### Mocking God and/or his people

**[2PE 3:3–3:4](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:3-2Pet3:4)** *See also* [PSA 35:15–35:16](https://ref.ly/Ps35:15-Ps35:16); [ACT 13:40–13:41](https://ref.ly/Acts13:40-Acts13:41); [HAB 1:5](https://ref.ly/Hab1:5)

### Evil behaviour

**[ISA 32:6–32:7](https://ref.ly/Isa32:6-Isa32:7)** *See also* [PRO 11:9](https://ref.ly/Prov11:9); [JON 1:1–1:2](https://ref.ly/Jonah1:1-Jonah1:2); [1TI 1:9–1:11](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:9-1Tim1:11)

-----------------------

[2CH 36:15–36:16](https://ref.ly/2Chr36:15-2Chr36:16) *the southern kingdom of Judah*; ; [PSA 52:1–52:3](https://ref.ly/Ps52:1-Ps52:3) *Doeg the Edomite*; ; [ISA 9:13–9:17](https://ref.ly/Isa9:13-Isa9:17) *the northern kingdom of Israel*; [JER 23:11–23:12](https://ref.ly/Jer23:11-Jer23:12), [JER 23:15–23:17](https://ref.ly/Jer23:15-Jer23:17); [1CO 6:9–6:11](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:9-1Cor6:11); ; [EPH 2:1–2:3](https://ref.ly/Eph2:1-Eph2:3); [2TI 3:1–3:5](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:1-2Tim3:5) *people in general during the last days (the time between the first and second comings of Jesus Christ)*; ; [HEB 12:16](https://ref.ly/Heb12:16) *Esau*
