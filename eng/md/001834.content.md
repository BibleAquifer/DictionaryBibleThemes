## 7431: Sabbath, sabbatical year (id: 7431)

Just as the seventh day was observed as a day of rest, so the OT regarded every seventh year as a sabbath year, to be set aside for rest.

The sabbath year
----------------

### As a time for leaving fields fallow

**[EXO 23:10–23:11](https://ref.ly/Exod23:10-Exod23:11)** *See also* [LEV 25:4](https://ref.ly/Lev25:4), [LEV 26:34](https://ref.ly/Lev26:34); [NEH 10:31](https://ref.ly/Neh10:31) *An important feature was the opportunity for the poor to benefit from unharvested fields.*

### As a time for cancelling debts

**[DEU 15:1](https://ref.ly/Deut15:1)** *See also* [DEU 15:9](https://ref.ly/Deut15:9), [DEU 31:10](https://ref.ly/Deut31:10); [NEH 10:31](https://ref.ly/Neh10:31)

### As a time for freeing slaves

**[DEU 15:12](https://ref.ly/Deut15:12)** *See also* [JER 34:14](https://ref.ly/Jer34:14)
