## 8209: commitment, to Jesus Christ (id: 8209)

Commitment to Jesus Christ is grounded in the knowledge of his saving power and divinity, and expresses itself in adoration and obedience to Jesus Christ.

Jesus Christ commanded people to make the commitment to follow him
------------------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 4:19](https://ref.ly/Matt4:19)** parallel [MRK 1:17](https://ref.ly/Mark1:17)**[MAT 9:9](https://ref.ly/Matt9:9)** parallels [MRK 2:14](https://ref.ly/Mark2:14), [LUK 5:27](https://ref.ly/Luke5:27) *See also* [MAT 19:21–19:22](https://ref.ly/Matt19:21-Matt19:22); [JHN 1:43](https://ref.ly/John1:43), [JHN 21:19](https://ref.ly/John21:19), [JHN 21:22](https://ref.ly/John21:22); [ROM 15:5](https://ref.ly/Rom15:5); [1CO 1:12](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:12) *There may have been a “Christ\-party” at Corinth, or in contrast with the sectarianism, Paul may be asserting his own commitment to Jesus Christ.*
