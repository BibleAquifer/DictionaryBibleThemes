## 5137: bones (id: 5137)

Associated in Hebrew thought with both death and life. A symbol of the whole person, and also the state of one's well\-being.

Bones represent dead bodies
---------------------------

### Human remains to be treated with respect

**[1SA 31:13](https://ref.ly/1Sam31:13)** parallel [1CH 10:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr10:12) *See also* [2SA 21:12–21:14](https://ref.ly/2Sam21:12-2Sam21:14); [1KI 13:31](https://ref.ly/1Kgs13:31); [2KI 23:18](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:18); [EZK 39:15](https://ref.ly/Ezek39:15)

### Non\-burial as a sign of disrespect

**[AMO 2:1–2:2](https://ref.ly/Amos2:1-Amos2:2)**

### Non\-burial as a sign of God's judgment

**[PSA 53:5](https://ref.ly/Ps53:5)** *See also* [2KI 9:34–9:37](https://ref.ly/2Kgs9:34-2Kgs9:37); [PSA 141:7](https://ref.ly/Ps141:7); [JER 8:1–8:2](https://ref.ly/Jer8:1-Jer8:2); [EZK 24:4–24:5](https://ref.ly/Ezek24:4-Ezek24:5), [EZK 24:10](https://ref.ly/Ezek24:10)

Bones were considered to be unclean
-----------------------------------

### Bones made anyone who touched them unclean

**[NUM 19:16](https://ref.ly/Num19:16)** *See also* [NUM 19:18](https://ref.ly/Num19:18); [MAT 23:27](https://ref.ly/Matt23:27)

### Bones used to desecrate objects of pagan worship

**[2KI 23:14](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:14)** *See also* [1KI 13:2](https://ref.ly/1Kgs13:2); [2KI 23:16](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:16), [2KI 23:20](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:20); [2CH 34:5](https://ref.ly/2Chr34:5); [EZK 6:5](https://ref.ly/Ezek6:5)

Bones as a sign of hope
-----------------------

### The hope of God's promise

**[GEN 50:25](https://ref.ly/Gen50:25)** *See also* [EXO 13:19](https://ref.ly/Exod13:19); [JOS 24:32](https://ref.ly/Josh24:32); [HEB 11:22](https://ref.ly/Heb11:22) *The presence of Joseph's bones among the Israelites was a constant reminder of God's promise to Joseph concerning the promised land.*

### The hope of life after death

**[EZK 37:1–37:14](https://ref.ly/Ezek37:1-Ezek37:14)** *See also* [2KI 13:21](https://ref.ly/2Kgs13:21)

### The hope of the Passover lamb

**[JHN 19:36](https://ref.ly/John19:36)** *See also* [EXO 12:46](https://ref.ly/Exod12:46); [NUM 9:12](https://ref.ly/Num9:12); [PSA 34:20](https://ref.ly/Ps34:20) *Jesus Christ fulfilled the symbolism of the unbroken bones of the Passover lamb.*
