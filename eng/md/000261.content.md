## 3203: Holy Spirit, and assurance (id: 3203)

The Holy Spirit assures believers of their standing in Christ and their eternal salvation.

The Holy Spirit makes an individual a member of Christ
------------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 8:9](https://ref.ly/Rom8:9)** *See also* [1CO 2:12](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:12), [1CO 6:11](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:11), [1CO 6:17](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:17); [1TH 4:8](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:8)

The Holy Spirit assures believers that they have been born anew
---------------------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 2:29](https://ref.ly/Rom2:29)** *The true sign of belonging to God is not any outward mark on the body, but the regenerating power of the Spirit within.* *See also* [JHN 3:5–3:6](https://ref.ly/John3:5-John3:6); [ROM 7:6](https://ref.ly/Rom7:6); [2CO 3:6](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:6); [GAL 4:29](https://ref.ly/Gal4:29); [TIT 3:5–3:7](https://ref.ly/Titus3:5-Titus3:7)

The Holy Spirit assures believers that they are God's children
--------------------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 8:14–8:16](https://ref.ly/Rom8:14-Rom8:16)** *See also* [GAL 4:6](https://ref.ly/Gal4:6)

The Holy Spirit assures believers that God loves them
-----------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 5:5](https://ref.ly/Rom5:5)**

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-----------------------------------------------------------------

**[GAL 3:5](https://ref.ly/Gal3:5)** *See also* [1CO 2:4–2:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:4-1Cor2:5)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 7:15–7:20](https://ref.ly/Matt7:15-Matt7:20)** *Spiritual gifts alone are not a sufficient basis for assurance.* *See also* [ACT 13:52](https://ref.ly/Acts13:52); [ROM 8:6](https://ref.ly/Rom8:6), [ROM 14:17](https://ref.ly/Rom14:17); [2CO 6:6](https://ref.ly/2Cor6:6); [GAL 5:16–5:25](https://ref.ly/Gal5:16-Gal5:25); [1TH 1:6](https://ref.ly/1Thess1:6)
