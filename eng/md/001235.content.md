## 5692: friends, bad (id: 5692)

Those who are close or who exercise influence, who prove to be unreliable or deceptive or who lead astray. Scripture provides numerous instructive examples of the negative effects of bad friends upon believers.

Friends who lead astray
-----------------------

### From worship

**[DEU 13:6–13:8](https://ref.ly/Deut13:6-Deut13:8)** *See also* [DEU 23:6](https://ref.ly/Deut23:6); [1KI 11:2](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:2)

### From good character

**[1CO 15:33](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:33)** *See also* [PRO 12:26](https://ref.ly/Prov12:26), [PRO 22:24–22:25](https://ref.ly/Prov22:24-Prov22:25)

### From friendship with God

**[JAS 4:4](https://ref.ly/Jas4:4)** *See also* [ISA 52:11](https://ref.ly/Isa52:11); [JER 9:4–9:5](https://ref.ly/Jer9:4-Jer9:5); [MIC 7:5](https://ref.ly/Mic7:5); [2CO 6:14–6:18](https://ref.ly/2Cor6:14-2Cor6:18), [2CO 7:1](https://ref.ly/2Cor7:1)
