## 5091: Deborah (id: 5091)

An outstanding leader in Israel when the nation faced a crisis of resurgent Canaanite power in the time of the judges. Her name means “bee”.

Deborah's calling
-----------------

### As a judge

[JDG 4:4–4:5](https://ref.ly/Judg4:4-Judg4:5), [JDG 5:7](https://ref.ly/Judg5:7) *She was probably called upon to settle intertribal disputes, “mother in Israel” meaning one who promotes the unity of God's family.*

### As a prophetess

[JDG 4:4](https://ref.ly/Judg4:4), [JDG 4:7](https://ref.ly/Judg4:7), [JDG 4:9](https://ref.ly/Judg4:9), [JDG 4:14](https://ref.ly/Judg4:14)

Deborah's response to the Canaanite threat
------------------------------------------

### She was petitioned by the people

[JDG 5:11–5:12](https://ref.ly/Judg5:11-Judg5:12)

### She appointed Barak commander\-in\-chief

[JDG 4:6](https://ref.ly/Judg4:6)

### She accompanied the army into battle

[JDG 4:8–4:10](https://ref.ly/Judg4:8-Judg4:10)

### She led the people in victory celebration

[JDG 5:1–5:3](https://ref.ly/Judg5:1-Judg5:3), [JDG 5:28–5:31](https://ref.ly/Judg5:28-Judg5:31) *In this respect she is like Moses and Miriam ([Ex 15:1,20](https://ref.ly/Exod15:1,Exod15:20)).*

Deborah's character
-------------------

### She was wise, decisive, courageous, inspiring and bold

[JDG 4:5–4:9](https://ref.ly/Judg4:5-Judg4:9), [JDG 4:14](https://ref.ly/Judg4:14), [JDG 5:9](https://ref.ly/Judg5:9), [JDG 5:15–5:17](https://ref.ly/Judg5:15-Judg5:17) *Her confidence was in God's word and promise and she did not shrink from rebuking God's people.*

### She gave credit to others

[JDG 5:14–5:15](https://ref.ly/Judg5:14-Judg5:15), [JDG 5:18–5:19](https://ref.ly/Judg5:18-Judg5:19), [JDG 5:24–5:27](https://ref.ly/Judg5:24-Judg5:27)

### She gave glory to God

[JDG 5:20–5:23](https://ref.ly/Judg5:20-Judg5:23) *These verses are a poetic acknowledgment that God has fought on Israel's behalf.*
