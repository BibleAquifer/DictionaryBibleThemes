## 4817: drought, spiritual (id: 4817)

Scripture indicates that believers, both as individuals and communities, may go through periods in which they fail to experience the refreshing and life\-giving presence of God. One cause of such spiritual thirst is unbelief. Scripture also indicates ways in which this thirst may be relieved.
