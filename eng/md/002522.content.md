## 9530: Hades (id: 9530)

A term used in the NT to refer to the place in which departed spirits rest, while waiting for judgment.

Hades is the place where the dead wait for judgment
---------------------------------------------------

**[REV 20:13–20:14](https://ref.ly/Rev20:13-Rev20:14)** *See also* [MAT 11:23](https://ref.ly/Matt11:23) parallel [LUK 10:15](https://ref.ly/Luke10:15) *translated “depths”*; [LUK 16:23](https://ref.ly/Luke16:23) *translated “hell”*[ACT 2:27](https://ref.ly/Acts2:27), [ACT 2:31](https://ref.ly/Acts2:31)[REV 6:8](https://ref.ly/Rev6:8)

Jesus Christ is Lord of Hades
