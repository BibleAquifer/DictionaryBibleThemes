## 4648: goad (id: 4648)

A spike used to drive animals on. The term is also figuratively applied to prompting and guiding people into action.

The goad as a farming implement
-------------------------------

### Used with animals

[1SA 13:21](https://ref.ly/1Sam13:21)

### Used as a weapon

[JDG 3:31](https://ref.ly/Judg3:31)

Goading as a symbol of discipline
---------------------------------

### God's word as a goad

**[ECC 12:11](https://ref.ly/Eccl12:11)** *See also* [ACT 2:37](https://ref.ly/Acts2:37)

### Conscience as a goad

**[ACT 26:14](https://ref.ly/Acts26:14)** *See also* [EXO 25:2](https://ref.ly/Exod25:2); [JOB 20:2](https://ref.ly/Job20:2); [ROM 2:15](https://ref.ly/Rom2:15)

### Encouragement as a goad

**[HEB 10:24](https://ref.ly/Heb10:24)** *See also* [1TH 1:3](https://ref.ly/1Thess1:3) *The promptings of love and faith may be seen as symbolic goads.*
