## 8666: praise, manner and methods of (id: 8666)

Praise is the natural response of believers to God at all times and in all places, involving adoration in music and song. God himself assists believers to praise him through his Spirit.

The manner of praise
--------------------

### God is praised in faith

**[PSA 28:7](https://ref.ly/Ps28:7), [PSA 106:12](https://ref.ly/Ps106:12)**

### God is praised through Jesus Christ

**[HEB 13:15](https://ref.ly/Heb13:15)** *See also* [PHP 2:9–2:11](https://ref.ly/Phil2:9-Phil2:11)

### God can be praised at any time and in any place

**[PSA 104:33](https://ref.ly/Ps104:33), [PSA 145:2](https://ref.ly/Ps145:2)** *See also* [PSA 146:2](https://ref.ly/Ps146:2)

**[PSA 51:15](https://ref.ly/Ps51:15)** *See also* [PSA 40:3](https://ref.ly/Ps40:3); [ISA 61:3](https://ref.ly/Isa61:3)
