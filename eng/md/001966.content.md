## 7936: love feast (id: 7936)

The first Christians generally celebrated the Lord's Supper with the accompaniment of a meal, called the “agape” or “love feast”.

OT antecedents of the love feast
--------------------------------

### The Passover meal

*See also* [EXO 12:3–12:11](https://ref.ly/Exod12:3-Exod12:11), [EXO 12:15–12:16](https://ref.ly/Exod12:15-Exod12:16); [MAT 26:17–26:18](https://ref.ly/Matt26:17-Matt26:18) parallels [MRK 14:12–14:15](https://ref.ly/Mark14:12-Mark14:15), [LUK 22:7–22:12](https://ref.ly/Luke22:7-Luke22:12); [1CO 11:23–11:25](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:23-1Cor11:25)
