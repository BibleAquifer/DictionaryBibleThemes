## 2218: Jesus Christ, Son of God (id: 2218)

A title emphasising Jesus Christ's deity, his office as Messiah and his pre\-eminence as the object of the church's faith and worship and as the content of its gospel.

\`\`Son of God'' in the OT
--------------------------

### Israel as God's son

**[EXO 4:22–4:23](https://ref.ly/Exod4:22-Exod4:23)** *See also* [JER 31:9](https://ref.ly/Jer31:9), [JER 31:20](https://ref.ly/Jer31:20); [HOS 11:1](https://ref.ly/Hos11:1)

### The future Messiah/king as God's Son

**[PSA 2:7](https://ref.ly/Ps2:7)** *See also* [PSA 89:27](https://ref.ly/Ps89:27); [ACT 13:33](https://ref.ly/Acts13:33); [HEB 1:5](https://ref.ly/Heb1:5), [HEB 5:5](https://ref.ly/Heb5:5) *Jesus Christ perfectly fulfils all the OT promises concerning God's son, whether referring originally to Israel or to the Messiah.*
