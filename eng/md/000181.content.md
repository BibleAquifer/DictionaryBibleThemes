## 2303: Jesus Christ, as creator (id: 2303)

Scripture identifies the pre\-existent Jesus Christ as involved in the work of creation, and relates this to his work in redemption, by which a new creation is brought out of the ruins of the old.

Jesus Christ's creation of the present world
--------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ created all things

**[JHN 1:3](https://ref.ly/John1:3)** *See also* [JHN 1:10](https://ref.ly/John1:10); [ACT 3:15](https://ref.ly/Acts3:15); [1CO 8:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:6); [COL 1:15–1:16](https://ref.ly/Col1:15-Col1:16); [HEB 1:2](https://ref.ly/Heb1:2)

### Jesus Christ sustains the created universe

**[HEB 1:3](https://ref.ly/Heb1:3)** *See also* [1CO 8:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:6); [COL 1:17](https://ref.ly/Col1:17); [REV 3:14](https://ref.ly/Rev3:14) *The word translated “ruler” can mean either “beginning” or “first in rank”.*

### Jesus Christ will bring the entire work of creation to perfection

**[EPH 1:9–1:10](https://ref.ly/Eph1:9-Eph1:10)** *See also* [ROM 8:19–8:22](https://ref.ly/Rom8:19-Rom8:22); [COL 1:20](https://ref.ly/Col1:20)

Jesus Christ makes a new creation possible
------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ recreates people through a new birth

**[2CO 5:17](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:17)** *See also* [JHN 1:12–1:13](https://ref.ly/John1:12-John1:13), [JHN 3:5–3:6](https://ref.ly/John3:5-John3:6); [GAL 6:15](https://ref.ly/Gal6:15); [JAS 1:18](https://ref.ly/Jas1:18)

### Jesus Christ's work of new creation should be evident in believers' lives

**[EPH 4:24](https://ref.ly/Eph4:24)** *See also* [EPH 3:15](https://ref.ly/Eph3:15); [COL 3:10](https://ref.ly/Col3:10)

### Through Jesus Christ a new heaven and earth will be created

**[2PE 3:13](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:13)** *The promise was given in [Isa 65:17](https://ref.ly/Isa65:17); [66:22](https://ref.ly/Isa66:22).* *See also* [REV 21:1](https://ref.ly/Rev21:1), [REV 21:4–21:5](https://ref.ly/Rev21:4-Rev21:5)
