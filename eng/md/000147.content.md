## 2021: Jesus Christ, faithfulness of (id: 2021)

The total reliability and constancy of Jesus Christ is shown in his personal character, and made known by his words and works.

Jesus Christ's faithfulness is seen in his character
----------------------------------------------------

### He is faithful by nature

**[REV 19:11](https://ref.ly/Rev19:11)** *A description of the exalted Christ.* *See also* [REV 1:5](https://ref.ly/Rev1:5), [REV 3:14](https://ref.ly/Rev3:14)

### The promised Messiah is faithful

**[ISA 11:5](https://ref.ly/Isa11:5)** *See also* [ISA 42:3](https://ref.ly/Isa42:3)

### Jesus Christ is faithful because he never changes

**[HEB 13:8](https://ref.ly/Heb13:8)** *See also* [HEB 1:11–1:12](https://ref.ly/Heb1:11-Heb1:12); [PSA 102:26–102:27](https://ref.ly/Ps102:26-Ps102:27)

### Jesus Christ is faithful because he is the truth

**[JHN 1:14](https://ref.ly/John1:14)** *“grace” and “truth” are often associated in Hebrew with love and faithfulness.* *See also* [JHN 1:17](https://ref.ly/John1:17), [JHN 14:6](https://ref.ly/John14:6)

Jesus Christ's faithfulness demonstrated in his obedience
---------------------------------------------------------

### He is faithful to his Father

**[HEB 3:6](https://ref.ly/Heb3:6)** *See also* [LUK 2:49](https://ref.ly/Luke2:49); [JHN 5:30](https://ref.ly/John5:30), [JHN 6:38](https://ref.ly/John6:38), [JHN 8:29](https://ref.ly/John8:29), [JHN 14:31](https://ref.ly/John14:31); [HEB 3:2](https://ref.ly/Heb3:2)

### He kept faithfully to his work

**[LUK 4:43](https://ref.ly/Luke4:43)** parallel [MRK 1:38](https://ref.ly/Mark1:38) *See also* [JHN 4:34](https://ref.ly/John4:34), [JHN 9:4](https://ref.ly/John9:4), [JHN 12:27](https://ref.ly/John12:27), [JHN 17:4](https://ref.ly/John17:4), [JHN 19:30](https://ref.ly/John19:30); [HEB 2:17–2:18](https://ref.ly/Heb2:17-Heb2:18)

Jesus Christ's faithfulness is seen in his words
------------------------------------------------

### In his predictions about events

**[MAT 16:28](https://ref.ly/Matt16:28)** parallels [LUK 9:27](https://ref.ly/Luke9:27), [MRK 9:1](https://ref.ly/Mark9:1) *This prediction could refer to either Jesus Christ's transfiguration or his ascension*; **[MAT 24:2](https://ref.ly/Matt24:2)** parallels [MRK 13:2](https://ref.ly/Mark13:2), [LUK 21:6](https://ref.ly/Luke21:6) *This prediction was fulfilled in A.D. 70 when the Romans destroyed Jerusalem and the temple.* *See also* [MAT 21:2](https://ref.ly/Matt21:2) parallels [MRK 11:2](https://ref.ly/Mark11:2), [LUK 19:30](https://ref.ly/Luke19:30) *finding a donkey and her colt*; [MAT 26:21](https://ref.ly/Matt26:21) parallels [MRK 14:18](https://ref.ly/Mark14:18), [LUK 22:21](https://ref.ly/Luke22:21), [JHN 13:21](https://ref.ly/John13:21) *Jesus Christ's betrayer*; [MAT 26:34](https://ref.ly/Matt26:34) parallels [MRK 14:30](https://ref.ly/Mark14:30), [LUK 22:34](https://ref.ly/Luke22:34), [JHN 13:38](https://ref.ly/John13:38) *Peter's denial*; [JHN 16:20](https://ref.ly/John16:20), [JHN 21:18–21:19](https://ref.ly/John21:18-John21:19)

### In his promises about eternal life

**[JHN 5:24](https://ref.ly/John5:24)** *See also* [MAT 19:28–19:29](https://ref.ly/Matt19:28-Matt19:29) parallels [MRK 10:29–10:30](https://ref.ly/Mark10:29-Mark10:30), [LUK 18:29–18:30](https://ref.ly/Luke18:29-Luke18:30); [LUK 23:43](https://ref.ly/Luke23:43); [JHN 6:47](https://ref.ly/John6:47), [JHN 6:53–6:54](https://ref.ly/John6:53-John6:54), [JHN 8:51](https://ref.ly/John8:51)

### In his promise of authority for believers

**[MAT 17:20](https://ref.ly/Matt17:20)** *See also* [MAT 16:19](https://ref.ly/Matt16:19), [MAT 18:18](https://ref.ly/Matt18:18), [MAT 21:21](https://ref.ly/Matt21:21) parallel [MRK 11:23](https://ref.ly/Mark11:23); [MAT 28:18–28:19](https://ref.ly/Matt28:18-Matt28:19); [LUK 10:19](https://ref.ly/Luke10:19)
