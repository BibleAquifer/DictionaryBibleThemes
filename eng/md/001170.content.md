## 5606: warfare, nature of (id: 5606)

Warfare is characteristic of a fallen world. Scripture describes how some wars are justified and others are not.

Warfare is characteristic of a fallen world
-------------------------------------------

### It is an aspect of life in a fallen world

[EXO 17:16](https://ref.ly/Exod17:16); ; [2SA 3:1](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:1); ; [1KI 14:30](https://ref.ly/1Kgs14:30)

### It is caused by sin

**[JAS 4:1–4:2](https://ref.ly/Jas4:1-Jas4:2)** *See also* [PSA 140:1–140:2](https://ref.ly/Ps140:1-Ps140:2); [PRO 10:12](https://ref.ly/Prov10:12), [PRO 29:22](https://ref.ly/Prov29:22); [1CO 3:3](https://ref.ly/1Cor3:3)

### It is horrific in nature

**[NAM 3:3](https://ref.ly/Nah3:3)** *See also* [2CH 25:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr25:12); [ZEC 14:2](https://ref.ly/Zech14:2)

Warfare as a sign of the end times
----------------------------------

**[MAT 24:6–24:7](https://ref.ly/Matt24:6-Matt24:7)** parallels [MRK 13:7–13:8](https://ref.ly/Mark13:7-Mark13:8), [LUK 21:9–21:10](https://ref.ly/Luke21:9-Luke21:10)

Sometimes God forbids war
-------------------------

**[DEU 1:41–1:42](https://ref.ly/Deut1:41-Deut1:42)** *See also* [1KI 12:24](https://ref.ly/1Kgs12:24); [1CH 22:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr22:8); [PSA 68:30](https://ref.ly/Ps68:30), [PSA 120:7](https://ref.ly/Ps120:7)

Sometimes God commands war
--------------------------

**[JOS 8:1](https://ref.ly/Josh8:1)** *See also* [NUM 31:7](https://ref.ly/Num31:7); [DEU 20:1–20:4](https://ref.ly/Deut20:1-Deut20:4); [JOS 10:40](https://ref.ly/Josh10:40); [JDG 6:16](https://ref.ly/Judg6:16); [PSA 144:1](https://ref.ly/Ps144:1); [ISA 13:3–13:4](https://ref.ly/Isa13:3-Isa13:4)

Warfare is sometimes a means of judgment
----------------------------------------

**[1SA 15:2](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:2)** *See also* [2KI 21:10–21:15](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:10-2Kgs21:15), [2KI 24:2–24:4](https://ref.ly/2Kgs24:2-2Kgs24:4); [ISA 10:12–10:19](https://ref.ly/Isa10:12-Isa10:19); [JER 4:14–4:18](https://ref.ly/Jer4:14-Jer4:18)
