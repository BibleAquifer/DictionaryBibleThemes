## 6237: sexual sin, nature of (id: 6237)

Any transgression of the limits that God has set for the enjoyment of sex. Such sins are often life\-dominating, but may be broken through the power and grace of Jesus Christ.

------------------------

### Fallen human nature

**[GAL 5:19](https://ref.ly/Gal5:19)** *See also* [ROM 13:12–13:14](https://ref.ly/Rom13:12-Rom13:14); [COL 3:5–3:6](https://ref.ly/Col3:5-Col3:6)
