## 1653: numbers, 6–10 (id: 1653)

The number seven is especially associated with completeness and perfection. The number ten is occasionally used as a symbol of completeness. Other numbers have a variety of associations.

Six
---

### The world was created in six days

**[EXO 20:11](https://ref.ly/Exod20:11)** *See also* [GEN 1:1–1:31](https://ref.ly/Gen1:1-Gen1:31); [EXO 31:17](https://ref.ly/Exod31:17)

[EXO 16:26](https://ref.ly/Exod16:26), [EXO 20:9](https://ref.ly/Exod20:9) parallel [DEU 5:13](https://ref.ly/Deut5:13); ; [EXO 23:12](https://ref.ly/Exod23:12), [EXO 31:15](https://ref.ly/Exod31:15), [EXO 34:21](https://ref.ly/Exod34:21), [EXO 35:2](https://ref.ly/Exod35:2); ; [LEV 23:3](https://ref.ly/Lev23:3); ; [LUK 13:14](https://ref.ly/Luke13:14)
