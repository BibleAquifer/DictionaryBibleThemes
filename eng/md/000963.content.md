## 5379: law, Jesus Christ's attitude to (id: 5379)

Jesus Christ accepted the authority of the OT law and saw himself as coming to fulfil its purpose.

Jesus Christ's disputes with the Pharisees and teachers of the law
------------------------------------------------------------------

### The Pharisees and teachers of the law accuse Jesus Christ's disciples of not following tradition

**[MRK 7:5](https://ref.ly/Mark7:5)** parallel [MAT 15:2](https://ref.ly/Matt15:2)

### Jesus Christ accuses the Pharisees and teachers of the law of hypocrisy

**[MRK 7:6–7:8](https://ref.ly/Mark7:6-Mark7:8)** parallel [MAT 15:7–15:9](https://ref.ly/Matt15:7-Matt15:9) *See also* [ISA 29:13](https://ref.ly/Isa29:13)

### Jesus Christ gives examples where human tradition is observed rather than God's law

[MRK 7:10–7:13](https://ref.ly/Mark7:10-Mark7:13) parallel [MAT 15:3–15:6](https://ref.ly/Matt15:3-Matt15:6); ; [MAT 23:1–23:36](https://ref.ly/Matt23:1-Matt23:36) parallels [MRK 12:38–12:39](https://ref.ly/Mark12:38-Mark12:39), [LUK 20:45–20:46](https://ref.ly/Luke20:45-Luke20:46)

### The Pharisees accuse Jesus Christ of breaking the Sabbath

**[MRK 2:23–2:24](https://ref.ly/Mark2:23-Mark2:24)** parallels [MAT 12:1–12:2](https://ref.ly/Matt12:1-Matt12:2), [LUK 6:1–6:2](https://ref.ly/Luke6:1-Luke6:2)

### Jesus Christ demonstrates his authority over the Sabbath

**[MRK 2:27–3:4](https://ref.ly/Mark2:27-Mark3:4)** parallels [MAT 12:8–12:10](https://ref.ly/Matt12:8-Matt12:10), [LUK 6:5–6:7](https://ref.ly/Luke6:5-Luke6:7)

### Jesus Christ challenges the religious leaders to think about principles not rules

**[MRK 3:4](https://ref.ly/Mark3:4)** parallels [LUK 6:9](https://ref.ly/Luke6:9), [MAT 12:11–12:12](https://ref.ly/Matt12:11-Matt12:12) *See also* [LUK 13:10–13:17](https://ref.ly/Luke13:10-Luke13:17)

Jesus Christ came to fulfil the law
-----------------------------------

**[MAT 5:17](https://ref.ly/Matt5:17), [MAT 5:21–5:22](https://ref.ly/Matt5:21-Matt5:22)** *Jesus Christ went beyond the literal rules of the law, to the thought behind the act.* *See also* [EXO 20:13](https://ref.ly/Exod20:13)**[MAT 5:27–5:28](https://ref.ly/Matt5:27-Matt5:28)** *See also* [EXO 20:14](https://ref.ly/Exod20:14)
