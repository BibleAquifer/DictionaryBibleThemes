## 5887: inexperience (id: 5887)

Lack of experience of life or a particular aspect of life. Inexperience is often associated with the young who are thus vulnerable and who should be taught by those older and wiser. God sometimes calls the inexperienced to leadership but usually also provides encouragement and support from more experienced leaders.

The inexperience of youth
-------------------------

**[1SA 3:4–3:7](https://ref.ly/1Sam3:4-1Sam3:7)**; **[JER 1:6](https://ref.ly/Jer1:6)** *See also* [DEU 1:39](https://ref.ly/Deut1:39); [1KI 3:7](https://ref.ly/1Kgs3:7); [1CH 29:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:1); [2CH 10:8–10:11](https://ref.ly/2Chr10:8-2Chr10:11) parallel [1KI 12:8–12:11](https://ref.ly/1Kgs12:8-1Kgs12:11); [2CH 13:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr13:7); [ISA 7:15–7:16](https://ref.ly/Isa7:15-Isa7:16)

The inexperienced may be led astray
-----------------------------------

**[2KI 17:41](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:41)** *Children often follow in the ways of their parents*; **[MAT 18:6](https://ref.ly/Matt18:6)** *See also* [1KI 22:53](https://ref.ly/1Kgs22:53); [2KI 24:9](https://ref.ly/2Kgs24:9); [MRK 9:42](https://ref.ly/Mark9:42); [LUK 17:2](https://ref.ly/Luke17:2)

God's call to the inexperienced
-------------------------------

**[JER 1:7–1:8](https://ref.ly/Jer1:7-Jer1:8)** *See also* [1SA 16:11–16:13](https://ref.ly/1Sam16:11-1Sam16:13) *David, Jesse's youngest son, is chosen by God*; [1SA 17:33](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:33) *David's inexperience in the face of Goliath*; [1TI 4:12](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:12) *Timothy was called to pastor a church despite his youth. In general, however, church leaders should be people of experience (see [1Ti 3:6](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:6)).*

-----------------------------

**[DEU 4:9–4:10](https://ref.ly/Deut4:9-Deut4:10)**; **[JOB 8:8–8:9](https://ref.ly/Job8:8-Job8:9)** *Each generation learns from the wisdom and experience of those going before.* *See also* [DEU 11:2–11:7](https://ref.ly/Deut11:2-Deut11:7), [DEU 31:13](https://ref.ly/Deut31:13); [JOB 32:6–32:7](https://ref.ly/Job32:6-Job32:7); [PSA 71:18](https://ref.ly/Ps71:18), [PSA 78:3–78:5](https://ref.ly/Ps78:3-Ps78:5); [PRO 5:1–5:2](https://ref.ly/Prov5:1-Prov5:2); [EPH 6:4](https://ref.ly/Eph6:4)
