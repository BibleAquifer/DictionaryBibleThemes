## 5547: speech, power and significance of (id: 5547)

A person's speech has an enormous influence for good or ill and is a sure guide to character.

The impact of speech
--------------------

### It has the power of life and death

**[PRO 18:21](https://ref.ly/Prov18:21)**; **[JAS 3:1–3:12](https://ref.ly/Jas3:1-Jas3:12)** *See also* [PRO 15:2](https://ref.ly/Prov15:2), [PRO 15:4](https://ref.ly/Prov15:4)

### It can bring ruin or joy to the speaker

**[PRO 12:13–12:14](https://ref.ly/Prov12:13-Prov12:14)** *See also* [PSA 140:9](https://ref.ly/Ps140:9); [PRO 10:14](https://ref.ly/Prov10:14), [PRO 13:3](https://ref.ly/Prov13:3), [PRO 14:3](https://ref.ly/Prov14:3), [PRO 18:6–18:7](https://ref.ly/Prov18:6-Prov18:7), [PRO 18:20](https://ref.ly/Prov18:20), [PRO 21:23](https://ref.ly/Prov21:23); [ECC 10:12–10:14](https://ref.ly/Eccl10:12-Eccl10:14)

### It can destroy others

**[PRO 11:9](https://ref.ly/Prov11:9)** *See also* [JOB 19:2](https://ref.ly/Job19:2); [PSA 55:20–55:21](https://ref.ly/Ps55:20-Ps55:21), [PSA 57:4](https://ref.ly/Ps57:4), [PSA 64:3–64:4](https://ref.ly/Ps64:3-Ps64:4), [PSA 140:3](https://ref.ly/Ps140:3); [PRO 11:11](https://ref.ly/Prov11:11), [PRO 12:6](https://ref.ly/Prov12:6), [PRO 12:18](https://ref.ly/Prov12:18), [PRO 16:27](https://ref.ly/Prov16:27)

### It can bring life to others

**[PRO 10:11](https://ref.ly/Prov10:11)** *See also* [PRO 10:20–10:21](https://ref.ly/Prov10:20-Prov10:21), [PRO 10:31–10:32](https://ref.ly/Prov10:31-Prov10:32), [PRO 16:21](https://ref.ly/Prov16:21), [PRO 16:23–16:24](https://ref.ly/Prov16:23-Prov16:24), [PRO 25:12](https://ref.ly/Prov25:12); [ISA 50:4](https://ref.ly/Isa50:4); [EPH 4:29](https://ref.ly/Eph4:29)
