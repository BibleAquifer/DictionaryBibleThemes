## 1428: prophecy, inspiration of OT (id: 1428)

The prophecies of biblical messengers are acknowledged as being of divine origin, inspired through the activity of the Holy Spirit.

God speaks through his prophets
-------------------------------

**[2PE 1:20–1:21](https://ref.ly/2Pet1:20-2Pet1:21)** *See also* [NEH 9:30](https://ref.ly/Neh9:30); [ZEC 7:12](https://ref.ly/Zech7:12); [HEB 1:1](https://ref.ly/Heb1:1)

God is the source of prophecy
-----------------------------

**[EZK 24:20–24:21](https://ref.ly/Ezek24:20-Ezek24:21)** *The phrase “the word of the LORD came to ...” is frequently used of (and by) the prophets to indicate that the message they proclaimed came directly from God.* *See also* [1SA 15:10](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:10); [2SA 7:4](https://ref.ly/2Sam7:4); [1KI 13:20](https://ref.ly/1Kgs13:20), [1KI 16:1](https://ref.ly/1Kgs16:1), [1KI 17:2](https://ref.ly/1Kgs17:2); [2KI 20:4](https://ref.ly/2Kgs20:4); [JER 1:2](https://ref.ly/Jer1:2); [EZK 6:1](https://ref.ly/Ezek6:1); [JON 1:1](https://ref.ly/Jonah1:1); [HAG 2:10](https://ref.ly/Hag2:10); [ZEC 1:1](https://ref.ly/Zech1:1)

Prophets inspired by God speak the truth
----------------------------------------

**[DEU 18:22](https://ref.ly/Deut18:22)** *See also* [JER 28:9](https://ref.ly/Jer28:9)
