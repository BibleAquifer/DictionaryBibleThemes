## 6243: adultery, spiritual (id: 6243)

In Scripture there is a close link between sexual sin and spiritual corruption and immorality as promoted by false teachers. Scripture frequently uses the language of sexual sin to expose the infidelity of God's people.

The link between sexual sin and spiritual adultery
--------------------------------------------------

### In practice

**[NUM 25:1–25:3](https://ref.ly/Num25:1-Num25:3)** *See also* [EXO 34:15](https://ref.ly/Exod34:15); [HOS 4:14](https://ref.ly/Hos4:14)

### In false teaching

**[REV 2:20–2:25](https://ref.ly/Rev2:20-Rev2:25)** *See also* [NUM 31:15–31:16](https://ref.ly/Num31:15-Num31:16); [2TI 3:6](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:6); [2PE 2:13–2:15](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:13-2Pet2:15); [JUD 1:4](https://ref.ly/Jude1:4), [JUD 1:8](https://ref.ly/Jude1:8); [REV 2:14–2:16](https://ref.ly/Rev2:14-Rev2:16)

Spiritual adultery and God's covenant relationship with his people
------------------------------------------------------------------

### God as a husband to his people

**[EZK 16:8](https://ref.ly/Ezek16:8)** *The spiritual relationship between God and his people is defined in terms of the intimate and exclusive bond of marriage.* *See also* [ISA 54:5](https://ref.ly/Isa54:5); [JER 3:14](https://ref.ly/Jer3:14), [JER 31:32](https://ref.ly/Jer31:32); [EPH 5:31–5:32](https://ref.ly/Eph5:31-Eph5:32)

### God is jealous for his people's affections

**[EXO 34:14](https://ref.ly/Exod34:14)** *Just as husband and wife are jealous for their relationship in love, so God is jealous for the affections of his people.* *See also* [EXO 20:4–20:5](https://ref.ly/Exod20:4-Exod20:5); [DEU 4:23–4:24](https://ref.ly/Deut4:23-Deut4:24), [DEU 6:13–6:15](https://ref.ly/Deut6:13-Deut6:15)
