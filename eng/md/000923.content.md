Highways for the large\-scale movement of people
------------------------------------------------

**[ISA 19:23](https://ref.ly/Isa19:23)** *Isaiah prophesies the future harmony that will exist between former enemies, who will then worship the LORD together.* *See also* [ISA 11:16](https://ref.ly/Isa11:16), [ISA 35:8–35:10](https://ref.ly/Isa35:8-Isa35:10), [ISA 49:11](https://ref.ly/Isa49:11), [ISA 62:10](https://ref.ly/Isa62:10); [JER 31:21](https://ref.ly/Jer31:21)

The \`\`highway for our God''
-----------------------------

**[ISA 40:3–40:4](https://ref.ly/Isa40:3-Isa40:4)** *Isaiah foresees how God will bring his people home from exile in Babylon.* *See also* [MRK 1:2–1:4](https://ref.ly/Mark1:2-Mark1:4) parallels [MAT 2:1–2:3](https://ref.ly/Matt2:1-Matt2:3), [LUK 3:2–3:6](https://ref.ly/Luke3:2-Luke3:6) *John the Baptist's call to repentance is seen as the necessary preparation for the coming of the Messiah.*
