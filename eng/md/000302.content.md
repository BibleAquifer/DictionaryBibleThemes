## 4017: life, animal and plant (id: 4017)

All living creatures and plants were made by God and are sustained by him.

Animal life
-----------

### Animals were created by God

**[GEN 2:19](https://ref.ly/Gen2:19)**; **[PSA 104:24](https://ref.ly/Ps104:24)** *See also* [GEN 1:20–1:25](https://ref.ly/Gen1:20-Gen1:25)

**[PSA 145:15–145:16](https://ref.ly/Ps145:15-Ps145:16)**; **[MAT 6:26](https://ref.ly/Matt6:26)** parallel [LUK 12:24](https://ref.ly/Luke12:24) *See also* [DEU 22:7](https://ref.ly/Deut22:7); [PSA 104:10–104:12](https://ref.ly/Ps104:10-Ps104:12), [PSA 104:14](https://ref.ly/Ps104:14), [PSA 104:17–104:18](https://ref.ly/Ps104:17-Ps104:18), [PSA 104:21](https://ref.ly/Ps104:21), [PSA 104:27](https://ref.ly/Ps104:27), [PSA 136:25](https://ref.ly/Ps136:25), [PSA 147:9](https://ref.ly/Ps147:9); [JON 4:11](https://ref.ly/Jonah4:11); [MAT 10:29](https://ref.ly/Matt10:29)

### Animal life is controlled by God

**[DAN 6:22](https://ref.ly/Dan6:22)** *See also* [NUM 22:28](https://ref.ly/Num22:28); [JON 1:17](https://ref.ly/Jonah1:17); [HEB 11:33](https://ref.ly/Heb11:33)

[GEN 3:21](https://ref.ly/Gen3:21); ; [MAT 21:1–21:3](https://ref.ly/Matt21:1-Matt21:3) parallels [MRK 11:1–11:3](https://ref.ly/Mark11:1-Mark11:3), [LUK 19:29–19:31](https://ref.ly/Luke19:29-Luke19:31)
