## 6610: adoption, descriptions of (id: 6610)

The words used by the NT to describe believers point to their status and privileges as the adopted children of God.

Believers are no longer slaves but sons
---------------------------------------

**[GAL 4:7–4:9](https://ref.ly/Gal4:7-Gal4:9)** *See also* [JHN 8:34–8:36](https://ref.ly/John8:34-John8:36); [ROM 8:15](https://ref.ly/Rom8:15); [PHM 1:16](https://ref.ly/Phlm1:16)

Believers are children of God
-----------------------------

**[1JN 3:1–3:2](https://ref.ly/1John3:1-1John3:2)** *The title “child of God” is thought by some to be distinct from that of “son of God” (see below). “child” is thought to emphasise God's fatherhood, whilst “son” is thought to emphasise the Christian's privileges. These terms are however, sometimes used interchangeably.* *See also* [LUK 20:36](https://ref.ly/Luke20:36); [ROM 8:21](https://ref.ly/Rom8:21); [PHP 2:15](https://ref.ly/Phil2:15); [1JN 3:2](https://ref.ly/1John3:2), [1JN 5:2](https://ref.ly/1John5:2)

Believers are sons of God
-------------------------

**[ROM 8:14](https://ref.ly/Rom8:14)** *See also* [ROM 9:26](https://ref.ly/Rom9:26); [GAL 3:26](https://ref.ly/Gal3:26)

[LUK 20:36](https://ref.ly/Luke20:36)
