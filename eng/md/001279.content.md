## 5741: vows (id: 5741)

Promises made to God, usually in the context of worship or religious practice. There was no requirement on any Israelite to make vows, but once made, they were binding and had to be kept.

Vows were binding
-----------------

**[DEU 23:21–23:23](https://ref.ly/Deut23:21-Deut23:23)** *See also* [NUM 30:2](https://ref.ly/Num30:2), [NUM 30:9](https://ref.ly/Num30:9); [PRO 20:25](https://ref.ly/Prov20:25); [ECC 5:4–5:6](https://ref.ly/Eccl5:4-Eccl5:6); [MAL 1:14](https://ref.ly/Mal1:14)

### Exceptions to keeping vows

**[NUM 30:3–30:8](https://ref.ly/Num30:3-Num30:8)** *In certain cases vows made by daughters or wives could be nullified by fathers or husbands respectively.* *See also* [NUM 30:10–30:15](https://ref.ly/Num30:10-Num30:15)
