## 5780: advisers (id: 5780)

Those who give advice and counsel. Human advice is desirable, but not always reliable. Only God's counsel is wholly dependable.

Human advisers
--------------

### It is wise to consult advisers

**[PRO 15:22](https://ref.ly/Prov15:22)** *See also* [PRO 12:15](https://ref.ly/Prov12:15), [PRO 13:10](https://ref.ly/Prov13:10), [PRO 15:12](https://ref.ly/Prov15:12), [PRO 19:20](https://ref.ly/Prov19:20), [PRO 20:18](https://ref.ly/Prov20:18), [PRO 24:6](https://ref.ly/Prov24:6); [ECC 4:13](https://ref.ly/Eccl4:13)
