## 5384: lawyer (id: 5384)

A lawyer as a person skilled in the Mosaic law
----------------------------------------------

[MAT 22:35](https://ref.ly/Matt22:35); ; [LUK 7:30](https://ref.ly/Luke7:30), [LUK 10:25](https://ref.ly/Luke10:25), [LUK 10:37](https://ref.ly/Luke10:37), [LUK 11:45–11:46](https://ref.ly/Luke11:45-Luke11:46), [LUK 11:52](https://ref.ly/Luke11:52), [LUK 14:3](https://ref.ly/Luke14:3); ; [TIT 3:13](https://ref.ly/Titus3:13)

A lawyer as a person trained in rhetoric
----------------------------------------

[ACT 24:1](https://ref.ly/Acts24:1) *The word translated “lawyer” means literally “orator”: a person trained in rhetoric who served as an attorney\-at\-law in a court trial.*

* **Associated Passages:** Matthew 22:35; Luke 7:30; Luke 10:25; Luke 10:37; Luke 11:45-46; Luke 11:52; Luke 14:3; Titus 3:13; Acts 24:1
