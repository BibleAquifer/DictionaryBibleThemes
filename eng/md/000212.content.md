## 2412: cross, Gospel accounts of (id: 2412)

Each of the four Gospels provides a detailed account of Jesus Christ's death on the cross.

The way to the cross
--------------------

### Simon is compelled to carry the cross

**[MRK 15:21](https://ref.ly/Mark15:21)** parallels [MAT 27:32](https://ref.ly/Matt27:32), [LUK 23:26](https://ref.ly/Luke23:26)

### Women follow the cross and Jesus Christ is offered relief from pain

**[LUK 23:27–23:31](https://ref.ly/Luke23:27-Luke23:31)** *See also* [MAT 27:34](https://ref.ly/Matt27:34) parallel [MRK 15:23](https://ref.ly/Mark15:23)

The crucifixion of Jesus Christ
-------------------------------

### Jesus Christ crucified between two criminals

**[MRK 15:22–15:27](https://ref.ly/Mark15:22-Mark15:27)** parallels [MAT 27:33–27:38](https://ref.ly/Matt27:33-Matt27:38), [LUK 23:32–23:43](https://ref.ly/Luke23:32-Luke23:43) *“Golgotha” is a transliteration of the Aramaic word for “a skull”. Its actual location is uncertain.*

### The humiliation of Jesus Christ on the cross

**[MRK 15:29–15:32](https://ref.ly/Mark15:29-Mark15:32)** parallels [MAT 27:39–27:44](https://ref.ly/Matt27:39-Matt27:44), [LUK 23:35–23:39](https://ref.ly/Luke23:35-Luke23:39)

### The words of Jesus Christ on the cross

**[JHN 19:30](https://ref.ly/John19:30)** *See also* [MAT 27:46](https://ref.ly/Matt27:46) parallel [MRK 15:34](https://ref.ly/Mark15:34); [MAT 27:50](https://ref.ly/Matt27:50); [MRK 15:37](https://ref.ly/Mark15:37); [LUK 23:28–23:31](https://ref.ly/Luke23:28-Luke23:31), [LUK 23:34](https://ref.ly/Luke23:34), [LUK 23:43](https://ref.ly/Luke23:43), [LUK 23:46](https://ref.ly/Luke23:46); [JHN 19:26–19:28](https://ref.ly/John19:26-John19:28)

### The death of Jesus Christ

**[MRK 15:33–15:37](https://ref.ly/Mark15:33-Mark15:37)** parallels [MAT 27:45–27:50](https://ref.ly/Matt27:45-Matt27:50), [LUK 23:44–23:46](https://ref.ly/Luke23:44-Luke23:46) *See also* [JHN 19:28–19:35](https://ref.ly/John19:28-John19:35) *According to John, Jesus Christ was on the cross while the Passover lambs were being sacrificed.*
