## 1135: God, suffering of (id: 1135)

God suffers on account of the failures and pain of his people, and especially through his Son, Jesus Christ. Scripture often likens God to a parent who suffers pain and grief on account of children.

God feels real suffering
------------------------

### He suffers pain because people sin

**[GEN 6:6](https://ref.ly/Gen6:6)** *See also* [1SA 15:10–15:11](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:10-1Sam15:11), [1SA 15:35](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:35)

### He is angered because people sin

**[PSA 78:58](https://ref.ly/Ps78:58)** *See also* [DEU 32:15–32:16](https://ref.ly/Deut32:15-Deut32:16); [JDG 2:12–2:13](https://ref.ly/Judg2:12-Judg2:13); [1KI 21:22](https://ref.ly/1Kgs21:22); [EZK 8:17](https://ref.ly/Ezek8:17); [HOS 12:14](https://ref.ly/Hos12:14)

God identifies with suffering
-----------------------------

### He suffers with his chosen people

**[ISA 63:9](https://ref.ly/Isa63:9)** *See also* [EXO 3:7](https://ref.ly/Exod3:7); [JDG 10:16](https://ref.ly/Judg10:16); [2CH 36:15](https://ref.ly/2Chr36:15)

### He suffers with all humanity

**[ISA 16:11](https://ref.ly/Isa16:11)** parallel [JER 48:31](https://ref.ly/Jer48:31) *See also* [2SA 24:15–24:17](https://ref.ly/2Sam24:15-2Sam24:17) parallel [1CH 21:14–21:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr21:14-1Chr21:17) *God suffers even with those who endure punishment from his own hand*; [ISA 16:9](https://ref.ly/Isa16:9); [JER 48:32–48:36](https://ref.ly/Jer48:32-Jer48:36); [HOS 11:8–11:9](https://ref.ly/Hos11:8-Hos11:9)

### He suffers as a parent

**[JER 31:20](https://ref.ly/Jer31:20)** *An indication that God suffers in both disciplining and loving.* *See also* [ISA 42:14](https://ref.ly/Isa42:14), [ISA 49:15](https://ref.ly/Isa49:15); [HOS 11:1–11:4](https://ref.ly/Hos11:1-Hos11:4)

God's plan of salvation causes him suffering
--------------------------------------------

### He suffered in giving his Son

**[ROM 8:32](https://ref.ly/Rom8:32)** *See also* [ISA 7:13–7:14](https://ref.ly/Isa7:13-Isa7:14); [JHN 3:16](https://ref.ly/John3:16); [ROM 3:25–3:26](https://ref.ly/Rom3:25-Rom3:26), [ROM 8:3](https://ref.ly/Rom8:3); [GAL 4:4](https://ref.ly/Gal4:4); [HEB 1:2](https://ref.ly/Heb1:2); [1JN 4:14](https://ref.ly/1John4:14)

### He suffers because he endures the rejection his Son suffers

**[LUK 10:16](https://ref.ly/Luke10:16)** *See also* [1PE 2:4](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:4)

God's creation reflects his suffering
-------------------------------------

**[ROM 8:22](https://ref.ly/Rom8:22)** *See also* [ROM 8:19–8:21](https://ref.ly/Rom8:19-Rom8:21)

God's people share his suffering
--------------------------------
