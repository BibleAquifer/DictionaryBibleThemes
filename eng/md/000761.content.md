## 5172: offal (id: 5172)

The internal organs of an animal, traditionally regarded as being inferior to meat, and used as a symbol of disgrace.

Offal is symbolic of disgrace
-----------------------------

**[MAL 2:3](https://ref.ly/Mal2:3)** *See also* [NAM 3:6](https://ref.ly/Nah3:6)

In animal sacrifices the offal was removed and burnt outside the camp
