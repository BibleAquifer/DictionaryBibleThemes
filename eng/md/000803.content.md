## 5214: attack (id: 5214)

A violent act of aggression often associated with intent to invade, sometimes as an act of retribution and occasionally as a strategic act of self\-defence. Scripture also recognises the possibility of spiritual aggression through attack by evil forces.

-----------------------------------------

[GEN 14:14–14:16](https://ref.ly/Gen14:14-Gen14:16) *Abraham attacks the captors of the king of Sodom*; ; [JDG 7:19–7:21](https://ref.ly/Judg7:19-Judg7:21) *Gideon attacks the Midianites*; ; [1SA 14:13–14:14](https://ref.ly/1Sam14:13-1Sam14:14) *Jonathan attacks the Philistines*; ; [2SA 5:6–5:7](https://ref.ly/2Sam5:6-2Sam5:7) parallel [1CH 11:4–11:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:4-1Chr11:5) *David attacks the Jebusites*; ; [DEU 20:1–20:20](https://ref.ly/Deut20:1-Deut20:20) *Moses gives detailed instruction on going to war.*
