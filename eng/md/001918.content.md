## 7755: preaching, importance of (id: 7755)

Preaching has a central place among God's people and is vital to their life and growth. It is authorised by God, empowered by the Holy Spirit and expressed supremely by Jesus Christ.

Preaching has its origin in God
-------------------------------

### It is a divine command

**[MRK 16:15](https://ref.ly/Mark16:15)** parallel [MAT 28:18–28:20](https://ref.ly/Matt28:18-Matt28:20) *See also* [JON 1:1–1:2](https://ref.ly/Jonah1:1-Jonah1:2); [MAT 10:5–10:7](https://ref.ly/Matt10:5-Matt10:7) parallels [MRK 6:7–6:12](https://ref.ly/Mark6:7-Mark6:12), [LUK 9:1–9:6](https://ref.ly/Luke9:1-Luke9:6)

### It is rooted in God's grace

**[EPH 3:7–3:9](https://ref.ly/Eph3:7-Eph3:9)** *See also* [ISA 6:1–6:10](https://ref.ly/Isa6:1-Isa6:10); [ROM 15:15–15:16](https://ref.ly/Rom15:15-Rom15:16)

### It is empowered by God's Spirit

**[ACT 1:8](https://ref.ly/Acts1:8)** *See also* [ISA 61:1–61:3](https://ref.ly/Isa61:1-Isa61:3); [LUK 24:46–24:49](https://ref.ly/Luke24:46-Luke24:49); [ACT 2:1–2:11](https://ref.ly/Acts2:1-Acts2:11), [ACT 4:8–4:12](https://ref.ly/Acts4:8-Acts4:12), [ACT 10:44](https://ref.ly/Acts10:44); [1CO 2:4–2:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:4-1Cor2:5)

--------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ himself came to preach

**[MRK 1:38](https://ref.ly/Mark1:38)** parallel [LUK 4:43](https://ref.ly/Luke4:43) *See also* [EPH 2:17](https://ref.ly/Eph2:17)

**[MAT 4:23](https://ref.ly/Matt4:23)** *See also* [MAT 11:1–11:5](https://ref.ly/Matt11:1-Matt11:5) parallel [LUK 7:18–7:22](https://ref.ly/Luke7:18-Luke7:22)

### Jesus Christ commissioned his disciples to preach

**[MRK 3:14–3:15](https://ref.ly/Mark3:14-Mark3:15)** *See also* [MAT 10:5–10:7](https://ref.ly/Matt10:5-Matt10:7) parallel [LUK 9:1–9:2](https://ref.ly/Luke9:1-Luke9:2)
