## 5587: trade (id: 5587)

Fair business transactions are essential to distribute the benefits of God's goodness between individuals and nations. Scripture insists on honesty in trade and warns against abuses.

Trade in ancient Israel
-----------------------

[LEV 25:14](https://ref.ly/Lev25:14) *Land transactions required the approval of the ruler of the state*; ; [2KI 4:7](https://ref.ly/2Kgs4:7), [2KI 7:1](https://ref.ly/2Kgs7:1); ; [NEH 3:31–3:32](https://ref.ly/Neh3:31-Neh3:32); [PRO 31:13–31:18](https://ref.ly/Prov31:13-Prov31:18), [PRO 31:24](https://ref.ly/Prov31:24); [ZEP 1:11](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:11); ; [MAT 21:12](https://ref.ly/Matt21:12) parallels [MRK 11:15–11:16](https://ref.ly/Mark11:15-Mark11:16), [LUK 19:45](https://ref.ly/Luke19:45), [JHN 2:14–2:16](https://ref.ly/John2:14-John2:16) *trade in Jerusalem in Jesus Christ's day*
