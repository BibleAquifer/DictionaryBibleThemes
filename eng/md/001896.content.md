## 7708: apostles, function of (id: 7708)

In the NT apostles are servants, first of Jesus Christ and then of the church. Apostolic service embraces a wide range of specific functions.

The functions of apostles during the earthly life and ministry of Jesus Christ
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Being with Jesus Christ

**[MRK 3:14](https://ref.ly/Mark3:14)** *See also* [MAT 17:1](https://ref.ly/Matt17:1); [MRK 6:30–6:32](https://ref.ly/Mark6:30-Mark6:32) parallel [LUK 9:10](https://ref.ly/Luke9:10)

### Learning from Jesus Christ

**[MRK 8:31](https://ref.ly/Mark8:31)** *See also* [MAT 18:1–18:4](https://ref.ly/Matt18:1-Matt18:4); [MRK 4:10–4:12](https://ref.ly/Mark4:10-Mark4:12); [ACT 1:2](https://ref.ly/Acts1:2)

### Being sent out on a mission by Jesus Christ

**[LUK 9:1–9:6](https://ref.ly/Luke9:1-Luke9:6)** parallels [MAT 10:5–10:10](https://ref.ly/Matt10:5-Matt10:10), [MRK 6:7–6:13](https://ref.ly/Mark6:7-Mark6:13) *As Jesus Christ's representatives their ministry was an extension of his.* *See also* [MRK 3:14–3:15](https://ref.ly/Mark3:14-Mark3:15)

### Being commissioned to witness to Jesus Christ

**[ACT 1:8](https://ref.ly/Acts1:8), [ACT 10:39](https://ref.ly/Acts10:39)** *See also* [MAT 28:18–28:20](https://ref.ly/Matt28:18-Matt28:20); [MRK 16:15](https://ref.ly/Mark16:15); [LUK 24:46–24:49](https://ref.ly/Luke24:46-Luke24:49); [JHN 17:18](https://ref.ly/John17:18), [JHN 20:21](https://ref.ly/John20:21)

Apostles in the early church
----------------------------

### They were witnesses of the resurrection

**[ACT 1:21–1:22](https://ref.ly/Acts1:21-Acts1:22)** *See also* [ACT 2:32](https://ref.ly/Acts2:32), [ACT 3:15](https://ref.ly/Acts3:15), [ACT 4:33](https://ref.ly/Acts4:33), [ACT 13:31](https://ref.ly/Acts13:31); [1PE 5:1](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:1)

### They were servants of Jesus Christ

**[2PE 1:1](https://ref.ly/2Pet1:1)** *See also* [ROM 1:1](https://ref.ly/Rom1:1); [1CO 4:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor4:1); [2CO 11:23](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:23); [PHP 1:1](https://ref.ly/Phil1:1); [TIT 1:1](https://ref.ly/Titus1:1)

### They were servants of the church

**[2CO 13:4](https://ref.ly/2Cor13:4)** *See also* [ROM 15:25](https://ref.ly/Rom15:25); [2CO 4:5](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:5), [2CO 11:8](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:8); [1PE 5:1–5:3](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:1-1Pet5:3)

### They were the foundations of the church

**[EPH 2:20](https://ref.ly/Eph2:20)** *Probably a reference to the apostolic preaching. The absence of the article before “prophets” suggests that “the apostles and prophets” represents one group of people, not two.* *See also* [1CO 12:28](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:28); [GAL 2:9](https://ref.ly/Gal2:9); [JUD 1:17](https://ref.ly/Jude1:17) *the prophetic ministry of the apostles*

### They preached the gospel

**[ROM 1:1–1:5](https://ref.ly/Rom1:1-Rom1:5)** *See also* [MRK 16:20](https://ref.ly/Mark16:20); [ROM 15:19–15:20](https://ref.ly/Rom15:19-Rom15:20); [1CO 1:17](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:17); [GAL 1:15–1:16](https://ref.ly/Gal1:15-Gal1:16)

### They were guardians and teachers of the truth

**[ACT 2:42](https://ref.ly/Acts2:42)** *See also* [JHN 16:13–16:15](https://ref.ly/John16:13-John16:15); [ACT 5:42](https://ref.ly/Acts5:42), [ACT 6:2](https://ref.ly/Acts6:2); [EPH 3:2–3:6](https://ref.ly/Eph3:2-Eph3:6); [2TH 2:15](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:15); [2TI 1:11](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:11)

### They founded churches

**[1CO 9:1–9:2](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:1-1Cor9:2)** *See also* [1CO 3:5–3:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor3:5-1Cor3:6), [1CO 3:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor3:10)

### They were authority figures within the church

[ACT 6:2–6:6](https://ref.ly/Acts6:2-Acts6:6) *The apostles took the initiative. Whilst they did not themselves appoint the Seven, they confirmed their appointment.*

### They were called to suffer

**[2TI 1:11–1:12](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:11-2Tim1:12)** *See also* [ACT 5:18](https://ref.ly/Acts5:18); [1CO 4:9](https://ref.ly/1Cor4:9); [2CO 11:23–11:29](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:23-2Cor11:29)

### They were empowered by the Spirit

**[ACT 4:8](https://ref.ly/Acts4:8)** *See also* [JHN 20:22](https://ref.ly/John20:22); [ACT 1:8](https://ref.ly/Acts1:8), [ACT 2:4](https://ref.ly/Acts2:4); [EPH 3:4–3:5](https://ref.ly/Eph3:4-Eph3:5)

### They were able to bestow the Spirit

[ACT 8:14–8:18](https://ref.ly/Acts8:14-Acts8:18) *Peter and John officially induct Samaritans as members of the body of Christ*; ; [ACT 19:6](https://ref.ly/Acts19:6)
