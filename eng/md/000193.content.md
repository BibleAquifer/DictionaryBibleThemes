## 2339: Jesus Christ, example of (id: 2339)

Jesus Christ sets his disciples an example, especially in servanthood, self\-denial and endurance of suffering, so that they may become like him.

Jesus Christ calls believers to follow his example
--------------------------------------------------

**[JHN 13:14–13:16](https://ref.ly/John13:14-John13:16)** *See also* [HEB 3:1](https://ref.ly/Heb3:1), [HEB 12:2–12:3](https://ref.ly/Heb12:2-Heb12:3); [1PE 2:21](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:21); [1JN 2:6](https://ref.ly/1John2:6)
