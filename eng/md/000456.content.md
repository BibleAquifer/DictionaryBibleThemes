## 4482: millstones (id: 4482)

Pairs of upper and lower millstones were used for grinding grain in the ancient East. Millstones were common features of the daily life of ordinary people.

Millstones as a means of earning a living
-----------------------------------------

**[DEU 24:6](https://ref.ly/Deut24:6)** *See also* [ISA 47:1–47:2](https://ref.ly/Isa47:1-Isa47:2); [JER 25:10](https://ref.ly/Jer25:10); [REV 18:22](https://ref.ly/Rev18:22)
