## 1445: revelation, responses to (id: 1445)

God requires and imparts a frame of mind that receives and responds to what he has made known.

God commands that his word be heeded
------------------------------------

**[MRK 4:3](https://ref.ly/Mark4:3)** *See also* [ISA 1:10](https://ref.ly/Isa1:10); [MRK 4:9](https://ref.ly/Mark4:9); [REV 2:7](https://ref.ly/Rev2:7)
