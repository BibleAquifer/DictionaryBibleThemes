## 7535: Greeks (id: 7535)

The inhabitants of Greece and her empire. The term is also used to describe non\-Jews, so that тАЬGreekтАЭ, in the NT, is virtually synonymous with тАЬGentileтАЭ. While distinctions between Greek and Jew on the one hand, and Greek and barbarian on the other, were important in the world at large, such divisions are to have no place within the church.
