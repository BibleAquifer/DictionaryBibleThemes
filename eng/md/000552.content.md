## 4827: frost (id: 4827)

A covering of the ground with fine particles of ice, characteristic of cold weather.

Frost is under God's control
----------------------------

**[PSA 147:16](https://ref.ly/Ps147:16)** *See also* [JOB 38:29](https://ref.ly/Job38:29)

Frost will be absent on the day of the LORD
