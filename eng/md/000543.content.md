## 4816: drought, physical (id: 4816)

Drought arises through the absence of rain, leading to severe hardship for those who are affected by it.

Drought may be a sign of God's displeasure
------------------------------------------

**[DEU 11:17](https://ref.ly/Deut11:17)** *See also* [DEU 28:22–28:24](https://ref.ly/Deut28:22-Deut28:24); [2SA 1:21](https://ref.ly/2Sam1:21); [1KI 17:1](https://ref.ly/1Kgs17:1); [PSA 107:33–107:34](https://ref.ly/Ps107:33-Ps107:34); [ISA 5:6](https://ref.ly/Isa5:6), [ISA 5:13](https://ref.ly/Isa5:13); [JER 2:2–2:3](https://ref.ly/Jer2:2-Jer2:3), [JER 14:1–14:6](https://ref.ly/Jer14:1-Jer14:6), [JER 50:38](https://ref.ly/Jer50:38); [EZK 22:23–22:24](https://ref.ly/Ezek22:23-Ezek22:24); [AMO 4:7](https://ref.ly/Amos4:7); [HAG 1:11](https://ref.ly/Hag1:11); [JAS 5:17](https://ref.ly/Jas5:17)
