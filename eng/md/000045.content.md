## 1205: God, titles and names of (id: 1205)

These reflect the great and varied aspects of God's being and character. They are particularly used by the psalmists to speak of God as a source of strength, security, blessing and hope.

Titles which denote God's greatness
-----------------------------------

### God Almighty

**[GEN 17:1](https://ref.ly/Gen17:1)** *This was the special name by which God revealed himself to the patriarchs. The Hebrew is “El\-Shaddai” possibly meaning “God the mountain”, emphasising God's might.* *See also* [GEN 28:3](https://ref.ly/Gen28:3), [GEN 48:3](https://ref.ly/Gen48:3); [EXO 6:3](https://ref.ly/Exod6:3); [RUT 1:20–1:21](https://ref.ly/Ruth1:20-Ruth1:21); [JOB 5:17](https://ref.ly/Job5:17)JOB [6:4](https://ref.ly/Job6:4), [JOB 37:23](https://ref.ly/Job37:23)[PSA 91:1](https://ref.ly/Ps91:1); [REV 16:14](https://ref.ly/Rev16:14), [REV 19:15](https://ref.ly/Rev19:15)
