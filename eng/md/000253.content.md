## 3045: Holy Spirit, sovereignty of (id: 3045)

The Holy Spirit has supreme power and acts in accordance with divine purposes, exercising authority and control in the world and over God's people.

The Holy Spirit is Lord
-----------------------

**[2CO 3:17–3:18](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:17-2Cor3:18)** *See also* [JDG 3:10](https://ref.ly/Judg3:10)

The Holy Spirit is sovereign in bringing spiritual life
-------------------------------------------------------

**[JHN 3:1–3:8](https://ref.ly/John3:1-John3:8)** *See also* [EZK 37:14](https://ref.ly/Ezek37:14); [JHN 6:63](https://ref.ly/John6:63); [ROM 8:2](https://ref.ly/Rom8:2); [TIT 3:5](https://ref.ly/Titus3:5); [1PE 1:2](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:2)

The Holy Spirit's will is sovereign
-----------------------------------

### He determines the distribution of spiritual gifts

**[1CO 12:11](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:11)** *See also* [1SA 10:6](https://ref.ly/1Sam10:6), [1SA 10:10](https://ref.ly/1Sam10:10), [1SA 19:20](https://ref.ly/1Sam19:20), [1SA 19:23](https://ref.ly/1Sam19:23); [HEB 2:4](https://ref.ly/Heb2:4)

### He controls the appointment of leaders

**[ACT 13:2](https://ref.ly/Acts13:2)** *See also* [ACT 20:28](https://ref.ly/Acts20:28)

The Holy Spirit demonstrates his sovereignty over creation
----------------------------------------------------------

### He participates in the work of creation

**[JOB 33:4](https://ref.ly/Job33:4)** *See also* [GEN 1:2](https://ref.ly/Gen1:2); [PSA 104:30](https://ref.ly/Ps104:30)

### He performs miraculous deeds

**[ACT 8:39](https://ref.ly/Acts8:39)** *See also* [1KI 18:12](https://ref.ly/1Kgs18:12); [2KI 2:16](https://ref.ly/2Kgs2:16); [MAT 12:28–12:38](https://ref.ly/Matt12:28-Matt12:38) parallels [MRK 3:22–3:30](https://ref.ly/Mark3:22-Mark3:30), [LUK 11:14–11:23](https://ref.ly/Luke11:14-Luke11:23)

The Holy Spirit exercises his rule in the lives of individuals
--------------------------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ is led by the Spirit

**[MAT 4:1](https://ref.ly/Matt4:1)** parallels [MRK 1:12](https://ref.ly/Mark1:12), [LUK 4:1](https://ref.ly/Luke4:1)

### God's people are led by the Spirit

**[GAL 5:18](https://ref.ly/Gal5:18)** *See also* [1CH 28:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr28:12); [PSA 143:10](https://ref.ly/Ps143:10); [MAT 10:20](https://ref.ly/Matt10:20); [LUK 2:27](https://ref.ly/Luke2:27); [JHN 16:13](https://ref.ly/John16:13); [ACT 8:29](https://ref.ly/Acts8:29), [ACT 20:22](https://ref.ly/Acts20:22)

### God's people are restrained by the Spirit

**[ACT 16:6–16:7](https://ref.ly/Acts16:6-Acts16:7)**

### God's people are given victory by the Spirit

**[JDG 3:10](https://ref.ly/Judg3:10)** *See also* [JDG 14:6](https://ref.ly/Judg14:6), [JDG 14:19](https://ref.ly/Judg14:19), [JDG 15:14](https://ref.ly/Judg15:14)
