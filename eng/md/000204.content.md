## 2372: Jesus Christ, victory of (id: 2372)

Jesus Christ triumphs over and disarms all the powers of evil arrayed against him. Believers are able to share in this victory through faith in Jesus Christ.

The scope of Jesus Christ's victory
-----------------------------------

### Over temptation

**[HEB 4:15](https://ref.ly/Heb4:15)** *See also* [MAT 4:1–4:11](https://ref.ly/Matt4:1-Matt4:11) parallel [LUK 4:1–4:13](https://ref.ly/Luke4:1-Luke4:13)

### Over sin

**[ROM 5:20–5:21](https://ref.ly/Rom5:20-Rom5:21)** *See also* [1JN 3:5](https://ref.ly/1John3:5), [1JN 3:8](https://ref.ly/1John3:8)

### Over Satan

**[JHN 12:31](https://ref.ly/John12:31)** *Satan is called “the prince of this world” in John's Gospel.* *See also* [LUK 10:18–10:19](https://ref.ly/Luke10:18-Luke10:19); [JHN 14:30](https://ref.ly/John14:30), [JHN 16:11](https://ref.ly/John16:11); [HEB 2:14–2:15](https://ref.ly/Heb2:14-Heb2:15)

### Over evil spirits

[MRK 1:23–1:27](https://ref.ly/Mark1:23-Mark1:27) parallel [LUK 4:33–4:36](https://ref.ly/Luke4:33-Luke4:36)

### Over all powers

**[COL 2:15](https://ref.ly/Col2:15)** *“powers and authorities” include evil spiritual beings.* *See also* [ROM 8:38–8:39](https://ref.ly/Rom8:38-Rom8:39); [1CO 15:24–15:25](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:24-1Cor15:25); [EPH 1:21–1:22](https://ref.ly/Eph1:21-Eph1:22), [EPH 4:8](https://ref.ly/Eph4:8); [PSA 68:18](https://ref.ly/Ps68:18)

### Over the world

**[JHN 16:33](https://ref.ly/John16:33)** *Here, as elsewhere in John's Gospel, “world” refers to the human race in rebellion against God.* *See also* [2TH 2:8](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:8) *“the lawless one” represents the leader of human forces of evil*; [1JN 5:4–5:5](https://ref.ly/1John5:4-1John5:5); [REV 17:12–17:14](https://ref.ly/Rev17:12-Rev17:14), [REV 19:11–19:21](https://ref.ly/Rev19:11-Rev19:21)

### Over death

**[ROM 6:9](https://ref.ly/Rom6:9)** *See also* [ACT 2:24](https://ref.ly/Acts2:24); [1CO 15:26](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:26), [1CO 15:54–15:57](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:54-1Cor15:57); [2TI 1:10](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:10); [REV 1:18](https://ref.ly/Rev1:18)

### By his death on the cross

**[HEB 2:14](https://ref.ly/Heb2:14)** *See also* [COL 2:15](https://ref.ly/Col2:15); [REV 12:11](https://ref.ly/Rev12:11)

### By God's authority

**[LUK 11:20](https://ref.ly/Luke11:20)** parallel [MAT 12:28](https://ref.ly/Matt12:28)

### By his spoken word

**[MAT 8:16](https://ref.ly/Matt8:16)** *See also* [MAT 8:8](https://ref.ly/Matt8:8) parallel [LUK 7:7](https://ref.ly/Luke7:7); [MAT 8:13](https://ref.ly/Matt8:13)
