## 4135: demons, Jesus Christ's authority over (id: 4135)

Demons are subject to the authority of Jesus Christ during his ministry and subsequently in the ministry of the church.

All spiritual powers are subject to Jesus Christ who has supreme authority
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[EPH 1:19–1:22](https://ref.ly/Eph1:19-Eph1:22)** *See also* [ROM 8:38](https://ref.ly/Rom8:38); [EPH 3:10](https://ref.ly/Eph3:10); [COL 1:16](https://ref.ly/Col1:16), [COL 2:10](https://ref.ly/Col2:10), [COL 2:15](https://ref.ly/Col2:15)
