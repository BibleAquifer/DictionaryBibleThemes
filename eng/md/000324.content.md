## 4114: angels, and praise of God (id: 4114)

Spiritual beings who praise God in a manner that is beyond human capacity.

Angels worship God in his presence
----------------------------------

**[REV 7:11](https://ref.ly/Rev7:11)** *See also* [REV 3:5](https://ref.ly/Rev3:5), [REV 8:2](https://ref.ly/Rev8:2)

Angels praise God for his works
-------------------------------

### Angels praise God's work of creation

**[JOB 38:4–38:7](https://ref.ly/Job38:4-Job38:7)** *See also* [PSA 148:1–148:5](https://ref.ly/Ps148:1-Ps148:5); [NEH 9:6](https://ref.ly/Neh9:6)

### Angels praise God's work of redemption

**[LUK 2:13–2:14](https://ref.ly/Luke2:13-Luke2:14)** *The angels are praising God for Jesus Christ's birth.* *See also* [ISA 44:23](https://ref.ly/Isa44:23), [ISA 49:13](https://ref.ly/Isa49:13); [LUK 15:10](https://ref.ly/Luke15:10); [HEB 1:6](https://ref.ly/Heb1:6); [DEU 32:43](https://ref.ly/Deut32:43)

### Angels praise God's kingdom

**[PSA 103:19–103:21](https://ref.ly/Ps103:19-Ps103:21)** *See also* [PSA 96:10–96:11](https://ref.ly/Ps96:10-Ps96:11) parallel [1CH 16:30–16:31](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:30-1Chr16:31); [PSA 145:10–145:11](https://ref.ly/Ps145:10-Ps145:11); [REV 11:15](https://ref.ly/Rev11:15)

Angelic beings praise God
-------------------------

**[ISA 6:2–6:4](https://ref.ly/Isa6:2-Isa6:4)** *See also* [REV 4:8](https://ref.ly/Rev4:8), [REV 5:8–5:10](https://ref.ly/Rev5:8-Rev5:10), [REV 7:11–7:12](https://ref.ly/Rev7:11-Rev7:12)

Thousands of angels worship God
-------------------------------

**[HEB 12:22](https://ref.ly/Heb12:22)** *See also* [DEU 33:2](https://ref.ly/Deut33:2); [PSA 68:17](https://ref.ly/Ps68:17); [DAN 7:10](https://ref.ly/Dan7:10); [REV 5:11–5:12](https://ref.ly/Rev5:11-Rev5:12)

Images of angelic beings decorate Israel's sanctuaries
------------------------------------------------------

**[1KI 6:23–6:29](https://ref.ly/1Kgs6:23-1Kgs6:29)** parallels [2CH 3:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr3:7), [2CH 3:10–3:13](https://ref.ly/2Chr3:10-2Chr3:13) *See also* [EXO 26:1](https://ref.ly/Exod26:1); [EZK 41:18–41:20](https://ref.ly/Ezek41:18-Ezek41:20), [EZK 41:25](https://ref.ly/Ezek41:25) *The images of the cherubim represent the angels who worship God in heaven.*

Angels are not to be worshipped
-------------------------------

**[REV 22:8–22:9](https://ref.ly/Rev22:8-Rev22:9)** *See also* [MAT 4:9–4:10](https://ref.ly/Matt4:9-Matt4:10) parallel [LUK 4:7–4:8](https://ref.ly/Luke4:7-Luke4:8); [ROM 1:25](https://ref.ly/Rom1:25); [COL 2:18](https://ref.ly/Col2:18)
