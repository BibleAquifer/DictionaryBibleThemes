## 8332: reputation (id: 8332)

The estimation of a person in the sight of God or other people. Scripture stresses the importance of believers having a good reputation with those outside the faith.

God's defence of his own reputation
-----------------------------------

### God's actions in history to uphold his reputation

**[EXO 9:13–9:16](https://ref.ly/Exod9:13-Exod9:16)** *See also* [EZK 20:9–20:10](https://ref.ly/Ezek20:9-Ezek20:10)

### God's rebukes his people for failing to uphold his reputation

**[MAL 2:2](https://ref.ly/Mal2:2)** *See also* [MAL 1:6–1:11](https://ref.ly/Mal1:6-Mal1:11)

### God's promises to uphold his reputation

**[EZK 36:22–36:23](https://ref.ly/Ezek36:22-Ezek36:23)** *See also* [ISA 42:8](https://ref.ly/Isa42:8)

The reputation of believers
---------------------------

### Believers must seek a good reputation with God

**[ACT 24:16](https://ref.ly/Acts24:16)** *See also* [2CO 5:9–5:10](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:9-2Cor5:10); [1TH 4:1](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:1)

### Believers must have a good reputation with outsiders

**[ACT 5:13–5:16](https://ref.ly/Acts5:13-Acts5:16)** *See also* [ACT 2:46–2:47](https://ref.ly/Acts2:46-Acts2:47), [ACT 24:16](https://ref.ly/Acts24:16); [1TI 3:7](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:7)

### Believers must not boast to improve their own reputation

**[2CO 10:17–10:18](https://ref.ly/2Cor10:17-2Cor10:18)** *See also* [1CO 3:5–3:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor3:5-1Cor3:7); [PHP 3:4–3:9](https://ref.ly/Phil3:4-Phil3:9)

### Believers must not seek a good reputation through outward show

**[MAT 6:16–6:18](https://ref.ly/Matt6:16-Matt6:18)** *See also* [MAT 6:1–6:8](https://ref.ly/Matt6:1-Matt6:8), [MAT 23:2–23:12](https://ref.ly/Matt23:2-Matt23:12)

### Believers may find that serving God leads to a bad reputation with unbelievers

**[ACT 17:5–17:8](https://ref.ly/Acts17:5-Acts17:8)** *See also* [1KI 19:1–19:5](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:1-1Kgs19:5); [LUK 23:1–23:2](https://ref.ly/Luke23:1-Luke23:2)
