## 4123: Satan, as deceiver (id: 4123)

Satan's character is deceitful, devious and cunning as in a variety of guises he seeks to influence people for his own ends.

Satan's deceitful character
---------------------------

### He is evil

**[MAT 6:13](https://ref.ly/Matt6:13)** *See also* [MAT 5:37](https://ref.ly/Matt5:37), [MAT 13:19](https://ref.ly/Matt13:19); [JHN 17:15](https://ref.ly/John17:15); [2TH 3:3](https://ref.ly/2Thess3:3); [1JN 2:13–2:14](https://ref.ly/1John2:13-1John2:14), [1JN 3:8](https://ref.ly/1John3:8), [1JN 3:12](https://ref.ly/1John3:12), [1JN 5:18](https://ref.ly/1John5:18)

### He is a liar

**[JHN 8:44](https://ref.ly/John8:44)** *See also* [GEN 3:4–3:5](https://ref.ly/Gen3:4-Gen3:5); [JOB 1:11](https://ref.ly/Job1:11), [JOB 2:5](https://ref.ly/Job2:5); [REV 3:9](https://ref.ly/Rev3:9)

### He is devious

**[2CO 11:14](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:14)** *See also* [2CO 11:3](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:3); [EPH 6:11](https://ref.ly/Eph6:11); [2TH 2:9](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:9); [1TI 3:7](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:7); [2TI 2:26](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:26)

### He is scheming

**[EPH 6:11](https://ref.ly/Eph6:11)** *See also* [2CO 2:10–2:11](https://ref.ly/2Cor2:10-2Cor2:11)

### He is a slanderer
