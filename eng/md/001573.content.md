## 6238: homosexuality (id: 6238)

Sexual relations between people of the same sex. The biblical emphasis upon the loving union of male and female, as an integral part of God's creation ordinance, establishes the context against which the censure of homosexual practice is to be set.

God's creation order for sexuality
----------------------------------

[GEN 1:27–1:28](https://ref.ly/Gen1:27-Gen1:28), [GEN 2:18–2:24](https://ref.ly/Gen2:18-Gen2:24); [MRK 10:6–10:9](https://ref.ly/Mark10:6-Mark10:9) parallel [MAT 19:4–19:5](https://ref.ly/Matt19:4-Matt19:5); ; [EPH 5:31](https://ref.ly/Eph5:31)

OT prohibitions of homosexual practice
--------------------------------------

**[LEV 18:22](https://ref.ly/Lev18:22)** *See also* [LEV 20:13](https://ref.ly/Lev20:13)

Homosexual practice is incompatible with the kingdom of God
-----------------------------------------------------------

**[1CO 6:9–6:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:9-1Cor6:10)** *The meanings of the Greek words for “male prostitutes” and “homosexual offenders” are disputed, but the terms are commonly associated with particular forms of homosexual behaviour.* *See also* [1TI 1:9–1:11](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:9-1Tim1:11)

Sexual disorder is one consequence of rejecting God
---------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 1:21–1:27](https://ref.ly/Rom1:21-Rom1:27)** [1KI 14:24](https://ref.ly/1Kgs14:24), [1KI 15:12](https://ref.ly/1Kgs15:12); ; [2KI 23:7](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:7)

Examples of homosexual practice
-------------------------------
