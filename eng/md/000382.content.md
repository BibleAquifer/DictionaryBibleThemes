## 4278: spring of water (id: 4278)

Springs of water were important to the life of Israel, providing fresh water for the people. Both God and the gospel are often compared to springs of living water, on account of their life\-bringing and refreshing qualities.

The symbolic importance of springs of water to Israel
-----------------------------------------------------

### Living water as a sign of hope and prosperity

**[ZEC 14:8](https://ref.ly/Zech14:8)** *See also* [ISA 12:3](https://ref.ly/Isa12:3), [ISA 49:10](https://ref.ly/Isa49:10); [JER 31:9](https://ref.ly/Jer31:9)

### Living water as a source of fertility

**[JER 17:8](https://ref.ly/Jer17:8)** *See also* [PSA 1:3](https://ref.ly/Ps1:3)

### Living water as a sign of God's presence and power

[EXO 17:1–17:7](https://ref.ly/Exod17:1-Exod17:7); ; [NUM 20:1–20:11](https://ref.ly/Num20:1-Num20:11)
