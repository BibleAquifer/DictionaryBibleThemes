## 7797: teaching (id: 7797)

The apostles' teaching formed the basis of the instruction given to the first Christians. Parts of the NT draw a distinction between preaching and teaching, seeing the former as a means of converting individuals, and the latter as a means of instructing them after conversion.

--------------------------

**[ECC 12:11–12:12](https://ref.ly/Eccl12:11-Eccl12:12)** *“goads” prod the sluggish to action, while “embedded nails” furnish a kind of mental anchorage. “Shepherd” is almost certainly a reference to God himself.* *See also* [PRO 1:8–1:9](https://ref.ly/Prov1:8-Prov1:9), [PRO 3:1–3:2](https://ref.ly/Prov3:1-Prov3:2), [PRO 4:1–4:4](https://ref.ly/Prov4:1-Prov4:4), [PRO 6:20–6:23](https://ref.ly/Prov6:20-Prov6:23); [MAT 5:19](https://ref.ly/Matt5:19); [1CO 14:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor14:6); [2TI 4:2–4:3](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:2-2Tim4:3)
