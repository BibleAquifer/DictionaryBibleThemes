## 5562: suffering, of the innocent (id: 5562)

Scripture does not teach that the wicked will suffer, whereas the innocent and blameless will prosper. It offers insights into the place of suffering in the lives of the innocent, and the apparent injustice of this situation.

The apparent injustice of suffering
-----------------------------------

**[HAB 1:13](https://ref.ly/Hab1:13)** *See also* [JOB 19:7](https://ref.ly/Job19:7), [JOB 24:1](https://ref.ly/Job24:1), [JOB 24:12](https://ref.ly/Job24:12); [PSA 59:3–59:4](https://ref.ly/Ps59:3-Ps59:4), [PSA 74:1](https://ref.ly/Ps74:1), [PSA 74:11](https://ref.ly/Ps74:11), [PSA 88:5](https://ref.ly/Ps88:5), [PSA 88:14](https://ref.ly/Ps88:14)
