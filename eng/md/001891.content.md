## 7632: Twelve, diverse characters of (id: 7632)

The Twelve were men of varied background, gifts and character.

The varied backgrounds of the Twelve
------------------------------------

Peter, Andrew, James and John were fishermen: [MAT 4:18](https://ref.ly/Matt4:18), [MAT 4:21](https://ref.ly/Matt4:21); [MAT 9:9](https://ref.ly/Matt9:9) *Matthew was a tax collector*; ; [MAT 10:4](https://ref.ly/Matt10:4) *Simon was a political activist.*

The varied roles of the Twelve
------------------------------

### Some took a prominent role in the group

**[GAL 2:9](https://ref.ly/Gal2:9)**

### Some played minor but significant roles

*See also* [JHN 1:40–1:42](https://ref.ly/John1:40-John1:42) *Andrew brought Peter to Jesus Christ*; [JHN 1:45–1:46](https://ref.ly/John1:45-John1:46) *Philip brought Nathanael*; [JHN 12:20–12:22](https://ref.ly/John12:20-John12:22) *Philip and Andrew brought the Greeks to Jesus Christ.*

The varied temperaments of the Twelve
-------------------------------------

[MAT 14:28](https://ref.ly/Matt14:28) *Peter was impetuous*; ; [LUK 9:54](https://ref.ly/Luke9:54) *James and John were fiery*; ; [LUK 22:4](https://ref.ly/Luke22:4) *Judas Iscariot was a traitorous loner*; ; [JHN 20:25](https://ref.ly/John20:25) *Thomas was cautious.*

The Twelve had a common call to discipleship and witness
--------------------------------------------------------

[ACT 1:21–1:22](https://ref.ly/Acts1:21-Acts1:22)

The fallibility of the Twelve
-----------------------------

[MRK 8:31–8:33](https://ref.ly/Mark8:31-Mark8:33) *Peter misunderstood*; ; [MRK 16:14](https://ref.ly/Mark16:14) *Jesus Christ rebuked the Eleven for not believing reports of his resurrection*; ; [LUK 9:12–9:13](https://ref.ly/Luke9:12-Luke9:13) *Jesus Christ tested their reaction to human need*; ; [JHN 14:8–14:9](https://ref.ly/John14:8-John14:9) *Philip asked the way to the Father.*

Jesus Christ gave the Twelve special teaching
---------------------------------------------

[MRK 4:10–4:11](https://ref.ly/Mark4:10-Mark4:11) *about his parables*; ; [MRK 9:35](https://ref.ly/Mark9:35) *about power and position*; ; [LUK 18:31](https://ref.ly/Luke18:31) *about his destiny*

Jesus Christ spent his last days and hours with the Twelve
----------------------------------------------------------

[MRK 11:11](https://ref.ly/Mark11:11) *after the triumphal entry into Jerusalem*; ; [MRK 14:17](https://ref.ly/Mark14:17) *at the Last Supper*

The Twelve failed Jesus Christ
------------------------------

### Jesus Christ's concern that they would leave him

[JHN 6:67–6:70](https://ref.ly/John6:67-John6:70)

### Judas betrayed him

[MAT 26:14–26:16](https://ref.ly/Matt26:14-Matt26:16) parallels [MRK 14:10](https://ref.ly/Mark14:10), [LUK 22:3–22:4](https://ref.ly/Luke22:3-Luke22:4)

### They failed to support him in his final agony of prayer

[MAT 26:40–26:41](https://ref.ly/Matt26:40-Matt26:41) parallels [MRK 14:37–14:38](https://ref.ly/Mark14:37-Mark14:38), [LUK 22:45–22:46](https://ref.ly/Luke22:45-Luke22:46)

### Peter disowned Jesus Christ

[MAT 26:74–26:75](https://ref.ly/Matt26:74-Matt26:75) parallels [MRK 14:71–14:72](https://ref.ly/Mark14:71-Mark14:72), [LUK 22:60–22:61](https://ref.ly/Luke22:60-Luke22:61)

### All abandoned Jesus Christ

[MAT 26:56](https://ref.ly/Matt26:56) *the exception was “the disciple whom Jesus loved”, present at the crucifixion ([Jn 19:25–27](https://ref.ly/John19:25-John19:27))*
