## 8618: prayerfulness (id: 8618)

An attitude by which a constant relationship between God and believers is developed.

Prayerfulness is a way of life
------------------------------

**[LUK 2:37](https://ref.ly/Luke2:37)** *See also* [PSA 5:3](https://ref.ly/Ps5:3), [PSA 55:17](https://ref.ly/Ps55:17), [PSA 109:4](https://ref.ly/Ps109:4); [DAN 6:10–6:11](https://ref.ly/Dan6:10-Dan6:11), [DAN 6:13](https://ref.ly/Dan6:13)

Prayerfulness arises from a desire to be with God
-------------------------------------------------

**[PSA 130:5–130:6](https://ref.ly/Ps130:5-Ps130:6)** *See also* [PSA 42:1–42:4](https://ref.ly/Ps42:1-Ps42:4), [PSA 84:1–84:2](https://ref.ly/Ps84:1-Ps84:2)

Prayerfulness arises from an awareness of need that can only be met by God
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[1TI 5:5](https://ref.ly/1Tim5:5)** *See also* [PSA 86:1](https://ref.ly/Ps86:1), [PSA 105:4](https://ref.ly/Ps105:4)

Prayerfulness demonstrates a continuing trust in God
----------------------------------------------------

**[JHN 15:5–15:8](https://ref.ly/John15:5-John15:8)** *See also* [PSA 63:1–63:8](https://ref.ly/Ps63:1-Ps63:8)

Prayerfulness involves a heart that is right with God
-----------------------------------------------------

**[ISA 1:15–1:17](https://ref.ly/Isa1:15-Isa1:17)** *See also* [MAT 6:7](https://ref.ly/Matt6:7); [MRK 12:40](https://ref.ly/Mark12:40) parallel [LUK 20:47](https://ref.ly/Luke20:47)

Prayerfulness involves the need to be alert
-------------------------------------------

**[MAT 26:41](https://ref.ly/Matt26:41)** parallels [MRK 14:38](https://ref.ly/Mark14:38), [LUK 22:40](https://ref.ly/Luke22:40) *See also* [MIC 7:7](https://ref.ly/Mic7:7); [LUK 21:36](https://ref.ly/Luke21:36); [COL 4:2](https://ref.ly/Col4:2)

Prayerfulness needs to be maintained especially in difficult circumstances
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[LUK 18:1](https://ref.ly/Luke18:1)** *See also* [HAB 3:16–3:19](https://ref.ly/Hab3:16-Hab3:19); [ACT 16:25](https://ref.ly/Acts16:25); [1TH 5:17](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:17); [1PE 4:7](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:7)
