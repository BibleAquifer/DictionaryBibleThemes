## 6611: adoption, privileges and duties of (id: 6611)

As adopted members of the family of God, believers receive both the privileges and responsibilities of being children of God.

The privileges received by believers through adoption
-----------------------------------------------------

### Believers are given the Spirit of adoption

**[GAL 4:6](https://ref.ly/Gal4:6)** *See also* [ROM 8:15](https://ref.ly/Rom8:15)

### Believers have access to their heavenly Father

**[EPH 2:18](https://ref.ly/Eph2:18)** *See also* [EPH 3:12](https://ref.ly/Eph3:12); [HEB 4:16](https://ref.ly/Heb4:16)

**[ROM 8:17](https://ref.ly/Rom8:17)** *See also* [GAL 3:29](https://ref.ly/Gal3:29), [GAL 4:7](https://ref.ly/Gal4:7); [COL 1:12](https://ref.ly/Col1:12); [1PE 1:4](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:4)
