## 4134: demons, exorcism of (id: 4134)

Jesus Christ drove out many demons during his earthly ministry and he delegated this authority to his disciples.

Jesus Christ understood exorcism as part of his ministry
--------------------------------------------------------

**[LUK 13:32](https://ref.ly/Luke13:32)** *See also* [LUK 4:18](https://ref.ly/Luke4:18)

### He drove out demons from individuals brought to him

**[MAT 8:16](https://ref.ly/Matt8:16)** parallels [MRK 1:32–1:34](https://ref.ly/Mark1:32-Mark1:34), [LUK 4:40–4:41](https://ref.ly/Luke4:40-Luke4:41) *See also* [MAT 4:24](https://ref.ly/Matt4:24)

### He drove out demons from individuals whom he encountered

**[MRK 1:23–1:26](https://ref.ly/Mark1:23-Mark1:26)** parallel [LUK 4:33–4:35](https://ref.ly/Luke4:33-Luke4:35) *See also* [MRK 1:39](https://ref.ly/Mark1:39)

Jesus Christ had power over demons
----------------------------------

### He was able to drive demons out at a distance

[MAT 15:22–15:28](https://ref.ly/Matt15:22-Matt15:28) parallel [MRK 7:25–7:30](https://ref.ly/Mark7:25-Mark7:30)

**[MAT 17:18](https://ref.ly/Matt17:18)** parallels [MRK 9:25](https://ref.ly/Mark9:25), [LUK 9:42](https://ref.ly/Luke9:42) *See also* [MRK 1:25](https://ref.ly/Mark1:25) parallel [LUK 4:35](https://ref.ly/Luke4:35); [LUK 4:41](https://ref.ly/Luke4:41)
