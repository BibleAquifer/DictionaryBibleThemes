## 8157: righteousness, as faith (id: 8157)

Full faith and trust make a person pleasing in the sight of God.

Human righteousness compared with God's righteousness
-----------------------------------------------------

### Human beings cannot by themselves achieve righteousness in the sight of God

**[ECC 7:20](https://ref.ly/Eccl7:20)**; **[ISA 64:6](https://ref.ly/Isa64:6)**; **[MAT 5:20](https://ref.ly/Matt5:20)** *See also* [PRO 21:2](https://ref.ly/Prov21:2); [DAN 9:18](https://ref.ly/Dan9:18); [MAT 23:28](https://ref.ly/Matt23:28); [LUK 16:15](https://ref.ly/Luke16:15), [LUK 18:9](https://ref.ly/Luke18:9); [ROM 3:10](https://ref.ly/Rom3:10), [ROM 3:20](https://ref.ly/Rom3:20); [PHP 3:6–3:7](https://ref.ly/Phil3:6-Phil3:7)

Righteousness and faith in Jesus Christ
---------------------------------------

### It is God\-given and not the result of human effort

**[ROM 1:17](https://ref.ly/Rom1:17)**; **[PHP 3:8–3:9](https://ref.ly/Phil3:8-Phil3:9)** *Paul is contrasting this righteousness with his own previous efforts.* *See also* [ACT 13:39](https://ref.ly/Acts13:39); [ROM 3:21](https://ref.ly/Rom3:21), [ROM 3:27–3:28](https://ref.ly/Rom3:27-Rom3:28) *It is apparent that faith is not regarded as another kind of “work” which earns salvation*; [ROM 4:1–4:8](https://ref.ly/Rom4:1-Rom4:8), [ROM 5:17](https://ref.ly/Rom5:17), [ROM 9:30–9:31](https://ref.ly/Rom9:30-Rom9:31); [GAL 3:11–3:12](https://ref.ly/Gal3:11-Gal3:12)

### Faith is centred on Jesus Christ and what he has accomplished

**[ROM 5:1–5:2](https://ref.ly/Rom5:1-Rom5:2)** *“justified” means “declared righteous” in a legal sense.* *See also* [ROM 3:21–3:26](https://ref.ly/Rom3:21-Rom3:26), [ROM 4:18–4:25](https://ref.ly/Rom4:18-Rom4:25), [ROM 10:6–10:10](https://ref.ly/Rom10:6-Rom10:10); [GAL 3:6–3:9](https://ref.ly/Gal3:6-Gal3:9)

### Saving faith is not mere belief, but acting on the basis of that belief

[JAS 2:21–2:24](https://ref.ly/Jas2:21-Jas2:24)
