## 8726: doubters (id: 8726)

Scripture provides examples of believers who express temporary doubts\-\-in particular about the willingness and power of God to deliver his people, the nature of his purpose, and their own place within the divine plan.

-----------------------------------------------------------

**[EXO 3:11](https://ref.ly/Exod3:11)** *See also* [EXO 4:1](https://ref.ly/Exod4:1), [EXO 4:10–4:13](https://ref.ly/Exod4:10-Exod4:13), [EXO 6:12](https://ref.ly/Exod6:12), [EXO 6:30](https://ref.ly/Exod6:30); [NUM 11:13–11:15](https://ref.ly/Num11:13-Num11:15)

### Gideon

Those doubting God's power
--------------------------

### Abraham and Isaac doubt God's protection

**[GEN 12:12–12:13](https://ref.ly/Gen12:12-Gen12:13)** *Fearing for his safety, Abraham resorts to deception.* *See also* [GEN 20:2](https://ref.ly/Gen20:2), [GEN 26:7](https://ref.ly/Gen26:7)

### Moses and Israel in the desert

**[EXO 5:22–5:23](https://ref.ly/Exod5:22-Exod5:23)** *See also* [EXO 14:10–14:11](https://ref.ly/Exod14:10-Exod14:11) *The Israelites doubt God's protection at the Red Sea*; [NUM 11:21–11:22](https://ref.ly/Num11:21-Num11:22) *Moses doubts God's provision of food*; [NUM 13:31–13:33](https://ref.ly/Num13:31-Num13:33) *The ten spies doubt God's power to give victory in Canaan*; [NUM 20:7–20:12](https://ref.ly/Num20:7-Num20:12) *Moses strikes the rock twice.*

### Others doubting God's deliverance

[1SA 17:11](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:11) *The people of Israel are dismayed by the challenge from Goliath*; ; [ISA 7:2](https://ref.ly/Isa7:2) *King Ahaz, afraid of the coalition of Syria and Israel turns to Assyria for help*; ; [MAT 14:29–14:31](https://ref.ly/Matt14:29-Matt14:31) *Peter, walking on water, is afraid and begins to sink*; ; [ACT 12:14–12:15](https://ref.ly/Acts12:14-Acts12:15) *The early church does not believe that God has released Peter from prison.*
