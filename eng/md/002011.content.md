## 8116: discipleship, cost of (id: 8116)

The denial of self\-interests and desires, and a total commitment to do the will of God, even to the point of death.

The cost of discipleship involves a denial of self\-interests and desires
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

### The cost involves self\-denial

**[MAT 16:24](https://ref.ly/Matt16:24)** parallels [MRK 8:34](https://ref.ly/Mark8:34), [LUK 9:23](https://ref.ly/Luke9:23) *See also* [MAT 10:38](https://ref.ly/Matt10:38); [LUK 14:27](https://ref.ly/Luke14:27)

### Self\-denial means not living for oneself

**[ROM 14:7](https://ref.ly/Rom14:7)** *See also* [2CO 5:15](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:15); [GAL 2:20](https://ref.ly/Gal2:20); [1PE 4:2](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:2)

The cost of discipleship is to be carefully considered
------------------------------------------------------

**[LUK 14:28–14:32](https://ref.ly/Luke14:28-Luke14:32)**

The cost of discipleship means total commitment to the will of God
------------------------------------------------------------------

### Total surrender is required

**[LUK 14:33](https://ref.ly/Luke14:33)** *See also* [PHP 3:7–3:8](https://ref.ly/Phil3:7-Phil3:8)

### The security of the world is to be resisted

**[MAT 8:19–8:20](https://ref.ly/Matt8:19-Matt8:20)** parallel [LUK 9:57–9:58](https://ref.ly/Luke9:57-Luke9:58)

### Jesus Christ must have first priority

**[LUK 9:59–9:60](https://ref.ly/Luke9:59-Luke9:60)** parallel [MAT 8:21–8:22](https://ref.ly/Matt8:21-Matt8:22) *See also* [MAT 19:16–19:21](https://ref.ly/Matt19:16-Matt19:21) parallels [MRK 10:17–10:21](https://ref.ly/Mark10:17-Mark10:21), [LUK 18:18–18:22](https://ref.ly/Luke18:18-Luke18:22); [COL 1:18](https://ref.ly/Col1:18)

### Jesus Christ must come before family ties

**[LUK 9:61–9:62](https://ref.ly/Luke9:61-Luke9:62)** *See also* [MAT 10:37](https://ref.ly/Matt10:37); [LUK 14:26](https://ref.ly/Luke14:26)

The cost of discipleship is constant
