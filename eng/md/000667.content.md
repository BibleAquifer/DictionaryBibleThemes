## 5063: spirit, nature of (id: 5063)

The life\-giving principle of human nature, to be distinguished from the purely physical aspects of life.

The human spirit as distinct from the body
------------------------------------------

**[MAT 26:41](https://ref.ly/Matt26:41)** parallel [MRK 14:38](https://ref.ly/Mark14:38)**[1TH 5:23](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:23)** *See also* [ROM 8:10](https://ref.ly/Rom8:10); [1CO 5:3–5:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor5:3-1Cor5:5), [1CO 7:34](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:34); [2CO 7:1](https://ref.ly/2Cor7:1); [COL 2:5](https://ref.ly/Col2:5); [HEB 4:12](https://ref.ly/Heb4:12) *“spirit” and “soul” may be two names for the inner being penetrated by the word of God, or they may distinguish the inner person in relation to God (“spirit”) from their relation to the world (“soul”)*; [JAS 2:26](https://ref.ly/Jas2:26); [REV 18:13](https://ref.ly/Rev18:13)

The human spirit vitalises the body
-----------------------------------

**[JAS 2:26](https://ref.ly/Jas2:26)** *See also* [GEN 2:7](https://ref.ly/Gen2:7); [JOB 34:14](https://ref.ly/Job34:14); [LUK 8:55](https://ref.ly/Luke8:55); [1CO 15:45](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:45); [REV 13:15](https://ref.ly/Rev13:15) *“breath” is the same word as “spirit” in Greek.*
