## 7768: priests, function in OT times (id: 7768)

Priests had a unique role: administering the sanctuary, offering sacrifice and leading and teaching the people. Accordingly they were set apart by a special code of conduct.

The priests were set apart
--------------------------

### They were consecrated to the LORD

**[LEV 22:9](https://ref.ly/Lev22:9)** *See also* [EXO 28:1](https://ref.ly/Exod28:1), [EXO 28:4](https://ref.ly/Exod28:4), [EXO 28:41](https://ref.ly/Exod28:41), [EXO 29:1](https://ref.ly/Exod29:1), [EXO 29:44](https://ref.ly/Exod29:44), [EXO 30:34](https://ref.ly/Exod30:34), [EXO 31:10](https://ref.ly/Exod31:10), [EXO 35:19](https://ref.ly/Exod35:19), [EXO 39:41](https://ref.ly/Exod39:41)

### They were not to incur uncleanness

**[LEV 21:1](https://ref.ly/Lev21:1)** *Some exceptions to uncleanness by touching a corpse are listed in verses 2–3\.* *See also* [EZK 44:25](https://ref.ly/Ezek44:25)

### They were not to shave or mutilate themselves

**[LEV 21:5](https://ref.ly/Lev21:5)** *See also* [EZK 44:20](https://ref.ly/Ezek44:20)

### They were not to marry any but a virtuous Israelite

**[LEV 21:7](https://ref.ly/Lev21:7)** *See also* [EZK 44:22](https://ref.ly/Ezek44:22)

### They were not to drink wine before entering the sanctuary

**[LEV 10:9](https://ref.ly/Lev10:9)** *See also* [EZK 44:21](https://ref.ly/Ezek44:21)

### They were disqualified by physical defects

**[LEV 21:17](https://ref.ly/Lev21:17)** *See also* [LEV 21:18–21:22](https://ref.ly/Lev21:18-Lev21:22)

### They were not to allow outsiders to eat the sacred offerings

**[LEV 22:15–22:16](https://ref.ly/Lev22:15-Lev22:16)** *See also* [LEV 24:9](https://ref.ly/Lev24:9); [1SA 21:4](https://ref.ly/1Sam21:4); [MAT 12:4](https://ref.ly/Matt12:4)

### They were not to own land in Israel

**[NUM 18:20](https://ref.ly/Num18:20)** *See also* [EZK 44:28](https://ref.ly/Ezek44:28)

### They were not to offer unacceptable sacrifices

**[LEV 22:20](https://ref.ly/Lev22:20)** *See also* [MAL 1:7–1:9](https://ref.ly/Mal1:7-Mal1:9)

### They were to obey God's law meticulously

**[LEV 22:31](https://ref.ly/Lev22:31)** *See also* [EZK 44:24](https://ref.ly/Ezek44:24)
