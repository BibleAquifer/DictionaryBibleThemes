## 1270: right hand of God (id: 1270)

A figure of speech that represents God's ultimate power and authority, and where the exalted Jesus Christ now sits.

The right hand of God as a symbol
---------------------------------

### A symbol of power

**[PSA 89:13](https://ref.ly/Ps89:13)**

### A symbol of protection

**[PSA 16:8](https://ref.ly/Ps16:8)** *See also* [PSA 63:8](https://ref.ly/Ps63:8), [PSA 139:10](https://ref.ly/Ps139:10); [ISA 41:13](https://ref.ly/Isa41:13); [LAM 2:3](https://ref.ly/Lam2:3)

### A symbol of God's presence

**[PSA 16:11](https://ref.ly/Ps16:11)** *See also* [PSA 48:10](https://ref.ly/Ps48:10), [PSA 73:23](https://ref.ly/Ps73:23); [ISA 62:8](https://ref.ly/Isa62:8)

### A symbol of God's saving power

**[PSA 60:5](https://ref.ly/Ps60:5)** parallel [PSA 108:6](https://ref.ly/Ps108:6) *See also* [PSA 17:7](https://ref.ly/Ps17:7), [PSA 20:6](https://ref.ly/Ps20:6), [PSA 80:15](https://ref.ly/Ps80:15), [PSA 98:1](https://ref.ly/Ps98:1), [PSA 138:7](https://ref.ly/Ps138:7)

### A symbol of victory

[PSA 18:35](https://ref.ly/Ps18:35), [PSA 44:3](https://ref.ly/Ps44:3), [PSA 78:54](https://ref.ly/Ps78:54); ; [ISA 41:10](https://ref.ly/Isa41:10)

### A symbol of the defeat of God's enemies

**[EXO 15:6](https://ref.ly/Exod15:6)** *See also* [EXO 15:12](https://ref.ly/Exod15:12); [PSA 21:8](https://ref.ly/Ps21:8), [PSA 74:11](https://ref.ly/Ps74:11)

### A symbol of God's mighty works

**[PSA 45:4](https://ref.ly/Ps45:4)** *See also* [PSA 77:10](https://ref.ly/Ps77:10), [PSA 118:15–118:16](https://ref.ly/Ps118:15-Ps118:16); [ISA 48:13](https://ref.ly/Isa48:13)

### A symbol of judgment

[LAM 2:4](https://ref.ly/Lam2:4); ; [HAB 2:16](https://ref.ly/Hab2:16)

Jesus Christ at God's right hand
--------------------------------

### Jesus Christ is exalted to God's right hand

**[HEB 1:13](https://ref.ly/Heb1:13)** *Comparison between Jesus Christ and the angels confirms Christ's absolute supremacy.* *See also* [PSA 110:1](https://ref.ly/Ps110:1); [MAT 22:44](https://ref.ly/Matt22:44) parallels [MRK 12:36](https://ref.ly/Mark12:36), [LUK 20:42](https://ref.ly/Luke20:42); [LUK 22:69](https://ref.ly/Luke22:69); [ACT 2:34](https://ref.ly/Acts2:34), [ACT 7:55–7:56](https://ref.ly/Acts7:55-Acts7:56); [COL 3:1](https://ref.ly/Col3:1); [HEB 1:3](https://ref.ly/Heb1:3), [HEB 12:2](https://ref.ly/Heb12:2)

### Jesus Christ rules every authority at God's right hand

**[1PE 3:22](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:22)** *See also* [EPH 1:20–1:21](https://ref.ly/Eph1:20-Eph1:21)

### Jesus Christ continues his work at God's right hand

**[ROM 8:34](https://ref.ly/Rom8:34)** *See also* [ACT 2:33](https://ref.ly/Acts2:33), [ACT 5:31](https://ref.ly/Acts5:31); [HEB 4:14–4:16](https://ref.ly/Heb4:14-Heb4:16), [HEB 8:1](https://ref.ly/Heb8:1)

### Jesus Christ will return at God's right hand

**[MAT 26:64](https://ref.ly/Matt26:64)** parallel [MRK 14:62](https://ref.ly/Mark14:62)

### Jesus Christ takes a scroll from God's right hand

**[REV 5:1–5:7](https://ref.ly/Rev5:1-Rev5:7)** *The scroll probably refers to God's decrees for the destiny of the world.*
