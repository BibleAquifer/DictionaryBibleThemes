## 8718: disobedience (id: 8718)

The refusal to obey someone, especially someone in a position of authority. Scripture insists on the need to obey God at all times.

Disobedience enters people's hearts for various reasons
-------------------------------------------------------

### Disobedience comes through greed and lust

**[GEN 3:6](https://ref.ly/Gen3:6)** *See also* [GEN 3:11–3:12](https://ref.ly/Gen3:11-Gen3:12); [EXO 16:19–16:20](https://ref.ly/Exod16:19-Exod16:20); [JOS 7:20–7:21](https://ref.ly/Josh7:20-Josh7:21); [2SA 11:2–11:4](https://ref.ly/2Sam11:2-2Sam11:4)

### Disobedience arises from impatience

**[EXO 32:23](https://ref.ly/Exod32:23)** *See also* [NUM 20:10–20:11](https://ref.ly/Num20:10-Num20:11); [1SA 13:8–13:14](https://ref.ly/1Sam13:8-1Sam13:14)
