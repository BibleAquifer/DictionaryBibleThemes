## 8492: watchfulness, of leaders (id: 8492)

Leaders must be alert to the needs of those they serve and also to the dangers that surround believers in the world. The OT teaching on watchmen provides an indication of the importance of watchfulness and the responsibility of those called to be watchful.

Leaders are required to be watchful
-----------------------------------

**[EZK 3:17](https://ref.ly/Ezek3:17)** *See also* [EZK 33:7–33:9](https://ref.ly/Ezek33:7-Ezek33:9); [JHN 21:16](https://ref.ly/John21:16)

### They are accountable to God

**[EZK 3:18](https://ref.ly/Ezek3:18)** *See also* [MRK 13:34–13:35](https://ref.ly/Mark13:34-Mark13:35); [HEB 13:17](https://ref.ly/Heb13:17)

### They must warn against false teachers

**[ACT 20:31](https://ref.ly/Acts20:31)** *Elders are called overseers in [Acts 20:28](https://ref.ly/Acts20:28); [1Ti 3:2](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:2); [Tit 1:7](https://ref.ly/Titus1:7); [1Pe 5:2](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:2).* *See also* [JHN 10:1–10:3](https://ref.ly/John10:1-John10:3); [ACT 20:29](https://ref.ly/Acts20:29); [2TI 4:2–4:5](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:2-2Tim4:5); [TIT 1:9–1:11](https://ref.ly/Titus1:9-Titus1:11)
