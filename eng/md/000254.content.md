## 3050: Holy Spirit, wisdom of (id: 3050)

The quality of being discerning and perceptive which is a characteristic of the Holy Spirit. The Holy Spirit is the source of human wisdom.

Those who knew and can know the wisdom of the Holy Spirit

**[DEU 34:9](https://ref.ly/Deut34:9)** *Either a wise human spirit or the Spirit of God who gives wisdom (NIV footnote).*
