## 5449: poverty, remedies for (id: 5449)

Although poverty can never be totally eliminated from this sinful world, it can be alleviated. Scripture provides guidance concerning how this may be done.

Poverty will always exist in this world
---------------------------------------

**[MAT 26:11](https://ref.ly/Matt26:11)** parallel [JHN 12:8](https://ref.ly/John12:8) *See also* [DEU 15:11](https://ref.ly/Deut15:11)
