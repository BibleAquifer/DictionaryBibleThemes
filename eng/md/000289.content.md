## 3287: Holy Spirit, sealing of (id: 3287)

Based on the practice of sealing letters, documents and property, the Holy Spirit is described as being a seal on the lives of God's people, affirming their Christian character, marking them out as belonging to God and guarding them from the world and the devil.

The Holy Spirit's seal on the life of Jesus Christ
--------------------------------------------------

**[JHN 6:27](https://ref.ly/John6:27)** *The Greek implies a once\-and\-for\-all sealing at a particular time in the past and probably alludes to the baptism of Jesus Christ when the Holy Spirit came upon him.* *See also* [ISA 42:1](https://ref.ly/Isa42:1), [ISA 61:1](https://ref.ly/Isa61:1)

The seal of the Holy Spirit in the life of believers
----------------------------------------------------

### The seal marks God's ownership

**[2CO 1:21–1:22](https://ref.ly/2Cor1:21-2Cor1:22)** *See also* [REV 7:3–7:4](https://ref.ly/Rev7:3-Rev7:4), [REV 9:4](https://ref.ly/Rev9:4), [REV 14:1](https://ref.ly/Rev14:1), [REV 22:4](https://ref.ly/Rev22:4)

### The seal acts as a guarantee of redemption

**[EPH 1:13–1:14](https://ref.ly/Eph1:13-Eph1:14)** *See also* [2CO 5:5](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:5); [EPH 4:30](https://ref.ly/Eph4:30); [1PE 1:5](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:5)
