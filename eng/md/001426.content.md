## 5910: motives, examples of (id: 5910)

Scripture reflects the wide range of motives which influence human behaviour.

Right actions can result from wrong motives
-------------------------------------------

**[PHP 1:15–1:18](https://ref.ly/Phil1:15-Phil1:18)** *See also* [1SA 18:20–18:23](https://ref.ly/1Sam18:20-1Sam18:23)

Right motives do not always produce right actions
-------------------------------------------------

**[1KI 8:17–8:19](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:17-1Kgs8:19)** parallel [2CH 6:7–6:9](https://ref.ly/2Chr6:7-2Chr6:9) *See also* [MAT 1:18–1:20](https://ref.ly/Matt1:18-Matt1:20)

-------------------------

### Malice and envy
