## 8160: seeking God (id: 8160)

God's desire is that all people should seek after him and find him. Those who seek God with all their heart are rewarded, but those who fail to seek him do so to their eternal loss.

God's heart is for people to seek him
-------------------------------------

### God wants people to seek him

**[ACT 17:27](https://ref.ly/Acts17:27)** *See also* [1CH 16:11](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:11), [1CH 22:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr22:19); [PSA 14:2](https://ref.ly/Ps14:2); [ACT 15:16–15:17](https://ref.ly/Acts15:16-Acts15:17); [AMO 9:11–9:12](https://ref.ly/Amos9:11-Amos9:12); [HEB 11:6](https://ref.ly/Heb11:6)

### God calls people to seek him

**[ISA 55:6–55:7](https://ref.ly/Isa55:6-Isa55:7)** *See also* [HOS 10:12](https://ref.ly/Hos10:12); [AMO 5:4–5:6](https://ref.ly/Amos5:4-Amos5:6)

### God promises that people will seek him

**[JER 50:4](https://ref.ly/Jer50:4)** *See also* [HOS 3:4–3:5](https://ref.ly/Hos3:4-Hos3:5), [HOS 5:13–5:15](https://ref.ly/Hos5:13-Hos5:15); [ZEP 2:3](https://ref.ly/Zeph2:3); [ZEC 8:20–8:23](https://ref.ly/Zech8:20-Zech8:23)

### God promises to be found by those who seek him

**[2CO 6:2](https://ref.ly/2Cor6:2)** *See also* [LUK 16:19–16:31](https://ref.ly/Luke16:19-Luke16:31)
