## 7944: ministry, qualifications for (id: 7944)

God, who calls his people to minister, also equips his people. The chief qualifications are a response to God's call, faithfulness, godliness and Christlikeness.

God calls people to minister
----------------------------

### Qualification is by call, not gifting or achievement

**[DEU 7:7–7:8](https://ref.ly/Deut7:7-Deut7:8)** *See also* [DEU 9:4–9:5](https://ref.ly/Deut9:4-Deut9:5)

### God calls those who the world regards as weak or foolish

[1CO 1:27–1:29](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:27-1Cor1:29)

### Feelings of inadequacy to God's call are common

[EXO 3:11](https://ref.ly/Exod3:11) *Moses*; ; [JDG 6:15](https://ref.ly/Judg6:15) *Gideon*; ; [1SA 9:21](https://ref.ly/1Sam9:21) *Saul*; ; [1SA 18:18](https://ref.ly/1Sam18:18) *David*; ; [1KI 3:7](https://ref.ly/1Kgs3:7) *Solomon*; ; [ISA 6:5](https://ref.ly/Isa6:5) *Isaiah*; ; [JER 1:6](https://ref.ly/Jer1:6) *Jeremiah*

Responding to God's call to minister
------------------------------------

### Readiness and availability

[1SA 3:10](https://ref.ly/1Sam3:10); ; [ISA 6:8](https://ref.ly/Isa6:8)

### Faith, rather than natural talent or moral perfection, is required

**[HEB 11:1–11:2](https://ref.ly/Heb11:1-Heb11:2)** *See also* [GEN 27:19–27:24](https://ref.ly/Gen27:19-Gen27:24) *Jacob was a deceiver*; [NUM 27:12–27:14](https://ref.ly/Num27:12-Num27:14) *Moses and Aaron disobeyed God.*[2SA 11:4](https://ref.ly/2Sam11:4), [2SA 11:14–11:15](https://ref.ly/2Sam11:14-2Sam11:15)[1KI 11:9–11:13](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:9-1Kgs11:13) *Solomon disobeyed God's command.*

NT ministers are recognised by call rather than their achievement
-----------------------------------------------------------------

### The Twelve

[MAT 10:1–10:4](https://ref.ly/Matt10:1-Matt10:4) parallels [MRK 3:14–3:19](https://ref.ly/Mark3:14-Mark3:19), [LUK 6:12–6:16](https://ref.ly/Luke6:12-Luke6:16) *The Twelve, including Peter and Judas, failed Jesus Christ at critical times.*

### Paul

[ACT 9:15](https://ref.ly/Acts9:15), [ACT 26:6](https://ref.ly/Acts26:6); ; [2CO 4:7–4:12](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:7-2Cor4:12), [2CO 12:7](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:7) *Though greatly gifted, Paul was kept humble by his sense of unworthiness, difficulties and disappointments and his “thorn in the flesh”*; ; [1TI 1:16](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:16)

### Serving the church

[ROM 15:31](https://ref.ly/Rom15:31), [ROM 16:1](https://ref.ly/Rom16:1); ; [1CO 16:15](https://ref.ly/1Cor16:15); ; [2CO 9:1](https://ref.ly/2Cor9:1); ; [EPH 6:21](https://ref.ly/Eph6:21); ; [COL 1:7](https://ref.ly/Col1:7), [COL 1:25](https://ref.ly/Col1:25)

Ministry is described in terms of its source, content or nature
---------------------------------------------------------------

### Its source

It is of the Spirit: [2CO 3:6](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:6), [2CO 3:8](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:8); [2CO 4:1](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:1) *It is from God.*

### Its content

[ACT 6:2–6:4](https://ref.ly/Acts6:2-Acts6:4) *the word of God*; ; [2CO 5:18](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:18) *reconciliation*

### Its nature

Apostolic: [ACT 1:25](https://ref.ly/Acts1:25); ; [GAL 2:8](https://ref.ly/Gal2:8); [ROM 15:16](https://ref.ly/Rom15:16) *priestly*
