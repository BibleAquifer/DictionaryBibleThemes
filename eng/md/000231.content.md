## 2535: Jesus Christ, family of (id: 2535)

Jesus Christ belonged to a human family and experienced family relationships during his earthly ministry. Such relationships underline the fact of Christ's humanity.

Jesus Christ was born into a family
-----------------------------------

### He is placed in a family tree

[MAT 1:1–1:17](https://ref.ly/Matt1:1-Matt1:17); ; [LUK 1:34–1:37](https://ref.ly/Luke1:34-Luke1:37), [LUK 3:23–3:38](https://ref.ly/Luke3:23-Luke3:38)

### His birth was a natural one

**[LUK 2:5–2:7](https://ref.ly/Luke2:5-Luke2:7)** *Jesus Christ's conception was miraculous, his birth natural.* *See also* [LUK 2:21–2:22](https://ref.ly/Luke2:21-Luke2:22) *see [Lev 12:2–4](https://ref.ly/Lev12:2-Lev12:4) for the instructions concerning purification after childbirth*

### He grew up within a family

**[LUK 2:48](https://ref.ly/Luke2:48)** *See also* [LUK 2:51–2:52](https://ref.ly/Luke2:51-Luke2:52)

Jesus Christ's family is mentioned during his earthly ministry
--------------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 13:55–13:56](https://ref.ly/Matt13:55-Matt13:56)** parallel [MRK 6:3](https://ref.ly/Mark6:3) *See also* [MAT 12:46–12:47](https://ref.ly/Matt12:46-Matt12:47) parallels [MRK 3:31–3:32](https://ref.ly/Mark3:31-Mark3:32), [LUK 8:19–8:20](https://ref.ly/Luke8:19-Luke8:20); [JHN 2:1](https://ref.ly/John2:1), [JHN 2:12](https://ref.ly/John2:12)

Jesus Christ's natural brothers
-------------------------------

### James

**[GAL 1:19](https://ref.ly/Gal1:19)** *This verse gives support to the traditional view that James, elder at Jerusalem and writer of the NT letter which bears his name was the Lord's brother. (Other references to him include [Ac 15:13](https://ref.ly/Acts15:13); [21:18](https://ref.ly/Acts21:18); [1Co 15:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:7); [Jas 1:1](https://ref.ly/Jas1:1).).* *See also* [MAT 13:55](https://ref.ly/Matt13:55) parallel [MRK 6:3](https://ref.ly/Mark6:3)

### Jude

**[JUD 1:1](https://ref.ly/Jude1:1)** *See also* [MAT 13:55](https://ref.ly/Matt13:55) parallel [MRK 6:3](https://ref.ly/Mark6:3) *Jude is understood to be a variation of Judas.*

The nature of Jesus Christ's relationships with his family
----------------------------------------------------------

### He was obedient to and caring towards his parents

**[LUK 2:51](https://ref.ly/Luke2:51)** *See also* [JHN 19:26–19:27](https://ref.ly/John19:26-John19:27)

### The will of his heavenly Father took precedence over the wishes of his natural family

**[JHN 2:3–2:4](https://ref.ly/John2:3-John2:4)** *In John's Gospel, Jesus Christ's “time” (or “hour”) is his cross. Here Christ insists that only his heavenly Father can dictate the course of his ministry.* *See also* [MAT 12:48–12:50](https://ref.ly/Matt12:48-Matt12:50) parallel [MRK 3:33–3:35](https://ref.ly/Mark3:33-Mark3:35); [LUK 2:48–2:49](https://ref.ly/Luke2:48-Luke2:49); [JHN 7:6–7:8](https://ref.ly/John7:6-John7:8)

### His family did not fully understand his ministry

**[JHN 7:3–7:5](https://ref.ly/John7:3-John7:5)** *See also* [MRK 3:21](https://ref.ly/Mark3:21)

### Jesus Christ's natural family were among the first Christians

**[ACT 1:14](https://ref.ly/Acts1:14)** *See also* [1CO 9:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:5)
