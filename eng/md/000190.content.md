## 2330: Jesus Christ, as shepherd (id: 2330)

A title for Jesus Christ indicating believers' dependence on him as he protects, tends and guides those whom he knows intimately.

Prophecies about the shepherd Messiah
-------------------------------------

**[EZK 34:23](https://ref.ly/Ezek34:23)** *See also* [EZK 37:24](https://ref.ly/Ezek37:24); [MIC 5:2](https://ref.ly/Mic5:2); [MAT 2:6](https://ref.ly/Matt2:6); [MIC 5:4](https://ref.ly/Mic5:4); [ZEC 13:7](https://ref.ly/Zech13:7); [MAT 26:31](https://ref.ly/Matt26:31) parallel [MRK 14:27](https://ref.ly/Mark14:27)
