## 1325: God, the Creator (id: 1325)

God's power is revealed in the initial creation and the continuing sustenance of the universe. Humanity represents the climax of the Creator's purposes.

God is the Creator
------------------

**[NEH 9:6](https://ref.ly/Neh9:6)** *See also* [GEN 1:1](https://ref.ly/Gen1:1); [ISA 45:7](https://ref.ly/Isa45:7), [ISA 66:2](https://ref.ly/Isa66:2); [EPH 3:9](https://ref.ly/Eph3:9); [REV 4:11](https://ref.ly/Rev4:11)

### God's creative activity demonstrates his uniqueness

**[PSA 96:5](https://ref.ly/Ps96:5)** *See also* [ISA 37:16](https://ref.ly/Isa37:16); [JER 10:11](https://ref.ly/Jer10:11)

### God's creative activity implies that he is good

**[1TI 4:4](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:4)** *See also* [GEN 1:4](https://ref.ly/Gen1:4), [GEN 1:31](https://ref.ly/Gen1:31)

God creates all things
----------------------

### He creates the physical world

**[ISA 45:18](https://ref.ly/Isa45:18)** *See also* [GEN 1:1–1:25](https://ref.ly/Gen1:1-Gen1:25)

### He creates the spiritual world

**[PSA 148:2–148:5](https://ref.ly/Ps148:2-Ps148:5)** *See also* [ROM 8:38–8:39](https://ref.ly/Rom8:38-Rom8:39); [COL 1:16](https://ref.ly/Col1:16)

### The human race is the apex of God's creation

**[PSA 8:3–8:8](https://ref.ly/Ps8:3-Ps8:8)** *See also* [GEN 1:26–1:28](https://ref.ly/Gen1:26-Gen1:28), [GEN 5:1](https://ref.ly/Gen5:1), [GEN 9:6](https://ref.ly/Gen9:6); [PSA 100:3](https://ref.ly/Ps100:3), [PSA 139:13](https://ref.ly/Ps139:13); [ISA 42:5](https://ref.ly/Isa42:5); [ACT 17:26–17:28](https://ref.ly/Acts17:26-Acts17:28)

The Creator's agents
--------------------

### He creates through Jesus Christ

**[1CO 8:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:6)** *See also* [JHN 1:3](https://ref.ly/John1:3); [COL 1:16](https://ref.ly/Col1:16); [HEB 1:2](https://ref.ly/Heb1:2)

### He creates by the Spirit

**[JOB 33:4](https://ref.ly/Job33:4)** *See also* [GEN 1:2](https://ref.ly/Gen1:2); [PSA 104:30](https://ref.ly/Ps104:30)

### He creates by his word

**[PSA 33:6](https://ref.ly/Ps33:6)** *See also* [JOB 38:8–38:11](https://ref.ly/Job38:8-Job38:11); [PSA 33:9](https://ref.ly/Ps33:9)

### He creates by wisdom

**[PRO 3:19](https://ref.ly/Prov3:19)** *Wisdom is personified and is active with the Creator.* *See also* [PRO 8:30](https://ref.ly/Prov8:30); [JER 10:12–10:13](https://ref.ly/Jer10:12-Jer10:13)

He creates from nothing
-----------------------

**[HEB 11:3](https://ref.ly/Heb11:3)** *God creates out of non\-existent material, which emphasises the absolute dependence of all creation upon him.* *See also* [JOB 26:7](https://ref.ly/Job26:7); [ROM 4:17](https://ref.ly/Rom4:17)
