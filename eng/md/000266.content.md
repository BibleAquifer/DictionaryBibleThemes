## 3218: Holy Spirit, and praise (id: 3218)

The Holy Spirit inspires believers to extol, worship and thank God. His aim is to glorify the Father and the Son.

The promise that the Holy Spirit will bring praise
--------------------------------------------------

**[ISA 61:1–61:3](https://ref.ly/Isa61:1-Isa61:3)**

The Holy Spirit inspires praise
-------------------------------

### True praise must be inspired by the Spirit

**[JHN 4:23–4:24](https://ref.ly/John4:23-John4:24)** *See also* [PHP 3:3](https://ref.ly/Phil3:3)

### The Spirit prompts praise

**[2SA 23:1–23:2](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:1-2Sam23:2)** [LUK 1:67–1:68](https://ref.ly/Luke1:67-Luke1:68), [LUK 2:27–2:28](https://ref.ly/Luke2:27-Luke2:28); [LUK 10:21](https://ref.ly/Luke10:21) *The Spirit prompts Jesus Christ to praise the Father.*
