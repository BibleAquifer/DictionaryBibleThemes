## 5899: lament (id: 5899)

A song of mourning or sorrow. Laments may be occasioned by bereavement, personal trouble, national disaster or the judgment of God.

-------------------------------------

**[2SA 3:33–3:34](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:33-2Sam3:34)** *See also* [GEN 50:10](https://ref.ly/Gen50:10); [JER 31:15](https://ref.ly/Jer31:15); [MAT 2:16–2:18](https://ref.ly/Matt2:16-Matt2:18), [MAT 9:23](https://ref.ly/Matt9:23) parallels [MRK 5:38](https://ref.ly/Mark5:38), [LUK 8:52](https://ref.ly/Luke8:52); [LUK 23:27](https://ref.ly/Luke23:27); [JHN 11:31–11:33](https://ref.ly/John11:31-John11:33)
