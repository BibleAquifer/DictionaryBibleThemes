## 5396: lordship, of Jesus Christ (id: 5396)

Jesus Christ has authority and dominion over the church and the world on account of his divine status, which was publicly demonstrated through his resurrection from the dead and exaltation to the right hand of God.

Lord as a title for Jesus Christ
--------------------------------

**[ROM 10:9](https://ref.ly/Rom10:9)** *In view of the OT background to the word “LORD” it is clear that this confession was not just of Jesus Christ's authority but also of his divinity*; **[2CO 4:5](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:5)** *See also* [MRK 2:28](https://ref.ly/Mark2:28) parallels [MAT 12:8](https://ref.ly/Matt12:8), [LUK 6:5](https://ref.ly/Luke6:5); [JHN 13:13](https://ref.ly/John13:13), [JHN 20:28](https://ref.ly/John20:28); [ACT 9:17](https://ref.ly/Acts9:17); [1CO 8:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:6), [1CO 12:3](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:3); [EPH 4:5](https://ref.ly/Eph4:5)
