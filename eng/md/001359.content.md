## 5838: disrespect (id: 5838)

The attitude of heart that leads people to treat God and others in ways that demonstrate a lack of esteem.

Causes of disrespect for God and Jesus Christ
---------------------------------------------

### Disrespect caused by a wrong view of God and Jesus Christ

**[ROM 1:21–1:23](https://ref.ly/Rom1:21-Rom1:23)** *See also* [LUK 23:8–23:11](https://ref.ly/Luke23:8-Luke23:11)

### Disrespect caused by disobedience to God

**[NEH 9:26](https://ref.ly/Neh9:26)** *See also* [2CH 24:17–24:20](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:17-2Chr24:20); [2TI 3:1–3:5](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:1-2Tim3:5)

### Disrespect caused by Satan

**[ACT 5:3](https://ref.ly/Acts5:3)** *See also* [MAT 8:28–8:29](https://ref.ly/Matt8:28-Matt8:29) parallels [MRK 5:1–5:7](https://ref.ly/Mark5:1-Mark5:7), [LUK 8:26–8:28](https://ref.ly/Luke8:26-Luke8:28)

Causes of disrespect for others
-------------------------------

### Disrespect for others caused by a wrong view of people

**[MAT 19:13–19:14](https://ref.ly/Matt19:13-Matt19:14)** parallels [MRK 10:13–10:14](https://ref.ly/Mark10:13-Mark10:14), [LUK 18:15–18:16](https://ref.ly/Luke18:15-Luke18:16) *The lack of respect for the children arose from the erroneous belief that they were not important enough for Jesus Christ to bother with.* *See also* [LUK 10:25–10:37](https://ref.ly/Luke10:25-Luke10:37) *Those who passed by failed to heed God's command to love their neighbours as themselves*; [JAS 2:1–2:4](https://ref.ly/Jas2:1-Jas2:4)

### Disrespect for others caused by seeking power and wealth

**[ECC 4:1](https://ref.ly/Eccl4:1)** *See also* [JAS 5:1–5:6](https://ref.ly/Jas5:1-Jas5:6)

### Disrespect for others caused by envy

**[MIC 2:1–2:2](https://ref.ly/Mic2:1-Mic2:2)** *See also* [GEN 37:3–37:4](https://ref.ly/Gen37:3-Gen37:4)

### Disrespect for others caused by pride and contempt

**[PSA 31:18](https://ref.ly/Ps31:18)** *See also* [MAT 27:27–27:31](https://ref.ly/Matt27:27-Matt27:31) parallel [MRK 15:16–15:20](https://ref.ly/Mark15:16-Mark15:20)

Disrespect shown in speech
--------------------------

**[JAS 3:9](https://ref.ly/Jas3:9)** *See also* [MAT 27:39–27:44](https://ref.ly/Matt27:39-Matt27:44) parallels [MRK 15:29–15:32](https://ref.ly/Mark15:29-Mark15:32), [LUK 23:35–23:39](https://ref.ly/Luke23:35-Luke23:39); [1TI 6:3–6:5](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:3-1Tim6:5)

The development of disrespect
-----------------------------

### Disrespect can grow insidiously

**[1KI 11:4](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:4)** *See also* [JER 2:1–2:11](https://ref.ly/Jer2:1-Jer2:11)MRK [11:15–11:17](https://ref.ly/Jer11:15-Jer11:17) parallels [MAT 21:12–21:13](https://ref.ly/Matt21:12-Matt21:13), [LUK 19:45–19:46](https://ref.ly/Luke19:45-Luke19:46), [JHN 2:13–2:17](https://ref.ly/John2:13-John2:17)

### Disrespect can spread infectiously

**[DEU 1:27–1:28](https://ref.ly/Deut1:27-Deut1:28)** *See also* [EST 1:16–1:18](https://ref.ly/Esth1:16-Esth1:18); [MRK 15:9–15:15](https://ref.ly/Mark15:9-Mark15:15) parallels [MAT 27:17–27:26](https://ref.ly/Matt27:17-Matt27:26), [LUK 23:13–23:25](https://ref.ly/Luke23:13-Luke23:25); [ROM 2:24](https://ref.ly/Rom2:24)

### Disrespect can end fatally
