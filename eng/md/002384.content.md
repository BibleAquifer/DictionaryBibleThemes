## 8755: folly (id: 8755)

A human attitude of lack of wisdom or contempt for wisdom, which shows itself in a lack of understanding and obedience. Scripture contrasts human foolishness with divine wisdom, and indicates the serious consequences of a rejection of the wisdom of God.
