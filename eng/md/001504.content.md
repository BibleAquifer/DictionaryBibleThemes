## 6030: sin, avoidance of (id: 6030)

God calls his people to avoid sin, and through Jesus Christ gives them the inner power to be victorious over it.

God's people are to resist sin
------------------------------

**[1PE 2:11](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:11)** *See also* [PSA 97:10](https://ref.ly/Ps97:10); [PRO 4:23–4:27](https://ref.ly/Prov4:23-Prov4:27); [1CO 15:34](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:34); [EPH 4:25–5:20](https://ref.ly/Eph4:25-Eph5:20); [JAS 1:21](https://ref.ly/Jas1:21)
