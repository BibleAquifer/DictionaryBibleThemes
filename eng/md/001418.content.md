## 5900: laughter (id: 5900)

The act or sound of laughing, often associated with joy and delight, yet occasionally linked with ridicule and scorn.

People laughing
---------------

### Out of pleasure

**[GEN 21:6](https://ref.ly/Gen21:6)** *See also* [PRO 31:25](https://ref.ly/Prov31:25); [ECC 3:4](https://ref.ly/Eccl3:4), [ECC 10:19](https://ref.ly/Eccl10:19)

### In scorn and derision

**[MAT 9:24](https://ref.ly/Matt9:24)** parallels [MRK 5:40](https://ref.ly/Mark5:40), [LUK 8:53](https://ref.ly/Luke8:53) *See also* [JOB 39:18](https://ref.ly/Job39:18), [JOB 41:29](https://ref.ly/Job41:29); [PSA 52:6](https://ref.ly/Ps52:6); [LAM 1:7](https://ref.ly/Lam1:7); [HAB 1:10](https://ref.ly/Hab1:10)

**[PSA 2:4](https://ref.ly/Ps2:4)** *See also* [PSA 37:13](https://ref.ly/Ps37:13), [PSA 59:8](https://ref.ly/Ps59:8); [PRO 1:26](https://ref.ly/Prov1:26)

**[PSA 126:2](https://ref.ly/Ps126:2)**; **[LUK 6:21](https://ref.ly/Luke6:21)** *See also* [JOB 5:22](https://ref.ly/Job5:22), [JOB 8:21](https://ref.ly/Job8:21); [JER 51:39](https://ref.ly/Jer51:39) *as a prelude to judgment*
