## 6026: sin, God's judgment on (id: 6026)

Sin comes under the judgment of God, in that it contradicts his nature and opposes his purposes.

God's judgment on sin
---------------------

### God is the judge of sin

**[ISA 26:21](https://ref.ly/Isa26:21)** *See also* [PSA 99:8](https://ref.ly/Ps99:8); [ISA 13:11](https://ref.ly/Isa13:11); [AMO 3:14](https://ref.ly/Amos3:14); [ZEP 1:12](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:12); [1TH 4:6](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:6)

### God's judgment is certain

**[ROM 2:12](https://ref.ly/Rom2:12)** *See also* [PSA 37:38](https://ref.ly/Ps37:38); [PRO 11:21](https://ref.ly/Prov11:21); [HEB 9:27](https://ref.ly/Heb9:27)

### God's judgment is in proportion to the seriousness of the sin

**[JER 21:14](https://ref.ly/Jer21:14)** *See also* [DEU 25:2](https://ref.ly/Deut25:2); [ISA 59:18](https://ref.ly/Isa59:18); [MAT 7:1–7:5](https://ref.ly/Matt7:1-Matt7:5); [LUK 12:47–12:48](https://ref.ly/Luke12:47-Luke12:48); [ROM 2:5–2:6](https://ref.ly/Rom2:5-Rom2:6)

### God's judgment under the Sinaitic covenant

**[EXO 20:5](https://ref.ly/Exod20:5)** parallel [DEU 5:9](https://ref.ly/Deut5:9) *The Sinaitic covenant makes references to the judgment of future generations for the sins of their parents but, as a principle, this is abolished by later prophets.* [LEV 26:14–26:39](https://ref.ly/Lev26:14-Lev26:39); ; [DEU 28:15–28:68](https://ref.ly/Deut28:15-Deut28:68); [JER 31:29–31:30](https://ref.ly/Jer31:29-Jer31:30); ; [EZK 18:1–18:32](https://ref.ly/Ezek18:1-Ezek18:32)

### John the Baptist and Jesus Christ warn of judgment

**[MAT 3:10](https://ref.ly/Matt3:10)** parallel [LUK 3:9](https://ref.ly/Luke3:9) *See also* [MAT 3:12](https://ref.ly/Matt3:12), [MAT 7:19](https://ref.ly/Matt7:19), [MAT 12:36–12:37](https://ref.ly/Matt12:36-Matt12:37); [MRK 9:42–9:49](https://ref.ly/Mark9:42-Mark9:49)
