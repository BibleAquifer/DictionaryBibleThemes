## 6634: deliverance (id: 6634)

God frequently rescues people from danger, suffering and sin, often through human agency.

The source of deliverance
-------------------------

### Deliverance comes from God

**[PSA 3:8](https://ref.ly/Ps3:8)** *See also* [EXO 3:7–3:8](https://ref.ly/Exod3:7-Exod3:8); [PSA 18:2](https://ref.ly/Ps18:2); [DAN 3:16–3:17](https://ref.ly/Dan3:16-Dan3:17); [JON 2:9](https://ref.ly/Jonah2:9)

### There is no deliverance by other gods

[2KI 18:33](https://ref.ly/2Kgs18:33) parallel [ISA 36:18](https://ref.ly/Isa36:18)
