## 5623: witnesses, legal (id: 5623)

God gave detailed instructions about the conduct of witnesses in the legal processes of Israel and also in the affairs of the church.

God himself as a witness
------------------------

[GEN 31:50](https://ref.ly/Gen31:50); ; [MAL 2:14](https://ref.ly/Mal2:14)

General legal requirements about witnesses
------------------------------------------

### A witness to a crime was required to give evidence

**[LEV 5:1](https://ref.ly/Lev5:1)** *See also* [PRO 29:24](https://ref.ly/Prov29:24)

### Two or more witnesses were required to prove someone guilty

**[DEU 19:15](https://ref.ly/Deut19:15)** *See also* [DEU 17:6](https://ref.ly/Deut17:6); [MAT 18:15–18:16](https://ref.ly/Matt18:15-Matt18:16); [2CO 13:1](https://ref.ly/2Cor13:1)

The role of witnesses in capital cases
--------------------------------------

### At least two witnesses were required

**[NUM 35:30](https://ref.ly/Num35:30)** *See also* [DEU 17:2–17:6](https://ref.ly/Deut17:2-Deut17:6); [HEB 10:28](https://ref.ly/Heb10:28)

### Witnesses were required to participate in the execution

**[DEU 17:7](https://ref.ly/Deut17:7)** *See also* [LEV 24:13–24:16](https://ref.ly/Lev24:13-Lev24:16); [DEU 13:6–13:11](https://ref.ly/Deut13:6-Deut13:11); [ACT 7:57–7:58](https://ref.ly/Acts7:57-Acts7:58)
