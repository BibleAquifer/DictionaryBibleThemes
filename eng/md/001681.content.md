## 6730: reinstatement (id: 6730)

Restoration to a previous state or position. It is demonstrated in human relationships and also by God in his love and grace restoring fallen humanity and lifting up his people to their former place within his service.

--------------------------

### God does not remember sin

*See also* [PSA 103:12](https://ref.ly/Ps103:12); [ISA 38:17](https://ref.ly/Isa38:17), [ISA 43:25](https://ref.ly/Isa43:25); [EZK 20:44](https://ref.ly/Ezek20:44)

[PSA 89:28](https://ref.ly/Ps89:28); ; [LAM 3:22](https://ref.ly/Lam3:22); ; [HOS 11:8](https://ref.ly/Hos11:8)
