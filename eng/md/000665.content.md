## 5061: sanctity of life (id: 5061)

Scripture treats human life as a divine gift and something for which humans are responsible. It is thus to be valued and respected. No human being has the right to take the life of another.

The sanctity of all human life
------------------------------

### It proceeds from God

**[GEN 2:7](https://ref.ly/Gen2:7)**; **[ACT 17:25](https://ref.ly/Acts17:25)**

### It is precious to God

**[GEN 1:27](https://ref.ly/Gen1:27)** *See also* [GEN 4:10–4:11](https://ref.ly/Gen4:10-Gen4:11); [2SA 18:12–18:13](https://ref.ly/2Sam18:12-2Sam18:13); [MAT 6:25–6:26](https://ref.ly/Matt6:25-Matt6:26)

### It is protected by God

**[DEU 22:8](https://ref.ly/Deut22:8)** *See also* [GEN 4:15](https://ref.ly/Gen4:15); [NUM 35:11–35:12](https://ref.ly/Num35:11-Num35:12); [JER 22:3](https://ref.ly/Jer22:3)

### Death is by God's appointment

**[HEB 9:27](https://ref.ly/Heb9:27)** *See also* [JOB 14:5](https://ref.ly/Job14:5); [PSA 90:3](https://ref.ly/Ps90:3), [PSA 90:5](https://ref.ly/Ps90:5)

### God prohibits unlawful taking of life

**[EXO 20:13](https://ref.ly/Exod20:13)** parallel [DEU 5:17](https://ref.ly/Deut5:17) *See also* [MAT 19:18](https://ref.ly/Matt19:18); [ROM 13:9](https://ref.ly/Rom13:9) *So precious is human life that some situations which required the death penalty in the OT are dealt with differently in the NT (e.g., [Jn 8:5,11](https://ref.ly/John8:5,John8:11)).*

### God punishes unlawful taking of life

**[GEN 9:6](https://ref.ly/Gen9:6)** *See also* [EXO 21:14](https://ref.ly/Exod21:14); [2SA 12:9](https://ref.ly/2Sam12:9); [2KI 21:16](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:16)
