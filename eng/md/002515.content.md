## 9430: paradise (id: 9430)

The destination of the righteous after death
--------------------------------------------

**[LUK 23:43](https://ref.ly/Luke23:43)** *This Persian term originally described a park or pleasure garden. It was used by the Jews of the Garden of Eden, and of the future blessedness of the saints.* *See also* [2CO 12:3–12:4](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:3-2Cor12:4); [REV 2:7](https://ref.ly/Rev2:7)

* **Associated Passages:** Luke 23:43; 2 Corinthians 12:3-4; Revelation 2:7
