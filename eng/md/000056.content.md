## 1260: finger of God (id: 1260)

A figure of speech which gives expression to God's creative power and authority over his creation.

God creates with his finger
---------------------------

**[PSA 8:3](https://ref.ly/Ps8:3)** *See also* [ISA 48:13](https://ref.ly/Isa48:13), [ISA 64:8](https://ref.ly/Isa64:8)

God writes with his finger
--------------------------

### He writes the law

**[EXO 31:18](https://ref.ly/Exod31:18)** *See also* [EXO 24:12](https://ref.ly/Exod24:12), [EXO 32:16](https://ref.ly/Exod32:16), [EXO 34:1](https://ref.ly/Exod34:1)

### He writes judgment

**[DAN 5:5](https://ref.ly/Dan5:5)** *See also* [DAN 5:24–5:28](https://ref.ly/Dan5:24-Dan5:28)
