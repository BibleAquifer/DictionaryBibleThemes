## 1403: God, revelation of (id: 1403)

God graciously makes himself known to humanity since people cannot discover him on their own. He achieves this in many ways, but supremely through his Son Jesus Christ.

The necessity of revelation
---------------------------

**[ISA 59:2](https://ref.ly/Isa59:2)** *See also* [DEU 31:18](https://ref.ly/Deut31:18); [ISA 1:15](https://ref.ly/Isa1:15), [ISA 45:15](https://ref.ly/Isa45:15), [ISA 64:7](https://ref.ly/Isa64:7); [EZK 39:23](https://ref.ly/Ezek39:23); [MIC 3:4](https://ref.ly/Mic3:4)
