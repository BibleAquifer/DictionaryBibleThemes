## 3275: Holy Spirit, in the church (id: 3275)

The church depends upon the activity of the Holy Spirit, without which its effective and faithful service is impossible.

The Holy Spirit forms the church
--------------------------------

**[1CO 12:13](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:13)** *See also* [ACT 2:1–2:4](https://ref.ly/Acts2:1-Acts2:4), [ACT 2:16–2:18](https://ref.ly/Acts2:16-Acts2:18); [JOL 2:28–2:29](https://ref.ly/Joel2:28-Joel2:29); [ACT 10:44–10:48](https://ref.ly/Acts10:44-Acts10:48); [EPH 2:21–2:22](https://ref.ly/Eph2:21-Eph2:22)

The Holy Spirit indwells the church
-----------------------------------

**[1CO 3:16](https://ref.ly/1Cor3:16)** *“you” here is plural and refers to the whole church.* *See also* [EZK 10:4](https://ref.ly/Ezek10:4); [JHN 16:14](https://ref.ly/John16:14); [1CO 6:19](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:19); [2CO 6:16](https://ref.ly/2Cor6:16); [1PE 4:14](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:14)
