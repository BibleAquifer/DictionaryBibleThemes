## 3230: Holy Spirit, and regeneration (id: 3230)

God's Spirit works to bring the gift of new birth and renewal to those who have been called to faith.

The Holy Spirit's work is promised
----------------------------------

**[EZK 36:26–36:27](https://ref.ly/Ezek36:26-Ezek36:27)** *See also* [EZK 37:14](https://ref.ly/Ezek37:14)

Jesus Christ proclaims the Holy Spirit's work
---------------------------------------------

**[JHN 3:5–3:8](https://ref.ly/John3:5-John3:8)**

The Holy Spirit's work brings salvation
---------------------------------------

**[TIT 3:5–3:6](https://ref.ly/Titus3:5-Titus3:6)** *“washing” (apparently a reference to baptism) signifies regeneration.* *See also* [EPH 5:25–5:27](https://ref.ly/Eph5:25-Eph5:27)

Regeneration is a creative work of God's Spirit alone
-----------------------------------------------------

### It cannot be achieved by human means

**[JHN 6:63](https://ref.ly/John6:63)** *See also* [JHN 1:13](https://ref.ly/John1:13), [JHN 3:6](https://ref.ly/John3:6)

### It cannot be achieved through the works of the law

**[2CO 3:6](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:6)** *See also* [ROM 2:29](https://ref.ly/Rom2:29), [ROM 7:6](https://ref.ly/Rom7:6), [ROM 8:2](https://ref.ly/Rom8:2); [GAL 3:2](https://ref.ly/Gal3:2)
