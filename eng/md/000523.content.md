## 4687: snake (id: 4687)

A reptile, often associated with cunning and danger, or occasionally with satanic temptation.

Snakes as crawling reptiles
---------------------------

**[GEN 3:14](https://ref.ly/Gen3:14)** *See also* [ISA 65:25](https://ref.ly/Isa65:25); [MIC 7:17](https://ref.ly/Mic7:17)

Snakes are dangerous
--------------------

### Both to animals and human beings

**[GEN 49:17](https://ref.ly/Gen49:17)** *See also* [GEN 3:15](https://ref.ly/Gen3:15); [EXO 4:3](https://ref.ly/Exod4:3), [EXO 7:9–7:13](https://ref.ly/Exod7:9-Exod7:13); [PRO 23:32](https://ref.ly/Prov23:32); [ECC 10:8](https://ref.ly/Eccl10:8), [ECC 10:11](https://ref.ly/Eccl10:11); [ISA 30:6](https://ref.ly/Isa30:6); [ACT 28:3–28:6](https://ref.ly/Acts28:3-Acts28:6)

### Some are poisonous

**[ISA 14:29](https://ref.ly/Isa14:29)** *See also* [DEU 8:15](https://ref.ly/Deut8:15), [DEU 32:33](https://ref.ly/Deut32:33); [JOB 20:14](https://ref.ly/Job20:14), [JOB 20:16](https://ref.ly/Job20:16); [REV 9:19](https://ref.ly/Rev9:19)

### They were sometimes used to bring God's judgment

**[1CO 10:9](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:9)** *See also* [NUM 21:4–21:6](https://ref.ly/Num21:4-Num21:6); [AMO 5:19](https://ref.ly/Amos5:19), [AMO 9:3](https://ref.ly/Amos9:3)

### They may be overcome

**[PSA 91:13](https://ref.ly/Ps91:13)** *See also* [MRK 16:18](https://ref.ly/Mark16:18); [LUK 10:19](https://ref.ly/Luke10:19)

### One day they will be rendered harmless

**[ISA 11:8](https://ref.ly/Isa11:8)**
