## 8770: idolatry, in NT (id: 8770)

The NT world included many religions which promoted the worship of idols. Idolatry was thus of continuing importance to NT writers, especially Paul.

Idolatry in the Gentile world
-----------------------------

**[ACT 17:16](https://ref.ly/Acts17:16)** *See also* [ACT 14:11–14:13](https://ref.ly/Acts14:11-Acts14:13), [ACT 17:22–17:23](https://ref.ly/Acts17:22-Acts17:23), [ACT 19:24](https://ref.ly/Acts19:24); [1CO 8:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:5); [GAL 4:8](https://ref.ly/Gal4:8)

Criticism of idolatry
---------------------

### Idolatry leads to other sinful behaviour

**[ROM 1:24](https://ref.ly/Rom1:24)** *Verses 22 and 24–25 show the link between idolatry and immoral conduct.*

### Idolatry is an offence against the doctrine of creation

**[ROM 1:20](https://ref.ly/Rom1:20)** *See also* [ACT 17:24–17:29](https://ref.ly/Acts17:24-Acts17:29)

### Idols are futile and degrading

**[ROM 1:22–1:23](https://ref.ly/Rom1:22-Rom1:23)** *See also* [1CO 8:4](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:4), [1CO 10:19](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:19), [1CO 12:2](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:2)

### Idolatrous worship of human beings

**[ACT 12:22](https://ref.ly/Acts12:22)** *See also* [LUK 20:24–20:25](https://ref.ly/Luke20:24-Luke20:25) *Jesus Christ opposes the hypocrisy which objected to Roman coinage as idolatrous on the grounds that the emperor's head infringed the Ten Commandments*; [ACT 28:6](https://ref.ly/Acts28:6)

### Demonic powers are involved with idolatry

**[1CO 10:20](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:20)** *See also* [REV 9:20](https://ref.ly/Rev9:20), [REV 13:4](https://ref.ly/Rev13:4)

### Food sacrificed to idols

**[ACT 15:20](https://ref.ly/Acts15:20)** *See also* [ROM 14:2–14:3](https://ref.ly/Rom14:2-Rom14:3), [ROM 14:6](https://ref.ly/Rom14:6); [1CO 8:4–8:13](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:4-1Cor8:13), [1CO 10:14–10:31](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:14-1Cor10:31)

### Encounters with idolatrous practice

**[ACT 19:28](https://ref.ly/Acts19:28)** *See also* [ACT 14:11–14:18](https://ref.ly/Acts14:11-Acts14:18), [ACT 17:18–17:31](https://ref.ly/Acts17:18-Acts17:31)

Spiritual idolatry
------------------

**[1JN 5:21](https://ref.ly/1John5:21)** *“idols” here may well refer to a wide range of God\-substitutes.*

The temptations of Jesus Christ present three main kinds of spiritual idolatry
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Possessions

**[MAT 4:3](https://ref.ly/Matt4:3)** parallel [LUK 4:3](https://ref.ly/Luke4:3) *See also* [MAT 6:24](https://ref.ly/Matt6:24) parallel [LUK 16:13](https://ref.ly/Luke16:13); [LUK 18:23](https://ref.ly/Luke18:23); [PHP 3:19](https://ref.ly/Phil3:19); [COL 3:5](https://ref.ly/Col3:5); [1TI 6:10](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:10)

### Prestige and self\-esteem

**[MAT 4:6](https://ref.ly/Matt4:6)** parallel [LUK 4:9](https://ref.ly/Luke4:9) *See also* [LUK 3:8](https://ref.ly/Luke3:8), [LUK 10:29](https://ref.ly/Luke10:29), [LUK 18:11–18:12](https://ref.ly/Luke18:11-Luke18:12), [LUK 18:21](https://ref.ly/Luke18:21); [ROM 2:19](https://ref.ly/Rom2:19)

### Power

**[MAT 4:8–4:9](https://ref.ly/Matt4:8-Matt4:9)** parallel [LUK 4:6–4:7](https://ref.ly/Luke4:6-Luke4:7) *See also* [LUK 9:54–9:55](https://ref.ly/Luke9:54-Luke9:55), [LUK 23:39](https://ref.ly/Luke23:39); [JHN 18:10–18:11](https://ref.ly/John18:10-John18:11), [JHN 19:10](https://ref.ly/John19:10); [PHP 2:6](https://ref.ly/Phil2:6); [JAS 4:6](https://ref.ly/Jas4:6), [JAS 4:10](https://ref.ly/Jas4:10)
