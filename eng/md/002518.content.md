## 9511: hell, as a place of punishment (id: 9511)

The place of eternal punishment in fire and darkness intended for Satan and his angels, but also for human beings who choose to reject God.

Hell was originally created for the devil and his angels
--------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 25:41](https://ref.ly/Matt25:41)** *See also* [LUK 8:31](https://ref.ly/Luke8:31); [2PE 2:4](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:4); [REV 20:1–20:3](https://ref.ly/Rev20:1-Rev20:3), [REV 20:10](https://ref.ly/Rev20:10)

Hell is a consequence of rejecting God
--------------------------------------

**[MAT 13:40–13:42](https://ref.ly/Matt13:40-Matt13:42)** *See also* [DEU 32:22](https://ref.ly/Deut32:22) *Fire is often referred to in the OT as a symbol of divine judgment*; [MRK 9:42–9:48](https://ref.ly/Mark9:42-Mark9:48); [JHN 3:36](https://ref.ly/John3:36); [ROM 2:8](https://ref.ly/Rom2:8); [1TH 5:9](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:9); [HEB 10:26–10:29](https://ref.ly/Heb10:26-Heb10:29); [2PE 3:7](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:7); [REV 20:15](https://ref.ly/Rev20:15), [REV 21:8](https://ref.ly/Rev21:8)

Jesus Christ possesses authority over hell
------------------------------------------

**[MAT 16:18](https://ref.ly/Matt16:18)**; **[REV 1:18](https://ref.ly/Rev1:18)** *The Greek word “Hades” often simply means “death”. However, at points it implies the experience of punishment after death.* *See also* [JHN 5:27](https://ref.ly/John5:27); [ACT 10:42](https://ref.ly/Acts10:42), [ACT 17:31](https://ref.ly/Acts17:31)
