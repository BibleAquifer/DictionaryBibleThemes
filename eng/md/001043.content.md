## 5468: promises, human (id: 5468)

Believers must be faithful in keeping promises made to others and to God, because they have been called to live in integrity and truth. False or broken promises are considered as sin.

Examples of promises between people
-----------------------------------

[GEN 14:22–14:24](https://ref.ly/Gen14:22-Gen14:24) *Abraham and the king of Sodom*; ; [GEN 21:22–21:24](https://ref.ly/Gen21:22-Gen21:24) *Abraham and Abimelech*; ; [GEN 47:29–47:31](https://ref.ly/Gen47:29-Gen47:31) *Jacob and Joseph*; ; [NUM 30:1–30:16](https://ref.ly/Num30:1-Num30:16) *in family life*; ; [JOS 2:12–2:21](https://ref.ly/Josh2:12-Josh2:21) *Rahab and the spies*
