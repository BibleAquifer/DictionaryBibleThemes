## 7781: seer (id: 7781)

Seer as an alternative title for a prophet
------------------------------------------

**[1SA 9:9](https://ref.ly/1Sam9:9)** *Literally, a “seer” is “one who sees”, especially one who claims to see into the future or to have insight into the will of God.* *See also* [2SA 24:11](https://ref.ly/2Sam24:11) parallel [1CH 21:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr21:9)
