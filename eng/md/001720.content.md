## 7130: flock, God's (id: 7130)

God's people are frequently portrayed as a flock of sheep, for whom he cares with the tenderness and concern of a loving shepherd.

**[PSA 23:1](https://ref.ly/Ps23:1)** *See also* [PSA 80:1](https://ref.ly/Ps80:1)

**[JHN 10:11](https://ref.ly/John10:11)** *See also* [MAT 26:31](https://ref.ly/Matt26:31); [ZEC 13:7](https://ref.ly/Zech13:7)
