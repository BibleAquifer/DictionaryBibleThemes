## 5329: guarantee (id: 5329)

A pledge given that a promise will be fulfilled; a deposit or down payment making fulfilment obligatory. All God's promises are guaranteed in and through Jesus Christ.

Laws relating to guarantees
---------------------------

### Promises must be kept

**[NUM 30:2](https://ref.ly/Num30:2)** *See also* [PSA 15:4–15:5](https://ref.ly/Ps15:4-Ps15:5)

### The poor must not be oppressed

**[EXO 22:26–22:27](https://ref.ly/Exod22:26-Exod22:27)** *See also* [DEU 24:6](https://ref.ly/Deut24:6), [DEU 24:10–24:12](https://ref.ly/Deut24:10-Deut24:12), [DEU 24:17](https://ref.ly/Deut24:17)

### Special laws relating to women

[NUM 30:3–30:16](https://ref.ly/Num30:3-Num30:16)

### Guarantees could be written or spoken

[NUM 30:2](https://ref.ly/Num30:2); ; [EZR 10:19](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:19)
