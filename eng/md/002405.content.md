## 8779: materialism, nature of (id: 8779)

The attitude to life that places particular emphasis on immediate, physical values rather than on future, spiritual ones or that regards the material world as the only reality.

The basic features of materialism
---------------------------------

### An attitude of godlessness

**[PSA 14:1–14:2](https://ref.ly/Ps14:1-Ps14:2)** parallel [PSA 53:1–53:2](https://ref.ly/Ps53:1-Ps53:2) *See also* [JOB 35:9–35:10](https://ref.ly/Job35:9-Job35:10); [PSA 10:4](https://ref.ly/Ps10:4), [PSA 10:11](https://ref.ly/Ps10:11); [ECC 1:1–1:11](https://ref.ly/Eccl1:1-Eccl1:11); [LUK 12:13–12:19](https://ref.ly/Luke12:13-Luke12:19); [ROM 1:20–1:22](https://ref.ly/Rom1:20-Rom1:22)

### A denial of the resurrection

**[MAT 22:23](https://ref.ly/Matt22:23)** *See also* [ECC 4:1–4:3](https://ref.ly/Eccl4:1-Eccl4:3), [ECC 9:3–9:6](https://ref.ly/Eccl9:3-Eccl9:6); [ACT 4:1–4:2](https://ref.ly/Acts4:1-Acts4:2), [ACT 23:6–23:8](https://ref.ly/Acts23:6-Acts23:8); [1CO 15:12–15:17](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:12-1Cor15:17); [2TI 2:17–2:19](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:17-2Tim2:19)
