## 5166: liver and kidneys (id: 5166)

The liver and kidneys as vital internal organs of the body
----------------------------------------------------------

**[PRO 7:23](https://ref.ly/Prov7:23)** *See also* [JOB 16:13](https://ref.ly/Job16:13), [JOB 20:24–20:25](https://ref.ly/Job20:24-Job20:25)

The liver and kidneys are symbolic of human emotions and the conscience
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

**[PSA 73:21](https://ref.ly/Ps73:21)** *The Hebrew words for liver and kidneys are occasionally translated as “heart”, “spirit” or “inmost being”.* *See also* [JOB 19:27](https://ref.ly/Job19:27); [PRO 23:16](https://ref.ly/Prov23:16); [JER 12:2](https://ref.ly/Jer12:2); [LAM 2:11](https://ref.ly/Lam2:11), [LAM 3:13](https://ref.ly/Lam3:13)

The liver and kidneys as offered in animal sacrifice
----------------------------------------------------

### Liver and kidneys offered in various sacrifices

**[LEV 3:3–3:5](https://ref.ly/Lev3:3-Lev3:5)** *The liver and kidneys are associated with the fat as the choice parts of the animal.* *See also* [LEV 3:9–3:11](https://ref.ly/Lev3:9-Lev3:11), [LEV 3:14–3:16](https://ref.ly/Lev3:14-Lev3:16), [LEV 4:8–4:10](https://ref.ly/Lev4:8-Lev4:10), [LEV 7:3–7:6](https://ref.ly/Lev7:3-Lev7:6)

### Liver and kidneys had a central role in the ordination services for priests

[EXO 29:13](https://ref.ly/Exod29:13), [EXO 29:22–29:25](https://ref.ly/Exod29:22-Exod29:25); ; [LEV 8:16–8:17](https://ref.ly/Lev8:16-Lev8:17), [LEV 8:25–8:28](https://ref.ly/Lev8:25-Lev8:28), [LEV 9:8–9:10](https://ref.ly/Lev9:8-Lev9:10), [LEV 9:19–9:21](https://ref.ly/Lev9:19-Lev9:21)

Divination by inspection of the liver
-------------------------------------

[EZK 21:21](https://ref.ly/Ezek21:21) *Divination by inspection of the liver (hepatoscopy) was a common practice. The future was predicted from the colour and configuration of the liver of a sacrificial animal, usually a sheep.*
