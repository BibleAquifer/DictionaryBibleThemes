## 5146: covering (id: 5146)

That which literally or metaphorically hides from the human or divine eye what should not be seen.

Covering for the human body
---------------------------

**[GEN 3:7](https://ref.ly/Gen3:7)**; **[EZK 16:8](https://ref.ly/Ezek16:8)** *See also* [GEN 9:23](https://ref.ly/Gen9:23); [EXO 22:26–22:27](https://ref.ly/Exod22:26-Exod22:27), [EXO 28:42](https://ref.ly/Exod28:42); [ISA 47:3](https://ref.ly/Isa47:3), [ISA 57:8](https://ref.ly/Isa57:8); [EZK 16:10](https://ref.ly/Ezek16:10); [HOS 2:9](https://ref.ly/Hos2:9); [JON 3:6](https://ref.ly/Jonah3:6); [REV 3:18](https://ref.ly/Rev3:18)

Covering as a sign of submission or humility
--------------------------------------------

**[ISA 6:2](https://ref.ly/Isa6:2)** *See also* [GEN 24:65](https://ref.ly/Gen24:65); [1CO 11:5–11:15](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:5-1Cor11:15) *Some suggest that this passage refers either to a woman's modesty or to her dignity considering her new Christian status of equality with man.*

The covering of human sin through atonement
-------------------------------------------

**[PSA 32:1–32:2](https://ref.ly/Ps32:1-Ps32:2)** *See also* [GEN 20:16](https://ref.ly/Gen20:16); [LEV 4:20](https://ref.ly/Lev4:20) *The word translated “atonement” literally means “covering”*; [NEH 4:5](https://ref.ly/Neh4:5); [JOB 14:17](https://ref.ly/Job14:17); [PSA 32:5](https://ref.ly/Ps32:5), [PSA 85:2](https://ref.ly/Ps85:2); [PRO 17:9](https://ref.ly/Prov17:9); [ROM 4:7–4:8](https://ref.ly/Rom4:7-Rom4:8); [JAS 5:20](https://ref.ly/Jas5:20); [1PE 4:8](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:8)

Natural phenomena as God's covering
