## 4808: crimson (id: 4808)

A deep red colour used interchangeably with scarlet and sometimes with purple. It has associations with blood and richness.

The richness of crimson used for ornamentation
----------------------------------------------

**[EXO 25:3–25:4](https://ref.ly/Exod25:3-Exod25:4), [EXO 28:6](https://ref.ly/Exod28:6)** *It was used extensively in the construction of the tabernacle*; **[2CH 2:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr2:7)** *See also* [2CH 2:14](https://ref.ly/2Chr2:14), [2CH 3:14](https://ref.ly/2Chr3:14)

Crimson associated with splendour and prosperity
------------------------------------------------

**[2SA 1:24](https://ref.ly/2Sam1:24)**; **[REV 18:16](https://ref.ly/Rev18:16)** *See also* [PRO 31:21](https://ref.ly/Prov31:21); [JER 4:30](https://ref.ly/Jer4:30); [NAM 2:3](https://ref.ly/Nah2:3); [MAT 27:28](https://ref.ly/Matt27:28) parallel [MRK 15:17](https://ref.ly/Mark15:17); [REV 18:12](https://ref.ly/Rev18:12)
