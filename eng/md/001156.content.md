## 5591: treasure (id: 5591)

Highly valued objects or accumulated riches. Treasure has at best only short\-term benefit but can bring spiritual dangers. Spiritual treasure is of eternal value and comes from knowing and serving God.

The limitations of accumulated treasure
---------------------------------------

### It is easily lost

**[ISA 64:11](https://ref.ly/Isa64:11)** *See also* [2KI 24:13](https://ref.ly/2Kgs24:13); [JER 15:13](https://ref.ly/Jer15:13), [JER 17:3](https://ref.ly/Jer17:3), [JER 20:5](https://ref.ly/Jer20:5); [LAM 1:10–1:11](https://ref.ly/Lam1:10-Lam1:11); [HOS 13:15](https://ref.ly/Hos13:15); [OBA 1:6](https://ref.ly/Obad1:6); [MAT 6:19](https://ref.ly/Matt6:19); [JAS 5:2–5:3](https://ref.ly/Jas5:2-Jas5:3); [REV 18:14](https://ref.ly/Rev18:14)

### It is no security against God's displeasure

**[PRO 11:4](https://ref.ly/Prov11:4)** *See also* [JOB 20:20](https://ref.ly/Job20:20); [JER 51:13](https://ref.ly/Jer51:13); [EZK 7:19](https://ref.ly/Ezek7:19); [HOS 9:6](https://ref.ly/Hos9:6); [ZEP 1:18](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:18); [LUK 12:16–12:21](https://ref.ly/Luke12:16-Luke12:21)

### It does not satisfy

**[ECC 5:10](https://ref.ly/Eccl5:10)** *See also* [PSA 39:6](https://ref.ly/Ps39:6); [HAG 1:6](https://ref.ly/Hag1:6)

The spiritual dangers of accumulated treasure
---------------------------------------------

### It can lead to pride

**[1TI 6:17](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:17)** *See also* [DEU 8:13–8:14](https://ref.ly/Deut8:13-Deut8:14); [EZK 7:20](https://ref.ly/Ezek7:20), [EZK 28:4–28:5](https://ref.ly/Ezek28:4-Ezek28:5); [DAN 4:30](https://ref.ly/Dan4:30)

### It can lead a person away from God

**[REV 3:17](https://ref.ly/Rev3:17)** *See also* [JOB 31:24–31:25](https://ref.ly/Job31:24-Job31:25), [JOB 31:28](https://ref.ly/Job31:28); [PRO 11:28](https://ref.ly/Prov11:28)

### It can lead to greed

**[LUK 12:15](https://ref.ly/Luke12:15)** *See also* [1TI 6:9–6:10](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:9-1Tim6:10)

### Acquiring it can lead to dishonesty

[JOS 7:21](https://ref.ly/Josh7:21); ; [PSA 62:10](https://ref.ly/Ps62:10); ; [PRO 21:6](https://ref.ly/Prov21:6)

### It can lead to idolatry

[EXO 32:2–32:4](https://ref.ly/Exod32:2-Exod32:4); ; [ISA 2:7–2:8](https://ref.ly/Isa2:7-Isa2:8); ; [EZK 7:20](https://ref.ly/Ezek7:20), [EZK 16:17](https://ref.ly/Ezek16:17)

Spiritual treasure
------------------

### Treasure consists in knowing God and his wisdom

### God's word is supreme treasure

**[MAT 13:44](https://ref.ly/Matt13:44)** *See also* [MAT 13:45–13:46](https://ref.ly/Matt13:45-Matt13:46), [MAT 13:52](https://ref.ly/Matt13:52)

**[HEB 11:26](https://ref.ly/Heb11:26)** *See also* [JOB 22:23–22:25](https://ref.ly/Job22:23-Job22:25); [MAT 13:44–13:46](https://ref.ly/Matt13:44-Matt13:46)

**[LUK 12:33](https://ref.ly/Luke12:33)** *See also* [MAT 6:20](https://ref.ly/Matt6:20); [1PE 1:4](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:4)
