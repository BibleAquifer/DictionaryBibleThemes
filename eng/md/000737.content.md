## 5148: ear (id: 5148)

The organ of hearing; the ear is referred to in various OT rites and is a symbol of attention, alertness or readiness to hear.

The ear in the OT rites
-----------------------

### Piercing the ear: a symbol of servanthood

**[PSA 40:6](https://ref.ly/Ps40:6)** *See also* [EXO 21:5–21:6](https://ref.ly/Exod21:5-Exod21:6); [DEU 15:16–15:17](https://ref.ly/Deut15:16-Deut15:17)

### Blood applied to the ear: a symbol of cleansing and ordination

**[LEV 8:23](https://ref.ly/Lev8:23)** *See also* [EXO 29:20](https://ref.ly/Exod29:20); [LEV 14:14](https://ref.ly/Lev14:14)

God's ear as a symbol of his alertness to humanity
--------------------------------------------------

**[PSA 116:1–116:2](https://ref.ly/Ps116:1-Ps116:2)** *See also* [2KI 19:16](https://ref.ly/2Kgs19:16); [2CH 7:15](https://ref.ly/2Chr7:15); [NEH 1:6](https://ref.ly/Neh1:6)JOB [34:28](https://ref.ly/Neh34:28); [JAS 5:4](https://ref.ly/Jas5:4)[PSA 5:1](https://ref.ly/Ps5:1), [PSA 31:2](https://ref.ly/Ps31:2), [PSA 34:15](https://ref.ly/Ps34:15), [PSA 88:2](https://ref.ly/Ps88:2), [PSA 130:1–130:2](https://ref.ly/Ps130:1-Ps130:2); [DAN 9:18](https://ref.ly/Dan9:18)

God's ear may be turned away from humanity
------------------------------------------

**[PSA 28:1](https://ref.ly/Ps28:1)** *The refusal to turn an ear towards someone was a symbol of refusing to listen.* *See also* [DEU 1:45](https://ref.ly/Deut1:45); [LAM 3:56](https://ref.ly/Lam3:56); [EZK 8:18](https://ref.ly/Ezek8:18) *God's attending ear is partly dependent on human attitudes to him.*

The human ear used symbolically
-------------------------------

### Human ears should be open to God

**[ISA 50:4–50:5](https://ref.ly/Isa50:4-Isa50:5), [ISA 55:3](https://ref.ly/Isa55:3)** *See also* [JOB 42:5](https://ref.ly/Job42:5); [PSA 81:8](https://ref.ly/Ps81:8); [ISA 30:21](https://ref.ly/Isa30:21), [ISA 32:3](https://ref.ly/Isa32:3); [MAT 13:9](https://ref.ly/Matt13:9) parallels [MRK 4:9](https://ref.ly/Mark4:9), [LUK 8:8](https://ref.ly/Luke8:8) *“He who has ears” implies a potential willingness to listen*; [MAT 13:16](https://ref.ly/Matt13:16); [MRK 9:7](https://ref.ly/Mark9:7); [REV 2:7](https://ref.ly/Rev2:7)

### Human ears may be closed against God

**[ZEC 7:11–7:13](https://ref.ly/Zech7:11-Zech7:13)** *See also* [PSA 58:4–58:5](https://ref.ly/Ps58:4-Ps58:5); [ISA 6:10](https://ref.ly/Isa6:10)

### The human ear should also be open to others

[ISA 48:8](https://ref.ly/Isa48:8); ; [JER 6:10](https://ref.ly/Jer6:10); ; [EZK 12:2](https://ref.ly/Ezek12:2); ; [ACT 7:51](https://ref.ly/Acts7:51), [ACT 7:57](https://ref.ly/Acts7:57); ; [2TI 4:4](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:4)**[PRO 21:13](https://ref.ly/Prov21:13)**
