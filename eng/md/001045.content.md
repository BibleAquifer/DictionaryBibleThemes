## 5471: proof (id: 5471)

A confirmation and verification of a belief, such as belief in the existence of God, in the trustworthiness of a witness or in the resurrection of Jesus Christ.

* **Associated Articles:** 5472: proof, as evidence (ID: `5472@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 5473: proof, through testing (ID: `5473@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
