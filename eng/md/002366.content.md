## 8735: evil, origins of (id: 8735)

Although Scripture does not reveal the ultimate origin of evil, it identifies a number of intermediate sources.

Satan as a source of evil
-------------------------

**[GEN 3:1](https://ref.ly/Gen3:1)** *Identified later ([Rev 12:9](https://ref.ly/Rev12:9)) with Satan*; **[MAT 4:1](https://ref.ly/Matt4:1)** parallels [MRK 1:13](https://ref.ly/Mark1:13), [LUK 4:2](https://ref.ly/Luke4:2)**[JHN 8:44](https://ref.ly/John8:44)**; **[1JN 3:8](https://ref.ly/1John3:8)**; **[REV 12:9](https://ref.ly/Rev12:9)** *See also* [1CH 21:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr21:1) *Satan incites David to take a census of Israel*; [JOB 1:11](https://ref.ly/Job1:11), [JOB 2:5](https://ref.ly/Job2:5); [MAT 5:37](https://ref.ly/Matt5:37), [MAT 6:13](https://ref.ly/Matt6:13), [MAT 13:38–13:39](https://ref.ly/Matt13:38-Matt13:39) *the parable of the weeds*; [LUK 13:16](https://ref.ly/Luke13:16) *Satan is responsible for a woman's crippling disease*; [JHN 13:2](https://ref.ly/John13:2) *The devil prompts Judas to betray Jesus Christ*; [JHN 17:15](https://ref.ly/John17:15); [ACT 5:3](https://ref.ly/Acts5:3) *Ananias lies to the Holy Spirit at Satan's instigation*; [2CO 4:4](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:4), [2CO 12:7](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:7) *Paul's thorn in the flesh from Satan*; [EPH 2:2](https://ref.ly/Eph2:2), [EPH 6:11](https://ref.ly/Eph6:11); [1TH 2:18](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:18); [1PE 5:8](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:8); [1JN 3:12](https://ref.ly/1John3:12), [1JN 5:19](https://ref.ly/1John5:19); [REV 2:10](https://ref.ly/Rev2:10), [REV 20:2](https://ref.ly/Rev20:2)
