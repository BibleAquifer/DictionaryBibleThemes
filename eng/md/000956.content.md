## 5372: knife (id: 5372)

The knife as a domestic cutting implement
-----------------------------------------

### Used for general purposes

[PRO 23:2](https://ref.ly/Prov23:2), [PRO 30:14](https://ref.ly/Prov30:14); ; [JER 36:23](https://ref.ly/Jer36:23) *cutting a scroll*

Knives used for shaving
-----------------------

[LEV 19:27](https://ref.ly/Lev19:27), [LEV 21:5](https://ref.ly/Lev21:5); ; [JDG 16:17](https://ref.ly/Judg16:17); ; [ISA 15:2](https://ref.ly/Isa15:2); ; [EZK 7:18](https://ref.ly/Ezek7:18); ; [MIC 1:16](https://ref.ly/Mic1:16)

Knives used in religious ceremonies
-----------------------------------

### In circumcision

Flint rather than metal knives were used because of their sharpness: [EXO 4:25](https://ref.ly/Exod4:25); ; [JOS 5:2–5:3](https://ref.ly/Josh5:2-Josh5:3)

### In sacrifice

[GEN 15:10](https://ref.ly/Gen15:10), [GEN 22:10](https://ref.ly/Gen22:10); ; [EXO 29:17](https://ref.ly/Exod29:17); ; [LEV 8:20](https://ref.ly/Lev8:20)

Knives used for wounding
------------------------

### For self\-mutilation

This practice was associated with pagan worship, sometimes as an expression of mourning: [LEV 19:28](https://ref.ly/Lev19:28), [LEV 21:5](https://ref.ly/Lev21:5); ; [DEU 14:1–14:2](https://ref.ly/Deut14:1-Deut14:2); ; [1KI 18:28](https://ref.ly/1Kgs18:28); ; [JER 16:6](https://ref.ly/Jer16:6), [JER 41:5](https://ref.ly/Jer41:5), [JER 47:5](https://ref.ly/Jer47:5), [JER 48:37](https://ref.ly/Jer48:37)

### In mutilation of others

As a punishment: [EXO 21:23–21:25](https://ref.ly/Exod21:23-Exod21:25); ; [LEV 24:17–24:20](https://ref.ly/Lev24:17-Lev24:20); ; [DEU 25:12](https://ref.ly/Deut25:12); [JDG 1:6](https://ref.ly/Judg1:6), [JDG 16:21](https://ref.ly/Judg16:21); [1SA 11:7](https://ref.ly/1Sam11:7)
