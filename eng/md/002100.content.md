## 8265: godliness (id: 8265)

Reverence for or devotion to God, producing a practical awareness of God in every aspect of life.

Examples of godliness in the OT
-------------------------------

[GEN 5:24](https://ref.ly/Gen5:24) *Enoch*; ; [GEN 6:9](https://ref.ly/Gen6:9) *Noah*; ; [1KI 18:3–18:4](https://ref.ly/1Kgs18:3-1Kgs18:4) *Obadiah*; ; [2CH 31:20–31:21](https://ref.ly/2Chr31:20-2Chr31:21) *Hezekiah*; ; [JOB 1:1](https://ref.ly/Job1:1) *Job*

Examples of godliness in the NT
-------------------------------

[LUK 2:25](https://ref.ly/Luke2:25) *Simeon*; ; [LUK 2:37](https://ref.ly/Luke2:37) *Anna*; ; [JHN 1:47](https://ref.ly/John1:47) *Nathanael*; [JHN 8:29](https://ref.ly/John8:29); ; [HEB 5:7](https://ref.ly/Heb5:7); [ACT 2:5](https://ref.ly/Acts2:5), [ACT 8:2](https://ref.ly/Acts8:2); [ACT 10:2](https://ref.ly/Acts10:2), [ACT 10:7](https://ref.ly/Acts10:7); [ACT 11:24](https://ref.ly/Acts11:24) *Barnabas*; ; [ACT 22:12](https://ref.ly/Acts22:12) *Ananias*

God has a special concern for the godly
---------------------------------------

**[PSA 4:3](https://ref.ly/Ps4:3)** *“the godly” (sometimes translated “the saints”) is often used in the OT to refer to God's people.* *See also* [PSA 32:6](https://ref.ly/Ps32:6); [JHN 9:31](https://ref.ly/John9:31)

Godly living
------------

### Godliness should be seen in the lives of believers

**[2PE 3:11](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:11)** *See also* [1TI 2:2](https://ref.ly/1Tim2:2), [1TI 6:11](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:11); [TIT 2:12](https://ref.ly/Titus2:12)

### Jesus Christ is the beginning and end of godliness

**[1TI 3:16](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:16)** *The model and the power for godly living derives from the incarnate Christ.* *See also* [HEB 5:7](https://ref.ly/Heb5:7); [2PE 1:3](https://ref.ly/2Pet1:3)

### The basis for godly living is true teaching about Jesus Christ

**[1TI 6:3–6:4](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:3-1Tim6:4)** *See also* [TIT 1:1](https://ref.ly/Titus1:1)

### Godly living demands self\-discipline

[1CO 9:24–9:27](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:24-1Cor9:27); ; [2CO 10:5](https://ref.ly/2Cor10:5); ; [1TI 4:7–4:8](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:7-1Tim4:8)

### Sorrow for sin is a sign of godliness

[2CO 7:10–7:11](https://ref.ly/2Cor7:10-2Cor7:11)
