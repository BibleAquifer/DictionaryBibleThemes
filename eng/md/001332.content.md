## 5810: complacency (id: 5810)

A self\-satisfied state of negligence or carelessness, especially in relation to one's personal situation.

Spiritual complacency
---------------------

**[ZEP 1:12](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:12)**; **[LUK 12:19](https://ref.ly/Luke12:19)** *See also* [DEU 8:14](https://ref.ly/Deut8:14); [2CH 20:33](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:33); [PSA 10:4](https://ref.ly/Ps10:4); [ISA 43:22](https://ref.ly/Isa43:22), [ISA 64:7](https://ref.ly/Isa64:7); [JER 10:21](https://ref.ly/Jer10:21); [EZK 33:31](https://ref.ly/Ezek33:31); [DAN 9:13](https://ref.ly/Dan9:13); [HOS 7:7](https://ref.ly/Hos7:7); [ZEP 1:6](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:6); [HEB 6:12](https://ref.ly/Heb6:12), [HEB 12:25](https://ref.ly/Heb12:25)
