## 7770: priests, tasks in NT times (id: 7770)

The priests mentioned in the NT performed various functions, all of which are fulfilled in Jesus Christ and the church, which is his body.

Priests offered sacrifices
--------------------------

### Sacrifices made by pagan priests

**[ACT 14:13](https://ref.ly/Acts14:13)**

### Jewish sacrifice

**[HEB 10:11](https://ref.ly/Heb10:11)**

### The sacrifice of Jesus Christ

**[HEB 9:14](https://ref.ly/Heb9:14)** *Jesus Christ is both priest and victim: his own body takes the place occupied by sacrifices in the OT ritual.* *See also* [HEB 10:10](https://ref.ly/Heb10:10)

### The Christian sacrifice of a surrendered life

**[ROM 12:1](https://ref.ly/Rom12:1)** *See also* [HEB 13:16](https://ref.ly/Heb13:16)

### The Christian sacrifice of praise

**[HEB 13:15](https://ref.ly/Heb13:15)**

### The burning of incense

**[LUK 1:9](https://ref.ly/Luke1:9)** *See also* [EXO 30:7–30:8](https://ref.ly/Exod30:7-Exod30:8)

### Christian intercession

**[REV 5:8](https://ref.ly/Rev5:8)** *See also* [1PE 2:5](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:5); [REV 1:6](https://ref.ly/Rev1:6), [REV 5:10](https://ref.ly/Rev5:10), [REV 20:6](https://ref.ly/Rev20:6)
