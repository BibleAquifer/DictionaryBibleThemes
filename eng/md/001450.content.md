## 5937: rivalry (id: 5937)

Viewing other people as a threat to oneself in the accomplishment of some aim, or being in active competition with others for recognition or achievement.

Examples of rivalry at a national level
---------------------------------------

### Between Israel and Judah

[1KI 15:25–15:31](https://ref.ly/1Kgs15:25-1Kgs15:31) *This is typical of the conflict that existed for 80 years after the division of the kingdom.*

### Within Israel

**[1KI 16:21–16:22](https://ref.ly/1Kgs16:21-1Kgs16:22)**

### Between Assyria and Judah

*See also* [2CH 32:13–32:22](https://ref.ly/2Chr32:13-2Chr32:22); [ISA 37:10–37:13](https://ref.ly/Isa37:10-Isa37:13) *Assyria claimed that behind the rivalry of the nations lay the rivalry of their gods.
