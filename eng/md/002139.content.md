## 8308: modesty (id: 8308)

An attitude of humility, avoiding improper self\-exaltation or excessive flamboyance. Scripture urges modesty in personal behaviour, forms of dress and forms of behaviour.

Modesty as an attitude of humility
----------------------------------

**[ROM 12:3](https://ref.ly/Rom12:3)** *See also* [PRO 25:6–25:7](https://ref.ly/Prov25:6-Prov25:7), [PRO 29:23](https://ref.ly/Prov29:23); [LUK 22:25–22:27](https://ref.ly/Luke22:25-Luke22:27) parallels [MAT 20:25–20:28](https://ref.ly/Matt20:25-Matt20:28), [MRK 10:42–10:45](https://ref.ly/Mark10:42-Mark10:45); [ROM 11:20–11:21](https://ref.ly/Rom11:20-Rom11:21); [2CO 10:13–10:16](https://ref.ly/2Cor10:13-2Cor10:16), [2CO 12:5–12:6](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:5-2Cor12:6); [PHP 2:5–2:8](https://ref.ly/Phil2:5-Phil2:8); [1PE 5:5](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:5)
