## 5463: proclamations (id: 5463)

Edicts or public declarations issued by the civil or religious authorities.

Examples of proclamations in biblical times
-------------------------------------------

[EXO 1:15–1:22](https://ref.ly/Exod1:15-Exod1:22) *Pharaoh's edict that all Hebrew boys were to be drowned*; ; [EXO 32:5](https://ref.ly/Exod32:5) *Aaron's proclamation of a festival to the LORD*; ; [1KI 21:9–21:10](https://ref.ly/1Kgs21:9-1Kgs21:10) *Jezebel's proclamation to ensnare Naboth*; ; [2KI 10:20](https://ref.ly/2Kgs10:20) *Jehu's proclamation of an assembly in honour of Baal*; ; [2CH 24:9](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:9) *Joash's proclamation of a tax for the LORD*; ; [2CH 30:1–30:10](https://ref.ly/2Chr30:1-2Chr30:10) *Hezekiah's invitation to celebrate the Passover*; [EZR 1:1](https://ref.ly/Ezra1:1), [EZR 5:13](https://ref.ly/Ezra5:13), [EZR 6:3](https://ref.ly/Ezra6:3), [EZR 7:13](https://ref.ly/Ezra7:13); [NEH 8:14–8:15](https://ref.ly/Neh8:14-Neh8:15) *the Levites' proclamation of festival arrangements*; [EST 1:20](https://ref.ly/Esth1:20), [EST 2:8](https://ref.ly/Esth2:8), [EST 3:14](https://ref.ly/Esth3:14), [EST 4:3](https://ref.ly/Esth4:3), [EST 8:11](https://ref.ly/Esth8:11); [DAN 6:7](https://ref.ly/Dan6:7), [DAN 6:15](https://ref.ly/Dan6:15); [JON 3:7](https://ref.ly/Jonah3:7) *the king of Nineveh's proclamation of a fast of repentance*; ; [LUK 2:1](https://ref.ly/Luke2:1) *Caesar Augustus' proclamation of a census*
