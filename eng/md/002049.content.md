## 8203: character (id: 8203)

The moral and mental features that define a person, whether good or evil. The term also means moral strength, which Scripture regards as something to be highly valued.

The character of God
--------------------

**[EPH 1:2](https://ref.ly/Eph1:2)** *See also* [GAL 1:1](https://ref.ly/Gal1:1); [COL 1:12](https://ref.ly/Col1:12); [1TH 1:3](https://ref.ly/1Thess1:3), [1TH 3:13](https://ref.ly/1Thess3:13)
