## 6021: sin, nature of (id: 6021)

Scripture portrays sin in terms of wrongness before God in a variety of different ways, such as uncleanness, guilt or rebellion.

The basic nature of sin
-----------------------

### All sin is directed against God

**[PSA 51:4](https://ref.ly/Ps51:4)** *See also* [GEN 13:13](https://ref.ly/Gen13:13); [EXO 10:16](https://ref.ly/Exod10:16); [JDG 10:10](https://ref.ly/Judg10:10); [PSA 41:4](https://ref.ly/Ps41:4); [LUK 15:18](https://ref.ly/Luke15:18)

### Sin is essentially a lack of faith in God

**[ROM 14:23](https://ref.ly/Rom14:23)** *See also* [HEB 11:6](https://ref.ly/Heb11:6)

Descriptions of sin
-------------------

### Corruption of God's good purposes

**[DEU 32:5](https://ref.ly/Deut32:5)** *See also* [GEN 6:11–6:12](https://ref.ly/Gen6:11-Gen6:12); [DEU 9:12](https://ref.ly/Deut9:12), [DEU 31:29](https://ref.ly/Deut31:29); [JDG 2:19](https://ref.ly/Judg2:19)

### Doing evil in God's sight

**[JHN 3:19–3:20](https://ref.ly/John3:19-John3:20)** *See also* [JDG 2:11](https://ref.ly/Judg2:11); [PSA 34:12–34:16](https://ref.ly/Ps34:12-Ps34:16); [PRO 8:13](https://ref.ly/Prov8:13); [ISA 59:6–59:7](https://ref.ly/Isa59:6-Isa59:7); [MAT 12:35](https://ref.ly/Matt12:35) parallel [LUK 6:45](https://ref.ly/Luke6:45)

### Ungodliness

**[JUD 1:14–1:15](https://ref.ly/Jude1:14-Jude1:15)** *See also* [ISA 9:17](https://ref.ly/Isa9:17), [ISA 32:6](https://ref.ly/Isa32:6); [1TI 1:9](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:9)

**[ISA 30:9](https://ref.ly/Isa30:9)** *See also* [DEU 9:7](https://ref.ly/Deut9:7); [1SA 15:23](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:23); [PSA 78:40](https://ref.ly/Ps78:40), [PSA 78:56](https://ref.ly/Ps78:56); [JER 3:13](https://ref.ly/Jer3:13); [HOS 7:13](https://ref.ly/Hos7:13)

### The breaking or transgression of God's laws

**[ISA 53:6](https://ref.ly/Isa53:6)** *See also* [PSA 58:3](https://ref.ly/Ps58:3), [PSA 95:10](https://ref.ly/Ps95:10), [PSA 119:10](https://ref.ly/Ps119:10), [PSA 119:21](https://ref.ly/Ps119:21), [PSA 119:118](https://ref.ly/Ps119:118)

### Incurring a debt

**[MAT 6:12](https://ref.ly/Matt6:12)** *See also* [MAT 18:21–18:35](https://ref.ly/Matt18:21-Matt18:35)

**[ROM 3:23](https://ref.ly/Rom3:23)** *The words most commonly used for “sin” in the OT and NT originally signified “missing the mark”.*
