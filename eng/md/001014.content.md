## 5436: pain (id: 5436)

A sense of physical suffering, anguish or distress, which has a number of causes. Scripture indicates the sources of pain and its potential spiritual implications and results.

Pain is universal
-----------------

**[ROM 8:22](https://ref.ly/Rom8:22)** *See also* [GEN 3:16–3:17](https://ref.ly/Gen3:16-Gen3:17); [JOB 5:7](https://ref.ly/Job5:7)
