## 2585: Jesus Christ, trial of (id: 2585)

Jesus Christ, falsely accused of blasphemy towards God and treason towards Rome, humbly endured his trial as a fulfilment of the purposes of God.

The preliminary hearing before Annas
------------------------------------

*See also* [JHN 18:12–18:14](https://ref.ly/John18:12-John18:14) *Annas, though deposed from office by the Romans, was probably still regarded by many as the true high priest*; [JHN 18:19–18:23](https://ref.ly/John18:19-John18:23)

The trial before Caiaphas and the Sanhedrin
-------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ is sent to the ruling high priest

[MAT 26:57](https://ref.ly/Matt26:57) parallels [MRK 14:53](https://ref.ly/Mark14:53), [JHN 18:24](https://ref.ly/John18:24); ; [LUK 22:54](https://ref.ly/Luke22:54)

### False evidence is sought

**[MAT 26:59–26:60](https://ref.ly/Matt26:59-Matt26:60)** parallel [MRK 14:55–14:56](https://ref.ly/Mark14:55-Mark14:56) *The Sanhedrin was the high court of the Jews and consisted of 71 chief priests, elders and teachers of the law, including the high priest who presided.*

### False accusations are made

*See also* [MAT 26:60–26:61](https://ref.ly/Matt26:60-Matt26:61) parallel [MRK 14:57–14:59](https://ref.ly/Mark14:57-Mark14:59) *A person could only be convicted on the evidence of two or more witnesses.*

Jesus Christ's trial centres upon his Messianic claims
------------------------------------------------------

### Jesus declares himself to be the Christ

**[MAT 26:63–26:64](https://ref.ly/Matt26:63-Matt26:64)** parallel [MRK 14:61–14:62](https://ref.ly/Mark14:61-Mark14:62) *See also* [LUK 22:66–22:70](https://ref.ly/Luke22:66-Luke22:70)

### Jesus Christ is charged with blasphemy

[MAT 26:65–26:66](https://ref.ly/Matt26:65-Matt26:66) parallel [MRK 14:63–14:64](https://ref.ly/Mark14:63-Mark14:64); ; [MAT 27:1](https://ref.ly/Matt27:1) parallel [MRK 15:1](https://ref.ly/Mark15:1) *The Sanhedrin decided that Jesus Christ deserved death but charged him with treason rather than blasphemy.*

### Jesus is mocked as the Christ

[MAT 26:67–26:68](https://ref.ly/Matt26:67-Matt26:68) parallels [MRK 14:65](https://ref.ly/Mark14:65), [LUK 22:63–22:65](https://ref.ly/Luke22:63-Luke22:65)
