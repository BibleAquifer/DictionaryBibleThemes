## 3254: Holy Spirit, fruit of (id: 3254)

The living presence of the Holy Spirit in believers leads to Christlike virtues within them, just as a living tree will bear good fruit.

God expects his people to bear spiritual fruit
----------------------------------------------

**[ISA 5:4](https://ref.ly/Isa5:4)** *See also* [MAT 7:12–7:20](https://ref.ly/Matt7:12-Matt7:20) *“fruit” is a term used to indicate the produce or outcome of a person's life*; [JHN 15:8](https://ref.ly/John15:8)

The fruit of the Spirit leads to believers becoming Christlike
--------------------------------------------------------------

### The gift of the Spirit begins this lifelong process

**[2CO 3:18](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:18)**

### Christlike qualities are contrasted with sinful ones

**[GAL 5:16–5:17](https://ref.ly/Gal5:16-Gal5:17)** *See also* [ROM 8:5–8:14](https://ref.ly/Rom8:5-Rom8:14); [1CO 12:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:7); [GAL 6:8](https://ref.ly/Gal6:8); [EPH 5:8–5:16](https://ref.ly/Eph5:8-Eph5:16); [COL 3:1–3:17](https://ref.ly/Col3:1-Col3:17)

The fruit of Spirit\-filled living
----------------------------------

**[GAL 5:22–5:23](https://ref.ly/Gal5:22-Gal5:23)** *“fruit” is singular, emphasising that these qualities are mutually dependent aspects of Christian living.* *See also* [EPH 5:9](https://ref.ly/Eph5:9)
