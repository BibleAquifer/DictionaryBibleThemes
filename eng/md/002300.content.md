## 8623: worship, of God alone (id: 8623)

God alone is worthy of worship; the worship of other gods is forbidden. In the NT worship is offered to the Son of God.

God alone is to be worshipped
-----------------------------

### He alone is worthy of worship

**[1CH 16:25](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:25)** *The English word “worship” means literally “worth\-ship”.* *See also* [PSA 48:1](https://ref.ly/Ps48:1), [PSA 96:4–96:5](https://ref.ly/Ps96:4-Ps96:5), [PSA 145:3](https://ref.ly/Ps145:3); [2SA 22:4](https://ref.ly/2Sam22:4)
