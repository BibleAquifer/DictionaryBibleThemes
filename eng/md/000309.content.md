## 4028: world, as redeemed (id: 4028)

Though fallen, the world is offered the hope of redemption and restoration through Jesus Christ.

This world has an end
---------------------

### It is temporary

**[1PE 1:24](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:24)** *See also* [ISA 40:6–40:7](https://ref.ly/Isa40:6-Isa40:7); [MAT 5:18](https://ref.ly/Matt5:18), [MAT 24:35](https://ref.ly/Matt24:35) parallels [MRK 13:31](https://ref.ly/Mark13:31), [LUK 21:33](https://ref.ly/Luke21:33); [1CO 7:31](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:31); [HEB 1:10–1:12](https://ref.ly/Heb1:10-Heb1:12), [HEB 12:26–12:27](https://ref.ly/Heb12:26-Heb12:27)

### It is under God's judgment

**[ISA 13:13](https://ref.ly/Isa13:13)** *See also* [ISA 34:4](https://ref.ly/Isa34:4), [ISA 51:6](https://ref.ly/Isa51:6); [JOL 2:30–2:31](https://ref.ly/Joel2:30-Joel2:31); [2PE 3:10](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:10); [1JN 2:17](https://ref.ly/1John2:17); [REV 20:11](https://ref.ly/Rev20:11)
