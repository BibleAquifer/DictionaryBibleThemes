## 6027: sin, God's remedy for (id: 6027)

Under the old covenant, sin was forgiven through sacrifice, prefiguring the atoning death of Jesus Christ, which brings forgiveness of sins under the new covenant.

Atonement for sins in the OT was through the shedding of blood
--------------------------------------------------------------

**[LEV 17:11](https://ref.ly/Lev17:11)**

Kinds of sin offering in the OT
-------------------------------

### The sin offering was for unintentional sins

**[LEV 4:1–5:13](https://ref.ly/Lev4:1-Lev5:13)** *See also* [NUM 15:22–15:31](https://ref.ly/Num15:22-Num15:31)

### The guilt offering was for unintentional sins, where restitution was required

**[LEV 5:14–6:7](https://ref.ly/Lev5:14-Lev6:7)** *See also* [NUM 5:5–5:10](https://ref.ly/Num5:5-Num5:10)

### The annual Day of Atonement cleansed the nation of sin

**[LEV 16:1–16:34](https://ref.ly/Lev16:1-Lev16:34)** *See also* [EXO 30:11–30:16](https://ref.ly/Exod30:11-Exod30:16); [LEV 23:26–23:32](https://ref.ly/Lev23:26-Lev23:32); [HEB 9:7](https://ref.ly/Heb9:7)

### The priests made atonement on occasions of deliberate national sin

[NUM 16:46–16:48](https://ref.ly/Num16:46-Num16:48) *Aaron stops the plague after Korah's sin*; ; [NUM 25:13](https://ref.ly/Num25:13) *Phinehas stops the plague after Moab caused Israel to sin.*

Sacrifices of atonement needed to be accompanied by repentance and a willingness to obey
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Worshippers recognised the need for repentance and obedience

**[PRO 21:3](https://ref.ly/Prov21:3)** *See also* [PSA 40:6–40:8](https://ref.ly/Ps40:6-Ps40:8), [PSA 51:16–51:17](https://ref.ly/Ps51:16-Ps51:17)

### The prophets declared the need for obedience

**[1SA 15:22](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:22)** *See also* [ISA 1:11–1:17](https://ref.ly/Isa1:11-Isa1:17); [JER 7:21–7:23](https://ref.ly/Jer7:21-Jer7:23); [HOS 6:6](https://ref.ly/Hos6:6); [MIC 6:6–6:8](https://ref.ly/Mic6:6-Mic6:8)

### The prophets warned of judgment to bring people to repentance

**[2KI 17:13](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:13)** *See also* [ISA 31:6–31:7](https://ref.ly/Isa31:6-Isa31:7); [JER 4:1–4:4](https://ref.ly/Jer4:1-Jer4:4), [JER 35:15](https://ref.ly/Jer35:15); [EZK 3:16–3:19](https://ref.ly/Ezek3:16-Ezek3:19); [JON 3:4–3:10](https://ref.ly/Jonah3:4-Jonah3:10)

The death of Jesus Christ brings forgiveness for sin
----------------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ died on behalf of sinful humanity

**[ROM 5:6](https://ref.ly/Rom5:6)** *See also* [MAT 26:26–26:28](https://ref.ly/Matt26:26-Matt26:28); [JHN 10:11](https://ref.ly/John10:11), [JHN 15:13](https://ref.ly/John15:13); [GAL 2:20](https://ref.ly/Gal2:20)

### The early church proclaimed that Christ died for the sins of others

**[1CO 15:3](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:3)** *See also* [ROM 4:25](https://ref.ly/Rom4:25); [GAL 1:4](https://ref.ly/Gal1:4); [1PE 3:18](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:18)

### Jesus Christ bore sin on the cross

**[2CO 5:21](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:21)** *See also* [ISA 53:10–53:12](https://ref.ly/Isa53:10-Isa53:12); [HEB 9:28](https://ref.ly/Heb9:28); [1PE 2:24](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:24)

### Jesus Christ has redeemed people by taking their place

**[MAT 20:28](https://ref.ly/Matt20:28)** parallel [MRK 10:45](https://ref.ly/Mark10:45) *See also* [GAL 3:13](https://ref.ly/Gal3:13); [1TI 2:6](https://ref.ly/1Tim2:6); [TIT 2:14](https://ref.ly/Titus2:14)

### Jesus Christ's death is sacrificial

**[ROM 3:25](https://ref.ly/Rom3:25)** *See also* [ROM 8:3](https://ref.ly/Rom8:3); [1CO 5:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor5:7); [EPH 5:2](https://ref.ly/Eph5:2); [HEB 7:27](https://ref.ly/Heb7:27), [HEB 10:5–10:13](https://ref.ly/Heb10:5-Heb10:13); [1JN 2:2](https://ref.ly/1John2:2), [1JN 4:10](https://ref.ly/1John4:10)
