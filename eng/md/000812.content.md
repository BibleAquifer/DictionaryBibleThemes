## 5224: barn (id: 5224)

Barns were associated with industry and prosperity
--------------------------------------------------

### Full barns were a sign of blessing

**[PRO 3:9–3:10](https://ref.ly/Prov3:9-Prov3:10)** *See also* [DEU 28:8](https://ref.ly/Deut28:8); [PSA 144:12–144:15](https://ref.ly/Ps144:12-Ps144:15)

### Empty barns were a sign of poverty

**[HAG 2:15–2:19](https://ref.ly/Hag2:15-Hag2:19)**

### The priority of spiritual over material prosperity

**[LUK 12:15–12:21](https://ref.ly/Luke12:15-Luke12:21)** *See also* [MAT 6:25–6:26](https://ref.ly/Matt6:25-Matt6:26); [LUK 12:23–12:24](https://ref.ly/Luke12:23-Luke12:24)

God's barn is a metaphor for the kingdom of heaven
--------------------------------------------------

**[LUK 3:16–3:17](https://ref.ly/Luke3:16-Luke3:17)** parallel [MAT 3:11–3:12](https://ref.ly/Matt3:11-Matt3:12) *See also* [MAT 13:24–13:30](https://ref.ly/Matt13:24-Matt13:30)
