## 8743: faithlessness, nature and causes of (id: 8743)

The abandoning of a commitment to the Lord, seen in disowning him, wandering from the truth and becoming entangled with the world, and resulting in judgment.

**[2TI 2:12–2:13](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:12-2Tim2:13)** *See also* [MAT 10:33](https://ref.ly/Matt10:33) parallel [LUK 12:9](https://ref.ly/Luke12:9); [LUK 9:62](https://ref.ly/Luke9:62); [HEB 10:35–10:39](https://ref.ly/Heb10:35-Heb10:39); [HAB 2:3–2:4](https://ref.ly/Hab2:3-Hab2:4)
