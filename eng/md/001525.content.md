## 6147: deceit, practice of (id: 6147)

Deceit is condemned by God as an attempt to deny his sovereignty and his revelation of himself.

Deceit is forbidden by God
--------------------------

### Condemnation of deception in general

**[LEV 19:11](https://ref.ly/Lev19:11)** *See also* [PRO 12:22](https://ref.ly/Prov12:22), [PRO 24:28](https://ref.ly/Prov24:28)

### False witnesses condemned

**[EXO 20:16](https://ref.ly/Exod20:16)** parallel [DEU 5:20](https://ref.ly/Deut5:20) *See also* [PRO 12:17](https://ref.ly/Prov12:17), [PRO 14:5](https://ref.ly/Prov14:5), [PRO 19:9](https://ref.ly/Prov19:9)

### False measures prohibited

**[LEV 19:35](https://ref.ly/Lev19:35)** *See also* [DEU 25:13](https://ref.ly/Deut25:13); [PRO 11:1](https://ref.ly/Prov11:1), [PRO 20:10](https://ref.ly/Prov20:10); [MIC 6:11](https://ref.ly/Mic6:11)

### Spiritual deception condemned

**[TIT 1:16](https://ref.ly/Titus1:16)** *See also* [MAL 1:14](https://ref.ly/Mal1:14); [MAT 23:27–23:28](https://ref.ly/Matt23:27-Matt23:28); [LUK 6:46](https://ref.ly/Luke6:46); [ROM 2:21](https://ref.ly/Rom2:21)

Deception must not be found in the people of God
------------------------------------------------

### God's people must not practise deceit

**[1PE 2:1](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:1)** *See also* [JOB 27:4](https://ref.ly/Job27:4); [PSA 24:3–24:4](https://ref.ly/Ps24:3-Ps24:4), [PSA 32:2](https://ref.ly/Ps32:2); [ZEP 3:13](https://ref.ly/Zeph3:13); [JHN 4:24](https://ref.ly/John4:24); [1CO 5:8](https://ref.ly/1Cor5:8); [EPH 4:25](https://ref.ly/Eph4:25); [COL 3:9](https://ref.ly/Col3:9); [1PE 3:10](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:10); [REV 14:5](https://ref.ly/Rev14:5)

### Christian leaders in particular must not deceive

**[2CO 4:2](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:2)** *See also* [2CO 1:12](https://ref.ly/2Cor1:12), [2CO 2:17](https://ref.ly/2Cor2:17); [1TH 2:3–2:5](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:3-1Thess2:5); [1TI 3:8](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:8); [TIT 1:7](https://ref.ly/Titus1:7)

**[EPH 5:6](https://ref.ly/Eph5:6)** *See also* [JER 29:8](https://ref.ly/Jer29:8); [1CO 6:9–6:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:9-1Cor6:10), [1CO 15:33](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:33); [GAL 6:7](https://ref.ly/Gal6:7); [COL 2:8](https://ref.ly/Col2:8); [2TH 2:3](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:3); [1JN 3:7](https://ref.ly/1John3:7)
