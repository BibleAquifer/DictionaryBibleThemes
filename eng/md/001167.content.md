## 5603: wages (id: 5603)

Payment by an employer for work done; all labourers should be paid fairly and regularly.

Laws relating to wages
----------------------

### Payment should be made regularly

**[DEU 24:14–24:15](https://ref.ly/Deut24:14-Deut24:15)** *See also* [LEV 19:13](https://ref.ly/Lev19:13)

### Regular rates of pay

[LEV 25:50](https://ref.ly/Lev25:50); ; [MRK 6:37](https://ref.ly/Mark6:37) parallel [JHN 6:7](https://ref.ly/John6:7); ; [MRK 14:5](https://ref.ly/Mark14:5) parallel [JHN 12:5](https://ref.ly/John12:5)

**[MAL 3:5](https://ref.ly/Mal3:5)**; **[JAS 5:4](https://ref.ly/Jas5:4)** *See also* [JER 22:13](https://ref.ly/Jer22:13)

**[MAT 20:2–20:4](https://ref.ly/Matt20:2-Matt20:4)** *A normal day's pay for a labourer in NT times was one denarius.* *See also* [COL 4:1](https://ref.ly/Col4:1)

### A fair day's work should be done

**[2TH 3:10–3:12](https://ref.ly/2Thess3:10-2Thess3:12)** *See also* [EPH 6:7–6:8](https://ref.ly/Eph6:7-Eph6:8); [COL 3:23](https://ref.ly/Col3:23)
