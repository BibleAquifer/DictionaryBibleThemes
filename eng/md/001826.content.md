## 7418: Purim (id: 7418)

A Jewish festival of rejoicing and feasting celebrated in the month of Adar (February\-March) commemorating the deliverance of the Jews recorded in the book of Esther.

The origins of Purim
--------------------

### Esther's position as queen

[EST 4:14](https://ref.ly/Esth4:14)

### Haman's death

[EST 7:10](https://ref.ly/Esth7:10)

### The right of the Jews to avenge themselves on their enemies

[EST 8:11](https://ref.ly/Esth8:11)

### Explanation of the name

**[EST 9:26](https://ref.ly/Esth9:26)** *The word “pur”, meaning “lots”, relates to the lots cast to determine the time set for the massacre.*
