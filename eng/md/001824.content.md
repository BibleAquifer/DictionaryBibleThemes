## 7414: priesthood, in NT (id: 7414)

Jesus Christ recognised the function of the OT priesthood, but the gospel belief in Christ as high priest and the priesthood of all believers superseded the earlier concept.

Jesus Christ acknowledged the role of the Jerusalem priesthood
--------------------------------------------------------------

*See also* [MAT 8:4](https://ref.ly/Matt8:4) parallels [MRK 1:44](https://ref.ly/Mark1:44), [LUK 5:14](https://ref.ly/Luke5:14)

Characteristics of the Levitical priesthood
-------------------------------------------

### It was equivalent to the order of Aaron

**[HEB 7:11](https://ref.ly/Heb7:11)**

### It was incompatible with membership of the tribe of Judah

**[HEB 7:14](https://ref.ly/Heb7:14)**

### It was not established by divine oath

**[HEB 7:20](https://ref.ly/Heb7:20)**

Characteristics of the Melchizedek priesthood
---------------------------------------------

**[HEB 7:15–7:16](https://ref.ly/Heb7:15-Heb7:16)**

How Jesus Christ's priesthood resembled the OT priesthood
---------------------------------------------------------

### He fulfilled the requirement of humanity

**[HEB 2:17](https://ref.ly/Heb2:17)** *See also* [HEB 5:1](https://ref.ly/Heb5:1)

### He was not self\-appointed

**[HEB 5:4–5:5](https://ref.ly/Heb5:4-Heb5:5)**

### He resembled Melchizedek

**[HEB 5:10](https://ref.ly/Heb5:10)**

How Jesus Christ's priesthood contrasts with the Levitical priesthood
---------------------------------------------------------------------

### It involves a change in the applicability of the law

**[HEB 7:12–7:13](https://ref.ly/Heb7:12-Heb7:13)**

### It lasts for ever

**[HEB 7:23–7:24](https://ref.ly/Heb7:23-Heb7:24)** *See also* [HEB 10:11–10:12](https://ref.ly/Heb10:11-Heb10:12)

### It was based on a once\-for\-all sacrifice, not of animals but of himself

**[HEB 9:26](https://ref.ly/Heb9:26)** *See also* [HEB 9:12](https://ref.ly/Heb9:12)

### It is effective, not simply illustrative

**[HEB 10:14](https://ref.ly/Heb10:14)** *See also* [HEB 8:5](https://ref.ly/Heb8:5), [HEB 10:1](https://ref.ly/Heb10:1), [HEB 10:4](https://ref.ly/Heb10:4)

### It is not that of a mere man, but of the Son of God

**[HEB 7:28](https://ref.ly/Heb7:28)** *See also* [HEB 4:14](https://ref.ly/Heb4:14), [HEB 7:26](https://ref.ly/Heb7:26)
