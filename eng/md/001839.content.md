## 7440: scapegoat (id: 7440)

A goat which was sent into the wilderness by the high priest, as a symbol of the removal of the sins of the people.

The scapegoat as part of an OT ritual
-------------------------------------

**[LEV 16:7–16:10](https://ref.ly/Lev16:7-Lev16:10), [LEV 16:20–16:22](https://ref.ly/Lev16:20-Lev16:22)** *See also* [LEV 16:26](https://ref.ly/Lev16:26); [ISA 53:4](https://ref.ly/Isa53:4)

The scapegoat anticipates Jesus Christ's atonement
--------------------------------------------------

[JHN 1:29](https://ref.ly/John1:29); ; [2CO 5:21](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:21); ; [HEB 9:11–9:14](https://ref.ly/Heb9:11-Heb9:14) *Jesus Christ's death is here contrasted with the OT sacrificial system.*
