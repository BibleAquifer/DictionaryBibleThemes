## 5077: Abraham, character of (id: 5077)

Despite some shortcomings as a husband and father, his obedience, faith, hospitality and effective intercession were exemplary. Such qualities led to his being called “friend of God”.

Abraham, the friend of God
--------------------------

### He was blessed by God

**[GEN 14:18–14:19](https://ref.ly/Gen14:18-Gen14:19)** *See also* [GEN 21:22](https://ref.ly/Gen21:22), [GEN 24:1](https://ref.ly/Gen24:1), [GEN 24:35](https://ref.ly/Gen24:35)

### He worshipped God

**[GEN 21:33](https://ref.ly/Gen21:33)** *See also* [GEN 12:8](https://ref.ly/Gen12:8), [GEN 13:4](https://ref.ly/Gen13:4), [GEN 13:18](https://ref.ly/Gen13:18)

### He had faith in God

**[GEN 15:6](https://ref.ly/Gen15:6)** *See also* [ROM 4:18–4:22](https://ref.ly/Rom4:18-Rom4:22); [HEB 6:15](https://ref.ly/Heb6:15) *Abraham's faith is seen in the length of time (25 years) that he waited for the realisation of the promise*; [HEB 11:8–11:12](https://ref.ly/Heb11:8-Heb11:12), [HEB 11:17–11:19](https://ref.ly/Heb11:17-Heb11:19)

### He was obedient

**[GEN 22:1–22:19](https://ref.ly/Gen22:1-Gen22:19)** *See also* [GEN 12:4](https://ref.ly/Gen12:4), [GEN 26:5](https://ref.ly/Gen26:5); [JAS 2:20–2:22](https://ref.ly/Jas2:20-Jas2:22)

### He was a prophet and intercessor

**[GEN 20:7](https://ref.ly/Gen20:7)** *See also* [GEN 18:16–18:33](https://ref.ly/Gen18:16-Gen18:33) *As a prophet, God reveals his will to Abraham; as an intercessor, Abraham brings the revelation back to God and pleads for him to change his mind.*

### He was called the friend of God

**[JAS 2:23](https://ref.ly/Jas2:23)** *See also* [GEN 15:6](https://ref.ly/Gen15:6); [2CH 20:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:7); [ISA 41:8](https://ref.ly/Isa41:8)
