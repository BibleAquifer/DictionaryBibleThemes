## 7709: apostles, authority of (id: 7709)

Apostolic authority derives directly from Jesus Christ, who commissions apostles. It is rigorously upheld within the church.

Apostolic authority is based on divine commission
-------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 1:5](https://ref.ly/Rom1:5)** *See also* [ROM 1:1](https://ref.ly/Rom1:1); [2CO 1:1](https://ref.ly/2Cor1:1); [GAL 1:1](https://ref.ly/Gal1:1); [EPH 1:1](https://ref.ly/Eph1:1); [COL 1:1](https://ref.ly/Col1:1); [1TI 1:1](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:1); [2TI 1:1](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:1)
