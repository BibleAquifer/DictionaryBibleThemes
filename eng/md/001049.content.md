## 5476: property, nature of (id: 5476)

Possessions are a gift from God to be used for his glory, for human pleasure and in benefiting others.

Possessions are a cause for thanksgiving
----------------------------------------

**[ECC 5:19](https://ref.ly/Eccl5:19)** *See also* [GEN 39:2–39:4](https://ref.ly/Gen39:2-Gen39:4); [DEU 8:18](https://ref.ly/Deut8:18); [1CH 29:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:12); [2CH 31:21](https://ref.ly/2Chr31:21); [HOS 2:8](https://ref.ly/Hos2:8)
