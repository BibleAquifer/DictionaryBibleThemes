## 5548: speech, divine (id: 5548)

Scripture assumes and affirms that God has spoken to many different people throughout the ages in a variety of ways.

Speech as a synonym for revelation
----------------------------------

**[HEB 1:1–1:2](https://ref.ly/Heb1:1-Heb1:2)** *See also* [DEU 8:3](https://ref.ly/Deut8:3); [1SA 3:1](https://ref.ly/1Sam3:1), [1SA 3:7](https://ref.ly/1Sam3:7); [PSA 147:19](https://ref.ly/Ps147:19); [JHN 17:6–17:8](https://ref.ly/John17:6-John17:8), [JHN 17:14](https://ref.ly/John17:14); [1CO 2:6–2:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:6-1Cor2:10)
