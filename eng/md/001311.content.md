## 5789: anger (id: 5789)

A state of indignation and outrage, often resulting from distress caused by injustice or insult. Scripture affirms God's righteous anger against sin and urges moderation in regard to human anger.

* **Associated Articles:** 5790: anger, divine (ID: `5790@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 5791: anger, human (ID: `5791@DictionaryBibleThemes`)

* **Associated ACAI Concepts:** Hell (ID: `place:Hell`)
