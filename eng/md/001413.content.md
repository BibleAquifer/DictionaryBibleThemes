## 5895: intimacy (id: 5895)

Close relationships are part of God's will for human life. Scripture speaks of intimate relationships between Jesus Christ and the Father, between God and his people, and between various individuals.

Jesus Christ's intimate relationship with his Father
----------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 11:27](https://ref.ly/Matt11:27)** *See also* [MAT 3:17](https://ref.ly/Matt3:17) parallels [MRK 1:11](https://ref.ly/Mark1:11), [LUK 3:22](https://ref.ly/Luke3:22); [JHN 5:20](https://ref.ly/John5:20), [JHN 10:30](https://ref.ly/John10:30), [JHN 10:38](https://ref.ly/John10:38), [JHN 14:10–14:11](https://ref.ly/John14:10-John14:11), [JHN 17:21](https://ref.ly/John17:21)
