## 5230: beggars (id: 5230)

People in great need who appeal for help, both from others and from God.

Appeals for material help
-------------------------

**[2KI 6:26](https://ref.ly/2Kgs6:26)** *During a time of famine*; **[LUK 16:3](https://ref.ly/Luke16:3)** *See also* [2KI 8:3–8:5](https://ref.ly/2Kgs8:3-2Kgs8:5); [JOB 19:16](https://ref.ly/Job19:16), [JOB 24:4–24:5](https://ref.ly/Job24:4-Job24:5), [JOB 29:11–29:12](https://ref.ly/Job29:11-Job29:12); [PSA 37:25](https://ref.ly/Ps37:25) *never having to beg seen as God's blessing*; [PSA 109:10](https://ref.ly/Ps109:10) *begging seen as God's judgment*; [LAM 4:4](https://ref.ly/Lam4:4)

Appeals for mercy from others
-----------------------------

**[EST 8:3](https://ref.ly/Esth8:3)** *See also* [1SA 15:25](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:25); [2SA 14:4](https://ref.ly/2Sam14:4); [2KI 1:13](https://ref.ly/2Kgs1:13); [EST 4:8](https://ref.ly/Esth4:8), [EST 7:7](https://ref.ly/Esth7:7); [JOB 30:28](https://ref.ly/Job30:28); [MAT 18:26–18:32](https://ref.ly/Matt18:26-Matt18:32)

Care for the destitute commended
