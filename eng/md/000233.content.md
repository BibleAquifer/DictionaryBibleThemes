## 2545: Jesus Christ, opposition to (id: 2545)

Jesus Christ encountered criticism, grumbling and plotting because he was seen as a threat to the religious and political hierarchy, and because he exposed sin.

Opposition by different groups
------------------------------

### By family and friends

**[LUK 4:24–4:29](https://ref.ly/Luke4:24-Luke4:29)** *See also* [MAT 13:55–13:57](https://ref.ly/Matt13:55-Matt13:57) parallel [MRK 6:3–6:4](https://ref.ly/Mark6:3-Mark6:4); [MRK 3:21](https://ref.ly/Mark3:21); [JHN 7:3–7:5](https://ref.ly/John7:3-John7:5)
