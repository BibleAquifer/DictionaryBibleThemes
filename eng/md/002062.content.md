## 8218: consecration (id: 8218)

The setting apart of people, things, times or places as sacred, for God to use.

Consecration is the requirement of a holy God
---------------------------------------------

**[LEV 20:7–20:8](https://ref.ly/Lev20:7-Lev20:8)** *See also* [LEV 11:44](https://ref.ly/Lev11:44), [LEV 22:32](https://ref.ly/Lev22:32)
