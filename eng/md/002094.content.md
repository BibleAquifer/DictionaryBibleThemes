## 8257: fruitfulness, natural (id: 8257)

In the OT fruitfulness is often used as a way of referring to God's material and physical blessings.

God's blessing on creation generally
------------------------------------

**[GEN 1:11–1:12](https://ref.ly/Gen1:11-Gen1:12), [GEN 1:22](https://ref.ly/Gen1:22)** *See also* [GEN 8:17](https://ref.ly/Gen8:17)

Fruitfulness in the promised land
---------------------------------

**[LEV 25:18–25:19](https://ref.ly/Lev25:18-Lev25:19)** *God's blessing was seen in terms of material prosperity.* *See also* [LEV 26:3–26:4](https://ref.ly/Lev26:3-Lev26:4); [DEU 32:13](https://ref.ly/Deut32:13), [DEU 33:13–33:16](https://ref.ly/Deut33:13-Deut33:16); [EZK 34:27](https://ref.ly/Ezek34:27); [AMO 9:14](https://ref.ly/Amos9:14); [ZEC 8:12](https://ref.ly/Zech8:12)
