## 8307: moderation (id: 8307)

The classic virtue of temperate conduct, especially the avoidance of extremes. Scripture commends moderation as a way of life for Christians, while stressing the need for total commitment to God.

The ideal of moderation
-----------------------

**[ECC 7:16–7:18](https://ref.ly/Eccl7:16-Eccl7:18)**

Moderation in a legal context
-----------------------------

**[EXO 21:23–21:25](https://ref.ly/Exod21:23-Exod21:25)** *The purpose of this legislation was not to encourage vengeance but to moderate or restrain it.* *See also* [LEV 24:17–24:22](https://ref.ly/Lev24:17-Lev24:22); [NUM 35:9–35:15](https://ref.ly/Num35:9-Num35:15) parallels [DEU 4:41–4:43](https://ref.ly/Deut4:41-Deut4:43), [DEU 19:1–19:10](https://ref.ly/Deut19:1-Deut19:10) *One purpose of cities of refuge was to moderate the revenge of families of the murdered.*
