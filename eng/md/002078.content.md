## 8237: doctrine, false (id: 8237)

Teaching that distorts or contradicts the revealed truth of God, causing division and distress in the church by leading people away from the truth.

False doctrine deviates from the truth
--------------------------------------

### It contradicts the gospel of Jesus Christ

**[JUD 1:3–1:4](https://ref.ly/Jude1:3-Jude1:4)** *See also* [1CO 3:10–3:13](https://ref.ly/1Cor3:10-1Cor3:13); [2CO 11:3–11:4](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:3-2Cor11:4); [GAL 1:6–1:7](https://ref.ly/Gal1:6-Gal1:7); [1TI 6:3–6:4](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:3-1Tim6:4); [2JN 1:7](https://ref.ly/2John1:7)

### It may be of human origin

**[COL 2:8](https://ref.ly/Col2:8)** *See also* [COL 2:20–2:23](https://ref.ly/Col2:20-Col2:23); [MAT 15:8–15:9](https://ref.ly/Matt15:8-Matt15:9) parallel [MRK 7:6–7:7](https://ref.ly/Mark7:6-Mark7:7); [ISA 29:13](https://ref.ly/Isa29:13)

It may be of demonic origin
---------------------------

**[1TI 4:1–4:5](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:1-1Tim4:5)** *See also* [1JN 4:2–4:3](https://ref.ly/1John4:2-1John4:3)

The dangers of false doctrine
-----------------------------

### It leads to instability and confusion

**[EPH 4:14](https://ref.ly/Eph4:14)** *See also* [ACT 15:24](https://ref.ly/Acts15:24); [GAL 1:7](https://ref.ly/Gal1:7); [1TI 1:18–1:20](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:18-1Tim1:20); [2TI 2:16–2:18](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:16-2Tim2:18); [TIT 1:10–1:11](https://ref.ly/Titus1:10-Titus1:11); [HEB 13:9](https://ref.ly/Heb13:9); [2PE 3:17](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:17)

### It causes division

**[ROM 16:17–16:18](https://ref.ly/Rom16:17-Rom16:18)** *See also* [1TI 1:3–1:4](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:3-1Tim1:4)

### It leads to severe punishment

**[GAL 1:8–1:9](https://ref.ly/Gal1:8-Gal1:9)** *See also* [2CO 11:13–11:15](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:13-2Cor11:15); [2PE 2:1](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:1), [2PE 2:17](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:17); [REV 2:14–2:16](https://ref.ly/Rev2:14-Rev2:16)

False doctrine is to be dealt with by the church
------------------------------------------------

**[TIT 1:10–1:11](https://ref.ly/Titus1:10-Titus1:11)** *See also* [1TI 1:3](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:3); [TIT 1:13–1:14](https://ref.ly/Titus1:13-Titus1:14)
