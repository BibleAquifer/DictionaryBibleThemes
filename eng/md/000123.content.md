## 1614: Scripture, understanding (id: 1614)

God intends his word to be understood and has provided the directions and means for understanding it.

Scripture is intended to be clearly understood
----------------------------------------------

### It is accessible to ordinary people

**[PSA 119:130](https://ref.ly/Ps119:130)** *See also* [PSA 19:7](https://ref.ly/Ps19:7)

### Some sections are difficult to understand

**[2PE 3:15–3:16](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:15-2Pet3:16)** *See also* [HEB 5:11](https://ref.ly/Heb5:11)

God has prescribed the way to understand Scripture
--------------------------------------------------

### Through public reading

**[1TI 4:13](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:13)** *See also* [NEH 8:2–8:8](https://ref.ly/Neh8:2-Neh8:8), [NEH 13:1–13:3](https://ref.ly/Neh13:1-Neh13:3)

### Through diligent study

**[2TI 2:15](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:15)** *See also* [DEU 17:18–17:20](https://ref.ly/Deut17:18-Deut17:20); [ACT 17:11](https://ref.ly/Acts17:11)

### Through thoughtful meditation

**[PSA 1:1–1:3](https://ref.ly/Ps1:1-Ps1:3)** *See also* [JOS 1:8](https://ref.ly/Josh1:8); [JOB 23:12](https://ref.ly/Job23:12); [PSA 119:15](https://ref.ly/Ps119:15), [PSA 119:27](https://ref.ly/Ps119:27), [PSA 119:48](https://ref.ly/Ps119:48), [PSA 119:97](https://ref.ly/Ps119:97), [PSA 119:148](https://ref.ly/Ps119:148)
