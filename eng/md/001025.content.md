## 5447: poverty, causes of (id: 5447)

To want for the necessities of life is ultimately the result of human sin.

Poverty arises from human sin
-----------------------------

**[GEN 3:17–3:19](https://ref.ly/Gen3:17-Gen3:19)** *Compare this with the description of Eden in [Ge 2:9–14](https://ref.ly/Gen2:9-Gen2:14).*

Causes of poverty
-----------------

### Divine retribution brings poverty

**[DEU 28:47–28:48](https://ref.ly/Deut28:47-Deut28:48)** *See also* [PRO 22:16](https://ref.ly/Prov22:16); [ISA 3:1](https://ref.ly/Isa3:1); [AMO 4:6](https://ref.ly/Amos4:6), [AMO 5:11](https://ref.ly/Amos5:11)

### Idleness brings poverty

**[PRO 6:10–6:11](https://ref.ly/Prov6:10-Prov6:11)** *See also* [PRO 10:4](https://ref.ly/Prov10:4), [PRO 14:23](https://ref.ly/Prov14:23), [PRO 20:13](https://ref.ly/Prov20:13), [PRO 24:33–24:34](https://ref.ly/Prov24:33-Prov24:34)

### Dissolute living brings poverty

**[PRO 21:17](https://ref.ly/Prov21:17)** *See also* [PRO 6:26](https://ref.ly/Prov6:26), [PRO 28:19](https://ref.ly/Prov28:19); [LUK 15:14](https://ref.ly/Luke15:14)

### Lack of discipline brings poverty

[PRO 13:18](https://ref.ly/Prov13:18), [PRO 21:5](https://ref.ly/Prov21:5)

### Rash promises bring poverty

[PRO 22:26–22:27](https://ref.ly/Prov22:26-Prov22:27)

### Debt brings poverty

[MAT 18:23–18:25](https://ref.ly/Matt18:23-Matt18:25)

### Neglect of God's law of giving brings poverty

[PRO 11:24](https://ref.ly/Prov11:24), [PRO 28:22](https://ref.ly/Prov28:22); ; [ACT 20:35](https://ref.ly/Acts20:35)

### Neglect of God's work brings poverty

[HAG 1:9](https://ref.ly/Hag1:9)

### Oppression brings poverty

[JDG 6:6](https://ref.ly/Judg6:6); ; [JOB 20:19](https://ref.ly/Job20:19); ; [PRO 13:23](https://ref.ly/Prov13:23); ; [ISA 1:7](https://ref.ly/Isa1:7); ; [JAS 5:4](https://ref.ly/Jas5:4)

### Famine brings poverty

[GEN 45:11](https://ref.ly/Gen45:11), [GEN 47:20–47:21](https://ref.ly/Gen47:20-Gen47:21)

### Misfortune brings poverty

[JOB 1:13–1:21](https://ref.ly/Job1:13-Job1:21) *These trials were inflicted by Satan but allowed by God as a test of Job's faith.*
