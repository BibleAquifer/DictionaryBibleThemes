## 2428: gospel, descriptions of (id: 2428)

The beauty, authority and importance of the gospel can be seen from the way it is described in Scripture.

--------------

**[MAT 4:23](https://ref.ly/Matt4:23)** *See also* [MRK 1:14](https://ref.ly/Mark1:14), [MRK 16:15](https://ref.ly/Mark16:15); [LUK 2:10–2:11](https://ref.ly/Luke2:10-Luke2:11)

**[JHN 17:16–17:18](https://ref.ly/John17:16-John17:18)** *See also* [JHN 8:28](https://ref.ly/John8:28), [JHN 12:49–12:50](https://ref.ly/John12:49-John12:50), [JHN 14:10](https://ref.ly/John14:10), [JHN 14:24](https://ref.ly/John14:24)

**[JHN 6:63](https://ref.ly/John6:63)** *See also* [JHN 3:11–3:15](https://ref.ly/John3:11-John3:15)
