## 1466: vision (id: 1466)

Scripture uses the term to refer to both the physical act of seeing, and also the gift of spiritual insight. All things, physical and spiritual, are seen by God.

God's vision
------------

### God views all life on earth

**[2CH 16:9](https://ref.ly/2Chr16:9)**; **[PSA 11:4](https://ref.ly/Ps11:4)** *See also* [GEN 6:11](https://ref.ly/Gen6:11); [EXO 3:9](https://ref.ly/Exod3:9); [PSA 31:22](https://ref.ly/Ps31:22), [PSA 34:15](https://ref.ly/Ps34:15), [PSA 139:16](https://ref.ly/Ps139:16); [ZEC 4:10](https://ref.ly/Zech4:10)

### God sees the hearts and thoughts of people

**[GEN 6:5](https://ref.ly/Gen6:5)** *See also* [1SA 2:3](https://ref.ly/1Sam2:3); [PSA 139:1–139:4](https://ref.ly/Ps139:1-Ps139:4), [PSA 139:23–139:24](https://ref.ly/Ps139:23-Ps139:24); [PRO 15:11](https://ref.ly/Prov15:11); [JER 20:12](https://ref.ly/Jer20:12); [MAT 6:8](https://ref.ly/Matt6:8), [MAT 6:18](https://ref.ly/Matt6:18); [LUK 16:15](https://ref.ly/Luke16:15); [HEB 4:13](https://ref.ly/Heb4:13); [1JN 3:20](https://ref.ly/1John3:20)

The physical vision of human beings
-----------------------------------

### It is given and can be taken by God

**[EXO 4:11](https://ref.ly/Exod4:11)**; **[PSA 146:8](https://ref.ly/Ps146:8)** *See also* [LEV 26:16](https://ref.ly/Lev26:16); [PRO 29:13](https://ref.ly/Prov29:13); [ACT 9:8–9:9](https://ref.ly/Acts9:8-Acts9:9) parallel [ACT 22:11](https://ref.ly/Acts22:11)

### Jesus Christ restores the sight of the blind

**[LUK 4:18](https://ref.ly/Luke4:18)** *See also* [MAT 9:30](https://ref.ly/Matt9:30); [JHN 9:7](https://ref.ly/John9:7); [ACT 9:17–9:18](https://ref.ly/Acts9:17-Acts9:18) parallel [ACT 22:13](https://ref.ly/Acts22:13)

### Physical sight diminishes with age

[GEN 27:1](https://ref.ly/Gen27:1), [GEN 48:10](https://ref.ly/Gen48:10); ; [1SA 3:2](https://ref.ly/1Sam3:2), [1SA 4:15](https://ref.ly/1Sam4:15); ; [1KI 14:4](https://ref.ly/1Kgs14:4); ; [ECC 12:3](https://ref.ly/Eccl12:3) *[Ecc 12:2–5](https://ref.ly/Eccl12:2-Eccl12:5) is an allegory on ageing. It gives a graphic description of a person's progressive deterioration.*
