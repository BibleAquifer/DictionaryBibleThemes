## 8353: tolerance (id: 8353)

The virtue of being prepared to accept people and their viewpoints. While commended within the church, Scripture insists that believers must have a vital concern for maintaining and defending the truth.

Toleration should be exercised for the benefit of those whose faith is weak
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 14:1](https://ref.ly/Rom14:1)** *See also* [ROM 14:2–14:6](https://ref.ly/Rom14:2-Rom14:6), [ROM 14:13–14:22](https://ref.ly/Rom14:13-Rom14:22); [1CO 8:8–8:13](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:8-1Cor8:13)
