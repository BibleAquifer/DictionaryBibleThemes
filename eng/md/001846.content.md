## 7452: snake, bronze (id: 7452)

The means of salvation for Israel in the wilderness, a parallel to be found in Jesus Christ.

Healing and salvation resulted from looking at the bronze snake
---------------------------------------------------------------

**[NUM 21:8–21:9](https://ref.ly/Num21:8-Num21:9)** *See also* [NUM 21:4–21:7](https://ref.ly/Num21:4-Num21:7)
