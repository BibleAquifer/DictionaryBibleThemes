## 8612: prayer, and faith (id: 8612)

Effective prayer depends on faith, especially on a willingness to trust in God's faithfulness to his promises to his people.

Faith is necessary in order to approach God
-------------------------------------------

**[HEB 11:6](https://ref.ly/Heb11:6)**

Faith is necessary to receive benefits from God
-----------------------------------------------

**[MRK 6:5–6:6](https://ref.ly/Mark6:5-Mark6:6)**; **[JAS 5:16–5:18](https://ref.ly/Jas5:16-Jas5:18)** *See also* [EPH 3:12](https://ref.ly/Eph3:12); [HEB 10:22](https://ref.ly/Heb10:22)

Faith is necessary for effective prayer
---------------------------------------

**[MAT 21:21–21:22](https://ref.ly/Matt21:21-Matt21:22)** parallel [MRK 11:22–11:24](https://ref.ly/Mark11:22-Mark11:24)**[JAS 1:5–1:8](https://ref.ly/Jas1:5-Jas1:8)** *See also* [JAS 5:14–5:15](https://ref.ly/Jas5:14-Jas5:15)
