## 5264: compensation (id: 5264)

Compensation under the law of Moses
-----------------------------------

### For personal injuries

**[EXO 21:26–21:27](https://ref.ly/Exod21:26-Exod21:27)** *See also* [EXO 21:32](https://ref.ly/Exod21:32)

### For the loss of an animal

**[EXO 21:33–21:34](https://ref.ly/Exod21:33-Exod21:34)** *See also* [EXO 21:35–21:36](https://ref.ly/Exod21:35-Exod21:36)

### For theft

**[EXO 22:1](https://ref.ly/Exod22:1), [EXO 22:3–22:4](https://ref.ly/Exod22:3-Exod22:4)** *See also* [EXO 22:7–22:9](https://ref.ly/Exod22:7-Exod22:9)

### For damage to property

[EXO 22:5–22:6](https://ref.ly/Exod22:5-Exod22:6)

### For breach of trust

[EXO 22:10–22:15](https://ref.ly/Exod22:10-Exod22:15); ; [LEV 6:1–6:7](https://ref.ly/Lev6:1-Lev6:7); ; [NUM 5:5–5:7](https://ref.ly/Num5:5-Num5:7) *Compensation was only paid when someone was at fault. Similar laws apparently existed in patriarchal times: see [Ge 31:39](https://ref.ly/Gen31:39).*

Compensation later in the OT

### The law applied by David

[2SA 12:4–12:6](https://ref.ly/2Sam12:4-2Sam12:6)

### Sevenfold compensation for theft

[PRO 6:30–6:31](https://ref.ly/Prov6:30-Prov6:31) *presumably “sevenfold” here means “manifold”; he will pay in full*

### Adultery beyond compensation

[PRO 6:32–6:35](https://ref.ly/Prov6:32-Prov6:35)

[EZK 33:15](https://ref.ly/Ezek33:15)

Compensation in the NT
----------------------

### For losses inflicted

**[LUK 19:8](https://ref.ly/Luke19:8)** *In his zeal, after being converted, Zacchaeus goes beyond the requirements of the law.* *See also* [PHM 1:18–1:19](https://ref.ly/Phlm1:18-Phlm1:19)

### For losses sustained for the gospel

**[MAT 19:29](https://ref.ly/Matt19:29)** parallel [LUK 18:29–18:30](https://ref.ly/Luke18:29-Luke18:30) *God is far more generous under grace than he required wrongdoers to be under law.*
