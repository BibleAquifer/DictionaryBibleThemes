## 7332: child sacrifice (id: 7332)

Child sacrifice is unconditionally condemned in Scripture. The few cases in which it is known to have taken place are probably due to the influence of Israel's pagan neighbours.

Laws forbidding child sacrifice
-------------------------------

**[LEV 18:21](https://ref.ly/Lev18:21)** *Molech was the god of the Ammonites.* *See also* [EXO 22:29](https://ref.ly/Exod22:29) *to be interpreted with reference to [Ex 13:12–16](https://ref.ly/Exod13:12-Exod13:16) and [Ex 34:19,22](https://ref.ly/Exod34:19,Exod34:22)*; [DEU 18:10](https://ref.ly/Deut18:10)

Instances of child sacrifice
----------------------------

[2KI 3:27](https://ref.ly/2Kgs3:27), [2KI 16:3](https://ref.ly/2Kgs16:3) parallel [2CH 28:3](https://ref.ly/2Chr28:3); ; [2KI 17:17](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:17), [2KI 17:31](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:31), [2KI 21:6](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:6) parallel [2CH 33:6](https://ref.ly/2Chr33:6); ; [2KI 23:10](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:10) *Josiah's reforms involved desecrating Topheth, where child sacrifices were offered*; ; [ISA 57:5](https://ref.ly/Isa57:5); ; [JER 7:31](https://ref.ly/Jer7:31)

Arguments against child sacrifice
---------------------------------

### Child sacrifice is the idolatrous practice of the nations

**[DEU 12:31](https://ref.ly/Deut12:31)** *See also* [LEV 20:2–20:5](https://ref.ly/Lev20:2-Lev20:5); [PSA 106:35–106:38](https://ref.ly/Ps106:35-Ps106:38); [JER 32:35](https://ref.ly/Jer32:35); [HOS 13:2](https://ref.ly/Hos13:2)

### It defiles God's people

**[EZK 20:26](https://ref.ly/Ezek20:26)** *See also* [EZK 20:31](https://ref.ly/Ezek20:31)

### It defiles God's sanctuary

[EZK 23:37](https://ref.ly/Ezek23:37), [EZK 23:39](https://ref.ly/Ezek23:39)

### It incurs God's anger

[JER 19:4–19:5](https://ref.ly/Jer19:4-Jer19:5); ; [EZK 16:20–16:21](https://ref.ly/Ezek16:20-Ezek16:21), [EZK 16:36–16:37](https://ref.ly/Ezek16:36-Ezek16:37); ; [HOS 13:2](https://ref.ly/Hos13:2)
