## 7480: wave offering (id: 7480)

A gift specially dedicated to God. The term itself may not be intended to convey a particular action.

The wave offering was part of every fellowship offering
-------------------------------------------------------

**[LEV 7:34](https://ref.ly/Lev7:34)** *Although given to the priests, the offering belonged to the LORD.* *See also* [LEV 7:28–7:32](https://ref.ly/Lev7:28-Lev7:32), [LEV 9:21](https://ref.ly/Lev9:21)

The wave offering was eaten by the priests
------------------------------------------

[EXO 29:26–29:28](https://ref.ly/Exod29:26-Exod29:28); ; [LEV 10:15](https://ref.ly/Lev10:15); ; [NUM 18:11](https://ref.ly/Num18:11), [NUM 18:18](https://ref.ly/Num18:18)

Occasions for making a wave offering
------------------------------------

### As a cereal offering when priests were consecrated

[EXO 29:23–29:24](https://ref.ly/Exod29:23-Exod29:24); ; [LEV 8:27–8:29](https://ref.ly/Lev8:27-Lev8:29)

### As a guilt offering sacrificed at a ceremonial cleansing

[LEV 14:12](https://ref.ly/Lev14:12), [LEV 14:21](https://ref.ly/Lev14:21), [LEV 14:24](https://ref.ly/Lev14:24)

### As part of the ritual when a woman was accused of adultery

[NUM 5:25](https://ref.ly/Num5:25)

### When celebrating harvest

[LEV 23:10–23:11](https://ref.ly/Lev23:10-Lev23:11) *the firstfruits of the harvest*; [LEV 23:15–23:17](https://ref.ly/Lev23:15-Lev23:17), [LEV 23:20](https://ref.ly/Lev23:20)

### When presenting a gift to God

Materials given for constructing the tabernacle: [EXO 35:22](https://ref.ly/Exod35:22), [EXO 38:24](https://ref.ly/Exod38:24), [EXO 38:29](https://ref.ly/Exod38:29)

### When dedicating the Levites to God

**[NUM 8:11](https://ref.ly/Num8:11)** *The dedication to God of the Levites symbolised the consecration of the whole nation.* *See also* [NUM 8:13](https://ref.ly/Num8:13), [NUM 8:15](https://ref.ly/Num8:15), [NUM 8:21](https://ref.ly/Num8:21)
