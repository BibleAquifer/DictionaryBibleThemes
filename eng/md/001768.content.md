## 7316: blood, and OT sacrifices (id: 7316)

The pouring out of animals' blood in sacrifice was God's provision, under the old covenant, for the atonement of sin.

God's provision of blood sacrifice to establish the old covenant
----------------------------------------------------------------

**[EXO 24:4–24:8](https://ref.ly/Exod24:4-Exod24:8)** *See also* [HEB 9:18–9:20](https://ref.ly/Heb9:18-Heb9:20)

God's provision of blood sacrifices within the old covenant
-----------------------------------------------------------

### Blood sacrifices and the priesthood

[EXO 29:10–29:21](https://ref.ly/Exod29:10-Exod29:21) parallel [LEV 8:14–8:24](https://ref.ly/Lev8:14-Lev8:24); ; [LEV 9:8–9:14](https://ref.ly/Lev9:8-Lev9:14), [LEV 16:1–16:14](https://ref.ly/Lev16:1-Lev16:14)

### Blood sacrifices and various offerings

[LEV 1:1–1:17](https://ref.ly/Lev1:1-Lev1:17) *the burnt offering, to gain favour or express devotion*; ; [LEV 3:1–3:17](https://ref.ly/Lev3:1-Lev3:17) *the fellowship, or peace, offering, to express gratitude*; ; [LEV 4:1–4:35](https://ref.ly/Lev4:1-Lev4:35) *the sin offering, to gain forgiveness*; ; [LEV 5:14–5:19](https://ref.ly/Lev5:14-Lev5:19) *the guilt offering, where restitution was required*; ; [LEV 16:15–16:22](https://ref.ly/Lev16:15-Lev16:22) *the Day of Atonement, for the cleansing of the nation from unintentional sin*

### Blood sacrifices and the Passover

**[EXO 12:1–12:14](https://ref.ly/Exod12:1-Exod12:14)** *See also* [2CH 30:15–30:20](https://ref.ly/2Chr30:15-2Chr30:20), [2CH 35:1–35:19](https://ref.ly/2Chr35:1-2Chr35:19); [EZR 6:19–6:21](https://ref.ly/Ezra6:19-Ezra6:21); [MRK 14:12](https://ref.ly/Mark14:12) parallel [LUK 22:7–22:8](https://ref.ly/Luke22:7-Luke22:8)

Blood sacrifices must come from animals without defect

Blood sacrifices must not come from human beings
------------------------------------------------
