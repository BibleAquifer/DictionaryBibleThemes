## 4175: mediums (id: 4175)

People who claim to be channels through whom the spirits of the dead speak to the living.

God's people must not be mediums
--------------------------------

**[DEU 18:10–18:12](https://ref.ly/Deut18:10-Deut18:12)**

God's people must not consult mediums
-------------------------------------

**[ISA 8:19–8:20](https://ref.ly/Isa8:19-Isa8:20)** *See also* [LEV 19:31](https://ref.ly/Lev19:31), [LEV 20:6](https://ref.ly/Lev20:6); [2KI 23:24](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:24); [JER 27:9](https://ref.ly/Jer27:9)
