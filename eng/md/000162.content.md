## 2066: Jesus Christ, power of (id: 2066)

The historical and ongoing effective authority of Jesus Christ over natural elements, over human life and ultimately over sin, forces of evil, Satan and death.

--------------------------------

**[ACT 10:38](https://ref.ly/Acts10:38)**

### God's creative power is exercised through Jesus Christ

**[JHN 1:3](https://ref.ly/John1:3)** *See also* [1CO 8:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:6); [COL 1:16](https://ref.ly/Col1:16); [HEB 1:2](https://ref.ly/Heb1:2)
