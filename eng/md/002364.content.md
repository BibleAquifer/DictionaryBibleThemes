## 8733: envy (id: 8733)

A desire for another's gifts, possessions, position or achievements, closely associated with jealousy.

Causes of envy
--------------

### Envy is the result of human sin

**[GAL 5:19–5:21](https://ref.ly/Gal5:19-Gal5:21)** *See also* [PRO 23:17](https://ref.ly/Prov23:17); [MAT 15:19–15:20](https://ref.ly/Matt15:19-Matt15:20) parallel [MRK 7:21–7:23](https://ref.ly/Mark7:21-Mark7:23); [ROM 1:29](https://ref.ly/Rom1:29); [1CO 3:3](https://ref.ly/1Cor3:3); [1TI 6:4](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:4); [TIT 3:3](https://ref.ly/Titus3:3); [1PE 2:1–2:2](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:1-1Pet2:2)
