## 8260: generosity (id: 8260)

The free and liberal bestowal of wealth, possessions or food upon others. The generosity of God is shown in his free bestowal of grace upon undeserving sinners.

* **Associated Articles:** 8261: generosity, God's (ID: `8261@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 8262: generosity, human (ID: `8262@DictionaryBibleThemes`)

* **Associated ACAI Concepts:** Shammah (ID: `person:Shammah.4`)
