## 7796: teaching (id: 7796)

The act of instructing someone in matters of faith and morals, especially in the home or church. Teaching was a major aspect of the ministry of Jesus Christ.

* **Associated Articles:** 7797: teaching (ID: `7797@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 7798: teaching, of Jesus Christ (ID: `7798@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
