## 6260: uncircumcised (id: 6260)

Those who are outside the covenant of God.

Circumcision marked the covenant people of God
----------------------------------------------

### God's covenant with Abraham

**[GEN 17:10–17:12](https://ref.ly/Gen17:10-Gen17:12), [GEN 17:14](https://ref.ly/Gen17:14)** *See also* [JOS 5:6–5:7](https://ref.ly/Josh5:6-Josh5:7)

### Uncircumcised aliens participating in Jewish religious practices had first to be circumcised

[EXO 12:48](https://ref.ly/Exod12:48); ; [EZK 44:6–44:7](https://ref.ly/Ezek44:6-Ezek44:7), [EZK 44:9](https://ref.ly/Ezek44:9); ; [ACT 11:3](https://ref.ly/Acts11:3), [ACT 21:21](https://ref.ly/Acts21:21)
