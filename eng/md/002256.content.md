## 8472: respect, for environment (id: 8472)

Although God created the universe and retains ultimate ownership of it, he has delegated responsibility for the well\-being of the earth to the human race. This stewardship includes the care of the animal kingdom and the earth's natural resources.

God's creation is to be respected
