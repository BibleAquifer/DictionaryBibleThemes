## 5923: public opinion (id: 5923)

The concepts and views held by the majority of the population. Scripture urges personal, independent thinking, based on God's word, rather than adherence to popular beliefs.

God's people should not be concerned about public opinion
---------------------------------------------------------

**[EXO 23:2](https://ref.ly/Exod23:2)** *See also* [JOB 31:33–31:34](https://ref.ly/Job31:33-Job31:34); [ECC 7:21–7:22](https://ref.ly/Eccl7:21-Eccl7:22)

Examples of the impact of public opinion
----------------------------------------

[1SA 14:45](https://ref.ly/1Sam14:45) *Public opinion saved Jonathan's life*; ; [1SA 15:24](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:24) *Public opinion led to Saul losing his kingdom*; ; [MAT 27:15–27:26](https://ref.ly/Matt27:15-Matt27:26) *Public opinion led to Jesus Christ being crucified*; ; [ACT 12:3](https://ref.ly/Acts12:3) *Public opinion led to Peter being imprisoned.*

Public opinion is often contrary to divine standards
----------------------------------------------------

**[HAG 1:2–1:11](https://ref.ly/Hag1:2-Hag1:11)** *See also* [NUM 13:21–14:10](https://ref.ly/Num13:21-Num14:10); [DEU 1:19–1:40](https://ref.ly/Deut1:19-Deut1:40); [1SA 8:4–8:9](https://ref.ly/1Sam8:4-1Sam8:9); [1KI 18:21](https://ref.ly/1Kgs18:21); [JER 2:31–2:35](https://ref.ly/Jer2:31-Jer2:35), [JER 26:7–26:16](https://ref.ly/Jer26:7-Jer26:16)

Public opinion is sometimes in accord with divine standards
-----------------------------------------------------------

**[DEU 5:28–5:29](https://ref.ly/Deut5:28-Deut5:29)** *See also* [JOS 24:19–24:24](https://ref.ly/Josh24:19-Josh24:24)
