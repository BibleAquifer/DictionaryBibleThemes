## 9024: death, spiritual (id: 9024)

A condition in this life of alienation from God because of sin. It leads ultimately to the “second death”, the permanent separation of unbelievers from God.

Spiritual death means alienation from God in this life

**[EPH 2:1–2:3](https://ref.ly/Eph2:1-Eph2:3)**; **[COL 1:21](https://ref.ly/Col1:21)** *See also* [MAT 8:22](https://ref.ly/Matt8:22); [ROM 6:23](https://ref.ly/Rom6:23); [COL 2:13](https://ref.ly/Col2:13)
