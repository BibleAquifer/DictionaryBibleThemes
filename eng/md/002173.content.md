## 8348: spiritual growth, nature of (id: 8348)

Having given spiritual life to his people, God expects them to grow to maturity.

God desires the spiritual growth of his people
----------------------------------------------

**[MAT 5:48](https://ref.ly/Matt5:48)**; **[HEB 6:1](https://ref.ly/Heb6:1)** *See also* [2CO 13:9–13:11](https://ref.ly/2Cor13:9-2Cor13:11); [EPH 1:4](https://ref.ly/Eph1:4), [EPH 2:10](https://ref.ly/Eph2:10), [EPH 3:17–3:19](https://ref.ly/Eph3:17-Eph3:19); [PHP 3:12](https://ref.ly/Phil3:12); [1TH 4:1](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:1), [1TH 4:7](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:7); [2TI 1:9](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:9)
