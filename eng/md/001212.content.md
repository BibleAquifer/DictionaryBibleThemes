## 5665: children, attitudes towards (id: 5665)

Christians must welcome children as Jesus Christ did. They are used by Christ as an example of how believers should receive the kingdom, and as an example of immaturity by Paul.

Children as a gift from God
---------------------------

**[PSA 113:9](https://ref.ly/Ps113:9), [PSA 127:3](https://ref.ly/Ps127:3)** *See also* [GEN 33:5](https://ref.ly/Gen33:5), [GEN 48:9](https://ref.ly/Gen48:9); [JOS 24:3](https://ref.ly/Josh24:3)

------------------------------

**[MAT 19:13–19:14](https://ref.ly/Matt19:13-Matt19:14)** parallels [MRK 10:13–10:16](https://ref.ly/Mark10:13-Mark10:16), [LUK 18:15–18:17](https://ref.ly/Luke18:15-Luke18:17)
