## 1085: God, love of (id: 1085)

The deepest possible expression of God's character. Though God loves all people, he is especially committed to sacrificial, loyal relationships with his people.

God's nature is love
--------------------

**[1JN 4:8](https://ref.ly/1John4:8)** *See also* [1JN 4:16](https://ref.ly/1John4:16)

### His love is perfectly expressed within the Trinity

**[MRK 1:10–1:11](https://ref.ly/Mark1:10-Mark1:11)** parallels [MAT 3:16–3:17](https://ref.ly/Matt3:16-Matt3:17), [LUK 3:21–3:22](https://ref.ly/Luke3:21-Luke3:22) *See also* [JHN 5:20](https://ref.ly/John5:20), [JHN 10:17](https://ref.ly/John10:17), [JHN 14:23](https://ref.ly/John14:23); [EPH 1:6](https://ref.ly/Eph1:6); [COL 1:13](https://ref.ly/Col1:13)
