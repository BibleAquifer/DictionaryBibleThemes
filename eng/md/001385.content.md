## 5866: gluttony (id: 5866)

Scripture condemns an excessive greed for food. Its unrestrained self\-indulgence often leads to deeper iniquity and represents a rejection of godly moderation.

Gluttony is a form of idolatry and therefore condemned as offensive to God
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[EPH 5:5](https://ref.ly/Eph5:5)**; **[PHP 3:19](https://ref.ly/Phil3:19)** *See also* [PSA 10:3](https://ref.ly/Ps10:3); [ISA 22:12–22:14](https://ref.ly/Isa22:12-Isa22:14) *Gluttony as escapism, trying to avoid God's requirements*; [ZEC 7:4–7:6](https://ref.ly/Zech7:4-Zech7:6) *Even their religious feasts were nothing but self\-indulgence*; [MRK 7:21–7:23](https://ref.ly/Mark7:21-Mark7:23); [1CO 6:9–6:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:9-1Cor6:10), [1CO 10:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:7); [COL 3:5](https://ref.ly/Col3:5); [JAS 5:5](https://ref.ly/Jas5:5)
