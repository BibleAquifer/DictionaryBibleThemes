## 5824: cruelty, examples of (id: 5824)

Scripture condemns cruelty, and gives examples of situations in which it was practised.

Cruelty is an expression of wickedness
--------------------------------------

**[PSA 71:4](https://ref.ly/Ps71:4)**; **[ROM 1:28–1:31](https://ref.ly/Rom1:28-Rom1:31)** *See also* [PSA 35:16](https://ref.ly/Ps35:16), [PSA 86:14](https://ref.ly/Ps86:14); [PRO 17:5](https://ref.ly/Prov17:5); [ECC 4:1](https://ref.ly/Eccl4:1); [AMO 5:12](https://ref.ly/Amos5:12)
