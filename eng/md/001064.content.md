## 5493: retribution (id: 5493)

Recompense for evil. In Scripture this is the prerogative of God and of the authorities ordained by God, and never of individuals.

The principle of retribution
----------------------------

**[EXO 20:5–20:6](https://ref.ly/Exod20:5-Exod20:6)**; **[ROM 12:19](https://ref.ly/Rom12:19)**; **[HEB 10:30](https://ref.ly/Heb10:30)** *See also* [DEU 32:35](https://ref.ly/Deut32:35); [EXO 32:33–32:34](https://ref.ly/Exod32:33-Exod32:34); [LEV 26:23–26:25](https://ref.ly/Lev26:23-Lev26:25); [DEU 7:9–7:10](https://ref.ly/Deut7:9-Deut7:10), [DEU 32:41–32:43](https://ref.ly/Deut32:41-Deut32:43); [JOB 34:11](https://ref.ly/Job34:11); [PRO 24:12](https://ref.ly/Prov24:12); [ISA 1:24](https://ref.ly/Isa1:24); [NAM 1:2](https://ref.ly/Nah1:2)

Retribution is placed in the hands of governments
-------------------------------------------------

**[GEN 9:6](https://ref.ly/Gen9:6)**; **[EXO 21:23–21:25](https://ref.ly/Exod21:23-Exod21:25)** *This “lex talionis” (law of exacting like for like) was not for private retribution. Prayer for retribution

**[PSA 28:4](https://ref.ly/Ps28:4)** *Christians can agree with these prayers, hating all that opposes the Lord and goodness, and gladly anticipating the ultimate vindication of God's glory. Christians leave judgment in God's hands, but nonetheless grieve over the world's pain and over the death of every unrepentant sinner*; **[2TH 1:6–1:10](https://ref.ly/2Thess1:6-2Thess1:10)** *See also* [PSA 79:10](https://ref.ly/Ps79:10), [PSA 94:1–94:2](https://ref.ly/Ps94:1-Ps94:2), [PSA 137:8–137:9](https://ref.ly/Ps137:8-Ps137:9); [LAM 3:64–3:66](https://ref.ly/Lam3:64-Lam3:66)
