## 1150: God, truth of (id: 1150)

God's unique integrity is displayed in his perfection of character and attributes. It is further emphasised by the consistent truth of his words and works, supremely shown in Jesus Christ.

God is the one and only true God
--------------------------------

### He alone is God

**[JER 10:10](https://ref.ly/Jer10:10)** *See also* [DEU 4:39](https://ref.ly/Deut4:39); [2CH 15:3](https://ref.ly/2Chr15:3); [ISA 43:10–43:11](https://ref.ly/Isa43:10-Isa43:11), [ISA 45:5–45:6](https://ref.ly/Isa45:5-Isa45:6), [ISA 45:14](https://ref.ly/Isa45:14), [ISA 45:21](https://ref.ly/Isa45:21); [ZEC 14:9](https://ref.ly/Zech14:9); [JHN 1:18](https://ref.ly/John1:18), [JHN 17:3](https://ref.ly/John17:3); [REV 6:10](https://ref.ly/Rev6:10)
