## 7760: preachers, responsibilities of (id: 7760)

Those entrusted with the responsibility of preaching are to discharge it faithfully, boldly and persistently in the power of the Holy Spirit. The Christian community should honour faithful preachers and challenge those who fail to honour their responsibility.

The responsibilities of preachers
---------------------------------

### They must be diligent in their preaching

**[2TI 2:15](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:15)** *See also* [1TI 4:13–4:16](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:13-1Tim4:16); [2TI 4:1–4:5](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:1-2Tim4:5)

### They must be faithful in their preaching

**[ACT 20:20](https://ref.ly/Acts20:20)** *See also* [JER 26:1–26:2](https://ref.ly/Jer26:1-Jer26:2), [JER 42:4](https://ref.ly/Jer42:4); [ACT 20:25–20:27](https://ref.ly/Acts20:25-Acts20:27)

### They must be persistent in their preaching

**[2TI 4:1–4:5](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:1-2Tim4:5)** *See also* [ACT 18:4–18:6](https://ref.ly/Acts18:4-Acts18:6), [ACT 20:31](https://ref.ly/Acts20:31); [1TH 2:1–2:2](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:1-1Thess2:2)

### They must be bold in their preaching

**[ACT 28:31](https://ref.ly/Acts28:31)** *See also* [JER 26:7–26:15](https://ref.ly/Jer26:7-Jer26:15); [AMO 7:10–7:17](https://ref.ly/Amos7:10-Amos7:17); [ACT 4:18–4:20](https://ref.ly/Acts4:18-Acts4:20), [ACT 5:27–5:29](https://ref.ly/Acts5:27-Acts5:29), [ACT 14:1–14:3](https://ref.ly/Acts14:1-Acts14:3)

### They must be encouraging in their preaching

**[ACT 14:21–14:22](https://ref.ly/Acts14:21-Acts14:22)** *See also* [ACT 15:32](https://ref.ly/Acts15:32); [1CO 14:3–14:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor14:3-1Cor14:5); [1TH 2:11–2:12](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:11-1Thess2:12); [2TI 4:2](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:2)

### They must be filled with the Holy Spirit

**[ACT 1:8](https://ref.ly/Acts1:8)**; **[1CO 2:4](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:4)** *See also* [ACT 4:31–4:33](https://ref.ly/Acts4:31-Acts4:33); [1TH 1:5](https://ref.ly/1Thess1:5); [1PE 1:12](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:12)

### They must be compassionate in their preaching

**[MRK 6:34](https://ref.ly/Mark6:34)** *See also* [MAT 9:35–9:38](https://ref.ly/Matt9:35-Matt9:38)

The responsibilities of the church towards preachers

**[1TI 5:17](https://ref.ly/1Tim5:17)** *See also* [MAT 13:53–13:57](https://ref.ly/Matt13:53-Matt13:57) parallel [MRK 6:2–6:4](https://ref.ly/Mark6:2-Mark6:4); [LUK 10:3–10:8](https://ref.ly/Luke10:3-Luke10:8); [HEB 13:7](https://ref.ly/Heb13:7)
