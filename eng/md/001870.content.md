## 7515: anti-semitism (id: 7515)

A modern term, used to refer to a hatred of Jews or Arabs. Traces of anti\-semitism may be discerned in biblical times.

The development of anti\-semitism
---------------------------------

**[EXO 1:8–1:14](https://ref.ly/Exod1:8-Exod1:14)** *Strictly speaking, this hostility towards the Israelites in Egypt after the death of Joseph was not anti\-semitism as this word is now understood.* *See also* [EXO 1:15–1:16](https://ref.ly/Exod1:15-Exod1:16), [EXO 1:22](https://ref.ly/Exod1:22), [EXO 3:9](https://ref.ly/Exod3:9), [EXO 5:6–5:9](https://ref.ly/Exod5:6-Exod5:9)
