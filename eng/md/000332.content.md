## 4125: Satan, agents of (id: 4125)

Satan works in this world through people and through those spiritual beings who acknowledge his authority. His agents oppose God's will and purposes.

Satan works through people
--------------------------

### Satan's human agents pursue his evil desires

**[JHN 8:44](https://ref.ly/John8:44)** *Jesus Christ speaking of the relationship of some unbelieving Jews to Satan.* *See also* [ACT 13:10](https://ref.ly/Acts13:10); [1JN 3:8–3:10](https://ref.ly/1John3:8-1John3:10), [1JN 3:12](https://ref.ly/1John3:12)

### Satan works through people to oppose God's purposes

[ACT 5:1–5:9](https://ref.ly/Acts5:1-Acts5:9) *Ananias and Sapphira*; ; [MAT 16:23](https://ref.ly/Matt16:23) parallel [MRK 8:33](https://ref.ly/Mark8:33) *Simon Peter*; [LUK 22:3](https://ref.ly/Luke22:3); ; [JHN 6:70](https://ref.ly/John6:70), [JHN 13:2](https://ref.ly/John13:2), [JHN 13:27](https://ref.ly/John13:27); [ACT 13:8–13:10](https://ref.ly/Acts13:8-Acts13:10) *Elymas, the sorcerer*
