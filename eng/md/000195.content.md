## 2345: Jesus Christ, kingdom of (id: 2345)

The present and future realm in which Jesus Christ exercises full authority, and through which he triumphs over all opposition.

Jesus Christ's kingdom is a heavenly kingdom
--------------------------------------------

### It is not of this world

**[JHN 18:36](https://ref.ly/John18:36)** *See also* [REV 5:6](https://ref.ly/Rev5:6), [REV 7:10](https://ref.ly/Rev7:10), [REV 21:1](https://ref.ly/Rev21:1), [REV 21:3](https://ref.ly/Rev21:3)

### The kingdom was given to him by God

**[DAN 7:14](https://ref.ly/Dan7:14)** *See also* [PSA 72:1](https://ref.ly/Ps72:1); [DAN 2:44](https://ref.ly/Dan2:44); [MAT 28:18](https://ref.ly/Matt28:18); [JHN 16:15](https://ref.ly/John16:15)

### The kingdom is his by right

**[COL 1:15–1:16](https://ref.ly/Col1:15-Col1:16)** *See also* [HEB 1:8](https://ref.ly/Heb1:8); [PSA 45:6](https://ref.ly/Ps45:6); [REV 22:13](https://ref.ly/Rev22:13)

Jesus Christ inherits the kingdom promised to David
---------------------------------------------------

**[LUK 1:32](https://ref.ly/Luke1:32)** *See also* [ROM 15:12](https://ref.ly/Rom15:12); [ISA 11:10](https://ref.ly/Isa11:10); [REV 2:27](https://ref.ly/Rev2:27); [PSA 2:9](https://ref.ly/Ps2:9)

Jesus Christ reveals God's kingdom on earth
-------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ brought in God's kingdom

**[MAT 4:17](https://ref.ly/Matt4:17)** *The expressions “kingdom of heaven” and the “kingdom of God” are interchangeable.* *See also* [MAT 3:2](https://ref.ly/Matt3:2), [MAT 10:7](https://ref.ly/Matt10:7), [MAT 12:28](https://ref.ly/Matt12:28) parallel [LUK 11:20](https://ref.ly/Luke11:20); [MRK 1:15](https://ref.ly/Mark1:15); [LUK 17:20–17:21](https://ref.ly/Luke17:20-Luke17:21)

### Miracles are a sign of the kingdom

**[JHN 6:14–6:15](https://ref.ly/John6:14-John6:15)** *See also* [MAT 8:26–8:27](https://ref.ly/Matt8:26-Matt8:27) parallels [MRK 4:39–4:41](https://ref.ly/Mark4:39-Mark4:41), [LUK 8:24–8:25](https://ref.ly/Luke8:24-Luke8:25) *authority over the created world*; [MAT 11:2–11:5](https://ref.ly/Matt11:2-Matt11:5) parallel [LUK 7:19–7:22](https://ref.ly/Luke7:19-Luke7:22) *authority to heal*[LUK 7:11–7:15](https://ref.ly/Luke7:11-Luke7:15); [REV 1:17–1:18](https://ref.ly/Rev1:17-Rev1:18)

### Parables are a sign of the kingdom

**[MAT 13:1–13:52](https://ref.ly/Matt13:1-Matt13:52)** *See also* [MRK 4:1–4:34](https://ref.ly/Mark4:1-Mark4:34); [LUK 8:4–8:15](https://ref.ly/Luke8:4-Luke8:15), [LUK 13:18–13:21](https://ref.ly/Luke13:18-Luke13:21)

Jesus Christ exercises kingdom authority today
----------------------------------------------

### Believers enter Jesus Christ's kingdom immediately

**[LUK 23:42–23:43](https://ref.ly/Luke23:42-Luke23:43)** *See also* [MAT 5:3](https://ref.ly/Matt5:3) parallel [LUK 6:20](https://ref.ly/Luke6:20); [MAT 11:11](https://ref.ly/Matt11:11)

### Believers are redeemed from the kingdom of darkness

**[COL 1:12–1:13](https://ref.ly/Col1:12-Col1:13)** *See also* [EPH 5:5](https://ref.ly/Eph5:5)

### Believers enjoy the blessings of Jesus Christ's kingdom now

**[MAT 16:19](https://ref.ly/Matt16:19)** *See also* [DAN 7:22](https://ref.ly/Dan7:22), [DAN 7:27](https://ref.ly/Dan7:27); [LUK 12:32](https://ref.ly/Luke12:32), [LUK 22:29–22:30](https://ref.ly/Luke22:29-Luke22:30); [JAS 2:5](https://ref.ly/Jas2:5); [REV 1:5–1:6](https://ref.ly/Rev1:5-Rev1:6), [REV 1:9](https://ref.ly/Rev1:9), [REV 3:21](https://ref.ly/Rev3:21)

### Jesus Christ rules over every authority now

**[1PE 3:22](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:22)** *See also* [EPH 1:20–1:23](https://ref.ly/Eph1:20-Eph1:23); [PHP 2:9](https://ref.ly/Phil2:9); [COL 2:10](https://ref.ly/Col2:10); [HEB 12:2](https://ref.ly/Heb12:2); [REV 12:10](https://ref.ly/Rev12:10), [REV 19:16](https://ref.ly/Rev19:16)

Jesus Christ's kingdom will be fully established at his return
--------------------------------------------------------------

### His kingdom will come with power at a specific moment

**[MAT 25:31](https://ref.ly/Matt25:31)** *See also* [ZEC 9:10](https://ref.ly/Zech9:10); [MAT 24:30–24:31](https://ref.ly/Matt24:30-Matt24:31) parallels [MRK 13:26–13:27](https://ref.ly/Mark13:26-Mark13:27), [LUK 21:27](https://ref.ly/Luke21:27); [2TI 4:1](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:1)

### His kingdom will replace all earthly authority

**[REV 11:15](https://ref.ly/Rev11:15)** *See also* [1CO 15:24–15:25](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:24-1Cor15:25), [1CO 15:50–15:52](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:50-1Cor15:52); [PHP 3:20–3:21](https://ref.ly/Phil3:20-Phil3:21)

### All creation will acknowledge Jesus Christ's kingship

**[PHP 2:10–2:11](https://ref.ly/Phil2:10-Phil2:11)** *See also* [PSA 2:6–2:8](https://ref.ly/Ps2:6-Ps2:8); [REV 5:13](https://ref.ly/Rev5:13)

### His kingdom lasts for ever

**[LUK 1:33](https://ref.ly/Luke1:33)** *See also* [ISA 9:7](https://ref.ly/Isa9:7)
