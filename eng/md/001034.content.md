## 5457: power, human (id: 5457)

All human power and authority comes from God. God also gives special power to his people.

All human power comes from God
------------------------------

**[DEU 8:17–8:18](https://ref.ly/Deut8:17-Deut8:18)**; **[ROM 13:1](https://ref.ly/Rom13:1)** *See also* [JDG 3:12](https://ref.ly/Judg3:12); [2KI 13:3](https://ref.ly/2Kgs13:3), [2KI 13:5](https://ref.ly/2Kgs13:5); [2CH 25:8](https://ref.ly/2Chr25:8); [DAN 2:37](https://ref.ly/Dan2:37), [DAN 4:29–4:32](https://ref.ly/Dan4:29-Dan4:32); [JHN 19:10–19:11](https://ref.ly/John19:10-John19:11); [ACT 4:28](https://ref.ly/Acts4:28); [COL 1:16](https://ref.ly/Col1:16); [1PE 2:13–2:14](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:13-1Pet2:14)
