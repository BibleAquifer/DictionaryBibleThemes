## 5705: inheritance, spiritual (id: 5705)

Believers are privileged to receive a spiritual inheritance from their heavenly Father as a result of their adoption into the family of God through faith.

Believers have an inheritance in Christ
---------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ as God's heir

**[HEB 1:2](https://ref.ly/Heb1:2)** *See also* [MAT 21:37–21:39](https://ref.ly/Matt21:37-Matt21:39) parallels [MRK 12:6–12:8](https://ref.ly/Mark12:6-Mark12:8), [LUK 20:13–20:15](https://ref.ly/Luke20:13-Luke20:15); [MAT 28:18](https://ref.ly/Matt28:18); [LUK 19:12](https://ref.ly/Luke19:12); [JHN 3:35](https://ref.ly/John3:35); [HEB 1:4–1:6](https://ref.ly/Heb1:4-Heb1:6); [PSA 2:7–2:8](https://ref.ly/Ps2:7-Ps2:8)

### Believers are co\-heirs with Christ

**[ROM 8:17](https://ref.ly/Rom8:17)** *See also* [EPH 3:6](https://ref.ly/Eph3:6)

### Believers are heirs under the covenant

[HEB 6:17](https://ref.ly/Heb6:17), [HEB 9:15](https://ref.ly/Heb9:15)

### Believers are heirs by grace

**[GAL 3:18](https://ref.ly/Gal3:18)** *See also* [ACT 20:32](https://ref.ly/Acts20:32); [COL 1:12](https://ref.ly/Col1:12)

### Believers are heirs through faith

**[GAL 3:29](https://ref.ly/Gal3:29)** *See also* [ROM 4:13–4:17](https://ref.ly/Rom4:13-Rom4:17); [HEB 11:7–11:10](https://ref.ly/Heb11:7-Heb11:10)

The nature of the spiritual inheritance of believers
----------------------------------------------------

### They inherit salvation

**[HEB 1:14](https://ref.ly/Heb1:14)**

### They inherit eternal life

*See also* [MAT 19:29](https://ref.ly/Matt19:29); [LUK 10:25](https://ref.ly/Luke10:25); [TIT 3:7](https://ref.ly/Titus3:7); [1PE 3:7](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:7)

### They have the full rights of sons

[GAL 4:4–4:7](https://ref.ly/Gal4:4-Gal4:7)

### They have the rights of the firstborn

**[HEB 12:22–12:23](https://ref.ly/Heb12:22-Heb12:23)** *See also* [EXO 4:22](https://ref.ly/Exod4:22); [2KI 2:9](https://ref.ly/2Kgs2:9)

### They inherit the earth

**[MAT 5:5](https://ref.ly/Matt5:5)** *See also* [PSA 37:11](https://ref.ly/Ps37:11)

### They inherit the kingdom

**[MAT 25:34](https://ref.ly/Matt25:34)** *See also* [LUK 22:28–22:30](https://ref.ly/Luke22:28-Luke22:30); [REV 3:21](https://ref.ly/Rev3:21)

### They inherit all things

[LUK 15:31](https://ref.ly/Luke15:31); ; [1CO 3:21–3:22](https://ref.ly/1Cor3:21-1Cor3:22); ; [REV 21:7](https://ref.ly/Rev21:7)

### Their inheritance is the word of God

[PSA 119:111](https://ref.ly/Ps119:111)

### They inherit the Lord himself

**[PSA 73:26](https://ref.ly/Ps73:26)** *See also* [NUM 18:20](https://ref.ly/Num18:20); [DEU 10:9](https://ref.ly/Deut10:9); [PSA 142:5](https://ref.ly/Ps142:5); [JER 10:16](https://ref.ly/Jer10:16), [JER 51:19](https://ref.ly/Jer51:19)

### It is a good inheritance

[PSA 16:6](https://ref.ly/Ps16:6)

The permanence of believers' spiritual inheritance
--------------------------------------------------

### It has been prepared from the creation

[MAT 25:34](https://ref.ly/Matt25:34)

### It is guaranteed for eternity

**[1PE 1:3–1:5](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:3-1Pet1:5)** *See also* [PSA 37:18](https://ref.ly/Ps37:18); [DAN 12:13](https://ref.ly/Dan12:13)

### It is sealed with God's promise

**[EPH 1:13–1:14](https://ref.ly/Eph1:13-Eph1:14)** *See also* [REV 7:3](https://ref.ly/Rev7:3), [REV 9:4](https://ref.ly/Rev9:4)

The conditions of spiritual inheritance
---------------------------------------
