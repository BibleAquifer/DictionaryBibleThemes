## 8678: waiting on God (id: 8678)

Being prepared to patiently look towards God for his guidance and accepting the timing he proposes. Waiting on God can lead to an atmosphere of expectation and confidence in God and a realisation of the unreliability of one's own judgment.

Waiting on God is something that is commanded
---------------------------------------------

**[HOS 12:6](https://ref.ly/Hos12:6)** *See also* [PSA 27:14](https://ref.ly/Ps27:14), [PSA 37:7](https://ref.ly/Ps37:7), [PSA 37:34](https://ref.ly/Ps37:34), [PSA 123:2](https://ref.ly/Ps123:2); [ISA 30:18](https://ref.ly/Isa30:18)

Reasons for waiting on God
--------------------------

[JER 14:22](https://ref.ly/Jer14:22) *for his providence*; ; [PSA 25:5](https://ref.ly/Ps25:5) *for his salvation*; ; [PSA 145:15–145:16](https://ref.ly/Ps145:15-Ps145:16) *for his blessings*

Waiting on Christ
-----------------

**[JAS 5:7–5:8](https://ref.ly/Jas5:7-Jas5:8)** *“Waiting on Christ” is often seen in terms of waiting patiently for God to bring about the end of all things through the promised second coming of Jesus Christ.* *See also* [LUK 12:35–12:40](https://ref.ly/Luke12:35-Luke12:40); [1CO 1:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:7), [1CO 4:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor4:5); [TIT 2:13](https://ref.ly/Titus2:13); [HEB 9:28](https://ref.ly/Heb9:28); [JUD 1:21](https://ref.ly/Jude1:21)
