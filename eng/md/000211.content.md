## 2411: cross, predictions of (id: 2411)

The death of Jesus Christ fulfils OT predictions and was clearly anticipated in his teaching.

The cross foreshadowed in the OT
--------------------------------

**[PSA 22:1](https://ref.ly/Ps22:1)** *See also* [GEN 3:15](https://ref.ly/Gen3:15); [PSA 22:6–22:7](https://ref.ly/Ps22:6-Ps22:7), [PSA 22:16–22:18](https://ref.ly/Ps22:16-Ps22:18), [PSA 31:5](https://ref.ly/Ps31:5), [PSA 69:21](https://ref.ly/Ps69:21); [ISA 52:12–52:13](https://ref.ly/Isa52:12-Isa52:13); [ZEC 12:10](https://ref.ly/Zech12:10), [ZEC 13:7](https://ref.ly/Zech13:7)

Other allusions to the cross
----------------------------

### In the teaching of Jesus Christ

**[MAT 26:31](https://ref.ly/Matt26:31)** *See also* [MAT 20:22](https://ref.ly/Matt20:22) parallel [MRK 10:38](https://ref.ly/Mark10:38); [MAT 26:2](https://ref.ly/Matt26:2); [MRK 2:19–2:20](https://ref.ly/Mark2:19-Mark2:20), [MRK 10:45](https://ref.ly/Mark10:45); [LUK 9:44](https://ref.ly/Luke9:44), [LUK 22:42](https://ref.ly/Luke22:42)JHN [3:14](https://ref.ly/Luke3:14), [JHN 8:28](https://ref.ly/John8:28), [JHN 12:32–12:33](https://ref.ly/John12:32-John12:33)[JHN 18:11](https://ref.ly/John18:11)

### In history

**[MAT 17:9–17:13](https://ref.ly/Matt17:9-Matt17:13)** *John the Baptist's fate indicates what is to happen to Jesus Christ.*

### In the experience of Jesus Christ

**[MAT 26:12](https://ref.ly/Matt26:12)** parallel [MRK 14:8](https://ref.ly/Mark14:8) *Jesus Christ's reaction to the woman anointing him seems to suggest that he will die the death of a criminal.*

**[MAT 26:26–26:28](https://ref.ly/Matt26:26-Matt26:28)** parallels [MRK 14:22–14:25](https://ref.ly/Mark14:22-Mark14:25), [LUK 22:19–22:20](https://ref.ly/Luke22:19-Luke22:20)
