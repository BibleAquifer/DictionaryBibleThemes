## 5699: guests (id: 5699)

People who receive hospitality, especially at others' homes. Guests are often invited to special celebrations such as weddings or birthdays. God invites all people as guests at his feast but many refuse.

Customs associated with receiving guests
----------------------------------------

### Anointing them with oil

**[PSA 23:5](https://ref.ly/Ps23:5)** *Anointing with oil was a customary treatment for honoured guests.* *See also* [PSA 92:10](https://ref.ly/Ps92:10); [LUK 7:46](https://ref.ly/Luke7:46); [JHN 12:3](https://ref.ly/John12:3)

### Washing their feet

**[GEN 18:4–18:5](https://ref.ly/Gen18:4-Gen18:5)** *See also* [GEN 19:2](https://ref.ly/Gen19:2), [GEN 24:32](https://ref.ly/Gen24:32), [GEN 43:24](https://ref.ly/Gen43:24); [1SA 25:41](https://ref.ly/1Sam25:41); [LUK 7:44](https://ref.ly/Luke7:44); [JHN 13:1–13:17](https://ref.ly/John13:1-John13:17) *It was usually the slave's duty to wash away dirt accumulated in travelling.*
