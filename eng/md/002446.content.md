## 8828: spite (id: 8828)

The malicious desire to retaliate in words or action. It may be in response to good or evil. Believers are to rid themselves of any such vindictive spirit.

Examples of people acting out of spite
--------------------------------------

### In response to good

[1KI 19:1–19:2](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:1-1Kgs19:2) *Jezebel*; ; [2CH 16:10](https://ref.ly/2Chr16:10) *Asa, king of Judah*; ; [EST 3:6](https://ref.ly/Esth3:6) *Haman*; ; [MAT 14:3–14:12](https://ref.ly/Matt14:3-Matt14:12) parallel [MRK 6:17–6:29](https://ref.ly/Mark6:17-Mark6:29) *Herodias*; ; [JHN 12:10](https://ref.ly/John12:10) *the chief priests*; [ACT 7:54](https://ref.ly/Acts7:54), [ACT 7:57–7:58](https://ref.ly/Acts7:57-Acts7:58)

### In response to evil

**[PSA 140:3](https://ref.ly/Ps140:3)** *See also* [GEN 27:41](https://ref.ly/Gen27:41) *Esau*[GEN 37:4](https://ref.ly/Gen37:4), [GEN 37:8](https://ref.ly/Gen37:8), [GEN 37:11](https://ref.ly/Gen37:11), [GEN 37:18–37:27](https://ref.ly/Gen37:18-Gen37:27)[EZK 25:15](https://ref.ly/Ezek25:15) *the Philistines*; [ACT 23:12–23:15](https://ref.ly/Acts23:12-Acts23:15) *some Jews*

Spite has no place in the lives of believers
--------------------------------------------

### Believers are to rid themselves of all spitefulness

**[EPH 4:31](https://ref.ly/Eph4:31)** *See also* [1CO 5:8](https://ref.ly/1Cor5:8); [COL 3:8](https://ref.ly/Col3:8); [1PE 2:1](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:1)
