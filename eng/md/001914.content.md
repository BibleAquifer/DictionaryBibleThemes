## 7745: ordination (id: 7745)

The appointment to a public ministry or office, including investing with the authority required for this ministry. The most important biblical model of ordination is provided by the OT priesthood.

Ordination of the OT priesthood
-------------------------------

### The ceremony of ordination

**[EXO 28:41](https://ref.ly/Exod28:41)** *See also* [EXO 29:29–29:46](https://ref.ly/Exod29:29-Exod29:46), [EXO 30:30](https://ref.ly/Exod30:30); [LEV 8:30](https://ref.ly/Lev8:30), [LEV 8:33](https://ref.ly/Lev8:33); [NUM 3:3](https://ref.ly/Num3:3)

### The role of the ordained priest

To properly apply the blood of the ordination offering: [EXO 29:20–29:21](https://ref.ly/Exod29:20-Exod29:21); ; [LEV 7:37](https://ref.ly/Lev7:37), [LEV 8:18](https://ref.ly/Lev8:18); [LEV 16:32](https://ref.ly/Lev16:32) *to make atonement*; ; [LEV 21:10–21:12](https://ref.ly/Lev21:10-Lev21:12) *to observe a strict code of behaviour*

Jesus is the Messiah, the anointed one
--------------------------------------

[MAT 16:16](https://ref.ly/Matt16:16) *The Greek word “Christos”, like the Hebrew word “Messiah”, means “anointed (by God)”.*

Ordination in the NT
--------------------

### Jesus Christ appointed disciples without reference to a ceremony of ordination

[MRK 3:14–3:15](https://ref.ly/Mark3:14-Mark3:15) parallel [LUK 6:13](https://ref.ly/Luke6:13)

### The apostles commissioned people for service by laying on of hands

**[ACT 6:6](https://ref.ly/Acts6:6)** *See also* [NUM 8:10–8:11](https://ref.ly/Num8:10-Num8:11), [NUM 27:18–27:23](https://ref.ly/Num27:18-Num27:23); [ACT 13:3](https://ref.ly/Acts13:3) *Barnabas and Saul were commissioned for missionary work.*

### The appointment of elders

[ACT 14:23](https://ref.ly/Acts14:23); ; [1TI 1:18](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:18), [1TI 4:14](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:14), [1TI 5:22](https://ref.ly/1Tim5:22); ; [2TI 1:6](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:6); ; [TIT 1:5](https://ref.ly/Titus1:5), [TIT 1:6–1:9](https://ref.ly/Titus1:6-Titus1:9) *Certain qualities are required in an appointed elder or minister.*
