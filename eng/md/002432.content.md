## 8811: riches, believers' attitudes towards (id: 8811)

God entrusts some of his people with material wealth. If this is to be a blessing, it must be used in God\-honouring ways.

Believers may be blessed with riches
------------------------------------

### Riches can be a blessing from God

**[1CH 29:16](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:16)** *See also* [1CH 29:14–29:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:14-1Chr29:15); [JOB 1:9–1:102](https://ref.ly/Job1:9-Job1:102)KI [4:1](https://ref.ly/Job4:1); [MAT 8:20](https://ref.ly/Matt8:20); [LUK 6:20](https://ref.ly/Luke6:20), [LUK 16:20–16:22](https://ref.ly/Luke16:20-Luke16:22); [ROM 15:26](https://ref.ly/Rom15:26)
