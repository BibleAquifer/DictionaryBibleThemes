## 8310: morality, and creation (id: 8310)

Human beings have been created moral creatures in the likeness of God, who is the source and standard of all that is good.

Morality is grounded in the nature of God himself
-------------------------------------------------

**[DEU 32:4](https://ref.ly/Deut32:4)**; **[LUK 18:18–18:19](https://ref.ly/Luke18:18-Luke18:19)** *See also* [EXO 33:19](https://ref.ly/Exod33:19), [EXO 34:5–34:7](https://ref.ly/Exod34:5-Exod34:7); [PSA 25:8–25:9](https://ref.ly/Ps25:8-Ps25:9), [PSA 119:68](https://ref.ly/Ps119:68)

Human beings are morally aware
------------------------------

### They are created in God's image

[GEN 1:26–1:27](https://ref.ly/Gen1:26-Gen1:27)

### They have a conscience

**[ROM 2:14–2:15](https://ref.ly/Rom2:14-Rom2:15)** *See also* [2CO 4:2](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:2)

### They have been given a moral law

[GEN 2:16–2:17](https://ref.ly/Gen2:16-Gen2:17); ; [MRK 10:6–10:9](https://ref.ly/Mark10:6-Mark10:9); ; [GEN 2:23–2:24](https://ref.ly/Gen2:23-Gen2:24) *Standards for marriage are implicit in the creation.*

### They are capable of sharing moral ideals

[JHN 1:9](https://ref.ly/John1:9); ; [ROM 13:1–13:7](https://ref.ly/Rom13:1-Rom13:7); ; [EPH 3:15](https://ref.ly/Eph3:15) *“whole family” is literally “all fatherhood”, implying that every understanding of fatherhood and family is derived from God*; ; [PHP 4:8](https://ref.ly/Phil4:8) *refering to pagan virtues*; ; [TIT 3:1](https://ref.ly/Titus3:1); ; [1PE 2:13–2:14](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:13-1Pet2:14)

### Immoral acts are unnatural

[ROM 1:26–1:27](https://ref.ly/Rom1:26-Rom1:27)

### God's judgment implies moral responsibility

[JER 17:10](https://ref.ly/Jer17:10); ; [EZK 18:20–18:21](https://ref.ly/Ezek18:20-Ezek18:21); ; [AMO 1:6](https://ref.ly/Amos1:6); ; [ROM 2:5–2:11](https://ref.ly/Rom2:5-Rom2:11)
