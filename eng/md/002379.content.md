## 8750: false teachings (id: 8750)

Scripture repeatedly warns against false teachings, which deny or distort some aspect of the gospel. The origin of such teachings is attributed either to human error or to demonic inspiration.

Examples of false teachings
---------------------------

**[REV 2:14–2:15](https://ref.ly/Rev2:14-Rev2:15)** *See also* [MAT 5:19](https://ref.ly/Matt5:19); [2TH 2:1–2:2](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:1-2Thess2:2); [1TI 4:1–4:3](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:1-1Tim4:3); [REV 2:20](https://ref.ly/Rev2:20)

Qualities of false teachings
----------------------------

### They are valueless

**[MAT 15:9](https://ref.ly/Matt15:9)** parallel [MRK 7:7](https://ref.ly/Mark7:7) *See also* [ISA 29:13](https://ref.ly/Isa29:13); [EPH 5:6](https://ref.ly/Eph5:6); [COL 2:20–2:23](https://ref.ly/Col2:20-Col2:23); [1TI 1:3–1:7](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:3-1Tim1:7)

### They are destructive in their nature

**[2PE 2:1](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:1)** *See also* [MAT 7:15](https://ref.ly/Matt7:15); [ACT 20:29–20:30](https://ref.ly/Acts20:29-Acts20:30); [TIT 1:10–1:11](https://ref.ly/Titus1:10-Titus1:11)

### They have the capacity to lead people astray

**[ACT 20:30](https://ref.ly/Acts20:30)** *See also* [EPH 4:14](https://ref.ly/Eph4:14); [1TI 6:20–6:21](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:20-1Tim6:21); [HEB 13:9](https://ref.ly/Heb13:9); [2PE 2:14–2:15](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:14-2Pet2:15); [1JN 2:26](https://ref.ly/1John2:26)

### They may be popular

**[2PE 2:2](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:2)** *See also* [MAT 24:4–24:5](https://ref.ly/Matt24:4-Matt24:5) parallel [MRK 13:5–13:6](https://ref.ly/Mark13:5-Mark13:6); [2TI 4:3](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:3); [2PE 2:18–2:19](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:18-2Pet2:19); [1JN 4:1–4:5](https://ref.ly/1John4:1-1John4:5)

### They may be accompanied by miraculous signs

**[MAT 24:24](https://ref.ly/Matt24:24)** parallel [MRK 13:22](https://ref.ly/Mark13:22) *See also* [2TH 2:9–2:10](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:9-2Thess2:10); [REV 13:11–13:15](https://ref.ly/Rev13:11-Rev13:15)

The origin of false teachings
-----------------------------

### Demonic inspiration

**[1TI 4:1–4:2](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:1-1Tim4:2)** *See also* [2CO 11:3–11:4](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:3-2Cor11:4); [1JN 4:1–4:3](https://ref.ly/1John4:1-1John4:3); [REV 16:13–16:14](https://ref.ly/Rev16:13-Rev16:14)

### Human error

**[2PE 2:3](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:3)** *See also* [COL 2:8](https://ref.ly/Col2:8); [1TI 6:20–6:21](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:20-1Tim6:21)

Discerning false teachings
--------------------------

### By their content

**[1JN 4:2–4:3](https://ref.ly/1John4:2-1John4:3)** *See also* [1CO 12:3](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:3); [1JN 2:20–2:23](https://ref.ly/1John2:20-1John2:23); [2JN 1:7–1:11](https://ref.ly/2John1:7-2John1:11)

### By the teacher's lifestyle

**[MAT 7:15–7:20](https://ref.ly/Matt7:15-Matt7:20)** *See also* [2CO 11:20](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:20); [1JN 3:7–3:10](https://ref.ly/1John3:7-1John3:10); [JUD 1:4](https://ref.ly/Jude1:4)

### By their effects

**[1TI 6:3–6:5](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:3-1Tim6:5)** *See also* [1TI 1:3–1:4](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:3-1Tim1:4); [2TI 2:16–2:18](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:16-2Tim2:18); [REV 2:20](https://ref.ly/Rev2:20)

The antidote to false teachings
-------------------------------

### Holding fast to the true gospel

**[1JN 2:24](https://ref.ly/1John2:24)** *See also* [GAL 1:6–1:9](https://ref.ly/Gal1:6-Gal1:9); [1TI 1:18–1:20](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:18-1Tim1:20); [2TI 1:13–1:14](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:13-2Tim1:14)

### Shunning false teachers

**[ROM 16:17–16:18](https://ref.ly/Rom16:17-Rom16:18)** *See also* [2TI 3:1–3:9](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:1-2Tim3:9); [TIT 3:9–3:11](https://ref.ly/Titus3:9-Titus3:11); [2JN 1:9–1:11](https://ref.ly/2John1:9-2John1:11)
