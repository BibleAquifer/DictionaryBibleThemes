## 5132: biting (id: 5132)

The use of teeth to harm, threaten or express frustration. It is illustrative of the effect of slander, cold wind, excessive wine or of God's judgment.

Biting used literally
---------------------

### An effect of demon\-possession

*See also* [MRK 9:18](https://ref.ly/Mark9:18)

### Biting by reptiles and animals

**[NUM 21:6](https://ref.ly/Num21:6)** *See also* [ECC 10:8](https://ref.ly/Eccl10:8), [ECC 10:11](https://ref.ly/Eccl10:11); [DAN 6:24](https://ref.ly/Dan6:24)

### Reptiles and animals prevented from biting

**[DAN 6:21–6:22](https://ref.ly/Dan6:21-Dan6:22)** *See also* [MRK 16:18](https://ref.ly/Mark16:18); [ACT 28:4–28:5](https://ref.ly/Acts28:4-Acts28:5); [HEB 11:33](https://ref.ly/Heb11:33)

**[JER 8:17](https://ref.ly/Jer8:17)** *See also* [JOL 1:6](https://ref.ly/Joel1:6); [AMO 5:19](https://ref.ly/Amos5:19), [AMO 9:3](https://ref.ly/Amos9:3)

**[ACT 7:54](https://ref.ly/Acts7:54)** *See also* [PSA 35:16](https://ref.ly/Ps35:16), [PSA 37:12](https://ref.ly/Ps37:12); [LAM 2:16](https://ref.ly/Lam2:16); [1CO 15:32](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:32) *A fulfilment of the prophecy of [Isa 22:13](https://ref.ly/Isa22:13), indicating that the devouring of God's people is a part of the greed of the wicked*; [2TI 4:17](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:17)
