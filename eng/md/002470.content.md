## 9022: death, of believers (id: 9022)

Believers in Jesus Christ will depart from this present life to the joy of God's presence.

Death is a normal experience for believers
------------------------------------------

**[ROM 14:8](https://ref.ly/Rom14:8)** *See also* [HEB 9:27](https://ref.ly/Heb9:27)

Death can be a blessing
-----------------------

### Because of the prospect of heaven

**[PHP 1:21–1:23](https://ref.ly/Phil1:21-Phil1:23)** *See also* [NUM 23:10](https://ref.ly/Num23:10); [PSA 49:15](https://ref.ly/Ps49:15), [PSA 116:15](https://ref.ly/Ps116:15); [LUK 2:29](https://ref.ly/Luke2:29); [2TI 4:6–4:8](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:6-2Tim4:8); [REV 7:13–7:17](https://ref.ly/Rev7:13-Rev7:17), [REV 14:13](https://ref.ly/Rev14:13)

### Because it brings relief from trouble and problems

[1KI 19:4](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:4); ; [JOB 3:20](https://ref.ly/Job3:20), [JOB 7:15](https://ref.ly/Job7:15); ; [2TH 1:7](https://ref.ly/2Thess1:7)

At death believers are with Christ
----------------------------------

### They are in his presence

**[LUK 23:43](https://ref.ly/Luke23:43)** *See also* [2CO 5:8](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:8); [1TH 5:10](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:10)

### They are safe and secure

**[REV 2:11](https://ref.ly/Rev2:11)** *The second death is the final banishment of unbelievers from God's presence.* *See also* [ROM 14:8](https://ref.ly/Rom14:8); [REV 20:6](https://ref.ly/Rev20:6)

### They have the prospect of a new order of things

**[REV 21:4](https://ref.ly/Rev21:4)** *See also* [2TI 4:6–4:8](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:6-2Tim4:8); [REV 22:3–22:5](https://ref.ly/Rev22:3-Rev22:5)

The defeat of death
-------------------

### Death is vanquished for believers

**[1CO 15:55](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:55)** *See also* [ROM 8:36](https://ref.ly/Rom8:36); [2TI 1:10](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:10); [HEB 2:14](https://ref.ly/Heb2:14); [REV 20:14](https://ref.ly/Rev20:14)

### Death is to be reversed in resurrection

Though some of the above passages suggest that eternal life in Jesus Christ's presence is experienced immediately at death, others such as the following link these blessings with resurrection at the final coming of Jesus Christ: [JHN 11:23](https://ref.ly/John11:23); ; [1CO 15:51–15:54](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:51-1Cor15:54); ; [1TH 4:16](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:16)

The death of believers is likened to sleep
------------------------------------------

### Implying rest, renewing and awaking

**[ACT 7:59–7:60](https://ref.ly/Acts7:59-Acts7:60)** *Sleep does not imply lack of consciousness but rather rest, renewing and awaking. In the NT it is only used of believers.* *See also* [DEU 31:16](https://ref.ly/Deut31:16); [DAN 12:2](https://ref.ly/Dan12:2); [MRK 5:39](https://ref.ly/Mark5:39); [JHN 11:11–11:14](https://ref.ly/John11:11-John11:14); [ACT 13:36](https://ref.ly/Acts13:36); [1CO 15:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:6), [1CO 15:17–15:18](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:17-1Cor15:18); [1TH 4:13–4:16](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:13-1Thess4:16)

### Implying peace and comfort

**[LUK 16:22](https://ref.ly/Luke16:22)** *Abraham's side suggests a welcome to a place of peace and protection.* *See also* [GEN 49:33](https://ref.ly/Gen49:33); [2KI 22:20](https://ref.ly/2Kgs22:20); [ISA 57:1–57:2](https://ref.ly/Isa57:1-Isa57:2); [LUK 16:25](https://ref.ly/Luke16:25)

Death and the recognition of others
-----------------------------------

**[1TH 4:17](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:17)** *“caught up together” implies recognition.* *See also* [GEN 25:8](https://ref.ly/Gen25:8) *“gathered to his people” (literally “to his father's kin”) is a common OT expression and implies continued existence together*; [JOB 3:13–3:14](https://ref.ly/Job3:13-Job3:14); [MAT 17:4](https://ref.ly/Matt17:4); [MRK 9:4](https://ref.ly/Mark9:4); [LUK 16:24](https://ref.ly/Luke16:24); [1CO 13:12](https://ref.ly/1Cor13:12)

Believers can face death without fear
-------------------------------------

### They die in relationship with God

**[HEB 2:14–2:15](https://ref.ly/Heb2:14-Heb2:15)** *See also* [PSA 23:4](https://ref.ly/Ps23:4); [PRO 14:32](https://ref.ly/Prov14:32)

### They die trusting the Lord

[HEB 11:13](https://ref.ly/Heb11:13), [HEB 11:21–11:22](https://ref.ly/Heb11:21-Heb11:22)

### They receive comfort in facing death

**[JHN 11:25–11:26](https://ref.ly/John11:25-John11:26)** *See also* [ISA 25:8](https://ref.ly/Isa25:8); [1CO 15:26](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:26); [REV 1:18](https://ref.ly/Rev1:18)

### In death they can glorify God

**[PHP 1:20](https://ref.ly/Phil1:20)**

### In this believers are to be envied

**[NUM 23:10](https://ref.ly/Num23:10)**

Believers often hated the idea of death
---------------------------------------

*See also* [JOB 17:13–17:16](https://ref.ly/Job17:13-Job17:16); [PSA 9:13](https://ref.ly/Ps9:13); [ISA 38:10](https://ref.ly/Isa38:10); [ROM 8:36](https://ref.ly/Rom8:36); [1CO 15:55–15:56](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:55-1Cor15:56); [2CO 4:11–4:12](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:11-2Cor4:12)

Martyred believers are specially honoured in Scripture
