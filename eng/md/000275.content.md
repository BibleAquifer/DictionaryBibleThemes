## 3245: Holy Spirit, blasphemy against (id: 3245)

The denial of the action of the Holy Spirit in the ministry of Jesus Christ, or the attribution of his works to demonic influence.

Jesus Christ warns against blasphemy
------------------------------------

**[MRK 3:29](https://ref.ly/Mark3:29)** parallels [MAT 12:31–12:32](https://ref.ly/Matt12:31-Matt12:32), [LUK 12:10](https://ref.ly/Luke12:10) *See also* [1JN 5:16](https://ref.ly/1John5:16)

Blasphemy as erroneously attributing the work of the Holy Spirit to demonic influence
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ is accused of being possessed by demons

**[MRK 3:22](https://ref.ly/Mark3:22)** parallels [MAT 12:24](https://ref.ly/Matt12:24), [LUK 11:15](https://ref.ly/Luke11:15) *See also* [MAT 10:25](https://ref.ly/Matt10:25); [JHN 7:20](https://ref.ly/John7:20), [JHN 8:48](https://ref.ly/John8:48), [JHN 8:52](https://ref.ly/John8:52), [JHN 10:20](https://ref.ly/John10:20)

### Jesus Christ is accused of casting out demons by demonic power

**[MAT 9:34](https://ref.ly/Matt9:34)** *See also* [MAT 12:24](https://ref.ly/Matt12:24) parallels [MRK 3:22](https://ref.ly/Mark3:22), [LUK 11:15](https://ref.ly/Luke11:15)

### Jesus Christ refutes these charges

[MAT 12:25–12:29](https://ref.ly/Matt12:25-Matt12:29) parallels [MRK 3:23–3:27](https://ref.ly/Mark3:23-Mark3:27), [LUK 11:17–11:22](https://ref.ly/Luke11:17-Luke11:22)
