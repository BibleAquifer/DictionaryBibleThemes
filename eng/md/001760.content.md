## 7302: altar (id: 7302)

A construction, usually of wood, stone or metal, for the offering of sacrifice. The conflict between true and false religion often focused on altars, which were of key significance in biblical times.

Early altars built to the LORD
------------------------------

**[GEN 8:20](https://ref.ly/Gen8:20)** *These early altars were erected at sites where God's presence or power had been experienced.* [GEN 12:7–12:8](https://ref.ly/Gen12:7-Gen12:8), [GEN 13:18](https://ref.ly/Gen13:18), [GEN 22:9](https://ref.ly/Gen22:9); [GEN 26:25](https://ref.ly/Gen26:25) *by Isaac*; [GEN 33:20](https://ref.ly/Gen33:20), [GEN 35:7](https://ref.ly/Gen35:7); [EXO 17:15](https://ref.ly/Exod17:15), [EXO 24:4](https://ref.ly/Exod24:4); [JOS 8:30](https://ref.ly/Josh8:30) *by Joshua*; ; [JDG 6:24](https://ref.ly/Judg6:24) *by Gideon*; ; [JDG 21:4](https://ref.ly/Judg21:4) *by unnamed Israelites*; ; [1SA 7:17](https://ref.ly/1Sam7:17) *by Samuel*; ; [1SA 14:35](https://ref.ly/1Sam14:35) *by Saul*; ; [2SA 24:25](https://ref.ly/2Sam24:25) parallel [1CH 21:26](https://ref.ly/1Chr21:26) *by David*
