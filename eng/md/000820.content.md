## 5232: book (id: 5232)

Used most frequently to refer to a particular book of the Bible being written, another book of the Bible or a source no longer available. It is used occasionally of books in general or metaphorically of God's record of events.

References to books being written
---------------------------------

[JER 25:13](https://ref.ly/Jer25:13), [JER 30:2](https://ref.ly/Jer30:2); ; [NAM 1:1](https://ref.ly/Nah1:1); ; [JHN 20:30](https://ref.ly/John20:30); ; [REV 22:7](https://ref.ly/Rev22:7), [REV 22:9–22:10](https://ref.ly/Rev22:9-Rev22:10), [REV 22:18–22:19](https://ref.ly/Rev22:18-Rev22:19)

References to books no longer available
---------------------------------------

### The Book of the Wars of the LORD

[NUM 21:14](https://ref.ly/Num21:14) *This was probably an ancient collection of war songs in praise of God.*

### The Book of Jashar

**[JOS 10:13](https://ref.ly/Josh10:13)** *An early account of Israel's wars, possibly in poetic form, but never a part of canonical Scripture.* *See also* [2SA 1:18](https://ref.ly/2Sam1:18)

### The book of the annals of the kings of Israel and Judah

**[2CH 16:11](https://ref.ly/2Chr16:11)** *These sources were probably records of the reigns of the kings of Israel and Judah, compiled by the succession of Israel's prophets spanning the kingdom period (see e.g., [1Ch 29:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:29); [2Ch 12:15](https://ref.ly/2Chr12:15); [20:34](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:34); [26:22](https://ref.ly/2Chr26:22); [32:32](https://ref.ly/2Chr32:32)).* *See also* [1KI 11:41](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:41), [1KI 14:19](https://ref.ly/1Kgs14:19), [1KI 14:29](https://ref.ly/1Kgs14:29); [2KI 1:18](https://ref.ly/2Kgs1:18), [2KI 8:23](https://ref.ly/2Kgs8:23); [1CH 9:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:1), [1CH 27:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:24); [2CH 24:27](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:27), [2CH 27:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr27:7)

### The book of the annals

[NEH 12:23](https://ref.ly/Neh12:23) *This was probably the official temple chronicle, containing various lists and records (compare with [Ne 7:5](https://ref.ly/Neh7:5)).*; [EST 2:23](https://ref.ly/Esth2:23), [EST 6:1](https://ref.ly/Esth6:1), [EST 10:2](https://ref.ly/Esth10:2)
