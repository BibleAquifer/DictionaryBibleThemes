## 6671: grace, and Christian life (id: 6671)

The Christian life, from its beginning to its end, is totally dependent upon the grace of God.

God's grace compensates for human weaknesses
--------------------------------------------

**[2CO 12:8–12:9](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:8-2Cor12:9)**; **[1PE 5:10](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:10)** *See also* [1CO 2:1–2:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:1-1Cor2:5); [2CO 9:8](https://ref.ly/2Cor9:8); [HEB 2:14](https://ref.ly/Heb2:14), [HEB 4:15](https://ref.ly/Heb4:15), [HEB 5:2](https://ref.ly/Heb5:2); [JAS 4:6](https://ref.ly/Jas4:6); [2PE 3:17–3:18](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:17-2Pet3:18)

Believers are to pray for grace
-------------------------------

**[HEB 4:16](https://ref.ly/Heb4:16)** *See also* [PSA 25:16](https://ref.ly/Ps25:16); [HOS 14:1–14:2](https://ref.ly/Hos14:1-Hos14:2); [COL 1:9](https://ref.ly/Col1:9), [COL 4:12](https://ref.ly/Col4:12)

-------------------------------------------------------

**[1CO 15:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:10)** *See also* [ACT 18:27](https://ref.ly/Acts18:27); [ROM 5:2](https://ref.ly/Rom5:2); [GAL 1:15](https://ref.ly/Gal1:15); [PHP 1:7](https://ref.ly/Phil1:7)

**[ACT 20:32](https://ref.ly/Acts20:32)** *See also* [ACT 13:43](https://ref.ly/Acts13:43); [COL 1:3–1:6](https://ref.ly/Col1:3-Col1:6); [HEB 13:9](https://ref.ly/Heb13:9); [1PE 5:12](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:12); [2PE 3:18](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:18)
