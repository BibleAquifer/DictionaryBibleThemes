## 7906: baptism, in the Gospels (id: 7906)

In the Gospels baptising is based on the symbolic practice in the OT and Judaism of cleansing with water.

Washing was the means of achieving ritual purity
------------------------------------------------

[EXO 30:19–30:20](https://ref.ly/Exod30:19-Exod30:20); ; [MRK 7:3–7:4](https://ref.ly/Mark7:3-Mark7:4) *The Greek word here translated “wash” is “baptizo”, from which the English “baptise” comes.*

Sprinkling with water symbolises spiritual cleansing
----------------------------------------------------

**[EZK 36:25](https://ref.ly/Ezek36:25)** *See also* [ZEC 13:1](https://ref.ly/Zech13:1); [HEB 10:22](https://ref.ly/Heb10:22)

Being baptised by John was a confession that cleansing was needed
-----------------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 3:11](https://ref.ly/Matt3:11)** *See also* [MAT 3:6](https://ref.ly/Matt3:6); [MRK 1:4–1:5](https://ref.ly/Mark1:4-Mark1:5) parallel [LUK 3:3](https://ref.ly/Luke3:3); [ACT 19:1–19:4](https://ref.ly/Acts19:1-Acts19:4)

John promised that the Messiah would cleanse with the baptism of the Holy Spirit
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 3:11](https://ref.ly/Matt3:11)** parallels [MRK 1:8](https://ref.ly/Mark1:8), [LUK 3:16](https://ref.ly/Luke3:16) *See also* [ISA 44:3](https://ref.ly/Isa44:3); [EZK 36:25–36:26](https://ref.ly/Ezek36:25-Ezek36:26)

Baptism involves both water and the Holy Spirit
-----------------------------------------------

**[JHN 3:5](https://ref.ly/John3:5)**

The disciples of Jesus Christ baptised like John's
--------------------------------------------------

*See also* [JHN 3:22–3:23](https://ref.ly/John3:22-John3:23), [JHN 4:1](https://ref.ly/John4:1)

Jesus Christ was baptised by John
---------------------------------

[MAT 3:13–3:17](https://ref.ly/Matt3:13-Matt3:17) parallels [MRK 1:9–1:11](https://ref.ly/Mark1:9-Mark1:11), [LUK 3:21–3:22](https://ref.ly/Luke3:21-Luke3:22), [JHN 1:32–1:33](https://ref.ly/John1:32-John1:33)
