## 7468: temple, rebuilding of (id: 7468)

Following the exile Solomon's ruined temple was rebuilt and became the focus of the Jewish faith worldwide.

Preparations for rebuilding the temple
--------------------------------------

[EZR 1:1–1:4](https://ref.ly/Ezra1:1-Ezra1:4) parallel [2CH 36:22–36:23](https://ref.ly/2Chr36:22-2Chr36:23) *Cyrus gave permission for the exiled Jews to return to Jerusalem to rebuild the temple (538 B.C.)*; ; [EZR 1:7](https://ref.ly/Ezra1:7) *He sent back the temple treasures.*

Rebuilding the temple under the leadership of Jeshua and Zerubbabel
-------------------------------------------------------------------
