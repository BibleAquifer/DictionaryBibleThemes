## 8345: servanthood, and worship of God (id: 8345)

The biblical understanding of servanthood has its foundation in people serving God in worship. The terms for “service” and “worship” are often interchangeable.

Worship is a central aspect of serving God
------------------------------------------

**[EXO 3:12](https://ref.ly/Exod3:12)** *See also* [EXO 4:23](https://ref.ly/Exod4:23), [EXO 8:1](https://ref.ly/Exod8:1), [EXO 8:20](https://ref.ly/Exod8:20), [EXO 10:8](https://ref.ly/Exod10:8); [JOS 22:27](https://ref.ly/Josh22:27); [PSA 100:2](https://ref.ly/Ps100:2), [PSA 102:22](https://ref.ly/Ps102:22); [LUK 4:8](https://ref.ly/Luke4:8)

Worship entails total obedience, not merely correct religious observances
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[DEU 10:12–10:13](https://ref.ly/Deut10:12-Deut10:13)** *See also* [AMO 5:22–5:24](https://ref.ly/Amos5:22-Amos5:24); [MIC 6:6–6:8](https://ref.ly/Mic6:6-Mic6:8)
