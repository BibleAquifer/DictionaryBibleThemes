## 9411: heaven, nature of (id: 9411)

Scripture refers to heaven as God's habitation but also uses the term as an alternative for God himself.

Heaven as God's habitation
--------------------------

### It is the place where he dwells

**[DEU 26:15](https://ref.ly/Deut26:15)** *See also* [GEN 28:17](https://ref.ly/Gen28:17); [2CH 6:21](https://ref.ly/2Chr6:21); [ECC 5:2](https://ref.ly/Eccl5:2); [REV 13:6](https://ref.ly/Rev13:6)

### It is insufficient as God's dwelling\-place

**[1KI 8:27](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:27)** parallel [2CH 6:18](https://ref.ly/2Chr6:18) *See also* [PSA 113:5–113:6](https://ref.ly/Ps113:5-Ps113:6)
