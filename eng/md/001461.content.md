## 5948: shrewdness (id: 5948)

Astuteness or craftiness in dealings with others, especially in using one's understanding and judgment to one's own advantage. Scripture commends it when it is seen in wise words and actions directed towards a worthy goal, but condemns it when it takes the form of cunning and deceitful scheming for sinful and selfish ends.

Shrewdness as astuteness
------------------------

### It is encouraged in believers

**[MAT 10:16](https://ref.ly/Matt10:16)**; **[LUK 16:8–16:9](https://ref.ly/Luke16:8-Luke16:9)** *Jesus Christ explains the parable of the dishonest manager ([Lk 16:1–7](https://ref.ly/Luke16:1-Luke16:7)) and urges his disciples to be shrewd in their use of worldly wealth.*

### Practical demonstrations of astuteness

**[MAT 7:24–7:25](https://ref.ly/Matt7:24-Matt7:25)** parallel [LUK 6:47–6:48](https://ref.ly/Luke6:47-Luke6:48) *See also* [PRO 12:23](https://ref.ly/Prov12:23), [PRO 14:15](https://ref.ly/Prov14:15), [PRO 15:5](https://ref.ly/Prov15:5), [PRO 18:15](https://ref.ly/Prov18:15); [MAT 24:45–24:47](https://ref.ly/Matt24:45-Matt24:47) parallel [LUK 12:42–12:44](https://ref.ly/Luke12:42-Luke12:44)

### Jesus Christ's wisdom in answering his enemies

[MAT 22:18–22:22](https://ref.ly/Matt22:18-Matt22:22) parallels [MRK 12:15–12:17](https://ref.ly/Mark12:15-Mark12:17), [LUK 20:23–20:26](https://ref.ly/Luke20:23-Luke20:26) *on the matter of paying taxes to Caesar*; ; [MAT 22:31–22:32](https://ref.ly/Matt22:31-Matt22:32) parallels [MRK 12:26–12:27](https://ref.ly/Mark12:26-Mark12:27), [LUK 20:37–20:38](https://ref.ly/Luke20:37-Luke20:38) *on the question of the resurrection*; ; [JHN 8:7](https://ref.ly/John8:7) *regarding the woman caught in adultery*

### Further examples of astuteness

[1SA 23:22–23:23](https://ref.ly/1Sam23:22-1Sam23:23) *David's shrewdness in fleeing from Saul*; ; [2SA 14:1–14:3](https://ref.ly/2Sam14:1-2Sam14:3) *Joab's ruse to persuade David to call Absalom back from exile*; ; [1KI 3:28](https://ref.ly/1Kgs3:28) *Solomon's wise judgment*; ; [2CH 11:23](https://ref.ly/2Chr11:23) *Rehoboam acts wisely to secure his kingdom*; ; [DAN 1:11–1:13](https://ref.ly/Dan1:11-Dan1:13) *Daniel avoids eating royal food and wine*; ; [MAT 2:16](https://ref.ly/Matt2:16) *Herod is outwitted by the Magi*; ; [MRK 7:27–7:29](https://ref.ly/Mark7:27-Mark7:29) parallel [MAT 15:26–15:28](https://ref.ly/Matt15:26-Matt15:28) *the Syro\-Phoenician woman's wise response*

Shrewdness as crafty and deceitful scheming
-------------------------------------------

### It demonstrates human sinfulness

**[PSA 64:6](https://ref.ly/Ps64:6)** *See also* [JOB 15:5](https://ref.ly/Job15:5); [PSA 83:3](https://ref.ly/Ps83:3), [PSA 140:1–140:3](https://ref.ly/Ps140:1-Ps140:3); [PRO 7:10](https://ref.ly/Prov7:10); [ACT 13:10](https://ref.ly/Acts13:10); [EPH 4:14](https://ref.ly/Eph4:14)

### It is condemned by God

**[2SA 22:26–22:27](https://ref.ly/2Sam22:26-2Sam22:27)** parallel [PSA 18:25–18:26](https://ref.ly/Ps18:25-Ps18:26) *God is shrewd in dealing with those who cleverly deceive others*; **[JOB 5:12–5:13](https://ref.ly/Job5:12-Job5:13)** *See also* [PSA 94:11](https://ref.ly/Ps94:11); [PRO 6:12–6:15](https://ref.ly/Prov6:12-Prov6:15), [PRO 12:2](https://ref.ly/Prov12:2); [ISA 5:21](https://ref.ly/Isa5:21), [ISA 44:24–44:25](https://ref.ly/Isa44:24-Isa44:25); [1CO 1:19](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:19); [ISA 29:14](https://ref.ly/Isa29:14); [1CO 3:19–3:20](https://ref.ly/1Cor3:19-1Cor3:20)

### Others dislike it

[PRO 14:17](https://ref.ly/Prov14:17), [PRO 25:23](https://ref.ly/Prov25:23)

### It is a characteristic of Satan

**[2CO 11:3](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:3)** *See also* [GEN 3:1](https://ref.ly/Gen3:1); [2CO 2:11](https://ref.ly/2Cor2:11), [2CO 11:13–11:15](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:13-2Cor11:15)

### The craftiness of Jesus Christ's enemies

[MAT 22:15–22:17](https://ref.ly/Matt22:15-Matt22:17) parallels [MRK 12:13–12:15](https://ref.ly/Mark12:13-Mark12:15), [LUK 20:20–20:22](https://ref.ly/Luke20:20-Luke20:22) *The Pharisees try to trap Jesus Christ over paying taxes*; ; [MAT 26:3–26:4](https://ref.ly/Matt26:3-Matt26:4) parallels [MRK 14:1–14:2](https://ref.ly/Mark14:1-Mark14:2), [LUK 22:1–22:2](https://ref.ly/Luke22:1-Luke22:2) *The religious leaders plot Jesus Christ's death.*

### Further examples of crafty scheming

[GEN 25:31–25:33](https://ref.ly/Gen25:31-Gen25:33) *Jacob buys Esau's birthright*; ; [GEN 27:15–27:16](https://ref.ly/Gen27:15-Gen27:16) *Isaac is tricked into blessing Jacob*; ; [EXO 1:10](https://ref.ly/Exod1:10) *Pharaoh plans to reduce the number of Israelites*; ; [JOS 9:3–9:6](https://ref.ly/Josh9:3-Josh9:6) *The Gibeonites deceive Joshua*; ; [2SA 13:3](https://ref.ly/2Sam13:3) *Jonadab plots for Amnon against Tamar*; ; [NEH 6:2–6:13](https://ref.ly/Neh6:2-Neh6:13) *schemes against Nehemiah*; ; [2CO 12:16](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:16) *Paul sarcastically echoes the slander against him that he tricked people into giving him money.*
