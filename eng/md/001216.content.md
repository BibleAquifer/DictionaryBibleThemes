## 5669: children, examples of (id: 5669)

In Scripture specific children are marked out as belonging to the Lord. Mention of others is made in association with miracles or notable situations.

The child Jesus Christ
----------------------

**[MAT 1:23](https://ref.ly/Matt1:23)** *See also* [MAT 2:11](https://ref.ly/Matt2:11); [LUK 2:34](https://ref.ly/Luke2:34), [LUK 2:49–2:51](https://ref.ly/Luke2:49-Luke2:51)

Other notable children
----------------------

[EXO 2:7–2:8](https://ref.ly/Exod2:7-Exod2:8) *Moses*; ; [JDG 11:36](https://ref.ly/Judg11:36) *Jephthah's daughter*; [1SA 2:18](https://ref.ly/1Sam2:18), [1SA 2:26](https://ref.ly/1Sam2:26); [1SA 16:11](https://ref.ly/1Sam16:11), [1SA 17:33](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:33), [1SA 17:41–17:42](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:41-1Sam17:42); [2KI 5:2](https://ref.ly/2Kgs5:2) *the young girl who helped Naaman*; ; [LUK 1:80](https://ref.ly/Luke1:80) *John the Baptist*; ; [2TI 3:15](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:15) *Timothy*
