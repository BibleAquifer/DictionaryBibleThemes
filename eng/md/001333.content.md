## 5811: compromise (id: 5811)

Usually a combination of contrasting values, practices or persons incurring God's disapproval, but it can also be a positive way to achieve a greater good.

God forbids compromising associations
-------------------------------------

### Israel was not to associate with paganism

**[EXO 20:3](https://ref.ly/Exod20:3)** parallel [DEU 5:7](https://ref.ly/Deut5:7) *See also* [EXO 23:32–23:33](https://ref.ly/Exod23:32-Exod23:33), [EXO 34:12–34:16](https://ref.ly/Exod34:12-Exod34:16); [DEU 7:2–7:4](https://ref.ly/Deut7:2-Deut7:4); [1KI 18:21](https://ref.ly/1Kgs18:21)

### Christians must not compromise their allegiance

**[JAS 4:4](https://ref.ly/Jas4:4)** *See also* [MAT 6:24](https://ref.ly/Matt6:24) parallel [LUK 16:13](https://ref.ly/Luke16:13); [ROM 13:14](https://ref.ly/Rom13:14); [2CO 6:14–6:16](https://ref.ly/2Cor6:14-2Cor6:16); [GAL 1:6–1:9](https://ref.ly/Gal1:6-Gal1:9); [JAS 4:8](https://ref.ly/Jas4:8)
