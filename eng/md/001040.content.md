## 5465: profit (id: 5465)

In the financial sense, gain through trading. The word is also used in Scripture of spiritual gain.

-----------------

### As the result of hard work

**[PRO 14:23](https://ref.ly/Prov14:23), [PRO 21:5](https://ref.ly/Prov21:5)** *See also* [PRO 31:18](https://ref.ly/Prov31:18)

### As the reward of generosity

[PRO 11:24](https://ref.ly/Prov11:24); ; [LUK 6:38](https://ref.ly/Luke6:38)

### As the gift of God

[DEU 8:18](https://ref.ly/Deut8:18)
