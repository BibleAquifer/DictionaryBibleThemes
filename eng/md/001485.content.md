## 5973: unreliability (id: 5973)

The quality of a person or thing that cannot be trusted or relied upon. Scripture notes the unreliability of human nature, while stressing the total reliability of God.

Human nature is unreliable
--------------------------

**[PSA 78:57](https://ref.ly/Ps78:57)** *See also* [ISA 49:15](https://ref.ly/Isa49:15); [HOS 6:4](https://ref.ly/Hos6:4); [2CO 1:9](https://ref.ly/2Cor1:9)
