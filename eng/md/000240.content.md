## 2580: Jesus Christ, transfiguration of (id: 2580)

The revelation of the glory of Jesus Christ, shortly before his death, at which his disciples caught sight of him in his full majesty. The transfiguration brought home to the disciples that Jesus Christ is the Son of God.

The glory of Jesus Christ is revealed at the transfiguration
------------------------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ prepares for his glory to be revealed

**[LUK 9:28](https://ref.ly/Luke9:28)** parallels [MAT 17:1](https://ref.ly/Matt17:1), [MRK 9:2](https://ref.ly/Mark9:2)

### Jesus Christ is visibly transfigured

**[MAT 17:2](https://ref.ly/Matt17:2)** parallels [MRK 9:2–9:3](https://ref.ly/Mark9:2-Mark9:3), [LUK 9:29](https://ref.ly/Luke9:29) *The transfiguration was a glimpse of Jesus Christ's glory whereby his physical appearance was visibly changed.*

### The disciples are eye\-witnesses of Jesus Christ's glory

**[MAT 17:6](https://ref.ly/Matt17:6)** *See also* [MRK 9:9](https://ref.ly/Mark9:9); [LUK 9:32](https://ref.ly/Luke9:32); [JHN 1:14](https://ref.ly/John1:14); [2PE 1:16](https://ref.ly/2Pet1:16)

Moses and Elijah appear at the transfiguration
----------------------------------------------

**[LUK 9:30–9:31](https://ref.ly/Luke9:30-Luke9:31)** parallels [MAT 17:3](https://ref.ly/Matt17:3), [MRK 9:4](https://ref.ly/Mark9:4) *“departure” refers to Jesus Christ's death.*

God's voice is heard at the transfiguration
-------------------------------------------

**[MAT 17:5](https://ref.ly/Matt17:5)** parallels [MRK 9:7](https://ref.ly/Mark9:7), [LUK 9:35](https://ref.ly/Luke9:35) *God the Father's voice is heard authenticating Jesus Christ's ministry.* *See also* [MAT 3:17](https://ref.ly/Matt3:17) parallels [MRK 1:11](https://ref.ly/Mark1:11), [LUK 3:22](https://ref.ly/Luke3:22); [2PE 1:17–1:18](https://ref.ly/2Pet1:17-2Pet1:18)

The disciples' response to the transfiguration
----------------------------------------------

[MAT 17:4](https://ref.ly/Matt17:4) parallels [MRK 9:5–9:6](https://ref.ly/Mark9:5-Mark9:6), [LUK 9:33](https://ref.ly/Luke9:33)

### Fear

[MAT 17:6–17:7](https://ref.ly/Matt17:6-Matt17:7); ; [MRK 9:6](https://ref.ly/Mark9:6); ; [LUK 9:34](https://ref.ly/Luke9:34)

### Questioning and confusion

[MAT 17:10](https://ref.ly/Matt17:10) parallel [MRK 9:10–9:11](https://ref.ly/Mark9:10-Mark9:11)

### They keep to themselves what they have seen and heard

**[LUK 9:36](https://ref.ly/Luke9:36)** parallels [MAT 17:9–17:10](https://ref.ly/Matt17:9-Matt17:10), [MRK 9:9–9:10](https://ref.ly/Mark9:9-Mark9:10) *These verses reiterate that Jesus Christ's full glory was hidden from the world until his resurrection.*
