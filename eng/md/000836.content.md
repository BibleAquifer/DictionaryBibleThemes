## 5248: cart (id: 5248)

General description of carts
----------------------------

*See also* [NUM 7:3](https://ref.ly/Num7:3); [1SA 6:7](https://ref.ly/1Sam6:7), [1SA 6:14](https://ref.ly/1Sam6:14) *made of wood*; [ISA 5:18](https://ref.ly/Isa5:18) *pulled by ropes*; [ISA 28:27–28:28](https://ref.ly/Isa28:27-Isa28:28) *equipped with heavy wheels*

Carts as a means of transport
-----------------------------

### Carts used to transport people

**[GEN 46:5](https://ref.ly/Gen46:5)** *See also* [GEN 45:19–45:21](https://ref.ly/Gen45:19-Gen45:21), [GEN 45:27](https://ref.ly/Gen45:27); [ISA 66:20](https://ref.ly/Isa66:20)

### Carts used as military transport

**[EZK 23:24](https://ref.ly/Ezek23:24)** *See also* [EZK 26:10](https://ref.ly/Ezek26:10)

**[AMO 2:13](https://ref.ly/Amos2:13)** *See also* [PSA 65:11](https://ref.ly/Ps65:11)

**[1SA 6:8](https://ref.ly/1Sam6:8)** *The Philistines send the ark back to Israel.* *See also* [1SA 6:10–6:11](https://ref.ly/1Sam6:10-1Sam6:11); [2SA 6:2–6:3](https://ref.ly/2Sam6:2-2Sam6:3) parallel [1CH 13:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr13:7) *The ark is started on its journey to Jerusalem.*
