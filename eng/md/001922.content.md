## 7759: preachers, qualifications for (id: 7759)

Those entrusted with the task of preaching must ensure that their lives are in line with their message. They must be of good character and conduct, be consistent in all they teach and do and be accountable to others.

Preachers must be of good character
-----------------------------------

**[1TI 4:12](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:12)** *See also* [1TH 2:9–2:10](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:9-1Thess2:10); [1TI 4:16](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:16); [2TI 2:20–2:26](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:20-2Tim2:26); [TIT 2:7–2:8](https://ref.ly/Titus2:7-Titus2:8); [JAS 3:1](https://ref.ly/Jas3:1) *That the judgment is based on character, and not just on teaching, seems clear from the references to guarding the tongue which follow.*

Preachers must practise what they preach
----------------------------------------

**[MAT 23:2–23:4](https://ref.ly/Matt23:2-Matt23:4)** *See also* [ROM 2:21–2:23](https://ref.ly/Rom2:21-Rom2:23); [GAL 2:11–2:14](https://ref.ly/Gal2:11-Gal2:14)

Preachers must be accountable to others and to God
--------------------------------------------------

**[MRK 6:30](https://ref.ly/Mark6:30)** parallel [LUK 9:10](https://ref.ly/Luke9:10) *See also* [ACT 14:26–14:27](https://ref.ly/Acts14:26-Acts14:27), [ACT 21:17–21:19](https://ref.ly/Acts21:17-Acts21:19); [GAL 2:1–2:2](https://ref.ly/Gal2:1-Gal2:2)

Preachers must not look for honour for themselves
-------------------------------------------------

**[MRK 12:38–12:40](https://ref.ly/Mark12:38-Mark12:40)** parallels [MAT 23:5–23:12](https://ref.ly/Matt23:5-Matt23:12), [LUK 20:45–20:47](https://ref.ly/Luke20:45-Luke20:47) *See also* [ACT 14:11–14:15](https://ref.ly/Acts14:11-Acts14:15); [1CO 3:5–3:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor3:5-1Cor3:6); [1TH 2:3–2:6](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:3-1Thess2:6)

Preachers must not look for personal gain
-----------------------------------------

**[2CO 2:17](https://ref.ly/2Cor2:17)** *See also* [ACT 20:33–20:35](https://ref.ly/Acts20:33-Acts20:35); [2CO 11:7–11:9](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:7-2Cor11:9); [1TH 2:6–2:9](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:6-1Thess2:9)
