## 6646: eternal life, gift of (id: 6646)

Eternal life cannot be earned or merited but must be received as a gift from God.

Fruitless attempts to earn eternal life
---------------------------------------

**[JHN 5:39–5:40](https://ref.ly/John5:39-John5:40)** *It is possible to study the mere letter of Scripture while ignoring coming to its focus, Jesus Christ*; **[GAL 3:11–3:12](https://ref.ly/Gal3:11-Gal3:12)** *See also* [ROM 3:20](https://ref.ly/Rom3:20), [ROM 3:28](https://ref.ly/Rom3:28)

Jesus Christ is the source of eternal life
------------------------------------------

**[1JN 5:11–5:12](https://ref.ly/1John5:11-1John5:12)** *See also* [JHN 11:25–11:26](https://ref.ly/John11:25-John11:26), [JHN 14:6](https://ref.ly/John14:6); [ROM 5:21](https://ref.ly/Rom5:21); [HEB 5:9](https://ref.ly/Heb5:9), [HEB 9:12](https://ref.ly/Heb9:12); [2TI 1:1](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:1), [2TI 1:10](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:10); [1JN 5:20](https://ref.ly/1John5:20)

Eternal life is a gift

**[ROM 6:23](https://ref.ly/Rom6:23)** *See also* [JHN 6:27](https://ref.ly/John6:27), [JHN 10:28](https://ref.ly/John10:28); [ROM 2:7](https://ref.ly/Rom2:7), [ROM 5:15–5:17](https://ref.ly/Rom5:15-Rom5:17); [EPH 2:8–2:9](https://ref.ly/Eph2:8-Eph2:9); [1PE 3:7](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:7); [2PE 1:3](https://ref.ly/2Pet1:3)
