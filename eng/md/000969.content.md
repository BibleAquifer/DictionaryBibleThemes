## 5386: leisure, nature and purpose of (id: 5386)

Scripture affirms that periods of rest and leisure are part of God's creation order. These periods of rest are to be used wisely.

The need for leisure
--------------------

### The Sabbath rest

**[GEN 2:2](https://ref.ly/Gen2:2)**; **[EXO 20:8–20:10](https://ref.ly/Exod20:8-Exod20:10)** parallel [DEU 5:12–5:15](https://ref.ly/Deut5:12-Deut5:15) *See also* [EXO 23:11](https://ref.ly/Exod23:11) *During the sabbath year, the labourers presumably also rested*; [LEV 23:3](https://ref.ly/Lev23:3)
