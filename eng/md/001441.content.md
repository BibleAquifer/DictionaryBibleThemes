## 5927: resentment (id: 5927)

A feeling of bitterness towards someone, usually as a result of their actions or words. Resentment is possible against God or against other people.

* **Associated Articles:** 5928: resentment, against God (ID: `5928@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 5929: resentment, against people (ID: `5929@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
