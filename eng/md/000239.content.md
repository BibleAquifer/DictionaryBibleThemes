## 2575: Jesus Christ, temptation of (id: 2575)

Repeated yet unsuccessful efforts made by Satan and by human beings to deflect Jesus Christ from his Father's will.

The wilderness temptations
--------------------------

**[MRK 1:13](https://ref.ly/Mark1:13)**

### Tempted to turn stones into bread

**[MAT 4:2–4:3](https://ref.ly/Matt4:2-Matt4:3)** parallel [LUK 4:2–4:3](https://ref.ly/Luke4:2-Luke4:3)

### Tempted to abuse miraculous power

**[MAT 4:5–4:6](https://ref.ly/Matt4:5-Matt4:6)** parallel [LUK 4:9–4:11](https://ref.ly/Luke4:9-Luke4:11) *See also* [MAT 12:38](https://ref.ly/Matt12:38), [MAT 16:1](https://ref.ly/Matt16:1) parallel [MRK 8:11](https://ref.ly/Mark8:11); [LUK 11:16](https://ref.ly/Luke11:16); [JHN 2:18](https://ref.ly/John2:18)

### Tempted by the offer of an easy route to power

**[MAT 4:8–4:9](https://ref.ly/Matt4:8-Matt4:9)** parallel [LUK 4:5–4:7](https://ref.ly/Luke4:5-Luke4:7)

### Tempted to doubt he was God's Son

**[MAT 4:3](https://ref.ly/Matt4:3)** parallel [LUK 4:3](https://ref.ly/Luke4:3) *See also* [MAT 4:6](https://ref.ly/Matt4:6) parallel [LUK 4:9](https://ref.ly/Luke4:9); [MAT 27:40](https://ref.ly/Matt27:40)
