## 5820: criticism (id: 5820)

The judgment or evaluation of others. The word often has negative associations, implying a harsh or disapproving estimate of people or their actions.

* **Associated Articles:** 5821: criticism, amongst believers (ID: `5821@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 5822: criticism, against believers (ID: `5822@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
