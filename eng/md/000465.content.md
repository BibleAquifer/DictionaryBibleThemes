## 4500: poison (id: 4500)

A substance obtained from plants or animals that can cause illness, pain, disability or death. It is often used symbolically to refer to distress or bitterness.

Kinds of poison
---------------

### Poison from plants, water or food

[EXO 15:23](https://ref.ly/Exod15:23); ; [DEU 32:32](https://ref.ly/Deut32:32); ; [2KI 2:19–2:22](https://ref.ly/2Kgs2:19-2Kgs2:22), [2KI 4:39–4:40](https://ref.ly/2Kgs4:39-2Kgs4:40); ; [HOS 10:4](https://ref.ly/Hos10:4)

### Poison from animals or reptiles

[NUM 21:6](https://ref.ly/Num21:6); ; [DEU 32:24](https://ref.ly/Deut32:24), [DEU 32:33](https://ref.ly/Deut32:33)

Poison used symbolically
------------------------

### To refer to distress

In these incidences God himself is portrayed as the one who brings such bitter experiences: [JOB 6:4](https://ref.ly/Job6:4); ; [JER 8:14](https://ref.ly/Jer8:14), [JER 9:15](https://ref.ly/Jer9:15), [JER 23:15](https://ref.ly/Jer23:15)

### To refer to evil and its effects

**[JOB 20:12–20:16](https://ref.ly/Job20:12-Job20:16)** *See also* [DEU 29:18](https://ref.ly/Deut29:18), [DEU 32:32–32:33](https://ref.ly/Deut32:32-Deut32:33); [PSA 58:4](https://ref.ly/Ps58:4); [PRO 23:32](https://ref.ly/Prov23:32); [ISA 14:29](https://ref.ly/Isa14:29); [JER 8:17](https://ref.ly/Jer8:17); [AMO 6:12](https://ref.ly/Amos6:12); [ACT 14:2](https://ref.ly/Acts14:2); [ROM 3:13](https://ref.ly/Rom3:13); [PSA 140:1–140:3](https://ref.ly/Ps140:1-Ps140:3); [JAS 3:8](https://ref.ly/Jas3:8); [REV 9:5](https://ref.ly/Rev9:5)
