## 5892: instinct (id: 5892)

An innate urge, rooted in natural conditioning rather than reason. Scripture likens sinners to creatures of instinct.

Animals are creatures of instinct
---------------------------------

**[2PE 2:12](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:12)** *See also* [JUD 1:10](https://ref.ly/Jude1:10)

Sinners are likened to creatures of instinct
--------------------------------------------

**[JUD 1:10](https://ref.ly/Jude1:10)**

People without the Holy Spirit are likened to creatures of instinct
-------------------------------------------------------------------

**[JUD 1:19](https://ref.ly/Jude1:19)**

Believers ought instinctively to obey God
-----------------------------------------

**[ISA 1:3](https://ref.ly/Isa1:3)** *Just as the animals instinctively know their masters, so Israel should know and obey God.* *See also* [JER 8:7](https://ref.ly/Jer8:7)
