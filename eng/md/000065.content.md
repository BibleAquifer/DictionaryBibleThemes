## 1330: God, the provider (id: 1330)

God supplies the needs of all creation, but gives special care to his own people.

God is called \`\`The LORD who provides''
-----------------------------------------

**[GEN 22:13–22:14](https://ref.ly/Gen22:13-Gen22:14)** *The name “Yahweh Yireh” (or “Jehovah Jireh”) is from the verb “to see”, and means “The LORD foresees” or “The LORD will see to it”.* *See also* [GEN 22:8](https://ref.ly/Gen22:8)
