## 5273: creativity (id: 5273)

Imagination and skill used in the expression of visual or performing arts. Human creativity has its source in God, but may be used either to his honour or dishonour.

God's creativity
----------------

**[ISA 64:8](https://ref.ly/Isa64:8)** *See also* [JOB 38:1–38:41](https://ref.ly/Job38:1-Job38:41); [PSA 139:13–139:16](https://ref.ly/Ps139:13-Ps139:16); [ECC 3:11](https://ref.ly/Eccl3:11); [ISA 45:7–45:12](https://ref.ly/Isa45:7-Isa45:12), [ISA 45:18](https://ref.ly/Isa45:18); [EPH 2:10](https://ref.ly/Eph2:10) *Redemption may be described in terms of re\-creation.*
