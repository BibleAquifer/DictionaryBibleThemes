## 6708: predestination (id: 6708)

God's foreordination of all events and circumstances for the good of his people and the glory of his name.

Predestination depends on God's sovereignty
-------------------------------------------

### He is the Creator of all things

**[JER 32:17–32:19](https://ref.ly/Jer32:17-Jer32:19)** *See also* [GEN 1:1](https://ref.ly/Gen1:1); [JOB 38:1–38:4](https://ref.ly/Job38:1-Job38:4); [ISA 44:24–44:28](https://ref.ly/Isa44:24-Isa44:28), [ISA 45:12–45:13](https://ref.ly/Isa45:12-Isa45:13), [ISA 48:12–48:14](https://ref.ly/Isa48:12-Isa48:14)

### He rules over nature and history

**[PRO 16:4](https://ref.ly/Prov16:4)** *See also* [GEN 18:10–18:14](https://ref.ly/Gen18:10-Gen18:14); [PSA 67:4](https://ref.ly/Ps67:4); [PRO 16:9](https://ref.ly/Prov16:9), [PRO 16:33](https://ref.ly/Prov16:33), [PRO 21:1](https://ref.ly/Prov21:1); [MAT 8:23–8:27](https://ref.ly/Matt8:23-Matt8:27) parallels [MRK 4:35–4:41](https://ref.ly/Mark4:35-Mark4:41), [LUK 8:22–8:25](https://ref.ly/Luke8:22-Luke8:25); [MAT 10:29–10:30](https://ref.ly/Matt10:29-Matt10:30); [ACT 17:26](https://ref.ly/Acts17:26)

**[GEN 18:25](https://ref.ly/Gen18:25)** *See also* [EXO 33:19](https://ref.ly/Exod33:19); [DEU 32:4](https://ref.ly/Deut32:4); [JOB 8:3](https://ref.ly/Job8:3); [PSA 119:137](https://ref.ly/Ps119:137); [DAN 4:37](https://ref.ly/Dan4:37)
