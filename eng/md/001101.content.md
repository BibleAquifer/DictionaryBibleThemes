## 5533: sleep, physical (id: 5533)

A gift from God providing necessary rest, usually at night, for the restoration of mind and body. It is sometimes denied, sometimes used by God for his purposes and sometimes abused by people.

### It is pleasant and refreshing

**[ECC 5:12](https://ref.ly/Eccl5:12)** *See also* [1KI 19:5](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:5); [JER 31:26](https://ref.ly/Jer31:26); [JHN 11:12–11:13](https://ref.ly/John11:12-John11:13)

### It is God's gift to his faithful people

**[PSA 127:2](https://ref.ly/Ps127:2)** *See also* [PRO 3:21–3:24](https://ref.ly/Prov3:21-Prov3:24)
