## 1355: providence (id: 1355)

The continuing and often unseen activity of God in sustaining his universe, providing for the needs of every creature, and preparing for the completion of his eternal purposes.

God's general providence
------------------------

### God sustains the created order

**[GEN 8:22](https://ref.ly/Gen8:22)** *A promise given by God to all peoples.* *See also* [NEH 9:6](https://ref.ly/Neh9:6); [ISA 40:26](https://ref.ly/Isa40:26); [COL 1:17](https://ref.ly/Col1:17); [HEB 1:3](https://ref.ly/Heb1:3)

God's providence through miraculous means
-----------------------------------------

**[JOB 5:9–5:10](https://ref.ly/Job5:9-Job5:10)** *God's regular activity in providing for human beings is regarded as being miraculous in just the same way as special demonstrations of his power.* *See also* [EXO 16:11–16:14](https://ref.ly/Exod16:11-Exod16:14); [NUM 16:28–16:35](https://ref.ly/Num16:28-Num16:35); [JDG 15:18–15:19](https://ref.ly/Judg15:18-Judg15:19); [1KI 17:5–17:6](https://ref.ly/1Kgs17:5-1Kgs17:6); [2KI 4:42–4:44](https://ref.ly/2Kgs4:42-2Kgs4:44)
