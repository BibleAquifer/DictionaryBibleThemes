## 2224: Jesus Christ, the Lord (id: 2224)

A title that signifies Jesus Christ's absolute authority and the basis on which people may know him. It is especially associated with his resurrection and return.

Lord as a title of respect for the earthly Jesus Christ
-------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 8:25](https://ref.ly/Matt8:25)** *See also* [MAT 15:25](https://ref.ly/Matt15:25), [MAT 15:27](https://ref.ly/Matt15:27) parallel [MRK 7:28](https://ref.ly/Mark7:28); [MAT 21:3](https://ref.ly/Matt21:3) parallels [MRK 11:3](https://ref.ly/Mark11:3), [LUK 19:31](https://ref.ly/Luke19:31)

Lord as a mark of Jesus Christ's authority as a teacher
-------------------------------------------------------

**[1TH 4:15](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:15)** *See also* [ACT 11:16](https://ref.ly/Acts11:16), [ACT 20:35](https://ref.ly/Acts20:35); [1CO 7:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:10)

Lord as a sign of Jesus Christ's divinity
-----------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ's divinity

**[JHN 20:28](https://ref.ly/John20:28)** *“Lord” is a common divine title in the Greek OT and is instinctively transferred to Jesus Christ in the NT.* *See also* [1CO 8:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:6); [2CO 3:17–3:18](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:17-2Cor3:18); [EPH 4:5](https://ref.ly/Eph4:5); [2TH 2:16](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:16); [2PE 1:2](https://ref.ly/2Pet1:2)

### The day of Christ Jesus

**[PHP 1:6](https://ref.ly/Phil1:6)** *The “day of Christ Jesus” is equivalent to the OT “day of the LORD”.* *See also* [1CO 1:8](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:8); [2CO 1:14](https://ref.ly/2Cor1:14); [PHP 1:10](https://ref.ly/Phil1:10), [PHP 2:16](https://ref.ly/Phil2:16); [REV 1:10](https://ref.ly/Rev1:10)

### Jesus Christ is ruler over all

**[REV 17:14](https://ref.ly/Rev17:14)** *See also* [DEU 10:17](https://ref.ly/Deut10:17); [REV 19:16](https://ref.ly/Rev19:16)

### Jesus Christ is the Saviour

**[ROM 10:13](https://ref.ly/Rom10:13)** *See also* [JOL 2:32](https://ref.ly/Joel2:32); [ACT 2:21](https://ref.ly/Acts2:21)

### Jesus Christ is Lord of the Sabbath

**[MAT 12:8](https://ref.ly/Matt12:8)** parallels [MRK 2:28](https://ref.ly/Mark2:28), [LUK 6:5](https://ref.ly/Luke6:5)

-------------------------

### \`\`Jesus is Lord'' is the basic Christian statement of faith
