## 5836: disgrace (id: 5836)

Shame and dishonour that can arise from sin and idolatry. The exile of Israel brought disgrace upon God's name. God's people may suffer disgrace for the sake of Jesus Christ.

Examples of disgrace
--------------------

### Brought on Israel

As the result of Achan stealing what was dedicated to God: [JOS 7:1–7:5](https://ref.ly/Josh7:1-Josh7:5), [JOS 7:11–7:15](https://ref.ly/Josh7:11-Josh7:15); [1SA 17:23–17:26](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:23-1Sam17:26) *as the result of Goliath's boasting*; ; [PSA 44:9–44:19](https://ref.ly/Ps44:9-Ps44:19) *when God no longer acted on Israel's behalf*
