## 5258: cloth (id: 5258)

A woven material from which garments or coverings were made. Different kinds of material are associated with different ranks and social positions.

Cloth for daily wear
--------------------

**[PRO 31:22](https://ref.ly/Prov31:22)** *See also* [NUM 15:38](https://ref.ly/Num15:38); [JDG 5:30](https://ref.ly/Judg5:30), [JDG 14:12](https://ref.ly/Judg14:12); [2SA 1:24](https://ref.ly/2Sam1:24); [1CH 15:27](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:27); [PSA 45:14](https://ref.ly/Ps45:14); [EZK 16:10](https://ref.ly/Ezek16:10), [EZK 16:13](https://ref.ly/Ezek16:13); [MAT 9:16](https://ref.ly/Matt9:16); [JHN 19:23](https://ref.ly/John19:23); [ACT 9:39](https://ref.ly/Acts9:39)
