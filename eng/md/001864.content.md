## 7482: Year of Jubilee (id: 7482)

Celebrated in the fiftieth year as the culmination of seven sabbatical years. During this year property was restored and Hebrew slaves were released. This year of liberation gave opportunity for a new start, and is alluded to as a type of the Messianic age.

The institution of the Year of Jubilee
--------------------------------------

**[LEV 25:8–25:10](https://ref.ly/Lev25:8-Lev25:10)**

Land was left fallow in the Year of Jubilee
-------------------------------------------

*See also* [LEV 25:11–25:12](https://ref.ly/Lev25:11-Lev25:12), [LEV 25:20–25:22](https://ref.ly/Lev25:20-Lev25:22) *These instructions apply to the sabbatical year. The Year of Jubilee may have been an additional fallow year, or may have coincided with the seventh sabbatical year.*

The restoration of property in the Year of Jubilee
--------------------------------------------------

**[LEV 25:13](https://ref.ly/Lev25:13)**

### Regulating the value of property

*See also* [LEV 25:15–25:16](https://ref.ly/Lev25:15-Lev25:16), [LEV 27:16–27:19](https://ref.ly/Lev27:16-Lev27:19), [LEV 27:23](https://ref.ly/Lev27:23)

### Underlining the fact that God is the true owner of the land

**[LEV 25:23–25:24](https://ref.ly/Lev25:23-Lev25:24)** *The restoration of property to those to whom God had originally entrusted it is a reminder that the land belongs to him.* *See also* [JOS 21:43](https://ref.ly/Josh21:43); [1CH 29:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:15); [HEB 11:13](https://ref.ly/Heb11:13)

### A means of preserving the inheritance from God

**[LEV 25:25–25:28](https://ref.ly/Lev25:25-Lev25:28)** *Families are urged to buy back land assigned to them by God. As a last resort, land is restored in the Year of Jubilee.* *See also* [LEV 25:32–25:33](https://ref.ly/Lev25:32-Lev25:33), [LEV 27:24](https://ref.ly/Lev27:24); [NUM 36:4](https://ref.ly/Num36:4), [NUM 36:7–36:9](https://ref.ly/Num36:7-Num36:9); [1KI 21:3](https://ref.ly/1Kgs21:3); [EZK 46:16–46:18](https://ref.ly/Ezek46:16-Ezek46:18)

The release of Hebrew slaves in the Year of Jubilee
---------------------------------------------------

**[DEU 15:12–15:15](https://ref.ly/Deut15:12-Deut15:15)** *The release of slaves reflects God's redemption and preserves the freedom he has given*; **[LEV 25:54–25:55](https://ref.ly/Lev25:54-Lev25:55)** *The Israelites may not be held permanently as slaves because they belong to God.* *See also* [LEV 25:39–25:43](https://ref.ly/Lev25:39-Lev25:43), [LEV 25:50–25:52](https://ref.ly/Lev25:50-Lev25:52); [JER 34:8–34:9](https://ref.ly/Jer34:8-Jer34:9), [JER 34:13–34:14](https://ref.ly/Jer34:13-Jer34:14)

The Year of Jubilee is the year of the Lord's favour
----------------------------------------------------

**[LUK 4:18–4:19](https://ref.ly/Luke4:18-Luke4:19)** *Jesus Christ alludes to the Year of Jubilee in proclaiming spiritual release and restoration.* *See also* [ISA 61:1–61:2](https://ref.ly/Isa61:1-Isa61:2)
