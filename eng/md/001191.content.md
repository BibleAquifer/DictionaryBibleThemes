## 5630: work, divine and human (id: 5630)

God has ordained that some of his purposes be fulfilled through the co\-operation (conscious or unconscious) of human beings.

God works in co\-operation with people's work
---------------------------------------------

### Human labour is futile apart from God

**[PSA 127:1](https://ref.ly/Ps127:1)** *See also* [GEN 11:8](https://ref.ly/Gen11:8); [JHN 15:5](https://ref.ly/John15:5)

**[PSA 77:20](https://ref.ly/Ps77:20)** *See also* [ACT 9:15](https://ref.ly/Acts9:15); [ROM 13:6](https://ref.ly/Rom13:6); [2CO 7:6](https://ref.ly/2Cor7:6)
