## 5467: promises, divine (id: 5467)

The promises of God reveal his particular and eternal purposes to which he is unchangeably committed and upon which believers can totally depend. These promises are, however, conditional upon obedience on the part of believers.

God's promises are irrevocable
------------------------------

### He is absolutely trustworthy

**[NUM 23:19](https://ref.ly/Num23:19)** *See also* [TIT 1:2](https://ref.ly/Titus1:2); [HEB 6:13–6:18](https://ref.ly/Heb6:13-Heb6:18)

### He is unchanging

[PSA 110:4](https://ref.ly/Ps110:4); ; [MAL 3:6–3:7](https://ref.ly/Mal3:6-Mal3:7); ; [JAS 1:17–1:18](https://ref.ly/Jas1:17-Jas1:18)

### He has the power and will to fulfil his promises

**[ISA 55:11](https://ref.ly/Isa55:11)** *See also* [ROM 4:21](https://ref.ly/Rom4:21)

### He is faithful in keeping all his promises

[JOS 21:45](https://ref.ly/Josh21:45), [JOS 23:14–23:15](https://ref.ly/Josh23:14-Josh23:15); ; [1KI 8:56](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:56); ; [PSA 145:13](https://ref.ly/Ps145:13); ; [HEB 10:23](https://ref.ly/Heb10:23)

### His promises stem from his goodness and glory

[2PE 1:3–1:4](https://ref.ly/2Pet1:3-2Pet1:4)

### God may confirm his promises with an oath

**[GEN 22:15–22:18](https://ref.ly/Gen22:15-Gen22:18)** *See also* [GEN 26:3](https://ref.ly/Gen26:3); [ISA 45:23](https://ref.ly/Isa45:23); [AMO 6:8](https://ref.ly/Amos6:8), [AMO 8:7](https://ref.ly/Amos8:7)
