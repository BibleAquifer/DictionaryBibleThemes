## 6728: regeneration (id: 6728)

The radical renewal of a person's inner being by the work of God's Spirit.

The need for regeneration
-------------------------

**[JHN 3:3](https://ref.ly/John3:3)** *See also* [EPH 2:1](https://ref.ly/Eph2:1), [EPH 2:5](https://ref.ly/Eph2:5); [COL 2:13](https://ref.ly/Col2:13)

Regeneration is a work of God
-----------------------------

### It originates in God the Father

**[JHN 1:12–1:13](https://ref.ly/John1:12-John1:13)** *Regeneration cannot be controlled by human actions or rituals; it is an act of God's sovereign will.*

### It is made possible by the resurrection of Jesus Christ

**[1PE 1:3](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:3)** *See also* [EPH 2:4–2:5](https://ref.ly/Eph2:4-Eph2:5)

### It occurs through the hearing of the Christian gospel

**[JAS 1:18](https://ref.ly/Jas1:18)** *See also* [1PE 1:23–1:25](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:23-1Pet1:25) *The “word of God” is the good news about Jesus Christ.*

### It is effected by God's Spirit

**[JHN 3:5–3:8](https://ref.ly/John3:5-John3:8)** *See also* [JHN 6:63](https://ref.ly/John6:63); [TIT 3:5](https://ref.ly/Titus3:5)

Regeneration is given to those who believe in Jesus Christ
----------------------------------------------------------

**[1JN 5:1](https://ref.ly/1John5:1)**

Baptism is the sign of regeneration
-----------------------------------

**[JHN 3:5](https://ref.ly/John3:5)** *Water here is seen by some, though not all, interpreters as a reference to baptism, administered by the church to new converts as a sign of their new birth and their entry into the Christian community.* *See also* [ACT 2:38–2:39](https://ref.ly/Acts2:38-Acts2:39); [EPH 5:25–5:26](https://ref.ly/Eph5:25-Eph5:26); [TIT 3:5](https://ref.ly/Titus3:5)

The results of regeneration
---------------------------

### Entry into God's kingdom

**[JHN 3:5](https://ref.ly/John3:5)**

### A new holiness of life

**[1JN 3:9](https://ref.ly/1John3:9)** *See also* [1JN 5:18](https://ref.ly/1John5:18); [1PE 2:1–2:2](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:1-1Pet2:2)

### Love for other people

**[1JN 4:7](https://ref.ly/1John4:7)** *See also* [1JN 5:2](https://ref.ly/1John5:2)

### Victory over the world's sinful pattern of life

**[1JN 5:4](https://ref.ly/1John5:4)**
