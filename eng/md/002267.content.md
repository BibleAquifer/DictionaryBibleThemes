## 8485: spiritual warfare, as conflict (id: 8485)

The warfare believers must fight is spiritual, personal, intense and continual. It calls for courage, determination and prayer, and therefore believers must stand in God's strength and use the armour he has provided.

**[EPH 6:12](https://ref.ly/Eph6:12)** *See also* [ZEC 4:6](https://ref.ly/Zech4:6); [2CO 10:3–10:4](https://ref.ly/2Cor10:3-2Cor10:4); [GAL 5:17](https://ref.ly/Gal5:17); [1PE 2:11](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:11)
