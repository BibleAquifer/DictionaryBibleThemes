## 5053: responsibility, for God's world (id: 5053)

All humanity has a moral obligation under God to one another, whether as national leaders, ministers, spouses, parents, children, believers or simply as human beings. This moral obligation is shown in the Scriptures to also include the animal kingdom and the environment.

The responsibility of national leaders
--------------------------------------

### To reward good and punish evil

**[ROM 13:3–13:4](https://ref.ly/Rom13:3-Rom13:4)** *See also* [PRO 16:12](https://ref.ly/Prov16:12)

### To administer justice

**[PRO 31:4–31:5](https://ref.ly/Prov31:4-Prov31:5), [PRO 31:8–31:9](https://ref.ly/Prov31:8-Prov31:9)** *See also* [DEU 27:19](https://ref.ly/Deut27:19); [PRO 14:31](https://ref.ly/Prov14:31), [PRO 17:5](https://ref.ly/Prov17:5), [PRO 29:4](https://ref.ly/Prov29:4), [PRO 29:14](https://ref.ly/Prov29:14)
