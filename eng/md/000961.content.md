## 5377: law, Ten Commandments (id: 5377)

The basic laws given to Israel through Moses following the exodus from Egypt ([Ex 20:1–17](https://ref.ly/Exod20:1-Exod20:17); [Dt 5:6–21](https://ref.ly/Deut5:6-Deut5:21)). The first four commandments safeguard Israel's special relation to God; the remaining six protect individuals within the community and promote their well\-being.

The Ten Commandments are to govern the life of Israel as the people of God
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Israel should obey God alone

**[EXO 20:2–20:3](https://ref.ly/Exod20:2-Exod20:3)** parallel [DEU 5:6–5:7](https://ref.ly/Deut5:6-Deut5:7) *See also* [DEU 6:13–6:15](https://ref.ly/Deut6:13-Deut6:15)

### Idolatry forbidden

**[EXO 20:4–20:6](https://ref.ly/Exod20:4-Exod20:6)** parallel [DEU 5:8–5:10](https://ref.ly/Deut5:8-Deut5:10) *See also* [EXO 32:1–32:8](https://ref.ly/Exod32:1-Exod32:8); [LEV 19:4](https://ref.ly/Lev19:4); [1CO 10:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:7)

### God's name should not be misused

**[EXO 20:7](https://ref.ly/Exod20:7)** parallel [DEU 5:11](https://ref.ly/Deut5:11) *See also* [MAT 7:21](https://ref.ly/Matt7:21)

### A day of rest is commanded

**[EXO 20:8–20:11](https://ref.ly/Exod20:8-Exod20:11)** parallel [DEU 5:12–5:15](https://ref.ly/Deut5:12-Deut5:15) *See also* [EXO 16:23](https://ref.ly/Exod16:23); [LEV 19:3](https://ref.ly/Lev19:3); [ISA 56:2](https://ref.ly/Isa56:2); [JER 17:21–17:22](https://ref.ly/Jer17:21-Jer17:22)

### Parents are to be honoured

**[EXO 20:12](https://ref.ly/Exod20:12)** parallel [DEU 5:16](https://ref.ly/Deut5:16) *See also* [MAT 15:4](https://ref.ly/Matt15:4); [EPH 6:1–6:3](https://ref.ly/Eph6:1-Eph6:3)

### Murder is forbidden

**[EXO 20:13](https://ref.ly/Exod20:13)** parallel [DEU 5:17](https://ref.ly/Deut5:17) *See also* [GEN 4:8–4:16](https://ref.ly/Gen4:8-Gen4:16); [MAT 5:21](https://ref.ly/Matt5:21)

### Adultery is forbidden

**[EXO 20:14](https://ref.ly/Exod20:14)** parallel [DEU 5:18](https://ref.ly/Deut5:18) *See also* [LEV 18:20](https://ref.ly/Lev18:20); [2SA 11:2–11:5](https://ref.ly/2Sam11:2-2Sam11:5); [MAT 5:27](https://ref.ly/Matt5:27); [HEB 13:4](https://ref.ly/Heb13:4)

### Stealing is forbidden

**[EXO 20:15](https://ref.ly/Exod20:15)** parallel [DEU 5:19](https://ref.ly/Deut5:19) *See also* [LEV 19:11](https://ref.ly/Lev19:11), [LEV 19:13](https://ref.ly/Lev19:13)

### False witness is forbidden

**[EXO 20:16](https://ref.ly/Exod20:16)** parallel [DEU 5:20](https://ref.ly/Deut5:20) *See also* [LEV 19:11](https://ref.ly/Lev19:11)

### Coveting is forbidden

**[EXO 20:17](https://ref.ly/Exod20:17)** parallel [DEU 5:21](https://ref.ly/Deut5:21) *See also* [LEV 19:17–19:18](https://ref.ly/Lev19:17-Lev19:18); [JOB 31:9–31:12](https://ref.ly/Job31:9-Job31:12); [ROM 7:7](https://ref.ly/Rom7:7)
