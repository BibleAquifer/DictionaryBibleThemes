## 5078: Abraham, significance of (id: 5078)

Whereas Jews see Abraham as the father of their nation on account of God's covenant with him, Christians see Abraham as the father of all who believe in Jesus Christ.

The significance of Abraham for nations other than Israel
---------------------------------------------------------

### Abraham was the father of many nations

**[GEN 17:3–17:6](https://ref.ly/Gen17:3-Gen17:6)** *See also* [GEN 17:20](https://ref.ly/Gen17:20), [GEN 25:1–25:4](https://ref.ly/Gen25:1-Gen25:4), [GEN 25:12–25:18](https://ref.ly/Gen25:12-Gen25:18) *Muslim Arabs trace their ancestry through Ishmael to Abraham.*

### Abraham was to be a means of blessing to many nations

**[GEN 12:3](https://ref.ly/Gen12:3)** *See also* [GEN 18:18](https://ref.ly/Gen18:18), [GEN 22:17–22:18](https://ref.ly/Gen22:17-Gen22:18); [ACT 3:25](https://ref.ly/Acts3:25)

The significance of Abraham for the people of Israel
----------------------------------------------------

### Israel traces its origins back to God's call of Abraham

**[GEN 12:1–12:2](https://ref.ly/Gen12:1-Gen12:2)** *See also* [JOS 24:2–24:3](https://ref.ly/Josh24:2-Josh24:3); [ACT 7:2–7:3](https://ref.ly/Acts7:2-Acts7:3); [HEB 11:8–11:10](https://ref.ly/Heb11:8-Heb11:10)

### God promises the land of Canaan to Abraham's descendants

**[NEH 9:7–9:8](https://ref.ly/Neh9:7-Neh9:8)** *See also* [GEN 15:1–15:21](https://ref.ly/Gen15:1-Gen15:21), [GEN 17:1–17:8](https://ref.ly/Gen17:1-Gen17:8); [EXO 6:8](https://ref.ly/Exod6:8); [DEU 9:5](https://ref.ly/Deut9:5); [ACT 7:4–7:5](https://ref.ly/Acts7:4-Acts7:5)

### God is subsequently known in Israel as the God of Abraham

**[1KI 18:36](https://ref.ly/1Kgs18:36)** *See also* [GEN 24:26–24:27](https://ref.ly/Gen24:26-Gen24:27), [GEN 26:24](https://ref.ly/Gen26:24), [GEN 31:53](https://ref.ly/Gen31:53); [PSA 47:9](https://ref.ly/Ps47:9); [ACT 7:32](https://ref.ly/Acts7:32); [EXO 3:6](https://ref.ly/Exod3:6)

### Abraham is seen as the father of the Jewish nation

**[JHN 8:39](https://ref.ly/John8:39)** *See also* [PSA 105:5–105:6](https://ref.ly/Ps105:5-Ps105:6); [ISA 41:8](https://ref.ly/Isa41:8), [ISA 51:1–51:2](https://ref.ly/Isa51:1-Isa51:2); [LUK 1:54–1:55](https://ref.ly/Luke1:54-Luke1:55); [JHN 8:33](https://ref.ly/John8:33); [ACT 13:26](https://ref.ly/Acts13:26); [ROM 11:1](https://ref.ly/Rom11:1)

### God deals with Israel on the basis of his covenant with Abraham

**[1CH 16:14–16:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:14-1Chr16:18)** parallel [PSA 105:7–105:11](https://ref.ly/Ps105:7-Ps105:11) *See also* [EXO 2:23–2:25](https://ref.ly/Exod2:23-Exod2:25); [2KI 13:22–13:23](https://ref.ly/2Kgs13:22-2Kgs13:23); [PSA 105:42–105:45](https://ref.ly/Ps105:42-Ps105:45); [LUK 1:68–1:75](https://ref.ly/Luke1:68-Luke1:75)

### Circumcision was the sign of participation in God's covenant with Abraham

**[GEN 17:9–17:14](https://ref.ly/Gen17:9-Gen17:14)** *See also* [LEV 12:3](https://ref.ly/Lev12:3); [JHN 7:22](https://ref.ly/John7:22); [ACT 15:1](https://ref.ly/Acts15:1)

The significance of Abraham for Christians
------------------------------------------

### Abraham was an ancestor of Jesus Christ

**[MAT 1:1](https://ref.ly/Matt1:1)** parallel [LUK 3:34](https://ref.ly/Luke3:34)

### Jesus Christ attacks Jewish confidence in mere physical descent from Abraham

**[MAT 3:9–3:10](https://ref.ly/Matt3:9-Matt3:10)** parallel [LUK 3:8–3:9](https://ref.ly/Luke3:8-Luke3:9) *See also* [JHN 8:33–8:41](https://ref.ly/John8:33-John8:41)

### Jesus Christ makes a point of identifying outcasts as children of Abraham

**[LUK 19:7–19:9](https://ref.ly/Luke19:7-Luke19:9)** *See also* [LUK 13:10–13:16](https://ref.ly/Luke13:10-Luke13:16)

### God's promise to Abraham is not inherited by those who obey the law

**[GAL 3:15–3:18](https://ref.ly/Gal3:15-Gal3:18)** *See also* [ROM 4:13–4:16](https://ref.ly/Rom4:13-Rom4:16); [GAL 4:21–4:31](https://ref.ly/Gal4:21-Gal4:31)

### God's promise to Abraham is fulfilled in Jesus Christ, who is Abraham's seed

**[GAL 3:16](https://ref.ly/Gal3:16)** *See also* [GEN 12:7](https://ref.ly/Gen12:7), [GEN 13:15](https://ref.ly/Gen13:15), [GEN 24:7](https://ref.ly/Gen24:7); [GAL 3:19](https://ref.ly/Gal3:19), [GAL 3:29](https://ref.ly/Gal3:29)

### Abraham is the father of those who are justified by faith

**[GAL 3:6–3:9](https://ref.ly/Gal3:6-Gal3:9)** *See also* [GEN 15:6](https://ref.ly/Gen15:6); [ROM 4:1–4:25](https://ref.ly/Rom4:1-Rom4:25)

### Abraham's obedience to God shows that faith must lead to good works

**[JAS 2:20–2:24](https://ref.ly/Jas2:20-Jas2:24)**
