## 9021: death, natural (id: 9021)

The end of life in the present world.

Death is natural to humanity as part of the created world
---------------------------------------------------------

**[ECC 12:7](https://ref.ly/Eccl12:7)** *See also* [PSA 39:5](https://ref.ly/Ps39:5), [PSA 104:29](https://ref.ly/Ps104:29); [ECC 3:18–3:21](https://ref.ly/Eccl3:18-Eccl3:21); [2PE 1:13–1:14](https://ref.ly/2Pet1:13-2Pet1:14)

Death is a result of Adam's sin
-------------------------------

**[ROM 5:12](https://ref.ly/Rom5:12)** *There is a tension between this text and those which suggest that death is a natural aspect of being human. Some suggest that the death referred to here is spiritual rather than physical.* *See also* [GEN 2:17](https://ref.ly/Gen2:17), [GEN 3:19](https://ref.ly/Gen3:19); [ROM 6:23](https://ref.ly/Rom6:23); [1CO 15:21–15:22](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:21-1Cor15:22)

Death is universal
------------------

### None can escape death

**[PSA 89:48](https://ref.ly/Ps89:48)** *See also* [GEN 6:3](https://ref.ly/Gen6:3); [JOS 23:14](https://ref.ly/Josh23:14); [2SA 14:14](https://ref.ly/2Sam14:14); [JOB 30:23](https://ref.ly/Job30:23)

### Death comes to all kinds of people

**[JOB 21:23–21:26](https://ref.ly/Job21:23-Job21:26)** *See also* [2SA 12:15–12:18](https://ref.ly/2Sam12:15-2Sam12:18); [2KI 4:20](https://ref.ly/2Kgs4:20); [PSA 37:35–37:36](https://ref.ly/Ps37:35-Ps37:36), [PSA 49:10](https://ref.ly/Ps49:10); [ECC 3:19](https://ref.ly/Eccl3:19); [LUK 16:22](https://ref.ly/Luke16:22)

### Only two people did not die

**[GEN 5:24](https://ref.ly/Gen5:24)**; **[2KI 2:11](https://ref.ly/2Kgs2:11)** *Enoch and Elijah died in the sense that their bodies were transformed from being suited to earthly life to life in heaven. They anticipated the death of Jesus Christ, whose body was not corrupted but transformed, and the transition to the new world of those who are alive when Jesus Christ comes again.*

Death is irreversible
---------------------

**[HEB 9:27](https://ref.ly/Heb9:27)** *The idea of reincarnation, that after death people repeatedly return to this life in a lower or higher form, is incompatible with death being once for all and an entrance into heaven or hell after the judgment.* *See also* [JOB 16:22](https://ref.ly/Job16:22); [PSA 115:17](https://ref.ly/Ps115:17)

Death is an aspect of human frailty
-----------------------------------

**[PSA 146:3–146:4](https://ref.ly/Ps146:3-Ps146:4)** *See also* [ISA 51:12](https://ref.ly/Isa51:12); [1PE 1:24](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:24); [ISA 40:6–40:8](https://ref.ly/Isa40:6-Isa40:8)

Death occurs when God determines it
-----------------------------------

**[JOB 14:5](https://ref.ly/Job14:5)** *See also* [DEU 32:39](https://ref.ly/Deut32:39); [JOB 38:17](https://ref.ly/Job38:17); [LUK 12:20](https://ref.ly/Luke12:20); [REV 1:18](https://ref.ly/Rev1:18)
