## 8823: self-righteousness (id: 8823)

A moral self\-confidence and superiority arising from satisfaction in one's own achievements. True righteousness can only be found in a relationship with Jesus Christ.

* **Associated Articles:** 8824: self-righteousness, nature of (ID: `8824@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 8825: self-righteousness, and the gospel (ID: `8825@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
