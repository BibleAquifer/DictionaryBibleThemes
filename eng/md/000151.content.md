## 2033: Jesus Christ, humanity of (id: 2033)

Scripture stresses the total humanity of Jesus Christ. Although sinless, Christ shared in the general condition of humanity, including suffering and death.

Jesus Christ was a man
----------------------

### By his own claims

**[JHN 8:40](https://ref.ly/John8:40)** *See also* [MAT 8:20](https://ref.ly/Matt8:20) parallel [LUK 9:58](https://ref.ly/Luke9:58)

### In statements made by others

**[JHN 19:5](https://ref.ly/John19:5)** *See also* [MAT 8:27](https://ref.ly/Matt8:27); [MRK 15:39](https://ref.ly/Mark15:39) parallel [LUK 23:47](https://ref.ly/Luke23:47) *the centurion*; [LUK 15:2](https://ref.ly/Luke15:2) *the Pharisees and teachers of the law*; [LUK 23:18](https://ref.ly/Luke23:18); [JHN 1:33](https://ref.ly/John1:33), [JHN 4:42](https://ref.ly/John4:42), [JHN 7:12](https://ref.ly/John7:12), [JHN 7:15](https://ref.ly/John7:15) *some Jews*; [JHN 7:27](https://ref.ly/John7:27), [JHN 9:33](https://ref.ly/John9:33), [JHN 11:47](https://ref.ly/John11:47); [ACT 2:22–2:23](https://ref.ly/Acts2:22-Acts2:23)
