## 8252: faithfulness, in human relationships (id: 8252)

Loyalty and commitment within human relationships is important in the life of God's people. It is seen particularly in fidelity within marriage, fulfilling family obligations, honouring vows, dutiful service and trustworthy speech and conduct.

Faithfulness within society
---------------------------

### It is important in the life of God's people

**[HOS 12:6](https://ref.ly/Hos12:6)** *The word translated “love” is the Hebrew word “hesed”, which here expresses the loyalty and faithfulness that should characterise the life of God's people.* *See also* [MIC 6:8](https://ref.ly/Mic6:8); [MAT 23:23](https://ref.ly/Matt23:23) parallel [LUK 11:42](https://ref.ly/Luke11:42)

### It is a reflection of God's faithfulness to his people

**[PSA 89:14](https://ref.ly/Ps89:14)** *See also* [DEU 32:4](https://ref.ly/Deut32:4); [ISA 11:4–11:5](https://ref.ly/Isa11:4-Isa11:5)
