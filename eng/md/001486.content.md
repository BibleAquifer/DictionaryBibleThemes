## 5974: value (id: 5974)

God places inestimable value on the human soul. He paid the redemption price of the blood of his son Jesus Christ to purchase his own people who are precious to him. Everything that achieves or maintains this relationship is to be valued.

Redemptive values of people, animals and property
-------------------------------------------------

[LEV 27:1–27:25](https://ref.ly/Lev27:1-Lev27:25) *These are the values for recovering or buying back what has been dedicated to God. They were designed to teach the Israelites the great cost of redemption.*
