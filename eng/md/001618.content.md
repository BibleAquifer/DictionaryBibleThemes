## 6639: election, to salvation (id: 6639)

God chooses to bring individuals to salvation through faith in Jesus Christ.

Election is part of God's eternal decree
----------------------------------------

### Election is from eternity

**[EPH 1:4](https://ref.ly/Eph1:4)** *See also* [2TH 2:13](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:13); [2TI 1:9](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:9)

### Election is God's sovereign prerogative

**[ROM 9:15–9:24](https://ref.ly/Rom9:15-Rom9:24), [ROM 11:1–11:6](https://ref.ly/Rom11:1-Rom11:6)** *See also* [EXO 33:19](https://ref.ly/Exod33:19); [ISA 65:1](https://ref.ly/Isa65:1); [JER 18:1–18:12](https://ref.ly/Jer18:1-Jer18:12); [JHN 15:16](https://ref.ly/John15:16), [JHN 17:6](https://ref.ly/John17:6); [ROM 9:10–9:13](https://ref.ly/Rom9:10-Rom9:13); [EPH 2:10](https://ref.ly/Eph2:10)

### God's election places individuals within the covenant of grace

**[NEH 9:7–9:8](https://ref.ly/Neh9:7-Neh9:8)** *See also* [GEN 15:7–15:8](https://ref.ly/Gen15:7-Gen15:8), [GEN 15:18–15:21](https://ref.ly/Gen15:18-Gen15:21), [GEN 18:19](https://ref.ly/Gen18:19); [GAL 3:29](https://ref.ly/Gal3:29)

### Election is not on the basis of merit

**[1CO 1:26–1:31](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:26-1Cor1:31)** *See also* [DEU 7:7–7:8](https://ref.ly/Deut7:7-Deut7:8), [DEU 9:4–9:6](https://ref.ly/Deut9:4-Deut9:6); [JAS 2:5](https://ref.ly/Jas2:5)

God's election of his people is the foundation of his saving action
-------------------------------------------------------------------

### Election does not suspend God's use of the means of salvation

**[2TH 2:13–2:14](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:13-2Thess2:14)** *See also* [MAT 1:21](https://ref.ly/Matt1:21); [EPH 2:8–2:10](https://ref.ly/Eph2:8-Eph2:10); [JAS 1:18](https://ref.ly/Jas1:18); [1PE 1:2](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:2)

### Election works in tandem with the call of the gospel

**[MAT 22:14](https://ref.ly/Matt22:14)** *See also* [ROM 8:29–8:30](https://ref.ly/Rom8:29-Rom8:30)

### Election is evidenced through a positive response to the gospel

**[1TH 1:4–1:5](https://ref.ly/1Thess1:4-1Thess1:5)** *See also* [JHN 6:37–6:40](https://ref.ly/John6:37-John6:40)

**[ROM 8:31–8:39](https://ref.ly/Rom8:31-Rom8:39)** *See also* [JHN 10:27–10:29](https://ref.ly/John10:27-John10:29), [JHN 17:2](https://ref.ly/John17:2)
