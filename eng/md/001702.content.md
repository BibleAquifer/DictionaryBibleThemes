## 7021: church, OT anticipations of (id: 7021)

As the people of God, the Christian church is continuous with Israel. The OT provides important anticipations of the church.

OT terms for the people of God
------------------------------

### The descendants of Abraham

[2CH 20:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:7); ; [ISA 41:8–41:9](https://ref.ly/Isa41:8-Isa41:9); ; [ROM 4:16](https://ref.ly/Rom4:16); ; [GAL 3:7](https://ref.ly/Gal3:7) *“children of Abraham” in the NT refers to those who, like Abraham, put their faith in God*

### The saints

The term for `saints'' means`set apart for God'', \`\`made holy'', and is the most frequently used NT term for Christians: [1SA 2:9](https://ref.ly/1Sam2:9); ; [2CH 6:41](https://ref.ly/2Chr6:41) parallel [PSA 132:9](https://ref.ly/Ps132:9); ; [PSA 16:3](https://ref.ly/Ps16:3), [PSA 31:23](https://ref.ly/Ps31:23); ; [DAN 7:18](https://ref.ly/Dan7:18)

### The assembly

`assembly'' means`a community gathered together in response to God's call to serve and worship him''. Its NT equivalents include the synagogue and the church: [PSA 1:5](https://ref.ly/Ps1:5), [PSA 107:32](https://ref.ly/Ps107:32), [PSA 149:1](https://ref.ly/Ps149:1)
