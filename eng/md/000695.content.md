## 5099: Mary, mother of Jesus Christ (id: 5099)

Mary, the mother of Jesus Christ, provides Christians with an example of obedience to God and trust in Christ. She shared in the sufferings of her son, and took her place alongside the other disciples.

### She was a virgin

**[LUK 1:34](https://ref.ly/Luke1:34)** *The Greek word translated “virgin” means “young”, “unmarried” and implies virginity. Betrothal might take place as early as 12 years old and lasted usually for a year, being as binding as a marriage contract, though without sexual relations.* *See also* [ISA 7:14](https://ref.ly/Isa7:14); [MAT 1:23](https://ref.ly/Matt1:23)
