## 8746: false Christs (id: 8746)

Mistaken and deceptive people claiming to be Jesus Christ. Jesus Christ warns his followers to be on their guard against the appearance of such people.

Jesus Christ warns that false Christs will appear
-------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 24:5](https://ref.ly/Matt24:5)** parallels [MRK 13:6](https://ref.ly/Mark13:6), [LUK 21:8](https://ref.ly/Luke21:8) *See also* [MAT 24:23](https://ref.ly/Matt24:23) parallel [MRK 13:21](https://ref.ly/Mark13:21)

False Christs will perform signs and miracles in order to deceive
-----------------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 24:24](https://ref.ly/Matt24:24)** parallel [MRK 13:22](https://ref.ly/Mark13:22)

False Christs can be recognised because the return of Jesus Christ will be obvious to everyone
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 24:27–24:31](https://ref.ly/Matt24:27-Matt24:31)** parallels [MRK 13:24–13:27](https://ref.ly/Mark13:24-Mark13:27), [LUK 21:25–21:28](https://ref.ly/Luke21:25-Luke21:28) *The personal return of the Lord Jesus Christ is not described in terms of an appearance to a select group, but to the whole world. The attempt of anyone to convince others that he is Christ therefore is a sure sign of deception.*
