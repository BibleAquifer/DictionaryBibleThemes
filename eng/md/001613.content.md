## 6629: conversion, examples of (id: 6629)

Scripture gives examples of individuals and peoples who have turned to God in conversion.

Examples of conversion in the OT
--------------------------------

### Peoples who turned to God

[ISA 19:22](https://ref.ly/Isa19:22) *Egypt*; ; [JON 3:5–3:10](https://ref.ly/Jonah3:5-Jonah3:10) *Nineveh*

Individuals who turned to God
-----------------------------

[2KI 5:15](https://ref.ly/2Kgs5:15) *Naaman*; ; [2CH 33:12–33:13](https://ref.ly/2Chr33:12-2Chr33:13) *Manasseh*; ; [DAN 4:34–4:37](https://ref.ly/Dan4:34-Dan4:37) *Nebuchadnezzar*; ; [JON 1:16](https://ref.ly/Jonah1:16) *sailors on board ship with Jonah*

Examples of conversion in the NT
--------------------------------

### Examples of people in general

[JHN 4:42](https://ref.ly/John4:42) *people of Sychar*; ; [ACT 2:41](https://ref.ly/Acts2:41) *3,000 people on the day of Pentecost*; ; [ACT 8:12](https://ref.ly/Acts8:12) *Samaritans*; ; [ACT 9:35](https://ref.ly/Acts9:35) *people of Lydda and Sharon*; ; [ACT 15:3](https://ref.ly/Acts15:3) *Gentile converts*

### Examples of individual converts

[LUK 15:18](https://ref.ly/Luke15:18) *the prodigal son*; ; [LUK 19:8–19:9](https://ref.ly/Luke19:8-Luke19:9) *Zacchaeus*; ; [ACT 8:35–8:38](https://ref.ly/Acts8:35-Acts8:38) *the Ethiopian eunuch*; [ACT 9:3–9:18](https://ref.ly/Acts9:3-Acts9:18) parallels [ACT 22:6–22:11](https://ref.ly/Acts22:6-Acts22:11), [ACT 26:12–26:18](https://ref.ly/Acts26:12-Acts26:18); [ACT 10:44–10:48](https://ref.ly/Acts10:44-Acts10:48) *Cornelius*; ; [ACT 13:12](https://ref.ly/Acts13:12) *the proconsul, Sergius Paulus*; ; [ACT 16:14–16:15](https://ref.ly/Acts16:14-Acts16:15) *Lydia*; ; [ACT 16:29–16:34](https://ref.ly/Acts16:29-Acts16:34) *the Philippian jailer*
