## 5701: heir (id: 5701)

Property and power in biblical times normally descended from father to son. Jesus Christ is therefore called God's heir, and believers are said to inherit in him.

The heir in ancient Israel
--------------------------

### The heir of property

**[GEN 15:3–15:4](https://ref.ly/Gen15:3-Gen15:4)** *See also* [PRO 13:22](https://ref.ly/Prov13:22), [PRO 19:14](https://ref.ly/Prov19:14)

### The heir of power

**[1KI 2:12](https://ref.ly/1Kgs2:12)** parallel [1CH 29:28](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:28) *See also* [1KI 11:43](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:43), [1KI 14:31](https://ref.ly/1Kgs14:31), [1KI 15:3](https://ref.ly/1Kgs15:3)

[DEU 25:5–25:6](https://ref.ly/Deut25:5-Deut25:6); ; [JDG 21:17](https://ref.ly/Judg21:17); ; [2SA 14:4–14:7](https://ref.ly/2Sam14:4-2Sam14:7) *The woman of Tekoa was interceding indirectly for Absalom, David's heir, who had been banished.*
