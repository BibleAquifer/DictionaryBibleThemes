## 8418: endurance (id: 8418)

The ability to persevere in a task or calling. The Christian is called to endure in the face of trial or opposition, and his endurance brings spiritual rewards.

Endurance commended as a virtue for God's people
------------------------------------------------

**[JOS 1:7](https://ref.ly/Josh1:7)**; **[GAL 6:9](https://ref.ly/Gal6:9)** *See also* [DEU 5:32](https://ref.ly/Deut5:32); [JOS 23:8](https://ref.ly/Josh23:8); [JOB 17:9](https://ref.ly/Job17:9); [ACT 11:23](https://ref.ly/Acts11:23), [ACT 13:43](https://ref.ly/Acts13:43), [ACT 14:22](https://ref.ly/Acts14:22); [EPH 4:14](https://ref.ly/Eph4:14); [1TI 6:11](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:11); [2TI 2:3](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:3); [1PE 2:20](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:20); [REV 13:10](https://ref.ly/Rev13:10)
