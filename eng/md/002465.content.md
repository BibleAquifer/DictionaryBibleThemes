## 8849: worry (id: 8849)

A sense of uneasiness and anxiety about the future. Scripture indicates that such anxiety is ultimately grounded in a lack of trust in God and his purposes.

The causes of worry
-------------------

### Being world\-centred

**[MAT 6:25](https://ref.ly/Matt6:25)** parallel [LUK 12:22](https://ref.ly/Luke12:22) *See also* [MAT 6:34](https://ref.ly/Matt6:34), [MAT 13:22](https://ref.ly/Matt13:22) parallels [MRK 4:19](https://ref.ly/Mark4:19), [LUK 8:14](https://ref.ly/Luke8:14); [LUK 12:29](https://ref.ly/Luke12:29), [LUK 21:34](https://ref.ly/Luke21:34); [1CO 7:33](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:33)

### Lacking confidence in God

**[JHN 14:1](https://ref.ly/John14:1)** *See also* [MAT 6:26–6:32](https://ref.ly/Matt6:26-Matt6:32) parallel [LUK 12:27–12:30](https://ref.ly/Luke12:27-Luke12:30); [MAT 8:25](https://ref.ly/Matt8:25) parallels [MRK 4:38](https://ref.ly/Mark4:38), [LUK 8:24](https://ref.ly/Luke8:24); [MAT 14:31](https://ref.ly/Matt14:31), [MAT 16:8](https://ref.ly/Matt16:8)

**[DEU 28:64–28:65](https://ref.ly/Deut28:64-Deut28:65)** *See also* [LAM 1:3](https://ref.ly/Lam1:3); [EZK 4:16–4:17](https://ref.ly/Ezek4:16-Ezek4:17), [EZK 12:19](https://ref.ly/Ezek12:19)
