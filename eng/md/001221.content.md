## 5675: divorce (id: 5675)

The legal dissolution of a marriage. Though not part of God's original intention for marriage, divorce is permitted under certain circumstances, especially marital unfaithfulness. In general, properly divorced parties are free to remarry.

* **Associated Articles:** 5676: divorce, in OT (ID: `5676@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 5677: divorce, amongst believers (ID: `5677@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
