## 5343: idleness (id: 5343)

Laziness or unproductiveness. As work is hallowed by God's working, idleness is regarded as a sin against oneself and society.

Idleness reproached
-------------------

### In terms of daily labour

**[1TH 5:14](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:14)** *Paul to the Christians at Thessalonica.* *See also* [PRO 19:24](https://ref.ly/Prov19:24), [PRO 10:4](https://ref.ly/Prov10:4), [PRO 22:13](https://ref.ly/Prov22:13), [PRO 26:13–26:16](https://ref.ly/Prov26:13-Prov26:16); [2TH 3:6–3:15](https://ref.ly/2Thess3:6-2Thess3:15); [1TI 5:13](https://ref.ly/1Tim5:13); [TIT 1:12](https://ref.ly/Titus1:12)

### In matters of faith
