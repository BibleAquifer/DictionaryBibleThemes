## 5153: foot-washing (id: 5153)

A practice of hospitality used by Jesus Christ to express his servanthood.

Foot\-washing as a sign of courteous hospitality
------------------------------------------------

**[GEN 18:4](https://ref.ly/Gen18:4)** *See also* [GEN 19:2](https://ref.ly/Gen19:2); [JDG 19:21](https://ref.ly/Judg19:21); [1SA 25:41](https://ref.ly/1Sam25:41); [LUK 7:44](https://ref.ly/Luke7:44)

Jesus Christ washes his disciples' feet
---------------------------------------

**[JHN 13:3–13:5](https://ref.ly/John13:3-John13:5)** *The washing of feet was a lowly task normally performed by a servant.*
