## 2206: Jesus, the Christ (id: 2206)

“Christ” means “Messiah” or “anointed one”. As the Christ, Jesus is the one who fulfils all OT expectations. Paul's phrase “in Christ” indicates the intimate relationship between Jesus Christ and his people.

------------------------------

**[1SA 16:13](https://ref.ly/1Sam16:13)** *Anointing symbolises God's calling and being equipped by his Spirit.* *See also* [EXO 40:15](https://ref.ly/Exod40:15); [LEV 8:12](https://ref.ly/Lev8:12); [1SA 10:1](https://ref.ly/1Sam10:1); [1KI 1:39](https://ref.ly/1Kgs1:39)
