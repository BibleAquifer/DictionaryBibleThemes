## 5592: treaty (id: 5592)

A formal agreement between individuals or nations, promising commitment to each other's interests. Israel was forbidden to enter into such agreements with other nations in the promised land.

Treaties between individuals
----------------------------

**[1SA 18:3–18:4](https://ref.ly/1Sam18:3-1Sam18:4)** *See also* [GEN 21:22–21:32](https://ref.ly/Gen21:22-Gen21:32), [GEN 26:26–26:31](https://ref.ly/Gen26:26-Gen26:31); [1SA 20:8](https://ref.ly/1Sam20:8), [1SA 20:16–20:17](https://ref.ly/1Sam20:16-1Sam20:17), [1SA 22:8](https://ref.ly/1Sam22:8), [1SA 23:18](https://ref.ly/1Sam23:18); [2SA 3:12–3:13](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:12-2Sam3:13)
