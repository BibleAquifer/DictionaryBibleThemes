## 5790: anger, divine (id: 5790)

The anger of God is directed against all human sin, disobedience, rebellion and wickedness, which threaten to thwart his purposes for his creation and people.

----------------------

### Disobedience

**[ISA 5:24–5:25](https://ref.ly/Isa5:24-Isa5:25)** *See also* [NUM 22:22](https://ref.ly/Num22:22); [JOS 7:1](https://ref.ly/Josh7:1); [2KI 22:13](https://ref.ly/2Kgs22:13); [PSA 78:21–78:22](https://ref.ly/Ps78:21-Ps78:22); [MIC 5:15](https://ref.ly/Mic5:15); [ZEC 7:12](https://ref.ly/Zech7:12); [MRK 3:5](https://ref.ly/Mark3:5); [ROM 2:5](https://ref.ly/Rom2:5); [EPH 5:5–5:6](https://ref.ly/Eph5:5-Eph5:6); [HEB 3:7–3:12](https://ref.ly/Heb3:7-Heb3:12)
