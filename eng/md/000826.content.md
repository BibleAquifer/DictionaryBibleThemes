## 5238: bribery (id: 5238)

The practice of offering an incentive, usually money, in order to dishonestly or illegally procure a decision or action in favour of the giver.

Bribery is sin
--------------

### It is forbidden by God on the grounds of truth and justice

**[EXO 23:8](https://ref.ly/Exod23:8)**; **[2CH 19:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr19:7)** *See also* [DEU 10:17](https://ref.ly/Deut10:17), [DEU 16:18–16:20](https://ref.ly/Deut16:18-Deut16:20); [JOB 36:18](https://ref.ly/Job36:18)

### The prevalence of the sin

**[PRO 17:8](https://ref.ly/Prov17:8)** *See also* [PRO 6:32–6:35](https://ref.ly/Prov6:32-Prov6:35), [PRO 21:14](https://ref.ly/Prov21:14)

### The seriousness of the sin

**[JOB 15:34](https://ref.ly/Job15:34)** *See also* [DEU 27:25](https://ref.ly/Deut27:25); [ISA 1:23–1:24](https://ref.ly/Isa1:23-Isa1:24), [ISA 5:22–5:23](https://ref.ly/Isa5:22-Isa5:23), [ISA 33:15](https://ref.ly/Isa33:15); [AMO 5:12](https://ref.ly/Amos5:12); [MIC 3:9–3:12](https://ref.ly/Mic3:9-Mic3:12)

The results of bribery
----------------------

### Personal corruption

**[ECC 7:7](https://ref.ly/Eccl7:7)** *See also* [PSA 26:9–26:10](https://ref.ly/Ps26:9-Ps26:10); [1TI 6:10](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:10)

### The perversion of justice

**[PRO 17:23](https://ref.ly/Prov17:23)** *See also* [EXO 23:8](https://ref.ly/Exod23:8); [DEU 16:19](https://ref.ly/Deut16:19)

### The decay of society

**[PRO 29:4](https://ref.ly/Prov29:4)** *See also* [EZK 22:12](https://ref.ly/Ezek22:12) *Corruption, violence and decay render society subject to God's judgment.*
