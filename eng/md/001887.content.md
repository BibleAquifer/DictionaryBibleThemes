## 7621: disciples, calling of (id: 7621)

The term is applied to Christians in general, not to the Twelve only. It emphasises the importance of Jesus Christ's followers learning from Christ as their teacher.

Disciples learning from their master
------------------------------------

**[ISA 8:16](https://ref.ly/Isa8:16)**; **[AMO 7:14](https://ref.ly/Amos7:14)** *See also* [1KI 20:35](https://ref.ly/1Kgs20:35); [NEH 3:8](https://ref.ly/Neh3:8); [ISA 19:11](https://ref.ly/Isa19:11); [MAT 10:24](https://ref.ly/Matt10:24); [MRK 2:18](https://ref.ly/Mark2:18) parallel [LUK 5:33](https://ref.ly/Luke5:33); [LUK 6:40](https://ref.ly/Luke6:40); [JHN 1:35](https://ref.ly/John1:35), [JHN 9:28](https://ref.ly/John9:28)

Those referred to as disciples in the NT
----------------------------------------

### The disciples as the apostles

**[MAT 10:1–10:2](https://ref.ly/Matt10:1-Matt10:2)** *The apostles were uniquely appointed as founding pillars of the church, but in some respects they were also representative disciples.*

### The disciples as believers in general

**[MAT 28:19](https://ref.ly/Matt28:19)**; **[ACT 19:1–19:2](https://ref.ly/Acts19:1-Acts19:2)** *Christian believers, not fully mature.* *See also* [LUK 6:13](https://ref.ly/Luke6:13), [LUK 6:17](https://ref.ly/Luke6:17); [ACT 6:2](https://ref.ly/Acts6:2), [ACT 11:26](https://ref.ly/Acts11:26)

### The disciples as an intimate circle of believers

[MAT 18:1](https://ref.ly/Matt18:1); ; [MRK 4:10](https://ref.ly/Mark4:10)

How disciples are called
------------------------

### It sometimes implies physically following Jesus Christ and leaving everything

[MAT 4:19](https://ref.ly/Matt4:19) parallel [MRK 1:16–1:20](https://ref.ly/Mark1:16-Mark1:20); ; [MAT 8:18–8:22](https://ref.ly/Matt8:18-Matt8:22) parallel [LUK 9:57–9:60](https://ref.ly/Luke9:57-Luke9:60); ; [MAT 9:9](https://ref.ly/Matt9:9) parallel [MRK 2:14](https://ref.ly/Mark2:14)

### Calling in a more general spiritual sense

**[JHN 21:19](https://ref.ly/John21:19)** *See also* [LUK 9:23](https://ref.ly/Luke9:23), [LUK 14:25–14:27](https://ref.ly/Luke14:25-Luke14:27); [JHN 8:12](https://ref.ly/John8:12), [JHN 12:26](https://ref.ly/John12:26), [JHN 21:22](https://ref.ly/John21:22)

How disciples learn from Jesus Christ the teacher
-------------------------------------------------

### By repeating the words of Jesus Christ

**[LUK 11:1–11:4](https://ref.ly/Luke11:1-Luke11:4)** *The rhythmical form of much of Jesus Christ's teaching suggests he intended his words to be remembered verbatim.* *See also* [MAT 5:3–5:10](https://ref.ly/Matt5:3-Matt5:10)

### By signs

**[JHN 2:11](https://ref.ly/John2:11)** *See also* [MRK 5:37](https://ref.ly/Mark5:37), [MRK 5:40](https://ref.ly/Mark5:40) parallel [LUK 8:51](https://ref.ly/Luke8:51)

### By understanding the OT Scriptures

**[JHN 1:45](https://ref.ly/John1:45)** *See also* [LUK 18:31–18:33](https://ref.ly/Luke18:31-Luke18:33), [LUK 24:27](https://ref.ly/Luke24:27), [LUK 24:32](https://ref.ly/Luke24:32), [LUK 24:44–24:45](https://ref.ly/Luke24:44-Luke24:45)

### By understanding the parables

**[MRK 4:11–4:12](https://ref.ly/Mark4:11-Mark4:12)** parallels [MAT 13:10–13:11](https://ref.ly/Matt13:10-Matt13:11), [LUK 8:9–8:10](https://ref.ly/Luke8:9-Luke8:10) *See also* [MRK 4:34](https://ref.ly/Mark4:34)

### By practical experience of mission

**[LUK 10:23–10:24](https://ref.ly/Luke10:23-Luke10:24)** *These words follow the sending out of the seventy\-two.* *See also* [MRK 6:6–6:11](https://ref.ly/Mark6:6-Mark6:11); [LUK 9:1–9:2](https://ref.ly/Luke9:1-Luke9:2) parallel [MAT 10:1](https://ref.ly/Matt10:1)

### In periods of being alone with Jesus Christ

**[MRK 6:31](https://ref.ly/Mark6:31)** *See also* [MAT 16:21](https://ref.ly/Matt16:21)
