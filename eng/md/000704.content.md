## 5109: Paul, apostle to Gentiles (id: 5109)

Paul saw his encounter with the risen Christ as a call to apostleship, with a special mission to proclaim the gospel to the Gentile world. Paul's complete dedication to Jesus Christ made his own personal weaknesses a vehicle for the power of God.

Paul and the example of Jesus Christ
------------------------------------

### His appeal to Jesus Christ as an example to the churches

**[2CO 8:9](https://ref.ly/2Cor8:9)** *Paul here uses the example of Jesus Christ in order to encourage the Corinthians in their giving.* *See also* [ROM 15:1–15:3](https://ref.ly/Rom15:1-Rom15:3); [PHP 2:5–2:11](https://ref.ly/Phil2:5-Phil2:11)

### His own exemplary imitation of Jesus Christ

**[1CO 11:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:1)** *See also* [1CO 4:14–4:17](https://ref.ly/1Cor4:14-1Cor4:17); [PHP 3:12–3:17](https://ref.ly/Phil3:12-Phil3:17), [PHP 4:8–4:9](https://ref.ly/Phil4:8-Phil4:9); [2TH 3:6–3:9](https://ref.ly/2Thess3:6-2Thess3:9)

### His sharing of Jesus Christ's sufferings

**[COL 1:24](https://ref.ly/Col1:24)** *Paul is not suggesting here that there is any deficiency in Jesus Christ's atoning sacrifice. Rather, there are some aspects of suffering which Christ could not experience because they arose only as a result of people preaching the message of the cross and suffering because of it.* *See also* [2CO 1:5–1:9](https://ref.ly/2Cor1:5-2Cor1:9), [2CO 4:7–4:12](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:7-2Cor4:12); [GAL 6:17](https://ref.ly/Gal6:17)
