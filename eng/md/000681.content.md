## 5082: Adam, significance of (id: 5082)

Throughout the NT, Adam is seen as a real historical figure, from whom all humanity is descended and by whose sin all humanity is affected.

Adam as the original member of the human race
---------------------------------------------

**[ACT 17:26](https://ref.ly/Acts17:26)**; **[1CO 15:45–15:49](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:45-1Cor15:49)** *See also* [GEN 2:7](https://ref.ly/Gen2:7); [LUK 3:23–3:38](https://ref.ly/Luke3:23-Luke3:38) *The genealogy of Jesus Christ is traced back to Adam.*

Adam's relationship with Eve
----------------------------

### Adam as one flesh with Eve

**[MAT 19:3–19:6](https://ref.ly/Matt19:3-Matt19:6)** parallel [MRK 10:2–10:9](https://ref.ly/Mark10:2-Mark10:9) *See also* [GEN 2:24](https://ref.ly/Gen2:24); [1CO 6:16](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:16); [EPH 5:31](https://ref.ly/Eph5:31)

### Adam as the head of Eve

[1CO 11:3](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:3), [1CO 11:8–11:12](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:8-1Cor11:12); ; [1TI 2:12–2:15](https://ref.ly/1Tim2:12-1Tim2:15)

The reality and results of Adam's sin
-------------------------------------

### Sin spread to all the human race

**[ROM 5:19](https://ref.ly/Rom5:19)** *See also* [GEN 4:3–4:16](https://ref.ly/Gen4:3-Gen4:16); [PSA 51:5](https://ref.ly/Ps51:5); [PRO 20:9](https://ref.ly/Prov20:9); [ROM 3:23](https://ref.ly/Rom3:23)

**[ROM 5:12](https://ref.ly/Rom5:12)** *See also* [ROM 5:14–5:17](https://ref.ly/Rom5:14-Rom5:17), [ROM 6:23](https://ref.ly/Rom6:23); [1CO 15:21–15:22](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:21-1Cor15:22)
