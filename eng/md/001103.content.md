## 5535: sleep, and death (id: 5535)

Scripture often uses sleep as an image of death, indicating that believers will be awakened to resurrection on the last day.

Death described in the imagery of sleep
---------------------------------------

### The sleep of death for human beings in general

**[PSA 90:3–90:6](https://ref.ly/Ps90:3-Ps90:6)** *See also* [JOB 7:21](https://ref.ly/Job7:21), [JOB 14:10–14:12](https://ref.ly/Job14:10-Job14:12); [PSA 13:3](https://ref.ly/Ps13:3); [DAN 12:2](https://ref.ly/Dan12:2); [MAT 9:24](https://ref.ly/Matt9:24) parallels [MRK 5:39](https://ref.ly/Mark5:39), [LUK 8:52–8:53](https://ref.ly/Luke8:52-Luke8:53)
