## 4029: world, human beings in (id: 4029)

The world is intended to be the dwelling\-place of humanity, as the height of God's creation. Human beings are organised within the world into groupings and nations and have a God\-given authority to rule.

The responsibility of human beings to rule
------------------------------------------

### Over the earth

**[GEN 2:15–2:16](https://ref.ly/Gen2:15-Gen2:16)** *See also* [GEN 1:25–1:26](https://ref.ly/Gen1:25-Gen1:26), [GEN 1:29](https://ref.ly/Gen1:29); [PSA 8:4–8:6](https://ref.ly/Ps8:4-Ps8:6)

### Over the animal kingdom

**[GEN 1:28](https://ref.ly/Gen1:28)** *“subdue” and “rule” do not give liberty to exploit and abuse but responsibility for caring for and bringing the best out of God's world.* *See also* [GEN 9:1–9:5](https://ref.ly/Gen9:1-Gen9:5); [DEU 22:6–22:7](https://ref.ly/Deut22:6-Deut22:7); [PRO 27:23–27:27](https://ref.ly/Prov27:23-Prov27:27); [MAT 6:25–6:26](https://ref.ly/Matt6:25-Matt6:26); [ACT 10:11–10:13](https://ref.ly/Acts10:11-Acts10:13)
