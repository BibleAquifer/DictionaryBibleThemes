## 8462: priority, of God (id: 8462)

Scripture provides examples of individuals who have allowed God to take priority in their lives, and also of those who have allowed something or someone else to take priority.

God's glory made a priority
---------------------------

**[MAT 6:9](https://ref.ly/Matt6:9)**

### Commands to put God first

**[MAT 4:10](https://ref.ly/Matt4:10)** parallel [LUK 4:8](https://ref.ly/Luke4:8) *See also* [DEU 6:13](https://ref.ly/Deut6:13); [1CH 16:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:29)

### Examples of putting God first

[GEN 5:22](https://ref.ly/Gen5:22), [GEN 5:24](https://ref.ly/Gen5:24) *Enoch*; ; [GEN 6:9](https://ref.ly/Gen6:9) *Noah*; ; [GEN 12:4](https://ref.ly/Gen12:4) *Abraham*; ; [JOS 24:15](https://ref.ly/Josh24:15) *Joshua*; ; [2KI 20:3](https://ref.ly/2Kgs20:3) *Hezekiah*; ; [DAN 3:16–3:18](https://ref.ly/Dan3:16-Dan3:18) *Shadrach, Meshach and Abednego*; ; [DAN 6:10](https://ref.ly/Dan6:10) *Daniel*; ; [JHN 3:29–3:30](https://ref.ly/John3:29-John3:30) *John the Baptist*

### Disapproval of not putting God first

**[EXO 20:3](https://ref.ly/Exod20:3)** *See also* [LUK 16:13](https://ref.ly/Luke16:13)

### Examples of not putting God first

**[ACT 12:22–12:23](https://ref.ly/Acts12:22-Acts12:23)** *See also* [1KI 11:4](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:4) *Solomon*; [1KI 15:3](https://ref.ly/1Kgs15:3) *Abijah*; [2CH 25:2](https://ref.ly/2Chr25:2) *Amaziah*; [JER 3:10](https://ref.ly/Jer3:10) *Judah*; [ACT 5:1–5:5](https://ref.ly/Acts5:1-Acts5:5) *Ananias and Sapphira*; [ROM 16:17–16:18](https://ref.ly/Rom16:17-Rom16:18)

### Worship commended as the highest human activity

**[PSA 95:6](https://ref.ly/Ps95:6)** *See also* [PSA 96:9](https://ref.ly/Ps96:9), [PSA 99:5](https://ref.ly/Ps99:5); [ZEC 14:17](https://ref.ly/Zech14:17); [JHN 4:24](https://ref.ly/John4:24)

### Outstanding examples of worship

**[2SA 6:14–6:15](https://ref.ly/2Sam6:14-2Sam6:15)** *David was prepared to be despised by his wife rather than inhibit his worship.* *See also* [EXO 3:6](https://ref.ly/Exod3:6); [ISA 6:3](https://ref.ly/Isa6:3); [EZK 1:28](https://ref.ly/Ezek1:28); [DAN 6:10](https://ref.ly/Dan6:10); [JHN 9:38](https://ref.ly/John9:38); [REV 1:17](https://ref.ly/Rev1:17)
