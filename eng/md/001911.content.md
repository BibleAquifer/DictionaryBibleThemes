## 7740: missionaries, call of (id: 7740)

God calls and sends out his people into the world. All believers are called to share the good news of the gospel, though some are specially commissioned by God, confirmed by the church and compelled by the gospel message to missionary service.

The call to all God's people
----------------------------

**[PSA 96:2–96:3](https://ref.ly/Ps96:2-Ps96:3)** parallel [1CH 16:23–16:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:23-1Chr16:24)**[MAT 28:19](https://ref.ly/Matt28:19)** *See also* [MRK 16:15](https://ref.ly/Mark16:15); [JHN 20:21](https://ref.ly/John20:21); [ACT 1:8](https://ref.ly/Acts1:8)

Missionaries in the OT
----------------------

### Those sent to their own people

**[ISA 6:8–6:10](https://ref.ly/Isa6:8-Isa6:10)** *See also* [2PE 2:5](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:5) *Noah*; [JER 2:1–2:4](https://ref.ly/Jer2:1-Jer2:4) *Jeremiah's ministry extended further but was primarily to his own people, Judah*; [EZK 2:3](https://ref.ly/Ezek2:3) *Ezekiel*

### Those sent to other peoples

**[2KI 17:27–17:28](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:27-2Kgs17:28)** *A priest from Samaria returns to teach the resettled population about the LORD.* [ISA 42:6](https://ref.ly/Isa42:6), [ISA 49:6](https://ref.ly/Isa49:6); ; [ACT 13:47](https://ref.ly/Acts13:47); [AMO 7:15](https://ref.ly/Amos7:15) *Amos, from Judah, is sent to the northern kingdom of Israel.*; [JON 1:1–1:2](https://ref.ly/Jonah1:1-Jonah1:2), [JON 3:1–3:2](https://ref.ly/Jonah3:1-Jonah3:2)

Missionaries in the NT
----------------------

### John the Baptist

**[JHN 1:6–1:7](https://ref.ly/John1:6-John1:7)** *See also* [MAL 4:5–4:6](https://ref.ly/Mal4:5-Mal4:6); [MAT 11:10](https://ref.ly/Matt11:10) parallel [LUK 7:27](https://ref.ly/Luke7:27); [MRK 1:2–1:4](https://ref.ly/Mark1:2-Mark1:4); [MAL 3:1](https://ref.ly/Mal3:1); [ISA 40:3](https://ref.ly/Isa40:3); [JHN 1:33](https://ref.ly/John1:33)

### Jesus Christ's disciples

**[LUK 9:1–9:6](https://ref.ly/Luke9:1-Luke9:6)** *Healing and deliverance accompanies preaching as part of the disciples' mission.* *See also* [MAT 10:5–10:16](https://ref.ly/Matt10:5-Matt10:16) *During Jesus Christ's earthly ministry the disciples' mission was limited to Israel*; [MRK 6:7–6:11](https://ref.ly/Mark6:7-Mark6:11); [LUK 10:1](https://ref.ly/Luke10:1)

### Other believers

**[ACT 11:19–11:20](https://ref.ly/Acts11:19-Acts11:20)** *See also* [ACT 8:5](https://ref.ly/Acts8:5) *Philip*; [ACT 18:24](https://ref.ly/Acts18:24) *Apollos*; [ACT 26:15–26:18](https://ref.ly/Acts26:15-Acts26:18) *Paul*

Receiving the missionary call
-----------------------------

### The call given by God

**[JER 1:4–1:10](https://ref.ly/Jer1:4-Jer1:10)** *See also* [MAT 4:19](https://ref.ly/Matt4:19) *Peter and Andrew*; [ACT 8:26](https://ref.ly/Acts8:26) *Philip*; [ACT 22:21](https://ref.ly/Acts22:21) *Paul*

### The call confirmed by the church

**[ACT 13:2–13:3](https://ref.ly/Acts13:2-Acts13:3)** *See also* [ACT 14:26–14:27](https://ref.ly/Acts14:26-Acts14:27), [ACT 15:40](https://ref.ly/Acts15:40), [ACT 22:14–22:15](https://ref.ly/Acts22:14-Acts22:15) *Paul's call to be a missionary to the Gentiles is communicated through Ananias*; [GAL 2:7–2:9](https://ref.ly/Gal2:7-Gal2:9) *Paul's mission to the Gentiles is recognised by the other apostles.*

### The call through circumstances

**[ACT 8:4](https://ref.ly/Acts8:4)** *Persecution drove the early church to wider mission.* *See also* [GEN 45:4–45:5](https://ref.ly/Gen45:4-Gen45:5) *Joseph sold into slavery in Egypt*; [DAN 1:3–1:6](https://ref.ly/Dan1:3-Dan1:6) *Daniel among those taken into exile in Babylon*; [ACT 18:1–18:2](https://ref.ly/Acts18:1-Acts18:2) *Priscilla and Aquila left Rome because of persecution.*

### Missionaries compelled by the message

**[ACT 4:18–4:20](https://ref.ly/Acts4:18-Acts4:20)** *See also* [1CO 9:16](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:16); [2CO 5:14](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:14)
