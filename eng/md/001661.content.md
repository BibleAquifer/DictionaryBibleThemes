## 6701: peace, human search for (id: 6701)

Scripture teaches that peace is only found in God, but people try to find it elsewhere. A person's desire for peace varies according to his or her circumstances.

The places where people search for peace
----------------------------------------

### People search for peace in others

**[GEN 5:28–5:29](https://ref.ly/Gen5:28-Gen5:29)** *See also* [SNG 8:10](https://ref.ly/Song8:10)

### People search for peace in material possessions

**[ECC 4:8](https://ref.ly/Eccl4:8)** *See also* [JOB 21:7–21:13](https://ref.ly/Job21:7-Job21:13); [DAN 4:4](https://ref.ly/Dan4:4); [MAT 19:16–19:22](https://ref.ly/Matt19:16-Matt19:22); [LUK 12:16–12:19](https://ref.ly/Luke12:16-Luke12:19)

### People search for peace in God

**[PSA 4:8](https://ref.ly/Ps4:8)** *See also* [JOB 22:21](https://ref.ly/Job22:21); [PRO 19:23](https://ref.ly/Prov19:23); [ISA 26:3](https://ref.ly/Isa26:3); [LUK 7:37–7:50](https://ref.ly/Luke7:37-Luke7:50)

The dangers in searching for peace
----------------------------------

### It can lead to greed

**[ECC 4:6](https://ref.ly/Eccl4:6)** *See also* [MAT 23:25](https://ref.ly/Matt23:25)

### It can lead to suffering and bondage

[GEN 42:1–42:17](https://ref.ly/Gen42:1-Gen42:17); ; [JOS 9:3–9:27](https://ref.ly/Josh9:3-Josh9:27); ; [MRK 5:25–5:26](https://ref.ly/Mark5:25-Mark5:26)

### It can lead to destruction

**[1TH 5:3](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:3)** *See also* [2KI 20:12–20:19](https://ref.ly/2Kgs20:12-2Kgs20:19); [LUK 16:19–16:26](https://ref.ly/Luke16:19-Luke16:26)

People's search for peace varies according to their circumstances
-----------------------------------------------------------------

### The desire can increase in times of suffering and difficulties

**[EXO 2:23](https://ref.ly/Exod2:23)** *See also* [JER 14:19](https://ref.ly/Jer14:19), [JER 47:2–47:6](https://ref.ly/Jer47:2-Jer47:6); [LAM 3:7–3:24](https://ref.ly/Lam3:7-Lam3:24); [EZK 7:23–7:27](https://ref.ly/Ezek7:23-Ezek7:27); [MRK 5:24–5:34](https://ref.ly/Mark5:24-Mark5:34) parallels [MAT 9:18–9:22](https://ref.ly/Matt9:18-Matt9:22), [LUK 8:42–8:48](https://ref.ly/Luke8:42-Luke8:48)

### The desire can diminish in times of comfort

**[EXO 8:12–8:15](https://ref.ly/Exod8:12-Exod8:15)** *See also* [JOB 12:5](https://ref.ly/Job12:5); [HOS 10:1](https://ref.ly/Hos10:1); [AMO 6:1](https://ref.ly/Amos6:1)
