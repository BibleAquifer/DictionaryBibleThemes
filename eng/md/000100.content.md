## 1444: revelation, in NT (id: 1444)

The NT fulfils and completes the revelation of God which began in the OT. Jesus Christ is the central focus of this self\-revelation of God.

The unity and progress of revelation
------------------------------------

### The unity of OT and NT

**[MAT 5:17–5:18](https://ref.ly/Matt5:17-Matt5:18)** *See also* [ROM 3:21–3:22](https://ref.ly/Rom3:21-Rom3:22); [2TI 3:14–3:15](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:14-2Tim3:15); [2PE 3:15–3:16](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:15-2Pet3:16) *The writings of Paul are presented as having equal status with the OT Scriptures*; [REV 22:18–22:19](https://ref.ly/Rev22:18-Rev22:19) *These sanctions have the same force as those of [Dt 4:2](https://ref.ly/Deut4:2); [12:32](https://ref.ly/Deut12:32).*
