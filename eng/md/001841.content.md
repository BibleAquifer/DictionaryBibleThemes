## 7444: sin offering (id: 7444)

Offerings to make atonement for both moral and ritual offences. The NT sees them as foreshadowing the death of Jesus Christ as an offering for human sin.

Occasions when a sin offering was required
------------------------------------------

Unintentional sins against God's laws: [LEV 4:2](https://ref.ly/Lev4:2); ; [HEB 9:7](https://ref.ly/Heb9:7); [LEV 5:1–5:4](https://ref.ly/Lev5:1-Lev5:4), [LEV 4:20](https://ref.ly/Lev4:20) *The consequence of the offering is atonement and forgiveness.*; [LEV 12:6–12:8](https://ref.ly/Lev12:6-Lev12:8); ; [LUK 2:24](https://ref.ly/Luke2:24); [LEV 14:13–14:31](https://ref.ly/Lev14:13-Lev14:31); ; [MRK 1:44](https://ref.ly/Mark1:44); [LEV 15:15](https://ref.ly/Lev15:15), [LEV 15:30](https://ref.ly/Lev15:30)

The form the sin offering took depended on who committed the sin
----------------------------------------------------------------

[LEV 4:3](https://ref.ly/Lev4:3) *a priest who sins*; ; [LEV 4:14](https://ref.ly/Lev4:14) *the sin of the community as a whole*; ; [LEV 4:22–4:23](https://ref.ly/Lev4:22-Lev4:23) *a leader who sins unintentionally*; [LEV 4:27–4:35](https://ref.ly/Lev4:27-Lev4:35), [LEV 5:5–5:6](https://ref.ly/Lev5:5-Lev5:6)

[LEV 5:5](https://ref.ly/Lev5:5)

### Male members of priestly families may eat what remains

[LEV 6:26](https://ref.ly/Lev6:26), [LEV 6:29](https://ref.ly/Lev6:29), [LEV 10:16–10:20](https://ref.ly/Lev10:16-Lev10:20)

[LEV 5:7–5:13](https://ref.ly/Lev5:7-Lev5:13)

The special ceremony for priests and the community presenting a sin offering
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Blood is taken into the sanctuary and placed on the altar

[LEV 4:5–4:7](https://ref.ly/Lev4:5-Lev4:7), [LEV 4:16–4:18](https://ref.ly/Lev4:16-Lev4:18)

### The rest of the animal is incinerated, not eaten

[LEV 4:11–4:12](https://ref.ly/Lev4:11-Lev4:12), [LEV 4:21](https://ref.ly/Lev4:21) *emphasising the identity between the victim and the sin of the community*

Significant occasions when sin offerings were made
--------------------------------------------------

[LEV 8:14–8:17](https://ref.ly/Lev8:14-Lev8:17) *at the ordination of Aaron and his sons*; ; [LEV 16:1–16:34](https://ref.ly/Lev16:1-Lev16:34) *on the Day of Atonement*; ; [2KI 12:16](https://ref.ly/2Kgs12:16) *during Josiah's reformation*; ; [2CH 29:21–29:24](https://ref.ly/2Chr29:21-2Chr29:24) *at the beginning of Hezekiah's reign*; ; [EZR 6:17](https://ref.ly/Ezra6:17) *at the dedication of the second temple*; ; [EZR 8:35](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:35) *when the exiles returned*; [EZK 40:39](https://ref.ly/Ezek40:39), [EZK 42:13](https://ref.ly/Ezek42:13), [EZK 43:19](https://ref.ly/Ezek43:19), [EZK 43:21](https://ref.ly/Ezek43:21)
