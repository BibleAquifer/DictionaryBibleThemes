## 9105: last things (id: 9105)

The doctrine of the last things (“eschatology”) includes the subjects of death, the second coming of Jesus Christ, the resurrection of the dead, the last judgment, heaven and hell.

Death
-----

### Physical death is universal

**[JOB 30:23](https://ref.ly/Job30:23)** *See also* [2SA 14:14](https://ref.ly/2Sam14:14); [ROM 5:12](https://ref.ly/Rom5:12)

### The timing of natural death is beyond human control

**[ECC 8:8](https://ref.ly/Eccl8:8)** *See also* [PSA 90:10](https://ref.ly/Ps90:10); [MAT 6:27](https://ref.ly/Matt6:27); [JAS 4:14](https://ref.ly/Jas4:14)

### Death is not to be feared by the believer

**[PSA 23:4](https://ref.ly/Ps23:4)** *See also* [PSA 116:15](https://ref.ly/Ps116:15); [PRO 14:32](https://ref.ly/Prov14:32); [ROM 14:8](https://ref.ly/Rom14:8); [PHP 1:21](https://ref.ly/Phil1:21); [REV 14:13](https://ref.ly/Rev14:13)

### For believers, death is likened to falling asleep

**[JHN 11:11–11:13](https://ref.ly/John11:11-John11:13)** *See also* [MRK 5:39](https://ref.ly/Mark5:39); [ACT 13:36](https://ref.ly/Acts13:36); [1CO 15:6](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:6)

### At the second coming, believers still living on earth will not experience death

**[1TH 4:15–4:17](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:15-1Thess4:17)** *See also* [1CO 15:51–15:52](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:51-1Cor15:52)

### Death is the penalty for unforgiven sin

**[ROM 6:23](https://ref.ly/Rom6:23)** *See also* [1CH 10:13](https://ref.ly/1Chr10:13); [PRO 11:19](https://ref.ly/Prov11:19); [ROM 5:12](https://ref.ly/Rom5:12)

The second coming of Jesus Christ
---------------------------------

### The second coming is foretold

**[MAT 26:64](https://ref.ly/Matt26:64)** parallel [MRK 14:62](https://ref.ly/Mark14:62) *See also* [LUK 21:27](https://ref.ly/Luke21:27); [ACT 1:11](https://ref.ly/Acts1:11); [HEB 9:28](https://ref.ly/Heb9:28)

### The timing of the second coming is known only to God the Father

**[MAT 24:36](https://ref.ly/Matt24:36)** parallel [MRK 13:32](https://ref.ly/Mark13:32) *See also* [MAL 3:1](https://ref.ly/Mal3:1); [MAT 24:44](https://ref.ly/Matt24:44) parallel [LUK 12:40](https://ref.ly/Luke12:40); [REV 16:15](https://ref.ly/Rev16:15)

### God's purpose at the second coming is to gather his people together, to reward the faithful and judge the wicked

**[MAT 16:27](https://ref.ly/Matt16:27)** *See also* [DAN 7:13–7:14](https://ref.ly/Dan7:13-Dan7:14); [MAT 25:31–25:32](https://ref.ly/Matt25:31-Matt25:32); [JHN 14:3](https://ref.ly/John14:3); [1CO 4:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor4:5); [1TH 4:16–4:17](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:16-1Thess4:17); [1PE 5:4](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:4); [1JN 3:2](https://ref.ly/1John3:2); [JUD 1:14–1:15](https://ref.ly/Jude1:14-Jude1:15)

### The right attitude towards the second coming

**[1JN 2:28](https://ref.ly/1John2:28)** *See also* [MRK 13:35](https://ref.ly/Mark13:35); [ACT 3:19–3:20](https://ref.ly/Acts3:19-Acts3:20); [1TI 6:13–6:14](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:13-1Tim6:14); [2PE 3:11](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:11)

The resurrection of the dead
----------------------------

### All will be raised

**[JHN 5:28–5:29](https://ref.ly/John5:28-John5:29)** *See also* [DAN 12:2](https://ref.ly/Dan12:2); [ACT 24:15](https://ref.ly/Acts24:15)

### Believers in Jesus Christ will be raised to eternal life

**[JHN 6:40](https://ref.ly/John6:40)** *See also* [JHN 11:25](https://ref.ly/John11:25); [2CO 4:14](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:14); [1TH 4:16](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:16)

### Unbelievers will be condemned

**[JHN 5:29](https://ref.ly/John5:29)** *See also* [MAT 25:46](https://ref.ly/Matt25:46)

### Believers will be given resurrection bodies

**[1CO 15:42–15:44](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:42-1Cor15:44)** *See also* [1CO 15:50–15:53](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:50-1Cor15:53)

The last judgment
-----------------

### All face judgment after death

**[HEB 9:27](https://ref.ly/Heb9:27)** *See also* [ROM 14:12](https://ref.ly/Rom14:12); [1PE 4:5](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:5); [REV 20:11–20:12](https://ref.ly/Rev20:11-Rev20:12)

### Judgment is entrusted to Jesus Christ

**[JHN 5:22](https://ref.ly/John5:22)** *See also* [ACT 10:42](https://ref.ly/Acts10:42), [ACT 17:31](https://ref.ly/Acts17:31); [REV 1:18](https://ref.ly/Rev1:18)

### Those who have not responded to Christ will be condemned

**[JHN 12:48](https://ref.ly/John12:48)** *See also* [2TH 1:7–1:8](https://ref.ly/2Thess1:7-2Thess1:8); [2PE 3:7](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:7); [JUD 1:15](https://ref.ly/Jude1:15); [REV 20:15](https://ref.ly/Rev20:15)

### Believers will not be judged for sin

**[JHN 5:24](https://ref.ly/John5:24)** *See also* [JHN 3:18](https://ref.ly/John3:18); [ROM 8:1–8:2](https://ref.ly/Rom8:1-Rom8:2), [ROM 8:33–8:34](https://ref.ly/Rom8:33-Rom8:34)

### Believers will be judged on how they have lived their Christian lives

**[2CO 5:10](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:10)** *See also* [1CO 4:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor4:5); [HEB 9:28](https://ref.ly/Heb9:28); [1JN 4:17](https://ref.ly/1John4:17)

Heaven
------

### God's throne is in heaven where he is continuously worshipped

**[REV 4:9–4:10](https://ref.ly/Rev4:9-Rev4:10)** *See also* [ISA 6:1–6:3](https://ref.ly/Isa6:1-Isa6:3)

### God's will is perfectly served in heaven

**[MAT 6:10](https://ref.ly/Matt6:10)** parallel [LUK 11:2](https://ref.ly/Luke11:2) *See also* [1KI 22:19](https://ref.ly/1Kgs22:19)

### At Jesus Christ's second coming, a new heaven and earth will replace the old

**[REV 21:1](https://ref.ly/Rev21:1)**

### The redeemed will enjoy life in the presence of God in the new heaven

**[1TH 4:17](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:17)** *See also* [MAT 5:8](https://ref.ly/Matt5:8); [PHP 3:20](https://ref.ly/Phil3:20); [JUD 1:24](https://ref.ly/Jude1:24); [REV 21:1–21:4](https://ref.ly/Rev21:1-Rev21:4)

### All life in heaven is sustained by God

**[REV 22:1–22:2](https://ref.ly/Rev22:1-Rev22:2)** *See also* [JHN 6:58](https://ref.ly/John6:58); [REV 2:7](https://ref.ly/Rev2:7), [REV 21:23](https://ref.ly/Rev21:23)

Hell
----

### Hell is the destiny of human beings who reject God

**[MAT 25:41](https://ref.ly/Matt25:41)** *Hell was originally meant for the punishment of the devil and his angels.* *See also* [MAT 13:41–13:42](https://ref.ly/Matt13:41-Matt13:42); [ROM 2:8](https://ref.ly/Rom2:8); [HEB 10:26–10:27](https://ref.ly/Heb10:26-Heb10:27); [JUD 1:6](https://ref.ly/Jude1:6); [REV 17:8](https://ref.ly/Rev17:8)

### Those in hell are finally separated from God

**[2TH 1:9](https://ref.ly/2Thess1:9)** *See also* [MAT 25:46](https://ref.ly/Matt25:46)

### Hell is a place of fire, darkness and weeping

**[REV 20:15](https://ref.ly/Rev20:15)** *See also* [MAT 8:12](https://ref.ly/Matt8:12); [2PE 3:7](https://ref.ly/2Pet3:7); [JUD 1:7](https://ref.ly/Jude1:7)

### Jesus Christ himself frequently warned about the dangers of hell

**[LUK 12:5](https://ref.ly/Luke12:5)** *See also* [MAT 5:29–5:30](https://ref.ly/Matt5:29-Matt5:30) *Jesus Christ is using Eastern hyperbole, not intended literally, to stress importance*; [MAT 13:40](https://ref.ly/Matt13:40)
