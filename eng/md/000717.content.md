## 5128: baldness (id: 5128)

Loss of hair on the head as the result of natural causes or deliberate action. Baldness may be used to signify judgment, mourning or shame.

Instructions regarding baldness within Jewish law
-------------------------------------------------

### Associated with idolatrous practice

*See also* [LEV 21:5](https://ref.ly/Lev21:5); [DEU 14:1](https://ref.ly/Deut14:1); [JER 16:6](https://ref.ly/Jer16:6) *In pagan Canaan mourners shaved their foreheads.*

### Regarding sores on bald heads

[LEV 13:40–13:42](https://ref.ly/Lev13:40-Lev13:42)

### Cleansing may involve shaving the head

[LEV 14:8–14:9](https://ref.ly/Lev14:8-Lev14:9); ; [NUM 6:9](https://ref.ly/Num6:9), [NUM 6:18](https://ref.ly/Num6:18); ; [DEU 21:12](https://ref.ly/Deut21:12)

### Vows that involve shaving the head

[JDG 13:5](https://ref.ly/Judg13:5), [JDG 16:17–16:19](https://ref.ly/Judg16:17-Judg16:19); ; [ACT 18:18](https://ref.ly/Acts18:18) *based on the Nazarite rule recorded in [Nu 6:1–21](https://ref.ly/Num6:1-Num6:21)*

Baldness may signify God's judgment
-----------------------------------

[EZK 5:1](https://ref.ly/Ezek5:1) *Ezekiel instructed to shave his head to symbolise this*

Baldness as a symbol of mourning
--------------------------------

**[JOB 1:20](https://ref.ly/Job1:20)** *Job here mourns the loss of his children, household and livestock.* *See also* [ISA 15:2](https://ref.ly/Isa15:2), [ISA 22:12](https://ref.ly/Isa22:12); [JER 48:37](https://ref.ly/Jer48:37); [EZK 27:31](https://ref.ly/Ezek27:31); [AMO 8:10](https://ref.ly/Amos8:10); [MIC 1:16](https://ref.ly/Mic1:16)
