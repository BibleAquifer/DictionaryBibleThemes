## 8658: Lord's Prayer, the (id: 8658)

The name given to the prayer Jesus Christ taught his disciples, probably as a model prayer for regular use.

Aspects of the Lord's Prayer
----------------------------

### A recognition of God as Father and Lord

**[MAT 6:9](https://ref.ly/Matt6:9)** parallel [LUK 11:2](https://ref.ly/Luke11:2) *See also* [MAT 7:21](https://ref.ly/Matt7:21), [MAT 16:17](https://ref.ly/Matt16:17); [LUK 10:21](https://ref.ly/Luke10:21); [EPH 3:14–3:15](https://ref.ly/Eph3:14-Eph3:15)

### A desire for God's perfect will to be done

**[MAT 6:10](https://ref.ly/Matt6:10)** parallel [LUK 11:2](https://ref.ly/Luke11:2) *See also* [MAT 12:50](https://ref.ly/Matt12:50), [MAT 26:39](https://ref.ly/Matt26:39), [MAT 26:42](https://ref.ly/Matt26:42); [ACT 21:14](https://ref.ly/Acts21:14); [COL 1:9](https://ref.ly/Col1:9); [HEB 10:9](https://ref.ly/Heb10:9)

### Asking for physical provision

**[MAT 6:11](https://ref.ly/Matt6:11)** parallel [LUK 11:3](https://ref.ly/Luke11:3) *See also* [MAT 6:31–6:32](https://ref.ly/Matt6:31-Matt6:32), [MAT 7:9–7:11](https://ref.ly/Matt7:9-Matt7:11)

### Asking for forgiveness

**[MAT 6:12](https://ref.ly/Matt6:12)** parallel [LUK 11:4](https://ref.ly/Luke11:4) *See also* [MAT 6:14–6:15](https://ref.ly/Matt6:14-Matt6:15), [MAT 18:23–18:35](https://ref.ly/Matt18:23-Matt18:35); [MRK 11:25](https://ref.ly/Mark11:25); [LUK 23:34](https://ref.ly/Luke23:34); [COL 3:13](https://ref.ly/Col3:13); [1JN 1:9](https://ref.ly/1John1:9)

### Asking for spiritual protection

**[MAT 6:13](https://ref.ly/Matt6:13)** parallel [LUK 11:4](https://ref.ly/Luke11:4) *See also* [2CO 1:10–1:11](https://ref.ly/2Cor1:10-2Cor1:11); [JUD 1:24](https://ref.ly/Jude1:24); [REV 3:10](https://ref.ly/Rev3:10)
