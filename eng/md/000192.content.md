## 2336: Jesus Christ, exaltation of (id: 2336)

Having completed his work on earth, Jesus Christ is raised to God's right hand where he receives honour, power and glory.

Jesus Christ is exalted to God's right hand
-------------------------------------------

**[1PE 3:21–3:22](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:21-1Pet3:22)** *God's right hand is the position of authority second only to the Father.* *See also* [MAT 26:64](https://ref.ly/Matt26:64) parallels [MRK 14:62](https://ref.ly/Mark14:62), [LUK 22:69](https://ref.ly/Luke22:69); [ACT 2:33](https://ref.ly/Acts2:33), [ACT 3:20–3:21](https://ref.ly/Acts3:20-Acts3:21), [ACT 7:55–7:56](https://ref.ly/Acts7:55-Acts7:56); [HEB 1:3](https://ref.ly/Heb1:3), [HEB 12:2](https://ref.ly/Heb12:2)
