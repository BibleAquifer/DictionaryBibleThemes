## 8431: fasting, reasons for (id: 8431)

Scripture identifies a number of situations in which fasting is appropriate.

Situations in which fasting is appropriate
------------------------------------------

### Bereavement

**[1CH 10:11–10:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr10:11-1Chr10:12)** parallel [1SA 31:11–31:13](https://ref.ly/1Sam31:11-1Sam31:13) *See also* [2SA 1:11–1:12](https://ref.ly/2Sam1:11-2Sam1:12), [2SA 3:31–3:35](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:31-2Sam3:35)

### Distress

**[EST 4:3](https://ref.ly/Esth4:3)** *See also* [1SA 1:7](https://ref.ly/1Sam1:7), [1SA 20:34](https://ref.ly/1Sam20:34); [1KI 21:4–21:6](https://ref.ly/1Kgs21:4-1Kgs21:6); [PSA 109:24](https://ref.ly/Ps109:24); [DAN 6:18](https://ref.ly/Dan6:18); [ACT 27:33](https://ref.ly/Acts27:33)

### Penitence

**[1SA 7:5–7:6](https://ref.ly/1Sam7:5-1Sam7:6)** *See also* [NEH 9:1–9:3](https://ref.ly/Neh9:1-Neh9:3); [DAN 9:3–9:6](https://ref.ly/Dan9:3-Dan9:6) *Daniel identifies himself with the sin of the people*; [DAN 9:20](https://ref.ly/Dan9:20); [JOL 1:13–1:14](https://ref.ly/Joel1:13-Joel1:14), [JOL 2:12–2:15](https://ref.ly/Joel2:12-Joel2:15); [JON 3:5–3:9](https://ref.ly/Jonah3:5-Jonah3:9)

### Seeking God's intervention

**[2SA 12:15–12:17](https://ref.ly/2Sam12:15-2Sam12:17)** *See also* [2CH 20:2–20:4](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:2-2Chr20:4); [EZR 8:21](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:21); [EST 4:15–4:16](https://ref.ly/Esth4:15-Esth4:16); [PSA 35:13–35:14](https://ref.ly/Ps35:13-Ps35:14)

### Seeking guidance

**[JDG 20:26–20:28](https://ref.ly/Judg20:26-Judg20:28)** *See also* [DEU 9:9](https://ref.ly/Deut9:9) *Moses fasts before the revelation of the Law*; [DAN 9:1–9:3](https://ref.ly/Dan9:1-Dan9:3), [DAN 9:20–9:23](https://ref.ly/Dan9:20-Dan9:23), [DAN 10:1–10:2](https://ref.ly/Dan10:1-Dan10:2), [DAN 10:12](https://ref.ly/Dan10:12); [ACT 13:1–13:3](https://ref.ly/Acts13:1-Acts13:3) *The church receives direction through the Holy Spirit while fasting.*
