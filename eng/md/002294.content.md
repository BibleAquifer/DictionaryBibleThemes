## 8616: prayerlessness (id: 8616)

Without prayer any relationship with God is impoverished because vital communication is lost.

Reasons for prayerlessness
--------------------------

### Unbelief

**[PSA 14:1–14:4](https://ref.ly/Ps14:1-Ps14:4)** parallel [PSA 53:1–53:4](https://ref.ly/Ps53:1-Ps53:4) *See also* [JOB 21:15](https://ref.ly/Job21:15); [ROM 3:11](https://ref.ly/Rom3:11)

### Self\-reliance

**[JER 17:5](https://ref.ly/Jer17:5)** *See also* [ZEP 3:2](https://ref.ly/Zeph3:2)

### Worship of other gods

**[DEU 32:17–32:18](https://ref.ly/Deut32:17-Deut32:18)** *See also* [JER 2:5–2:6](https://ref.ly/Jer2:5-Jer2:6), [JER 2:8](https://ref.ly/Jer2:8)

### Guilt as a result of disobedience

[GEN 3:9–3:12](https://ref.ly/Gen3:9-Gen3:12) *Adam and Eve did not respond to their sin by praying. On the contrary they sought to hide from God*; ; [JON 1:1–1:6](https://ref.ly/Jonah1:1-Jonah1:6) *Jonah tried to run away from God.*

### Tiredness

**[LUK 22:45](https://ref.ly/Luke22:45)** *See also* [MAT 26:40](https://ref.ly/Matt26:40) parallel [MRK 14:37](https://ref.ly/Mark14:37); [MAT 26:43–26:45](https://ref.ly/Matt26:43-Matt26:45) parallel [MRK 14:40–14:41](https://ref.ly/Mark14:40-Mark14:41)

### Discord in relationship

[1PE 3:7](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:7)

Examples of prayerlessness
--------------------------

### Joshua, after a great victory

[JOS 7:2–7:5](https://ref.ly/Josh7:2-Josh7:5) *Joshua devised his own plan in attacking Ai and did not seek the LORD until after he suffered a disastrous defeat.*

### King Ahaziah, turning to idols

**[2KI 1:16](https://ref.ly/2Kgs1:16)** *See also* [2KI 1:1–1:6](https://ref.ly/2Kgs1:1-2Kgs1:6)

### The nominal religion of Israel

**[ISA 43:22](https://ref.ly/Isa43:22)** *This verse implies that prayer involves effort\-\-the people only paid lip service to God.* *See also* [ISA 1:14](https://ref.ly/Isa1:14), [ISA 64:7](https://ref.ly/Isa64:7)

### Israel trusts in other people rather than God

[ISA 30:1–30:2](https://ref.ly/Isa30:1-Isa30:2), [ISA 31:1](https://ref.ly/Isa31:1)

### Israel's failure to repent of their sins

**[DAN 9:13](https://ref.ly/Dan9:13)** *See also* [ISA 9:13](https://ref.ly/Isa9:13); [JER 5:3](https://ref.ly/Jer5:3)

### The lack of an intercessor among God's people

[EZK 22:30](https://ref.ly/Ezek22:30)

Consequences of prayerlessness
------------------------------

### Alienation from God

**[JHN 15:6](https://ref.ly/John15:6)** *See also* [PSA 73:27](https://ref.ly/Ps73:27)

### God's judgment

**[ZEP 1:4–1:6](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:4-Zeph1:6)** *See also* [JER 10:21](https://ref.ly/Jer10:21)

### Disloyalty to Jesus Christ

**[MAT 26:74](https://ref.ly/Matt26:74)** parallels [MRK 14:71](https://ref.ly/Mark14:71), [LUK 22:60](https://ref.ly/Luke22:60), [JHN 18:27](https://ref.ly/John18:27) *Peter had previously failed to pray in Gethsemane.*

### Spiritual poverty

**[JAS 4:2](https://ref.ly/Jas4:2)** *See also* [2KI 17:15](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:15); [JER 2:5](https://ref.ly/Jer2:5)

### Ineffective ministry

[1SA 2:12](https://ref.ly/1Sam2:12) *This literally means they had no fellowship with the LORD*; ; [1SA 12:23](https://ref.ly/1Sam12:23); ; [JHN 15:4–15:5](https://ref.ly/John15:4-John15:5)

### Conflicts with others because of unfulfilled desires

[JAS 4:1–4:4](https://ref.ly/Jas4:1-Jas4:4)
