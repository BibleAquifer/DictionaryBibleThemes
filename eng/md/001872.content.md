## 7525: exclusiveness (id: 7525)

The keeping of a distinctive group identity by drawing clear boundary lines, to separate those within the group from outsiders with whom they are unwilling to associate.

The practice of exclusiveness by God's people, grounded in God's claim to exclusive worship
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[EXO 20:3](https://ref.ly/Exod20:3)** parallel [DEU 5:7](https://ref.ly/Deut5:7) *See also* [EXO 34:14](https://ref.ly/Exod34:14); [DEU 6:14–6:15](https://ref.ly/Deut6:14-Deut6:15); [2KI 17:35–17:39](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:35-2Kgs17:39); [ISA 42:8](https://ref.ly/Isa42:8)
