## 4833: light (id: 4833)

The brightness that enables sight in the darkness. Scripture often uses light as a symbol of the saving presence of God in a fallen world, with darkness being used as a symbol of sin or the absence of God.

* **Associated Articles:** 4834: light, natural (ID: `4834@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 4835: light, spiritual (ID: `4835@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 4836: light, and people of God (ID: `4836@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
