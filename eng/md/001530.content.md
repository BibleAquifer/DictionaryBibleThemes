## 6157: fall, of Satan and his angels (id: 6157)

Satan and his angels rebelled against God and were removed from their positions of authority. They are now destined for judgment.

The rebellion of Satan and his angels
-------------------------------------

**[ISA 14:12–14:15](https://ref.ly/Isa14:12-Isa14:15)** *Written about a king in Isaiah's own time, this passage has been used by both Jews and Christians as a description of the fall of Satan*; **[JUD 1:6](https://ref.ly/Jude1:6)** *See also* [JOB 4:18](https://ref.ly/Job4:18); [EZK 28:15](https://ref.ly/Ezek28:15); [LUK 10:18](https://ref.ly/Luke10:18); [REV 12:7–12:9](https://ref.ly/Rev12:7-Rev12:9), [REV 12:12](https://ref.ly/Rev12:12)
