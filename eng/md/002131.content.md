## 8299: love, in relationships (id: 8299)

Human love is ennobled by being patterned on God's love for his people. It is also safeguarded by God's commands.

The love between husband and wife
---------------------------------

### Conjugal love is commanded

**[COL 3:18–3:19](https://ref.ly/Col3:18-Col3:19)** *See also* [GEN 2:24](https://ref.ly/Gen2:24); [DEU 24:5](https://ref.ly/Deut24:5); [PRO 5:18–5:20](https://ref.ly/Prov5:18-Prov5:20); [ECC 9:9](https://ref.ly/Eccl9:9); [EPH 5:22](https://ref.ly/Eph5:22), [EPH 5:28](https://ref.ly/Eph5:28), [EPH 5:33](https://ref.ly/Eph5:33); [1PE 3:7](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:7)
