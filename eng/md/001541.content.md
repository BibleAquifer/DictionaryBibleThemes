## 6182: ignorance, and human situation (id: 6182)

Ignorance is part of the human condition: as finite creatures, human knowledge is incomplete, but human beings can trust God, whose knowledge is complete, to guide their steps.

Ignorance of God
----------------

### Finite human beings cannot know God completely

**[ECC 11:5](https://ref.ly/Eccl11:5)** *See also* [JOB 38:1–38:7](https://ref.ly/Job38:1-Job38:7); [ECC 3:11](https://ref.ly/Eccl3:11)

### This ignorance will be dispelled at Jesus Christ's return

**[1CO 13:9–13:12](https://ref.ly/1Cor13:9-1Cor13:12)**

Ignorance of the future
-----------------------

### Human beings are ignorant of future events

**[ECC 8:7–8:8](https://ref.ly/Eccl8:7-Eccl8:8)** *See also* [ECC 3:22](https://ref.ly/Eccl3:22), [ECC 9:12](https://ref.ly/Eccl9:12), [ECC 10:14](https://ref.ly/Eccl10:14), [ECC 11:2](https://ref.ly/Eccl11:2), [ECC 11:6](https://ref.ly/Eccl11:6); [ACT 20:22](https://ref.ly/Acts20:22)

### Human beings are ignorant of the time of Jesus Christ's return

**[MRK 13:32–13:36](https://ref.ly/Mark13:32-Mark13:36)** parallel [MAT 24:36–24:44](https://ref.ly/Matt24:36-Matt24:44)

### Presumptious plans should therefore be avoided

**[JAS 4:13–4:16](https://ref.ly/Jas4:13-Jas4:16)** *See also* [PSA 39:6](https://ref.ly/Ps39:6); [PRO 27:1](https://ref.ly/Prov27:1); [LUK 12:16–12:21](https://ref.ly/Luke12:16-Luke12:21)

### God's guidance is essential

**[PRO 20:24](https://ref.ly/Prov20:24)** *See also* [PRO 19:21](https://ref.ly/Prov19:21); [JER 10:23](https://ref.ly/Jer10:23)

Ignorance of doctrinal matters
------------------------------

### Within the church

**[1TH 4:13–4:18](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:13-1Thess4:18)** *See also* [1CO 10:1–10:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:1-1Cor10:10), [1CO 12:1–12:11](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:1-1Cor12:11)

### God has established teachers to combat ignorance

**[1CO 12:28](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:28)** *See also* [EPH 4:11–4:13](https://ref.ly/Eph4:11-Eph4:13); [1TI 4:13](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:13), [1TI 5:17](https://ref.ly/1Tim5:17); [2TI 3:16–3:17](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:16-2Tim3:17)
