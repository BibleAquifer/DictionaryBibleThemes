## 7110: body of Christ (id: 7110)

The term refers both to the physical body of Jesus Christ, which was broken on the cross in order that redemption might be accomplished through him, and to the church.

Jesus Christ's physical body
----------------------------

### His birth

**[JHN 1:14](https://ref.ly/John1:14)** *See also* [ROM 1:3](https://ref.ly/Rom1:3); [GAL 4:4](https://ref.ly/Gal4:4); [1TI 3:16](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:16)

### His bodily needs

[MAT 8:24](https://ref.ly/Matt8:24) parallels [MRK 4:38](https://ref.ly/Mark4:38), [LUK 8:23](https://ref.ly/Luke8:23); ; [MRK 11:12](https://ref.ly/Mark11:12) parallel [MAT 21:18](https://ref.ly/Matt21:18); ; [LUK 4:2](https://ref.ly/Luke4:2) parallel [MAT 4:2](https://ref.ly/Matt4:2); ; [JHN 4:6](https://ref.ly/John4:6), [JHN 19:28](https://ref.ly/John19:28)
