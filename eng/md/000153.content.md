## 2039: Jesus Christ, joy of (id: 2039)

Jesus Christ brings joy to his people, who are able to rejoice at the coming of their Saviour and Lord, and all the benefits which he brings to them.

Jesus Christ's joy originates in God
------------------------------------

### From his intimate relationship with his Father and the Spirit

**[LUK 10:21](https://ref.ly/Luke10:21)** parallel [MAT 11:25–11:26](https://ref.ly/Matt11:25-Matt11:26) *See also* [ISA 11:2–11:3](https://ref.ly/Isa11:2-Isa11:3); [MAT 3:16–3:17](https://ref.ly/Matt3:16-Matt3:17) parallels [MRK 1:10–1:11](https://ref.ly/Mark1:10-Mark1:11), [LUK 3:22](https://ref.ly/Luke3:22); [MAT 12:18](https://ref.ly/Matt12:18); [ISA 42:1](https://ref.ly/Isa42:1); [ACT 2:28](https://ref.ly/Acts2:28); [PSA 16:11](https://ref.ly/Ps16:11)

### From his awareness of God's purposes

**[HEB 12:2](https://ref.ly/Heb12:2)** *See also* [JHN 8:29](https://ref.ly/John8:29)
