## 7251: pillar of cloud and fire (id: 7251)

A physical manifestation of the presence of God among his people; a special form of theophany particularly associated with Israel being offered guidance on the exodus journey.

Functions of the pillar of cloud and fire
-----------------------------------------

### To lead Israel

**[EXO 13:21–13:22](https://ref.ly/Exod13:21-Exod13:22)** *See also* [EXO 40:36–40:37](https://ref.ly/Exod40:36-Exod40:37); [NUM 9:17](https://ref.ly/Num9:17), [NUM 14:14](https://ref.ly/Num14:14); [NEH 9:12](https://ref.ly/Neh9:12), [NEH 9:19](https://ref.ly/Neh9:19)

### To separate and protect Israel from the armies of Egypt

**[EXO 14:19–14:20](https://ref.ly/Exod14:19-Exod14:20)** *This was just before the crossing of the Red Sea.*

### To represent God's presence, especially to Moses

**[EXO 33:9](https://ref.ly/Exod33:9)** *See also* [NUM 12:5–12:6](https://ref.ly/Num12:5-Num12:6); [DEU 31:15–31:16](https://ref.ly/Deut31:15-Deut31:16); [PSA 99:7](https://ref.ly/Ps99:7)

Effects of the pillar of cloud and fire
---------------------------------------

### It evoked worship

[EXO 33:10](https://ref.ly/Exod33:10)

### It caused the nations to speak of God

[NUM 14:14](https://ref.ly/Num14:14)

### Experiencing the cloud did not guarantee acceptance by God

[1CO 10:1–10:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:1-1Cor10:5)
