## 7456: synagogue (id: 7456)

A word meaning literally “a gathering together”. In the NT it sometimes refers to a group of Jews meeting for worship but most often to the building in which they met.

Synagogue worship may have begun during the exile
-------------------------------------------------

**[EZK 14:1](https://ref.ly/Ezek14:1)** *Unable to sacrifice in the temple, the community met together with spiritual leaders.* *See also* [EZK 20:1](https://ref.ly/Ezek20:1)

A synagogue might be established anywhere
-----------------------------------------

**[MRK 1:21](https://ref.ly/Mark1:21)** *See also* [ACT 6:9](https://ref.ly/Acts6:9) *in Jerusalem*[ACT 16:12–16:13](https://ref.ly/Acts16:12-Acts16:13), [ACT 16:16](https://ref.ly/Acts16:16)[ACT 17:1](https://ref.ly/Acts17:1), [ACT 17:10](https://ref.ly/Acts17:10), [ACT 18:19](https://ref.ly/Acts18:19)

The role of the synagogue
-------------------------

### It was a focus for prayer and the reading of Scripture

**[ACT 13:15](https://ref.ly/Acts13:15)** *See also* [NEH 8:2–8:8](https://ref.ly/Neh8:2-Neh8:8), [NEH 9:3](https://ref.ly/Neh9:3); [LUK 4:15–4:33](https://ref.ly/Luke4:15-Luke4:33)

### It was a centre for community affairs

[MAT 10:17](https://ref.ly/Matt10:17); ; [MRK 13:9](https://ref.ly/Mark13:9); ; [LUK 12:11](https://ref.ly/Luke12:11)

### Exclusion from the synagogue was used as a punishment

[JHN 9:22](https://ref.ly/John9:22), [JHN 12:42](https://ref.ly/John12:42)

The leading of synagogue worship
--------------------------------

### It was the responsibility of the synagogue ruler

[MRK 5:22](https://ref.ly/Mark5:22); ; [ACT 18:8](https://ref.ly/Acts18:8)

### Qualified laymen were allowed to teach

**[LUK 4:16](https://ref.ly/Luke4:16)** *See also* [ACT 13:15](https://ref.ly/Acts13:15)

Attendance at the synagogue
---------------------------

### It was the practice of Jesus Christ

**[MAT 9:35](https://ref.ly/Matt9:35)** *See also* [MRK 6:2](https://ref.ly/Mark6:2); [LUK 4:16](https://ref.ly/Luke4:16); [JHN 6:59](https://ref.ly/John6:59)

### It was the practice of Paul and other Jewish Christians

**[ACT 9:19–9:20](https://ref.ly/Acts9:19-Acts9:20)** *See also* [ACT 14:1–14:2](https://ref.ly/Acts14:1-Acts14:2), [ACT 17:2](https://ref.ly/Acts17:2), [ACT 19:8](https://ref.ly/Acts19:8)

### The first Christians continued to worship in the synagogues

[LUK 24:53](https://ref.ly/Luke24:53); ; [ACT 19:8–19:9](https://ref.ly/Acts19:8-Acts19:9)
