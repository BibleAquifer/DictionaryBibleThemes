## 5947: shame (id: 5947)

An uncomfortable feeling of guilt and humiliation, usually arising from sin or failure.

Shame is to be avoided
----------------------

**[PSA 25:2](https://ref.ly/Ps25:2)** *See also* [PSA 25:20](https://ref.ly/Ps25:20), [PSA 31:1](https://ref.ly/Ps31:1), [PSA 31:17](https://ref.ly/Ps31:17), [PSA 44:15](https://ref.ly/Ps44:15), [PSA 119:31](https://ref.ly/Ps119:31), [PSA 119:80](https://ref.ly/Ps119:80)
