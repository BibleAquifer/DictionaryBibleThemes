## 8709: astrology (id: 8709)

The study of the stars and planets, based on the pagan assumption that they exercise a decisive influence over human destinies. Scripture rejects such beliefs, insisting upon the total sovereignty of God over his entire creation.

Astrology is to be rejected
---------------------------

### The worship of heavenly bodies is forbidden

**[DEU 4:19](https://ref.ly/Deut4:19)**; **[2KI 17:16](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:16)** *See also* [2KI 21:3–21:5](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:3-2Kgs21:5) parallel [2CH 33:3–33:5](https://ref.ly/2Chr33:3-2Chr33:5); [2KI 23:4](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:4), [2KI 23:11](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:11); [JOB 31:26–31:28](https://ref.ly/Job31:26-Job31:28); [EZK 8:16–8:18](https://ref.ly/Ezek8:16-Ezek8:18); [ZEP 1:5](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:5)

### The consultation of stars is condemned

**[ISA 47:13](https://ref.ly/Isa47:13)** *See also* [JER 8:2](https://ref.ly/Jer8:2)
