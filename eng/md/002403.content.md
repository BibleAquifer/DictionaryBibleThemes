## 8777: lust (id: 8777)

An overpowering and compulsive desire or passion, especially of a sexual nature. Scripture condemns lust of all kinds, and urges believers to show self\-control.

Lust has its origins in the heart and mind
------------------------------------------

**[PRO 6:25–6:29](https://ref.ly/Prov6:25-Prov6:29)**; **[MAT 5:28](https://ref.ly/Matt5:28)** *See also* [GEN 3:6](https://ref.ly/Gen3:6); [JOB 31:1](https://ref.ly/Job31:1); [JAS 1:13–1:15](https://ref.ly/Jas1:13-Jas1:15); [1JN 2:16](https://ref.ly/1John2:16)

Lust is natural to unbelievers
------------------------------

**[ROM 1:21–1:27](https://ref.ly/Rom1:21-Rom1:27)** *See also* [ROM 7:5](https://ref.ly/Rom7:5); [1CO 6:9–6:10](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:9-1Cor6:10); [1PE 4:3](https://ref.ly/1Pet4:3); [2PE 2:14–2:18](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:14-2Pet2:18)

Believers can and must fight against lust by showing self\-control
------------------------------------------------------------------

**[GAL 5:16–5:21](https://ref.ly/Gal5:16-Gal5:21)**; **[COL 3:5](https://ref.ly/Col3:5)** *See also* [1CO 9:27](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:27); [GAL 5:24](https://ref.ly/Gal5:24); [EPH 4:22](https://ref.ly/Eph4:22); [1TH 4:4–4:5](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:4-1Thess4:5); [2TI 2:22](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:22); [TIT 2:12](https://ref.ly/Titus2:12); [1PE 2:11](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:11)

Examples of lust
----------------

### Lust expressed as sexual desire

[GEN 39:6–39:12](https://ref.ly/Gen39:6-Gen39:12) *Potiphar's wife and Joseph*; ; [2SA 11:2–11:5](https://ref.ly/2Sam11:2-2Sam11:5) *David and Bathsheba*

### Lust for money

**[1TI 6:9–6:10](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:9-1Tim6:10)** *See also* [1TI 3:3](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:3)

### The lustful desire of Israel and Judah for alliances

[EZK 23:1–23:21](https://ref.ly/Ezek23:1-Ezek23:21) *Both kingdoms sought security from such alliances, rather than trusting in God.*
