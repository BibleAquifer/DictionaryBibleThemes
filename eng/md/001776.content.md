## 7330: chief priests (id: 7330)

A group of individuals in charge of temple worship in Jerusalem, and regarded as leading representatives of the Jewish people, who came into conflict with Jesus Christ and plotted his death.

OT references to the chief priest
---------------------------------

**[2CH 19:11](https://ref.ly/2Chr19:11)** *See also* [2KI 25:18](https://ref.ly/2Kgs25:18) parallel [JER 52:24](https://ref.ly/Jer52:24) *Seraiah*[2CH 24:6](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:6), [2CH 24:11](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:11)[2CH 31:10](https://ref.ly/2Chr31:10) *Azariah*; [EZR 7:5](https://ref.ly/Ezra7:5) *Ezra was descended from Aaron the chief priest*

The chief priests were in charge of temple worship in Jerusalem
---------------------------------------------------------------

[MAT 2:4](https://ref.ly/Matt2:4) *They were expected to pronounce on matters of Jewish law and interpretation of Scripture*; ; [MAT 21:15](https://ref.ly/Matt21:15) *Temple discipline was their responsibility.*

Jesus Christ predicted his suffering at the hands of the chief priests and elders
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 16:21](https://ref.ly/Matt16:21)** parallels [MRK 8:31](https://ref.ly/Mark8:31), [LUK 9:22](https://ref.ly/Luke9:22) *See also* [MAT 20:18](https://ref.ly/Matt20:18) parallel [MRK 10:33](https://ref.ly/Mark10:33)

The response of the chief priests to Jesus Christ
