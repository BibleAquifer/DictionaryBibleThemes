## 7424: ritual law (id: 7424)

The rules and regulations regarding the conduct of the priests and people of Israel in approaching God, especially in worship. They were to be holy as God is holy. The perfect sacrifice of Jesus Christ on the cross is seen by NT writers as fulfilling this demand for holiness and obedience.

The ritual law and the priesthood
---------------------------------

### The priests were to be careful to obey God's commandments

**[LEV 22:9](https://ref.ly/Lev22:9)** *See also* [LEV 10:10](https://ref.ly/Lev10:10), [LEV 22:3](https://ref.ly/Lev22:3)

### The priests had to avoid ceremonial uncleanness

Contact with the dead caused uncleanness and was forbidden to the high priest: [LEV 21:1–21:3](https://ref.ly/Lev21:1-Lev21:3), [LEV 21:11](https://ref.ly/Lev21:11); ; [NUM 19:11](https://ref.ly/Num19:11), [NUM 19:14](https://ref.ly/Num19:14); [LEV 21:16–21:23](https://ref.ly/Lev21:16-Lev21:23) *No\-one with a physical defect could serve as a priest*; ; [LEV 22:20–22:25](https://ref.ly/Lev22:20-Lev22:25) *The sacrifices offered by the priests also had to be without defect.*

The ritual law and the people of Israel
---------------------------------------

### The people were called to holiness

**[LEV 19:2](https://ref.ly/Lev19:2), [LEV 20:26](https://ref.ly/Lev20:26)** *See also* [LEV 11:44–11:45](https://ref.ly/Lev11:44-Lev11:45), [LEV 20:7](https://ref.ly/Lev20:7), [LEV 21:8](https://ref.ly/Lev21:8), [LEV 22:32](https://ref.ly/Lev22:32)

### The ritual law stipulated what they could eat

**[LEV 11:46–11:47](https://ref.ly/Lev11:46-Lev11:47)** *See also* [GEN 7:2](https://ref.ly/Gen7:2), [GEN 8:20](https://ref.ly/Gen8:20); [LEV 11:1–11:23](https://ref.ly/Lev11:1-Lev11:23) parallel [DEU 14:3–14:20](https://ref.ly/Deut14:3-Deut14:20); [DEU 14:21](https://ref.ly/Deut14:21)

### The ritual law regarding childbirth

[LEV 12:1–12:5](https://ref.ly/Lev12:1-Lev12:5); ; [LUK 2:22–2:24](https://ref.ly/Luke2:22-Luke2:24)
