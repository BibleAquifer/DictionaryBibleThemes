## 7382: house of God (id: 7382)

The place where God was seen to dwell with his people, initially the tabernacle and subsequently the temple at Jerusalem.

Early references to the house of God
------------------------------------

**[GEN 28:16–28:17](https://ref.ly/Gen28:16-Gen28:17)** *“house of God” was first used by Jacob to commemorate the place where he met with God*; **[GEN 28:19](https://ref.ly/Gen28:19)** *“Bethel” means “house of God”.* *See also* [GEN 28:22](https://ref.ly/Gen28:22)

The tabernacle as the house of God
----------------------------------

### The tabernacle was referred to as such before its construction

**[EXO 23:19](https://ref.ly/Exod23:19)** *See also* [EXO 34:26](https://ref.ly/Exod34:26)

### In the wilderness God said he would dwell with his people in the tabernacle

**[EXO 25:8–25:9](https://ref.ly/Exod25:8-Exod25:9)**

### God's presence came and dwelt in the tabernacle when it was completed

**[EXO 40:33–40:34](https://ref.ly/Exod40:33-Exod40:34)** *See also* [EXO 40:35–40:38](https://ref.ly/Exod40:35-Exod40:38)
