## 5959: submission (id: 5959)

A humble attitude where obedience is rendered within a relationship; whether it be to God, authorities or other people at work, in the church, in marriage or in the family.

God requires submission
-----------------------

### From all people

**[PSA 2:9–2:11](https://ref.ly/Ps2:9-Ps2:11)** *See also* [JOB 22:21](https://ref.ly/Job22:21); [1PE 5:6](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:6)

### From Christians especially

**[MAT 6:9–6:10](https://ref.ly/Matt6:9-Matt6:10)** *See also* [HEB 12:9](https://ref.ly/Heb12:9); [JAS 4:7](https://ref.ly/Jas4:7)

The submission of Jesus Christ to his Father
--------------------------------------------

**[LUK 22:42](https://ref.ly/Luke22:42)** parallels [MAT 26:39](https://ref.ly/Matt26:39), [MRK 14:36](https://ref.ly/Mark14:36) *See also* [JHN 5:19](https://ref.ly/John5:19), [JHN 12:49–12:50](https://ref.ly/John12:49-John12:50); [1CO 15:27–15:28](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:27-1Cor15:28); [HEB 5:7–5:8](https://ref.ly/Heb5:7-Heb5:8), [HEB 10:5–10:7](https://ref.ly/Heb10:5-Heb10:7)

The submissive spirit required in the church
--------------------------------------------

### In believers' relationships with one another

**[EPH 5:21](https://ref.ly/Eph5:21)** *See also* [PHP 2:5–2:7](https://ref.ly/Phil2:5-Phil2:7)

### To Jesus Christ

**[EPH 5:24](https://ref.ly/Eph5:24)** *See also* [EPH 1:22–1:23](https://ref.ly/Eph1:22-Eph1:23); [COL 1:18](https://ref.ly/Col1:18) *As the body is subject to the head, so the church is subject to Christ.*

### To the Scriptures

**[JOS 1:7–1:8](https://ref.ly/Josh1:7-Josh1:8)** *See also* [PSA 119:133](https://ref.ly/Ps119:133); [COL 3:15](https://ref.ly/Col3:15); [JAS 1:22–1:25](https://ref.ly/Jas1:22-Jas1:25); [REV 22:18–22:19](https://ref.ly/Rev22:18-Rev22:19)

### To church leaders

**[HEB 13:17](https://ref.ly/Heb13:17)** *See also* [ROM 13:1](https://ref.ly/Rom13:1); [1CO 16:15–16:16](https://ref.ly/1Cor16:15-1Cor16:16); [1TH 5:12–5:13](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:12-1Thess5:13)

### In prophecy

**[1CO 14:32](https://ref.ly/1Cor14:32)** *The implication is that individual prophecies must be submitted to the overall judgment of the church.*
