## 1090: God, majesty of (id: 1090)

The greatness and splendour of God, revealed in his creation and mighty works of deliverance. On account of his majesty, God is worthy of praise and adoration from all people.

**[HEB 8:1](https://ref.ly/Heb8:1)** *See also* [1SA 15:29](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:29); [PSA 24:10](https://ref.ly/Ps24:10) *The word translated “glory” also means “majesty”*; [HEB 1:3–1:4](https://ref.ly/Heb1:3-Heb1:4)
