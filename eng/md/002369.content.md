## 8738: evil, victory over (id: 8738)

Although evil continues to be present and active in the world, Scripture gives an assurance that God will finally triumph over evil in all its forms.

Satan's power is already limited by God
---------------------------------------

**[JOB 1:12](https://ref.ly/Job1:12)** *See also* [GEN 3:14–3:15](https://ref.ly/Gen3:14-Gen3:15); [JOB 2:6](https://ref.ly/Job2:6); [ZEC 3:2](https://ref.ly/Zech3:2); [REV 12:9](https://ref.ly/Rev12:9)
