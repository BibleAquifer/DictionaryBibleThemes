## 6253: temptation, avoiding causing (id: 6253)

Any behaviour which might cause others to do wrong is to be avoided.

Warnings not to cause temptation
--------------------------------

**[LUK 17:1](https://ref.ly/Luke17:1)**

Special care is needed to avoid tempting those whose faith is young
-------------------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 18:6](https://ref.ly/Matt18:6)** *See also* [MRK 9:42](https://ref.ly/Mark9:42); [LUK 17:2](https://ref.ly/Luke17:2)

Temptation is linked to passing judgment on others
--------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 14:13](https://ref.ly/Rom14:13)**

Allowance must be made to avoid causing temptation to those whose faith is weak
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[ROM 14:15](https://ref.ly/Rom14:15), [ROM 14:21](https://ref.ly/Rom14:21)**

Avoiding causing temptation by exercising Christian freedom responsibly
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
