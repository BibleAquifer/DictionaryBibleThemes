## 5291: defence (id: 5291)

A means of protection and security against the attacks of others. Scripture insists that the most reliable form of defence for believers is trust in the Lord.

* **Associated Articles:** 5292: defence, divine (ID: `5292@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 5293: defence, human (ID: `5293@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
