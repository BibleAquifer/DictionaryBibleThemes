## 8367: wisdom, importance of human (id: 8367)

True wisdom may bring material reward but its value far exceeds earthly riches. To those who receive it, it opens a path to life and security and equips for leadership and right conduct.

The supreme value of true wisdom
--------------------------------

### It is priceless

**[PRO 4:7](https://ref.ly/Prov4:7)** *See also* [PRO 3:13](https://ref.ly/Prov3:13), [PRO 8:10–8:11](https://ref.ly/Prov8:10-Prov8:11)

### It leads to life

**[PRO 15:24](https://ref.ly/Prov15:24)** *See also* [PRO 11:30](https://ref.ly/Prov11:30), [PRO 13:14](https://ref.ly/Prov13:14), [PRO 16:22](https://ref.ly/Prov16:22), [PRO 24:14](https://ref.ly/Prov24:14); [DAN 12:3](https://ref.ly/Dan12:3)

### It brings prosperity

**[PRO 8:18](https://ref.ly/Prov8:18)** *The benefits of wisdom may include material prosperity, but more important is the moral and spiritual prosperity which it brings.* *See also* [PRO 3:1–3:2](https://ref.ly/Prov3:1-Prov3:2), [PRO 19:8](https://ref.ly/Prov19:8), [PRO 19:8](https://ref.ly/Prov19:8), [PRO 21:20–21:21](https://ref.ly/Prov21:20-Prov21:21), [PRO 24:3](https://ref.ly/Prov24:3); [JER 10:21](https://ref.ly/Jer10:21)

### It gives security

**[PRO 4:6](https://ref.ly/Prov4:6)** *See also* [PRO 1:33](https://ref.ly/Prov1:33), [PRO 2:6–2:11](https://ref.ly/Prov2:6-Prov2:11), [PRO 14:3](https://ref.ly/Prov14:3), [PRO 28:26](https://ref.ly/Prov28:26); [ECC 8:5](https://ref.ly/Eccl8:5)
