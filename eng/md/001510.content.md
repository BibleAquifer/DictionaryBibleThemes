## 6112: banishment (id: 6112)

A form of punishment in which the guilty party is expelled from a region and forbidden to return. An important example is the banishment of Adam and Eve from Eden as a result of their disobedience to God.

Banishment is a punishment for disobedience
-------------------------------------------

**[EZR 7:26](https://ref.ly/Ezra7:26)** *See also* [EXO 12:15](https://ref.ly/Exod12:15), [EXO 31:14](https://ref.ly/Exod31:14); [LEV 7:20–7:21](https://ref.ly/Lev7:20-Lev7:21); [NUM 15:30](https://ref.ly/Num15:30)

Banishment by God
-----------------

### Adam and Eve were banished from Eden because of their disobedience

**[GEN 3:22–3:24](https://ref.ly/Gen3:22-Gen3:24)**

### God banished kings as a punishment

Nebuchadnezzar was banished from every human being because of his sin: [DAN 4:25](https://ref.ly/Dan4:25), [DAN 4:32–4:33](https://ref.ly/Dan4:32-Dan4:33); [EZK 28:16–28:17](https://ref.ly/Ezek28:16-Ezek28:17) *The king of Tyre was banished from God's presence because of his sin.*

### God punished the people of Judah by banishing them from their land

**[JER 23:39](https://ref.ly/Jer23:39)** *God also promised their return ([Jer 32:37](https://ref.ly/Jer32:37)).* *See also* [JER 8:3](https://ref.ly/Jer8:3), [JER 24:9](https://ref.ly/Jer24:9), [JER 25:10](https://ref.ly/Jer25:10), [JER 27:10](https://ref.ly/Jer27:10)

### At the final judgment, God will banish all evildoers

**[MAT 7:23](https://ref.ly/Matt7:23)** *See also* [MAT 8:12](https://ref.ly/Matt8:12), [MAT 22:13](https://ref.ly/Matt22:13), [MAT 25:46](https://ref.ly/Matt25:46)

Kings used banishment as a punishment
-------------------------------------

Saul expelled all mediums and spiritists from the land: [1SA 28:3](https://ref.ly/1Sam28:3), [1SA 28:9](https://ref.ly/1Sam28:9); [1KI 15:12](https://ref.ly/1Kgs15:12) *Asa banished all the shrine\-prostitutes from the land.*

The psalmists recognised banishment as the severest of punishments
------------------------------------------------------------------

[PSA 5:10](https://ref.ly/Ps5:10), [PSA 31:22](https://ref.ly/Ps31:22), [PSA 37:22](https://ref.ly/Ps37:22), [PSA 37:28](https://ref.ly/Ps37:28), [PSA 37:34](https://ref.ly/Ps37:34), [PSA 37:38](https://ref.ly/Ps37:38), [PSA 51:11](https://ref.ly/Ps51:11), [PSA 71:9](https://ref.ly/Ps71:9), [PSA 125:5](https://ref.ly/Ps125:5); ; [JON 2:4](https://ref.ly/Jonah2:4) *This portion of the book of Jonah is written in the same manner as the Psalms, i.e., as Hebrew poetry.*

The church is to use banishment as a means of discipline
--------------------------------------------------------
