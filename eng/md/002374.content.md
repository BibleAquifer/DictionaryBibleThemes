## 8744: faithlessness, as disobedience (id: 8744)

A lack of faith and obedience to God, which is seen especially in the lives of unbelievers. However, Scripture makes clear that believers can occasionally lapse into faithlessness towards God.

Faithlessness among the ungodly
-------------------------------

### In their character and lifestyle

**[PSA 101:3–101:5](https://ref.ly/Ps101:3-Ps101:5), [PSA 119:158](https://ref.ly/Ps119:158)** *See also* [PSA 10:4](https://ref.ly/Ps10:4), [PSA 14:1](https://ref.ly/Ps14:1); [JHN 16:8–16:9](https://ref.ly/John16:8-John16:9); [2TH 3:2](https://ref.ly/2Thess3:2); [TIT 1:15–1:16](https://ref.ly/Titus1:15-Titus1:16)

### Examples of faithless men

[1SA 25:2–25:3](https://ref.ly/1Sam25:2-1Sam25:3) *Nabal*; ; [EST 3:5–3:6](https://ref.ly/Esth3:5-Esth3:6) *Haman*; ; [MAT 2:16](https://ref.ly/Matt2:16) *Herod the Great*; ; [ACT 19:23–19:27](https://ref.ly/Acts19:23-Acts19:27) *Demetrius*

### The faithless appear to prosper

**[JER 12:1](https://ref.ly/Jer12:1)** *See also* [JOB 21:7](https://ref.ly/Job21:7); [PSA 73:3–73:5](https://ref.ly/Ps73:3-Ps73:5), [PSA 73:12](https://ref.ly/Ps73:12); [MAL 3:15](https://ref.ly/Mal3:15)

### Faithlessness results in judgment

**[PRO 14:14](https://ref.ly/Prov14:14)**; **[JHN 3:36](https://ref.ly/John3:36)** *See also* [PSA 73:17–73:18](https://ref.ly/Ps73:17-Ps73:18), [PSA 73:27](https://ref.ly/Ps73:27); [MAL 4:1](https://ref.ly/Mal4:1); [MRK 16:16](https://ref.ly/Mark16:16); [JHN 8:24](https://ref.ly/John8:24); [2TH 2:12](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:12); [REV 21:8](https://ref.ly/Rev21:8)

Faithlessness among the Jews
----------------------------

### Their failure to trust God for deliverance

**[ISA 31:1](https://ref.ly/Isa31:1)** *See also* [ISA 22:8–22:11](https://ref.ly/Isa22:8-Isa22:11), [ISA 30:1–30:2](https://ref.ly/Isa30:1-Isa30:2), [ISA 30:12–30:13](https://ref.ly/Isa30:12-Isa30:13)

### Their rejection of the gospel

**[JHN 12:37–12:38](https://ref.ly/John12:37-John12:38)** *See also* [ISA 53:1](https://ref.ly/Isa53:1); [JHN 3:11–3:12](https://ref.ly/John3:11-John3:12), [JHN 5:37–5:40](https://ref.ly/John5:37-John5:40), [JHN 8:45](https://ref.ly/John8:45), [JHN 10:25–10:26](https://ref.ly/John10:25-John10:26)

### Specific examples

[MAT 13:58](https://ref.ly/Matt13:58) parallel [MRK 6:5–6:6](https://ref.ly/Mark6:5-Mark6:6) *the people of Nazareth*; ; [MAT 19:21–19:22](https://ref.ly/Matt19:21-Matt19:22) parallels [MRK 10:21–10:22](https://ref.ly/Mark10:21-Mark10:22), [LUK 18:22–18:23](https://ref.ly/Luke18:22-Luke18:23) *the rich young ruler*; ; [ACT 28:24](https://ref.ly/Acts28:24) *Many Jews reject Paul's ministry in Rome*; ; [1TI 1:13](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:13) *Paul's own persecution of the church arose from unbelief in Jesus Christ.*

### Israel's estrangement from God

**[MAT 21:43](https://ref.ly/Matt21:43)** *See also* [PSA 118:22](https://ref.ly/Ps118:22); [MAT 21:41](https://ref.ly/Matt21:41) parallel [MRK 12:9](https://ref.ly/Mark12:9); [ROM 9:32–9:33](https://ref.ly/Rom9:32-Rom9:33)

### The acceptance of the Gentiles

[ROM 11:17–11:21](https://ref.ly/Rom11:17-Rom11:21) *The Jews' rejection of the gospel opens the way for Gentiles to be incorporated into the people of God, represented here as branches grafted onto a cultivated olive tree.*

### Israel's faithlessness is partial and temporary

[ROM 11:5](https://ref.ly/Rom11:5) *Though the people as a whole have rejected Jesus Christ, a faithful remnant remains*; ; [ROM 11:11–11:12](https://ref.ly/Rom11:11-Rom11:12), [ROM 11:23–11:27](https://ref.ly/Rom11:23-Rom11:27) *God's purpose for the faithful of Israel is their ultimate salvation.*

Faithlessness among believers
-----------------------------

### Lack of faith is rebuked

**[MAT 14:30–14:31](https://ref.ly/Matt14:30-Matt14:31)** *See also* [MAT 6:30–6:32](https://ref.ly/Matt6:30-Matt6:32) parallel [LUK 12:28–12:30](https://ref.ly/Luke12:28-Luke12:30) *worrying about food and clothing*; [MAT 8:24–8:26](https://ref.ly/Matt8:24-Matt8:26) parallels [MRK 4:38–4:40](https://ref.ly/Mark4:38-Mark4:40), [LUK 8:23–8:25](https://ref.ly/Luke8:23-Luke8:25) *Fear during a storm at sea*; [MAT 17:17–17:20](https://ref.ly/Matt17:17-Matt17:20) parallels [MRK 9:17–9:19](https://ref.ly/Mark9:17-Mark9:19), [LUK 9:40–9:42](https://ref.ly/Luke9:40-Luke9:42) *inability to cast out a demon*

### Causes of faithlessness

[MAT 16:8–16:10](https://ref.ly/Matt16:8-Matt16:10) *failure to learn from the lessons of the past*; ; [LUK 24:25](https://ref.ly/Luke24:25) *failure to accept teaching that has been given*
