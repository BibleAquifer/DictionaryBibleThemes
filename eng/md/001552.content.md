## 6199: imperfection (id: 6199)

The state of incompletion or decay that characterises the fallen world. Although God created the world perfect, it lapsed into its present state of imperfection through human sin.

* **Associated Articles:** 6200: imperfection, influence of (ID: `6200@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 6201: imperfection, and God's purposes (ID: `6201@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
