## 8225: devotion (id: 8225)

Wholehearted commitment to God, to another person or to a task. God's people are encouraged to show such commitment.

God's people are to be wholehearted in their devotion to him

**[DEU 6:5](https://ref.ly/Deut6:5)**; **[ROM 12:1](https://ref.ly/Rom12:1)** *See also* [DEU 30:1–30:3](https://ref.ly/Deut30:1-Deut30:3); [JDG 5:2](https://ref.ly/Judg5:2); [PSA 119:2](https://ref.ly/Ps119:2); [JER 29:13](https://ref.ly/Jer29:13)
