## 6221: rebellion (id: 6221)

A state of revolt against established divine or human authority. Scripture condemns rebellion against God and indicates the futility of Israel's frequent rebellions against him.

* **Associated Articles:** 6222: rebellion, against God (ID: `6222@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 6223: rebellion, of Israel (ID: `6223@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 6224: rebellion, against human authority (ID: `6224@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
