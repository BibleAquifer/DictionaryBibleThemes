## 5477: property, land (id: 5477)

All land belongs to God, and he allows people to care for it and use it for their own needs and the needs of others. The land promised to Israel is a special case illustrating the spiritual inheritance of all believers and God's ultimate purpose for the world.

All land belongs to God
-----------------------

### It is in his possession and care

**[PSA 24:1](https://ref.ly/Ps24:1)** *See also* [EXO 9:29](https://ref.ly/Exod9:29); [PSA 33:5](https://ref.ly/Ps33:5); [1CO 10:26](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:26)

### It is given to human beings to manage

**[GEN 2:15](https://ref.ly/Gen2:15)** *All exploitation of land is therefore contrary to God's purpose.* *See also* [PSA 8:6](https://ref.ly/Ps8:6), [PSA 115:16](https://ref.ly/Ps115:16)
