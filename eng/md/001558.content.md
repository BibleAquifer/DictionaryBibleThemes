## 6212: participation (id: 6212)

Sharing in actions or identifying with actions or people. All human beings share in Adam's fall and its consequences; believers share in Christ and his life.

* **Associated Articles:** 6213: participation, in sin (ID: `6213@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 6214: participation, in Christ (ID: `6214@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
