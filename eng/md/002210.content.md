## 8420: equipping (id: 8420)

The providing of all necessary facilities for achieving a task. Scripture suggests that God does not provide tasks for believers without also providing the gifts which are needed to equip believers for those tasks.

* **Associated Articles:** 8421: equipping, physical (ID: `8421@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 8422: equipping, spiritual (ID: `8422@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
