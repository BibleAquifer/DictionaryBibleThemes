## 8314: orthodoxy (id: 8314)

The holding of religious beliefs that are in harmony with the accepted views of the community of faith. Right belief (orthodoxy) is to show itself in right practice (orthopraxis).

* **Associated Articles:** 8315: orthodoxy, in OT (ID: `8315@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 8316: orthodoxy, in NT (ID: `8316@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
