## 7520: dispersion, the (id: 7520)

A technical term used to refer to God's people as they are scattered throughout the world.

The dispersion of the Jews
--------------------------

### Promised and predicted

**[DEU 4:25–4:28](https://ref.ly/Deut4:25-Deut4:28)**; **[1KI 14:15](https://ref.ly/1Kgs14:15)** *See also* [DEU 28:15–28:68](https://ref.ly/Deut28:15-Deut28:68); [LEV 26:27–26:35](https://ref.ly/Lev26:27-Lev26:35); [NEH 1:8–1:9](https://ref.ly/Neh1:8-Neh1:9); [JER 13:24](https://ref.ly/Jer13:24); [EZK 6:8–6:10](https://ref.ly/Ezek6:8-Ezek6:10), [EZK 20:23–20:24](https://ref.ly/Ezek20:23-Ezek20:24), [EZK 22:15](https://ref.ly/Ezek22:15)

### The prediction fulfilled

**[JER 50:17](https://ref.ly/Jer50:17)**; **[DAN 9:7](https://ref.ly/Dan9:7)** *See also* [EST 3:8](https://ref.ly/Esth3:8); [PSA 44:9–44:16](https://ref.ly/Ps44:9-Ps44:16); [LAM 4:16](https://ref.ly/Lam4:16); [EZK 36:17–36:20](https://ref.ly/Ezek36:17-Ezek36:20); [ZEP 3:10](https://ref.ly/Zeph3:10); [ZEC 1:19](https://ref.ly/Zech1:19); [JHN 7:35](https://ref.ly/John7:35) *In NT times there were more Jews living outside Palestine than within.*

### The promise of return

**[ISA 11:12](https://ref.ly/Isa11:12)** *See also* [DEU 4:29–4:31](https://ref.ly/Deut4:29-Deut4:31); [JER 31:10](https://ref.ly/Jer31:10); [EZK 11:16–11:17](https://ref.ly/Ezek11:16-Ezek11:17)
