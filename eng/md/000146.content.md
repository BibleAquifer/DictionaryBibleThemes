## 2018: Jesus Christ, divinity of (id: 2018)

The equality and identity of Jesus Christ as God is clearly stated in the NT, and is also implied by the words and deeds of Jesus Christ. The OT prophecies also point to the divinity of the coming Messiah.

Jesus Christ's names and titles point to his divinity
-----------------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ as judge

**[JHN 5:27](https://ref.ly/John5:27)** *In the OT, final responsibility for judgment is assigned to God.* *See also* [MAT 25:31–25:33](https://ref.ly/Matt25:31-Matt25:33); [MRK 8:38](https://ref.ly/Mark8:38) parallels [MAT 16:27](https://ref.ly/Matt16:27), [LUK 9:26](https://ref.ly/Luke9:26); [ACT 17:31](https://ref.ly/Acts17:31); [ROM 2:16](https://ref.ly/Rom2:16); [2CO 5:10](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:10)

### Jesus Christ as \`\`I am''

**[JHN 11:25](https://ref.ly/John11:25)** *“I am” is the meaning of God's name in the OT (see [Ex 3:14](https://ref.ly/Exod3:14)).* *See also* [JHN 6:35](https://ref.ly/John6:35), [JHN 8:12](https://ref.ly/John8:12), [JHN 10:7](https://ref.ly/John10:7), [JHN 10:11](https://ref.ly/John10:11), [JHN 14:6](https://ref.ly/John14:6), [JHN 15:1](https://ref.ly/John15:1), [JHN 18:5–18:6](https://ref.ly/John18:5-John18:6)

**[ROM 10:9](https://ref.ly/Rom10:9)** *“Lord” was equivalent to God's name in the OT.* *See also* [LUK 1:43](https://ref.ly/Luke1:43), [LUK 2:11](https://ref.ly/Luke2:11); [JHN 13:13](https://ref.ly/John13:13); [1CO 12:3](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:3); [2CO 4:5](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:5); [REV 19:16](https://ref.ly/Rev19:16)
