## 7230: genealogies (id: 7230)

In Scripture, lists of the descendants or forebears of an individual, or a record of the names of particular groups of people.

Genealogies as records of ancestors and lines of descent
--------------------------------------------------------

**[MAT 1:1–1:17](https://ref.ly/Matt1:1-Matt1:17)** parallel [LUK 3:23–3:38](https://ref.ly/Luke3:23-Luke3:38) *See also* [GEN 10:1–10:32](https://ref.ly/Gen10:1-Gen10:32), [GEN 11:10–11:32](https://ref.ly/Gen11:10-Gen11:32), [GEN 36:10–36:43](https://ref.ly/Gen36:10-Gen36:43); [1CH 1:1–6:30](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:1-1Chr6:30), [1CH 9:35–9:44](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:35-1Chr9:44); [HEB 7:3](https://ref.ly/Heb7:3)
