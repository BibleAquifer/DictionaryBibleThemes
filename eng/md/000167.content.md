## 2081: Jesus Christ, wisdom of (id: 2081)

Jesus Christ's deep understanding of God, people and situations derived from his relationship with God his Father and from knowledge of his word. Christ's wisdom is seen in his words, actions and dealings with people.

The wisdom of Messiah was foretold and recognised in Jesus Christ
