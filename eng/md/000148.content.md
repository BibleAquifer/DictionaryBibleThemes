## 2024: Jesus Christ, glory of (id: 2024)

Jesus Christ's radiance and splendour reflects the glory of the Father, and is both revealed to and reflected in the lives of believers.

Jesus Christ's glory existed before the incarnation
---------------------------------------------------

**[JHN 17:5](https://ref.ly/John17:5)** *See also* [JHN 1:14](https://ref.ly/John1:14), [JHN 17:24](https://ref.ly/John17:24)

Jesus Christ's glory is God's glory
-----------------------------------

### God's glory is reflected in Jesus Christ

**[HEB 1:3](https://ref.ly/Heb1:3)** *See also* [JHN 12:41](https://ref.ly/John12:41) *John makes no distinction between God's glory revealed in [Isa 6:3](https://ref.ly/Isa6:3) and the glory of Jesus Christ*; [JHN 13:32](https://ref.ly/John13:32); [2CO 4:4](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:4), [2CO 4:6](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:6)

### Jesus Christ brings glory to his Father

**[JHN 14:13](https://ref.ly/John14:13)** *See also* [JHN 13:31](https://ref.ly/John13:31), [JHN 17:1](https://ref.ly/John17:1), [JHN 17:4](https://ref.ly/John17:4); [ROM 16:27](https://ref.ly/Rom16:27); [EPH 1:12](https://ref.ly/Eph1:12); [JUD 1:25](https://ref.ly/Jude1:25)

### The Holy Spirit glorifies Jesus Christ

**[JHN 16:14](https://ref.ly/John16:14)**

Jesus Christ's glory is revealed on earth
-----------------------------------------

### Through his miracles

**[JHN 2:11](https://ref.ly/John2:11)** *See also* [JHN 11:4](https://ref.ly/John11:4), [JHN 11:40](https://ref.ly/John11:40)

### To his disciples

**[LUK 9:28–9:32](https://ref.ly/Luke9:28-Luke9:32)** parallels [MAT 17:1–17:2](https://ref.ly/Matt17:1-Matt17:2), [MRK 9:2–9:3](https://ref.ly/Mark9:2-Mark9:3) *See also* [JHN 1:14](https://ref.ly/John1:14); [ACT 9:3](https://ref.ly/Acts9:3), [ACT 22:6](https://ref.ly/Acts22:6), [ACT 26:13](https://ref.ly/Acts26:13); [2PE 1:17](https://ref.ly/2Pet1:17)

### In his death and resurrection

**[JHN 12:23](https://ref.ly/John12:23)** *One of the major themes of John's Gospel is that Jesus Christ is glorified through his death and resurrection.* *See also* [JHN 7:39](https://ref.ly/John7:39), [JHN 12:16](https://ref.ly/John12:16); [ACT 3:13–3:15](https://ref.ly/Acts3:13-Acts3:15); [1PE 1:21](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:21); [HEB 2:9](https://ref.ly/Heb2:9)
