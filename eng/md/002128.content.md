## 8296: love, nature of (id: 8296)

Scripture offers an understanding of the source, character and value of love, based on the nature and actions of God.

God is the source of love
-------------------------

### His very character is love

**[1JN 4:7–4:8](https://ref.ly/1John4:7-1John4:8)** *See also* [1TH 3:12](https://ref.ly/1Thess3:12); [2TI 1:7](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:7); [1JN 4:16](https://ref.ly/1John4:16), [1JN 4:19](https://ref.ly/1John4:19)

**[1JN 3:16](https://ref.ly/1John3:16), [1JN 4:10](https://ref.ly/1John4:10)** *See also* [JHN 15:13](https://ref.ly/John15:13); [EPH 5:2](https://ref.ly/Eph5:2), [EPH 5:25](https://ref.ly/Eph5:25)

**[GAL 5:22](https://ref.ly/Gal5:22)** *See also* [ROM 5:5](https://ref.ly/Rom5:5), [ROM 15:30](https://ref.ly/Rom15:30); [COL 1:8](https://ref.ly/Col1:8)
