## 5040: murder (id: 5040)

The unlawful killing of individual human beings, forbidden by God and carrying the threat of severe punishment.

Murder forbidden by the law
---------------------------

**[EXO 20:13](https://ref.ly/Exod20:13)** parallel [DEU 5:17](https://ref.ly/Deut5:17) *See also* [MAT 5:21](https://ref.ly/Matt5:21), [MAT 19:18](https://ref.ly/Matt19:18) parallels [MRK 10:19](https://ref.ly/Mark10:19), [LUK 18:20](https://ref.ly/Luke18:20); [ROM 13:9](https://ref.ly/Rom13:9); [1TI 1:9](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:9); [JAS 2:11–2:12](https://ref.ly/Jas2:11-Jas2:12)
