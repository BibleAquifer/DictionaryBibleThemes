## 9136: immortality, in OT (id: 9136)

The OT stresses that God alone is immortal and indicates that this gift may be granted to human beings under certain conditions.

Immortality is an attribute of God
----------------------------------

### God is everlasting in himself

**[PSA 90:2](https://ref.ly/Ps90:2)** *See also* [NEH 9:5](https://ref.ly/Neh9:5); [PSA 41:13](https://ref.ly/Ps41:13); [ISA 9:6](https://ref.ly/Isa9:6), [ISA 40:28](https://ref.ly/Isa40:28); [HAB 1:12](https://ref.ly/Hab1:12)

### God is everlasting in his character and actions

**[DEU 33:27](https://ref.ly/Deut33:27)** *The way in which Scripture emphasises the character of God in these contexts shows that immortality is about a quality of being as well as about infinite duration.* [GEN 9:16](https://ref.ly/Gen9:16), [GEN 17:7](https://ref.ly/Gen17:7), [GEN 17:13](https://ref.ly/Gen17:13), [GEN 17:19](https://ref.ly/Gen17:19); ; [1CH 16:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:17) parallel [PSA 105:10](https://ref.ly/Ps105:10); [PSA 9:7](https://ref.ly/Ps9:7), [PSA 10:16](https://ref.ly/Ps10:16), [PSA 132:12](https://ref.ly/Ps132:12); ; [DAN 7:14](https://ref.ly/Dan7:14), [DAN 7:27](https://ref.ly/Dan7:27); [PSA 52:8](https://ref.ly/Ps52:8), [PSA 103:17](https://ref.ly/Ps103:17); ; [ISA 54:10](https://ref.ly/Isa54:10); ; [JER 31:3](https://ref.ly/Jer31:3); [PSA 119:142](https://ref.ly/Ps119:142); ; [DAN 9:24](https://ref.ly/Dan9:24); [ISA 45:17](https://ref.ly/Isa45:17) *God's everlasting salvation*; ; [ISA 54:8](https://ref.ly/Isa54:8) *God's everlasting kindness*; ; [ISA 60:19–60:20](https://ref.ly/Isa60:19-Isa60:20) *the LORD as the everlasting light*

Immortality is conditionally offered to human beings
----------------------------------------------------

**[GEN 3:22–3:23](https://ref.ly/Gen3:22-Gen3:23)** *See also* [PSA 6:5](https://ref.ly/Ps6:5), [PSA 88:5](https://ref.ly/Ps88:5), [PSA 88:10](https://ref.ly/Ps88:10), [PSA 115:17](https://ref.ly/Ps115:17); [DAN 12:2](https://ref.ly/Dan12:2) *The most specific OT reference to immortality. Note that while resurrection to judgment is for all, immortality is only for the righteous ([Pr 12:28](https://ref.ly/Prov12:28)).*
