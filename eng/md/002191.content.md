## 8369: worthiness (id: 8369)

Only God truly merits honour and praise but he graciously considers those who humbly serve him with wholehearted devotion to be worthy disciples.

God the Father is worthy of honour and praise
---------------------------------------------

**[PSA 145:3](https://ref.ly/Ps145:3)** *See also* [2SA 22:4](https://ref.ly/2Sam22:4) parallel [PSA 18:3](https://ref.ly/Ps18:3); [1CH 16:25](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:25) parallel [PSA 96:4](https://ref.ly/Ps96:4); [PSA 48:1](https://ref.ly/Ps48:1); [REV 4:11](https://ref.ly/Rev4:11)

Jesus Christ is worthy of honour and praise
-------------------------------------------

**[REV 5:12](https://ref.ly/Rev5:12)** *See also* [MAT 3:17](https://ref.ly/Matt3:17) parallels [MRK 1:11](https://ref.ly/Mark1:11), [LUK 3:22](https://ref.ly/Luke3:22); [MAT 17:5](https://ref.ly/Matt17:5) parallels [MRK 9:7](https://ref.ly/Mark9:7), [LUK 9:35](https://ref.ly/Luke9:35); [PHP 2:9–2:11](https://ref.ly/Phil2:9-Phil2:11); [HEB 2:9](https://ref.ly/Heb2:9), [HEB 3:3](https://ref.ly/Heb3:3); [2PE 1:17](https://ref.ly/2Pet1:17); [REV 5:9](https://ref.ly/Rev5:9)

People are considered worthy by others
--------------------------------------

**[LUK 7:4–7:5](https://ref.ly/Luke7:4-Luke7:5)** *Note that the centurion did not consider himself to be worthy ([Lk 7:7](https://ref.ly/Luke7:7)).* *See also* [GEN 39:4](https://ref.ly/Gen39:4), [GEN 41:38–41:40](https://ref.ly/Gen41:38-Gen41:40); [1SA 16:6](https://ref.ly/1Sam16:6); [1KI 1:42](https://ref.ly/1Kgs1:42); [DAN 1:18–1:20](https://ref.ly/Dan1:18-Dan1:20), [DAN 2:46–2:48](https://ref.ly/Dan2:46-Dan2:48), [DAN 3:28–3:30](https://ref.ly/Dan3:28-Dan3:30); [ACT 14:11–14:13](https://ref.ly/Acts14:11-Acts14:13)

God considers no person (other than Jesus Christ) to be worthy by nature
------------------------------------------------------------------------

**[REV 5:4](https://ref.ly/Rev5:4)** *See also* [PSA 14:3](https://ref.ly/Ps14:3), [PSA 143:2](https://ref.ly/Ps143:2); [ECC 7:20](https://ref.ly/Eccl7:20); [ROM 3:10–3:12](https://ref.ly/Rom3:10-Rom3:12), [ROM 3:23](https://ref.ly/Rom3:23)
