## 5634: work, and the fall (id: 5634)

Human work, which was once a pleasure, became a burden only on account of human disobedience.

Work is under God's judgment
----------------------------

**[GEN 3:17–3:19](https://ref.ly/Gen3:17-Gen3:19)** *See also* [GEN 5:29](https://ref.ly/Gen5:29)

Work is often frustrating
-------------------------

**[ECC 2:22–2:23](https://ref.ly/Eccl2:22-Eccl2:23)** *See also* [ECC 2:11](https://ref.ly/Eccl2:11), [ECC 2:18](https://ref.ly/Eccl2:18), [ECC 4:8](https://ref.ly/Eccl4:8), [ECC 5:16–5:17](https://ref.ly/Eccl5:16-Eccl5:17)
