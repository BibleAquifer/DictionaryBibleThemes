## 6661: freedom, and the law (id: 6661)

Jesus Christ sets his people free from the condemnation and oppression of the law. Through the Holy Spirit, believers are able to fulfil the law obediently according to God's intentions.

Jesus Christ sets believers free from the law
---------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ's life of perfect obedience to the law was the fulfilment of it

**[MAT 5:17](https://ref.ly/Matt5:17)** *See also* [GAL 4:4–4:5](https://ref.ly/Gal4:4-Gal4:5); [1PE 2:22](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:22)

### Jesus Christ's death fulfils the demands of the law

**[2CO 5:21](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:21)** *See also* [HEB 7:27](https://ref.ly/Heb7:27), [HEB 9:11](https://ref.ly/Heb9:11), [HEB 9:26–9:28](https://ref.ly/Heb9:26-Heb9:28), [HEB 10:11–10:14](https://ref.ly/Heb10:11-Heb10:14); [1PE 2:24](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:24), [1PE 3:18](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:18)

### On account of Jesus Christ's death, believers can be righteous independently of the law

**[ROM 3:21–3:26](https://ref.ly/Rom3:21-Rom3:26)** *See also* [JHN 1:17](https://ref.ly/John1:17); [ROM 10:4](https://ref.ly/Rom10:4); [PHP 3:9](https://ref.ly/Phil3:9)
