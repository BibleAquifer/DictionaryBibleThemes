## 7476: thank-offering (id: 7476)

Together with vows and freewill offerings this was one of the three kinds of fellowship offering.

Presenting a thank\-offering
----------------------------

### An animal sacrifice

**[LEV 7:15](https://ref.ly/Lev7:15)** *See also* [LEV 22:29](https://ref.ly/Lev22:29)

### Accompanied by bread, wafers and cakes

[LEV 7:12–7:13](https://ref.ly/Lev7:12-Lev7:13) *Cakes made with yeast can be offered, since these are not to be burned on the altar.*

### The priest shares the meal

[LEV 7:14](https://ref.ly/Lev7:14), [LEV 22:29–22:30](https://ref.ly/Lev22:29-Lev22:30)

The occasions for presenting thank\-offerings
---------------------------------------------

### For deliverance from distress, death or sickness

**[PSA 50:23](https://ref.ly/Ps50:23), [PSA 107:21–107:22](https://ref.ly/Ps107:21-Ps107:22)** *See also* [PSA 56:12–56:13](https://ref.ly/Ps56:12-Ps56:13) *deliverance from death*; [PSA 116:17](https://ref.ly/Ps116:17) *Deliverance from sickness; the earlier verses show the psalmist has been ill.*

### Following a vow

**[PSA 7:17](https://ref.ly/Ps7:17)** *A vow to praise God in anticipation of an answer to prayer.* *See also* [PSA 66:13–66:14](https://ref.ly/Ps66:13-Ps66:14)

### At times of religious renewal

[2CH 29:31](https://ref.ly/2Chr29:31) *Hezekiah's reformation*; ; [2CH 33:15–33:16](https://ref.ly/2Chr33:15-2Chr33:16) *Manasseh's repentance*; [JER 17:26](https://ref.ly/Jer17:26), [JER 33:11](https://ref.ly/Jer33:11)
