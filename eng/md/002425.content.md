## 8803: pride, evil of (id: 8803)

Pride is viewed as a great evil because it involves pretending to a greatness and glory that belong rightly to God alone.

The sinfulness of pride
-----------------------

### It is condemned as evil

**[1SA 15:23](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:23)**; **[PRO 21:4](https://ref.ly/Prov21:4)**; **[JAS 4:16](https://ref.ly/Jas4:16)** *See also* [MRK 7:22–7:23](https://ref.ly/Mark7:22-Mark7:23); [ROM 1:29–1:30](https://ref.ly/Rom1:29-Rom1:30); [2CO 12:20](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:20); [2TI 3:1–3:2](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:1-2Tim3:2); [1JN 2:16](https://ref.ly/1John2:16)
