## 5288: dead, the (id: 5288)

Human beings who have died. Scripture provides illustrations of the manner in which the dead are to be buried, as well as explaining the doctrines of resurrection and judgment.

Preparation of the dead for burial
----------------------------------

### Washing

**[ACT 9:37](https://ref.ly/Acts9:37)** *A custom common to both Jews and Greeks.*

### Embalming

**[MAT 26:12](https://ref.ly/Matt26:12)** *See also* [GEN 50:2](https://ref.ly/Gen50:2), [GEN 50:26](https://ref.ly/Gen50:26); [2CH 16:14](https://ref.ly/2Chr16:14)

### Grave clothes

**[JHN 11:44](https://ref.ly/John11:44)** *See also* [MRK 15:46](https://ref.ly/Mark15:46) parallels [MAT 27:59](https://ref.ly/Matt27:59), [JHN 19:40](https://ref.ly/John19:40); [JHN 20:7](https://ref.ly/John20:7); [ACT 5:6](https://ref.ly/Acts5:6)

Provision for the dead
----------------------

### Burial

**[GEN 49:29–49:30](https://ref.ly/Gen49:29-Gen49:30)** *It was the normal practice for the people of God to be buried.* *See also* [JOS 24:30](https://ref.ly/Josh24:30); [RUT 1:17](https://ref.ly/Ruth1:17); [1KI 11:15](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:15); [ACT 8:2](https://ref.ly/Acts8:2)

### Cremation

**[JOS 7:25](https://ref.ly/Josh7:25)** *Cremation was not a Hebrew practice, except in cases of difficulty.* *See also* [1SA 31:12](https://ref.ly/1Sam31:12); [AMO 6:10](https://ref.ly/Amos6:10)

The plight of the unburied dead
-------------------------------

**[PSA 79:2](https://ref.ly/Ps79:2)** *To be unburied was a disgrace, a mark of judgment.* *See also* [ECC 6:3](https://ref.ly/Eccl6:3); [ISA 14:19](https://ref.ly/Isa14:19); [JER 7:33](https://ref.ly/Jer7:33), [JER 16:6](https://ref.ly/Jer16:6)

Personal contact with a dead body conferred uncleanness
-------------------------------------------------------

**[NUM 19:11](https://ref.ly/Num19:11)** *See also* [LEV 21:11](https://ref.ly/Lev21:11); [NUM 6:6](https://ref.ly/Num6:6)

The place of the dead
---------------------

### Sheol and Hades

**[EZK 31:16](https://ref.ly/Ezek31:16)** *Sheol is the OT term for the place of the dead; a grave, an underworld, a pit. Hades is the NT equivalent.* *See also* [2KI 21:26](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:26); [PSA 88:3–88:4](https://ref.ly/Ps88:3-Ps88:4), [PSA 89:48](https://ref.ly/Ps89:48), [PSA 116:3](https://ref.ly/Ps116:3); [ECC 9:10](https://ref.ly/Eccl9:10); [ISA 14:9–14:10](https://ref.ly/Isa14:9-Isa14:10); [ACT 2:31](https://ref.ly/Acts2:31)

### A place of judgment

**[PSA 49:13–49:14](https://ref.ly/Ps49:13-Ps49:14)** *See also* [PSA 31:17](https://ref.ly/Ps31:17), [PSA 88:3–88:5](https://ref.ly/Ps88:3-Ps88:5); [NAM 1:14](https://ref.ly/Nah1:14); [MAT 5:22](https://ref.ly/Matt5:22), [MAT 11:23](https://ref.ly/Matt11:23) parallel [LUK 10:15](https://ref.ly/Luke10:15); [MAT 16:18](https://ref.ly/Matt16:18); [REV 1:18](https://ref.ly/Rev1:18), [REV 20:13–20:14](https://ref.ly/Rev20:13-Rev20:14)

### A place of deliverance

**[PSA 139:8](https://ref.ly/Ps139:8)** *See also* [PSA 16:10](https://ref.ly/Ps16:10), [PSA 30:3](https://ref.ly/Ps30:3), [PSA 86:13](https://ref.ly/Ps86:13), [PSA 107:20](https://ref.ly/Ps107:20); [HOS 13:14](https://ref.ly/Hos13:14); [JON 2:2](https://ref.ly/Jonah2:2); [ACT 2:27](https://ref.ly/Acts2:27)

The plight of the dead outside of Christ
----------------------------------------

### Physical death is the result of sin

**[ROM 6:23](https://ref.ly/Rom6:23)** *See also* [GEN 2:17](https://ref.ly/Gen2:17); [EZK 18:20](https://ref.ly/Ezek18:20); [ROM 1:32](https://ref.ly/Rom1:32), [ROM 5:12](https://ref.ly/Rom5:12), [ROM 8:6](https://ref.ly/Rom8:6); [JAS 1:15](https://ref.ly/Jas1:15)

### Final judgment will follow resurrection

**[REV 21:8](https://ref.ly/Rev21:8)** *The “second death” is the throwing of the lost into the lake of fire.* *See also* [MAT 10:28](https://ref.ly/Matt10:28), [MAT 25:41](https://ref.ly/Matt25:41); [1JN 5:16–5:17](https://ref.ly/1John5:16-1John5:17); [JUD 1:12](https://ref.ly/Jude1:12)
