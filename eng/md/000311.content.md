## 4030: world, human behaviour in (id: 4030)

Human behaviour in the world is now characterised by self\-centredness as a result of sin. Believers are called upon to renounce such sinful behaviour.

Human passion in the world is expressed in various ways
-------------------------------------------------------

### In physical desires
