## 9414: heaven, as community of the redeemed (id: 9414)

The community of the redeemed in heaven will represent all peoples and languages. They will owe this solely to the sacrifice of Jesus Christ. They will share in the divine life in perfect fellowship with God, free for ever from suffering and death.

Heaven as a divine gift
-----------------------

**[ROM 6:23](https://ref.ly/Rom6:23)** *See also* [LUK 12:32](https://ref.ly/Luke12:32), [LUK 22:28–22:30](https://ref.ly/Luke22:28-Luke22:30); [JHN 17:2](https://ref.ly/John17:2)

Divine preparations made for believers in heaven
------------------------------------------------

**[JHN 14:2](https://ref.ly/John14:2)** *See also* [2CO 5:1](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:1); [HEB 11:16](https://ref.ly/Heb11:16)

The redeemed in heaven come from all peoples
--------------------------------------------

**[REV 7:9–7:10](https://ref.ly/Rev7:9-Rev7:10)** *See also* [ISA 59:19](https://ref.ly/Isa59:19); [MAL 1:11](https://ref.ly/Mal1:11); [MAT 8:11](https://ref.ly/Matt8:11) parallel [LUK 13:29](https://ref.ly/Luke13:29); [REV 5:9](https://ref.ly/Rev5:9)

The redeemed owe their place in heaven solely to Jesus Christ
-------------------------------------------------------------

**[REV 7:14](https://ref.ly/Rev7:14)** *Washing one's robes in the blood of the Lamb symbolises cleansing through the sacrifice of Jesus Christ.* *See also* [1PE 1:18–1:19](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:18-1Pet1:19); [REV 22:14](https://ref.ly/Rev22:14)

The redeemed are identified with Jesus Christ
---------------------------------------------

### Jesus Christ acknowledges the redeemed as his own

**[LUK 12:8](https://ref.ly/Luke12:8)** *See also* [MAT 7:21–7:23](https://ref.ly/Matt7:21-Matt7:23)

### Believers possess the family likeness to Jesus Christ

**[2PE 1:4](https://ref.ly/2Pet1:4)** *See also* [REV 2:7](https://ref.ly/Rev2:7), [REV 3:21](https://ref.ly/Rev3:21)

Believers on earth at the second coming will be taken up to heaven
------------------------------------------------------------------

**[REV 11:12](https://ref.ly/Rev11:12)** *See also* [JHN 11:26](https://ref.ly/John11:26); [1TH 4:16–4:17](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:16-1Thess4:17) *Believers living on earth when Jesus Christ returns will not experience death, but be taken straight to heaven.*
