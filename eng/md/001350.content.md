## 5829: defiance (id: 5829)

Open refusal to obey any authority. Defiance is seen in the Bible as evil when authority is legitimate but good when authority is ungodly.

Defiance of God is condemned
----------------------------

**[JER 50:29](https://ref.ly/Jer50:29)** *See also* [1KI 13:21–13:22](https://ref.ly/1Kgs13:21-1Kgs13:22), [1KI 13:26](https://ref.ly/1Kgs13:26); [PSA 139:19–139:22](https://ref.ly/Ps139:19-Ps139:22); [ISA 3:8](https://ref.ly/Isa3:8), [ISA 65:7](https://ref.ly/Isa65:7); [JER 48:26](https://ref.ly/Jer48:26), [JER 48:42](https://ref.ly/Jer48:42)
