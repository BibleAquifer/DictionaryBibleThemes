## 8809: riches, nature of (id: 8809)

God has created an abundance of good things. Those who have material wealth should understand its true nature and acknowledge God.

God and riches
--------------

### All riches belong to God the Creator

**[PSA 24:1–24:2](https://ref.ly/Ps24:1-Ps24:2)** *See also* [JOB 41:11](https://ref.ly/Job41:11); [PSA 50:12](https://ref.ly/Ps50:12); [HAG 2:8](https://ref.ly/Hag2:8)

### God gives of his riches

**[1SA 2:7](https://ref.ly/1Sam2:7)** *See also* [ECC 5:19](https://ref.ly/Eccl5:19); [1CH 29:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:12); [2CH 1:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr1:12)

### God gives the ability to produce wealth

**[DEU 8:17–8:18](https://ref.ly/Deut8:17-Deut8:18)** *See also* [GEN 26:12–26:13](https://ref.ly/Gen26:12-Gen26:13); [DEU 26:9–26:10](https://ref.ly/Deut26:9-Deut26:10)

### Riches may be a sign of God's blessing

**[PRO 10:22](https://ref.ly/Prov10:22)** *See also* [2CH 25:9](https://ref.ly/2Chr25:9); [JOB 1:9–1:10](https://ref.ly/Job1:9-Job1:10); [PSA 112:1–112:3](https://ref.ly/Ps112:1-Ps112:3), [PSA 128:1–128:2](https://ref.ly/Ps128:1-Ps128:2); [PRO 8:17–8:18](https://ref.ly/Prov8:17-Prov8:18)

### Riches should be thankfully received

**[1TI 4:4–4:5](https://ref.ly/1Tim4:4-1Tim4:5)** *See also* [MAT 14:19](https://ref.ly/Matt14:19); [ROM 1:21](https://ref.ly/Rom1:21); [COL 3:17](https://ref.ly/Col3:17)
