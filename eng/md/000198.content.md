## 2354: Jesus Christ, mission of (id: 2354)

The work that Jesus Christ was sent to do, including both his healing and preaching ministry, but particularly his work of salvation. Jesus Christ sends Christians to continue his work by proclaiming his message of salvation.

Jesus Christ was called to his mission from the beginning of time
-----------------------------------------------------------------

**[ISA 49:1](https://ref.ly/Isa49:1)** *See also* [MIC 5:2](https://ref.ly/Mic5:2); [1PE 1:20](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:20)

--------------------------------------

Aspects of Jesus Christ's mission
---------------------------------

### Preaching and teaching

**[LUK 4:43](https://ref.ly/Luke4:43)** parallel [MRK 1:38](https://ref.ly/Mark1:38) *See also* [MAT 4:17](https://ref.ly/Matt4:17), [MAT 5:2](https://ref.ly/Matt5:2); [MRK 1:21](https://ref.ly/Mark1:21); [JHN 8:2](https://ref.ly/John8:2), [JHN 18:37](https://ref.ly/John18:37)

### Sacrificial service

**[MAT 20:28](https://ref.ly/Matt20:28)** parallel [MRK 10:45](https://ref.ly/Mark10:45) *See also* [LUK 22:27](https://ref.ly/Luke22:27); [JHN 13:13–13:16](https://ref.ly/John13:13-John13:16); [PHP 2:7](https://ref.ly/Phil2:7)

### Healing

**[MAT 4:23](https://ref.ly/Matt4:23)** *A healing ministry was one of the signs of the expected Messiah.* *See also* [MAT 14:14](https://ref.ly/Matt14:14); [MRK 1:34](https://ref.ly/Mark1:34); [LUK 4:40](https://ref.ly/Luke4:40), [LUK 5:15](https://ref.ly/Luke5:15); [JHN 9:4–9:7](https://ref.ly/John9:4-John9:7)

### Judgment

**[JHN 9:39](https://ref.ly/John9:39)** *Jesus Christ does not come to condemn, but the response people make to him is the ultimate criterion of judgment. Those truly seeking God turn to faith in Jesus Christ; unbelievers, by their rejection of him, are confirmed in their unbelief.* *See also* [LUK 12:49](https://ref.ly/Luke12:49); [JHN 3:17–3:18](https://ref.ly/John3:17-John3:18), [JHN 5:22](https://ref.ly/John5:22), [JHN 8:10–8:11](https://ref.ly/John8:10-John8:11), [JHN 8:26](https://ref.ly/John8:26), [JHN 12:47–12:48](https://ref.ly/John12:47-John12:48)
