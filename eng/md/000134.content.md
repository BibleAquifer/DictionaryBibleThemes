## 1656: numbers, combinations (id: 1656)

Numbers may be combined for making a comparison. When they are combined in such a pattern as “two or three” the emphasis usually falls on the second figure.

-------------

**[DEU 19:15](https://ref.ly/Deut19:15)** *See also* [NUM 35:30](https://ref.ly/Num35:30); [DEU 17:6](https://ref.ly/Deut17:6); [MAT 18:16](https://ref.ly/Matt18:16); [2CO 13:1](https://ref.ly/2Cor13:1); [1TI 5:19](https://ref.ly/1Tim5:19); [HEB 10:28](https://ref.ly/Heb10:28)
