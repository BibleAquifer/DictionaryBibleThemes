## 3040: Holy Spirit, promise of (id: 3040)

The OT foretold and Jesus Christ reaffirmed that the Holy Spirit would be poured out on God's people, transforming, empowering and gifting them.

OT promises of the Holy Spirit fulfilled in the OT
--------------------------------------------------

### The promised Spirit enables prophecy

**[1SA 10:6](https://ref.ly/1Sam10:6)** *See also* [1SA 10:10](https://ref.ly/1Sam10:10)

### The Spirit fulfils the promised presence of the LORD

**[EXO 33:14](https://ref.ly/Exod33:14)** *See also* [ISA 63:11](https://ref.ly/Isa63:11), [ISA 63:14](https://ref.ly/Isa63:14); [HAG 2:5](https://ref.ly/Hag2:5)

OT promises of the Holy Spirit fulfilled in the NT
--------------------------------------------------

### The Spirit implements the promised new covenant

**[JER 31:33–31:34](https://ref.ly/Jer31:33-Jer31:34)** *See also* [ROM 8:2](https://ref.ly/Rom8:2), [ROM 8:9–8:10](https://ref.ly/Rom8:9-Rom8:10); [2CO 3:3](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:3), [2CO 3:6](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:6); [HEB 8:10–8:11](https://ref.ly/Heb8:10-Heb8:11)

### The promised Spirit will renew Israel

**[EZK 36:26–36:27](https://ref.ly/Ezek36:26-Ezek36:27)** *See also* [EZK 11:19](https://ref.ly/Ezek11:19), [EZK 37:14](https://ref.ly/Ezek37:14)

### The promised Spirit will empower the life and work of the Messiah

**[ISA 61:1–61:2](https://ref.ly/Isa61:1-Isa61:2)** *See also* [ISA 11:2](https://ref.ly/Isa11:2); [LUK 4:18–4:19](https://ref.ly/Luke4:18-Luke4:19)

**[JOL 2:28–2:29](https://ref.ly/Joel2:28-Joel2:29)** *See also* [ACT 2:17–2:18](https://ref.ly/Acts2:17-Acts2:18)
