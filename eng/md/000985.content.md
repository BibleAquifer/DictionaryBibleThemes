## 5404: masters (id: 5404)

Those who obtain work from others, whether in the capacity of unpaid service or paid employment. Scripture sets out both the obligations and rights of masters in relation to those who work for them.

Masters have obligations to their servants
------------------------------------------

### To deal fairly with them

**[JOB 31:13–31:14](https://ref.ly/Job31:13-Job31:14)** *See also* [2KI 22:7](https://ref.ly/2Kgs22:7); [JOB 31:38–31:40](https://ref.ly/Job31:38-Job31:40); [MAT 10:10–10:11](https://ref.ly/Matt10:10-Matt10:11) parallel [LUK 10:7](https://ref.ly/Luke10:7); [1TI 5:18](https://ref.ly/1Tim5:18)

### To be generous

**[DEU 15:12–15:15](https://ref.ly/Deut15:12-Deut15:15), [DEU 15:18](https://ref.ly/Deut15:18)** *See also* [PHM 1:12–1:16](https://ref.ly/Phlm1:12-Phlm1:16)

### To make provision for rest and worship

**[DEU 5:12–5:15](https://ref.ly/Deut5:12-Deut5:15)** parallel [EXO 20:8–20:11](https://ref.ly/Exod20:8-Exod20:11) *See also* [EXO 12:43–12:44](https://ref.ly/Exod12:43-Exod12:44); [DEU 16:11–16:16](https://ref.ly/Deut16:11-Deut16:16)

### Not to defraud them

**[LEV 19:13](https://ref.ly/Lev19:13)** *Day labourers were paid in the evening (see [Mt 20:8](https://ref.ly/Matt20:8)). Delaying payment could cause great hardship to a poor man.* *See also* [DEU 24:15](https://ref.ly/Deut24:15); [MAL 3:5](https://ref.ly/Mal3:5); [JAS 5:4](https://ref.ly/Jas5:4)

### Not to exploit them

**[DEU 24:14](https://ref.ly/Deut24:14)** *See also* [GEN 31:7](https://ref.ly/Gen31:7), [GEN 31:41](https://ref.ly/Gen31:41); [ISA 58:3](https://ref.ly/Isa58:3); [JER 22:13](https://ref.ly/Jer22:13); [ACT 16:16](https://ref.ly/Acts16:16), [ACT 16:19](https://ref.ly/Acts16:19)

### Not to neglect them

**[DEU 12:19](https://ref.ly/Deut12:19)** *See also* [DEU 14:27](https://ref.ly/Deut14:27)

### Not to oppress them

**[EPH 6:9](https://ref.ly/Eph6:9)** *See also* [GEN 16:6](https://ref.ly/Gen16:6); [EXO 1:11–1:14](https://ref.ly/Exod1:11-Exod1:14), [EXO 21:20–21:21](https://ref.ly/Exod21:20-Exod21:21), [EXO 21:26–21:27](https://ref.ly/Exod21:26-Exod21:27); [LEV 25:39–25:55](https://ref.ly/Lev25:39-Lev25:55); [1KI 12:4](https://ref.ly/1Kgs12:4), [1KI 12:10–12:14](https://ref.ly/1Kgs12:10-1Kgs12:14); [ISA 30:12–30:13](https://ref.ly/Isa30:12-Isa30:13)
