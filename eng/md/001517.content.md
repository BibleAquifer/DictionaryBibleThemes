## 6130: corruption (id: 6130)

A state of spiritual decay and moral dishonesty, arising from the effects of sin, which expresses itself in disobedience towards God.

Corruption of nature as a result of the fall
--------------------------------------------

**[GEN 6:11](https://ref.ly/Gen6:11)**
