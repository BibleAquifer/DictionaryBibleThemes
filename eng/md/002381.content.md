## 8752: false worship (id: 8752)

Forms of worship that are not worthy of God, especially worship offered without sincerity and obedience or that is contrary to the commands of God.

Examples of false worship
-------------------------

### Worship conducted in disobedience to God

**[LEV 10:1–10:2](https://ref.ly/Lev10:1-Lev10:2)** *See also* [NUM 3:2–3:4](https://ref.ly/Num3:2-Num3:4); [1SA 15:1–15:23](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:1-1Sam15:23); [2KI 17:33](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:33)

### Worship and sacrifices offered by those who were not priests

**[1SA 13:11–13:14](https://ref.ly/1Sam13:11-1Sam13:14)** *See also* [2CH 26:19–26:20](https://ref.ly/2Chr26:19-2Chr26:20)

### Insincere worship

**[ISA 29:13](https://ref.ly/Isa29:13)** *See also* [MAT 15:8–15:9](https://ref.ly/Matt15:8-Matt15:9); [MRK 7:6–7:7](https://ref.ly/Mark7:6-Mark7:7); [COL 2:23](https://ref.ly/Col2:23)

### Worship lacking in due humility and reverence

**[MAT 6:5](https://ref.ly/Matt6:5)** *See also* [LUK 18:10–18:14](https://ref.ly/Luke18:10-Luke18:14)

### Worship conducted in ignorance of God

**[JHN 4:22](https://ref.ly/John4:22)** *See also* [ACT 17:22–17:23](https://ref.ly/Acts17:22-Acts17:23)

Scripture vividly contrasts false worship with true worship
-----------------------------------------------------------

### True worship involves the worshipper's heart, soul and mind

[DEU 6:5](https://ref.ly/Deut6:5) parallels [MAT 22:37](https://ref.ly/Matt22:37), [MRK 12:30](https://ref.ly/Mark12:30), [LUK 10:27](https://ref.ly/Luke10:27)

### True worship is costly

[2SA 24:24](https://ref.ly/2Sam24:24); ; [MRK 12:41–12:44](https://ref.ly/Mark12:41-Mark12:44); ; [ROM 12:1](https://ref.ly/Rom12:1) *True worship involves the whole of a person's life and not just formal acts of worship.*

### True worship is in the spirit

**[JHN 4:23–4:24](https://ref.ly/John4:23-John4:24)** *See also* [ISA 29:23](https://ref.ly/Isa29:23); [PHP 3:3](https://ref.ly/Phil3:3); [1TI 2:9](https://ref.ly/1Tim2:9); [HEB 12:28](https://ref.ly/Heb12:28)
