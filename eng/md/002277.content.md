## 8497: witnessing, approaches to (id: 8497)

Scripture provides teaching, supported by examples, concerning when believers should witness, the methods they are to use, and the manner of doing it.

When and where to witness
-------------------------

### In the home and family

**[MRK 5:18–5:19](https://ref.ly/Mark5:18-Mark5:19)** parallel [LUK 8:38–8:39](https://ref.ly/Luke8:38-Luke8:39) *See also* [2KI 5:1–5:3](https://ref.ly/2Kgs5:1-2Kgs5:3); [JHN 1:41](https://ref.ly/John1:41)

### To individuals

**[JHN 1:40–1:42](https://ref.ly/John1:40-John1:42)** *See also* [JHN 4:7–4:26](https://ref.ly/John4:7-John4:26); [ACT 8:30–8:35](https://ref.ly/Acts8:30-Acts8:35)

### To assembled groups

**[ACT 17:17](https://ref.ly/Acts17:17)** *See also* [ACT 5:42](https://ref.ly/Acts5:42), [ACT 16:13](https://ref.ly/Acts16:13), [ACT 19:8–19:9](https://ref.ly/Acts19:8-Acts19:9)
