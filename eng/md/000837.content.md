## 5249: census (id: 5249)

The counting of individuals, usually for the purpose of war or taxation.

Motivations for a census
------------------------

### To count the number of fighting men available

**[NUM 1:1–1:4](https://ref.ly/Num1:1-Num1:4), [NUM 26:1–26:4](https://ref.ly/Num26:1-Num26:4)** *See also* [NUM 1:5–1:49](https://ref.ly/Num1:5-Num1:49), [NUM 26:5–26:65](https://ref.ly/Num26:5-Num26:65)

### For ascertaining those qualified to be included in the Jewish community

[EZR 2:1–2:65](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:1-Ezra2:65) parallel [NEH 7:6–7:67](https://ref.ly/Neh7:6-Neh7:67)

### For purposes of taxation

**[LUK 2:2](https://ref.ly/Luke2:2)**; **[ACT 5:37](https://ref.ly/Acts5:37)** *See also* [EXO 30:12](https://ref.ly/Exod30:12)

God's anger at David's unauthorised census
------------------------------------------

[2SA 24:1–24:9](https://ref.ly/2Sam24:1-2Sam24:9) parallel [1CH 21:1–21:6](https://ref.ly/1Chr21:1-1Chr21:6) *David's sin in taking this census was probably that he did so from a motive of self\-sufficiency rather than trusting in God.*
