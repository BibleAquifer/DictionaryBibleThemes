## 5220: authority, abuse of (id: 5220)

The misuse of a person's freedom of action in order to exploit, oppress or treat other people in a way that is not for their good.

Warnings against the abuse of authority
---------------------------------------

### By employers over employees

**[EPH 6:9](https://ref.ly/Eph6:9)** *See also* [LEV 25:43](https://ref.ly/Lev25:43); [DEU 24:14](https://ref.ly/Deut24:14)

### By fathers over children

**[EPH 6:4](https://ref.ly/Eph6:4)** parallel [COL 3:21](https://ref.ly/Col3:21)

### By husbands over wives

**[COL 3:19](https://ref.ly/Col3:19)**

### By elders over the church

**[1PE 5:2–5:3](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:2-1Pet5:3)** *See also* [EZK 34:2–34:7](https://ref.ly/Ezek34:2-Ezek34:7)

### By those mistreating foreigners

**[EXO 22:21](https://ref.ly/Exod22:21)** *See also* [EXO 23:9](https://ref.ly/Exod23:9)
