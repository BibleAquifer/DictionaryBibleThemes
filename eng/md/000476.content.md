## 4522: threshing (id: 4522)

The process of beating out or separating grain from harvested stalks.

Threshing and the harvest
-------------------------

**[LEV 26:5](https://ref.ly/Lev26:5)** *See also* [JDG 6:11](https://ref.ly/Judg6:11); [1CH 21:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr21:20); [1CO 9:9](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:9); [DEU 25:4](https://ref.ly/Deut25:4); [1TI 5:18](https://ref.ly/1Tim5:18) *Paul is adapting a humane OT law to the support of Christian workers.*

Threshing implements
--------------------

**[2SA 24:22](https://ref.ly/2Sam24:22)** parallel [1CH 21:23](https://ref.ly/1Chr21:23) *A threshing\-sledge was a board studded underneath with sharp stones which was dragged by oxen over the wheat ears in order to separate the kernels.* *See also* [JOB 41:30](https://ref.ly/Job41:30); [ISA 28:27–28:28](https://ref.ly/Isa28:27-Isa28:28), [ISA 41:15](https://ref.ly/Isa41:15)
