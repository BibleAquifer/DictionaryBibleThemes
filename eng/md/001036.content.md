## 5460: prison (id: 5460)

A place of confinement, such as a jail or dungeon, used to imprison individuals.

Irregular prisons
-----------------

**[GEN 37:22](https://ref.ly/Gen37:22)** *A cistern was usually a plaster\-coated underground rock cavity used for storing water.* *See also* [NUM 12:14–12:15](https://ref.ly/Num12:14-Num12:15) *Miriam's confinement may have been as much isolation as punishment*; [JER 38:6](https://ref.ly/Jer38:6), [JER 38:13](https://ref.ly/Jer38:13)
