## 8608: prayer, and worship (id: 8608)

Worship is turning to God in awe, praise and joy, as his people realise how wonderful he is. Prayer is a natural part of worship: to know God is to want to worship him and pray to him.

Worship is a fundamental requirement of life
--------------------------------------------

### All nations are exhorted to worship God

**[1CH 16:28–16:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:28-1Chr16:29)** *See also* [PSA 29:1–29:2](https://ref.ly/Ps29:1-Ps29:2), [PSA 96:9](https://ref.ly/Ps96:9)

### Israel is commanded to worship God

**[2KI 17:36](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:36)** *See also* [PSA 95:6–95:7](https://ref.ly/Ps95:6-Ps95:7), [PSA 99:4–99:5](https://ref.ly/Ps99:4-Ps99:5)

Right attitudes in worship are imperative
-----------------------------------------

### Reverence and humility characterise acceptable worship

**[HEB 12:28–12:29](https://ref.ly/Heb12:28-Heb12:29)** *See also* [PSA 5:7](https://ref.ly/Ps5:7), [PSA 95:6](https://ref.ly/Ps95:6), [PSA 138:2](https://ref.ly/Ps138:2); [ECC 5:1](https://ref.ly/Eccl5:1)

### Honesty, without hypocrisy, characterises acceptable worship

**[AMO 5:21–5:24](https://ref.ly/Amos5:21-Amos5:24)** *See also* [MAT 15:7–15:9](https://ref.ly/Matt15:7-Matt15:9) parallel [MRK 7:6–7:7](https://ref.ly/Mark7:6-Mark7:7); [ISA 29:13](https://ref.ly/Isa29:13); [LUK 18:9–18:14](https://ref.ly/Luke18:9-Luke18:14); [JHN 4:24](https://ref.ly/John4:24)
