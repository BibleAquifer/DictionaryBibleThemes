## 5739: twins (id: 5739)

Two children born of the same parents at the same time. Figuratively used to describe a matching pair.

Examples of twins
-----------------

**[GEN 25:21–25:26](https://ref.ly/Gen25:21-Gen25:26)** *Although Jacob and Esau were twins, their very different characters indicate that God deals with and relates to people individually.* *See also* [GEN 38:27–38:30](https://ref.ly/Gen38:27-Gen38:30); [JHN 20:24](https://ref.ly/John20:24) *Didymus means “twin”. It is not known who his twin was*; [JHN 21:2](https://ref.ly/John21:2); [ACT 28:11](https://ref.ly/Acts28:11) *the mythical twin sons of Zeus regarded as special protectors of sailors*

Twins used to illustrate God's election
---------------------------------------

[ROM 9:10–9:16](https://ref.ly/Rom9:10-Rom9:16)
