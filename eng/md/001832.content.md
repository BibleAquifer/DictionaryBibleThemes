## 7429: Sabbath, in OT (id: 7429)

The Sabbath of rest is grounded in God's work of creation. Observance of a Sabbath day is distinctive of the people of God.

The Sabbath grounded in creation itself
---------------------------------------

**[GEN 2:3](https://ref.ly/Gen2:3)** *See also* [PSA 118:24](https://ref.ly/Ps118:24)

The purpose of the Sabbath
--------------------------

### To remember God's work in creation

**[EXO 20:8–20:11](https://ref.ly/Exod20:8-Exod20:11)** *See also* [GEN 2:2](https://ref.ly/Gen2:2); [EXO 35:2](https://ref.ly/Exod35:2)

### To remember the exodus

**[DEU 5:12–5:15](https://ref.ly/Deut5:12-Deut5:15)** *The idea of rest relates the concept of the Sabbath to the ultimate conquest of Canaan under David and to the return from exile.* *See also* [GEN 8:4](https://ref.ly/Gen8:4); [2SA 7:1](https://ref.ly/2Sam7:1), [2SA 7:11](https://ref.ly/2Sam7:11); [PSA 95:10–95:11](https://ref.ly/Ps95:10-Ps95:11); [HEB 4:9](https://ref.ly/Heb4:9); [REV 14:13](https://ref.ly/Rev14:13)

### To be a sign of the relationship between Israel and God and to give refreshment

**[EXO 31:17](https://ref.ly/Exod31:17)** *See also* [DEU 5:12–5:14](https://ref.ly/Deut5:12-Deut5:14)

The Law required the Sabbath to be a holy day free from work
------------------------------------------------------------

**[LEV 23:3](https://ref.ly/Lev23:3)** *See also* [EXO 34:21](https://ref.ly/Exod34:21), [EXO 35:3](https://ref.ly/Exod35:3); [LEV 23:38](https://ref.ly/Lev23:38); [ISA 56:2](https://ref.ly/Isa56:2), [ISA 58:13](https://ref.ly/Isa58:13)
