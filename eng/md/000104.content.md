## 1450: signs, kinds of (id: 1450)

Signs are miraculous events, supernatural phenomena or everyday things that are given a special significance. The term is used in John's Gospel to describe the miracles of Jesus Christ.

Miraculous signs
----------------

**[2CO 12:12](https://ref.ly/2Cor12:12)** *See also* [EXO 10:1](https://ref.ly/Exod10:1); [PSA 78:43](https://ref.ly/Ps78:43), [PSA 105:27](https://ref.ly/Ps105:27); [MAT 24:24](https://ref.ly/Matt24:24) parallel [MRK 13:22](https://ref.ly/Mark13:22); [MRK 16:17](https://ref.ly/Mark16:17); [ACT 2:43](https://ref.ly/Acts2:43); [ROM 15:19](https://ref.ly/Rom15:19)

The signs of Jesus Christ in John's Gospel
------------------------------------------

**[JHN 2:11](https://ref.ly/John2:11)** *John refers to the miracles of Jesus Christ as “signs” in order to bring out the significance of these deeds.* *See also* [JHN 6:26](https://ref.ly/John6:26) *Jesus Christ, the bread of life*; [JHN 9:16](https://ref.ly/John9:16) *Jesus Christ, the light of the world*; [JHN 11:47](https://ref.ly/John11:47) *Jesus Christ, the resurrection and the life*
