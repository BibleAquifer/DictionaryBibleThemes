## 8776: lies (id: 8776)

Statements or answers which are untrue. Lying, false witness, deceit and false dealings are all condemned in Scripture.

Lying is condemned
------------------

**[PSA 34:12–34:13](https://ref.ly/Ps34:12-Ps34:13)**; **[EPH 4:25](https://ref.ly/Eph4:25)**; **[COL 3:9–3:10](https://ref.ly/Col3:9-Col3:10)** *See also* [EXO 20:16](https://ref.ly/Exod20:16) parallel [DEU 5:20](https://ref.ly/Deut5:20); [LEV 19:11–19:12](https://ref.ly/Lev19:11-Lev19:12); [PRO 6:16–6:17](https://ref.ly/Prov6:16-Prov6:17), [PRO 12:22](https://ref.ly/Prov12:22); [JER 17:9–17:10](https://ref.ly/Jer17:9-Jer17:10)
