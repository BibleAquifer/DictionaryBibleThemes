## 8801: presumption (id: 8801)

A form of self\-confidence, which makes overconfident assumptions concerning one's importance and rights. It is criticised as a form of arrogance that is unacceptable among believers, whose lives should be characterised by humility.

Presumption is unacceptable
---------------------------

**[PSA 5:5](https://ref.ly/Ps5:5)**; **[ISA 13:11](https://ref.ly/Isa13:11)** *See also* [NUM 15:30](https://ref.ly/Num15:30); [DEU 6:16](https://ref.ly/Deut6:16), [DEU 18:20](https://ref.ly/Deut18:20); [ISA 45:9](https://ref.ly/Isa45:9); [ROM 2:4](https://ref.ly/Rom2:4), [ROM 9:20](https://ref.ly/Rom9:20); [1CO 10:9](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:9); [JAS 4:13](https://ref.ly/Jas4:13); [2PE 2:10](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:10)

Examples of presumption
-----------------------

[GEN 11:1–11:4](https://ref.ly/Gen11:1-Gen11:4) *those preparing to build the tower of Babel in defiance of God*; ; [NUM 14:40–14:45](https://ref.ly/Num14:40-Num14:45) *the Israelites, ignoring Moses' orders*; ; [LUK 12:16–12:21](https://ref.ly/Luke12:16-Luke12:21) *the rich fool*
