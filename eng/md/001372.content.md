## 5851: excuse (id: 5851)

The attempt to deny responsibility for an action or for a lack of action. There is no excuse for sin.

Examples of excuses given for wrong actions
-------------------------------------------

[GEN 3:12–3:13](https://ref.ly/Gen3:12-Gen3:13) *by Adam and Eve for eating the forbidden fruit*; ; [EXO 32:22–32:24](https://ref.ly/Exod32:22-Exod32:24) *by Aaron for making the golden calf*; ; [1SA 13:11–13:12](https://ref.ly/1Sam13:11-1Sam13:12) *by Saul for acting as priest*; [1SA 15:20–15:21](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:20-1Sam15:21), [1SA 15:24–15:25](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:24-1Sam15:25)

Examples of excuses given for inaction
--------------------------------------

By Moses: [EXO 3:11](https://ref.ly/Exod3:11), [EXO 4:10](https://ref.ly/Exod4:10); [JDG 6:15](https://ref.ly/Judg6:15) *by Gideon*; [PRO 22:13](https://ref.ly/Prov22:13), [PRO 26:13](https://ref.ly/Prov26:13); [JER 1:6](https://ref.ly/Jer1:6) *by Jeremiah*; [MAT 25:14–25:30](https://ref.ly/Matt25:14-Matt25:30); ; [LUK 19:12–19:27](https://ref.ly/Luke19:12-Luke19:27); [MAT 25:41–25:45](https://ref.ly/Matt25:41-Matt25:45) *by the ignorant*
