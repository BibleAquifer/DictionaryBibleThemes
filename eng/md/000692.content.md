## 5095: Jacob, life and character of (id: 5095)

The grandson of Abraham and son of Isaac, who was chosen by God, despite his personal faults and shortcomings, to be the recipient of the promises made to Abraham. His name means “he grasps the heel”, an allusion to the circumstances of his birth ([Ge 25:25–26](https://ref.ly/Gen25:25-Gen25:26)).
