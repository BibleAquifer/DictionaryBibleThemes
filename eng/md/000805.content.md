## 5216: authority, nature of (id: 5216)

The God\-given freedom of action expressed in rightful power to control the actions of others.

Ultimate authority belongs to God, who does as he pleases
---------------------------------------------------------

**[PSA 115:3](https://ref.ly/Ps115:3)** *See also* [2CH 20:6](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:6); [JOB 9:12](https://ref.ly/Job9:12), [JOB 23:13](https://ref.ly/Job23:13), [JOB 42:2](https://ref.ly/Job42:2); [PSA 135:6](https://ref.ly/Ps135:6); [ISA 14:27](https://ref.ly/Isa14:27), [ISA 43:13](https://ref.ly/Isa43:13), [ISA 45:9](https://ref.ly/Isa45:9), [ISA 46:10](https://ref.ly/Isa46:10); [DAN 4:35](https://ref.ly/Dan4:35) *Nebuchadnezzar acknowledges God's authority*; [ROM 9:19–9:21](https://ref.ly/Rom9:19-Rom9:21); [EPH 1:11](https://ref.ly/Eph1:11) *God's authority is demonstrated in election.*

Human authority
---------------

### All human authority is given and established by God

**[ROM 13:1](https://ref.ly/Rom13:1)** *See also* [DAN 2:21](https://ref.ly/Dan2:21), [DAN 4:17](https://ref.ly/Dan4:17), [DAN 5:18–5:21](https://ref.ly/Dan5:18-Dan5:21); [JHN 19:11](https://ref.ly/John19:11)

### It involves the rightful freedom to give orders

**[MAT 8:9](https://ref.ly/Matt8:9)** parallel [LUK 7:8](https://ref.ly/Luke7:8) *The centurion to Jesus Christ.* *See also* [GEN 41:35](https://ref.ly/Gen41:35); [NUM 27:20](https://ref.ly/Num27:20); [DEU 1:15](https://ref.ly/Deut1:15) *Moses delegates authority*; [JDG 9:29](https://ref.ly/Judg9:29); [EZR 7:24](https://ref.ly/Ezra7:24); [NEH 3:7](https://ref.ly/Neh3:7); [EST 9:29](https://ref.ly/Esth9:29); [ISA 22:21](https://ref.ly/Isa22:21); [JER 38:10](https://ref.ly/Jer38:10); [MAT 25:23](https://ref.ly/Matt25:23) parallel [LUK 19:17](https://ref.ly/Luke19:17) *the parable of the talents*; [MRK 13:34](https://ref.ly/Mark13:34); [LUK 20:20](https://ref.ly/Luke20:20), [LUK 23:7](https://ref.ly/Luke23:7); [JHN 19:10](https://ref.ly/John19:10)ACT [9:14](https://ref.ly/John9:14), [ACT 26:10](https://ref.ly/Acts26:10), [ACT 26:12](https://ref.ly/Acts26:12)[1PE 2:13](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:13)

### It is limited, unlike God's authority

**[JHN 19:10–19:11](https://ref.ly/John19:10-John19:11)**

Responsibilities of those with authority
----------------------------------------

### They are to act as servants, not lording it over people

**[MAT 20:25–20:28](https://ref.ly/Matt20:25-Matt20:28)** parallel [MRK 10:42–10:45](https://ref.ly/Mark10:42-Mark10:45) *See also* [JHN 13:4–13:15](https://ref.ly/John13:4-John13:15)

### Their authority is to be used for the benefit of others

**[2CO 10:8](https://ref.ly/2Cor10:8)** *See also* [2CO 13:10](https://ref.ly/2Cor13:10)

### It is sometimes necessary to withhold exercising authority for others' good

**[1CO 9:4–9:18](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:4-1Cor9:18)** *See also* [1CO 6:1–6:7](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:1-1Cor6:7), [1CO 8:8–8:13](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:8-1Cor8:13), [1CO 10:23–10:24](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:23-1Cor10:24)
