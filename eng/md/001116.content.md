## 5550: speech, negative aspects of (id: 5550)

The sinfulness of fallen human nature can express itself in speech, provoking God's displeasure and causing enmity and division amongst people.

Evil speech comes from a corrupt heart

**[MAT 12:33–12:35](https://ref.ly/Matt12:33-Matt12:35)** *See also* [PRO 2:12–2:15](https://ref.ly/Prov2:12-Prov2:15), [PRO 24:1–24:2](https://ref.ly/Prov24:1-Prov24:2); [MAT 15:10–15:11](https://ref.ly/Matt15:10-Matt15:11) parallel [MRK 7:14–7:15](https://ref.ly/Mark7:14-Mark7:15); [LUK 6:43–6:45](https://ref.ly/Luke6:43-Luke6:45)
