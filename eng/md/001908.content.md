## 7734: leaders, spiritual (id: 7734)

Under the old covenant God called priests and prophets as spiritual leaders. Later the Pharisees gave spiritual leadership. After Jesus Christ had inaugurated the new covenant, authority was given to the apostles, who in turn appointed leaders in the churches they founded.

Priests as spiritual leaders
----------------------------

### They were called by God

**[HEB 5:4](https://ref.ly/Heb5:4)** *See also* [EXO 28:1](https://ref.ly/Exod28:1); [DEU 18:5](https://ref.ly/Deut18:5)

### The high priest represented the people in the presence of God

**[HEB 5:1](https://ref.ly/Heb5:1)** *See also* [EXO 28:29–28:30](https://ref.ly/Exod28:29-Exod28:30); [NUM 27:21](https://ref.ly/Num27:21); [HEB 8:3](https://ref.ly/Heb8:3)

### Priests taught and explained God's law

**[MAL 2:7](https://ref.ly/Mal2:7)** *See also* [LEV 10:11](https://ref.ly/Lev10:11); [HAG 2:11–2:13](https://ref.ly/Hag2:11-Hag2:13)

### Examples of priests as spiritual leaders
