## 7322: burnt offering (id: 7322)

Probably the earliest and most basic form of sacrifice. It is seen fundamentally as a gift to God, either in thanksgiving for his goodness or for atonement for sin.

Hebrew names for the burnt offering
-----------------------------------

**[DEU 33:10](https://ref.ly/Deut33:10)** *The rarer Hebrew expression means something whole or complete*; **[PSA 66:13](https://ref.ly/Ps66:13)** *The commoner expression in Hebrew means that which “goes up” to God.* *See also* [1SA 7:9](https://ref.ly/1Sam7:9); [PSA 51:19](https://ref.ly/Ps51:19)
