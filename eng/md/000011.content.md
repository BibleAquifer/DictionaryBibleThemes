## 1050: God, goodness of (id: 1050)

All God's perfect qualities are made freely available to all for the benefit of the whole world.

God alone is good
-----------------

**[MRK 10:18](https://ref.ly/Mark10:18)** parallels [MAT 19:17](https://ref.ly/Matt19:17), [LUK 18:19](https://ref.ly/Luke18:19)
