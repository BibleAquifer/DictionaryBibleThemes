## 5489: rank (id: 5489)

A person's seniority and/or status, especially in military, government or priestly positions. Christians should not be concerned about rank or status.

Officers in the NT church
-------------------------

In contrast to the priesthood, offices within the NT Church are according to gifts; individuals with different gifts are listed but do not form a hierarchy: [ROM 12:4–12:8](https://ref.ly/Rom12:4-Rom12:8); ; [1CO 12:28–12:30](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:28-1Cor12:30); ; [EPH 4:11](https://ref.ly/Eph4:11); [PHP 1:1](https://ref.ly/Phil1:1); ; [1TI 3:1–3:13](https://ref.ly/1Tim3:1-1Tim3:13)

### Some elders are worthy of double honour

[1TI 5:17](https://ref.ly/1Tim5:17) *Comparison between verses 17 and 28 of [Acts 20](https://ref.ly/Acts20:1-Acts20:999) shows that the same individuals could be called “elders”, “overseers” or “shepherds” (pastors).*
