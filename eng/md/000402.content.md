## 4336: iron (id: 4336)

Iron ore is mined from the earth
--------------------------------

**[JOB 28:2](https://ref.ly/Job28:2)** *See also* [DEU 8:7–8:9](https://ref.ly/Deut8:7-Deut8:9)

Iron ore is refined by smelting in a furnace
--------------------------------------------

### Such imagery is often used of God's testing of his people

**[EZK 22:20](https://ref.ly/Ezek22:20)** *See also* [NUM 31:21–31:23](https://ref.ly/Num31:21-Num31:23); [JER 6:27–6:29](https://ref.ly/Jer6:27-Jer6:29); [EZK 22:18](https://ref.ly/Ezek22:18)
