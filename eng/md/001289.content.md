## 5764: attitudes, negative to God (id: 5764)

Scripture identifies a range of negative attitudes towards God, which believers are encouraged to avoid.

Rejection
---------

**[ISA 53:3](https://ref.ly/Isa53:3)** *See also* [LEV 26:15–26:16](https://ref.ly/Lev26:15-Lev26:16); [1SA 8:7](https://ref.ly/1Sam8:7); [EZK 5:6](https://ref.ly/Ezek5:6), [EZK 20:15–20:16](https://ref.ly/Ezek20:15-Ezek20:16); [JHN 1:11](https://ref.ly/John1:11)
