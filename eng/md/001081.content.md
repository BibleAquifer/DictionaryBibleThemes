## 5512: scales and balances (id: 5512)

Weighing devices designed to ensure fair dealing. The terms are also used metaphorically in Scripture.

Scales and balances were used in business transactions
------------------------------------------------------

[JER 32:10](https://ref.ly/Jer32:10)

Scales and balances should not be used dishonestly
--------------------------------------------------

### Exhortations to honest dealing

**[LEV 19:35–19:36](https://ref.ly/Lev19:35-Lev19:36)** *See also* [EZK 45:10](https://ref.ly/Ezek45:10)

### The LORD hates dishonest dealing

**[PRO 20:10](https://ref.ly/Prov20:10)** *See also* [PRO 11:1](https://ref.ly/Prov11:1), [PRO 16:11](https://ref.ly/Prov16:11), [PRO 20:23](https://ref.ly/Prov20:23)

### Dishonest dealing was one of the reasons for God's judgment on his people

**[JER 5:1](https://ref.ly/Jer5:1)** *See also* [HOS 12:6–12:7](https://ref.ly/Hos12:6-Hos12:7); [AMO 8:4–8:8](https://ref.ly/Amos8:4-Amos8:8); [MIC 6:10–6:13](https://ref.ly/Mic6:10-Mic6:13)

Scales and balances used metaphorically
---------------------------------------

### To emphasise Job's distress

**[JOB 6:2](https://ref.ly/Job6:2)**

### To highlight God's greatness

**[ISA 40:15](https://ref.ly/Isa40:15)** *See also* [ISA 40:12](https://ref.ly/Isa40:12)

### Of God's judgment

**[DAN 5:25–5:30](https://ref.ly/Dan5:25-Dan5:30)** *See also* [EZK 5:1–5:2](https://ref.ly/Ezek5:1-Ezek5:2); [REV 6:5–6:6](https://ref.ly/Rev6:5-Rev6:6)
