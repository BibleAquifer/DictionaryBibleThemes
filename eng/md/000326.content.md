## 4116: angels, opposed to God (id: 4116)

Spiritual beings who fell with Satan. They are under God's judgment and are subject to the authority of believers.

Spiritual authorities led by Satan
----------------------------------

**[2CO 11:14](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:14)** *See also* [JHN 12:31](https://ref.ly/John12:31), [JHN 16:11](https://ref.ly/John16:11); [EPH 6:12](https://ref.ly/Eph6:12); [REV 9:11](https://ref.ly/Rev9:11)

Angels opposed to God will be judged
------------------------------------

**[JUD 1:6](https://ref.ly/Jude1:6)** *See also* [JOB 4:18](https://ref.ly/Job4:18), [JOB 15:15](https://ref.ly/Job15:15); [MAT 25:41](https://ref.ly/Matt25:41); [2PE 2:4](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:4); [REV 20:10](https://ref.ly/Rev20:10)

Jesus Christ has defeated Satan and his angels
----------------------------------------------

**[COL 2:15](https://ref.ly/Col2:15)** *See also* [GEN 3:15](https://ref.ly/Gen3:15); [EPH 1:19–1:21](https://ref.ly/Eph1:19-Eph1:21)

Satan's angels are resisted by God's faithful angels
----------------------------------------------------

**[REV 12:7–12:9](https://ref.ly/Rev12:7-Rev12:9)** *See also* [DAN 10:13](https://ref.ly/Dan10:13), [DAN 10:20](https://ref.ly/Dan10:20) *The princes of Persia and Greece appear to be spiritual powers who resist God's rule over these regions.*
