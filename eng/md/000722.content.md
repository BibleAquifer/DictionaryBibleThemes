## 5133: blindness (id: 5133)

The inability on the part of someone to see properly. Blindness is often used in Scripture as a symbol of human sin.

* **Associated Articles:** 5134: blindness, natural (ID: `5134@DictionaryBibleThemes`); 5135: blindness, spiritual (ID: `5135@DictionaryBibleThemes`)
